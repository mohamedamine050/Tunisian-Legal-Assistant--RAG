When the term of an arbitrator ends in accordance with Article 58 or Article 59 of this Code, or due to his resignation for any other reason, or due to his removal by agreement of the parties, or in any other case of termination of the assignment, a substitute arbitrator shall be appointed according to the rules that were applicable to the appointment of the replaced arbitrator.  The appointment shall be made in accordance with the principles applicable to appointing a suitable individual for a position that aligns with the qualifications and competencies required to perform its duties.
