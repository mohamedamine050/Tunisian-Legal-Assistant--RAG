The arbitral tribunal shall render its award by a majority vote after deliberation. The award shall include all the particulars required by Article 123 of the Code of Civil Procedure, taking into account the provisions of Article 14 of the Code of Arbitration concerning conciliator arbitrators.

The award must be signed by the arbitrators.

If one or more arbitrators refuse or are unable to sign, this shall be stated in the award.

The award shall be valid if signed by a majority of the arbitrators.

If no majority is formed, the chairman of the arbitral tribunal shall state this and render the award according to his own opinion. In this case, his signature alone shall suffice on the award.
