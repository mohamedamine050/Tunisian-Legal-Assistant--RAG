## English Translation:

1. **Subject to the provisions of this Chapter, the parties may agree on the procedures to be followed by the arbitral tribunal.**

2. **If they do not agree, the arbitral tribunal, subject to the provisions of this Law, may conduct the arbitration in any manner it deems appropriate.**  **The powers vested in the arbitral tribunal include the power to determine the form of communication of information, which shall be formulated in a semi-finalized manner for future access.**  **Furthermore, the arbitral tribunal has the power to decide on the admissibility of evidence presented, its relevance to the subject matter, and its probative value and importance.**
