The Arbitral Tribunal shall consider the request for amendment, interpretation or supplementation within thirty days from the date it undertakes the request, which must be submitted to it by the most diligent party, within a period not exceeding twenty days from the date it is notified of the Arbitral Tribunal's decision.

If the Arbitral Tribunal is unable to reconvene, the decision amending, interpreting or supplementing the decision shall be made by the President of the court in whose jurisdiction the arbitration was held, within a period not exceeding thirty days.
