The arbitral tribunal may, upon a request submitted by a party within twenty days from the date of notification of the award and after notifying the other party to present any observations within fifteen days of receiving the notification, perform the following actions without reopening the proceedings for argument and discussion:

1. Correct any clerical or computational error, or any material error that has crept into the award. 

2. Explain a specific part of the award. 

3. Issue a supplementary decision on a part of the original request that was inadvertently omitted from the award.

The decision rendered in any of the aforementioned forms shall be an integral part of the original award.
