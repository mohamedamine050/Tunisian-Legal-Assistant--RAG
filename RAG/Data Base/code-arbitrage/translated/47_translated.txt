## English Translation:

1. The provisions of this chapter apply to international arbitration, taking into account the international agreements that the Tunisian State has committed to implement.

2. With the exception of the provisions of Articles 53, 54, 80, 81, and 82 of this Code, the provisions of this chapter shall only apply if the place of arbitration is located within the Tunisian territory, or if these provisions are chosen, either by the parties or by the arbitral tribunal.
