## English Translation:

1. The arbitral tribunal may:

   a) Appoint one or more experts to provide a report, or any form of information transmission, which is formulated in a semi-final format to reach the future, on specific matters determined by it.

   b) Request any party to provide the expert with any relevant information, or allow the expert to access any document for examination, or view any goods or other assets for inspection.

2. After the submission of the report, or any form of information transmission, which is formulated in a semi-final format to reach the future, the expert shall attend a hearing to discuss the examination he conducted, and to hear the testimony of other experts on the matter, if requested by one of the parties or deemed necessary by the arbitral tribunal. 

All of the above shall apply unless otherwise agreed by the parties.
