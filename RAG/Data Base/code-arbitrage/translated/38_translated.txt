The arbitral tribunal shall deliver a copy of the corrective, interpretative or supplementary award to the parties within 15 days of its issuance. Within the same timeframe, the original of such award shall be deposited with the registry of the competent court in exchange for a receipt. This deposit shall not be subject to any fee.

The original corrective, interpretative or supplementary award shall remain deposited with the registry of the court along with the original arbitral award. The clerk shall mention the corrective, interpretative or supplementary award in the minutes of the latter.

An application for enforcement of the corrective, interpretative or supplementary award shall be considered together with the original award.
