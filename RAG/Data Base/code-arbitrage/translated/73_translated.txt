## English Translation:

1. The arbitral tribunal shall decide the dispute in accordance with the provisions of the law designated by the parties.

2. If the parties do not specify the applicable law, the arbitral tribunal shall apply the law it deems appropriate.

3. The arbitral tribunal may decide the dispute according to principles of equity if the parties expressly authorize it to do so.

4. In all cases, the arbitral tribunal shall decide the dispute in accordance with the terms of the contract, taking into account the relevant trade usage applicable to the transaction.
