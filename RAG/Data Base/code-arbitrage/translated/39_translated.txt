**Appeals are not permitted:**

1. Against the judgments of amicable arbitrators.

2. Against arbitration awards unless the arbitration agreement expressly provides otherwise.

In the latter case, the appeal shall be conducted in accordance with the rules prescribed for judicial judgments in the Code of Civil Procedure.

If the Court of Appeal upholds the challenged arbitration award, it shall authorize its enforcement.

If the Court of Appeal overturns the award, it shall decide on the merits of the case by a judicial judgment.
