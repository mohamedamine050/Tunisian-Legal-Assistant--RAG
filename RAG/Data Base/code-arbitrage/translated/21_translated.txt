If the arbitrator becomes legally or practically unable to perform his duty, or fails to perform it within a period of thirty days, this duty shall terminate by his relinquishment thereof; otherwise, he shall be liable to dismissal.

Dismissal shall be by agreement of all parties. In the absence of agreement, dismissal shall be by order of the court upon the request of the most diligent party, and this order shall be **not subject to any form of appeal**.

The competent court, if not designated in the arbitration agreement, shall be the **court of first instance** where the seat of arbitration is located.

The matter shall be decided as soon as possible and in any event within a period not exceeding three months from the date of filing the request.

If recourse is made to a specific **arbitration institution**, the request for dismissal shall be considered according to its rules.
