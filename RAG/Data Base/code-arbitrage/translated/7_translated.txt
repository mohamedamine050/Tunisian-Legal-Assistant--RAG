## English Translation:

Arbitration shall not be permitted:

Firstly, in matters relating to public order,

Secondly, in disputes concerning nationality,

Thirdly, in disputes concerning personal status, except for financial disputes arising therefrom,

Fourthly, in matters where reconciliation is not permitted,

Fifthly, in disputes concerning the State, public institutions with an administrative character, and local communities, except when these disputes arise from economic, commercial, or financial international relations regulated by Chapter III of this Code.
