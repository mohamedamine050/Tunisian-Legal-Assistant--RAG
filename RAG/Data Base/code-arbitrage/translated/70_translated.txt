## English Translation:

1. If the claimant fails to submit their pleadings without a justifiable excuse, in accordance with Article 68(1) of this Code, the arbitral tribunal shall terminate the arbitration proceedings. 

2. If the defendant, the person against whom the claim is directed, or the person claiming to be entitled to the claim, fails to submit their defense without a justifiable excuse, in accordance with Article 68(1) of this Code, the arbitral tribunal shall continue the proceedings without considering this default as an admission of the validity of the claim.

3. If one of the parties fails to attend a hearing or to submit their documents without a justifiable excuse, the arbitral tribunal may continue the proceedings and issue its award based on the evidence available to it.

All of the above shall apply unless the parties agree otherwise.
