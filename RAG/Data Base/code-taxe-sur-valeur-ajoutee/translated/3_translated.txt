## English Translation:

**I.**  The following transactions are considered finalized in Tunisia:

- **Regarding sales:** Any transaction where the delivery of goods occurs in Tunisia.
- **Regarding any other transaction:**  Whenever the services provided, the sold right, or the leased item is used or exploited in Tunisia.

**II.** Goods intended for export are considered delivered in Tunisia for the seller, unless they have explicitly declared the export in their own name.

**III.** Goods imported are considered delivered in Tunisia if they are delivered to a person other than the one who used the customs tariff number to complete customs procedures.
