## English Translation:

1. **The suspension of the value-added tax (VAT) payment applies to the acquisition of locally manufactured equipment necessary for new investments acquired before the actual commencement of activities in economic sectors, excluding the sectors of on-site consumption, commercial activities, finance, non-renewable energy, mining, and telecommunications network operators.**

2. **The suspension of the VAT payment applies to the supply and local acquisition of equipment necessary for investments in the sectors of agricultural development, traditional industries, air transport, sea transport, international road freight transport, pollution control, and support activities as defined in the Law on Personal Income Tax and Corporate Tax.**

**The conditions and procedures for benefitting from the privileges stipulated in this chapter, as well as the lists of relevant equipment, shall be determined by government order.**
