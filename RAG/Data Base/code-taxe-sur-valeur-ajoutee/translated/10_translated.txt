The right to deduct value-added tax is not granted to the employee: 

1)  On passenger cars not subject to exploitation, including the rental of passenger cars and all expenses incurred for their operation and maintenance.

2) On products delivered and services rendered by the persons specified in sub-paragraph 2 of paragraph 1 of Chapter II of this Code, as well as by the persons subject to the estimated tax provided for in Chapter 44 bis of the Code of Tax on Income of Natural Persons and Corporate Tax (amended by Article 30 of Law No. 88 of 1997 dated December 29, 1997 and by Article 37 of Law No. 58 of 2010 dated December 17, 2010).

3) (Added by Article 34 of Law No. 54 of 2013 dated December 30, 2013 and repealed by Article 360 of Decree No. 79 of 2022 dated December 22, 2022).

4) (Added by Article 34 of Law No. 78 of 2016 dated December 17, 2016 and amended by Article 35-6 of Law No. 56 of 2018 dated December 27, 2018). On amounts paid to persons residing or domiciled in a country or territory with a preferential tax regime within the meaning of Article 12 of Chapter 14 of the Code of Tax on Income of Natural Persons and Corporate Tax.
