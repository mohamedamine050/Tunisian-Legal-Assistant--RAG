(Repealed and replaced by Article 26 of Decree No. 21 of 2021 dated December 28, 2021). The following operations are exempt from Value Added Tax (VAT): domestic supply and acquisition of equipment, including aircraft and their engines, and all equipment intended for integration into the aircraft, as well as materials, equipment, and services necessary for the activity performed by international air transport institutions.

Likewise, the following entities are exempt from VAT: entities providing ground services within airports, catering, maintenance, repair, and technical supervision services for aircraft for the benefit of international air transport institutions under contracts or agreements concluded for this purpose, in relation to the domestic supply and acquisition of materials, equipment, and services performed under the aforementioned contracts or agreements. This exemption shall be granted based on a certificate issued by the competent tax authority, either generally or specifically, as the case may be.
