Subject to the provisions of Article 19 of this Code, State agencies, local authorities, public institutions and establishments shall deduct 25% from the amount of Value Added Tax levied on amounts equal to or exceeding 1,000 dinars, including the Value Added Tax paid on the purchase of goods, equipment, facilities, services, real estate and business assets. (Amended by Article 42 of Decree-Law No. 27 of 2012 dated December 29, 2012).

The aforementioned deduction shall apply even if the amounts are paid on behalf of third parties. (Added by Article 51 of Decree-Law No. 54 of 2013 dated December 30, 2013).

These provisions shall not apply to amounts paid:

- For telephone, water, electricity and gas subscriptions.

- Under financial lease contracts, lease contracts, Murabaha sales contracts, manufacturing contracts, and Salam sales contracts concluded by lending institutions and by microfinance institutions as defined by Decree No. 117 of 2011 dated November 5, 2011 concerning the regulation of microfinance activities. (Added by Article 56 of Decree-Law No. 101 of 2002 dated December 17, 2002 and amended by Article 72 of Decree-Law No. 80 of 2003 dated December 29, 2003, by Article 37 of Decree-Law No. 7 of 2011 dated December 31, 2011 and by Article 16 of Decree-Law No. 53 of 2015 dated December 25, 2015).

- For the purchase of products and services subject to administrative price approval with a gross profit margin not exceeding 7% (1) in accordance with the current legislation and regulations. (Added by Article 51 of Decree-Law No. 54 of 2013 dated December 30, 2013).

- In respect of commissions payable to authorized distributors of public telecommunications network operators. (Added by Article 34-2 of Decree-Law No. 53 of 2015 dated December 25, 2015).
