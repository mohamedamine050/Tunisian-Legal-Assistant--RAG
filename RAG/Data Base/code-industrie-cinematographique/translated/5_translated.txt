The screening of motion pictures is contingent upon obtaining a permit issued by the Secretary of State for Information and Guidance, after reviewing the opinion of the Committee. 

The Committee, formed through an official procedure, comprises individuals with relevant expertise drawn from various parts of the institution. This committee's role is mandated by Article Six. 

Any distributor seeking to obtain the permit must present the film(s) covered by the permit to the aforementioned committee for review, at their own expense.
