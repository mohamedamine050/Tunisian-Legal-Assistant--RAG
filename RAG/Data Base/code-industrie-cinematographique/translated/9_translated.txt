Every film that has obtained a visa in the Tunisian territory must be presented to the public in the form approved by the censorship board. This board is an official method for gathering individuals with relevant expertise from various parts of the institution. The film must be shown without any cuts, additions, or alterations, except for those that have been authorized or approved at the time of issuing the visa, and in accordance with the conditions upon which the issuance of the visa is contingent.
