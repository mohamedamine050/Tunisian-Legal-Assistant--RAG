The production or filming of any film or part of a film, cinematic or television, in the Tunisian Republic is subject to a prior license issued by the Secretary of State for News and Information. 

The applicant shall submit a file containing all necessary documents to enable the Secretary of State to make a decision, particularly the names, titles, addresses, and nationalities of the producer(s), actors, and main contributors, the title of the film, a statement of its length in meters, the location(s) of filming, a copy of the screenplay, and a copy of the financing plan.

The Secretary of State for News and Information may request, prior to issuing the license, any further documentation considered necessary. 

Filming permits in museums, certain areas or points of military significance, and aerial filming are all subject to the applicable regulations concerning the subject matter.
