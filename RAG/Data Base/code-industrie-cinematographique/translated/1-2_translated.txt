As amended by Law No. 20 of 2010 dated April 26, 2010, an applicant for a license to establish a private film and television production company shall submit an application accompanied by all documents and information relating to the company to be established, its managers, its place of business. A list of these documents and information, as well as specifications relating to the building that will house the company, its equipment and necessary facilities, shall be determined by an Order.  The monitoring of the operation of private film and television production companies shall also be regulated by Order.
