(Amended by Law No. 20 of 2010 dated April 26, 2010)

The legal representative of a private institution for the production of cinematographic and television films cannot be a person who has been declared bankrupt or against whom a final judgment has been issued for a felony or misdemeanor that is detrimental to honor and trust, and for which a sentence of imprisonment exceeding three months without suspension of execution has been imposed.

Nor can the legal representative of a private institution for the production of cinematographic and television films be a person who has been permanently banned from practicing the activity of producing cinematographic and television films pursuant to a disciplinary sanction issued against him.
