The Secretary of State for Information and Guidance shall manage the Film Production Development Fund with the assistance of an advisory committee constituted in the following manner:

* A representative of the Secretary of State for Information and Guidance
* A representative of the Secretary of State for Finance and Commerce
* A representative of the Secretary of State for National Education
* A representative of the Secretary of State for Industry and Transport.

The committee shall be officially constituted by bringing together individuals with relevant expertise from various sectors of the institution. It shall express its opinion on every matter related to the development of the film industry, particularly regarding the granting of financial subsidies and assistance, as stipulated in Articles 14 and 22 of this law.
