**(Added by Law No. 20 of 2010 dated April 26, 2010)**

The owner of a private institution for the production of film and television shall conclude insurance contracts.

**Contract**

By this contract, the insurer undertakes to pay the insured or the beneficiary a sum of money, a fixed income, or any other financial compensation in the event of the occurrence of the insured event, in exchange for premiums or any other financial payment made by the insured to the insurer.

This insurance contract is necessary to cover the risks of fire and the consequences of civil and professional liability resulting from the activities of the institution, and damages and losses that may be inflicted upon the institution's clients or third parties in accordance with the legislation and regulations in force.
