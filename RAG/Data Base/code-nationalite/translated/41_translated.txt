The Secretary of State for Justice shall refuse to register any declaration that does not meet the legal requirements of the declarant.  The declarant shall be notified of the refusal by proxy and, in this case, may appeal the matter to the Court of First Instance. The Court shall determine whether the declaration is valid or invalid. The Public Prosecutor, responsible for defending the interests of society and applying the law before the courts, shall be entered into the case and shall submit reasoned observations.
