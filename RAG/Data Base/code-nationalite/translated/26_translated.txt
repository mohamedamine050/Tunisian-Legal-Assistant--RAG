A foreign national who has acquired Tunisian nationality remains subject to the restrictions hereinafter set forth for a period of five years commencing from the date of the naturalization order:

1)  He/She cannot be appointed to a position/office or elected to an office requiring Tunisian nationality.

2)  He/She cannot be a voter if Tunisian nationality is required for registration on the electoral rolls. 

3)  He/She cannot hold a vacant position in Tunisian institutions.
