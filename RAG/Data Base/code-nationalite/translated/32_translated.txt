(Amended by Law No. 81 of 1984, dated November 30, 1984). - A Tunisian citizen who, acting in a public service for a foreign state or in a foreign army, maintains such employment after a period of one month from the date of notification by the Tunisian government to relinquish it, shall lose his/her Tunisian nationality, unless he/she proves that such relinquishment was impossible. In this case, the one-month period shall only begin to run from the date the impediment is lifted. A Tunisian citizen who has received a judgment of a foreign court depriving him/her of his/her Tunisian nationality or an order from the Tunisian government depriving him/her of his/her nationality shall be relieved of his/her Tunisian nationality from the date of the order issued for the loss of nationality.
