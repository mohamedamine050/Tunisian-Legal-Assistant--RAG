A person who acquires Tunisian nationality enjoys, from the date of acquisition, all the rights enjoyed by a Tunisian national, taking into account the restrictions specific to naturalized citizens. 

**Explanation of Terms:**

* **الجنسية (jinsiyya):**  Nationality.
* **رابطة قانونية وسياسية (rabita qanuniyya wa siyasiyya):** Legal and political bond.
* **فرد (fard):**  Individual.
* **دولة (dawla):** State.
* **عداد (a'dad):** Number.
* **سكان (sukan):**  Population.
* **حقوق (huquq):**  Rights.
* **التزامات (altزامات):** Obligations.
* **متجنسين (mutjanisin):** Naturalized citizens.
* **تحاجير (tahajur):** Restrictions.
