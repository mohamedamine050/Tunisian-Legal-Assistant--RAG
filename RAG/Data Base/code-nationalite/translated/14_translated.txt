A foreign woman married to a Tunisian, who, pursuant to her national law, retains her original nationality despite marrying a foreigner, may apply for Tunisian nationality by declaration, subject to the conditions stipulated in Article 39 of this Code, provided that the spouses have been residing in Tunisia for at least two years. She shall acquire Tunisian nationality from the date of registration of the declaration, subject to the provisions of Articles 15 and 41 of this Code.
