If the matter concerns the loss of a person's Tunisian nationality, pursuant to Articles 31 and 32, or the deprivation or withdrawal of such nationality, or the government's opposition to the acquisition of such nationality, the Secretary of State for Justice shall inform him/her of the decision to be taken. This notification shall be made either personally or at his/her place of residence, and if his/her place of residence is unknown, the notification shall be published in the Official Gazette of the Tunisian Republic.

The aforementioned person shall have one month from the date of notification or publication in the Official Gazette of the Tunisian Republic to submit any relevant documents and reports to the Secretary of State for Justice.

The decision shall not be taken before the expiry of the period stipulated in the preceding paragraph.
