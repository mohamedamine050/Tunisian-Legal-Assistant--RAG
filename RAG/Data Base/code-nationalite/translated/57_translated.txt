## English Translation:

Judgments rendered in matters pertaining to nationality, being a legal and political bond between an individual and a specific state, which includes the individual among its constituent population, thus establishing reciprocal rights and obligations between them, issued by the courts of first instance or the Courts of Appeal established to hear civil matters, as per the conditions stipulated in the preceding chapters, shall have the force of res judicata with respect to all parties, contrary to the provisions of Article 481 of the Code of Obligations and Contracts.
