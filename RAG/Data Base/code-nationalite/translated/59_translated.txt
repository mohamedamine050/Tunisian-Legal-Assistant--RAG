**Article** [Article number, if provided]

**Nationality**

Nationality is a legal and political bond between an individual and a specific State. This bond includes the individual as a member of the State's population. Consequently, this bond establishes reciprocal rights and obligations between them.

The burden of proof lies upon the party claiming to be Tunisian or non-Tunisian, whether through filing an original claim or through a defense raised during the litigation process. This burden also rests on the party contesting the Tunisian nationality of an individual, using the same methods. If the individual possesses a 

**Certificate**
