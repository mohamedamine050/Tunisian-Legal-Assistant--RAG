Every order issued regarding naturalization in Tunisian nationality, its loss, revocation, withdrawal, or the government's opposition to its acquisition shall be proven by presenting either a copy thereof conforming to the original or the official gazette of the Tunisian Republic in which it was published. If it is impossible to present these documents, they may be replaced by a certificate issued by the Secretary of State for Justice to any person who requests it, attesting to the existence of that order.
