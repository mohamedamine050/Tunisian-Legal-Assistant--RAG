**(Amended by Law No. 12 of 1971 dated March 9, 1971).** - If the Minister of Justice or the Tunisian diplomatic and consular representatives abroad or the governors of the regions refuse to issue a certificate of nationality, the interested party may bring his action before the competent Court of First Instance in accordance with Article 48 and subsequent Articles of this Code. The silence of the authorities mentioned in the preceding paragraph for one month from the date of the request shall be considered a refusal.
