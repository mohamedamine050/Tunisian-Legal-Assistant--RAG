A person shall be considered Tunisian if they were born in Tunisia and their father and paternal grandfather were also born in Tunisia. However, they have the right to renounce Tunisian nationality during the year preceding the attainment of the age of majority, provided that they were not born after the date of entry into force of this Code. They shall be released from their Tunisian nationality from the date of their declaration of renunciation.  

This right of renunciation is not applicable to a minor who voluntarily enlists in the army or participates in mobilization operations without previously exercising this right. The provisions of this article shall not apply to the children of diplomatic or consular personnel.
