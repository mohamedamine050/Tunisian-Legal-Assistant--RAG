(Amended by Article 3 of Law No. 55 of 2010 dated December 1, 2010). - The President of the Republic, in the manner prescribed in Article 14, may object by decree to the acquisition of Tunisian nationality. This decree must be issued within a maximum period of two years from the date of the declaration referred to in Article 14 or from the day on which the judicial ruling declaring the validity of the acquisition of nationality becomes final, if the registration of the application is refused pursuant to the provisions of Article 41 of this Code. If the President of the Republic issues an objection within the time limit prescribed in the preceding paragraph, the person concerned shall be deemed not to have acquired Tunisian nationality.
