## English Translation:

Naturalization in Tunisian nationality may be granted without the residency requirement stipulated in the preceding article to:

1) The individual whose original nationality was established to be Tunisian.

2) The foreigner married to a Tunisian woman, provided that the couple is residing in Tunisia at the time of the application.

3) The foreigner who has rendered distinguished services to Tunisia or whose naturalization in Tunisian nationality would be of great benefit to Tunisia. In such cases, naturalization shall be granted pursuant to a reasoned decision issued by the State Secretary for Justice.
