Every person has the right to initiate, before the **Court of First Instance**, a claim intended to directly and initially obtain a **judgment** establishing their Tunisian **nationality** or its denial. The **agent** of the Republic must be a party to the case, regardless of the right of any interested party to intervene.  The **judgment** is a written decision by the court that resolves a legal or factual issue. The **Court of First Instance** is typically the first court to consider a dispute and issue a decision. **Nationality** is a legal and political bond between an individual and a particular state, which includes the individual in the population that constitutes it. This bond creates reciprocal rights and obligations between them.
