A person may be deprived of Tunisian nationality by order if they: 

1. Have been **convicted** of an act constituting a felony or misdemeanor against the internal or external security of the State.
2. Have acted for the benefit of a foreign state in a manner incompatible with their status as a Tunisian citizen and detrimental to the interests of Tunisia.
3. Have been **convicted** in Tunisia or abroad of an act that constitutes a felony under Tunisian law and results in a prison sentence of at least five years. 
4. Have been **convicted** of refusing to perform their **legal** military service obligation.
