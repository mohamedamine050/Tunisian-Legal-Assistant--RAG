A person may not be granted Tunisian nationality unless:

(1) He is of sound mind.

(2) It is proven that he has knowledge of the Arabic language commensurate with his social status.

(3) He is of sound mind.

(4) His health status demonstrates that he will not be a burden on society or a danger to it.

(5) He is of good character and morals, has not previously been sentenced to imprisonment for a term exceeding one year, and has not had his punishment expunged by virtue of the restoration of rights. It is permissible to take into account judgments issued abroad.
