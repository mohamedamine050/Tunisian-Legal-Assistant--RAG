Every declaration aiming to request Tunisian nationality or renounce it must fulfill the following conditions:

1) It must be written on stamped paper in two copies.

2) It must designate a chosen domicile for the individual.

3) It must be signed and acknowledged by the signature of its author, unless it is drawn up by a public official.

4) It must be accompanied by all supporting documents, in particular civil status documents.

5) It must be submitted to the Ministry of Justice or sent to it by registered mail with notification of receipt.
