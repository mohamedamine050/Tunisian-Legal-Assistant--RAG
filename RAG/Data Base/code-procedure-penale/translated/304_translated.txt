It is not permissible for any judge to recuse himself except after obtaining authorization from the First President of the Court of Appeal, who shall issue a decision in this regard after obtaining the opinion of the Public Prosecutor; this decision shall be not subject to any form of appeal.  It is not permissible for the Presiding Judges of the Court of Cassation and its Counsellors to recuse themselves except after obtaining authorization from the First President of the aforementioned Court after obtaining the opinion of the Attorney General.
