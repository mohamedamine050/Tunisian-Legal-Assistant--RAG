If the Criminal Court legally undertakes a case it considers a felony, and it becomes apparent from the pleadings that it is merely a misdemeanor, it shall impose the punishment and decide, as appropriate, on the claim.  A felony is a serious crime punishable under the law and subject to severe penalties such as imprisonment.  Misdemeanors are defined as crimes punishable by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars.  Offenses are defined as crimes punishable by imprisonment not exceeding fifteen days or a fine not exceeding sixty dinars. If the Criminal Court legally undertakes a case it considers a misdemeanor, and it becomes apparent from the pleadings that it is merely an offense, it shall impose the punishment and decide, as appropriate, on the claim.
