The guarantee ensures: Firstly, the production of the suspect; the term "suspect" refers to the suspicion of committing a crime before the charge is filed, for all investigative procedures and for the execution of the judgment. Secondly, the payment of the following in the order stated: a) the expenses incurred by the State treasury; b) the expenses advanced by the claimant; c) fines. The decision granting provisional release shall specify the amount relating to each part of the guarantee.
