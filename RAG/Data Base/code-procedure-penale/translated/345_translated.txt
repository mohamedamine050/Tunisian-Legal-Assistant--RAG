(Amended by Law No. 90 of 1999 dated August 2, 1999). - Imprisonment or community service cannot be imposed against the following persons: 1) those civilly liable; 2) those exercising a personal right; 3) those convicted who were under the age of eighteen at the time of committing the acts that led to prosecution; 4) those convicted who have reached the age of seventy; 5) the husband and wife simultaneously, even for the recovery of sums resulting from different proceedings.
