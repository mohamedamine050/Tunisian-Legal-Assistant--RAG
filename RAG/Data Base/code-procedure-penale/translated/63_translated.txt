The Investigating Judge may hear, for guidance purposes and without oath, the following: First, the party with a personal right; Second, persons whose testimony is inadmissible under the rules of the Civil Code of Procedure; Third, persons prohibited from giving testimony before the courts by law or by court order; Fourth, persons who have voluntarily reported a crime and its perpetrator, and who were entitled to a reward for such reporting, but were not obligated to do so by virtue of their duties.
