If the act constitutes a felony, within the meaning of this law,  felonies are defined as crimes punishable by death or imprisonment exceeding five years.  Misdemeanors are defined as crimes punishable by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars.  Offenses are defined as crimes punishable by imprisonment not exceeding fifteen days or a fine not exceeding sixty dinars. Article 122 of the Code of Criminal Procedure. If related to a misdemeanor, the court shall rule on it by a single judgment, which shall be subject to appeal in its entirety.
