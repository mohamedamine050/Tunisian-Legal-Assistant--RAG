It is not permissible to pursue a foreign national for a felony, a serious crime punishable under the law and subject to severe penalties such as imprisonment; or a misdemeanor, defined as crimes punishable by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars, committed within the territory of the Republic of Tunisia, if it is proven that the judiciary has definitively dealt with the matter abroad, and in the event of a sentence being issued against him, that he has served the imposed sentence, or that this sentence has lapsed due to the passage of time, or has been covered by an amnesty.
