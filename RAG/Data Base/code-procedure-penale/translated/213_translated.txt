The appeal shall be inadmissible except in cases of force majeure, if it is not filed within a maximum period of ten days from the date of issuance of the judgment rendered in the presence of the parties, or from the date of notification of the judgment considered as rendered in the presence of the parties within the meaning of the first paragraph of Article 175, or from the date of expiry of the period for appealing against judgments rendered in absentia, or from the date of notification of the judgment dismissing the appeal. This period shall be extended to sixty days from the date of issuance of the judgment for appeals filed by the Public Prosecutor and the Public Prosecutors at the Courts of Appeal. They must notify the accused and the civilly liable parties of this appeal within the aforementioned period; otherwise, their right to appeal shall lapse.
