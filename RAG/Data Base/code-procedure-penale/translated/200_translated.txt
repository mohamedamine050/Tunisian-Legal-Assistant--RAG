The District Governor undertakes offenses: Firstly, by direct referral from the Attorney General or from public administrations and financial branches in cases where the law authorizes them to initiate public prosecution directly, or from the injured party; Secondly, by referral from the Investigating Judge or from another court; Thirdly, by referral of the offender by the Attorney General in the cases specified in Article 202. However, the Governor may proceed without referral in cases of apprehension in the act of committing the offense.
