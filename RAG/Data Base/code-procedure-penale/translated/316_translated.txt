The request for surrender shall be addressed to the Tunisian Government through diplomatic channels, accompanied by the original or an official copy of the enforceable judgment, or the arrest warrant, or any other document of equal force issued in accordance with the prescribed forms under the legislation of the requesting State.  The request shall also specify, as accurately as possible, the circumstances surrounding the acts for which surrender is sought, including the date and place of their commission and the legal description of the crime, with reference to the applicable legal provisions. A copy of the legal provisions applicable to the crime shall be appended to the request.
