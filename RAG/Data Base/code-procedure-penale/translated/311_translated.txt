Extradition is granted: Firstly, if the crime for which extradition is sought is punishable under Tunisian law by a criminal or penal sanction; secondly, if the punishment prescribed by the requesting State's law is a custodial sentence equal to or exceeding six months for the totality of the crimes subject to the request. In the event of trial, the punishment imposed by the court of the requesting State must be a custodial sentence equal to or exceeding two months. Acts constituting an attempt or participation are subject to the aforementioned rules, provided they are punishable under the law of the requesting State and under Tunisian law.
