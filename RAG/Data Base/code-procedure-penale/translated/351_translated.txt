Civil Fines

The material aspects of civilization, such as infrastructure, communication, and entertainment, are matched by the intellectual, spiritual, and ethical aspects of civilization.  Civil fines resulting from decisions or judgments issued in criminal, misdemeanor, and infraction cases that have become final are annulled in accordance with the rules stipulated in the Code of Civil Procedure.
