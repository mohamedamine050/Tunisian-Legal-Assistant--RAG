Witnesses and experts, after being called by name, shall be placed in a room designated for them from which they shall not leave except to give their testimony consecutively before the court in the presence of the parties.  The court shall receive their testimony in the manner prescribed by Articles 64 to 66 of this Law, except as regards the drafting of minutes. They must remain in the courtroom after giving their testimony until the public pleadings are concluded, unless they are granted special permission to leave.  Their departure may be requested or authorized while another witness is giving their testimony.
