[2] A claim is the right of anyone with a request to submit it to the court for a judgment on its subject matter. [2] Damage is the loss or damage to property, deprivation of comfort, or harm to physical well-being, reputation, or the like.  - In crimes and quasi-crimes, damage is the loss actually suffered by the plaintiff, the necessary expenses incurred or to be incurred to repair the consequences of the act that caused the harm, and any benefit deprived within the normal limits of the consequences of that act.
