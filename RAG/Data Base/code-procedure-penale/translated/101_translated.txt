The Investigating Judge may, when necessary, instruct an expert or several experts to conduct certain technical tests, which he shall specify for them. Except in the case of confirmation, the Public Prosecutor and the party with standing shall be notified thereof.  They shall have the right to object to the selection within four days, stating their grounds. The Judge shall rule on this by a decision not subject to appeal. The suspect's lawyer may request access to the file within forty-eight hours of notification.
