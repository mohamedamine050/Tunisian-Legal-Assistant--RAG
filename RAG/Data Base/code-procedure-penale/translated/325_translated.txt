Upon verification and based on a direct request issued by the judicial authorities of the requesting State, Public Prosecutors may authorize the preventive detention of a foreign national upon their contact with the notification via mail or any other faster means leaving a written trace indicating the existence of the documents specified in Article 316.  A legal request for extradition must simultaneously be addressed via diplomatic channels to the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.  Public Prosecutors must inform the Attorney General of the Republic of the detention.
