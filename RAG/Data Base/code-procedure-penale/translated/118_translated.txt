If the Indictment Chamber finds that the acts constitute:

a misdemeanor (جنحة) described in this law as crimes punishable by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars; or

an offense (مخالفة) described in this law as crimes punishable by death or imprisonment exceeding five years;

or a misdemeanor (جنحة) described as crimes punishable by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars;

or an offense (مخالفة) described as crimes punishable by imprisonment not exceeding fifteen days or a fine of sixty dinars; (Article 122 of the Code of Criminal Procedure)

then it shall decide to refer the case to the Criminal Court or a court (محكمة), a place where litigation takes place between the parties, a civil court under the judicial authority which must have its independence within the three branches of government.
