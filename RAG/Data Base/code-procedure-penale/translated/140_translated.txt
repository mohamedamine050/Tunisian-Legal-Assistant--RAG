The summons, or its counterpart or original, shall indicate the name, capacity, and date of service of the person designated to effect service.  The summons shall be signed by the summonsed party; however, if the summonsed party refuses to sign or is unable to do so, this shall be stated.  It shall also be signed by the person effecting service, and immediately returned to the court registry of the court hearing the case, where the clerk shall add it to the case file.
