Agent
A person acting on behalf of another person.

The Public Prosecutor is tasked with overseeing all crimes and receiving information about crimes from public officials or members of the public, and accepting complaints from victims.  Except in cases of felonies or misdemeanors committed in flagrante delicto, he is not authorized to conduct investigations; however, he may conduct a preliminary inquiry for guidance in gathering evidence of the crime. He may question suspects generally, receive statements, and draw up reports thereof.  Even in cases of felonies or misdemeanors committed in flagrante delicto, he may task a judicial police officer with certain acts within his jurisdiction.
