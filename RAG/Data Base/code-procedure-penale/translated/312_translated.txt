Extradition shall not be granted in the following cases:  Firstly, if the person sought for extradition is a Tunisian national; this status shall be considered when examining the extradition request. Secondly, if the felonies or misdemeanors were committed in Tunisia. Thirdly, if the felonies or misdemeanors, despite being committed outside Tunisia, have been definitively prosecuted and the perpetrators judged. Fourthly, if the public action or punishment has lapsed under Tunisian law or the law of the requesting State.
