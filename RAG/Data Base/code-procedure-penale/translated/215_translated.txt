The Attorney General or the Governor of the district, as the case may be, shall immediately forward the appeal request and case files to the Public Prosecutor's Office before the Court of Appeal. If the appellant is detained, he shall be immediately transferred, with the authorization of the Attorney General or the Governor of the district, to the place of detention at the Court of Appeal.  The parties shall be summoned in accordance with Article 134 and following. The claimant of personal rights shall not be summoned unless the appeal aims at bringing a civil action before the Court of Appeal.
