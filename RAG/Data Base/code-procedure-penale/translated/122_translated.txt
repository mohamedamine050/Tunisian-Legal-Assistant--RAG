(Amended by Law No. 23 of 1989 dated February 27, 1989) - Crimes that warrant punishment by death or imprisonment exceeding five years are classified as felonies under this Law.  Crimes that warrant punishment by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or a fine exceeding sixty dinars, are classified as misdemeanors. Crimes that warrant punishment not exceeding fifteen days' imprisonment or sixty dinars' fine are classified as offenses.
