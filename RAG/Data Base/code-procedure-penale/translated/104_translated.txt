After completion of the proceedings in the case, the Investigating Judge shall forward the file to the Public Prosecutor, who must, within eight days, submit written requests aiming either to refer the case to the competent court, to dismiss it, to order further investigation, or to abandon it for lack of jurisdiction.  Upon submission of these requests by the Public Prosecutor, the Investigating Judge shall issue a decision concerning all the accused, all charges brought against them, and all requests made by the Public Prosecutor. The decision shall include the name, surname, age, birthplace, residence, profession of the suspect, a summary of the claim, the legal description of the act, the reasons for the existence or absence of sufficient evidence for the aforementioned act, and the opinion of the Investigating Judge.
