The Claim
The Claim

[1] A request by one party for his right from another in the presence of a judge; the requestor is called the claimant and the requested party is called the defendant. [2] A claim is the right of every claimant to bring his claim before the court for a judgment on its subject matter.  It is, for the opposing party, the right to present grounds for defense or arguments aimed at refuting that claim.

Civil
The material aspect of civilization, such as infrastructure, means of communication, and entertainment, as opposed to the intellectual, spiritual, and moral aspects of civilization, which are to be pursued concurrently with the claim.

Public Prosecutor
Public Prosecutor
The person entrusted by the judiciary or the Public Prosecutor's Office to investigate the case.

According to Article 7 of this Law, the actions can be carried out either during the publication of the case before the Investigating Judge or before the court seized of the case. Associations may exercise their civil right regarding acts that fall within the scope of their subject matter and objectives as stipulated in their statutes. (Added by Article 2 of Law No. 5 of 2016 dated February 16, 2016).
