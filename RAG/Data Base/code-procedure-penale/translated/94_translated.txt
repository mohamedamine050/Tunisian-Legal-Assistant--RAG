Search
The legal search of property by the authorities for the purpose of investigation.

Residential premises are the properties of the Investigating Judge.

Investigating Judge
The person entrusted by the judiciary or the Public Prosecution to investigate the case, to the exclusion of all others.  However, the search of residential premises may be carried out by: Firstly – Judicial police officers in the case of a felony or misdemeanor caught in flagrante delicto and in accordance with the conditions stipulated in this law; Secondly – Judicial police officers specified in articles 2 to 4 of Article 10 and entrusted with the task by the Investigating Judge; Thirdly – Administrative officials and their agents authorized to do so by a special provision.
