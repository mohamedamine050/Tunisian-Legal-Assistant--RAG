(Amended by Law No. 43 of 2000 dated April 17, 2000) - The primary court shall hear and render a first instance decision in all misdemeanors, except those falling under the purview of the district judge.  The primary court shall hear, as a court of appeal, and definitively rule on all appeals from decisions issued by the district judges within its jurisdiction.  The primary court, which has a criminal division, shall also hear at first instance cases of felonies. (Paragraph three amended by Law No. 41 of 2010 dated July 26, 2010).
