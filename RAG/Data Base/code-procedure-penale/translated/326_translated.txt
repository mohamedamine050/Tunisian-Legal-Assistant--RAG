The temporarily detained person may be released according to the conditions stipulated in the preceding article, if he is a legal resident in Tunisia and the Tunisian government has not received, within one month of the date of his detention, which was based on a request from a foreign state, one of the documents specified in Article 316. Release shall be effected upon a simple request submitted to the Investigating Chamber, which shall decide thereon within eight days by a decision not subject to any form of appeal after hearing the observations of the Public Prosecutor.  If the Tunisian government subsequently receives the aforementioned documents, the proceedings shall resume in accordance with Article 317 and following.
