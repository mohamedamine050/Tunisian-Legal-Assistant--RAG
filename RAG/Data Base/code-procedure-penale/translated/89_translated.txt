Security is obtained either by securing a sum of money, or certified checks, or state-guaranteed bonds; or by the commitment of a solvent person to produce the suspect.  Suspect, in this context, means suspicion of committing a crime before the charge is filed. In all investigative proceedings, or by paying the amount determined by the judge to the state treasury in case of failure to produce the suspect. The decision regarding temporary release shall include specifying the appropriate placement of the individual in a position that matches the requirements and necessities of performing it, in terms of qualifications and competencies. The type of security that should be given and, if necessary, its amount.
