The record shall not be relied upon as evidence unless, from a formal perspective, it is drawn up in accordance with the law and contains therein what its author heard or witnessed personally while performing his duties in a matter within his jurisdiction.  Statements of the accused, his confessions, or witness testimonies shall be considered void if it is proven that they were obtained as a result of torture or coercion. (The second paragraph was added by Decree No. 106 of 2011 dated October 22, 2011).
