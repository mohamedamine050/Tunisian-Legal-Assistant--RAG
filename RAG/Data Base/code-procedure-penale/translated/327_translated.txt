Transit through Tunisian territory is permitted for a Muslim individual from another government based solely on a request submitted through diplomatic channels and supported by the necessary documents proving that the crime is not purely political or military.  Transit through Tunisian territory is not permitted for a Tunisian national. Transportation shall be carried out by Tunisian officials and at the expense of the requesting state. In the case of using air transport, the following provisions apply: Firstly, if the aircraft landing is unexpected, the requesting state shall inform the Tunisian government and provide one of the documents stipulated in Article 316. In the case of an emergency landing, this notification shall have the effect of the temporary suspension request referred to in Article 325. The requesting state shall submit a legal request for transit. Secondly, if the aircraft landing is expected, the requesting state shall submit a request in accordance with the provisions of the first paragraph of this Article.
