The judicial police officers, under the supervision of the Public Prosecutor and the Public Prosecutors at the Courts of Appeal, each within their respective jurisdictions, shall perform their duties.  Those mentioned are: 1) Public Prosecutors and their assistants; 2) District Governors; 3) Police Governors, officers, and station chiefs; 4) National Guard officers, non-commissioned officers, and station chiefs; 5) Local chiefs; 6) Administrative officials granted the necessary power to investigate certain crimes or prepare reports thereon by virtue of special laws; 7) Investigating Judges in the circumstances stipulated by this law.
