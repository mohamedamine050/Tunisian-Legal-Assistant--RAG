Any request aiming at disparagement, conduct inciting or instigating hatred, disgust, contempt, or severe mockery due to a person's race or religion, submitted to the First President of the Court of Appeal, or the President of a Chamber in the Court of Cassation, or a Counselor thereof, must be presented in the form of a petition to the First President of the Court of Cassation, who shall decide thereon after obtaining the opinion of the Attorney General at the aforementioned Court, by a decision not subject to any form of appeal. The provisions of Article 299 apply in this instance.
