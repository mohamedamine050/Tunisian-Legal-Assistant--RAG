(Amended by Law No. 15 of 1980, dated April 3, 1980.) - The District Governor shall have final jurisdiction over misdemeanors and shall have original jurisdiction:  First: in misdemeanors punishable by imprisonment not exceeding one year or a fine not exceeding one thousand dinars (1,000 DT).  The primary court shall retain jurisdiction exceptionally in misdemeanors described as crimes which warrant punishment by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars;  assault causing injury through negligence and unintentional arson. Second: in misdemeanors assigned to his jurisdiction by a specific provision.
