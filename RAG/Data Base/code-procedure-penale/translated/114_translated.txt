The Public Prosecutor at the Court of Appeal, if the case is referred to it in accordance with the conditions set forth in the second paragraph of Article 107, shall conclude it within ten days and forward it to the Indictment Chamber accompanied by his requests. This Chamber shall decide on it in the week following the day it receives the Public Prosecutor's report, without the presence of the parties.  The counsel for the suspect and the party exercising private prosecution shall have the right to access the case file and to submit written requests.
