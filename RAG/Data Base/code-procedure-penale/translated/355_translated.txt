The provisions of Articles 353 and 354 above may be disregarded in the following circumstances:

First: If the convicted person, a defendant against whom a judgment has been rendered determining his responsibility for a crime and imposing a penalty or precautionary measure, has reached the age of sixty full years on the date of his conditional release.

Second: If the convicted person, a defendant against whom a judgment has been rendered determining his responsibility for a crime and imposing a penalty or precautionary measure, has not reached the age of twenty full years on the same date.

Third: If he is suffering from a serious disability or incurable illness.
