Oil and gas sector service companies are subject to the supervision and monitoring of the granting authority. This supervision consists of verifying the conformity of their activities with the provisions of this Code.

The incentives stipulated in Articles 4.130 and 5.130 of this Code shall be withdrawn from the beneficiaries thereof in the event of non-compliance with the provisions of this Code, or failure to commence the actual activity for one year from the date of authorization, or in the event of an unlawful diversion of the original purpose. In these cases, late payment penalties relating to the applicable taxes and duties shall be calculated in accordance with the legislation in force.
