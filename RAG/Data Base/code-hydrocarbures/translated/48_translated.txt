**(Amended by Article 1. 48 of Law No. 23 of 2002, dated February 14, 2002)**

**1. 48** - An exploitation permit shall be granted by a decision of the Minister in charge of hydrocarbons, based on a favorable opinion from the Hydrocarbons Advisory Committee, and shall be published in the Official Gazette of the Tunisian Republic.

**2. 48** - The exploitation permit shall be granted for a maximum period of thirty years, commencing from the date of publication of the decision establishing it in the Official Gazette of the Tunisian Republic.
