## English Translation:

1. Article 49 grants the holder of the exploitation concession the exclusive right to conduct exploitation activities within the public space surrounding the area of the concession. Furthermore, the concession holder may also carry out exploration activities for other geological horizons not covered by the exploitation concession and undertake assessment works to verify the extent of the field, both before and after the commencement of production.

2. Article 49 grants the concession holder the right to dispose of the hydrocarbons extracted under the concession, particularly for export purposes, subject to fulfilling their obligations, notably the payment of royalties in kind as stipulated in Article 101 of this Code, and contributing to supplying the domestic market in accordance with the conditions specified in Article 50 of this Code as amended and defined by the specific agreement.
