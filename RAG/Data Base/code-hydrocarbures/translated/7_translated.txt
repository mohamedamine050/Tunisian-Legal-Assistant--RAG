The activities of exploration, research, and exploitation of hydrocarbons may only be undertaken by:

(a) The Tunisian state, according to methods determined in each specific case. 
(b) Public or private institutions, Tunisian or foreign, which possess sufficient financial resources and technical capacity to carry out these activities under the best conditions. The granting authority may at any time require, as part of the grant, a guarantee provided by an entity approved by it, to ensure the fulfillment of obligations relating to expenses and/or the minimum works.
