1. **Article 53:** Oil and gas fields are considered real estate. In addition to buildings, machinery, equipment, and all installations permanently fixed to the site for the purpose of carrying out exploitation activities are also considered real estate. Machinery, equipment, and materials intended for the conduct of the aforementioned activities, which are not permanently fixed to the site, are also considered real estate by law. 

2. **Article 53:** The real estate defined in this chapter is not subject to any form of appeal under the law relating to registered real estate and cannot be mortgaged.

3. **Article 53:** Extracted oil and gas products, consumable materials, and other equipment, as well as shares and rights in any company carrying out exploitation activities, are considered movable property.
