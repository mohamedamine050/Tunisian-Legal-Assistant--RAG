If the licensee, the contractor, or the quasi-contractor wishes to transfer the goods supplied or purchased from the local market under the system referred to in Chapter 116 of this Code, he shall:

(a) Make a declaration of transfer to the customs authorities if the transfer is made in favor of a transferee who enjoys the same exemption and supply freedom regimes as the transferor. 

(b) Before the transfer, complete the foreign trade procedures and pay the duties and fees due upon supply based on the value of those goods at the date of the transfer, if the transfer is made in favor of a transferee other than (a) of this Chapter.
