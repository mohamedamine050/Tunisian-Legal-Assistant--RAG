## English Translation:

1. **Article 69:** Upon entering into an agreement with the granting authority regarding the production and promotion program, as stipulated in Article 68 of this Code, the licensee shall, at its own expense, complete a comprehensive evaluation program regarding the discovery of gas within the timeframes specified in Article 40 of this Code. At the end of this program, the licensee shall submit to the granting authority a technical and economic report containing the elements mentioned in the development plan referred to in Article 47 of this Code. 

2. **Article 69:** The granting authority may confirm the proven reserves, as well as the expected production rate, through an independent consulting firm of its own choosing and at its own expense. In such a case, the licensee shall provide the consulting firm selected by the granting authority with all necessary information and documents.
