## English Translation:

1. Article 16: The granting of a research license shall not prejudice any rights previously acquired by the holder of an exploration, research, or exploitation concession.

2. Article 16: If an application for a research license relates to an area exceeding the boundaries of an exploration, research, or exploitation concession, the license shall only be granted for the area external to these concessions or the concession.

3. Article 16: If the overlapping is not acknowledged until after the granting of the research license, the correction of the license boundaries shall be made by a decision taken by the Minister responsible for hydrocarbons, either automatically or upon request from the interested party.

4. Article 16: In all cases, the research license shall be granted after safeguarding the prior rights of the license holders.
