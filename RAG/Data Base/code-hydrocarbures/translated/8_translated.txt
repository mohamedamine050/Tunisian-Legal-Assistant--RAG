## English Translation:

**Article 8**

**(1)** A **Petroleum Advisory Committee** shall be established by formal means to bring together experts from various parts of the institution.  The opinion of this Committee shall be sought in all cases stipulated by the provisions of this Code. The Minister responsible for Petroleum may also seek the opinion of this Committee on any other matter related to Petroleum. 

**(2)** The composition and operating procedures of the Petroleum Advisory Committee shall be determined by a Decree.
