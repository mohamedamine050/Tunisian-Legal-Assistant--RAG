Companies providing services in the hydrocarbons sector are considered to be those companies that carry out the following activities:

A- Providing geological and geophysical services, drilling, well maintenance, engineering, the construction and preparation of exploitation facilities;

B- Providing services related to drilling operations, consisting of geological drilling monitoring, electrical logging, well lining and cementing, and well testing;

C- Supplying hydrocarbon exploration, research, and exploitation sites with materials, equipment, and products directly related to the services provided to hydrocarbon exploration, research, and exploitation companies operating in Tunisia, within the framework of the provisions of this code. The activity of service companies in the hydrocarbons sector headquartered in Tunisia may include companies established outside Tunisia.
