This Law authorizes companies providing services in the hydrocarbons sector to supply all equipment, materials, and vehicles intended for actual use in the context of their activity without undertaking foreign trade procedures as defined in Article 130 of this Code.  Service companies in the hydrocarbons sector benefit, with respect to the equipment, materials, and vehicles necessary for their activity, from:

A. Exemption from customs duties, value-added tax, and the levy for the Industrial Competitiveness Development Fund, applicable to imported equipment, materials, and vehicles that have no equivalent manufactured locally.

B. Exemption from value-added tax and the levy for the Industrial Competitiveness Development Fund, applicable to locally manufactured equipment, materials, and vehicles.
