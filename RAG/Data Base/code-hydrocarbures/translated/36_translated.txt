1. Article 36: A research permit may be relinquished at any time upon a written declaration of relinquishment, provided that the permit holder has fulfilled their minimum commitments in terms of works and expenses related to the period covered by the relinquishment. 

2. Article 36: If the permit holder has not fulfilled their minimum commitments in terms of works and expenses, or both, they may relinquish the research permit after paying a compensatory fine to the granting authority equal to the difference between the minimum amount of expenses to be achieved and the amount of expenses actually incurred, or the amount necessary to complete the specified works for the duration of the permit's validity during which the relinquishment took place.
