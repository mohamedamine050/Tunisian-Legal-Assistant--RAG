Employees and agents of the departments responsible for hydrocarbons are authorized to freely enter the licensee's offices, yards, and appurtenances.  The licensee is obligated to provide them with all information and documents available on site relating to the works in progress and to provide them with all necessary facilities to perform their duties.  Furthermore, the licensee is obligated to make available the officials responsible for the works, whose assistance in accompanying them on their visits shall be ensured.
