The holder of the exploitation concession must commence development works within a maximum period of two years from the date of the grant of the exploitation concession. In case of violation, 

Crimes punishable by death or imprisonment exceeding five years are classified as felonies within the meaning of this law.

Crimes punishable by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars are classified as misdemeanors.

Crimes punishable by imprisonment not exceeding fifteen days or by a fine not exceeding sixty dinars are classified as offenses. (Article 122 of the Code of Criminal Procedure) 

In such a case, the granting authority may revoke the concession and dispose of it freely without any compensation being granted.
