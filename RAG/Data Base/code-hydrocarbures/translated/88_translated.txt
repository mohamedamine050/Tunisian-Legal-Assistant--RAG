The holder of an exploration license, a research license, and/or an exploitation concession, upon authorization from the granting authority and subject to the provisions of the Forestry Code and the specific provisions relating to State lands with an agricultural character, maritime public domain, and the rights of third parties, and within the scope of the needs of the activities referred to in Article 84 of this Code, may occupy the international territory as well as the maritime public domain, in accordance with the general conditions applicable to such occupation. However, no exploration, research, or exploitation activities for hydrocarbons may be carried out on lands belonging to the general or private military domain without prior authorization from the Minister in charge of National Defense. The authorization referred to in this article shall specify the special rules governing the execution of such works.
