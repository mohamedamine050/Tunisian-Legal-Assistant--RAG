An applicant for a research license must have a principal or chosen domicile in Tunisia.  If the applicant is unable to appoint a representative residing in Tunisia to act on their behalf, all notices and communications issued by third parties relating to the procedural actions of this Code shall be addressed to this domicile.  If it is impossible to deliver these notices and communications to the domicile as stipulated above, they shall be deposited at the headquarters of the Governorate of Tunis.
