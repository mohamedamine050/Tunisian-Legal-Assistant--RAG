Export sales and services rendered outside Tunisia by services companies in the hydrocarbons sector established in Tunisia, as well as sales and services rendered in Tunisia but intended for use abroad, are considered export operations.  Profits derived from the aforementioned export operations are subject to corporate tax at a rate of 10%, for profits realized starting from January 1, 2008.
