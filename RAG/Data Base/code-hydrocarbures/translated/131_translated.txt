The exploration, research, and exploitation of hydrocarbons, as well as the offices, yards, and appurtenances where these activities are carried out, are subject to administrative oversight regarding compliance with technical regulations and the preservation of fields, the safety of personnel, facilities, the population, and buildings. This oversight is in addition to that carried out by the competent administrative authorities stipulated in the applicable legal and regulatory provisions.  The granting authority exercises this oversight under the supervision of the Minister responsible for hydrocarbons and through the Head of the departments responsible for hydrocarbons and the personnel under his orders and delegated for this purpose.  Administrative decisions of a general nature, and not relating to a specific person, are subject to this oversight.
