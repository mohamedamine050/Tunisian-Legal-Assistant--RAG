## English Translation:

1. **Article 39.** The holder of a valid exploration license who discovers a hydrocarbon field within the area of his license, which is deemed commercially viable and meets the conditions stipulated in this Code, the regulations enacted for its implementation, and the relevant special agreement, shall be granted the exploitation concession.

2. **Article 39.** The Tunisian State may, under pre-agreed conditions within the framework of a special agreement, grant a concession to any entity possessing the necessary technical and financial capacity to exploit a concession that has been returned, abandoned, or relinquished. The Tunisian State may also, under pre-agreed conditions and within the same framework, grant an exploitation concession to any entity possessing the necessary technical and financial capacity, for a discovery not covered by an exploration license, a research license, or an exploitation concession.
