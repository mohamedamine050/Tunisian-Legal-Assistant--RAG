## English Translation:

**Article 112**

1.  To encourage exploration activities in areas with difficult access or aimed at gas targets or deep geological objectives, the Minister responsible for hydrocarbons may grant the exploration permit holder an increase of 10% to 30% in exploration expenses for fiscal consumption. However, benefiting from the increase referred to in this Article excludes benefiting from the provisions referred to in the second and third paragraphs of Article 110 of this Code.

2. The conditions for granting this concession, the definition of areas with difficult access, as well as the depth considered to be deep drilling are determined by a decision of the Minister responsible for hydrocarbons, based on an opinion conforming to the Advisory Committee for Hydrocarbons.

3. The provisions of this Article apply to holders of exploration permits granted both before and after the issuance of this Code.
