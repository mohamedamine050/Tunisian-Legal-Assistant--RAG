## English Translation:

**Article 15** 

1. The exploration permit is granted based on the technical and financial capabilities of the applicant, the importance and nature of the proposed work program, as well as the level of contribution of the national entity or the conditions for sharing hydrocarbon production as stipulated in Chapter Two of Title Six of this Code. In all cases, the exploration permit is granted at the discretion of the granting authority. This choice does not give rise to any right to compensation for the applicant whose request has been rejected in whole or in part.

2. The concerned party shall be directly informed of the rejection of their application by the Minister in charge of hydrocarbons.

3. The registration fee paid to the State upon filing the application, as referred to in sub-paragraph 1 of Article 101 of this Code, is not refundable if the application is rejected or cancelled.
