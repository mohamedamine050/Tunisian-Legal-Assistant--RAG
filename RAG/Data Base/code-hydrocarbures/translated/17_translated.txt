1. Article 17: The exploration license is granted by a decision taken by the Minister in charge of hydrocarbons, based on an opinion of approval issued by the Hydrocarbons Advisory Committee. This decision is published in the Official Journal of the Tunisian Republic. 

2. Article 17: The exploration license is granted for an initial period of a maximum of five years, subject to renewal. This renewal may be repeated, in accordance with the conditions of this law, the regulations enacted for its implementation, and the specific agreement.
