If the license holder fails to achieve the minimum commitment for expenditures and the work program, or either of them, without prejudice to the conditions stipulated in Chapter 23 of this Code in paragraphs B, W, and J, he is entitled to renew his exploration license after paying the granting authority the difference between the minimum amount of expenses that should have been incurred and the amount of expenses incurred, or the amount necessary to complete the work as stipulated in the relevant agreement. The aforementioned payments shall be mandatory even if the license holder relinquishes his license or decides not to renew it.
