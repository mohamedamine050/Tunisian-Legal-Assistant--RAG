Anyone against whom a judgment has been issued for an offense described as a felony, in the meaning of this law,  — felonies being crimes that warrant punishment by death or imprisonment exceeding five years —;  and offenses described as misdemeanors, being crimes that warrant punishment by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars; and offenses described as infractions, being crimes warranting punishment not exceeding fifteen days' imprisonment or sixty dinars fine (Article 122 of the Code of Criminal Procedure).  As stipulated in Article 138 of this Code, and relating to the commission of the same infraction within twelve months from the day the judgment became final, shall be punished with the maximum penalties stipulated in that article.
