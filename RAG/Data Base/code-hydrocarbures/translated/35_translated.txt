The holder of an exploration license shall be entitled, at any time, to voluntarily reduce the area of their license, provided they notify the granting authority of this reduction, specifying precisely the perimeters they intend to relinquish. In this case, the areas to be retained shall be adjusted at each renewal, without consideration for the areas voluntarily reduced. This shall not affect the minimum commitments regarding works and expenses specified for each validity period. 

2. 35 The holder of an exploration license shall be entitled, at any time, to voluntarily reduce the validity period of their license, provided they notify the granting authority and have fulfilled the minimum commitment for works and expenses, or both, relating to the period subject to reduction. 

3. 35 A decision issued by the Minister responsible for hydrocarbons shall adjust the area to be retained and/or the remaining validity period of the license.
