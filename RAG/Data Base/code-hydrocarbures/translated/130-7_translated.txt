The seconded agents, in accordance with the provisions of Article 6.130 of this Code, benefit from temporary exemption from the payment of duties and taxes due upon the importation of personal belongings and one private passenger car per person.  The transfer of the vehicle or imported belongings belonging to a resident is subject to the current foreign trade regulations and to the payment of the duties and taxes due on the date of transfer, based on the value of the vehicle or belongings on that date.
