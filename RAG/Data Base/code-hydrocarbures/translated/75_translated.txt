## English Translation:

1. **Article 75:** The transportation of liquid, gaseous, dissolved or dissolved under pressure fuels is **mandatory subject to** the legislation and regulations in force in the field of environmental protection, resource conservation, accident prevention and protection of third parties. It is also **subject to** technical standards, technical rules, and safety regulations in force in the field of construction and operation of pipelines and related facilities.

2. **Article 75:** **Shall benefit from** all provisions relating to **right of way** contained in the legislation and regulations in force for the benefit of fuel transportation facilities of public interest, any facility dedicated to the transportation of fuels for the purpose of developing and exploiting one or more concessions **held by** the owners of these facilities and **licensed by** the granting authority, pursuant to the provisions of this Code.
