Article 133 empowers the granting authority to prohibit any action that contravenes the provisions of this Code and the regulations adopted for its implementation, without prejudice to the right to compensation for damages and the penalties stipulated in Article 138 of this Code. 2. Article 133. Regardless of the proceedings and penalties provided for in this Code, current legislation, and regulations, the granting authority may order the immediate cessation of works in the event of serious violations affecting the safety of third parties and the environment and/or resources, particularly those related to non-compliance with environmental protection procedures stipulated in the environmental impact assessment study as approved by the competent authority.
