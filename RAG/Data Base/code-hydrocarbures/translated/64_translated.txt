1. Article 64: The license holder shall provide the granting authority, in accordance with a model approved by it, with a quarterly report and an annual report relating to the activities and expenses incurred under the programs and annual budgets that have been notified to it. 

2. Article 64: The license holder shall notify the granting authority of all service contracts, works, and equipment contracts exceeding the amount specified in the special agreement. The granting authority may request from the license holder all justifications for these expenses, including those incurred by the parent company or subsidiaries of the same group.
