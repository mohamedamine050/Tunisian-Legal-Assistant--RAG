**Article 102**

1.  If the concession relates primarily to the exploitation of crude oil with associated or dissolved gas, the tax on profits stipulated for liquid hydrocarbons shall apply and the relative royalty rates stipulated in paragraphs "a" and "b" of Article 4.2.101 of this Code shall apply, as the case may be, for liquid or gaseous hydrocarbons.

2. If the concession relates primarily to the exploitation of non-associated gas, the tax on profits stipulated for gaseous hydrocarbons shall apply, and the relative royalty rates stipulated in sub-paragraphs "a" and "b" of Article 4.2.101 of this Code shall apply, as the case may be, for extracted liquids or gaseous hydrocarbons.
