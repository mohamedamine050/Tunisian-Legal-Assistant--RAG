The licensee or contractor, as defined in this Code, may be a resident or non-resident.  A licensee or contractor operating within a company subject to Tunisian law shall be considered a non-resident if its capital is owned by non-resident Tunisians or foreigners, subscribed through the inflow of convertible currency, to the extent of at least 66% of the capital. The contribution of resident persons to the capital of a non-resident licensee's or contractor's company shall be in accordance with the applicable exchange regulations. Establishments created by legal persons whose registered office is located abroad shall be considered non-resident with regard to exchange regulations. The contribution of the registered office must be financed by the inflow of convertible currency.
