**To determine the profit:**

Profit is the excess amount owed to the company above its liabilities, calculated by including the company's capital within its debts at the end of the financial year.

The activities subject to tax on the profits referred to in paragraph three of Chapter 101 of this Code shall be treated by the licensee as independent from his other activities in the Tunisian Republic. To this end, the licensee must maintain an accounting in dinars in Tunisia, in accordance with the current legislation, under the heading of activities subject to tax on the profits referred to in paragraph three of Chapter 101 of this Code.
