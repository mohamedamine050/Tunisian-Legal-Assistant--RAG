1. **Article 41**: Upon completion of the evaluation works, the licensee is entitled to obtain an exploitation concession covering the discovered field, subject to the conditions stipulated in this Law, its implementing regulations, and the specific agreement, if it is deemed economically viable to exploit the discovery. 

2. **Article 41**: The licensee is entitled to obtain an exploitation concession in accordance with the conditions referred to in the first paragraph of this chapter if they demonstrate that the discovery can be exploited economically without conducting additional evaluation works.
