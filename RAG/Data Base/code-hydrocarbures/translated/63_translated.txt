The holder of an exploration license, a search license, and/or an exploitation concession shall notify the granting authority of all geological, geophysical, hydrological, drilling, and exploitation information in their possession. This information shall not be published or disclosed to third parties without the prior consent of the license holder, except for general statistics, general geology, and inventory of water resources. However, this consent shall not be required if the information pertains to areas of the licenses and/or exploitation concessions that have been returned to the granting authority.
