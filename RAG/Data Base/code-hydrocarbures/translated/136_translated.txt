The courts shall consider offenses.  Offenses described as felonies, within the meaning of this law, are crimes punishable by death or imprisonment exceeding five years.  Offenses described as misdemeanors are crimes punishable by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars. Offenses described as infractions are crimes punishable by imprisonment not exceeding fifteen days or a fine not exceeding sixty dinars.  Article 122 of the Code of Criminal Procedure governs the provisions of this Code and the regulations adopted for its implementation.
