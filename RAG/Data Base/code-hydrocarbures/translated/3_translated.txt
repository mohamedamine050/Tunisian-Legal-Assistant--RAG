(Added by Article 2 of Law No. 23 of 2002 dated February 14, 2002) The provisions of this Code, except for those specifically related to the activities of the licensee, shall apply to any works or services entity that replaces the licensee in managing and carrying out the activities of exploration, research and/or exploitation.  "Works and/or services entity" as used in this Article shall mean:

- The contractor, if it is a production-sharing contract. - The company established by the national institution and its partners, if it is a partnership contract. - Any company to which the licensee, after obtaining the approval of the granting authority, assigns the role of general contractor for the works of exploration, research and/or exploitation.
