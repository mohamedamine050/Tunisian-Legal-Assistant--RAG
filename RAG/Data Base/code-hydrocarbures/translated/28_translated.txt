If the license holder fails to meet the minimum expenditure commitment or the work program, or either of them, without prejudice to the provisions stipulated in Article 23 of this Code, paragraphs (b), (c), (d) and (e), he is entitled to renew his exploration license after paying the granting authority the difference between the minimum amount of expenses required to be completed and the amount of expenses actually completed, or the amount necessary to complete the works as stipulated in the relevant agreement. The aforementioned payments shall be mandatory even if the license holder relinquishes his license or decides not to renew it.
