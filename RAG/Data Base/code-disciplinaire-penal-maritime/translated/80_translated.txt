If, as a result of the crimes stipulated in Article 79 of this Code or any other negligence attributed to the captain, the chief officer, or the pilot, a ship or any other ship suffers a collision, grounding, or striking against a known or visible obstacle, or serious damage to the ship or its cargo, the perpetrator of the crime shall be punished by a fine of three hundred dinars and imprisonment for a period of six months, or by one of these two penalties.

If the crime results in the loss of the ship, its complete unsuitability for navigation, or the loss of the cargo, or if it results in serious injuries or the death of one or more persons, the perpetrator of the crime shall be punished by a fine of six hundred dinars and imprisonment for a period of two years, or by one of these two penalties.
