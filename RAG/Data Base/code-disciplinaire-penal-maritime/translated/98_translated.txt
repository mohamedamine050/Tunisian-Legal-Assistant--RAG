## English Translation:

**Article 122 of the Code of Criminal Procedure**

Any person who commits an offense described as a misdemeanor, as defined in this law, shall be punished by a fine of one hundred and twenty dinars and imprisonment for a period of six months, or by one of these penalties.

Crimes that warrant punishment by death or imprisonment for a period exceeding five years shall be described as felonies within the meaning of this law.

Crimes that warrant punishment by imprisonment for a period exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars, shall be described as misdemeanors.

Crimes that warrant punishment not exceeding fifteen days of imprisonment or sixty dinars of fine shall be described as infractions.

**Article 99**

Except for employees and agents of the public authorities concerned, any person who boards a ship without a ticket shall be punished by a fine of one dinar. 

This applies to the provisions of Article 12 of the Maritime Labor Code.
