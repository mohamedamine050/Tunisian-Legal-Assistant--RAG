The third serious offense, as defined in Chapter IX of this Code, as well as serious offenses related to it committed during the same voyage, shall be punishable by imprisonment for a period of six months. However, the maritime authority representative concerned may, if in his opinion the nature of the offense and the circumstances surrounding it are not sufficient to warrant prosecution,  leave the crime classified as a misdemeanor.

Crimes punishable by death or imprisonment for a term exceeding five years shall be classified as felonies under the meaning of this law. 

Crimes punishable by imprisonment for a term exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars shall be classified as misdemeanors.

Crimes punishable by imprisonment for a term not exceeding fifteen days or by a fine not exceeding sixty dinars shall be classified as offenses. (Article 122, Code of Criminal Procedure).

Minor offenses that are considered serious offenses under the first paragraph of Article 9 of this Code shall not be classified as misdemeanors.

Disciplinary penalties prescribed in Chapter X of this Code shall be imposed upon such offenses.
