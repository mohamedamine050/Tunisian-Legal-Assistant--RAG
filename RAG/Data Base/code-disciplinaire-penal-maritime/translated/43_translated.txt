Any person who, being a mariner, shall wilfully corrupt provisions, beverages, or other consumable materials by mixing them with non-poisonous substances shall be punished by imprisonment for a term of six months.

If poisonous substances are used, the punishment shall be imprisonment for a term of five years. If such act results in the serious injury of one or more persons, the punishment shall be hard labor for a term of ten years.

If the act results in unintentional death, the punishment shall be hard labor, the term of which shall be doubled.
