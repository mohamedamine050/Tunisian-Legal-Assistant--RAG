A captain, assigned by the competent authority pursuant to the provisions of Article 21 of this Code, who, without a legitimate reason, refuses to accept a search file, seized goods, or to transport a suspect, or who fails to deliver the suspect or the file in his possession to the designated maritime authority for acceptance, shall be punished by a fine of seven hundred dinars. Notwithstanding the situation of escape or assistance in escape, the provisions of Articles 146 to 149 of the Criminal Code shall apply to the suspect and his accomplices.
