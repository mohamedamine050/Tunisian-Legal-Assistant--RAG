An appeal filed in Tunisia or abroad by a person punished for a disciplinary offense, for a violation described as a felony within the meaning of this law â€“ offenses punishable by death or imprisonment exceeding five years, is to be filed with the Maritime Authority within one month. The appeal is deemed inadmissible if this deadline is not met. The date on which the party concerned is notified of the penalty shall be the starting point for this period.

Crimes punishable by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars, are classified as misdemeanors.

Crimes punishable by imprisonment not exceeding fifteen days or a fine of sixty dinars are classified as offenses. (Article 122 of the Code of Criminal Procedure)

The Minister responsible for Merchant Shipping or his deputy shall hear the authority that made the decision as well as the accused. The Minister shall receive all evidence that he considers useful and shall issue a reasoned decision.

The appeal filed pursuant to the provisions of this chapter shall not be subject to any form of appeal.
