Any person, regardless of nationality, who sails on board a Tunisian or foreign vessel and fails to comply with regulations, general administrative decisions not pertaining to a specific individual, or orders issued by the maritime authority within Tunisian territorial waters, pertaining to the regime of waters or ports or to the administrative regime of maritime navigation, shall be punished with a fine of two hundred dinars and imprisonment for a period of six months, or with one of these penalties.

The same penalties shall also be applied to any person, regardless of nationality, who sails on board a Tunisian vessel and fails to comply with orders issued by the maritime authority outside of Tunisian territorial waters.
