The provisions of Articles 78 to 85 of this Code shall apply to persons, including foreigners, present on board a foreign vessel if the crime was committed within Tunisian territorial waters.

By way of exception to the first paragraph of Article 1 of this Code, the provisions of Articles 78 to 85, as well as the provisions of Article 66 of this Code, shall apply to persons present on board any vessel or machine referred to in Article 8 B of the Maritime Commerce Code. In such cases, the master shall be deemed to be the person who is actually steering the vessel or machine.

The penalty shall be reduced if one of the crimes set forth in Articles 78 to 85 is committed by a person exercising command under the illicit circumstances regulated by Article 61 of this Code.
