Any ship captain or security chief who commits a violation of the rules imposed to avoid collisions at sea, whether regarding navigation lights at night, mandatory signals during fog, or the course to be followed or the actions to be taken upon encountering another vessel, shall be punished by a fine of two hundred dinars and imprisonment for three months, or by one of these punishments.

**Crimes** as defined in this law refer to offenses punishable by death or imprisonment for a period exceeding five years.

**Misdemeanors** refer to offenses punishable by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars.

**Violations** refer to offenses punishable by imprisonment not exceeding fifteen days or a fine not exceeding sixty dinars. 122 Code of Criminal Procedure.
