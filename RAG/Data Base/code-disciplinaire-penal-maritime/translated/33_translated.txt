Any master who, without lawful excuse, refuses or neglects to:

1.  Prepare and submit to the maritime authority the following documents:
    *  Statements of condition
    *  Loss reports
    *  Reports of illnesses, injuries, and deaths that occur on board the vessel 

2.  Maintain the ship's logbook, disciplinary logbook, and other legal documents in a proper manner,

shall be punished with a fine of fifty dinars and/or imprisonment for a period of one month.
