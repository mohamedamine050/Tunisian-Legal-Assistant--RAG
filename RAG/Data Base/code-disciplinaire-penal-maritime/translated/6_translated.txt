## Minor Disciplinary Offences:

1) Simple non-compliance with any work-related order without resisting an official written warning issued in the presence of witnesses by the Head of a government entity, according to established procedures.
2) Intoxication on board the vessel without causing disturbance and outside of working hours, except for the scenario described in Article 48 of this Code.
3) Unauthorized absence from the vessel for a period not exceeding four hours, committed by the seaman outside or during working hours, without being assigned to a guard or security post, provided that the absence does not cause the vessel to depart.
4) Arguments and altercations without violence.
5) Any offence described as a felony under the meaning of this law - crimes punishable by death or imprisonment exceeding five years; or as a misdemeanor - crimes punishable by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days and not exceeding five years or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars; or as an infraction - crimes punishable by imprisonment not exceeding fifteen days or by a fine not exceeding sixty dinars (Article 122 of the Code of Criminal Procedure) - which are not considered serious and are not listed in Chapter IX of this Code.
