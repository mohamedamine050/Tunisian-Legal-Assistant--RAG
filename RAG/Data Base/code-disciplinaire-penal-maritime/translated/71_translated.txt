If a stowaway who disembarked in Tunisia is returned to one of the countries mentioned in Article 70 of this Code, and that country refuses to bear the expenses incurred in returning the stowaway outside of Tunisia, then those expenses shall be deducted from the ship on which the stowaway embarked. This is in addition to the owner of the ship being liable to the state of which the stowaway is a national.

However, the owner of the ship or his representative shall not be liable for the costs of care for more than three days beginning from the delivery of the stowaway to the competent authority at the port of disembarkation.
