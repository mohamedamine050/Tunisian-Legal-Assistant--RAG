Theft committed aboard a ship shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of Articles 258 to 282 of the Penal Code.

However, the aggravating circumstances stipulated in the third and fourth paragraphs of Article 263 of the Penal Code do not change the nature of the crime, which remains a misdemeanor.

Misdemeanors are defined as crimes punishable by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars.

The perpetrator shall be subject to the penalties stipulated in Article 264 of the Penal Code.

The provisions of the preceding paragraphs do not preclude the application of Articles 7 and 10 of this Code.
