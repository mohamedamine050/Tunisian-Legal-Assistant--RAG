Any person, other than employees and agents of the public services concerned, who boards a ship without a travel ticket or entry permit, or without the authorization of the captain or the outfitter, or who is not summoned for the needs of operation, shall be punished with a fine of two hundred dinars.

The fine shall be doubled in the event of a recurrence in the same year, and the court may, in addition, impose a prison sentence of one month.
