**English Translation:**

Any ship's captain who fails to deposit the crew logbook and disciplinary logbook with the Maritime Authority office within the timeframes stipulated in the following paragraph shall be subject to a fine of one hundred dinars, except in cases of legal impediment.

The deadline for depositing the documents mentioned in the aforementioned paragraph shall be 24 hours from the ship's arrival, provided that the vessel's stay in port exceeds 24 hours, excluding official holidays. 

If the ship's stay is less than 24 hours, the deposit shall be made immediately upon arrival.
