All persons, regardless of their nationality, who are present on board any Tunisian ship holding a crew register, except warships, are subject to the provisions of this Code, whether these persons are registered in the crew register or are actually present on board the ship as passengers or for a trip, wherever the ship may be.

**Exception to the foregoing provisions:**

Soldiers of the land, sea, or air forces who sail in any capacity on board one of the ships referred to in the preceding paragraph shall be tried for misdemeanors or felonies set forth in this Code by military courts. The procedure for investigating and examining misdemeanors or felonies set forth in the preceding paragraph, as well as the conditions for imposing disciplinary penalties set forth in this Code, which are committed by soldiers of the land, sea, or air forces, shall be regulated by law.
