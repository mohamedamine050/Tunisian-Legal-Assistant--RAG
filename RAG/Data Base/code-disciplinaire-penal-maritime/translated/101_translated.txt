##  Statutes of Limitation

**Statutes of Limitation** refer to the loss of the right to pursue a claim due to failure to meet the prescribed deadlines. 

**For public actions, execution of punishment, and personal actions**, with regard to felonies and misdemeanors defined in this Code, the statutes of limitation applicable to the courts of general jurisdiction shall apply.

**The time limits for declaring punishment, executing punishment, and pursuing personal actions**, regarding serious disciplinary offenses, are those specified for offenses.  These time limits shall only begin to run from the day following the commission of the act and the ship's arrival at a port with maritime authority.
