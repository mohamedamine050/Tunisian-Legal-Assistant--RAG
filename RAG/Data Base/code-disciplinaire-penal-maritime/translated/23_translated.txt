The Public Prosecutor with territorial jurisdiction may not initiate proceedings for misdemeanors provided for in Articles 76 to 83 and 88 of this Code except in light of an investigation into the suspected individualâ€™s presence conducted by the maritime authority having knowledge of the crime, following the expiration of eight days from the date of his request for written observations concerning the investigation through a registered letter with acknowledgment of receipt.

The maritime authority may be heard by the court in relation to all misdemeanors provided for in this Code.

The methods of the in-person investigation provided for in the first paragraph of this Article are regulated by an order issued upon the proposal of the Minister in charge of Merchant Shipping.
