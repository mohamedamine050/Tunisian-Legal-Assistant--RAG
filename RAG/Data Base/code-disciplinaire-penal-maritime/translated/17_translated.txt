The investigation of felonies and misdemeanors committed on board and their examination, whether following a lawsuit:

A legal action initiated by a plaintiff against another party, which may be an individual or a private institution, seeking a right or compensation for a loss caused by them,

shall be conducted by any concerned person or automatically:

1) By judicial police officers.

2) By sworn officers of the merchant navy.

3) By crew members of national maritime vessels.

4) By sworn officers of the national coastal guard service.

5) By coastguard officers regarding the crimes stipulated in articles 38 and 62 of this code.

6) By the ship's captain of the vessel on which the felonies and misdemeanors were committed, in the case of vessels not intended for coastal navigation.

7) By sworn officers of the maritime fishing administration in the case of vessels equipped for coastal fishing.
