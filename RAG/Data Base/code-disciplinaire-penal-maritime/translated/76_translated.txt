Any person who assists, whether by sea or by land, a traveler in hiding to embark or disembark, or who frightens them or provides them with food without the knowledge of the captain, shall be subject to a fine of three hundred dinars and imprisonment for a period of six months, or to one of these two penalties.

When several people gather to facilitate the clandestine embarkation, the fine is raised to six hundred dinars and the imprisonment to one year, or to one of these two penalties.

In the event of recidivism within one year, the penalties stipulated in the preceding paragraphs shall be doubled.
