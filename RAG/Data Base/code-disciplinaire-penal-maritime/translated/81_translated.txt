Any member of the crew, except the captain, chief mate, or pilot, who commits, during work, a negligent act without justification, lack of attention, or any other breach of his duties, which causes any vessel to collide, run aground, or strike a known and apparent obstacle, or results in serious damage to the vessel or its cargo, shall be punished by a fine of two hundred dinars and imprisonment for a period of three months, or by one of these punishments.

If the crime results in the loss of the vessel, its complete unfitness for navigation, the loss of the cargo, or if it results in serious injuries or the death of one or more persons, the crime shall be punished by a fine of four hundred dinars and imprisonment for a period of six months, or by one of these punishments.
