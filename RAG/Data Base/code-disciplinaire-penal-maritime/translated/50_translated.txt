Any sailor who commits violence shall be punished with a fine of one hundred dinars and imprisonment for one year, or with one of these penalties, for the types of violence stipulated in Article 319 of the Penal Code. For the types of violence stipulated in Article 218 of the Penal Code, the fine shall be increased to seven hundred dinars and imprisonment to five years.

When violence is followed by disfigurement or ugliness in the face, disability or persistent illness, the aggressor shall be punished with hard labor for a period of ten years.

The punishment is reduced when violence results in death.

Any murder committed on board a ship intentionally and with premeditation shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of Articles 201 and 202 of the Penal Code.
