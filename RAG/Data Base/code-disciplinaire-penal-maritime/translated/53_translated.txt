Any person who, while sailing from a Tunisian port, participates in an illegal strike or work stoppage, and occupies places or leadership positions during the illegal strike or work stoppage, as well as uses the equipment on board the ship, navigation devices, and machinery for purposes other than those intended, shall be punished by imprisonment for a period of three months and a fine of two hundred and forty dinars, or by one of these penalties. If such use results in either disruption of the ship's operations and exploitation, or disruption of order on board the ship, the person shall be punished with the penalties stipulated in the preceding paragraph.
