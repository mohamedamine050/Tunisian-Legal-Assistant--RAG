Upon surrendering a stowaway to the competent authority, the master shall provide the authority with a declaration containing all the information he knows about the stowaway, particularly related to his nationality or nationalities, his port of embarkation, the date and time of his discovery, and the geographic location of the vessel at that time. The declaration shall also mention the vessel's port of departure and the ports of call along with the dates of arrival and departure.

Except in cases where the stowaway is the subject of an individual measure of expulsion or refusal of entry, the competent authority of each Tunisian port shall accept any stowaway surrendered to it in accordance with the provisions of this section and shall treat the stowaway in accordance with the provisions of this chapter.
