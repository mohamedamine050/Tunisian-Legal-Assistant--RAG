Any captain who, in a situation of danger, neglects to organize the rescue of the crew and passengers before abandoning the vessel shall be punished by imprisonment for a term of two years.

The punishment shall be imprisonment for a term of three months if the captain neglects to rescue the vessel's papers, postal mail, and the most valuable cargo.

Any captain who is compelled to abandon the vessel, and who is not the last to leave, shall be punished by imprisonment for a term of two years.
