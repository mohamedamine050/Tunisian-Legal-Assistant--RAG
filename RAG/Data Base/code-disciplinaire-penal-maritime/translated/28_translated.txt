## English Translation:

**Article 1:** Any officer, master, or crew member who absents himself from the ship in a Tunisian or foreign port without lawful reason, while assigned to a security or safety post, shall be punished by imprisonment for a term of six months.

**Article 2:** An officer or master who absents himself from the ship in a Tunisian or foreign port without lawful reason, whether during or outside working hours, shall be punished by a fine of fifty dinars. A crew member who absents himself under the same circumstances shall be punished by a fine of twenty dinars. This applies only if his absence causes the ship to depart without him.
