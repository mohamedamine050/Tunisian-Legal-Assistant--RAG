With regard to the members of the ship's crew referred to in the first chapter of this Code, summonses, procedural documents, and judgments shall be exempt from stamp duty and shall be registered free of charge.

Summonses shall be delivered to the addressees and served without cost by the officers of the merchant navy. Notification of judicial decisions shall be effected by registered letter bearing the names of the parties and the subject matter of the case.

Such notification shall open the timeframes for objection, appeal, and appeal to the Court of Cassation.

Any punishment imposed on a sailor for a felony or misdemeanor as provided for in this Code shall be subject to a registered letter containing the judicial decision, which shall be addressed to the Directorate of the Merchant Navy for recording purposes.
