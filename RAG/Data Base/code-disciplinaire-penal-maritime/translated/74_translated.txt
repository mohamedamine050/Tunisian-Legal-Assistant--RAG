The authority granted to the ship's master and the competent authority regarding the fate of a stowaway, as provided for in the provisions of this chapter, is added to all rights and obligations in this regard, without the possibility of modification.

For the application of the provisions of this section, the ship's master and the port of disembarkation authority shall take into account the reasons given by the stowaway for not disembarking or returning to certain ports and countries referred to in Chapter 70 of this Code.
