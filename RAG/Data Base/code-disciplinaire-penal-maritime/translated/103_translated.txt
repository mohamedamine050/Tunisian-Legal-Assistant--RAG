The amounts resulting from fines and penalties prescribed under this Code shall be paid as follows:

(a) In the case of wages withheld in the event of detention without arrest, wages, and disciplinary fines stipulated in Article 10 of this Code, to the account of the designated fund, "Fund for the Assistance of Maritime Organizations," opened in the account of the Secretary General of the Tunisian Treasury.

(b) In the case of criminal fines, to the general state budget.
