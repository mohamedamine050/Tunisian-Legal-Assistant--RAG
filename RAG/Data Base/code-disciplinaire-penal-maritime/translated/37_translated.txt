Any person who intentionally assists in the commission of the offense of driving a vessel without a license shall be punished by a fine of two hundred dinars and imprisonment for a period of six months, or by one of these two penalties.

The same penalty may be imposed, with an increase in the fine to seven hundred dinars, against any person who illegally takes control of the vessel, as well as against the person who equips the vessel and assists in the commission of the offense.
