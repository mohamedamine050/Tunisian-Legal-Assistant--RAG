A captain who, without a legitimate reason, refuses or neglects to carry out the necessary inspections on board a vessel shall be punished by a fine of five hundred dinars and imprisonment for a term of one year, or by either of these penalties.

**Definitions:**

* **Felony (جناية):** A serious crime punishable by law, subject to severe penalties such as imprisonment.
* **Misdemeanor (جنحة):** A crime that warrants a prison sentence exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or a fine exceeding sixty dinars.
