The penalties prescribed in Articles 172 to 175 of the Criminal Code shall apply to any master, officer, captain, or crew member who, on the legal documents of the vessel, fraudulently records false or misleading facts that are contrary to the truth.

Crimes that warrant punishment by death or imprisonment exceeding five years shall be described as felonies within the meaning of this law.

Crimes that warrant punishment by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars, shall be described as misdemeanors.

Crimes that warrant punishment not exceeding fifteen days of imprisonment or sixty dinars of fine shall be described as offenses. 122 Code of Criminal Procedure.
