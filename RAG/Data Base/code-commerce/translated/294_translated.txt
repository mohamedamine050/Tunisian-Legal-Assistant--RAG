(Paragraph 2 was amended by Law No. 61 of 2000 dated 20 June 2000). 

The holder of a bill of exchange payable on a specific date, or on a certain date from a specified date, or after sight, shall present the bill for payment on the due date or on one of the business days following. If the bill of exchange is presented through a clearing house or by means of an electronic information exchange system which substitutes for the physical presentation of the bill, this shall constitute a presentation for payment.
