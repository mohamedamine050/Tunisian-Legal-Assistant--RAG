Upon the occurrence of opposition to payment of the price, the seller may, in all stages of the case and after the expiry of the twenty-day period stipulated in Article 192, proceed summarily before the President of the Court with a request for payment of the price notwithstanding the opposition, provided that he deposits with the Deposit and Consignment Fund or another person other than the defendants entrusted with receiving this deposit, the sufficient amount determined by the summary judge to guarantee any debts that may arise from the opposition, which the seller acknowledges or a judgment is issued confirming their existence against him.
