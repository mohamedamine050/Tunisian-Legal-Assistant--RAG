## English Translation:

**Appeals against judgments rendered in collective proceedings**

**shall be governed by the provisions stipulated in this title.**

**Appeals against decisions issued by the Court President in amicable settlement and judicial settlement**

**shall follow the procedures laid down in the Code of Civil Procedure.**

**Appeals against decisions issued by the Court President in granting authorizations for claims in commercial matters** 

**shall be governed by the provisions stipulated in the Code of Civil Procedure.**
