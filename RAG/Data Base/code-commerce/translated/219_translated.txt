Upon completion of the sale, the seller and the buyer shall sign the accounting records maintained by the seller, which cover the three preceding fiscal years prior to the sale and the current fiscal year.

The ownership of the accounting records referred to in the preceding paragraph shall pass to the buyer of the commercial enterprise, unless otherwise stipulated in the contract.

The buyer shall enable the seller to review the aforementioned records for a period of ten years following the sale. If the seller retains ownership of the accounting records, he shall enable the buyer to access them for a period of ten years following the sale.

Any clause contradicting the provisions of the third and fourth paragraphs above shall be considered null and void.
