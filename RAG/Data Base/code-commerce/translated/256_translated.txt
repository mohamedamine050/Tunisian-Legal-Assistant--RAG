The successful bidder is obligated to accept the equipment and goods present at the premises upon taking possession thereof, at the price determined by experts by mutual agreement or upon order of the judge in the presence of both the purchaser of the auction and the seller and the successful bidder. In addition to the price he paid in the bid, he shall be obligated to pay the purchaser who was deprived of possession the expenses and costs of his purchase contract, along with the expenses of risk, registration, and advertisement, and to pay the expenses arising from the second sale to the person entitled to them.
