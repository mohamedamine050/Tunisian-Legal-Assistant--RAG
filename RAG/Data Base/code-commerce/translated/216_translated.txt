The clerks of the courts of first instance shall provide each applicant with a list of the records, along with the information relating to precedence, partial or complete dismissal, or the settlement of some of the creditors in place of others for the whole debt or part thereof, or a certificate stating the absence of records or their existence without details. They shall also provide each applicant with a list of the records or data contained in the registry of the Industrial Property Office affiliated with the court, pursuant to Article 208. 

The public administrators charged with the sale of a commercial business may, when they deem it beneficial, obtain from the competent clerk a copy of the sale contracts deposited with the court registry and relating to the commercial business.
