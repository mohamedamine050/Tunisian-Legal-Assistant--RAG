**(Paragraph one amended by Law No. 61 of the year 2000 dated June 20, 2000)**

The registration must be effected within one month of the date of the contract, otherwise, the mortgage shall be null and void.

Any person having an interest, including the debtor himself, may invoke this nullity.

In case of bankruptcy, the provisions of Articles 462 and 463 of this Code shall apply to the mortgage of commercial assets.
