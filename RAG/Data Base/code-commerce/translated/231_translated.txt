(Amended by Article 2 of Law No. 61 of the year 2000, dated June 20, 2000) The lessee shall have the status of a merchant and shall be subject to all his obligations. He shall, in particular, register his name in the commercial register.

Every lease agreement concerning a commercial establishment shall be published within fifteen days from the date of its conclusion by publishing a summary thereof, specifying the mortgages and registered creditors, if any, in the Official Gazette of the Tunisian Republic and in two daily newspapers, one of which shall be in Arabic.

The lessee shall, within the same time limit, register his name in the commercial register or amend his personal registration therein, with an explicit mention of the lease of the commercial establishment. The termination of the lease period shall be published by the aforementioned means.
