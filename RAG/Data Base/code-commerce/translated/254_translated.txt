The auctioneer shall endeavor to fulfill the obligations pertaining to the procedures and sale. Should the auctioneer neglect these obligations, the secured creditor or the buyer shall be bound by them at the expense of the auctioneer, who shall bear all the consequences. The guarantor's undertaking shall remain in effect in accordance with the rules stipulated in paragraphs five, six, seven, and eight of Article 243, Articles 244 and 245, and paragraph four of Article 248.
