The provisions of this chapter shall apply to all:

**Legal Persons:**

Legal persons possessing legal personality, with their own resources and an independent financial capacity distinct from the financial capacities of their members or shareholders.

**Natural Persons:**

Natural persons subject to taxation under the real system who engage in commercial activities as defined in Article 2 of this Code, or artisanal activities, as well as commercial companies, regardless of their form, that engage in agricultural or fishing activities.

Excluded from the provisions of this law are public institutions and establishments as defined in Law No. 9 of 1989 dated February 1st, 1989, concerning public institutions and establishments.
