In the event of the commencement of judicial settlement or bankruptcy proceedings, a creditor who has accepted within the framework of a judicially approved amicable settlement agreement to provide new funds, movable or immovable property, or to provide services to assist the enterprise in continuing its activities, shall be given priority and shall recover his debts before other debts, with the exception of debts enjoying a privileged right of payment.

The provisions of the preceding paragraph shall not apply to debts existing prior to the judicially approved amicable settlement agreement and contributions granted by way of an increase in the capital of the enterprise.
