The President of the Court, the supervising judge, or the Court shall draft a report, a form of information transmission, formulated in a semi-final state for delivery to the future. This report shall be immediately submitted to the Attorney General whenever it becomes evident from the file documents that there are embezzlements or other acts that could constitute a crime related to the administration of the institution, as defined by the legislation in force.

The Public Prosecutor may request that the Urgent Judge place the movable or immovable gains or financial balances belonging to the suspect responsible for these acts under sequestration.
