If it appears that the general economic situation has undergone a significant change that has had a substantial impact on the enterprise's ability to implement the rescue program, the court, upon the request of the debtor, the Public Prosecution Office, the entity responsible for defending the interests of society and enforcing the law before the judicial courts, or the creditor(s) whose debts represent fifteen percent of the total debt, may amend the rescue program after obtaining the consent of the creditor(s) whose debts represent at least fifty percent of the total debt.
