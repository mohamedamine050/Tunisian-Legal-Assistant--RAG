The judicial administrator shall personally inventory the assets of the entity in the presence of its owner or manager, or after legally summoning them, immediately upon issuance of the decision to open judicial settlement proceedings. The judicial administrator may seek assistance from any qualified individuals in inventorying and valuing the assets. A copy of the inventory list shall be deposited with the court clerk. 

The judicial administrator shall submit a preliminary report to the presiding judge of the court within two months of his appointment, outlining the true economic, financial, and social situation of the entity.
