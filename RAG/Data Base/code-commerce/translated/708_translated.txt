The documents relating to the aforementioned transaction must be drawn up, otherwise, they will be void.

These documents shall include:

1) A statement of the mortgaged securities;

2) The name and address of the owner of the securities;

3) The amount of the credit granted and its conditions;

4) The value of the securities considered in granting the credit;

5) The percentage of the required guarantee;

6) A provision, where applicable, that the beneficiary of the credit undertakes to reimburse the bank upon the first request made by the bank in order to maintain the guarantee limit at the agreed level.  The omission of any of the aforementioned particulars may result in the legal termination of the contract at the request of the beneficiary.
