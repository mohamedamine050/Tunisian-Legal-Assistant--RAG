The omission of one or more of the statements listed in the tables referred to in Article 207 shall result in the annulment of the registration if it causes harm to non-contracting parties. No person may claim the annulment of the registration except those who have suffered harm as a result of the omission or breach of the formalities. The courts may decide, based on the severity and nature of the harm, to annul the registration or limit its consequences.
