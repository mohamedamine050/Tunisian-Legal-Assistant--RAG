A current account shall be considered a civil or commercial contract depending on the nature of the parties. Each payment received shall be categorized according to the nature of the account in which it is deposited.

A statement must be issued once a month, detailing all transactions that occurred during the preceding period, indicating the balance that is to be credited to the account of the appellant, unless otherwise agreed upon.

No request for correction of the account shall be accepted after three years have elapsed, unless one of the parties has raised objections within the aforementioned period by registered mail with acknowledgment of receipt.
