An appeal against a judgment rendered in a lawsuit, a legal action initiated by a plaintiff before a court against another party, whether an individual or a private entity, claiming a right or compensation for a loss caused by 
the annulment of a consensual settlement agreement shall be filed within twenty days of the date of its issuance.

An appeal to the Court of Cassation, a remedy allowing a party to challenge a court decision deemed to be contrary to law or where procedures were not properly followed, against the judgment of the Court of Appeal shall be filed within twenty days of the date of its issuance.
