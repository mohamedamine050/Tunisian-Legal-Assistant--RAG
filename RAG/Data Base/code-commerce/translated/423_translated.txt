The administrator or the owner of the establishment stipulated in the preceding article may submit to the President of the Court a written request to benefit from amicable settlement in accordance with the provisions of Article 417 of this Code.

The President of the Court shall determine the fees of the conciliators who shall be appointed by the debtor. The conciliation shall be free of charge if it is carried out by the [**Insert Name of Committee**] for the follow-up of economic establishments.

However, the President of the Court may compensate the conciliators at the request of the debtor for a serious reason within a period not exceeding eight days from the date of their appointment.
