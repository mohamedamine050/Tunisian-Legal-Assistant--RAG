The protest against acceptance or payment is drawn up by the executing officers. The protest must be lodged at the place of residence of the person who was obliged to pay the bill of exchange, or at any other known place of residence, and at the place of residence of the persons named in the bill of exchange to pay what is due, as needed, and at the place of residence of the foreigner who accepted it by intervention. All of this must be drawn up in a single document.
