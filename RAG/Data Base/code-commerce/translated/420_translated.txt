The auditor shall inquire in writing from the manager of the institution about any data or actions that he observes in the performance of his duties that threaten the continued operation of the institution. The manager shall respond within eight days of the date of receipt of the auditor's request.

If the manager fails to respond or if the response is unsatisfactory, the auditor shall refer the matter to the board of directors or the supervisory board of the institution, or, if necessary, convene a general meeting of shareholders within a period not exceeding one month from the date of receipt of the response or the expiration of the response period.

If the auditor observes the continued existence of the same risks, he shall submit a written report to the President of the Court of Cassation and send a copy thereof to the  Economic Institutions Monitoring Committee within one month from the date of completion of the procedures specified in the preceding paragraph.
