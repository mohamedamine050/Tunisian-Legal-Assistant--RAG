The provisions relating to negotiable instruments payable to a foreigner or in a place other than the domicile of the drawee (Articles 270 and 286) also apply to a bill of exchange. The provisions relating to the requirement of endorsement (Article 271), the discrepancy in the details concerning the amount payable (Article 272), and the provisions relating to the consequences of placing the signature on the conditions set out in Article 273, as well as the consequences of a person signing without authorization or exceeding the limits of their mandate (Article 273) shall also apply.
