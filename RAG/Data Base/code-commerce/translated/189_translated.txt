The contents of commercial goodwill include movable assets intended for the practice of commercial activities.

Commercial goodwill shall necessarily include goodwill and commercial reputation.

It shall also include, unless otherwise provided by law, all other things necessary for the exploitation of the commercial goodwill such as the business address, the trade name, the right to lease, equipment, machinery, goods, patents, trademarks, designs, models, and intellectual and artistic property rights.
