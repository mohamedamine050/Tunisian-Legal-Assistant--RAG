Every act concerning a commercial business, pursuant to the provisions of Article 190 mentioned above, must be declared within fifteen days of its date, and the content of the transfer must be published in one of the daily newspapers and in the Official Gazette of the Tunisian Republic, at the buyer's expense.

The aforementioned transfer notice must include, or it shall be null and void, the date of the contract, its subject matter, a statement of the transaction that took place between the parties, the date and number of the contract registration, the register in which it is recorded, the name, surname, and address of both the new owner and the previous owner, the type and location of the commercial business, the deadline for objections, and the chosen domicile within the jurisdiction of the court.

The content published in the Official Gazette of the Tunisian Republic shall also include the name and date of the daily newspaper in which it was published.
