In the event of the lapse of the right to claim the time limit referred to in Articles 646, 652, and 666, the creditor shall not be entitled to assert his right to bring an action, even an action in opposition to an original action brought against him, or to exercise this right against an action:
 
A legal procedure initiated by the plaintiff before the court against another party, which may be an individual or a private entity, in which the plaintiff claims a right or compensation for a loss caused by the defendant.
