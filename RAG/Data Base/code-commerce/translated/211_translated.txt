If negotiable instruments are created to evidence the debt owed, in accordance with the terms of the sale agreement, the right to benefit from the documentation shall pass to the holders of the instruments in succession.

If multiple instruments are created to evidence the debt, the priority right attached to it shall be granted to the first claimants, for the account of all of them, and to ensure the full payment of the debt.

All holders of the aforementioned instruments shall participate in the exercise of the priority right, regardless of the maturity date of the instruments in their possession.
