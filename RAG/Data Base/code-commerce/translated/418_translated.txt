The **[Committee Name]** is a formal mechanism for bringing together individuals with relevant expertise from across the institution. 

The **[Committee Name]** is responsible for monitoring economic institutions, through a **National Observatory**, which does not belong to any specific nation or geographic region, with the exception of other countries. It shall collect, analyze, and exchange information regarding economic institutions facing difficulties within an information network with the relevant stakeholders. The Committee shall provide the President of the **Court of First Instance** with all available information whenever requested. The Committee shall also be obliged to notify the President of the Court of every institution that has incurred losses exceeding one-third of its capital, as well as in cases where situations or actions threaten its continued activity, based on a reasoned **Report**. 

The supervisory authority over the Committee, its composition, and its operating procedures shall be determined by a government decree.
