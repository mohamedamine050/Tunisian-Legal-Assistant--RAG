The execution supervisor shall, upon the expiration of the lease or free agency period, submit a report to the President of the Court that issued the judgment within a period not exceeding fifteen days. This report shall detail the results of the process and whether all debts have been settled.

The debtor shall retrieve the tangible and intangible assets of the leased or entrusted establishment under a free agency agreement. The execution supervisor shall prepare an inventory of these assets under the supervision of the Court.
