The creditors shall deliver to the trustee, from the date of the bankruptcy judgment, their evidence together with a schedule showing the documents submitted and the amounts claimed. This schedule shall be certified as correct by the bankruptcy trustee and as corresponding to reality, and shall be signed by him or his agent, who shall attach a power of attorney to it.

The trustee shall deliver a receipt for the submission of the evidence.

The aforementioned evidence may be delivered to the trustee by a bailiff.
