The document for the order shall contain the following:

(1) The wording of the order condition or the designation of the document in the body of the text in the language used for its drafting.

(2) The promise without any restriction or condition to pay a specific amount.

(3) The designation of the due date for payment.

(4) The designation of the place where payment shall be made.

(5) The designation of the person to whom payment shall be made.

(6) The designation of the place and date on which the document was drawn.

(7) The signature of the drafter of the document, i.e., (the obligor).
