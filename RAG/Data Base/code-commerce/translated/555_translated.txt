Specifically, a claim may be made for commercial instruments or other negotiable instruments for which payment has not been made, which were delivered to the debtor and found in his possession at the time of the declaration of bankruptcy, if their owner had delivered them to him for the purpose of collecting their amounts and keeping them safe on the condition that they remain under the control of the owner, or if he had delivered them to him and specifically designated them for the satisfaction of a particular debt.
