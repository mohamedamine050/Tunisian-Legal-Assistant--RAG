The delegated judge, upon the request of the bailiff, may exempt the bailiff from placing seals on the following objects or may authorize the removal of seals from them:

1. Movable property and essential belongings of the debtor or his family, based on the list submitted to him.

2. Objects that are in danger of deterioration or are subject to a definite decrease in their value.

3. Objects suitable for the exercise of an activity if a permit to continue practicing it has been issued.

The bailiff shall immediately record the objects mentioned in this chapter, assess their value in the presence of the delegated judge or his deputy, and affix his signature to the inventory record.
