The Chamber of Counsel, upon a request from the President of the First Instance Court, or from the supervising judge, pursuant to the provisions of Article 436 above, may authorize, at any time, the transfer of the enterprise to a third party in accordance with the procedures provided for in the first paragraph of Section 4 of this Chapter, even without the passage of the supervision period if it is clear that this is the only solution to save the enterprise, or by bankruptcy if its conditions are met, or by the suspension of judicial settlement proceedings if the enterprise is no longer insolvent.
