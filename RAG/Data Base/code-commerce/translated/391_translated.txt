The holder of the check may claim from the party against whom the action for recourse is brought the following:

(1) The amount of the check not paid; (2) the interest due thereon, commencing from the date of presentation, calculated at the legal rate applicable to checks drawn on Tunisian territory and payable therein, and at a rate of 6% for other checks.

(3) Expenses of protest, notifications issued and other expenses.
