The objection shall include the text of the check verbatim, including any endorsements, and a notice of the payment of the value of the check, specifically a statement of the reasons for refusing payment and the inability to endorse or refusal to endorse, and the amount paid of the check in the case of partial payment.

The enforcing bailiffs are required to note the check with their signature on the objection, along with its date, failing which they shall be liable for damages.
