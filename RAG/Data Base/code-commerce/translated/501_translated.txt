## English Translation:

**The Court, in its bankruptcy judgment, shall appoint one or several trustees who shall act as judicial agents for both the debtor and the creditors.**

These trustees shall be subject to the provisions of Chapter 568 of the Code of Obligations and Contracts.

The number of trustees may be increased at any time, provided that it does not exceed three. The expenses and fees of the trustees shall be determined by the designated judge, in accordance with the standards set forth in the relevant legislation, with the trustee's fee not exceeding twenty percent (20%) of the total funds recovered in all cases. These fees shall be reduced by twenty percent (20%) annually.
