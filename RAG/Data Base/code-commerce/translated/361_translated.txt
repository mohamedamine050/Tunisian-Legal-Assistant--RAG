The renunciation must be unconditional and without any stipulation, and any stipulation attached to the renunciation shall be considered as if it did not exist.

Partial renunciation is void.

Renunciation in favor of the one who has been deprived of the right is also void.

Renunciation in favor of the holder is considered as a renunciation in blank.

Renunciation in favor of the one who has been deprived of the right is considered as a discharge, except in a case where the one who has been deprived of the right has several rights over the property and the renunciation was made for the benefit of a right other than the one the Sheikh had deprived him of.
