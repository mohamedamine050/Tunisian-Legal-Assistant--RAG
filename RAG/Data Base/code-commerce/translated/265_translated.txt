If an objection is filed against the distribution list ordered by the designated judge, the judge must submit a written report to the court on the objections within fifteen days following the expiration of the time limit granted to the parties to raise their objections. The judge shall refer the matter to the court and shall notify the parties at their chosen addresses by registered letters with acknowledgment of receipt. The clerk shall notify the parties of the date of the hearing at least eight days in advance.
