The execution supervisor shall distribute the proceeds of the sale of the enterprise to the creditors within one month of the expiry of the appeal period or the issuance of the Court of Appeal judgment in case of appeal, if there is no dispute. 

In the event of a dispute, the judicial supervisor shall prepare a report detailing the objections raised, and the distribution file shall be referred to the court competent for settlement. This court shall rule on the distribution and objections within one month, in the presence of the Public Prosecutor and after summoning all creditors.  In case of appeal, the Court of Appeal shall rule within one month from the date of the appeal.

The execution supervisor may advance payments to creditors who request it, as determined by the judicial supervisor. These advances represent a percentage of their debts and shall be paid from the proceeds of the secured sale.
