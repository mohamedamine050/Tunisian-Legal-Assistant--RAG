The drawer, upon payment of the amount due on the bill of exchange, has the right to request the holder to deliver it to him after marking it as "paid." 

The holder cannot refuse to accept partial payment.

In case of partial payment, the drawer has the right to request a marking on the bill of exchange indicating "paid" and to receive a receipt.

Any amount paid towards the principal amount of the bill of exchange shall release the drawer and endorsers from their liability.

The holder shall issue a protest for the remaining amount.
