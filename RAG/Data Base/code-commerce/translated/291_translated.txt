If the sight bill of exchange is payable on demand, it shall be due upon presentation and must be presented for payment within one year from its date. The drawer may shorten this period or stipulate a longer period. The endorsers may also shorten the aforementioned periods.

The drawer of a sight bill of exchange may stipulate that it shall not be presented for payment before a certain date. In this case, the period of presentation begins on the date of its maturity.
