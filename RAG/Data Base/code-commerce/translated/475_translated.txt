The court shall declare a merchant or individuals specified in the provisions of Article 416 of this Code bankrupt if it is established that they are in a state of cessation of payments and in a hopeless situation.

The individuals specified in the preceding paragraph who have ceased their activities or have passed away may be declared bankrupt within one year following the date of their cessation of activities or their death if their cessation of payment of their debts precedes these events.

A company may be declared bankrupt even if it is in the process of liquidation.
