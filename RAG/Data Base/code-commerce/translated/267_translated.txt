The judge assigned to the case shall, within eight days following the expiration of the appeal period or, if an appeal has been filed, within eight days following the date of issuance of the Court of Appeal's judgment, prepare a final list ranking the debts included in the original audit, including principal amounts, interest, and costs, in accordance with their priority. The procedure stipulated in Article 264, paragraph 5, shall then be applied. 

The deadline for lodging an appeal to the Court of Cassation commences from the date of issuance of the Court of Appeal's judgment.
