The schedules of distribution are delivered to the receivership from the Deposit and Trust Fund with respect to the amounts insured with it, and from the custodian with respect to the amounts that he is required to receive and pay to the creditors in accordance with their ranking in the order of their rights recognized to them in the creditors' list of ranking. 

Commercial instruments representing the portion of the price whose payment is deferred must be deposited with the custodian.

The custodian may discount or pay the commercial instruments deposited with him in order to discharge a debt.

He must also renew the registration of the seller's privilege before it lapses, with respect to the due portion of the price.
