It is not permissible to appoint as trustee in bankruptcy the debtor's spouse, any of their ascendants or descendants, relatives, or in-laws up to the fourth degree, or anyone who was their employee during the five years preceding the bankruptcy judgment, or any of their creditors. It is also not permissible to appoint the auditor of the bankrupt company as trustee in bankruptcy.

Furthermore, it is not permissible to appoint as trustee in bankruptcy any person who has any relationship whatsoever with the bankrupt company's agent, general manager, managing director, any member of its Board of Directors, any member of its collective management body, its sole general manager, any member of its Supervisory Board, or with the general partner or any partner.
