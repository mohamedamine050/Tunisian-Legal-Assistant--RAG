Creditors who have not submitted proof of their claims within fifteen days from the date of publication of the bankruptcy judgment in the Official Gazette of the Tunisian Republic shall be notified at the end of this period by means of publication in newspapers and by registered letter with acknowledgment of receipt from the bailiff. They shall be required to submit their claims with the relevant statement within fifteen days from the date of notification.

This period shall be extended by sixty days for creditors residing outside Tunisian territory and for tax debts, and for claims of the National Social Security Fund. The provisions of the preceding two paragraphs shall not apply to creditors who have previously declared their debts within the framework of the settlement procedures provided for in the first chapter of this book, unless the amount of their debts has changed by increase or decrease.
