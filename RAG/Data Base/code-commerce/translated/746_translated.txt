The discounting bank shall have against the original debtors on the discounted bills, the beneficiary of the discount, and any other obligors therein, all the rights arising from the discounted bills.

The discounting bank shall also have against the beneficiary of the discount an independent right to collect the sums placed at his disposal, together with the interest and commissions collected. It has the right to exercise this right to the value of the unpaid bills, whatever the reason for non-payment. If there is a current account between the parties, the discounting bank shall collect its right in accordance with the provisions of Articles 740 to 742.
