In the case outlined in the first paragraph of Article 682 aforementioned, if the total amount of executable transfer orders collectively exceeds the available balance in the account, the holders of those orders shall have the right to share the aforementioned amount in proportion to their respective debts.

This distribution shall only occur on the first business day following the day on which the partial balance is insufficient, unless the balance is replenished.

The provisions of Article 683 mentioned above, specifically those found in the second and third paragraphs, shall apply in this instance.
