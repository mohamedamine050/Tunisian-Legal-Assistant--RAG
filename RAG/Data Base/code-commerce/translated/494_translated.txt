The following acts performed by the debtor, commencing from the date set by the court for the suspension of debt payments, shall be deemed null and void:

First: Donations and gratuitous transfers, with the exception of customary trivial gifts.

Second: All payments of debts not yet due, in any manner whatsoever.

Third: All performances of obligations in kind or all payments of debts due in cash, with anything other than cash, bills of exchange, promissory notes, checks, bank drafts, or bank cards, or any other customary means of payment in the debtor's field of activity, taking into account the rights acquired in good faith by third parties.

Fourth: The establishment of a mortgage and the arrangement of a guarantee on the debtor's assets to secure a prior debt.
