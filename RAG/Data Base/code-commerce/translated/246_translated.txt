The Court hearing a claim for payment of a debt secured by a commercial asset, if it renders a judgment ordering payment, may authorize the sale of the commercial asset by its judgment, at the request of the creditor, and shall issue its judgment on the terms set out in Articles 5 and 6 of Chapter 243, specifying the time limit upon the expiration of which the sale proceedings may be continued in case of non-payment of the debt. The provisions of Article 8 of Chapter 243 and the provisions of Chapter 245 shall apply to the sale authorized by the Court in this case.
