The declaration aiming to obtain a judgment of bankruptcy must bear the signature of the partner or partners who hold the right to sign in the company, if it is a partnership or a limited partnership. It must also bear the signature of the agent or agents of the company if it is a company with limited liability, or the signature of the Chief Executive Officer or the General Manager of the company, or a member of the Board of Directors pursuant to a decision by the Board of Directors or the Chairman of the Collective Management Board or the sole General Manager if it is a company with a hidden name.
