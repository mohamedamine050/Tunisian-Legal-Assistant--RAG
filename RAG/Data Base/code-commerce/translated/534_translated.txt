Every creditor who has verified his claim or has been included in the register may, within ten days from the date of publication mentioned in the previous article, submit to the court clerk his objections, either personally or through an attorney.
An attorney is a person who acts on behalf of another.
He shall include his objections in the debt schedule.

The debtor also has the right to do so.

Upon the expiration of the aforementioned period, the designated judge shall definitively decide to close the debt schedule. The registrar shall ensure the execution of this decision by including in the schedule the debts that are not subject to any form of appeal, and shall indicate the creditor’s acceptance in the settlement and the amount of his approved claim. He shall draw up a minutes of the creditors’ statements and their observations.
