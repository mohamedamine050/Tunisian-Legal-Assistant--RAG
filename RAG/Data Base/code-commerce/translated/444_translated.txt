Upon appointment, the supervising judge shall contact the Committee for Monitoring Economic Institutions and any other relevant entity to request information about the debtor and the possibility of rescuing the institution.

The judicial administrator shall compile a list of creditors under the supervision of the supervising judge.

Creditors may be grouped into different categories based on their interests. Each category of creditors shall appoint a representative to raise their observations to the supervising judge. Each creditor or representative of the creditors may access the settlement proceedings and all documents filed with the court in writing (amended by Law No. 47 of 2019 dated May 29, 2019).

The supervising judge shall submit a report to the court on all disputes that may arise during the judicial settlement and all information that he deems useful to present to it.
