An appeal may be lodged against judgments rendered in matters of judicial settlement by the debtor, the creditors, the assignee, the lessee, or the Public Prosecutor, within a period of twenty days from the date of their publication in the Official Gazette of the Republic of Tunisia. Third parties may also object to these judgments within the same period.

An appeal may be lodged against judgments rendered in matters of bankruptcy by the debtor, the creditors, or the Public Prosecutor, within a period of twenty days from the date of the pronouncement of the judgment or from the date of publication of its content in the Official Gazette of the Republic of Tunisia, if it is subject to publication requirements.
