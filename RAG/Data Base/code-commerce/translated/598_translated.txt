## Proof of Commercial Contracts:

1) By an official document 

2) By handwritten documents 

3) By a list delivered or a record established by the exchange broker or securities broker, signed by the contracting parties as required by law

4) By a goods list coupled with acceptance

5) By correspondence

6) By the contracting parties' ledgers

7) By witness testimony and circumstantial evidence, if the Court deems their admission necessary.

All of the above are subject to exceptions provided by law.
