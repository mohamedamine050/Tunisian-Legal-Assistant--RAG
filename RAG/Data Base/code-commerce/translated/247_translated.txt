If the successful bidder fails to fulfill the conditions required of him under the final bid, the commercial asset shall be re-sold by nullifying the first auction, in accordance with the provisions of Article 245.

The seller in the re-sale shall be liable to the original seller's creditor and to the original seller for the difference between the price the original bidder had offered and the price obtained from the re-sale. He shall not be entitled to claim any amount by which the price obtained in the re-sale may exceed the original bid price.
