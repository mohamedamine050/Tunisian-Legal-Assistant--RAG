The enforcement officer shall collect the funds arising from rent or from free agency and distribute them to the creditors within fifteen days, respecting the payment deadlines. In the event of remaining funds or a legal dispute between parties, the provisions of Article 573 of this Code shall apply.

Periodic rent payments shall be distributed to the creditors in accordance with their ranking, after deducting expenses, by adopting a full-term rent payment as determined by the court as the basis for calculation. The total amount shall be divided among the creditors according to their ranking, and then divided by the number of rent installments.
