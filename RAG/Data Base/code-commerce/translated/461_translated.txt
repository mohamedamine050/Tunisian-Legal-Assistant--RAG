The Court may, pursuant to a written judgment, order the transfer of the institution to another party if its activity cannot be continued in accordance with the foregoing provisions and if this transfer ensures the continuation of its activity and the preservation of all of its employees and the settlement of its debts.

The Court shall determine the ongoing contracts with the institution that are necessary for the continuation of its activity based on the request of the bidders.

If the matter relates to the transfer of an institution that exploits public agricultural land or any other institution that requires administrative licenses for its activity, the legislation and regulations in force regarding the required administrative licenses must be respected.
