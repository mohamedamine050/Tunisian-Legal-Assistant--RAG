The holder may demand from the drawee who has refused payment:

-1. The amount of the bill of exchange which has not been accepted or paid, together with interest, if stipulated;

-2. Interest at the legal rate, from the date of maturity;

-3. Costs of protest, notices, and other expenses.

If the action for recourse is instituted before the maturity date, an amount representing a discount will be deducted from the amount of the bill of exchange. The discount shall be calculated at the official discount rate of the Central Bank of Tunisia in effect on the date of the institution of the action at the place where the holder is domiciled.
