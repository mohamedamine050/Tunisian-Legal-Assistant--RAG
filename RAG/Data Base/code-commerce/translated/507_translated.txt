It is required to appoint one or more observers from among the creditors who have applied for this role, by a decision of the appointed judge. If none of the creditors have applied, the appointed judge shall appoint one of them.

The creditors shall appoint a representative or representatives from among them if their number exceeds ten, within ten days from the date of notification by the appointed judge in bankruptcy. If the appointment is not made, the appointed judge shall make it himself. The person appointed by the group to act on its behalf shall be considered as the observer. The person appointed by the group to act on its behalf shall be responsible for verifying the payroll and shall raise the creditors' observations to the appointed judge.
