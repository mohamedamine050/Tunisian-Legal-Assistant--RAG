**Brokerage is a contract.**

**Contract**

A contract is a legally binding agreement that obligates the contracting parties and defines their obligations and rights with respect to a specific transaction. 

In a brokerage contract, one party, referred to as the broker, undertakes to search for a person to establish a connection between them and another person for the purpose of entering into an agreement.
