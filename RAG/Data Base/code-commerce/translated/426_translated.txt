The President of the Court, regardless of all legal provisions, may request any information on the status of the institution from any public or financial administration or institution or from the Committee for Monitoring Economic Institutions, which is a formal method of gathering individuals with relevant expertise from various parts of the institution. 

He may also request the Committee for Monitoring Economic Institutions to conduct a diagnosis and study within a period not exceeding one month of its mandate. The Committee, regardless of all legal provisions, may request information on the status of the institution from any public or financial administration or institution.

The President of the Court shall immediately forward the information and diagnosis and study to the authorities.
