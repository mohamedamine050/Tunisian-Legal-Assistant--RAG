**On Natural or Legal Persons Obliged to Keep Accounts:**

(1) They shall record daily in a daily register all the transactions they carry out, or they shall record them monthly in aggregate, according to the final registers, subject to the same conditions governing subsidiary registers, provided that these are based on the results of these transactions if the daily register is not kept as intended.

(2) They shall prepare at least once a year an inventory of their assets and liabilities in their business, and shall transfer the details of this inventory to the inventory register.

(3) They shall preserve for a period of ten years all documents proving the transactions recorded in the registers referred to.
