## Payment Orders May Be Made Payable to:

(1) A named person, with an express condition indicated by the word "to order" or without it.

(2) A named person, with a condition indicated by the words "not to order" or any other wording conveying this meaning.

(3) The bearer.

A check drawn in favor of a named person and specifying "or bearer" or wording to that effect, is considered a check payable to the bearer.

A check not mentioning the name of the beneficiary is considered a check payable to the bearer.
