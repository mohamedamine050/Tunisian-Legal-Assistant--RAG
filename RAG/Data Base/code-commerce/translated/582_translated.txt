Following the sale of real estate and the final settlement procedures between the preferred creditors or mortgagees of the property, it shall not be permissible for any of them who is entitled according to their ranking to recover the entirety of their debt from the proceeds of the mortgaged property to collect their share in the distribution of the proceeds until they have deducted any amounts they have already collected in conjunction with the ordinary creditors. However, the amounts deducted in this manner shall not remain with the mortgagees of the property but shall revert to the ordinary creditors.
