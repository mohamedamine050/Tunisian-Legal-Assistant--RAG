Perishable goods or those facing a definite decline in value, or those requiring substantial expenses for preservation, shall be sold at the initiative of the trustee, upon obtaining authorization from the delegated judge.

The Court shall not authorize the continuation of the debtor's activity at the request of the trustee, except upon a report from the delegated judge, and if the public interest or the interest of the creditors necessitates it, for a period of three months, extendable once for the same period exceptionally.
