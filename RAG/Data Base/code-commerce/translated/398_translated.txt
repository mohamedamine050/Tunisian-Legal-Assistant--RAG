Actions for recourse brought by the holder against the drawer and the endorser shall lapse after six months from the date of the expiry of the presentation period. As for actions for recourse brought by different obligors to pay the check against each other, they shall lapse after six months from the date on which the obligor paid the amount of the check or from the date on which he brought the action.

Actions for recourse brought by the holder of the check against the drawee shall lapse after three years from the date of the expiry of the presentation period. However, in the event of the right lapsing or the passage of time, the right to bring an action against the drawer who failed to provide sufficient funds or against other obligors who have been unjustly enriched shall remain.
