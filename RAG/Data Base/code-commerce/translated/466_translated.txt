The court may decide by means of a written judgment, preparatory ruling, or decision resolving a legal or factual issue, to lease the establishment, transfer it to a third party within the framework of a free agency, or lease it with a transfer if it becomes impossible to continue its activity in accordance with the aforementioned provisions, and if such action would ensure the continuity of its activity and preserve all jobs within it, or some of them, and settle its debts. 

The court shall decide to lease the establishment, to lease it with a transfer, or to transfer it within the framework of a free agency to the person submitting the best offer, in accordance with the provisions of Article 460 of this Code.
