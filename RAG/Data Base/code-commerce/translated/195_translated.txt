The public officer entrusted with conducting the sale shall not accept bids from individuals who have not deposited with the Deposit and Consignment Fund an amount not less than half the purchase price agreed upon by the contracting parties, except if the purchase price exceeds or falls short of the price determined in the initial sale, or the portion of the purchase price agreed upon by the contracting parties, except if the purchase price exceeds or falls short of the price determined in the initial sale, which was stipulated to be paid in advance with a one-sixth increase, and allocated specifically for the payment of the sale price.
