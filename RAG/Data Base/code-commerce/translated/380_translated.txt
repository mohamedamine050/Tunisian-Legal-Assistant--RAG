If the requested party refuses to pay after being requested in the manner set forth in the preceding section, the owner of the lost or stolen animal, in order to preserve all his rights, must prove this by a written complaint filed, at the latest, on the first working day following the expiration of the deadline for the presentation. As for the notifications mentioned in Article 388, they must be addressed to the keeper and the persons who have seen it within the deadlines specified in the aforementioned article.
