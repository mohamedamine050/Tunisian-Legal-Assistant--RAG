The check contains the following information:

1) The word “check” is included in the text of the instrument itself, in the language in which it was written.

2) An absolute, unconditional mandate to pay a specific amount.

3) The name of the person obligated to pay (the drawee).

4) Designation of the place of payment.

5) Designation of the date and place of creation of the check.

6) Signature of the issuer of the check (the drawer).
