It is stipulated by virtue of 
Agreement


The agreement is a binding legal contract that obligates the contracting parties and defines their obligations and rights with respect to the specific transaction. 

The deposit referred to above is a bank account in which the bank records all transactions it undertakes with the depositor himself or with others on his behalf. This account is divided into two sections, one for his credit and the other for his debit with the bank.

Transactions that the parties agree to keep outside the account shall not be included.
