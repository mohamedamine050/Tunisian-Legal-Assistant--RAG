The deliberations of the Municipal Council shall be recorded in the register of deliberations in chronological order, and shall be signed by the attending members of the Council, or the reasons for non-signature shall be stated. The content of the minutes of the session shall be posted at the entrance of the municipality's headquarters and its departments, if applicable, for a period of two months, within a maximum period of eight days from the date of its holding. It shall also be published on the municipality's dedicated website.
