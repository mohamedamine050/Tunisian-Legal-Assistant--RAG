The management of all local public utilities shall be governed by the following principles and rules: 

* **Equality** among its users and contractors,
* **Continuity** of services,
* **Adaptability**,
* **Sustainable development**,
* **Transparency**,
* **Accountability**,
* **Impartiality**,
* **Integrity**,
* **Efficiency** and safeguarding public funds,
* **Open governance**.
