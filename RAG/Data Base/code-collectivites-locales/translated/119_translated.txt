The local community shall mandatorily adopt participatory approaches in preparing development plans, in accordance with the provisions of the law and the mechanisms established by its elected councils for the effective engagement of residents and civil society organizations. This includes inviting them to contribute to the formulation of concepts, the definition of major development choices, the derivation of practical methods for preparing development plans, and the monitoring of their implementation. Local communities are committed to respecting the principles of sustainable development in the preparation of development plans.
