## English Translation:

Municipalities shall prepare model plans, as provided for in the legislation governing planning and development, which fall within their purview and are approved by their elected councils. When preparing and implementing these plans, the municipality shall take into account the urban aesthetics and architectural character of the area. Neighboring municipalities may prepare a joint urban planning plan encompassing the territories of these municipalities, subject to the approval of their respective elected councils. Coordination shall be undertaken between the central authority and municipalities, as well as between municipalities themselves, when carrying out the actions outlined in the first and third paragraphs of this chapter.
