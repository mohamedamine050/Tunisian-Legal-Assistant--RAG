The Municipal Council shall develop urban planning examples through the adoption of participatory democracy mechanisms. It shall ensure respect for the rules governing territorial planning and the urban planning documents relating to the protection of archaeological and historical heritage, and all other rules stipulated by the legislation and regulations in force.

The Municipal Council shall approve any urban development operations that it deems necessary relating to planning, beautification, or urban renewal, either independently or in partnership, in coordination with the central or regional ministry responsible for urban planning.

The Municipal Council shall prepare local building regulations and regulations concerning the preservation of the architectural characteristics of the municipality. These regulations shall be in the form of administrative decisions of a general nature and shall not be directed at any specific individual.
