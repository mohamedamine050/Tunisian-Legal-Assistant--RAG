## English Translation of the Arabic Legal Text:

Crimes described as **felonies** within the meaning of this law are those punishable by death or imprisonment exceeding five years. Crimes described as **misdemeanors** are those punishable by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars. Crimes described as **offenses** are those punishable by imprisonment not exceeding fifteen days or a fine not exceeding sixty dinars. [122 Code of Criminal Procedure]
