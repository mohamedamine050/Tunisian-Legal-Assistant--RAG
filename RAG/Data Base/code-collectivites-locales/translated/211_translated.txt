The Municipal Council shall appoint the Chairpersons and Secretaries of the Committees based on the principle of proportional representation. In the event of the resignation or absence of a Chairperson or Secretary of a Committee, the President of the Municipal Council shall appoint a suitable person from among those possessing the necessary qualifications and competencies to fill the vacancy, subject to the official procedures for gathering individuals with relevant expertise from various parts of the institution. The President shall submit the vacancy to the Municipal Council in its first meeting. The Municipal Council shall fill the vacancy in the Committees.
