The Committee undertakes to study economic, social, employment, and environmental issues on its own initiative or at the request of one of the heads of the local communities or the governor responsible for the territory. The Committee periodically undertakes to study the employment situation in the region and submits reports to the Regional Council. The Committee also undertakes to study the circumstances of the completion of economic and social projects at the request of the parties concerned and recommends measures to ensure their completion in the best conditions. The Committee appoints one of its members as rapporteur and submits reports on its activities and proposals to the Regional Council, which may authorize their publication on the region's website. The Committee's working documents are kept at the headquarters of the region.
