The candidate files are opened by a committee, officially constituted to bring together individuals with relevant experience from various parts of the institution. This committee is composed of:

* A Chair appointed by the President of the relevant local community or his/her representative;
* Two members of the community council appointed by the local community council;
* Two technical experts appointed by the local community office.

The General Secretary or Executive Director of the local community, or their representative, acts as the committee's secretary. The community accountant attends the committee's sessions and has an advisory role.
