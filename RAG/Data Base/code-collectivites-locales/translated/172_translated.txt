The President of the Local Community shall present the draft budget to the Community Council for discussion and approval at a meeting held before December 1st.  In the event that the President of the Local Community does not present the draft budget to the Community Council within the time limit stipulated in the first paragraph of this chapter, the Council shall meet within three days upon the request of one-third of its members. If the Council fails to approve the budget, the Governor with territorial jurisdiction shall issue a warning to the Council to approve the budget no later than December 15th.
