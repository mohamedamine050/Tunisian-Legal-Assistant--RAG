**English Translation:**

By virtue of a joint decision of the municipal councils, inter-municipal cooperation institutions are legally established as a local community with legal personality and administrative and financial autonomy. They are entrusted with managing municipal affairs in accordance with the principle of free management. They strive to promote economic, social, cultural, environmental, and urban development of the region, provide services to their constituents, address their concerns, and involve them in the management of local affairs. It is agreed that these actions will be carried out within a specified timeframe.
