## English Translation: 

Local communities are obligated to uphold their commitments to foreign parties and diligently protect the sovereignty and reputation of the Tunisian Republic. 

**Sovereignty** refers to the supreme and independent authority of the ruling body within its own territory, free from external interference. 

All individuals and parties involved in partnership and cooperation relationships are obligated to refrain from any actions that could damage the reputation or dignity of the Tunisian Republic.
