The President of the local community shall ensure that the administration of the local community prepares a preliminary document in preparation for the budget draft, based on available statistics, data, and initial estimates of resources to be transferred by the Ministry of Finance after consultation with its relevant departments. The central authority shall inform local communities before June 30th of the preliminary estimates of the appropriations to be transferred for the following fiscal year, and shall inform them before September 10th of the final appropriations allocated to them in accordance with the provisions of the first paragraph of this chapter.
