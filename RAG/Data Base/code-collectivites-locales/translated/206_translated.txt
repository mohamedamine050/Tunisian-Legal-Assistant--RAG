## English Translation:

Any member of the Municipal Council who, without a legitimate excuse, fails to perform the duties entrusted to him by law shall be notified in writing by the Mayor to fulfill his obligations. If he fails to respond, the Municipal Council may decide to relieve him of his duties by a majority of three-fifths of its members, after hearing him. The person concerned may appeal to the competent **Administrative Court** having territorial jurisdiction. 

Any member of the Municipal Council who loses his status as an elector or who fulfills the conditions of incompatibility under the provisions of the electoral law shall lose his membership in the Council by virtue of law. The Municipal Council shall declare this during its first meeting.
