The resignation of the Head of the Region or the resignation of his assistants shall be submitted to the members of the Regional Council, who shall convene compulsorily within a period not exceeding fifteen days to discuss the matter. If the resignation is accepted or the resignees refuse to withdraw it, the Governor and the Regional Finance Officer with territorial jurisdiction shall be notified of the vacancy. The resigning officials shall continue to perform their duties until the date of the installation of their successors. In the event that the resigning President is confirmed or refuses to continue managing the regional affairs, the oldest member of the Regional Council shall undertake the management of the regional affairs with the assistance of the Executive Director.
