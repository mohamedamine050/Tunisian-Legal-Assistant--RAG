The Higher Council of Local Communities shall be vested with the following powers:

- To consider matters relating to development and balance between regions.
- To ensure the coordination between public policies, plans, programs, and local and national projects.
- To coordinate with constitutional bodies and relevant ministries regarding local affairs and decentralized international cooperation.
- To study ways of cooperation and coordination between various local communities.
- To follow up on training programs for local elected officials and employees of local communities.

The Council may submit proposals in this regard to public authorities.
