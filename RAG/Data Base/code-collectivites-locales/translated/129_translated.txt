## English Translation:

The local community accountant is a public accountant under the jurisdiction of the state and holds the position of a principal accountant. They are exclusively responsible for keeping the accounts of the local communities. Their appointment is made by a decision of the Minister of Finance after prior notification to the head of the concerned local community.

The local community accountant, under their responsibility, undertakes all necessary measures to collect the amounts and entitlements due to the local community. The public accountant assists the local community in preparing the budget, examining the revenue, costs, and circumstances of a given project within a specific timeframe, managing funds, following up on debts, executing expenses in accordance with the law, calculating the impact of proposed actions, and ensuring the financial protection of the local community and its assets.
