The Mayor, his Assistants, Deputies, and Council Members designated by him shall have the status of Civil Status Officers. They shall be known by their signatures and shall attest to the conformity of copies with the original in accordance with the applicable legislation. The Mayor shall delegate to the officials the authority to identify signatures, attest to the conformity of copies with the original, and sign Civil Status documents.

The material aspect of civilization, such as infrastructure, communication, and entertainment, is matched by the intellectual, spiritual, and moral aspects of civilization. This applies except for the conclusion of marriage contracts.
