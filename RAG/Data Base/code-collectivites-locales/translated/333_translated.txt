The Head of the Entity may, by decision, delegate the right to sign under their supervision and responsibility, and within the scope of their powers, to:

* The Executive Director of the Entity;
* Officials holding a specific job position within the Entity.

Delegation of the right to sign regulatory decisions is not permitted.  Similarly, delegation to any of the individuals mentioned in this section is prohibited if they have a direct or indirect interest in the decision-making process.  However, the person acting as Head of the Entity may sign regulatory decisions.
