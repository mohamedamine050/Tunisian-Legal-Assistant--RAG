Credits may be transferred from one section to another within the first title and within the second title, and from one department to another within each section. The transfer of credits referred to shall be proposed with justification by the head of the local community or by the committee in charge of financial and economic affairs and the monitoring of operations, and shall be presented by the head of the local community to the Council for approval, attached with the regional treasurer's comments. However, credits cannot be transferred for expenses paid from transferred credits allocated to a specific project or funded by earmarked resources, except with the approval of the administration that transferred the credits.
