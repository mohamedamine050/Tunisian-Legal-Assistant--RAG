The Municipal Council exercises the powers that may be delegated to it by the central authority, particularly in the following areas: 

* The construction, maintenance, and repair of health institutions and centers. 
* The construction, maintenance, and repair of educational institutions.
* The construction, maintenance, and repair of cultural facilities.
* The construction, equipping, maintenance, and repair of sports facilities. 

Any transfer of powers is obligatorily accompanied by the transfer of the necessary financial and human resources for their exercise.  

Projects and facilities are carried out within the framework of an agreement concluded between the central authority and the municipality. 

The specific characteristics of islands are taken into account when transferring powers from the central authority to the relevant local authorities.
