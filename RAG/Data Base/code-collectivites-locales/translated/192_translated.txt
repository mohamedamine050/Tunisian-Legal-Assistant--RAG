In addition to exercising his financial responsibilities, the accountant shall oversee the accounting of materials by the head of the local community under his responsibility, for the purpose of recording the gains of the local community and its movable and immovable property. The head of the local community, upon the proposal of the accountant, shall appoint a person from among the public officials or from among members of the accounting profession, in accordance with the rules of competition, to conduct an annual general inventory of such property. The local community shall bear the cost of hiring the latter.
