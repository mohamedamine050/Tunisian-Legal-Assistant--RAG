## English Translation:

The regulatory decisions of local communities shall be published in the Official Gazette of Local Communities in Arabic. They shall also be published in one or more other languages for informational purposes. The official printing press of the Tunisian Republic shall undertake the electronic publication of the regulatory decisions of local communities in the Official Gazette of Local Communities free of charge, within a period not exceeding five working days from the date of receiving a copy of these decisions by any secure means. At the request of the local community, the decisions shall be published in a printed version, with the concerned community bearing the publication costs.
