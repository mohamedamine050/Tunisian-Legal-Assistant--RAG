The draft budget of the local community, accompanied by a comprehensive explanatory document, shall be submitted to the Regional Treasurer competent for the territory by October 15th. The Regional Treasurer shall express their opinion on the draft budget within a period not exceeding one month from the date of submission of the draft budget. The same documents shall also be sent to the members of the local community council at least 15 days before the session for discussion and approval of the budget.
