A member of the Regional Council may submit their resignation to the Head of the Region, who shall present it to the Regional Council at its first meeting for review. The competent Governor shall be notified accordingly. 

Except in cases of filling vacancies in accordance with the electoral law, the Regional Council shall be dissolved by the collective resignation or the simultaneous resignation of a majority of its members, which shall be submitted to the competent Governor. 

The Regional Council shall be considered dissolved 15 days from the date of notification to the Governor. The Governor shall notify both the Independent High Electoral Commission and the Higher Council of Local Authorities of all cases of vacancy and dissolution.
