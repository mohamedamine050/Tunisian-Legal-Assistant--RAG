## English Translation:

Summons for **public sessions** held by competent bodies, in accordance with the procedural rules set out in this guide, are issued with the objective of obtaining specific information regarding the activities or operations undertaken by the public administration. This is to ensure the integrity, transparency, and impartiality of the performance, and its consistency with the requirements of the public interest.

**Municipal Council**  shall attach explanatory notes to matters to be discussed during the session.  Draft contracts or other agreements shall be submitted to **Municipal Council** members **no less than five days prior to the session**.  Each member of the **Municipal Council**, in the exercise of his duties, has the right to access all documents and data relating to municipal matters that are the **subject matter** of the discussion.
