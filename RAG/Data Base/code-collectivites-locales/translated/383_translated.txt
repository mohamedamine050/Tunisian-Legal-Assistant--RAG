The provisions of this Organic Law relating to each category of local communities shall enter into force gradually after the announcement of the final results of the elections for each category. The provisions relating to the preparation of the budget and its approval shall not enter into force until the 1st of January of the year following the announcement of the final results of the elections for each category of local communities. Until the entry into force of the Decentralization, Adjustment, and Solidarity Support Fund for Local Communities, the central authority shall, starting from the fiscal year following the year in which the elections were held, allocate an annual financial support to local communities equal to the amount of support granted to them for the year 2018, plus a general increase rate to be determined by the Finance Law.
