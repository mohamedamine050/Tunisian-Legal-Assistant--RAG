A place shall be designated for a representative of the Regional Council to attend public sessions held by civil society organizations, according to the procedural rules set forth in this guide, with the aim of obtaining specific information regarding the work or activities undertaken by the public administration, to ensure the integrity, transparency, and compliance of performance with the requirements of the public interest. 

The Municipal Council shall designate a place for civil society organizations and media outlets during its meetings, in the capacity of observers.
