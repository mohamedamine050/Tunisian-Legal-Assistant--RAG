Fees, charges, and rights for which the authorities are authorized to set amounts or definitions shall be regulated by deliberations published in the official gazette of local communities and shall be communicated to residents by all available means, particularly the following:

* Fees for the delivery of certificates and various documents.
* Fees for administrative permits.
* Fees for the exploitation of property and spaces belonging to the authority.
* Fees for various services or exploitations for a fee.
* Fees for accepting or processing various waste products from polluting production units.
* Annual fees for authorization to engage in hazardous economic activities or those with an unusual negative impact on the environment, as regulated by the current legislation. 
* All other fees.
