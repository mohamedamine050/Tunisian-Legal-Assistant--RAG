The resignation of the Mayor or his assistants shall be submitted to the members of the Municipal Council, which shall convene obligatorily within a period not exceeding fifteen days to deliberate on it. If the resignation is accepted, or if the resigning parties refuse to withdraw their resignation, the Governor and the Regional Treasurer competent in the territory shall be notified of the vacancy. The resigning officials shall continue to carry out their duties until the date of the installation of their successors. In the event that the resigning Mayor is confirmed or refuses to continue the management of municipal affairs, the oldest member of the Municipal Council shall undertake the management of the municipality's affairs with the assistance of the Secretary General.
