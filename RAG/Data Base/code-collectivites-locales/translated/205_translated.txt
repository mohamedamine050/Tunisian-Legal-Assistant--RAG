A member of the municipal council may submit their resignation to the mayor, who shall present it to the municipal council at its first meeting for consideration. The competent governor shall be notified thereof.  Except in cases of filling the vacancy pursuant to the electoral law, the municipal council shall be dissolved by collective resignation or by the simultaneous resignation of a majority of its members, which shall be addressed to the competent governor. The municipal council shall be considered dissolved after fifteen days from the date of notification to the governor. The governor shall inform both the Independent High Authority for Elections and the Supreme Council of Local Authorities of all cases of vacancy and dissolution.
