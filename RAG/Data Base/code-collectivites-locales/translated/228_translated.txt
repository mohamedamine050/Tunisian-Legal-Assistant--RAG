The Mayor of the Municipality is a Civil Status Officer within the limits of his jurisdiction. The Mayor and his assistants exercise their duties as Civil Status Officers within the entire territory of the municipality.  The Mayor may delegate the authority or responsibility to another person to carry out certain activities.  By decision of the Mayor, with his signature on documents related to matters concerning the municipality, the delegation does not apply to decisions of an organizational nature or in areas where delegation is not legally permissible. The Mayor may authorize, by decision of the Mayor with his signature,  the officers reporting to him within the municipality of classes "A" and "B", in the areas to which the delegation applies, to delegate the authority or responsibility to another person to carry out certain activities.
