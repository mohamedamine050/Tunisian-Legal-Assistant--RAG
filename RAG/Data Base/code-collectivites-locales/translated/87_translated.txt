The following persons are ineligible to be candidates for public service concession contracts:

* Any person who has been sentenced by a written judgment of a court to a prison term exceeding six months, subject to execution, with the exception of persons convicted of involuntary offenses that do not involve fleeing, if the sentence does not exceed five years or a fine exceeding sixty dinars.
* Any person subject to a judicial investigation for bankruptcy or any person involved in procedures for rescuing businesses experiencing economic difficulties.
* Any person who does not provide evidence of the settlement of their tax situation with the state and local communities.
* All heads, members, and assistants of local councils. 
* The accountant of the local community.
* All cases of conflict of interest, in accordance with current legislation.
