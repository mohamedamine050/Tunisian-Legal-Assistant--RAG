The following expenditures shall be mandatory for local communities:

* **Rental expenses**, including amounts deducted for taxes and social contributions.
* **Repayment of principal and interest on loans** originally required.
* **Payment of due receivables**.
* **Expenses for cleaning, maintenance and upkeep of roads, pavements, public lighting networks, sanitation and drainage channels, and green spaces**.
* **Expenses for preservation of contracts, documents, and archives belonging to the local community**.
* **Expenses for the maintenance of the local community headquarters and its various buildings and facilities**.
* **All expenses incurred by the local community and related to its area of competence, in accordance with applicable legislation**.
