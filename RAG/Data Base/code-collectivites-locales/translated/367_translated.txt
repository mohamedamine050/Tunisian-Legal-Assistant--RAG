In the event of the President of the Region's absence, suspension from duty, removal from office, or any other impediment resulting in temporary vacancy, the President shall be replaced in all his functions by the Assistant, in the order of their designation. Should it be impossible to replace the President with the Assistant, he shall be replaced by a member of the Regional Council elected by the Council by a majority of the members present.

A temporary vacancy shall be deemed to have occurred in the event of the President of the Region's absence for health reasons or travel for a period exceeding one month, or suspension from the performance of his duties. The temporary replacement shall not exceed a period of six months. Upon the expiration of the aforementioned period, the provisions of Article 364 of this law shall apply.
