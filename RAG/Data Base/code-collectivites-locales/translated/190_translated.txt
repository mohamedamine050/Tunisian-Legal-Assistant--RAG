## English Translation:

The local community accountant shall maintain the accounts in accordance with the same rules stipulated for the maintenance of state accounts and the accounting system of local communities, except for what is exempted by special provisions. The accounting must clearly, comprehensively, and truthfully reflect the true financial position of the local community, its assets, and its liabilities, in a manner that facilitates the monitoring and verification of the cost of services and projects, controlling them, calculating consumables gradually, evaluating the extent to which objectives and feasibility are achieved, streamlining the management of the local community's finances, and ensuring their sound use.

The accounts of agencies shall be maintained in accordance with the accounting system for economic institutions. A single bank account shall be opened for each agency, for its exclusive use and no other purpose. Checks and transfers shall be signed by both the agency manager and the head of its financial department.
