## English Translation:

**Offers for the delegation of public services are subject to a call for tenders, which must be published on the municipality's dedicated website, in at least two daily newspapers, and posted at the municipality's headquarters. The call for tenders must include the appropriate deadline for the competition, taking into account the importance of the contract.**

**The announcement for the call for tenders shall include the following:**

* **The subject of the contract.**
* **The location where the specifications can be accessed and collected.**
* **The deadline, date, and time for submitting bids.**
* **The location, date, and time of the bid opening session.**
* **The period for which the candidates remain bound by their bids.**
* **The supporting documents to be provided regarding the technical specifications and required financial guarantees.**

**The principles set out in Article 75 of this law shall be taken into account.**
