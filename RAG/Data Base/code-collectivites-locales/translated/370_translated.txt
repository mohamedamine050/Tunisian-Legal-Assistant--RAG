The Regional Head may delegate, through a decision bearing his signature under his supervision and responsibility, and within the scope of his duties, the authority or responsibility for the implementation of specific activities to:

* The Executive Director of the Region,
* The staff members holding a specific position within the Region. 

The right to sign regulatory decisions cannot be delegated. Moreover, delegation cannot be made to any of the individuals mentioned in this section if they have a direct or indirect interest in the decision-making process.
