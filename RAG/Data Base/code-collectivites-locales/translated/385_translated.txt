Within a maximum period of nine months from the date of publication of this law, and until the establishment of the  **High Administrative Court**  and the  **Supreme Council of Local Authorities** and the  **High Council of Local Finance**, government orders stipulated by this law shall be issued based on the opinion of the **High Administrative Court**. These orders shall remain in effect until the issuance of government orders replacing them, in accordance with the provisions of this law.
