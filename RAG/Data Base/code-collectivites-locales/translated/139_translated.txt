The elected councils of local communities are vested with the authority to set the amounts or tariffs of various duties, fees, rights, and participation in the costs of works, irrespective of their designation, that are levied under the heading of exploitation, utilization, service, obtaining a benefit, or a license, and which do not bear the character of payments and contributions as stipulated in Article 65 of the Constitution. The council of the local community shall determine the circumstances for exemption or reduction from various duties, fees, rights, and participation in the costs of works.
