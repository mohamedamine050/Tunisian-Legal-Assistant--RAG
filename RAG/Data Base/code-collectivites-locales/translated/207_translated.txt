A temporary management committee shall be appointed by an official method to gather individuals with relevant expertise from various parts of the institution. The committee's president shall be appointed by a government order upon the proposal of the Minister in charge of local communities, after consulting the president of the Higher Council of Local Communities in the following cases:

* Dissolution of the municipal council or its dissolution,
* Complete annulment of the results of the municipal council elections,
* Creation of a new municipality,
* Merger of municipalities.
