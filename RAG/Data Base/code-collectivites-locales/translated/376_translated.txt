The Regional Administration is committed to serving all residents within the framework of applying the law in accordance with the principles of neutrality, equality, integrity, transparency, accountability, continuity of public service, and efficiency. It endeavors to protect the interests of the region and its properties. The regional authorities are committed to applying the law in accordance with the goals of the public interest and to assisting in the completion of projects and the provision of services within their deadlines. Any unjustified delay in the provision of services constitutes an error that may rise to the level of serious misconduct, subject to legal accountability.
