The provisions of Articles 46 to 95 of the Local Taxation Code issued pursuant to Law No. 11 of 1997 dated February 3, 1997, and their implementing regulations shall cease to be effective upon the entry into force of decisions of each local municipality relating to the fixing of taxes, fees, and rights, whatever their designation, as provided for in these Articles.  Exceptionally, by government orders, taken upon the opinion of the Higher Council of Local Finance, for a maximum period of five years from the date of entry into force of the provisions relating to the budget, the following shall be fixed: - The license tax levied on beverage outlets. - The tax for signature authentication. - The tax for certifying the conformity of copies with the original. - The delivery of certificates and various documents.
