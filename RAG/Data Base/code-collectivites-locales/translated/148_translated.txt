The resources of the Decentralization, Amendment, and Solidarity Fund between Local Communities are derived from:

- **Allocations in the financial laws**,
- **Allocation of a percentage of tax revenue**,
- **If necessary, the law shall allocate a percentage of the state's revenue derived from the exploitation of natural resources, in accordance with Article 136 of the Constitution**.

All resources allocated to this fund shall be distributed among the categories of local communities as follows:

- **70% of the funds for municipalities**,
- **20% of the funds for regions**,
- **10% of the funds for provinces.**
