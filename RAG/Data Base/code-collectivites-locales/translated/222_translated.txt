The internal regulations of the Municipal Council shall set forth the conditions and procedures for raising oral questions concerning municipal affairs or organizing public sessions held by civil society organizations, in accordance with the procedural rules set forth in this guide, with the aim of obtaining specific information regarding the works or activities undertaken by the public administration, in order to ensure the integrity, transparency, and compliance of performance with the requirements of the public interest. This includes listening to the President of the Council or one of his assistants, the heads of departments, and the chairs of committees. The internal regulations also establish the procedures for the annual sessions dedicated by the Council to discussing committee reports.
