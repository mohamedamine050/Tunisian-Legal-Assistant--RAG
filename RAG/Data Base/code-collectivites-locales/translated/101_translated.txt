This text defines a contract, a partnership, and obligations. Here is a translation:

**Contract** 

A contract is a legally binding agreement that obligates the contracting parties and defines their obligations and rights in relation to the specific transaction.

**Partnership**

A partnership is a legal entity formed by two or more individuals or entities who agree to share in the profits and losses of a business venture. 

**Obligations**

Obligations are commitments or undertakings that an entity has towards third parties in exchange for receiving goods, services, or loans from them.
