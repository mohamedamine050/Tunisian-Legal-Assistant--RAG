Until the enactment of the law organizing the Court of Audit and its assumption of its duties, the Audit Department shall assume the powers and duties assigned to the Court of Audit by this Basic Law. Appeals against initial judgments issued by the Audit Department shall be filed before the Plenary Session provided for in Article 40 of Law No. 8 of 1968 dated March 8, 1968, relating to the organization of the Audit Department.
