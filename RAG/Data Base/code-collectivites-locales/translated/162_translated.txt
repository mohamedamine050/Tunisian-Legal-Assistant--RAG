Pursuant to the principle of positive discrimination, the state shall bear the salaries of the general secretaries of municipalities that record a growth index lower than the national development rate, according to the statistical data of the National Institute of Statistics. The wages and grants for the general secretaries of the municipalities referred to in this chapter shall be granted based on criteria that take into account the geographic particularities, area, and population of the municipalities. The grants and wages shall be regulated by a government order based on the opinion of the **Supreme Administrative Court**.
