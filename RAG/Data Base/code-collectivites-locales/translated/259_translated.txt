The Mayor shall issue demolition orders for unauthorized constructions or those violating the issued permit, following the issuance of warning notices and based on legal reports. The head of the territorial security unit is required to submit a report to the Mayor regarding the outcome of the execution of the demolition orders mentioned in the first paragraph, within two months from the date of receipt of the aforementioned decision. Settlement of violations related to urban planning can only occur according to the terms and procedures established by the legislation governing urban planning and construction.
