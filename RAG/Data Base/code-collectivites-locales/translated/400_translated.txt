The Land Cadastre and Surveying Office, the National Cartography Center, and the Remote Sensing Center shall be responsible for placing boundary markers for the territorial domain of municipalities whenever necessary. Such placement shall be approved by a government decree, and the central authority shall bear the costs of the aforementioned tasks. This basic law shall be published in the Official Gazette of the Tunisian Republic and shall be implemented as a law of the State. Tunis, May 9, 2018. President of the Republic, Béji Caïd Essebsi.
