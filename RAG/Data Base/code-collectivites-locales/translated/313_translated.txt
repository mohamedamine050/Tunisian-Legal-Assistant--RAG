## English Translation: 

Summons to public sessions held by competent bodies, in accordance with the procedural rules set out in this guide, are to be attached. The purpose of these sessions is to obtain specific information regarding the works or activities undertaken by the public administration, to ensure the soundness, integrity, transparency, and consistency of performance with the requirements of the public interest.

Regional Council members shall be provided with explanatory notes on the matters to be discussed during the session. Draft transactions or other contracts shall be submitted to the members of the Regional Council, as a matter of obligation, at least five days before the session. In the exercise of their duties, each member of the Regional Council has the right to access all documents and data related to regional matters that are the subject of discussion.
