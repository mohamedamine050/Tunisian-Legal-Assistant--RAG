The grantee bears the responsibility of responding to the actions of the person before the law or compensating for any damage caused by the exploitation of the public facility. 

The grantee is responsible, in accordance with the current legislation, for the buildings, installations, and fixed equipment used within the framework of the authorization. They must ensure their civil liability throughout the duration of the authorization against risks arising from the works they carry out and the exploitation of the aforementioned buildings, installations, and equipment, pursuant to a contract. This insurance must include a condition that prohibits its cancellation or the introduction of significant changes without the prior consent of the grantor.

The local community remains responsible for the operation of the public facility towards its users, but they can pursue the grantee in court.
