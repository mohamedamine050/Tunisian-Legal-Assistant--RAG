The central authority is tasked with instructing the financial inspection and control bodies to conduct a subsequent audit to verify the extent to which the local community adheres to the provisions of the law and financial regulations. The local community is obliged to facilitate the tasks of inspection and control. The results of these actions shall be forwarded to the local community to exercise its right of reply and to take the measures required by law. Inspection and control actions that conclude with a suspicion of violations or crimes against the concerned local community shall be referred to the competent courts.
