The region shall be responsible for the following:

* **Developing plans to drive development in the region, adopting participatory democracy mechanisms while taking into account the requirements of sustainable development and the green economy.**
* **Supervising the management of various public services and facilities with a regional dimension, particularly those related to distribution networks, the environment, culture, sports, youth, social affairs, employment, and the elderly, for the benefit of the region's residents, institutions, and associations established therein, and maintaining its facilities.**
* **Organizing and supporting non-urban transportation within the region and working to improve its services.**
