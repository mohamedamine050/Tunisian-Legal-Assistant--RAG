The central authority or its representative in the region shall notify the local communities in writing of the following breaches when carrying out any of the acts stipulated in this chapter:

- Violation of national legislation concerning territorial jurisdiction.
- Failure to respect public interest easements.
- Inconsistency of their actions with examples of neighboring local communities.
- Damage to projects of public interest.

Crimes that warrant punishment by death or imprisonment exceeding five years are described as felonies within the meaning of this law.

Crimes that warrant punishment by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars are described as misdemeanors.

Crimes that warrant punishment not exceeding fifteen days of imprisonment or sixty dinars fine are described as violations. (Article 122 of the Code of Criminal Procedure)
