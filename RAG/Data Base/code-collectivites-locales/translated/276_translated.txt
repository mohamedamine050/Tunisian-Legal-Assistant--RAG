Municipal by-laws shall come into effect five days after the date of their publication on the official website of the local authorities’ gazette. The municipality is obliged to post a summary of the deliberations and a copy of the by-laws at its headquarters and at the headquarters of the municipal districts under its jurisdiction. The regional treasurer shall be notified of decisions with financial implications and contracts concluded with the municipality within ten days of their adoption. Notwithstanding the provisions of Article 46 of this law, the municipal council, in case of certainty, may decide by a majority of three-fifths of its members to make a by-law effective upon its posting and deposit with the governor and informing the public by any means available, provided that it is subsequently published on the official website of the local authorities’ gazette.
