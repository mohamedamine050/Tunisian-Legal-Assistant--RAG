If the budget of a local community is not approved for any reason whatsoever by December 31st, the mandatory revenues and expenditures as stipulated in the previous year's budget shall continue to be implemented at a monthly rate, by decision of the President of the local community. A copy of the decision shall be sent to both the Wali (Governor) and the Regional Treasurer competent for the area. 

In the event that the budget is not approved by the end of March of the current year, the Community Council shall be legally dissolved.
