The Administrative Court of First Instance in Tunis shall consider disputes concerning jurisdiction between local authorities and the central government. It shall render its decision within a maximum period of one month from the date of its cognizance. This decision is subject to appeal before the Supreme Administrative Court, which shall render its decision within a maximum period of two months.  The Administrative Court having territorial jurisdiction shall adjudicate disputes concerning jurisdiction between local authorities among themselves in accordance with the timeframes and procedures stipulated in Article 143 of this Law.
