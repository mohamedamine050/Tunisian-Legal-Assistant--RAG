In each district, an advisory body shall be established, called the District Council, comprising at least five members appointed by the Mayor from among the members of the Municipal Council, by decision of the Municipal Council after deliberation.  The principle of parity and youth representation shall be taken into account when appointing members of the District Council. The principle of placing the right person in the job that matches their qualifications and competencies shall be applied. The District Council shall be chaired by the District Head.
