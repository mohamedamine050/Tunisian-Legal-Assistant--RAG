The Grantor retains, permanently, the right to exercise general economic, technical, and financial oversight authority related to the obligations arising from the contract, as well as the right to amend the terms of the contract in accordance with the requirements of the public interest, while ensuring the right to financial balance of the contract. For this purpose, it may use experts or assistants whom it chooses and notifies the Grantee of. The oversight authority shall not disrupt the normal operation of the subject matter of the contract.
