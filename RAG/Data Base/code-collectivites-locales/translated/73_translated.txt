The head of the local community shall maintain the registers of real estate and movable property and shall update them promptly. He shall submit a periodic report on this matter to the local council. A copy of the registers shall be forwarded to the public accountant of the concerned local community. An insured electronic system may be adopted for keeping these registers. The model of the aforementioned registers shall be determined by a governmental order after consultation with the Supreme Council of Local Communities and based on the opinion of the Supreme Administrative Court.
