The authorized party shall personally perform the contract, unless the contract permits the delegation of a part of its obligations, subject to the prior consent of the grantor. In all cases, the authorized party remains personally liable to the grantor and third parties for fulfilling all obligations imposed upon it by the contract. During the execution of the authorization contract and until its termination, the authorized party shall maintain the buildings, structures, and equipment necessary for the completion and operation of the subject matter of the contract. The authorized party shall also comply with the principles of equality in treatment and continuity in providing services. Regardless of the contractual provisions, ownership of all buildings, structures, and facilities shall revert to the local community upon termination of the contract.
