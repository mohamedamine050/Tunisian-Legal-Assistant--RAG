## English Translation:

The central authority shall annually allocate an appropriation from the state budget to local authorities, specifically for the following purposes:

- **To finance interventions aimed at meeting the specific and urgent needs of local authorities and the relevant public institutions**, this appropriation shall be equal to the previous year’s allocation plus an increase determined by the Finance Law. These appropriations are allocated from the budget of the ministry responsible for local affairs. 

- **To contribute to the financing of expenses incurred by local authorities,** a specific amount shall be allocated to the Local Authorities Loan Fund and Assistance Fund. This amount shall be used for:
    - **Development expenditures, both operational and non-operational.**
    - **Exceptional support equal to the previous year’s allocation plus an increase determined by the Finance Law.**
