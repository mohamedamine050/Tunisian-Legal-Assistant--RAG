## English Translation:

An application for a Certificate of Completion and Conformity of Works shall be submitted to the Mayor. The applicant shall attach to their application a certificate of conformity of the completed works to the approved plans, issued by a qualified architect. The Mayor shall authorize the competent municipal officers or request the relevant regional equipment management to task officers from state departments with inspecting the conformity of the works to the authorized plans within one month from the date of submitting the application for a Certificate of Completion and Conformity of Works. The municipality's silence for two months from the date of submitting the application for a Certificate of Completion and Conformity of Works shall be considered a refusal.
