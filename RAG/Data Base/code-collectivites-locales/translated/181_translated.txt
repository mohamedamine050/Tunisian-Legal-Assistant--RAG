Within the framework of the budget-allocated appropriations, the total amount of commitments made during the year under the first heading shall not exceed the amount of actual receipts at the level of this heading.  Moreover, the total amount of commitments charged to the second heading shall be limited as follows: 

- For expenses financed by own resources, within the limits of resources available under this heading;

- For expenses financed by loans, grants, or contributions under sections 6 and 7 of Part 3, within the limits of the amounts committed for transfer under this heading by the relevant funding department;

- For expenses listed in sections 6 and 7 referred to above and expenses charged to Part 5, within the limits of the amount of appropriations allocated under this heading.
