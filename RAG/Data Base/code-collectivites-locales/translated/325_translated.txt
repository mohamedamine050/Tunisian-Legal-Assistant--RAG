In the event of a vacancy in the presidency of the Regional Council, the Regional Council shall convene to elect a president from among its members. The election session shall be validly constituted by the members present. If no candidate obtains an absolute majority of the members of the Council in the first round, a second round shall be held with the two candidates who received the highest number of votes. If the votes are tied in the second round, the younger candidate shall be declared the winner.
