## English Translation:

Subject to the provisions of this law, procurement contracts for materials, services, studies, and works by local authorities shall be concluded on the basis of the principles of free participation, competition, transparency, equality, equal opportunity, and integrity. The legal regime for the conclusion, execution, and monitoring of public contracts for local authorities shall be regulated by a government decree based on the opinion of the Supreme Council of Local Authorities and the opinion of the **Administrative Court**.

**Administrative Court**

**A court that considers matters related to public administration.**
