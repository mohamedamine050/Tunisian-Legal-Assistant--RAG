Local communities work to support the social and solidarity economy and sustainable development projects through agreements concluded for this purpose and in accordance with the current legislation. Local communities are committed to allocating funds to support social and solidarity economy projects and projects aimed at integrating rural women and persons with disabilities into economic and social life. The local community council works to encourage economic institutions to invest, particularly in projects that fall within the scope of the green economy and renewable energies. Projects related to the green economy and renewable energies enjoy priority support from local communities. The state is committed to supporting social and solidarity economy projects, sustainable development projects, and projects for the effective integration of women into economic and social life through contracts concluded for this purpose with the relevant local community.
