The internal regulations of the Regional Council shall specify the conditions and procedures for raising oral questions related to the affairs of the region or organizing open sessions held by civil society organizations, in accordance with the procedural rules set out in this guide, with the aim of obtaining specific information regarding the work or activities carried out by the public administration, to ensure the soundness, integrity, transparency, and alignment of performance with the requirements of the public interest. 

The internal regulations shall also specify the procedures for the annual sessions that the Council dedicates to discussing the reports of the committees. The sessions shall include hearing the President of the Council, one of his deputies, and the chairpersons of the committees.
