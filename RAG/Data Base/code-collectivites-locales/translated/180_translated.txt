During the fiscal year of budget implementation, if necessary, the appropriations allocated under the first heading for emergency expenditure and not yet distributed shall be used to open appropriations under the other items of this heading, to cover confirmed expenditures for which no budget provision was made or for which the allocated appropriations prove insufficient. The decision to open appropriations shall be taken by the President of the local community in the purpose based on a report of the committee in charge of financial and economic affairs and the follow-up of expenditure. The Regional Treasurer shall be informed of the decision to open appropriations. Similarly, subject to the same conditions laid out in this chapter, appropriations allocated under the second heading for emergency development expenditure and not yet distributed shall be used to open appropriations under items of sections six and seven.
