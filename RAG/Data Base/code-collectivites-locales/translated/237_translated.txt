The Municipal Council is responsible for managing and deciding upon municipal affairs. It is specifically entrusted with the following:

* **Financial commitments of the municipality**,
* **Setting taxes, fees, and various rights, regardless of their name, including those related to the placement of advertising signs within the municipal territory and for services rendered**,
* **Financial decisions, including alienation, compensation, authorization, granting of exploitation, and participation in local public facilities and other economic projects**,
* **Alienation and compensation concerning real estate**,
* **Terms of lease contracts exceeding two years in duration**,
* **Arranging parts of the public domain of the municipality, including roads, public squares, green spaces, and others, as well as their development and rearrangement, along with the establishment and modification of examples of the arrangement of municipal public roads**,
* **Conciliation agreements**.
