The Regional Council shall appoint the chairpersons and rapporteurs of the committees based on the principle of proportional representation. In the event of the resignation or absence of a chairperson or rapporteur of a committee, the President of the Regional Council shall appoint a suitable individual to fill the position, ensuring that the appointee possesses the qualifications and competencies required for the role. The President shall present the vacancies to the Regional Council at its first meeting. The Council shall be responsible for filling any vacancies that arise within the committees.
