The President or one of his assistants may be suspended from exercising their duties by a reasoned decision of the Minister in charge of local communities for a maximum period of three months after consulting the Office of the Supreme Council of Local Communities, and after hearing them or requesting them to provide written statements regarding any serious errors attributed to them that constitute a violation of the law and have caused serious harm to the public interest.

**Definitions:**

* **Felonies:** Crimes punishable by death or imprisonment for more than five years. 
* **Misdemeanors:** Crimes punishable by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars. 
* **Offenses:** Crimes punishable by imprisonment not exceeding fifteen days or a fine of sixty dinars.

**Suspension:**

* The President or his assistants may be dismissed by a reasoned governmental order after hearing them and consulting the Office of the Supreme Council of Local Communities, if their responsibility in the actions mentioned in the first paragraph is established.
* The Office of the Supreme Council of Local Communities shall provide its reasoned opinion within ten days of receiving the consultation from the Head of Government.

**Appeals:**

* Decisions regarding suspension or dismissal may be appealed before the competent Administrative Court of First Instance.
* Those concerned may request the suspension of the execution of the decisions within five days of being notified of the decisions.
* The President of the competent Administrative Court shall rule on the request for suspension of execution within ten days of the submission of the request.

**Effectiveness of Decisions:**

* Decisions regarding suspension or dismissal shall not become effective until the President of the competent Administrative Court issues a decision rejecting the request for suspension of execution, or upon the expiration of the deadline for submitting the request pursuant to the preceding paragraph of this chapter.

**Consequences of Dismissal:**

* Dismissal shall result in the inability of the dismissed individual to be elected for the remainder of their term, unless the dismissal order is annulled by the Administrative Court.
