The resources of inter-municipal cooperation institutions consist of:

- Contributions from participating municipalities, regardless of their source.
- Revenue from the institution's own immovable and movable property provided by the municipalities.
- Income from various public administrations, associations, and private entities in exchange for the services it provides to them.
- Allocations designated for it by the State.
- Donations and bequests, taking into account the provisions of the legislation in force.
