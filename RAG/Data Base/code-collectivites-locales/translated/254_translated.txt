## English Translation:

In the event that the Mayor is absent, suspended, removed from office, or any other impediment arises resulting in a temporary vacancy, he shall be replaced in all his duties by the Assistant, according to the order of designation. If the replacement by the Assistant is impossible, he shall be replaced by a member of the Municipal Council elected by the Council by a majority of those present. The Wali (Governor) and the Regional Treasurer with territorial jurisdiction shall be notified.

The absence of the Mayor for health reasons or for travel exceeding one month, or suspension from his duties, shall be considered a temporary vacancy. The temporary replacement shall not exceed a period of six months. Upon the expiry of the aforementioned period, the provisions of Article 247 of this law shall apply.
