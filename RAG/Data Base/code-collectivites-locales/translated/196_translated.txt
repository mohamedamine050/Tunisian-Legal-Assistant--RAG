The Head of the local community shall refer the financial accounts, the decision approving them, and the report of the committee entrusted with financial and economic affairs and the monitoring of the management to the Regional Treasurer competent in the area, for the purpose of endorsing the approved accounts and issuing a certificate confirming their conformity with the accountant's records, within a period not exceeding fifteen days. The Head of the local community shall submit a copy of the financial accounts, certified as conforming to the original, to the Court of Auditors competent in the area, within a period not exceeding July 31st of the year following the year of the transaction.
