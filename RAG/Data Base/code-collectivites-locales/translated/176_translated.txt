## English Translation:

**Budget**

The term "budget" refers to a study of the revenues, costs, and conditions of a project over a specific period. 

**Approval and Access**

The budget is approved through the local community's designated electronic platform. A copy of the approved budget is made available to the public at the local community's general secretariat for review.

**Preservation and Archiving**

The budget documents and accounting documents are kept at the headquarters of the relevant local community.  A copy of these documents is also sent to the relevant departments of the Ministry of Finance. Local communities are responsible for digitizing and archiving these documents. They are required to send copies of the digitized documents to the National Archives and the National Institute of Statistics.
