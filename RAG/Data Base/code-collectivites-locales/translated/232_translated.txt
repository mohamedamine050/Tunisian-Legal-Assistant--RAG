## Minutes of Public Sessions Held by Competent Bodies

The minutes of public sessions held by competent bodies, in accordance with the procedural rules outlined in this guide, are recorded in a dedicated, numbered register. These sessions are held to obtain specific information regarding the operations or activities carried out by public administration, aiming to ensure the integrity, transparency, and compliance of performance with the requirements of the public interest. The minutes of these sessions are signed by the head of the division, who is responsible for transmitting reports and minutes to the mayor within eight days of the date of the session.
