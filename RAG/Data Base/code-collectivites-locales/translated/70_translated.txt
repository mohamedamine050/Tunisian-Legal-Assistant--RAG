The following properties shall become the property of the local public domain:

* Properties that have been expropriated for the purpose of transferring them to local communities for the construction of public utility facilities, or that have been acquired by local communities and designated for the same purpose.
* Properties resulting from subdivisions.
* Donations and bequests of real estate or buildings granted to local communities.
* Sports, cultural, and childcare facilities constructed by local communities or that are owned by them at the date of entry into force of this law.
* State public property that is transferred to the local community.
* Properties classified as such by law.
