If the election is annulled or the President or his assistants resign from their positions, the Regional Council shall be convened by the President of the Region or, if applicable, by the Wali whose jurisdiction includes the regional headquarters, for the purpose of filling the vacancy. The election session shall be chaired by the oldest member, assisted by the youngest member. The election session must be held within a maximum period of 15 days from the date the vacancy occurred.
