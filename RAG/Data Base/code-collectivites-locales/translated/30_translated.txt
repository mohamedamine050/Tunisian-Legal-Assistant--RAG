The local community shall keep a register containing the components of civil society concerned with local affairs upon their request. It shall also compulsorily maintain a register containing the opinions and inquiries of residents and civil society components and the responses thereto. An electronic system may be adopted to keep the same register. A summary of the observations and their outcome shall be presented at the beginning of each meeting of the local community council. Local communities shall publish draft regulatory decisions on their websites and by all available means and shall post them at their offices before submitting them for discussion by their elected councils at least fifteen days before the date of the meeting for discussion. The conditions for implementing this chapter and its procedures shall be determined by a governmental decree after consulting with the Supreme Council of Local Communities and based on the opinion of the **Administrative Court**, which is a court that considers matters related to public administration.
