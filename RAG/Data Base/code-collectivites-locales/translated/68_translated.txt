The House of Representatives may request from the **Court of Audit** a report that constitutes a form of information delivery, drafted in a semi-final format for future reference. This report should be a  performance evaluation of the outcome of the Decentralization Support and Development Program for a specific period. The **Court of Audit**,  if necessary, should provide practical proposals to improve the performance of local communities within the report. The report is to be published for the public.

The **Court of Audit** is a judicial institution entrusted with the examination of public accounts and assets, verifying the legality of government expenditures, and ensuring efficient and transparent management of public funds. It is a court of law where disputes between parties are settled. It is a civil court that falls under the judicial authority which must be independent within the three branches of government.
