The Municipal Administration is committed to serving all residents within the framework of applying the law, adhering to the principles of neutrality, equality, integrity, transparency, accountability, continuity of public service, and efficiency. It endeavors to protect the interests of the municipality and its properties.

The Municipal Administration is bound to implement the law in accordance with the objectives of the public interest, and to assist in completing projects and providing services within their designated timeframes.

Delaying the provision of services without justification constitutes an error that can escalate to the level of a serious error, rendering the responsible party accountable under the law.
