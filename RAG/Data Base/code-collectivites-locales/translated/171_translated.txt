## English Translation:

These documents are considered part of the budget project:

* **Financial statements** containing:
    * **The budget:** A study of revenues, costs, and conditions of a project over a specific period.
    * **The balance sheet**
    * **The statement of financial performance**
    * **The statement of comparison of budget to expenses**
    * **The statement of cash flows**
    * **The statement of commitments**
    * **Explanatory notes** for the above-mentioned statements.
* **List of transfers and grants for the benefit of the local community**
* **List of assistance provided by the concerned community to various associations, structures, and institutions**
* **List of real estate purchases and sales**
* **Annual investment program**
