The Regional Council President is assisted in managing the affairs of the region by an Office composed, in addition to the President, of assistants and committee chairs. The Office meets at least once a month. It is presided over by the Regional Council President, or his/her designated representative in case of unavailability, in accordance with the provisions of this law. The Regional Executive Director shall keep the minutes of the Office’s deliberations under the same conditions applicable to the Regional Council’s secretariat. The minutes of the Office’s deliberations are included in a numbered register signed by the Regional President, and the rest of the Regional Council members have access to these minutes.
