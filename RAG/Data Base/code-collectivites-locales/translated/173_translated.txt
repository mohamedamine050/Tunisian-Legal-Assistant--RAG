During the local community council meeting designated for approving the budget, the secretary of the committee tasked with financial and economic affairs and following up on expenditure shall present the draft budget and read out the opinion of the regional treasurer on the draft budget, if applicable. After opening the debate in accordance with the internal regulations, the draft budget shall be submitted to a vote. During the council meeting, no proposals that could disrupt the balance of the budget or create new burdens that the local community budget cannot afford shall be presented. Voting on resource estimates shall take place for each budget heading by section and category. Voting on expenditure estimates shall take place for each budget heading by division and chapter. The plenary session of the local community council shall remain open until the draft budget is approved by a majority of the members present, provided that their number is not less than one-fifth of the council members.
