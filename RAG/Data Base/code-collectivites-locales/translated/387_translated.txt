Pending the establishment of a Court of Appeal, which is a civil court subject to the judicial authority and must be independent within the three branches of government, the Court of Cassation, which is a supreme administrative court, shall consult with the Court of Appeal on all matters relating to public administration in the areas stipulated in this law, pursuant to the provisions of Law No. 40 of 1972 dated June 1, 1972, concerning the Court of Cassation.
