The State adopts a decentralized system in accordance with the provisions of Chapter VII of the Constitution, and progressively provides it with the necessary elements of effectiveness and efficiency. Upon the proposal of the government, the House of People's Representatives shall, during the first year of each legislative term, approve a plan for a decentralization support and development program within a **framework law** that defines the objectives and means mobilized for the entire legislative term.  The government shall prepare an **annual evaluation report** on the progress made in implementing and supporting decentralization and shall present it to the House of People's Representatives before February 15th of the following year.
