In the event of the absence, suspension, dismissal, or any other impediment preventing the President of the Region from carrying out their duties, resulting in a temporary vacancy, they shall be replaced in all their functions by the Assistant, according to the order of designation. If the replacement by the Assistant is not possible, the vacancy shall be filled by a member of the Regional Council, elected by the Council by a majority of those present. The competent Regional Governor and Treasurer shall be informed.

A temporary vacancy shall be considered to exist in the case of the President of the Regionâ€™s absence for health reasons or for travel exceeding one month, or in the case of their suspension from exercising their duties. The temporary replacement shall not exceed six months in duration. Upon the expiry of the aforementioned period, the provisions of Article 324 of this Law shall apply.
