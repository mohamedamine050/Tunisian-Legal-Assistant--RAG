## English Translation:

A **temporary committee** shall be appointed by a **governmental decree** proposed by the Minister in charge of local communities, after consultation with the President of the **Supreme Council of Local Communities**, in the following circumstances:

- **Dissolution** of the regional council.
- **Collective resignation** or resignation of the majority of the members of the regional council.
- Cases of **death** or **incapacity**.
- **Annulment** of the election of the regional council.
- The establishment of a **new region**.

This committee shall be constituted by a **formal procedure** to gather individuals with relevant expertise from different parts of the institution. The **president** of the committee shall be designated by the governmental decree. 

**Consultation** refers to the act of seeking advice and opinions.
