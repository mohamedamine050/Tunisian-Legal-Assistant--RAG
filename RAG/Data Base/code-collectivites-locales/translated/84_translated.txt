## English Translation:

Local communities are authorized to utilize some of their public facilities, property, markets, parking areas, shelters, advertising spaces, or rights associated with them for advertising purposes or for the construction of real estate projects on their property through lease agreements, in accordance with applicable legislative and regulatory provisions, provided that such utilization does not conflict with the principle of free administration. 

The Councils of Local Communities are responsible for deliberating on lease agreements, their duration, financial aspects, and their approval, after resorting to competitive bidding and adhering to transparency principles. Local communities may consult with the relevant bodies and services of central administrations.

Both the lessor and the lessee shall ensure the financial balance of the contract, within the limits required by the needs of the public service, the contract itself, and the compensation received by the lessee.
