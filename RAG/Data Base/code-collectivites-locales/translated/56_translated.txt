The President of the Higher Council of Local Communities may be invited to attend the deliberations of the House of People's Representatives and to be heard when discussing draft laws relating to local communities. The Bureau of the Council may also be invited to attend:

- Public sessions held by civil society organizations, in accordance with the procedural rules set out in this Guide, for the purpose of obtaining specific information regarding the work or activities carried out by the public administration, in order to ensure the sound performance, integrity, transparency, and consistency of such work or activities with the requirements of the public interest.

- A meeting of one of the committees of the House of People's Representatives to hear the views of its members or to communicate the concerns of local communities.
