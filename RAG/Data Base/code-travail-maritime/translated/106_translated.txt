The outfitter shall provide on board the ship, for the seamen, decent quarters that are well-ventilated and illuminated, and suitable for the number of seamen on board, specifically for their use. An order shall be issued setting out the conditions that must be met in these quarters. In ships not equipped for the purpose of tourist navigation, the outfitter shall, unless otherwise agreed, provide the seamen with bedding in accordance with the regulations concerning the preservation of health on board the ship. The bedding provided by the outfitter shall be placed under the responsibility of the seaman, who is liable to pay damages in the event of unusual absence or damage caused through his fault. With respect to ships equipped for the purpose of tourist navigation, this matter remains subject to local customs.
