## English Translation:

**Disputes referred to in Article 151 above shall be settled by way of amicable settlement.**

**Amicable settlement:**

[1] A contract concluded to resolve a dispute and terminate litigation, wherein both parties in amicable settlement waive some of their claims or deliver a sum of money or a right. 

[2] A contract by which the parties end an existing dispute or prevent a potential dispute, wherein each party reciprocally waives its right.

**[This shall be done] by the port maritime authority at the port where the ship is located.**
