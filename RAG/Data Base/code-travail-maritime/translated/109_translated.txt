The supplier shall provide each crew member, on the first of May of each year, with two work uniforms, two shirts, a pair of shoes, and a hat, all conforming to the usual standards of the profession. The cost of these items shall be borne equally by the supplier and the sailor. The latter's share shall be deducted from his wages in installments over a period of at least four months at sea. However, these provisions shall not be contravened by provisions which are more beneficial to the sailors and which result from:

* General administrative regulations that do not concern any specific individual, 
* Agreements, or 
* Customs.
