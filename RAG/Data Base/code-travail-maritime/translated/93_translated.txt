(Official Gazette No. 59 of 1995 dated July 3, 1995). The equipping party shall care for any seafarer who becomes ill or injured during the period of the employment contract. The care shall include:

a. Medical treatment, provision of medicines, and other adequate therapeutic means, both in terms of quantity and quality. 

b. Food, accommodation, and transportation necessary for treatment. This obligation ends upon the seafarer's recovery, the permanent nature of his illness or disability being established, or when he is placed under the care of a social security institution.
