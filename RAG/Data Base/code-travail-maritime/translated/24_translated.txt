A maritime lease contract must be executed in writing. 

The contract is a legally binding agreement that obligates the contracting parties and defines their obligations and rights in relation to the specific transaction.

The maritime lease contract shall be drafted in terms that define the rights and obligations of each party. A copy of the contract shall be kept on board the vessel, certified as authentic, and made accessible to the crew members. In the event that this is not feasible, the captain shall keep the copy in safekeeping on behalf of the maritime authority.
