(Amended by Law No. 48 of 1989 dated March 8, 1989.) The profession of maritime trade is free, however, it is prohibited for a seafarer to work on board a foreign vessel without a license from the maritime authorities of the port of departure. The maritime authorities of the port of departure must record this license in the registration book of seafarers, which is kept at each maritime center. The crew members must be Tunisian nationals, the percentage of whom is to be determined by a decision of the Minister in charge of maritime trade.
