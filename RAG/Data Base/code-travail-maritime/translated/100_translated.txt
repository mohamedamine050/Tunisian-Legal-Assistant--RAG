The food provided to seafarers shall be of good quality, sufficient in quantity, and of a good and suitable type for the voyage undertaken. The maritime authority may at any time monitor the food provided to crew members, including the types of food it comprises. This decision is a joint one by the Minister of State responsible for the commercial fleet and the Deputy Minister of Public Health.

**Appointment**

The appointment of the appropriate individual to the position that matches the qualifications and competencies required to perform it. 

The minimum components of a daily meal, on a continuous basis, shall include [a list of food items]. A copy of this decision shall be displayed in the sections designated for the accommodation of deck crew, engine room crew, and general service crew. Each category of personnel shall be appointed in rotation and at the time of each food distribution, one of them shall be tasked with verifying the quantity of food distributed and, if necessary, its type.
