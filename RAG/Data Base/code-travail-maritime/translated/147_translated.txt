Every commercial vessel with a gross tonnage exceeding fifty-two tons shall have on board a maritime apprentice or trainee in deck service, provided that the number of deck crew members does not exceed fifteen. If the number of deck crew members exceeds fifteen, an additional maritime apprentice or trainee shall be provided for every group of ten crew members exceeding fifteen. For the purpose of calculating the number of crew members, the deck officer shall be counted, excluding maritime apprentices and trainees who have previously sailed.
