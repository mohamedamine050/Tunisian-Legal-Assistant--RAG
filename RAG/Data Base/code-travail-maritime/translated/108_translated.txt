If a mariner, whether in a Tunisian port or a foreign port, wishes to file a complaint regarding a breach of the provisions of this chapter, he shall submit his matter to the maritime authority. The maritime authority shall immediately conduct an investigation, with the assistance of technical experts if necessary, and may determine the appropriate measures to be taken.

Its actions shall be in accordance with the provisions of Articles 152 and following of this Code. Any mariner who is proven to have been abusive in his complaint, subjecting him to disciplinary sanctions, shall, in addition to the compensation for damages, be liable to a fine.
