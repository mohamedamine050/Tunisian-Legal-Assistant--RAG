A Committee, established through an official procedure to gather individuals with relevant expertise from various parts of the institution, serves as an Advisory Committee for Joint Agreements in Maritime Employment. This Committee is entrusted with providing reasoned opinions on the matters stipulated in Articles 133, 134, 135, 136, and 137 of this journal (1). This Committee may examine Joint Agreements, considering their impact on prices, production, and the cost of living. The Secretary of State for Youth, Sports, and Social Affairs may also consult the Committee on any other matter relating to the conclusion or application of Joint Agreements. The Committee may also request relevant administrations to undertake all necessary investigations and procure all required documentation to complete its task, particularly regarding the economic situation of the branch(es) of activity concerned.
