## English Translation:

**Advance payments and amounts transferred that have been paid and become due are not refundable in the event of termination of the contract.**

**Contract**

A contract is a legally binding agreement that obligates the contracting parties and defines their obligations and rights regarding the specific transaction.

**Sea charter by the supplier or captain, and likewise if the contract is terminated due to force majeure or an unforeseen event, is not subject to any form of appeal. The maritime party may claim reimbursement in the event of termination, up to the amount exceeding the due wages when the account is settled, without prejudice to:**

- Judicial proceedings and disciplinary sanctions.
- All compensation for damages.
