The **contract** of **lease**, regardless of its type, shall expire:

(1) Upon the arrival of the agreed-upon term or the end thereof.

(2) By mutual consent of the parties.

(3) By notice of termination given by one party to the other in accordance with the provisions of this Code.

(4) By application of the provisions of Chapter 34.

(5) Upon the sailor's arrival on land due to illness or injury.

(6) By a court order of dissolution or by a ruling permitting it.

(7) By the sailor's call to military service.

(8) By the sailor's death.

(9) By the damage or unseaworthiness of the ship as officially certified, or by its seizure.
