Also considered as grounds for lawful termination, without any right to compensation for the seaman, are the following:

(1) The detention of the seaman accused of a felony or misdemeanor, which is defined as a misdemeanor that warrants imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not more than five years, or a fine exceeding sixty dinars.

(2) Any serious misconduct which is legally established and results in disciplinary action against the seaman.
