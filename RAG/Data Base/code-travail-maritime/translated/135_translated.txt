Joint agreements must include, at a minimum, the following provisions:

a. Freedom of association and freedom of opinion.

b. Wages applicable to each category of seafarers, the scale of standards applicable to each industrial specialization, and procedures for the registration of seafarers in each of the aforementioned categories.

c. Notice period for departure.

d. Appointment and discharge of seafarers.  However, the aforementioned provisions shall not infringe upon freedom of association or freedom of opinion.

e. An official method of forming a joint committee composed of individuals with relevant experience from various parts of the enterprise, with equal representation, responsible for resolving difficulties arising from the application or interpretation of the agreement.
