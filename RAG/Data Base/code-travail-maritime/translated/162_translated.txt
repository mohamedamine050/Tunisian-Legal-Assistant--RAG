Customarily, and with respect to the crew, if it is impossible to deliver notices to the captain on board the ship, they are legally deemed to have chosen the office of the maritime authority as their domicile, even for the purpose of notification of a final judgment, unless they have chosen that same domicile and notices are delivered to the parties administratively.
