Every commercial vessel with a gross tonnage exceeding two hundred and fifty tons shall have on board a maritime apprentice or a trainee in the service of the propulsion machinery if the number of the aforementioned machinery crew members ranges between ten and fifteen men, and two maritime apprentices or trainees in the service of the propulsion machinery starting from sixteen men. For the purpose of counting the crew members, the propulsion machinery officer and his crew shall be counted, excluding the maritime apprentices or trainees who have sailed previously.
