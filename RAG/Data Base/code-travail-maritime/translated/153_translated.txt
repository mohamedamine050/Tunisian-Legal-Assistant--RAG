##  Request for Conciliation

**Conciliation**

[1] An agreement concluded to settle a dispute and end litigation. This is achieved by each of the parties involved relinquishing part of their claim, or by the handover of money or a right.

[2] An agreement by which the parties end an existing dispute or prevent a potential one, each reciprocally relinquishing their right.

Conciliation may occur either by voluntary appearance or simply through a request, even if oral, submitted by the most interested party to the maritime authority, which shall summon the other party by administrative means.

Attendance for conciliation before the maritime authority is mandatory. 

The master is required to provide the seafarer with all necessary facilities for the aforementioned request.
