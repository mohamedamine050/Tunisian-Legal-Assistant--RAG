It is prohibited for the sailor to carry alcoholic beverages aboard the vessel without the permission of the captain. - It is prohibited for any outfitter to do the following: 

1) To operate on land a store where he sells directly or indirectly to the sailors employed by him or to their families, foodstuffs or goods of any kind whatsoever. 2) To compel the aforementioned sailors to spend their wages, in whole or in part, at specific stores belonging to him.
