The heirs of a mariner, in the event of the loss of the vessel due to its disappearance without any news, are entitled to the following in addition to the wages due as of the date of the last news:

- A penalty equivalent to one month's wages if the mariner was employed on a vessel engaged in domestic coastal navigation.

- A penalty equivalent to three months' wages for a vessel that traverses long distances or is engaged in international coastal navigation.

- A penalty equal to half the wages earned for the distance not covered if the mariner was employed on a voyage.
