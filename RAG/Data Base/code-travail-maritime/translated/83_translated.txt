Wage installments paid on account during the voyage shall be recorded in the ship's logbook and in the crew logbook, signed by the sailor if possible, otherwise by two witnesses chosen from among the crew members. The installments cannot exceed three-quarters of the wages due to the sailor when requesting the installment, after deducting any advance payments and transfers. The decision to grant the installment, in full or in part, is at the discretion of the master.
