The ship's logs or identity documents of deceased, missing, or discharged crew members shall be delivered without delay to the maritime authority of the first port of call. 

The logs or identity documents of deceased crew members may be canceled and subsequently delivered to their heirs. As for the logs or identity documents of crew members responsible for the termination of their employment contract as provided for in paragraph 4 of Article 30 of this Code, they shall only be delivered to them upon authorization from the Secretary of State responsible for the merchant fleet.
