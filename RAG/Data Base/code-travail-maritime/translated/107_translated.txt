The outfitter shall be liable for the loss or damage of the seaman's belongings due to the sinking of the vessel, a fire on board, or any other force majeure or unforeseen event. If the seaman disembarks leaving his belongings on board the vessel, an inventory shall be drawn up by the captain, assisted by two other seamen. The belongings, which must be handed over together with the inventory, shall be sealed and delivered to the maritime authority at the first port of call. The maritime authority shall forward them to the seaman's registration area or to his port of embarkation.
