In the event of the termination of a contract, the contract being a legally binding agreement that obligates the contracting parties and defines their obligations and rights in relation to the specific transaction.

**Lease:**

In case of termination of the lease contract due to the fault or negligence of the supplier or its representative, the seafarer shall be entitled to his wages for the days spent in the service of the vessel.  In addition, and in all cases, he shall be entitled to a dismissal penalty equivalent to thirty days' wages, taking into account joint or special agreements that may grant him more than that.
