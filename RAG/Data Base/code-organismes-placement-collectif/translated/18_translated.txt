The administrator and the custodian shall be jointly and severally liable to third parties and to the holders of shares for any violation of the legislative and regulatory provisions applicable to the common fund for investment in securities, or for any breach of its internal regulations, or for any errors committed against it.

For the purposes of this law, crimes that warrant a penalty of death or imprisonment for more than five years are classified as felonies. 

Crimes that warrant a penalty of imprisonment for more than fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or a fine exceeding sixty dinars, are classified as misdemeanors.

Crimes that warrant a penalty not exceeding fifteen days of imprisonment or sixty dinars in fine are classified as offenses. (Article 122, Code of Criminal Procedure).
