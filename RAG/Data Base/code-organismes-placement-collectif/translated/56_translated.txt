Any person managing a collective employment agency who fails to endeavor to place the appropriate individual in the job that matches the qualifications and skills required for its performance shall be punished by imprisonment for a period ranging from sixteen days to six months and a fine ranging from five hundred to five thousand dinars, or by one of these penalties only. The same penalty shall apply to an accountant of a collective employment agency. Upon recidivism, the penalty shall be doubled. 

Any accountant who intentionally provides or endorses false information about the status of a collective employment agency or fails to inform the Republic of criminal events that have come to his knowledge shall be punished by imprisonment for a period ranging from one year to five years and a fine ranging from one thousand two hundred to five thousand dinars, or by one of these penalties only. Upon recidivism, the penalty shall be doubled.
