The formation of a debt mutual fund or its pre-liquidation, except in the case provided for in the internal regulations, 

as well as the formation of a company managing the debt mutual fund, shall be subject to a license granted by the Capital Market Authority.

The founders of debt mutual funds and the companies managing these funds shall submit an application for this 

purpose accompanied by documents, the list of which shall be determined by the Capital Market Authority in an order.

The Capital Market Authority shall respond to the license application within a maximum period of three months from the date of the application's submission 

accompanied by the necessary documents.
