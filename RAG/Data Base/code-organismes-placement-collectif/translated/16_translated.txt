The manager of the common fund for investment in securities shall be a bank or a stock exchange broker in the form of a limited liability company or a management company as provided for in Article 31 of this Code. The manager shall manage the fund on behalf of the shareholders in accordance with the provisions of this Code and its internal regulations. In this context, the manager shall, in particular:

Represent the shareholders in all legal proceedings, whether in the capacity of plaintiff or defendant, and in all matters concerning their rights and obligations. In particular, the manager shall exercise the rights related to the securities included in the fund.

The manager shall not be authorized to borrow on behalf of the common fund for investment in securities.
