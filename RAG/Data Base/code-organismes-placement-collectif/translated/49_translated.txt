## English Translation:

Creditors whose claims arise from the preservation or administration of the assets of a collective employment institution shall only have the right to pursue those assets. Shareholders and holders of shares shall not claim debts of this institution except within the limits of its assets and proportionally to their contribution and shares. Personal creditors of the administrator and the depositor with him cannot pursue their claims against the assets of the collective employment institution.

**Extraction**
Extraction is the process by which a creditor obtains repayment of the financial debt contracted by his debtor.
