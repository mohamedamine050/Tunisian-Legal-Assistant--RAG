The Joint Fund for Investment in Securities shall be established by the joint initiative of the Manager and the Depositor, referred to in Chapter 28 of this Code, who shall be responsible for preparing the Internal Regulations.

The Internal Regulations shall define the duration of the Joint Fund for Investment in Securities, the rights and obligations of the Shareholders, and the Manager. The Capital Market Authority shall set the mandatory provisions thereof in order.

The subscription to the shares of the Joint Fund for Investment in Securities shall constitute acceptance of its Internal Regulations after reviewing them.
