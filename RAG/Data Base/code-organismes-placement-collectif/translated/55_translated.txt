## English Translation:

Any legal or actual manager of an institution engaging in collective securities placement activities without obtaining a license, or who continues to engage in such activities after the withdrawal of their license upon the expiration of the year mentioned in Article 33 of this Code, shall be punished by imprisonment for a term of sixteen days to one year, and a fine ranging from two thousand to twenty thousand dinars, or by one of these penalties only.  The penalty shall be doubled upon repetition.

The same penalty shall apply to anyone engaging in the activity of managing a common debt fund without obtaining a license, and to anyone placing shares issued by the fund without obtaining an issuance permit referred to in Article 43 of this Code.
