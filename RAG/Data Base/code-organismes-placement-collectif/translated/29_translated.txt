The assets of collective investment institutions in securities shall consist permanently and primarily of securities, and secondarily of liquidity, in accordance with conditions and limits to be determined by order.

Collective investment institutions in securities shall not be permitted to hold more than 10% of the same type of securities from a single source, except in the case of the State, local authorities, or securities guaranteed by the State.

They may not use more than 10% of their assets in bonds issued or guaranteed by a single institution, except in the case of the State, local authorities, or bonds guaranteed by the State.
