If the manager of a Collective Investment Institution in Securities is affiliated with a lending institution, within the meaning of paragraph three of Chapter 10 of Law No. 117 of 1994 dated November 14, 1994 relating to the reorganization of the financial market, then this institution shall not be permitted to hold more than 5% of the shares of any debt mutual fund to which the relevant lending institution has granted loans.

The same prohibition applies if the managers or actual persons responsible for the management of its assets are linked to the aforementioned lending institution.
