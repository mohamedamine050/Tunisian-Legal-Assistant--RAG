The minimum capital of investment companies with variable capital upon incorporation shall not be less than one million dinars.

The capital shall at all times equal the value of net assets, minus the funds intended for distribution as determined by Article 27 of this Law.

The minimum amount of capital below which shares may not be repurchased, as authorized by Article 5 of this Law, shall not be less than five hundred thousand dinars. The board of directors or collective management body of the company shall dissolve the company if its capital remains below one million dinars for a period of ninety days.
