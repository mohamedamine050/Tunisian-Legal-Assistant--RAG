A specialized institution registered on a list established by a ministerial decision of the Minister of Finance, after consulting with the Capital Market Authority, shall be responsible for:

- Evaluating the characteristics of the shares to be issued by the common debt fund and the debts the fund intends to acquire, as well as assessing the risks associated with those debts.

The results of the valuation and assessment shall be included in a document attached to the issuance prospectus referred to in Chapter 43 of this Journal and delivered to the subscribers of the shares.

The aforementioned institution shall also monitor the level of guarantees provided by the issued shares. The public shall be informed regularly of the results of this monitoring.
