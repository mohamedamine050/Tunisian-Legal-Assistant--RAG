The manager of the joint debt fund shall, within six weeks from the end of every six months, prepare an inventory of the assets of the joint debt fund under the supervision of the custodian. The auditor shall draw the attention of the managers of the joint debt fund manager to any matters that he observes that are contrary to the regulations or incorrect during the performance of his duties. The auditor shall be appointed by the board of directors of the joint debt fund manager or the collective management body.
