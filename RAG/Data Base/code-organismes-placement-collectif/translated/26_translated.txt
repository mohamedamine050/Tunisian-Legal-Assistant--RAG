The constitutive contracts and internal regulations shall define the payment deadlines relating to subscription and repurchase operations, the conditions for the distribution of funds intended for distribution, and the conditions for the valuation of assets, which must be in accordance with the accounting standards in force and must be standardized for all securities of the same type traded on the same market.

Collective investment institutions in securities that choose a system of distribution of funds intended for distribution must distribute these funds within a maximum period of five months after the close of the fiscal year.
