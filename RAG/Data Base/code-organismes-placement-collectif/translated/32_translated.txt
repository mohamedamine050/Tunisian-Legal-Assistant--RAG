The formation or liquidation of collective investment institutions in securities and the formation of companies dealing in these institutions is subject to a license granted by the Capital Market Authority.

The founders of collective investment institutions in securities and companies dealing in these institutions must submit an application for this purpose, accompanied by documents whose list is determined by the Capital Market Authority in order.

The Capital Market Authority shall respond to the licensing application within a maximum period of three months from the date of filing of the application, accompanied by the necessary documents.
