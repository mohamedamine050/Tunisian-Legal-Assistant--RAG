The Joint Debt Fund shall be established through a joint initiative of the Administrator and the Depositor, as referred to in Article 44 of this Code, and shall be responsible for preparing: 

- the internal regulations of the Joint Debt Fund, 

- a publication of an issuance intended to inform subscribers in advance about the process, in accordance with the provisions of Article 2 of Law No. 117 of 1994 dated November 14, 1994, concerning the reorganization of the financial market.
