Investment companies with variable capital shall not be permitted to own real estate except as necessary for the conduct of their business.

They shall not be permitted to create savings or reserves.

The ordinary general meeting shall be held and its deliberations shall be valid regardless of the proportion of capital represented. The extraordinary general meeting shall also be held upon the second call and its deliberations shall be valid regardless of the proportion of capital represented.

One natural person may manage three investment companies with variable capital at the same time, in addition to what is permitted by the Commercial Companies Code.
