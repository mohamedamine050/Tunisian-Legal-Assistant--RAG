The constituent contract or internal regulations of a collective investment undertaking in transferable securities shall specify the original value of the share or unit.

The issuance or repurchase of shares or units of a collective investment undertaking in transferable securities shall be made only in cash.

Shares or units of collective investment undertakings shall be fully paid up upon subscription, except for units of joint investment funds in development capital and units of start-up assistance funds, which shall be released according to the needs of the projects contributing to their capital (as amended by Law No. 78 of 2008 dated December 22, 2008).
