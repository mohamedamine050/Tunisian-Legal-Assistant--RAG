A precautionary lien on the property may be registered:

Firstly:  Lawsuits aiming to obtain a written judgment from the court that resolves a legal issue or fact concerning the nullity, cancellation, rescission, revocation, or amendment of registered real rights,  registered by way of transfer by death, or the cancellation of the registration, or the exercise of pre-emption, the rectification of registration, or the cancellation of registration.

Secondly: Lawsuits aiming to register all acts that require the transfer of or allocation of property.

Thirdly: Extraordinary appeals against registered judgments.
