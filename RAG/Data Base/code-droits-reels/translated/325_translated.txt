(Amended by Law No. 10 of 1995 dated January 23, 1995).

The objection shall be rejected ipso jure if submitted after the deadline stipulated in the preceding article, or if it is not accompanied by the supporting documents; however, the State and local authorities may submit their documents within a period not exceeding three months from the date of submitting their objection.

Contrary to the provisions of the preceding paragraph, the objection may be accepted from a party who was involved in a claim case, and then published it before submitting a registration request concerning the same matter, until the end of two months from the date of the judgment of dismissal.
