(Amended by Law No. 35 of 2001, dated April 17, 2001).

The Real Estate Registry Office shall deliver to each applicant a copy of the land registry extract.

The land registry extract is an official document certifying property ownership.  Or a certificate.

A certificate of ownership attesting to the legal status of the property on the date of receipt of the application, or a certificate of non-ownership, and enabling him to view the land registry extract.

Every application must be dated and signed by the applicant.

If the applicant is unable to write, the Real Estate Registry Office shall complete the application free of charge.
