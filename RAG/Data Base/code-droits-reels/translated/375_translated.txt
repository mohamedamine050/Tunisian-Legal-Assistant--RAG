(Amended by Law No. 35 of 2001 dated April 17, 2001).

Documents shall be in the form of official instruments and judgments submitted for registration to the Real Estate Registry Administration, either originals or copies.

If the documents are in the form of non-official instruments, they must be submitted as originals.

The Real Estate Registry Administration shall retain the documents and supporting evidence related to accepted claims and shall return the documents related to rejected claims to the party who submitted them.
