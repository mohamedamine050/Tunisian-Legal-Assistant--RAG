(Amended by Law No. 35 of 2001 dated April 17, 2001).

Registration is effected in the Land Registry consecutively and shall specify the following:

- The deed, its date, and its registration references in the financial department, and the type of accepted transaction.

- The identity of the party concerned with the registration of their rights.

- The identity of the party benefiting from the registration.

- The subject matter of the registration and its references in the Land Registry.

- The charges and easements affecting the property or imposed upon it.

- The price or value of the transaction, and the percentage and duration of any surplus in the case of a mortgage.

- References of the deposit of the accepted registration and the number and date of the cancelled and delivered title deed.
