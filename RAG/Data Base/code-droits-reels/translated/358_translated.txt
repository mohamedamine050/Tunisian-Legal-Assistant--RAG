(Amended by Law No. 35 of 2001 dated April 17, 2001).

The Land Registry Administration shall establish a land register for each registered property.

This register shall contain a statement of the property's name, location, area, and contents, and shall specify all rights relating to the property and subject to registration under this Code.

The cadastral plan shall remain attached to the land register.

Each land register shall have an identifier consisting of a serial number and the name of the governorate as a territorial reference.
