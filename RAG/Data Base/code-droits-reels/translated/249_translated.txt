He who has a registered mortgage; a registered mortgage is a contract by which the creditor acquires, on an immovable property allocated to the satisfaction of his debt, a real right enabling him to take precedence over ordinary creditors and creditors subsequent to him in rank in the recovery of his right from the price of that property, in whosever hands it may be. This right is extinguished by sale. A registered mortgage is a contract by which the creditor acquires, on an immovable property allocated to the satisfaction of his debt, a real right enabling him to take precedence over ordinary creditors and creditors subsequent to him in rank in the recovery of his right from the price of that property, in whosever hands it may be.  The right is terminated by the creditor's satisfaction or by security in the ways permitted to the debtor. The third party who satisfies the debt takes the place of the creditor in his rights against the debtor, according to the conditions stipulated in Articles 226 and 227 of the Code of Obligations and Contracts.
