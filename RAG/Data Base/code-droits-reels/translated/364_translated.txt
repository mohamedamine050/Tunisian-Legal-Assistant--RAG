(Amended by Law No. 10 of 1995 dated January 23, 1995).

Each owner shall be issued a deed conforming to the data recorded in the land register, signed and certified as authentic by the land registry keeper.

If the deed is proven lost or damaged, the owner shall publish the content of the title deed in the Official Gazette of the Tunisian Republic and in a daily newspaper.  After one month from the date of publication, the owner shall be issued a copy of the deed which shall stand in lieu of the original.  The land register must indicate the delivery of the copy.

The form, content, and substance of the deed and its copy shall be determined by an order.
