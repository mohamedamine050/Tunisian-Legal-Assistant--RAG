The designated judge is specifically tasked with ensuring, during registration proceedings, that no real estate right belonging to a minor or an absentee is infringed.  To this end, he shall conduct all necessary investigations and research, and he has absolute authority in carrying out this task.

Upon a request from the designated judge issued in favor of minors or absentees, the President of the Real Estate Court may, by a reasoned decision and after assessing the facts according to his absolute discretion, extend the time limit for the purpose of lodging an objection on their behalf to the registration application.
