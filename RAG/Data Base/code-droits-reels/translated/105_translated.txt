Right of Pre-emption (Shuf'a)

The right of pre-emption (Shuf'a) is a financial guarantee provided by a third party to ensure the performance of a contractual obligation.  In the case of a sale of property, it extends to the appurtenant rights. If the appurtenant rights are sold without the principal property, the right of pre-emption (Shuf'a) is permissible.
