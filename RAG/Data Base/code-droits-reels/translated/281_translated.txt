If the person who takes possession of the mortgaged property does not undertake the procedures outlined below to clear its title, then, by virtue of registration, he shall be obliged to pay the debts secured by that property and shall benefit from the same timeframes granted to the original debtor.

Any person to whom ownership of this property, or any other real right thereon capable of mortgage, has passed for any reason whatsoever shall be considered to be in possession of the property.
