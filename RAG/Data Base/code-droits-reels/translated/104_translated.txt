A partner, within the meaning of the preceding article, shall be:

First: Every owner or heir of an undivided share in a jointly owned property.

Second: The owner of the airspace above the sold land.

Third: The owner of the land with respect to the airspace sold above it.

Fourth: Every co-owner of a dwelling intended for habitation, not subject to the provisions of stratified ownership, who occupies a part thereof to the exclusion of the remainder.
