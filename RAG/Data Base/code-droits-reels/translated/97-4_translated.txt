(Added by Law No. 78 of 2005 dated August 4, 2005). - Imprisonment from sixteen days to two months and a fine from three hundred to three thousand dinars, or one of the two penalties, shall be imposed on anyone who practices the profession of a professional real estate agent in violation of the provisions of paragraph four of Article 97 (new) of this Code. The same penalty applies to anyone who fails to comply with the temporary or final cessation of work order referred to in Article 97 (thirdly) of this Code.
