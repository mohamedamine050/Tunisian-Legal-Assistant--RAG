A precautionary restriction may also apply to:

1) Rights in rem created and transferred, coupled with a suspensive condition, as well as promises to sell, except for the right granted to a lessee or an emphyteutic tenant to purchase the property.

2) The transfer of plots extracted from a property register and allocated to them, as well as easements imposed on unspecified plots, provided that proof of a filing for subdivision is submitted.

3)  Farming contracts and lawsuits aiming to recognize the right of the farmer or the division of the farmed land.
