The request for sale must be notified by the bailiff to both the purchaser and the registered creditors or holders of a caveat before the expiration of the forty-day period referred to in the previous article; otherwise, the request shall lapse. The request shall include a summons to appear before the Primary Court.

The Primary Court

The Primary Court is usually the first court to hear the dispute and render a decision regarding the location of the property to order the public auction requested.
