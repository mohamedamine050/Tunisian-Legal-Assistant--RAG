The new owner who wishes to clear the property of registered mortgages must, within one year from the date of registration of his title deed or within one month from the date of the first notice issued to him pursuant to Article 283, address a notification to all registered creditors including:

1) A copy of the title deed specifying its date, nature, the names of the parties, and the date of its registration.

2) A statement of:
    the price;
    any difference between the agreed price and the value of the property, whether an increase or decrease;
    and the related costs and their value, as well as the value of the property if its transfer was not by sale.

3) A certificate of registered mortgages on the property, if the property is registered.

4) The choice of a location within the jurisdiction of the Primary Court where the property is located.
