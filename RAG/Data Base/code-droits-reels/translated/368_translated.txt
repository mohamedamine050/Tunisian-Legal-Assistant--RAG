If the case was not previously registered with a precautionary entry, then the judgment shall not have effect against third parties except from the date of its registration.  [1] A claim is one party's request for their right from another in the presence of a judge; the requester is called the plaintiff and the party against whom the request is made is called the defendant. [2] A claim is the right of anyone with a request to submit it to the court for judgment on its subject matter.  It is, for the opponent, the right to present reasons for defense or arguments aimed at refuting that request.
