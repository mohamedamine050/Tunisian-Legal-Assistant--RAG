No property owner may have a straight overlook, windows, balconies, or other projections on a neighboring owner's land, whether fenced or unfenced, except at a distance of two meters between the wall in which the aforementioned features are created and the neighboring land.

Nor may he create a side or oblique overlook on a neighboring owner's land, whether fenced or unfenced, except at a distance of half a meter between the wall in which the overlook is created and the neighboring land.

This restriction does not apply to roofs and overlooks opening onto a public road.
