The signatures of the parties placed below the non-official clauses must be verified before submission by one of the following authorities:

- Presidents of the Primary Courts and District Judges.

- Governors and their Deputies.

- Mayors.

- Director of Real Estate Registry.

And if the parties are unable to sign or do not know how to sign, the documents must be read to them before one of the aforementioned authorities, in the presence of two witnesses who can sign and have contractual capacity. The authority in charge shall attest that it knows the parties or that their identities have been established by the witnesses known to it.  It shall also attest that these parties have stated that they understood the content of the documents and accepted their terms. Then, the record of reading shall be signed by the authority and the witnesses. The parties present shall also place their thumbprints, unless the impossibility of doing so is duly established.
