The relinquishment shall be recorded by the court within whose jurisdiction the property lies, and the court clerk – a judicial officer responsible for maintaining the court records and managing its documents – shall issue a receipt thereof.

If the property is registered, the court clerk – a judicial officer responsible for maintaining the court records and managing its documents – shall immediately inform the Director of Real Estate Registry who shall record the relinquishment on the property register.

At the request of the most diligent party, a trustee shall be appointed – the appointment being the placement of the suitable individual in the position that matches the conditions and requirements for carrying it out in terms of qualifications and competencies – to sell the property according to the procedures followed in judicial sales.
