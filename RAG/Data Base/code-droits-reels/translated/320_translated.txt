The applicant for registration shall submit, along with the declaration, all ownership fees, contracts, and public or private instruments, and any other documents that would identify the property rights attached to the property.

Any third party holding the aforementioned fees or documents shall submit them to the Court Clerk, a judicial officer responsible for maintaining the court records and managing court documents, within eight days following the notice served upon them by the applicant for registration; otherwise, they may be liable for damages.  The Court Clerk shall issue a receipt therefor without any charge.
