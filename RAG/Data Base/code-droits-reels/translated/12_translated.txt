Real estate property rights are:

- Ownership

- Usufruct and superficies, and their income

- Right of usufruct

- Right of use

- Right of habitation

- Air rights

- Long-term lease (emphyteusis)

- Right of easement

- Mortgage

- Mortgage

- A formal mortgage is a contract by which the creditor acquires, over a property designated for the repayment of his debt, a real right allowing him to take precedence over ordinary creditors and subsequent creditors in the order of priority in recovering his debt from the proceeds of that property, regardless of who possesses it.
