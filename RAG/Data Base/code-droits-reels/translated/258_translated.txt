Whoever pledges something may pledge another thing of a second rank upon it. In this case, the official mortgage remains...  An official mortgage is a contract by which the creditor acquires, on real estate allocated to the repayment of his debt, a real right that allows him to take precedence over ordinary creditors and creditors subsequent to him in rank in recovering his right from the price of that real estate, in whichever hands it may be.  This is under the hand of the first creditor in his right and the right of the second creditor, simply by informing him in the required manner by the debtor or the second creditor with the authorization of the latter of the existence of the official mortgage.
