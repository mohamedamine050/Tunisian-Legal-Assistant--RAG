I.  The following are exempt from the tax: natural persons and legal persons who are not established and not resident in Tunisia, as defined by the provisions of the Tax Code on the Income of Natural Persons and the Corporate Tax Code (amended by Article 80 of Law No. 90 of 2004 dated December 31, 2004).  Tourist establishments subject to the tax on hotels as stipulated in Article 41 of this Code.

II. Establishments benefiting from a special regime under special legislative texts or under special agreements remain subject to the provisions contained therein.
