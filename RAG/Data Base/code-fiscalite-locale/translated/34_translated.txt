I. The following are exempt from the tax:  Natural and legal persons who are not established and not resident in Tunisia, within the meaning of the provisions of the Tax Code on the Income of Natural Persons and the Corporate Tax Code (amended by Article 80 of Law No. 90 of 2004 dated December 31, 2004).  Tourist establishments subject to the tax on hotels as provided for in Article 41 of this Code.

II. Establishments benefiting from a special regime by virtue of special legislative texts or special agreements remain subject to the provisions contained therein.
