The aforementioned information is required from industrial, commercial, or professional entities, namely:  natural persons subject to income tax on industrial and commercial profits and non-commercial professional income; legal persons subject to corporate tax; and economic interest groupings, partnerships, and joint ventures engaged in commercial activity or a non-commercial profession (amended by Article 65 of Law No. 123 of 2001, dated December 28, 2001).  The aforementioned information is required even in the case of exemption of the persons referred to in this article from personal income tax or corporate tax.
