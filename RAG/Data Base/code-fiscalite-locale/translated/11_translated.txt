The former owner, in the event of a transfer of real estate ownership, must inform the relevant local municipality of the transfer of property ownership by means of a declaration provided by the administration in exchange for a receipt, or by registered mail.  The declaration shall not be considered unless it is supported by a legally valid document. The former owner shall be jointly and severally liable with the new owner for the payment of the due amount for the period between the date of the transfer of ownership and the date of the declaration.
