Subject to the provisions of the second paragraph of this chapter, the tax levied on industrial, commercial, or professional entities shall be calculated based on the number of raw transactions achieved by the entities subject to the tax (amended by Article 49 of Law No. 54 of 2013 dated December 30, 2013).  The tax shall be calculated based on income tax, taxes withheld on the total income or income components, or corporate tax for natural persons referred to in Article 44 bis of the Income Tax Code for Natural Persons and Corporate Tax, as well as for entities that record a proven loss according to accounting compliant with the accounting legislation for entities (1) (amended by Article 23 of Law No. 27 of 2012 dated December 29, 2012).
