The claimant is required to prepare and file a declaration with the competent authorities of the local municipalities, according to a model provided by the administration, in exchange for a receipt, or by registered mail with acknowledgment of receipt, within thirty days following the commencement of the census process.  The declaration shall include: 1. The name, surname, and address of the owner, possessor, or occupant, and the number of their national identity card and its date of issue, or identification elements for legal entities; 2. The capacity of the declarant; 3. The location of the building: street and number; 4. The total area of the property; 5. The covered area as defined in paragraph II of Article 4 of this Code; 6. The composition of the building and its appurtenances; 7. The intended use of the property.
