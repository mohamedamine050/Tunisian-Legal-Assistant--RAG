I- Omissions observed in the basis of the property tax levied on built properties, as well as errors committed in applying the rates, may be rectified until the end of the third year following the year for which the tax is due.  II- The statute of limitations, the loss of the right to pursue a claim due to non-compliance with deadlines, is interrupted by notification containing the amount of tax actually due, sent by registered mail with acknowledgment of receipt, or by a signed acknowledgement of receipt from the person concerned, or by a bearer of a bailiff's card (*), in accordance with the provisions of the Code of Civil Procedure.
