I- Omissions noted in the assessment basis of built properties, as well as errors committed in applying percentages, may be rectified until the end of the third year following the year in which the tax is due.

II- The statute of limitations, the loss of the right to pursue a claim due to failure to comply with deadlines, is interrupted by notification of a registered letter with acknowledgment of receipt containing the amount of tax actually due, notification of receipt, or by a copy of a notice signed by the party concerned, or by a bearer of a judicial card in accordance with the provisions of the Code of Civil Procedure.
