The Review Committee shall consider the appeals submitted to it by the information claimants.  This Committee, which is a formally constituted body bringing together experienced individuals from various parts of the institution, shall be composed of: 1. The Mayor of the local municipality or his/her representative; 2. Two members of the municipal council or the regional council, appointed by the council president; 3. The financial officer or his/her representative; 4. The general secretary or his/her representative, without voting rights. The Committee shall be chaired by the Mayor of the local municipality or his/her representative.  Its decisions shall be made by a majority vote of the members present; in the case of a tie, the chair's vote shall be decisive.
