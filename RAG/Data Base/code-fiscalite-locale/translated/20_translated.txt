Any claimant may submit a request for review of the information to the competent court within sixty days from the date of announcement of the completion of the census operations referred to in Article 9 of this Code or the expiration of the deadlines set for notifying the decisions of the review committee concerning objections received outside the census operations referred to in Article 25 of this Code.  The aforementioned request shall only be accepted if the concerned party proves that they submitted an objection to the review committee in accordance with the conditions stipulated in Article 25 of this Code.  A request for review before the competent court shall not suspend the collection process. The judgment issued by this court shall be final. Collection is the process by which a creditor obtains payment of the debt owed to them by their debtor.  The subject matter of the dispute is the encumbered information.
