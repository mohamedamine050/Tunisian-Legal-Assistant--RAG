The aforementioned information is required from industrial, commercial, or professional entities:  – natural persons subject to income tax on industrial and commercial profits and profits from non-commercial professions; – legal persons subject to corporate tax; – economic interest groups, partnerships, and joint ventures engaging in commercial activities or non-commercial professions (amended by Article 65 of Law No. 123 of 2001 dated December 28, 2001).  The aforementioned information is required even in the case of exemption of the persons referred to in this article from income tax for natural persons or from corporate tax.
