Notification of the claimant shall be effected by registered letter with acknowledgement of receipt, or by notification signed by the person concerned on a copy thereof by the tax official at their property, specifying the elements used in its assessment and the deadlines for lodging appeals with the Review Committee provided for in Article 24 of this Code. Notification shall be addressed to the address declared by the claimant in accordance with Article 14 of this Code, unless the latter requests notification to be sent to another address; in the absence of such a request, notification shall be addressed to the address of the property subject to the tax.
