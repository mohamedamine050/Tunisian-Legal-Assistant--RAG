In all proceedings involving the child, the party entrusted with the responsibility of responding to the actions of the person before the law or compensating for the damage caused shall inform the child and his/her parents or legal guardian in detail about the content and different stages of the proceedings. This shall be done in a manner that ensures the child and his/her parents or legal guardian are fully aware of their rights and guarantees under the law, including their right to legal representation and to request the review or annulment of decisions made in this regard.
