The **Judge** of the **Family Court** shall hear the child and his/her legal guardian, custodian, presenter, or sponsor. The Judge shall receive observations from the representative of the **Public Prosecutor's Office**, the entity responsible for defending the interests of society and enforcing the law before the judicial courts, as well as the child protection representative.  When necessary, the Judge may also hear the child's lawyer. The Judge may decide to conduct the proceedings without the child's presence, taking into account the child's best interests.
