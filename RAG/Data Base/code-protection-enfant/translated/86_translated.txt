If a case involves a child and defendants who have reached the age of eighteen, the necessary investigative procedures shall be carried out in accordance with the provisions of Article 85 of this Code. If the Public Prosecutor decides to pursue the defendants who have reached the full age of eighteen by means of arrest or direct referral, a separate file shall be created for the child. If an investigation has already been opened, the investigating judge competent to hear the case against the defendants who have reached the full age of eighteen shall, at the earliest opportunity, relinquish jurisdiction over all defendants to the investigating judge competent to hear the case against the child. If the case involves a child and a military personnel, the Public Prosecutor or the investigating judge before the Military Court shall, within forty-eight hours of taking cognizance of the case, separate the file and relinquish jurisdiction over the child to the competent children's court.
