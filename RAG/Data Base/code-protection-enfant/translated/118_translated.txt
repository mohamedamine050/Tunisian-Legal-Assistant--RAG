Any person who prevents a child protection delegate from carrying out their duties or obstructs the proper conduct of investigations and inquiries, such as making false statements or intentionally concealing the true situation of the child, shall be punished by a fine ranging from 100 to 200 dinars, without prejudice to the application of the provisions of the Penal Code punishing the violation of the sanctity of a public official while performing their duties. In the case of recidivism, the penalty shall be doubled.
