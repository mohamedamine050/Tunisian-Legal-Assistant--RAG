## English Translation:

**Judge**

A judge is a legal official who makes decisions in court and contributes to the enforcement of the law and the realization of justice.

**Court**

A court is a designated place where legal disputes between parties are resolved. It is a civil body that falls under the judicial authority, which must be independent within the three branches of government.

**Child Judge**

The Child Judge is tasked with overseeing the implementation of the procedures and punishments issued by him or her and the Court.  He or she must follow up on decisions regarding the child in collaboration with relevant agencies by visiting the child to assess their situation and their acceptance of the authorized procedure. The Child Judge is authorized, when necessary, to order medical, psychological, or social investigations.
