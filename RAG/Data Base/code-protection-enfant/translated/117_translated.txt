## English Translation:

The following are not subject to any form of appeal:

**Reconciliation Agreements:**

* **[1]** An agreement concluded to resolve a dispute and end litigation, whereby each party involved in the reconciliation agrees to waive some of their claims or to deliver a sum of money or a right. 
* **[2]** An agreement by which parties end an existing dispute or prevent a potential dispute, whereby each party reciprocally waives their right.
