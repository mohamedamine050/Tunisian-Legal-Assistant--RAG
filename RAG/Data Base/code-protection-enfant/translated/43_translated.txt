The Child Protection Delegate may propose one of the following consensual measures: 

a) Keeping the child within the family with the parents' commitment to take the necessary measures to eliminate the imminent danger threatening the child, within specific deadlines and subject to periodic monitoring by the Child Protection Delegate. 

b) Keeping the child within the family while organizing appropriate social intervention methods, in collaboration with the body responsible for providing the necessary social services and assistance to the child and their family. 

c) Keeping the child within the family while taking necessary precautions to prevent any contact between the child and individuals who may endanger their physical or mental health or well-being.

d) Placing the child temporarily with another suitable family, social or educational institution, public or private, or, where necessary, in a healthcare facility, in accordance with the applicable regulations.
