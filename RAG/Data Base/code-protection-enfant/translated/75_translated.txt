(Repealed and replaced by Article 4 of Law No. 41 of 2010 dated 26 July 2010). - Each Court of First Instance shall be established in a location where litigation between parties takes place. It is a civil court falling under the judicial authority, which must be independent within the three branches of government. The Court of First Instance shall be presided over by a single judge or several judges, and shall be assisted by one or more assistants to the Public Prosecutor for cases involving children. Their selection shall be based on their interest in such cases and their training and experience.
