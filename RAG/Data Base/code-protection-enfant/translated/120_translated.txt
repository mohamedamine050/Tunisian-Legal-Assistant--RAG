It is forbidden for anyone to publish a summary of the pleadings and decisions issued by the judicial bodies stipulated in this Code, which concern a child and are likely to harm his or her honor or reputation, or the honor or reputation of his or her family.

Anyone who violates the provisions of this chapter shall be punished by imprisonment for a term of 16 days to one year and a fine of one hundred dinars to one thousand dinars, or by one of these penalties only.
