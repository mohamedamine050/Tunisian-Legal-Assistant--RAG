The right of appeal is granted to the parents, guardian, presenter, custodian, caretaker of the child, or the child who has reached the age of discernment, or their representative. The appeal shall be submitted in writing to the competent Court, which is a civil court operating under the judicial authority that must be independent within the three branches of government. The appeal must be filed within ten days of the issuance of the judgment. The Court shall decide within a maximum period of forty-five days from the date of submission of the appeal request.
