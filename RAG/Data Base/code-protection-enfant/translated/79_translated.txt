## English Translation:

**Judge**

A judge is a judicial officer who makes legal decisions in court and contributes to the enforcement of law and the attainment of justice.

**Court**

A court is a venue for litigation between disputing parties. It is a civil entity that falls under the judicial authority and must maintain its independence within the three branches of government.

**Children**

Children are subject to appropriate measures of prevention, aid, monitoring, and education.  In exceptional circumstances, and based on the specific facts of the case and the child's personality, the court may impose a criminal penalty on a child who has reached the age of fifteen. In such instances, the punishment is to be carried out in a suitable and specialized institution.
