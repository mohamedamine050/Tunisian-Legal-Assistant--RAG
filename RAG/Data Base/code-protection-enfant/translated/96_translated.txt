Each case shall be adjudicated individually and without the presence of defendants in other cases. Only the witnesses of the case, the child's relatives, the child's representative, the child's legal guardian, the child's custodian, or those authorized by the child who are of sound mind, experts, lawyers, representatives of interests, representatives of institutions concerned with the child, and representatives of supervised liberty shall be allowed to attend the session. The verdict shall be reached by a majority vote of the judicial members in criminal cases. In all cases, non-judicial members shall have an advisory opinion. The verdict shall be pronounced in an open session.
