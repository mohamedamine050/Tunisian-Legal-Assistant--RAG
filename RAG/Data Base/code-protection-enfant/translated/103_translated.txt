(The third and fourth paragraphs were added to Article 2 of Law No. 53 of the year 2000 dated May 22, 2000) - Decisions concerning temporary measures authorized by the Children's Court are subject to appeal before the President of the Court of Appeal. The Court of Appeal shall hear appeals against decisions concerning temporary measures authorized by the Children's Court or the Children's Investigation Unit. The Court of Appeal shall review decisions issued in the original instance by the Children's Court and adjudicate the case in accordance with the provisions of this Code. The Court of Appeal shall hear appeals before the Court of Appeal in criminal judgments appealed originally in the first instance from the Children's Court. The Court of Appeal shall also hear appeals before the Court of Appeal in criminal judgments appealed from the decisions of the Children's Court issued by the Court of First Instance.
