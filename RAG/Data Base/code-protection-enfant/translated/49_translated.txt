This Convention shall enter into force on the thirtieth day following the date of deposit of the twentieth instrument of ratification or accession with the Secretary-General of the United Nations. 2. For States which ratify or accede to this Convention after the deposit of the twentieth instrument of ratification or accession, the Convention shall enter into force on the thirtieth day following the date of deposit by such State of its instrument of ratification or accession.
