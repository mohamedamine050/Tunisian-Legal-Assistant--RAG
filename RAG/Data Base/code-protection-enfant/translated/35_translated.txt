The individuals permitted to visit the event are: 1) parents and grandparents, 2) brothers and sisters, 3) paternal uncles and maternal uncles, 4) paternal aunts and maternal aunts, 5) stepfather or stepmother, 6) legal guardian, 7) in-laws of the first degree, 8) any person with a relationship to the event who is approved by the General Directorate of Prisons and Rehabilitation in the case of those who have no relatives in the area.
