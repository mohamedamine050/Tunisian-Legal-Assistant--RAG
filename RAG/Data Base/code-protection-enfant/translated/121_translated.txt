Whoever obtains or attempts to obtain access to the private life of a child, whether by publishing or disseminating news concerning the proceedings in which children's cases are dealt with, through books, newspapers, radio, television, cinema, or any other means, or by publishing or disseminating texts or images that may disclose the identity of the child, whether accused or victim, shall be punished by imprisonment for a term of 16 days to one year and a fine of one hundred dinars to one thousand dinars, or by either of these penalties only.
