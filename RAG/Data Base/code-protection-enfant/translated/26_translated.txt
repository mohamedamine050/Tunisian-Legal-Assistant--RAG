"Economic exploitation" shall mean exposing a child to begging or employing him/her in circumstances that violate the law or assigning him/her work that is likely to impede his/her education or be harmful to his/her physical or mental health or safety.

Crimes punishable by death or imprisonment for more than five years shall be considered felonies within the meaning of this law. 

Crimes punishable by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars, shall be considered misdemeanors.

Crimes punishable by imprisonment not exceeding fifteen days or by a fine not exceeding sixty dinars shall be considered offenses. 122 Code of Criminal Procedure
