Children between the ages of thirteen and eighteen who are alleged to have committed offenses described as felonies, as defined by this penal code, which carry a penalty of death or imprisonment exceeding five years, shall not be referred to ordinary criminal courts. Instead, they shall be referred to the Children's Court. 

Offenses described as misdemeanors are those which carry a penalty of imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or a fine exceeding sixty dinars. Offenses described as violations are those which carry a penalty not exceeding fifteen days of imprisonment or a fine of sixty dinars.  (Article 122, Code of Criminal Procedure) 

A felony is a serious crime punishable by law and subject to severe penalties, such as imprisonment.
