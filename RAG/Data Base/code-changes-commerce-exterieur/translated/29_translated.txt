The pursuit of offenses against exchange regulations may only be undertaken following a complaint by the Minister of Finance or a representative designated by the group to act on its behalf, who is qualified for this purpose. The provisions of Title 13 (Disputes) of the Gambling Regulations apply to these offenses, unless the offense constitutes a felony as defined by this criminal law, which entails a penalty of death or imprisonment for a term exceeding five years.

Offenses that carry a penalty of imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years or a fine exceeding sixty dinars are classified as misdemeanors.

Offenses that carry a penalty not exceeding fifteen days of imprisonment or a fine of sixty dinars are classified as violations. This is in accordance with Article 122 of the Code of Criminal Procedure, specifically the provisions of this title of this law.
