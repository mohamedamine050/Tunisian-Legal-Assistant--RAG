Any act involving counterfeit coins, banknotes, or securities, which, in its other elements, constitutes a crime against the monetary system, shall subject the perpetrator to the penalties stipulated in this law.

Prosecutions shall be brought against all those who participate in the crime, regardless of whether they know or are ignorant of the counterfeit nature of these coins or securities. 

These prosecutions shall be pursued under the provisions of this law, irrespective of any other prosecutions arising from any other crimes committed.
