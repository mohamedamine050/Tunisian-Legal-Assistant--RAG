Every natural person whose ordinary residence is in Tunisia, and every legal person, whether Tunisian or foreign, with respect to its establishments in Tunisia, shall repatriate to Tunisia all currencies obtained from the export of goods abroad or in return for the provision of services abroad, and in general, all income and proceeds generated abroad, in accordance with the conditions and within the deadlines set by the Central Bank of Tunisia.

Exempted from the obligation to repatriate income from abroad are natural persons of Tunisian nationality returning to Tunisia from abroad, and foreign natural persons residing in Tunisia with respect to their assets acquired abroad before the date of change of residence (added by Article 2 of Decree No. 98 of 2011 dated October 24, 2011).
