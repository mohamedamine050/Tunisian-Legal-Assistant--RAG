Every natural person or legal entity that possesses within Tunisian territory, by any title whatsoever, foreign movable property or (equivalent thereof) belonging to another:

1) Shall not deliver this property to its owner unless the latter is a duly authorized intermediary.

2) Shall, unless they are themselves a duly authorized intermediary, undertake on behalf of the owner of this property the deposit operation stipulated in the aforementioned sections.
