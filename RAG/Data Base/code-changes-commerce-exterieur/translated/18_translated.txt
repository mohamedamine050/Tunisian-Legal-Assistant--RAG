**(Amended by Section 1 of Decree No. 98 of 2011 dated October 2011).** - Every legal entity with legal personality and its own resources, and a financial status independent of the financial status of its members or shareholders, that is foreign in relation to any new establishment to be created in Tunisia, must, if necessary, make the declaration provided for in Section Sixteen within a period not exceeding six months from the date of establishment of the new entity.
