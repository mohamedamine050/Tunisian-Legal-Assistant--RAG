The various media rights granted to the tax administration, which collects funds for the state in the form of taxes, and the tax is a monetary amount paid by individuals and companies proportionally to their income or profits, may be exercised by the officials mentioned in Article 24 for the purpose of monitoring the application of the exchange regulations.

These officials may request any information necessary to carry out their duties from all public services without the possibility of being opposed by professional secrecy.

Any obstruction of this investigation (such as refusing to provide documents, concealing documents or transactions) originating from the persons concerned, including members of boards of directors, managers, directors and employees of legal entities, shall be recorded in a report and pursued as obstruction of duty under the same conditions specified in Articles 42, 281, and 300 of the Code of Commerce.
