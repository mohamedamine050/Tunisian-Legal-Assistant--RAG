Pursuant to orders issued upon the proposal of the Minister of Finance, after consultation with the Central Bank of Tunisia, it may be imposed upon natural persons of Tunisian nationality residing in Tunisia and upon legal persons whose registered office is located in Tunisia, the obligation to repatriate to Tunisia their assets held abroad, whether in the form of cash in Tunisian or foreign currency, or in the form of Tunisian or foreign movable assets, or to establish arrangements for retaining these assets abroad.
