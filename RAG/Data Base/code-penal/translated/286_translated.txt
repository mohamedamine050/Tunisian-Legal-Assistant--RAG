Imprisonment for a period of one year and a fine of one hundred and twenty dinars shall be imposed upon anyone who, with the intent of acquiring all or part of another's land or property, removes, moves, deletes, or alters the markers of its demarcation or its natural or man-made boundaries.  The same penalty shall be imposed upon anyone who unlawfully appropriates public or private water. If the act is committed using violence or threats against persons, the penalty for this act alone shall be imprisonment for a period of two years and a fine of two hundred and forty dinars, without prejudice to the application of more severe penalties prescribed for assaults on persons, if necessary.  Attempt is punishable.
