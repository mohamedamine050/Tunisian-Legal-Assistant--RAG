(Amended by Law No. 23 of 1989 dated February 27, 1989).  Any person committing an offense against external state security shall be punished by imprisonment for a term of twelve years if committed during wartime and for a term of five years if committed during peacetime.  The attempt is punishable. The provisions of Article 53 may be applied, and in all cases, additional supplementary penalties provided for in Article 5 of this Code may be imposed for a minimum term of five years and a maximum term of twenty years.
