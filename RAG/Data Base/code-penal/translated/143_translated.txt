Whoever, being able, refuses or neglects to render services, assistance, or aid when called upon to do so in the event of accidents, congestion, drowning, flooding, fire, or other disasters, as well as in cases of robbery, looting, or when apprehending a criminal in the act, or when the public pursues him while shouting behind him, or during the execution of a judicial order, shall be punished by imprisonment for a period of one month and a fine of forty-eight dinars.
