An artisan who manufactures locks or other artisans who sell or deliver lock picks to a person without verifying their identity, or who manufactures keys of any kind for anyone other than the owner of the premises or the owner of the item for which they are intended, or for a representative of the owner known to them, based on wax impressions or other molds or examples, shall be punished by imprisonment for two months.  The punishment for the aforementioned lock manufacturers and other artisans shall be imprisonment for one month if they open locks without verifying the identity of the person who requested it.
