(Amended by Law No. 33 of 1998 dated May 23, 1998)

If a public official or a person assimilated to a public official accepts gifts or promises of gifts, or presents or benefits of any kind whatsoever, in consideration for an act related to his duties, for which he is not entitled to remuneration, or for abstaining from an act which he was duty-bound not to perform, he shall be punished by imprisonment for five years and a fine of five thousand dinars.
