(Added by Organic Law No. 43 of 2001, dated May 3, 2001, concerning the amendment of the Press Code).  Imprisonment from sixteen days to one year and a fine from sixty dinars to six hundred dinars shall be imposed upon anyone who knowingly sells, distributes, or transfers prohibited publications, or publishes or promotes prohibited publications under a different title. The Ministry of the Interior shall carry out the administrative seizure of copies of the prohibited publications and any derivative works thereof.
