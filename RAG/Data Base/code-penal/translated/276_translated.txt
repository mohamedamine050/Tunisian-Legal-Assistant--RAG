A person previously punished with a physical penalty for an assault on property, who is found in possession of money, financial documents, or goods inconsistent with their circumstances and cannot prove their legitimate source, shall be punished by imprisonment for six months.  A person found in possession of tools inherently designed for opening or picking locks, and who cannot explain their intended use, shall be punished by imprisonment for one year, with confiscation of the money, its equivalent in value, and the objects or tools.
