Added by order dated January 10, 1957. - Any Tunisian or foreign national who commits an offense against the external security of the state shall be punished by the penalties provided for in Article 62 of this Code:

Firstly: Attempts, by any means whatsoever, to compromise the integrity of Tunisian territory.

Secondly: Establishes contacts with agents of a foreign state, the purpose or result of which is to harm the Tunisian state in military or diplomatic terms. (Paragraph 2 was added by Law No. 35 of 2010, dated June 29, 2010, and repealed by Article 4 of Decree No. 106 of 2011, dated October 22, 2011).
