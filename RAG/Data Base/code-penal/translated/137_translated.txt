Whoever intentionally, with the intent to infringe upon the freedom of work, damages or attempts to damage goods, materials, or equipment used for transporting or generating energy, machinery, or any other means intended for manufacturing, lighting, transportation, or water supply, shall be punished by imprisonment for a period of five years and a fine of two hundred and forty dinars.  The supplementary penalties provided for in Article 5 of this Code may also be imposed.
