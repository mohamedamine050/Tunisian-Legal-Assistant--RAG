Those who commit the offenses stipulated in this instrument shall be punished regardless of intent to cause harm.  Crimes that warrant punishment by death or imprisonment exceeding five years are described as felonies within the meaning of this law. Crimes that warrant punishment by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars, are described as misdemeanors.  Crimes that warrant punishment not exceeding fifteen days imprisonment or sixty dinars fine are described as violations.  Article 122 of the Code of Criminal Procedure.
