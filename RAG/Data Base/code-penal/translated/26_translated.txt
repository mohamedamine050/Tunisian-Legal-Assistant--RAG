(Amended by Law No. 63 of July 5, 1966).  In the event of a judgment imposing a penalty pursuant to the provisions of Articles 60 to 79 or Articles 231 to 235 of this Code, or issued for an offense described as a felony under the meaning of this law—crimes which warrant punishment by death or imprisonment exceeding five years—are classified as felonies.  Crimes which warrant punishment by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars, are classified as misdemeanors. Crimes which warrant punishment not exceeding fifteen days' imprisonment or sixty dinars fine are classified as infractions.  Article 122 of the Code of Criminal Procedure.  Regarding legislation concerning narcotics, administrative supervision is mandatory for a period of ten years unless the Court orders otherwise.
