(Added by Organic Law No. 43 of 2001, dated May 3, 2001, concerning the amendment of the Press Code). The distribution, sale, public display, and possession with intent to distribute, sell, or publicly display for advertising purposes, of foreign-sourced or other pamphlets, leaflets, and writings that could disturb public order or undermine good morals is prohibited.  Any violation of the prohibition stipulated in the preceding paragraph may result in, in addition to immediate seizure, punishment by imprisonment from six months to five years and a fine from one hundred and twenty dinars to one thousand two hundred dinars.
