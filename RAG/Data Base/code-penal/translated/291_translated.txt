(Amended by the order dated October 8, 1935),

Whoever uses a fraudulent name or false attributes, or resorts to tricks and deceptions intended to convince others of the existence of projects without any real basis, or fictitious influence or reliance, or which are intended to inspire hope in the success of a purpose or fear of failure or the occurrence of injury or other imaginary incidents, and who has received or attempted to receive money, movable property, documents, possessions, financial papers, promises, receipts, or releases, and has embezzled or attempted to embezzle all or part of another's property by one of these means, shall be punished by imprisonment for five years and a fine of two thousand four hundred dinars.
