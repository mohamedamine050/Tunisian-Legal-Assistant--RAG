(Amended by Law No. 23 of 1989, dated February 27, 1989).  Whoever commits theft under the following five circumstances shall be punished by life imprisonment:

First: The use of serious violence or the threat of serious violence against the victim of the theft or their relatives;

Second: The use of climbing, making underground openings, breaking and entering, using false keys, or breaking seals;  in an occupied premises or while in possession of the title or uniform of a public official or falsely claiming authorization from a public authority;

Third: The occurrence of the theft at night;

Fourth: The participation of several individuals;

Fifth: The carrying of an apparent or concealed weapon by one or more of the perpetrators.
