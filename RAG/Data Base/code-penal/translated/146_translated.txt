Any detainee who escapes from the place of his detention or frees himself from the hands of his guards by violence, threat, or by breaking out of prison shall be punished by imprisonment for one year.  Attempting to escape is punishable. If bribery or attempted bribery of the guard occurs, the punishment is five years' imprisonment.  "Any prisoner transferred to a clinic or hospital who escapes by any means whatsoever or attempts to escape from the place to which he was transferred shall be punished by imprisonment for one year." (Added by the order dated December 20, 1945).  The period of detention of any detainee who escapes, in any manner whatsoever, shall not be considered.
