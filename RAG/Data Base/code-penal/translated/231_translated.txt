(Repealed by the order dated May 26, 1949, then added by Law No. 34 of 1964, dated July 2, 1964, and subsequently amended by Law No. 1 of 1968, dated March 8, 1968)

Women who, outside the situations provided for by current regulations, offer themselves by gesture or word, or engage in prostitution, even incidentally, shall be punished by imprisonment from six months to two years and a fine from twenty dinars to two hundred dinars.  Any person who has sexual contact with one of these women shall be considered a participant and punished with the same penalties.
