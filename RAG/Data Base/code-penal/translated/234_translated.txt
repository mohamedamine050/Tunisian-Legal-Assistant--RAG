(Repealed by the order dated May 26, 1949, and then added by Law No. 34 of 1964, dated July 2, 1964),

Without prejudice to the more severe penalties provided for in the preceding article, imprisonment from one to three years and a fine from one hundred dinars to five hundred dinars shall be imposed on anyone who assaults morality by inciting minor boys or girls to debauchery, or by assisting them in it, or facilitating it for them.
