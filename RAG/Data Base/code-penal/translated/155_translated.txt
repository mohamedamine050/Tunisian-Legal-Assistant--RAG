He shall be punished by imprisonment for one year and a fine of seventy-two dinars, the court clerks, those entrusted with the archives, the bailiffs, the assistants, and other trustees, if their negligence results in the embezzlement, destruction, removal, or alteration of evidence, criminal procedure materials, or other documents, registers, contracts, and objects deposited in the archives or registry of a court, public repositories, or entrusted to an agent of the public authority or a public trustee in that capacity.  A court is a place where litigation takes place between the parties; it is a civil institution belonging to the judicial authority, which must have its independence within the three branches of government.
