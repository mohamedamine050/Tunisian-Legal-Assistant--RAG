(Added by Law No. 56 of 1977 dated August 3, 1977)

Whoever maliciously disseminates false news thereby endangering the safety of any land, sea, or air transport shall be punished by imprisonment from one to five years and a fine from two hundred dinars to four thousand dinars.  Whoever maliciously informs or disseminates false news in order to induce others to believe in the existence of a criminal act subject to criminal punishment targeting persons or property shall be punished by imprisonment from six months to five years and a fine from two hundred dinars to two thousand dinars.  Attempt is punishable.
