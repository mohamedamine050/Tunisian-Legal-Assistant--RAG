(Amended by Law No. 23 of 1989 dated February 27, 1989).  Imprisonment for a term of six years shall be imposed on anyone who uses, or attempts to use, to the detriment of the rights or interests of others, any seals, stamps, or original marks of public authorities prepared as mentioned in Articles 179, 180, and 181 of this Code. The punishment shall be imprisonment for a term of two years if the aforementioned seals are not those of public authorities.
