Whoever damages, spoils, demolishes, impairs, or defaces, in a manner whose effects are not removable, structures or other things intended for public utility or ornamentation, constructed by the public authority or with its authorization, including ancient buildings, columns, and attached building parts intended for their ornamentation, as well as mosaics, inscriptions, and sculptures, shall be punished by imprisonment for one year and a fine of one hundred and twenty dinars.  The attempt is also punishable.
