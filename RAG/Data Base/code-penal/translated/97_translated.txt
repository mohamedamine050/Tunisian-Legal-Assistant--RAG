(Amended by Law No. 85 of 1985, dated August 11, 1985).  Any person mentioned in the preceding article who takes or accepts any profit for himself or another, by any means whatsoever, in a matter which he has managed, supervised, or safeguarded, wholly or partially, or who takes any benefit whatsoever in a matter which he is entrusted to authorize payment in or to liquidate, shall be punished by imprisonment for five years and a fine equal to the value of the profit obtained.
