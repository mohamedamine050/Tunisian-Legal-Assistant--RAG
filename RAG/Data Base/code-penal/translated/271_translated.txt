Breaking and entering is defined as any breaking, dismantling, damaging, demolishing, or removal of walls, ceilings, halls, doors, windows, locks of any type, or any other devices designed for locking or preventing passage, or any other fences whatsoever, whether inside or outside dwellings, courtyards, poultry yards, stables, or their appurtenances.  Breaking and entering is also defined as the breaking open of safes, chests, and other closed containers. Included within the scope of what is defined as breaking and entering is the mere lifting of boxes, packages, and rolls wrapped in cloth and tied with rope, or any other closed containers containing items of any kind whatsoever, even if the aforementioned breaking did not occur at the location.
