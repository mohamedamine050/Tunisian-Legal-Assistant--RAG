(Added by Organic Law No. 43 of 2001 dated May 3, 2001), the Mayor shall, for the municipal area, and the Governor shall, for other areas, designate the locations specifically reserved for the posting of texts issued by the public authority.  The posting of private advertisements is prohibited in these locations.  Texts issued by the public authority shall be posted exclusively on white paper. Any violation of the provisions of this section shall be punishable by a fine of twenty to two hundred dinars, and upon recurrence, by a fine of forty to four hundred dinars.
