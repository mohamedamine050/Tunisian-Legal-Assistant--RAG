(Amended by Law No. 23 of 1989, dated February 27, 1989),

The applicable punishment shall be imprisonment for a term of twenty years if the sabotage or destruction was effected by an explosive device, without prejudice to the application of the penalties prescribed for homicide if the death of a person resulted from such sabotage or destruction.  The perpetrator of merely placing an explosive device in a public place or an inhabited place for a criminal purpose shall be punished by imprisonment for a term of twelve years.
