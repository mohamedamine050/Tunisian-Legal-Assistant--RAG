(Amended by Decree No. 106 of 2011 dated October 22, 2011).  A public official who infringes upon the personal liberty of another without legal grounds, or who personally, or through another, commits an act of violence or ill-treatment against an accused person, a witness, or an expert to obtain a confession or statement from them, shall be punished by imprisonment for five years and a fine of one hundred and twenty dinars.  If only the threat of violence or ill-treatment is committed, the punishment shall be reduced to six months.
