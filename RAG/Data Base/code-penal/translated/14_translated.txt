(Amended by the order dated September 15, 1923). - Punishable by a minimum prison sentence of five years for crimes considered felonies by law.

A felony is a serious crime punishable under the law and subject to severe penalties such as imprisonment.

According to Article 122 of the Code of Criminal Procedure, the minimum punishment is sixteen days for misdemeanors and one day (twenty-four hours) for infractions; a month is considered thirty days.
