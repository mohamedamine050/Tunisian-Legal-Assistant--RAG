Whoever commits the crime of defamation, which occurs through any assertion or attribution of a matter to the public that violates the honor or reputation of a person or official body, shall be punished by imprisonment for six months and a fine of two hundred and forty dinars.  Whoever commits the crime of slander, a form of defamation that involves the dissemination of false information to harm someone's reputation, shall be punished by imprisonment for one year and a fine of two hundred and forty dinars.
