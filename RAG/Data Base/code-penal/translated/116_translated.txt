Whoever assaults, or threatens to assault, a public official in the exercise of his legal duties, or any person legally called upon to assist that official, shall be punished by imprisonment for six months and a fine of forty-eight dinars.  The same punishment prescribed in the preceding paragraph shall apply to whoever assaults, or threatens to assault, a public official to compel him to do or refrain from doing something related to his duties.  The punishment shall be imprisonment for three years and a fine of one hundred and twenty dinars if the offender is armed.
