A public official or a person with similar status shall be punished by imprisonment for a period of two years if, using one of the means specified in Article 103 of this Code, he purchased immovable or movable property without the owner's consent, or seized it without justification, or compelled the owner to sell it to a third party.  In addition to the punishment, the court shall order the return of the seized property or the payment of its value if it is not found in kind, without prejudice to the rights of third parties acting in good faith.
