A person who, without intent to kill, intentionally gives another person substances and intentionally performs acts or operations that cause him illness or incapacity for service shall be subject to the penalties prescribed for assault and battery, according to the distinctions prescribed in Articles 218 and 219 of this Law.  The punishment shall be life imprisonment if death results.
