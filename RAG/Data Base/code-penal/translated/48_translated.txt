In assessing recidivism, the following are not considered:

First:  The penalties stipulated in Book Three of this Code.

Second: Penalties imposed by military courts, unless they are for crimes of public law.

Third: Penalties incurred due to the crimes stipulated in Articles 217 and 225 of this Code, and generally due to crimes which do not indicate intent, except if the current trial concerns an accused person previously convicted of a crime of the same type.
