(Amended by Law No. 23 of 1989, dated February 27, 1989).  Imprisonment for life shall be imposed on anyone who counterfeits a stamp of the public authority or counterfeits or forges financial documents or other documents issued by the Treasury or public funds.  The same punishment shall be imposed on anyone who intentionally uses a counterfeit stamp of the public authority or forged financial documents, or introduces them into Tunisian territory.  In addition to the aforementioned, the judgment shall include all or some of the supplementary penalties provided for in Article 5.
