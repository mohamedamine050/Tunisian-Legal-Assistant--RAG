(Added by Organic Law No. 43 of 2001, dated May 3, 2001, concerning the amendment of the Press Code)

Anyone who, without authorization from the administration, places posters and uses any means to write or draw marks or images on movable property or real estate belonging to the State or public institutions, or on property allocated for a public service, as well as anyone who, without being the owner of real estate or beneficiary of its revenue or lessee thereof, and without authorization from one of the aforementioned persons, places posters and uses any means to write or draw marks or images, shall be punished by a fine of twenty-four to two hundred and forty dinars and imprisonment of sixteen days to one month, or by one of these penalties only.
