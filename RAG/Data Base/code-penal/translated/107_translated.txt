A strike agreed upon by two or more public officials or those similar to them, with the intent to disrupt the implementation of laws or a public service, whether by collectively absenting themselves from service or otherwise, shall result in the perpetrator being punished by imprisonment for a period of two years.  "The provisions of this article shall not prevent public officials from exercising their trade union rights in defense of their industrial interests within the framework of the laws governing the exercise of said right."
