(Repealed by the order dated May 26, 1949, then added by Law No. 34 of 1964, dated July 2, 1964)

The penalties stipulated in the preceding Articles 232, 233, and 234 shall be imposed even if the various acts constituting the elements of the crime were committed in different countries.  A sentence of house arrest for a period not exceeding ten years shall be imposed on those who commit the crimes stipulated in the aforementioned articles.
