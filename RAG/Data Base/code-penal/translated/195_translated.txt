(Amended by the order dated January 18, 1947).  A public official who issues a passport, a travel permit within Tunisian territory, a firearms license, or any other licenses or certificates to a person unknown to him without seeking to identify him through two witnesses known to him, shall be punished by imprisonment from six months to one year and a fine of one hundred and twenty dinars. The punishment shall be imprisonment for three years and a fine of two hundred and forty dinars if the official knowingly issued the document under a false name.
