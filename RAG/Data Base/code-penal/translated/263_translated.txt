Whoever commits theft under the following circumstances shall be punished by imprisonment for a term of ten years:

Firstly: During a fire, after an explosion, flood, sinking, accident affecting the railway, riot, upheaval, or any other type of disorder.

Secondly: From the owners of hotels and other establishments engaged in this activity, and owners of cafes or establishments open to the public.

Thirdly: By an employee or servant against his employer or a person present in his employer's house.

Fourthly: By someone who habitually serves in the dwelling where the theft was committed.
