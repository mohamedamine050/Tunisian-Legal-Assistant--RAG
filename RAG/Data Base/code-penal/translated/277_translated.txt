He who, before the division, fraudulently disposes of all or part of the jointly-owned property in an inheritance, or claims entitlement thereto, shall be punished by imprisonment for six months and a fine equal to one-quarter of the value of what is ordered to be returned to the partner in the inheritance.  The same punishment stipulated in the preceding paragraph shall apply to a co-owner or shareholder who fraudulently disposes of the jointly-owned property or the company's assets.
