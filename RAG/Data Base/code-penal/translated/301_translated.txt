(Amended by the order dated October 8, 1935, and supplemented by the order dated February 1, 1945)

Imprisonment for a period of three years and a fine of one hundred dinars shall be imposed on anyone who exploits the inexperience, recklessness, or need of a person lacking the right to dispose of their property and induces them to sign a financial commitment or other contract restricting their property.  The punishment shall be imprisonment for five years and a fine of two hundred dinars if the victim is under the supervision or authority of the offender.  Attempt is punishable.
