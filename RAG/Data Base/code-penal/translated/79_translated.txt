Anyone who is part of a gathering intended to disrupt public order, and whose purpose is to commit a crime or to attack the implementation of the law, shall be punished by imprisonment for a period of two years.  The punishment shall be imprisonment for a period of three years if at least two of them are carrying a visible or concealed weapon. This shall not prevent the application of the provisions of Law No. 4 of 1969, dated January 24, 1969, concerning meetings, processions, parades, demonstrations, and gatherings.
