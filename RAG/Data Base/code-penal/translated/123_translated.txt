The punishment—a penalty and remedy imposed in the name of society upon a person criminally responsible for a crime committed, based on a judicial ruling—imposed on a prisoner for insubordination is added to the punishment—a penalty and remedy imposed in the name of society upon a person criminally responsible for a crime committed, based on a judicial ruling—he is currently serving.  If the insubordinate is in pre-trial detention, the punishment—a penalty and remedy imposed in the name of society upon a person criminally responsible for a crime committed, based on a judicial ruling—for insubordination is added to the punishment—a penalty and remedy imposed in the name of society upon a person criminally responsible for a crime committed, based on a judicial ruling—that will be imposed.  In the event of dismissal of charges or a judgment of non-suit, the insubordinate serves his punishment for insubordination before his release.
