(Amended by Law No. 23 of 1989, dated February 27, 1989).  Public officials or those with similar status who take illicit funds, by ordering their collection, or receiving or accepting what they know to be unwarranted, or exceeding the amount due to the administrations to which they belong, shall be punished by imprisonment for a term of fifteen years and a fine equal to the amount ordered to be reimbursed.  Furthermore, they may be subject to the supplementary penalties provided for in Article 5 of this Code.
