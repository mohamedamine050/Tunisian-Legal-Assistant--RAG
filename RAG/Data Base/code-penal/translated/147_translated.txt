(Amended by Law No. 23 of 1989 dated February 27, 1989).  One year shall be added to the sentence of a convicted person

accused against whom a judgment has been rendered determining his responsibility for a crime, and imposing a penalty or precautionary measure; imprisonment for a specified period, who escaped or attempted to escape. The increase shall be three years if violence occurred, or prison was broken, or

an agreement between two or more persons to commit a felony or a misdemeanor of theft, fraud, or forgery, whether specified or unspecified, or preparatory or facilitating acts to commit them, when the agreement is organized, even in its initial formation, and ongoing, even for a short period,

between prisoners. The increase shall be five years in the event of bribery or attempted bribery of a guard.
