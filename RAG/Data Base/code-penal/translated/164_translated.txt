Whoever intentionally, other than in the manner specified in Article 137 of this Code and without using explosives, demolishes all or part of buildings, dams, bridges, paved roads, classified public roads, barriers, or other structures intended for public disaster relief, or equipment intended for warning, or signals intended for public services, or water or gas lines, or electrical lines, or other facilities intended for irrigation or lighting, shall be punished by imprisonment for a term of ten years and a fine of two hundred and forty dinars.  The punishment shall be reduced by half if the demolition only results in their damage.  The attempt is punishable.
