(Added by Organic Law No. 43 of 2001, dated May 3, 2001),

Anyone who removes, tears, covers, or defaces in any way advertisements posted with the authorization of the administration in designated places, with the intent of altering them or rendering them unreadable, shall be punished by a fine of twelve to one hundred and twenty dinars.  In the event that this act is committed by an employee or agent of the authorities, the punishment shall be a fine of twenty-four to two hundred and forty dinars and imprisonment from sixteen days to one month, or one of these penalties only.
