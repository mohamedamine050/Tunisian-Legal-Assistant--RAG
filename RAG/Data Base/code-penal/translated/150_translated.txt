He who violates a ban on residence or who is placed under administrative supervision and commits an offense shall be punished by imprisonment for one year.

The convicted person is a defendant against whom a judgment has been rendered, determining his responsibility for a crime, and imposing a penalty or precautionary measure.

Crimes punishable by death or imprisonment exceeding five years are described as felonies within the meaning of this law.  Crimes punishable by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars, are described as misdemeanors.  Crimes punishable by imprisonment not exceeding fifteen days or by a fine not exceeding sixty dinars are described as infractions.
