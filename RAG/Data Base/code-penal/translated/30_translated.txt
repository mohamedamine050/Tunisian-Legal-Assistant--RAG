(Amended by Law No. 23 of 1989 dated February 27, 1989).  He shall be necessarily under guardianship.  Guardianship. Containment or isolation measures. Every person convicted of a felony; a felony is a serious crime punishable under the law and subject to severe penalties such as imprisonment.  Anyone sentenced to imprisonment for a period exceeding ten years from the date of his sentence until the completion of his sentence. A manager shall be appointed to manage his assets, and he may not dispose of them except by authorization, nor may he receive any amount, however partial, of their income. His assets shall be returned to him upon completion of his sentence, and the manager shall then account to him for his management during the period of his appointment.
