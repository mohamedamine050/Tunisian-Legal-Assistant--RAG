(Amended by Law No. 93 of 1995, dated November 9, 1995),

Imprisonment for a period of three years and a fine of two hundred dinars shall be imposed upon anyone who directly or indirectly exposes, or directly or indirectly abandons through negligence, in a populated place, a child unable to protect himself or a disabled person.  The penalty shall be imprisonment for five years and a fine of two hundred dinars if the offender is one of the parents, or a person having authority over the child or disabled person, or entrusted with their guardianship. The penalty in both aforementioned cases shall be doubled if the exposure or abandonment occurs in an unpopulated place.  Attempt is punishable.
