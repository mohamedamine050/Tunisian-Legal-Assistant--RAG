Whoever commits any of the following acts shall be punished by imprisonment for five years and a fine of four hundred and eighty dinars:

First:  Whoever counterfeits non-permanent stamps or other tax stamps.

Second: Whoever removes the cancellation mark from used tax stamps with the intent of reusing them.

Third: Whoever uses counterfeit forest stamps or counterfeit tax stamps, or reuses stamps that have already been used.  The counterfeiting of marks intended for stamping gold and silver articles remains subject to the legislation in force concerning them.
