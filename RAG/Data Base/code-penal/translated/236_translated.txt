(Amended by Law No. 1 of 1968, dated March 8, 1968)

Adultery by either spouse shall be punishable by imprisonment for five years and a fine of five hundred dinars.  Prosecution shall only be permissible upon the request of the spouse, who alone has the right to discontinue the prosecution or suspend the execution of the punishment. If the adultery is committed in the marital home, the provisions of Article 53 of this Law shall not apply. The accomplice shall be punished with the same punishment prescribed for the spouse committing the crime.
