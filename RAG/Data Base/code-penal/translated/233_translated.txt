(Repealed by the order dated May 26, 1949, then added by Law No. 34 of 1964, dated July 2, 1964). The punishment shall be imprisonment from three to five years and a fine from five hundred dinars to one thousand dinars in the following cases:

1) If the crime was committed against a minor,

2) If the commission of the crime was accompanied by coercion, abuse of authority, or fraud,

3) If the perpetrator of the crime was carrying a visible or concealed weapon,

4) If the perpetrator of the crime was the spouse of the victim, one of his ascendants, his guardian, or had authority over him, or was his servant, employee, teacher, official, or one of the religious leaders, or if he enlisted the assistance of one or more persons.
