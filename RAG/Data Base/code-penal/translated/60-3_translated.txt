(Added by the order dated January 10, 1957).  Any foreigner who commits any of the acts referred to in paragraphs 2, 3, 4, and 5 of Article 60 and in Article 60 bis of this Code is considered to have committed espionage and shall be punished by death. The same punishment prescribed for the crimes stipulated in Articles 60 and 60 bis of this Code shall apply to anyone who incites or attempts to commit them.
