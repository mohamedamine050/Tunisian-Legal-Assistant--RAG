(Added by Law No. 56 of 1977 dated August 3, 1977, and amended by Law No. 23 of 1989 dated February 27, 1989)

Any person who seizes or takes control of a land, sea, or air vehicle by means of threat or violence shall be punished by imprisonment for a term of ten years.  The punishment shall be imprisonment for a term of twenty years if such acts result in injury or illness. The punishment shall be life imprisonment if death results therefrom. This does not preclude the application of Articles 28, 201, 203, and 204 of this Code, as appropriate.
