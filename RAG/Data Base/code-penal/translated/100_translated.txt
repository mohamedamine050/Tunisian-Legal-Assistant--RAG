(Amended by Law No. 23 of 1989, dated February 27, 1989). Any public official, or equivalent thereof, who steals, embezzles, or removes documents or records entrusted to him by virtue of his position shall be punished by imprisonment for a term of ten years and a fine of one thousand dinars.  In addition to this, he may be sentenced to the supplementary penalties provided for in Article 5 of this Code.
