Whoever intentionally breaks, removes, or attempts to break or remove external markers such as strips, stamps, or seals prepared by an administrative or judicial authority to prevent entry to places, or removes movable property in the context of a judicial investigation, inventory, deposit, or lien, shall be punished by imprisonment for a term of three years.  If the custodian himself broke or attempted to break the seals, or participated in breaking them, the punishment shall be imprisonment for a term of five years and a fine of four hundred and eighty dinars.
