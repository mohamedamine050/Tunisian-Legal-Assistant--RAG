Article 82 (Amended by Law No. 33 of 1998, dated May 23, 1998)

A public official to whom the provisions of this law apply is any person entrusted with public authority powers or working for a department of the State, a local authority, a public institution, a public establishment, or any other entity contributing to the management of a public service.  A person with the status of a public officer, a person elected to represent a public service, or a person appointed by the judiciary to carry out a judicial mission shall be considered equivalent to a public official.
