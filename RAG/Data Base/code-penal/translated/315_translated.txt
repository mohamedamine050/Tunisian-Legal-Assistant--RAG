Shall be punished by imprisonment for a period of fifteen days and a fine of four dinars and eight hundred millimes:

First:  Persons who fail to comply with the orders of laws and decisions issued by the competent authority.

Second: Persons who refuse to state their names and addresses when legally required to do so, or who provide false names or addresses.

Third: Persons who, without committing the crime stipulated in Article 126 of this Code, obstruct the course of justice by causing disturbances in the courtroom or elsewhere.

Fourth: Persons who sell food or foodstuffs at a price higher than that determined by the competent authority, except where the increase or decrease is due to factors agreed upon by the contracting parties.

Fifth: Persons who prevent a public official from entering a place while in the performance of their legal duties.
