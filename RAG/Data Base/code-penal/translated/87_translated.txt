(Amended by Law No. 33 of 1998, dated May 23, 1998)

Any person who exploits his influence or real or fictitious connections with a public official or a person assimilated thereto, and accepts for himself or through another, gifts or promises of gifts, or presents or benefits of any kind whatsoever, on the pretext of obtaining rights or privileges for the benefit of a third party, even if it is a legitimate right, shall be punished by imprisonment for three years and a fine of three thousand dinars.  The attempt shall be punishable. The punishment shall be doubled if the offender is a public official or a person assimilated thereto.
