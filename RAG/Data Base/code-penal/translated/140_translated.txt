(Amended by the order dated February 18, 1927). The penalty shall be imprisonment from one to three years and a fine from one thousand two hundred dinars to thirty-six thousand dinars if the increase, decrease, or attempted increase or decrease relates to grains, flour, foodstuffs, beverages, fuels, or fertilizers. The penalty shall be increased to imprisonment for five years and a fine of forty-eight thousand dinars if the foodstuffs or goods are not within the usual scope of the offender's activity.  The court may, in addition, order a ban on residence for a period not less than five years and not exceeding ten years.
