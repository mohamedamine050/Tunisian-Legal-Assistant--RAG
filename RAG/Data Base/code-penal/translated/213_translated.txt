(Amended by Law No. 93 of 1995, dated November 9, 1995),

Whoever commits the acts stipulated in Article 212 of this Code shall be punished by imprisonment for a term of twelve years if, due to negligence, the child or disabled person is left with a limb amputated or broken, or suffers a physical or mental disability.  Imprisonment for life shall be the punishment if death results therefrom.
