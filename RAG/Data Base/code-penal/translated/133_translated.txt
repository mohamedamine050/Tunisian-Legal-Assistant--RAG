Amended by Law No. 23 of 1989, dated February 27, 1989.  Any person who intentionally prepares a place for a meeting of members of a criminal gang, or assists them with money or benefits from the proceeds of their criminal acts, or provides them with a place to live or hide, shall be punished by the penalties stipulated in the first paragraph of the preceding article. The duration of these penalties shall be twelve years for the leaders of the aforementioned gang.
