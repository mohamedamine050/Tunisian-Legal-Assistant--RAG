(Amended by the order dated September 15, 1923, and by the order dated January 18, 1947). Imprisonment from one to three years shall be imposed upon:

First:  Anyone who assumes a fictitious name in order to obtain one of the documents mentioned in Article 193 of this Code, or participates in its delivery under a fictitious name;

Second: Anyone who uses or attempts to use one of those documents belonging to another person;

Third: Anyone who assumes a fictitious name in circumstances which led, or could have led, to his registration with the judicial identification service under a name other than his own.
