(Amended by Law No. 23 of 1989 dated February 27, 1989).  A perpetrator of an assault rendering a person castrated or impotent shall be punished by imprisonment for twenty years. The punishment shall be life imprisonment if death results. The same punishment shall be imposed on the perpetrator of an assault if it results in mutilation or partial or total amputation of the woman's genitalia (added by Law No. 58 of 2017 dated August 11, 2017).
