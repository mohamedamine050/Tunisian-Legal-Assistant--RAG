A person who feigns physical injury or wounds with the intent of obtaining alms shall be punished by imprisonment for a term of six months.  The punishment shall be increased to one year for anyone who:

First:  Intentionally resorts to threats or enters a dwelling without the owner's permission;

Second:  Begs while carrying weapons or tools inherently capable of facilitating theft;

Third: (Amended by Law No. 93 of 1995 dated November 9, 1995 and repealed by Law No. 61 of 2016 dated August 3, 2016);

Fourth: Begs while carrying forged certificates or other forged documents intended for identification.
