(Added by Law No. 33 of 1998, dated May 23, 1998). Any public official who, while in service, out of service, or seconded, undertakes a private activity for remuneration directly related to their duties without prior authorization shall be punished by imprisonment for two years and a fine of two thousand dinars.  The conditions for obtaining authorization from the administration and its procedures shall be determined by order.  The same penalty shall apply to any public official who commits this act within five years of their final cessation of duties and who was not legally authorized to do so.
