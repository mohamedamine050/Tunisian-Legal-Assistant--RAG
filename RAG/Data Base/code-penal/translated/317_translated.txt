The following punishments are imposed:

Firstly: On persons who provide alcoholic beverages to Muslims or to persons who are intoxicated.

Secondly: On anyone found in a state of evident intoxication in a public road or any other public place. (Amended by the order dated April 13, 1943).

Thirdly: On persons who mistreat the animals of others, without prejudice to the application of Articles 25 and 26 of the order issued on December 15, 1896.

Fourthly: On persons who mistreat domestic animals belonging to them or entrusted to their care. In the case of repeated offenses, a prison sentence shall always be imposed.
