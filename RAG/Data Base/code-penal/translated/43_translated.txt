(Amended by Law No. 23 of 1989 dated February 27, 1989, and by Law No. 93 of 1995 dated November 9, 1995).  Criminal law, the branch of law dealing with criminal offenses and punishments, applies to accused persons who are older than thirteen years of age but younger than eighteen years of age. However, if the prescribed punishment is the death penalty or life imprisonment, this punishment shall be replaced with a prison sentence of ten years.  And if the prescribed punishment is imprisonment for a specified period, its duration shall be reduced by half, provided that the imposed sentence does not exceed five years.  Supplementary penalties provided for in Article 5 of this Code, as well as recidivism rules, shall not apply.
