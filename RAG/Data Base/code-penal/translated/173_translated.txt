A public official, or an official of similar standing, or a judicial officer who, in the course of drafting contracts related to their duties, intentionally distorts the substance or subject matter thereof, shall be punished by the penalties stipulated in Article 172 of this Code.  This distortion may occur by including agreements not specified or dictated by the parties, or by recording false facts as if they were true and occurred in their presence, or as if they were admitted, when in fact they were not admitted, or by intentionally omitting statements received.
