The provisions stipulated in the current tax legislation in force in this matter, relating to duties, monitoring, penalties, disputes, and limitation periods, shall apply to the taxes, fees, and charges referred to in Chapter 95 of this Code.

The provisions in force in the matter of income tax on natural persons and corporate tax shall apply to mining royalties and the tax on profits as stipulated in Chapter 96 of this Code.

This applies in relation to duties, monitoring, penalties, and disputes.  The correction of any omissions, errors, or concealments found in the assessment of the profit tax and mining royalties, or in the application of their rates or calculation, shall be subject to a limitation period of fifteen years from the year in which the profits or turnover were realized.
