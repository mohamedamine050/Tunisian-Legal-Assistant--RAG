The exploration license may cover areas that are or are not subject to a mining concession. This license applies to mineral resources classified as "mines" belonging to one or several groups, but does not include the group subject to a valid exploration license or exploitation concession.

This license may be granted to several applicants for the same area.

In all cases, the rights of the holder of the exploration license remain fully intact, especially if the activities of the holder of this license cause material and direct disturbance to the activities of the holder of the exploration license.
