An exploration license is granted to enable applicants to conduct the necessary investigations to prepare applications for exploration permits. This exploration license relates to geological works and studies of strategic importance aimed at identifying promising mining locations and zones, potentially leading to the development of programs for the exploration of mineral resources classified as "mines." This license does not include excavation or mining works.

The exploration license is granted by a decision of the Minister in charge of Mines for a maximum period of one year, renewable once for the same duration. In the event of the application being rejected, the Ministry in charge of Mines shall notify the applicant within one month of the date of filing the application.
