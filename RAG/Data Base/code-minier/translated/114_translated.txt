The officials and employees of the Ministry responsible for mines are authorized to enter, at any time, the offices, premises, and appurtenances of the license holder, pursuant to a mission authorization. The license holder is obligated to provide them with all information and documents available on site related to their activities, and to facilitate their mission. The license holder is also obligated to make available the officials responsible for the works whose assistance is deemed necessary to accompany them during their visits.
