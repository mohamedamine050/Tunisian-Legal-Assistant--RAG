If it becomes apparent that it is necessary to undertake works aimed at connecting underground mining operations of different mines for ventilation or water drainage, or to open tunnels for ventilation, water drainage, or relief for the benefit of a neighboring mine, the license holder shall facilitate the execution of these works and contribute to their costs in proportion to the benefit he receives. If no agreement is reached by mutual consent, these works shall be ordered by a decision of the Minister in charge of mines, after hearing the license holder. In case of extreme urgency, the Ministry in charge of mines may order the execution of these works by official correspondence.
