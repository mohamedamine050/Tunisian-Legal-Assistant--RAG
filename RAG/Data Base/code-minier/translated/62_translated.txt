In the event of a total or partial transfer of an exploitation concession, the transferee assumes all obligations or commitments incumbent upon the establishment towards third parties in exchange for the receipt of goods, services, or loans, and enjoys all rights relating to the entire concession or the transferred part thereof arising from the provisions of this Code and the implementing regulations.

The total or partial transfer of rights and obligations resulting from an exploitation concession shall not entail the payment of any tax, fee, or duty whatsoever, whether existing or created subsequently.

In the event of a total or partial transfer of the rights and obligations arising from an exploitation concession, the transferee may apply deductions according to the provisions of this Code, under the heading of expenses incurred by the transferor which have not been settled or consumed.
