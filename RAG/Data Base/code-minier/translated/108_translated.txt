Foreign auxiliaries who are not residents before their assignment or incorporation into the Tunisian Republic and who are appointed to carry out exploration, research, and exploitation activities may:

1. Choose to join a social security system other than the Tunisian system. In this case, neither the employer nor the employee is required to pay contributions under the social security system in Tunisia.

2. Benefit from exemption from tax on natural persons' income in respect of wages and salaries paid to them. In return, they are subject to a provisional tax contribution at a rate of twenty percent of the gross wage, including the value of any benefits in kind.

3. Benefit from a temporary exemption from duties and taxes payable on the importation of personal effects and a private passenger car. Any transfer of the imported car or personal effects to a resident is subject to foreign trade procedures and the payment of duties and taxes in force at the date of transfer, calculated on the basis of the value of the car and personal effects or the value of either at that date.
