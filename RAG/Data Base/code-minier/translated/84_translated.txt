The license holder shall not occupy public or private lands belonging to the state, local authorities, or install facilities thereon without obtaining prior authorization from the authority owning the property or entrusted with its disposal. No exploration, research, or exploitation activities for mineral resources classified as "mines" may be undertaken on lands belonging to the public or private military domain without prior authorization from the Minister in charge of National Defense. This authorization shall regulate the specific rules to be followed in carrying out these works.
