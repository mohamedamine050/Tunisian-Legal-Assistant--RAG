The initial perimeter referred to in Article 22 of this Act shall be square-shaped with an area of four hundred hectares, except for the initial perimeters related to minerals belonging to group six, whose area has been determined to be one hundred hectares (1 km2).

The sides of these perimeters shall be oriented according to the true north-south and east-west directions, and shall be composed of segments of parallel lines and dividing lines. Their angles shall be determined by geographic coordinates and by marker numbers fixed by order.

The area of exploration permits from group six shall be determined by using the metric distance between each of their sides and the same single reference point, which shall be physically fixed and mandatory on one of the topographic maps of Tunisia at a scale of 1:25,000 or 1:50,000 or 1:100,000.
