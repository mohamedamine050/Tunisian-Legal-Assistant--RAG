The holder of the license is exempt, with respect to the mining exploration and exploitation activities carried out by them, from all direct and indirect taxes, fees, and duties imposed or to be imposed by the Tunisian State or any local structures or communities, except for those specifically mentioned in Articles 95 and 96 of this Code.

Amendments to the rates and definitions of taxes, fees, and duties stipulated in Article 95 of this Code shall not be applied to mining exploration and exploitation activities unless they are applied uniformly to other categories of activities in Tunisia.
