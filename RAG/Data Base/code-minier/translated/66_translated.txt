The exploitation concession shall revert to the granting authority upon the expiry of its validity period, free from any obligation or burden, without relieving the licensee from his obligations, especially those stipulated in Article 73 of this Code. The status of real estate, equipment and property allocated for mining exploitation shall be settled as follows:

- The remaining mining reserves, state-owned lands, road networks, communication lines, resources, equipment for water disposal, and equipment necessary for safety and access to the mine shall revert to the granting authority.

- Private lands and buildings, as well as equipment used for exploitation, shall revert to their respective owners.
