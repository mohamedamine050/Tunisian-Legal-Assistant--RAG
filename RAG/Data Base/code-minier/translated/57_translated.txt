The licensee shall commence development works on the exploitation concession within a maximum period of two years from the date of granting the concession.

In case of a violation, the following shall be considered:

* **Crimes:** Crimes punishable by death or imprisonment exceeding five years.
* **Misdemeanors:** Crimes punishable by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars.
* **Infractions:** Crimes punishable by imprisonment not exceeding fifteen days or a fine not exceeding sixty dinars. (122 Code of Criminal Procedure)

In such a case, the granting authority, after hearing the licensee, may revoke the exploitation concession and dispose of it freely without granting any compensation.
