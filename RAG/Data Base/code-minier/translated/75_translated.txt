The license holder shall give priority in employment to Tunisians whenever this is consistent with the smooth operation of their activities. In the event that no Tunisian personnel are available with the required skills, the Ministry responsible for employment, after consulting with the granting authority, may authorize the temporary use of foreign personnel in accordance with the applicable law.

The license holder is also required to ensure the training of Tunisian personnel in all the skills required for their activities, according to a training program previously approved by the competent departments of the Ministry responsible for vocational training, after consulting with the granting authority.

The license holder is further required to give priority to using equipment and materials produced in Tunisia and to resorting to the services of Tunisian institutions or subcontracting companies whenever their offers are comparable to foreign offers in terms of price, quality, and delivery deadlines.
