The exploitation concession may be revoked in the following cases:

a. If the concession holder no longer possesses the technical and financial capabilities necessary to fulfill their commitments;

b. If the concession holder fails to pay the royalty on production stipulated in this Code;

c. If the concession holder refuses to replace a withdrawing partner in accordance with the terms stipulated in this Code regarding their rights and obligations;

d. If the concession holder refuses to provide the information and documents relating to the exploitation, as per the provisions of Article 76 of this Code; 

e. If the concession holder refuses to comply with the measures determined by the Ministry in charge of mines, as per the conditions laid down in this Code.
