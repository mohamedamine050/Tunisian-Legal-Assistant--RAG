The Ministry responsible for mines shall register the application for an exploitation concession on the date of its deposit and shall deliver a receipt to the applicant in this regard.

Registration shall be refused if the applicant fails to produce a receipt for the payment of the fixed fee provided for in the first paragraph of Article 96 of this Code.

If the application is not decided upon within the validity period of the license under which it was submitted, the validity of this license shall be implicitly extended without any further action until the Minister responsible for mines decides on it.
