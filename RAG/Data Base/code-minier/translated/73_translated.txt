The license holder, upon the expiration of the exploration license, whether due to its expiry, abandonment, cancellation, or when the holder of the exploitation concession decides to terminate its activities pursuant to the provisions of Article 66 of this Code, shall restore the exploited areas to their original condition to avoid harm to the health and safety of others, the environment, and resources.

The license holder shall remain liable for five years for any damages that may be found to have resulted from the exploitation of the mine. This period does not apply to diseases arising from occupational accidents and occupational diseases, which remain subject to the legislation in force.
