No drilling, excavation, or extraction activities can be undertaken on or below the surface of the ground in an area within a distance of fifty meters of properties enclosed by walls or similar structures, villages, residential areas, tourist and archaeological sites, religious landmarks, cemeteries, communication networks, protected natural spaces, and in general, all facilities built for the benefit of the public interest or technical facilities, **except** with the consent of the owner for private property or the ministry responsible for managing the relevant public property.

The execution of mining works that could damage water facilities in areas designated for the protection of surface water facilities (dams, lakes, canals, etc.) and within the radius of influence of wells is prohibited **unless** prior authorization is obtained from the ministry responsible for public water property before commencing works.
