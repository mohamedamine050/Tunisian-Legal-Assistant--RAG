The non-resident license holder is not subject to the requirement to bring the proceeds of his exports of mineral raw materials into the Tunisian territory.

However, he must make payments due to the State and all other payments for local expenses through foreign currency accounts or convertible dinar accounts.

The non-resident license holder may market his production in the domestic market. Sales in the domestic market, which must be settled financially in foreign currency, are carried out in accordance with the applicable exchange and foreign trade regulations.
