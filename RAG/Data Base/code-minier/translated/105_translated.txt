If the holder of the license or their contractor decides to transfer the goods imported or purchased from the local market under the system referred to in Chapter 104 of this Journal, they must:

A.  Submit a declaration of transfer to the customs authorities if the transfer is made to a transferee who enjoys the same exemption and freedom of supply systems enjoyed by the transferor. 

B.  Before the transfer, complete the foreign trade procedures and pay the duties and fees required upon importation based on the value of those goods at the time of transfer, if the transfer is made in favor of a transferee other than the one mentioned in point A of this chapter.
