The license holder shall comply with the procedures and regulations ordered by the Ministry in charge of mines in accordance with the provisions of this Code and the regulations enacted for its implementation.

If it becomes certain that immediate intervention is necessary, or if the license holder refuses to comply with the Ministry's orders, the Ministry in charge of mines may take and implement the necessary measures at the expense of the license holder.

In the event of an imminent danger, the officers of the Ministry in charge of mines shall immediately take the necessary measures to eliminate the danger. They may, if necessary, request the local authorities to provide, without delay, all necessary assistance for this purpose.
