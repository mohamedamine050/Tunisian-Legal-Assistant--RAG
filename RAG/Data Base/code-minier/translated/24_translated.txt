The application for obtaining or renewing a research license must be accompanied by a commitment outlining the research work that the applicant undertakes to carry out within the boundaries of the requested area, for the entire duration of the license's validity.

This commitment shall specify:

- The nature of the intended research work and its implementation schedule, the minimum expenses intended to be spent on the actual work,

- The team supervising and managing the work.  Proof of fulfilling this commitment shall be subject to an annual report submitted by the license holder to the Ministry responsible for mines.
