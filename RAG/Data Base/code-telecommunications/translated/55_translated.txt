Notwithstanding the provisions that may be decided pursuant to the texts adopted for the implementation of this Code regarding the establishment and operation of radio communication equipment on board aircraft or vessels navigating in the airspace or territorial waters of the Republic of Tunisia, foreign aircraft and vessels shall not be authorized to use their radio communication equipment except for navigation purposes or for the operation of those aircraft or vessels, and only if such aircraft or vessels do not have any other means of communication with the ground. In all cases, they are obliged to comply with any silence order issued by the Tunisian civil or military authorities.

Any violation of the provisions of this section shall constitute a felony, as defined in this Criminal Code, offenses which are punishable by death or imprisonment for more than five years.

Offenses punishable by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars shall be classified as misdemeanors.

Offenses punishable by imprisonment not exceeding fifteen days or by a fine not exceeding sixty dinars shall be classified as violations.

In addition to the penalties provided for in this Code, the provisions of this section shall result in the closure and sealing of the equipment until the aircraft or vessel that committed the violation leaves the airspace or territorial waters of the Republic of Tunisia.
