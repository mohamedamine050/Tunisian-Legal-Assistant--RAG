(Added by Law No. 1 of 2008 dated January 8, 2008, and repealed and replaced by Law No. 10 of 2013 dated April 12, 2013.) - Excess capacity of communication resources available on public utility networks may be leased to public communications network operators. The technical and financial bids relating to the leasing of excess capacity of the resources mentioned in the first paragraph of this chapter shall be published after approval by the National Communications Authority. The leasing of excess capacity of communication resources available on public utility networks shall be governed by an agreement that defines the technical and financial conditions of use. A copy of this agreement shall be submitted to the National Communications Authority for information.
