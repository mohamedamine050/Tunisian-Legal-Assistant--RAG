Notwithstanding radio equipment intended for connection to public telecommunications networks and equipment referred to in Chapter 33 of this Code, the manufacture, supply, installation, and operation of telecommunications and broadcasting equipment using radio frequencies shall be subject to the approval of the National Frequency Agency, after obtaining the opinion of the Ministers responsible for national defense and interior affairs. This approval shall specify the frequencies used, the power of the equipment, and its coverage area. The same procedures shall apply to any relocation of such equipment, any modification to any part of it, and any damage to it.
