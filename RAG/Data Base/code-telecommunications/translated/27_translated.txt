The establishment and operation of "independent private networks" (*) are subject to prior authorization by the Minister responsible for Communications, after obtaining the opinions of the Ministers of National Defense, Interior, and the National Communications Authority. This authorization does not exempt the operator from following the necessary procedures for the establishment of network components, particularly those related to the passage of the network through public roads, the construction of infrastructure, and the introduction of modifications thereto. This authorization is subject to the payment of a fee set by decree of the Minister responsible for Communications, after obtaining the opinion of the National Communications Authority. The general conditions for the establishment and operation of "independent private networks" (*) are set by order.  The establishment and operation of internal private networks are not subject to any form of authorization. (The last paragraph was added by Article 2 of Law No. 1 of 2008, dated January 8, 2008.)
