Irrespective of the criminal penalties stipulated in this Code, the Minister in charge of Communications may, after hearing the offender, impose one of the following administrative penalties on those who violate the provisions of this Code and its implementing regulations:

*  **Limiting the license and conditions of its exploitation, temporarily or permanently.**
*  **Temporary withdrawal of the license.**
*  **Permanent withdrawal of the license, with the affixation of seals.**
