The function of a member of the National Communications Authority conflicts with any direct or indirect ownership of interests in an entity operating in the field of communications. 

Any party concerned may lodge a complaint against any member of the Authority through a written request bearing the signature of the author, or an electronic request with a digital signature, submitted to the President of the Authority, who shall rule on it within five days after hearing both parties. In the event that the President of the Authority is the subject of the complaint, the Vice-President shall act in their place.
