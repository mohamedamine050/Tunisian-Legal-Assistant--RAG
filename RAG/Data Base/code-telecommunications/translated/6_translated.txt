Subject to the provisions of Chapter 3 of this Code, the provision of "comprehensive services"(*) in telecommunications is subject to the following conditions:

- Provision of points of contact throughout the Tunisian Republic that are open on a regular basis.
- Ensuring equality in the provision of services between all users.
- Development of these services in accordance with technological, economic, and social developments, and the needs of users.

The list of these services is determined by a decision of the Minister in charge of Telecommunications, after consulting with the National Telecommunications Authority. This list shall obligatorily include basic telephone services, the relaying of emergency calls, the provision of directory assistance, and the directory of subscribers in printed or electronic form.
