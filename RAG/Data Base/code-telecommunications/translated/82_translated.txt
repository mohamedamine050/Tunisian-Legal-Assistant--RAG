Any person who uses, manufactures, imports, exports, possesses for the purpose of sale or distribution, whether free of charge or for a consideration, offers for sale, or sells encryption tools or services, or who modifies or damages such tools or services without regard to the provisions of Article 9 of this Code, shall be punished by imprisonment for a period ranging from six months to five years and by a fine of one thousand to five thousand dinars, or by one of these two penalties.
