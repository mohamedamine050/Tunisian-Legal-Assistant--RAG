**(Added by Article 2 of Law No. 10 of 2013 dated April 12, 2013).** - The provision of an Internet traffic exchange point shall be subject to prior authorization by the Minister in charge of Communications, after consulting the National Communications Authority. The conditions and procedures for granting this authorization shall be determined by Order. The granting of the authorization shall be subject to the payment of a fee, which shall be fixed according to criteria to be determined by the Order referred to in the first paragraph of this Article.
