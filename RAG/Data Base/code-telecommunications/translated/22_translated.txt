Article 2 of Law No. 1 of 2008 dated January 8, 2008, is hereby amended as follows:

Public communications network and access network operators shall be obligated to adopt analytical accounting that allows for the differentiation of each network and each service separately. They shall desist from all practices that violate competition rules, particularly cross-subsidization. 

The general conditions for the exploitation of public communications networks and access networks shall be regulated by decree.
