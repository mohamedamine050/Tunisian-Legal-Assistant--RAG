**(Added by Article 2 of Law No. 10 of 2013 dated April 12, 2013)** - The activity of Internet service providers shall be subject to prior authorization from the Minister responsible for communications after obtaining the opinion of the Minister of the Interior and the National Communications Authority. The conditions and procedures for granting this authorization shall be determined by Decree. The granting of the authorization shall be subject to the payment of a fee, the amount of which shall be determined by the Decree referred to in the first paragraph of this article.
