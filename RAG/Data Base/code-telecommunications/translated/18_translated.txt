The "license"(*) is granted on a personal basis and does not confer any exclusive rights on its holder. It may not be transferred to another party except with the approval of the Minister responsible for Communications, after consulting with the relevant bodies. The "license"(*) is transferred pursuant to an agreement approved by ministerial order.
