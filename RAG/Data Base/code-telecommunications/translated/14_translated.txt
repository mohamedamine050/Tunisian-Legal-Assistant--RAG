**[Repealed and Replaced by Article 1 of Law No. 1 of 2008 dated January 8, 2008]** - The provisions of Article 5 of this Code do not apply to comprehensive telecommunications services, radio and television broadcasting services, and any other telecommunications service regulated by decree. The provision of these services shall be subject to the provisions stipulated in Articles 10, 12, and 91 of this Code.
