**(Added by Article 2 of Law No. 1 of 2008 dated January 8, 2008)** - The establishment and operation of access networks are subject to a license granted by decision of the Minister in charge of Communications, following a call for tenders. The rules and procedures for the call for tenders are regulated by an order. The granting of the license is subject to the payment of a fee, in accordance with the conditions stipulated in the license.
