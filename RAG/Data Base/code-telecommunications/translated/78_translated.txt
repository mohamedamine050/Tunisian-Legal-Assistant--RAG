Any person who, for the purpose of sale or distribution, free of charge or for a fee, offers for sale or sells terminal equipment or radio equipment as provided for in Article 32 of this Code, or connects such equipment to a public telecommunications network without obtaining authorization, shall be punished by imprisonment for a period of one to six months and a fine of one thousand to ten thousand dinars, or by one of these penalties. The same penalty shall apply to any person who advertises for the sale of unauthorized equipment.
