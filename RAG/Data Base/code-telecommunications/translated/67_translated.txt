**Repealed and replaced by Article 1 of Law No. 1 of 2008 dated January 8, 2008.** - The body shall take its decisions by a majority vote and shall issue them in person. Each member shall have one vote and, in the event of a tie, the vote of the president shall prevail. The decision issued by the body shall be reasoned and shall necessarily include a resolution of the dispute. It shall include the following information: - The names and registered offices of the parties to the dispute and, where applicable, the names of their lawyers and legal representatives; - A detailed presentation of the parties' requests and supporting documents; - The date and place of issuance of the decision; - The names of the members who participated in issuing the decision.
