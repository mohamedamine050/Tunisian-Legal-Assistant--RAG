(Repealed and replaced by Article 1 of Law No. 1 of 2008 dated January 8, 2008). - A General Rapporteur and Rapporteurs are appointed to the National Communications Authority, appointed by order from among judges and employees of "A" category. The General Rapporteur is responsible for coordinating and monitoring the work of the Rapporteurs and supervising them. The President of the Authority may appoint  contracted Rapporteurs, selected based on their experience and expertise in the field of communications. The Rapporteur conducts investigations into matters assigned to them by the President of the Authority that fall within their purview.
