The Ministry entrusted with communications may amend certain provisions of the "license"(*) during its period of validity if such amendment becomes necessary to protect the public interest and the requirements of national defense and public security. If the amendment of the license results in a reduction in the rights granted, the holder of the "license" shall receive appropriate compensation for the resulting loss. The license shall specify the terms and method of awarding such compensation.
