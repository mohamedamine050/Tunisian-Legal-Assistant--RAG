Public telecommunications network operators shall enjoy rights of way that arise when necessary and after authorization for the public interest of the planned works, in accordance with the legislation in force, for the purpose of:

-  Installing, operating, and maintaining connection lines and equipment for public telecommunications networks in public state property and public state property for roads,
-  Installing, operating, and maintaining connection lines and equipment for public telecommunications networks in private property,
-  Installing, operating, and maintaining radio equipment and protecting it from obstacles, electromagnetic disturbances, and other forms of interference.

The application of the provisions of this chapter shall be regulated by order.
