The National Frequency Agency shall perform the following tasks:

- Prepare the national radio frequency plan in coordination with the relevant entities.
- Manage radio frequencies in coordination with the relevant entities.
- Monitor the technical conditions of radio equipment and ensure the protection of radio frequency use.
- Monitor the use of frequencies in accordance with licenses granted and frequency plan registrations.
- Ensure the implementation of international agreements and treaties in the field of radiocommunications.
- Register radio frequencies with the competent international bodies.
- Ensure the protection of national interests in the field of use of registered radio frequencies and orbital positions allocated to the Tunisian Republic.
- Contribute to research, training and study activities related to radiocommunications, and generally any other activity entrusted to it by the supervisory authority and related to its field of intervention.

It is subject to the supervision of the Ministry responsible for Communications.
