The contracted architect, when engaged for a specific task, shall practice his profession within the framework of the **contract**.

The **contract** is a legally binding agreement that obligates the contracting parties and defines their obligations and rights in relation to the specific transaction.

If fully compliant with the terms, the contractor shall practice his profession with the qualifications specified in Article 11 of this Code, unless restricted by specific provisions in the **contract**.
