The architect shall not use their title in any manner that facilitates or guarantees the practice of the profession by a person who is not qualified to practice the profession of architecture. Any violation of this provision shall be deemed a felony, as defined by this law, for offenses punishable by death or imprisonment exceeding five years. 

Offenses punishable by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars, shall be deemed misdemeanors. 

Offenses punishable by imprisonment not exceeding fifteen days or by a fine not exceeding sixty dinars shall be deemed violations. (122, Code of Criminal Procedure) For the purposes of this provision, the architect shall be subject to disciplinary sanctions.  Under no circumstances may an architect sign a project that they did not participate in preparing.  Signing as a courtesy is prohibited.
