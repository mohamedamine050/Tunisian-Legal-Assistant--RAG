Architects or architectural associations may also partner with natural or legal persons engaged in the study of construction projects. Under no circumstances can these associations, which aim to create multidisciplinary teams, enable the unauthorized practice of the profession of architect.

The association 
The association agreement between two or more persons operating permanently to achieve goals, except for profit-making, aims to accomplish a construction project within the scope of the proposed objectives and within the permitted percentage set by the project owner. Architects must inform the Council of the Order of any participation in such an association.
