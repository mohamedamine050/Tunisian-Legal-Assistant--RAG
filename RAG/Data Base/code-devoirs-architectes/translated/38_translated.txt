In the event that an architect is entrusted with a complete task relating to a construction process, the services for which he is responsible are as follows:

a) Architectural design of the work, which includes drawing up the initial general plan and the execution project, and drafting the contract documents. b) Coordination of studies. c) General supervision of execution, which includes management, monitoring, inspection, and coordination of the implementation of various professional bodies and proposals for the payment of expenses.
