The relationships that may give rise to a conflict of personal or professional interest, as mentioned in Article 23 above, are:

a) The relationship of kinship between the architect and a person who professionally participates in an activity aimed at directly or indirectly benefiting from the construction, and this includes the architect's branches, origins, or descendants. 

b) Any legal entity that has a legal personality, its own resources, and a financial capacity independent from the financial capacity of its members or shareholders, whose activity aims to directly benefit from the construction, at least in terms of capital.
