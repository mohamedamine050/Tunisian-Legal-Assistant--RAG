An architect who invents an invention has the right to profit from it lawfully. Therefore, charging for copyright fees does not constitute a violation of the rules of good practice in his profession.

Crimes that warrant the death penalty or imprisonment for more than five years are classified as felonies according to this law.

Crimes that warrant imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or a fine exceeding sixty dinars, are classified as misdemeanors.

Crimes that warrant a penalty not exceeding fifteen days of imprisonment or a fine of sixty dinars are classified as offenses. (Article 122 of the Code of Criminal Procedure)
