**Council of the Body**

The Council of the Body is a regulatory body of a federation, or a designation for a space where a group of people meet to discuss or make decisions.

The Council of Architects is qualified in accordance with Law No. 46 of 1974 dated May 22, 1974 regulating the profession of architect, and specifically Articles 27 and 37 of Chapter VI, to penalize any violation of the rules stipulated in this law.
