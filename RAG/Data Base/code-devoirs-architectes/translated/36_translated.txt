In the event that the project owner is a contractor, developer, or is building for the purpose of sale, the architect may not accept assignments as stipulated in paragraph (a) of Article 38, except in an independent capacity. In such a case, the Council of the Architectural Association shall be entitled to review the contract concluded between the architect and the project owner. In carrying out such assignments, the architect shall consider the interests of those who will acquire the buildings entrusted to him.
