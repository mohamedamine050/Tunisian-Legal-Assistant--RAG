If the architect is rendered incapable of completing his duties due to cancellation, serious illness, or any other reason as defined in Articles 7 and 39, and if his compensation has not been determined by an agreement, the Board of the Institute shall, exceptionally and in all cases with the agreement of the project owner, have the right to assign an architect to continue the services of the prevented architect. In the case of death, the Board of the Institute shall, in conjunction with the architect assigned as prescribed above, be qualified to determine the rights of the deceased architect's heirs and the procedures taken for their benefit.
