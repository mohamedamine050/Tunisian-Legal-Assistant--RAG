The Code of Professional Obligations, without prejudice to the application of the law, sets out the rules relating to the rights and obligations of architects, as well as the rules governing the practice of their profession. It also sets out, on the other hand, the rules governing the relationships between architects and employers, contractors, material suppliers, design offices, consulting engineers, and all those involved in the profession. It further regulates the relationships between architects themselves, and their relationship with the [insert name of regulatory body].
