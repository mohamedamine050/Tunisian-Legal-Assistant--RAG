In the event that the architect advises the project owner to engage the services of a design firm, consulting engineer, or any other party, 

he shall record in a written agreement the nature of such intervention, either with the project owner, the contractor, or with himself, in a manner that predetermines each service, the role of each party, and the boundaries of such tasks and responsibilities of all parties involved. The aforementioned agreement shall also specify the amount of compensation and the method of payment to the design firm, engineer, or any other party.
