## English Translation:

The practice of the profession of architect with the following professions:

1. Contractor for works. 2. Real estate brokers. 3. Trader in building materials. 4. Manufacturer of building materials. 5. Agent acting on behalf of another person in the service of a natural person.  Moreover, the architect must obtain prior authorization from the Tunisian Architects Association before owning more than 15% of the social capital or a number of social shares that make him the owner of the majority of shares in a legal entity practicing the professions referred to in paragraphs 1 to 4 above, or more generally, professions that would generate direct benefits for him from the construction industry.
