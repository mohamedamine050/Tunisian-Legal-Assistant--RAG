Regardless of the legal form adopted for the architectural firm within the association, whether it is an agreement between two or more individuals working permanently to achieve goals, excluding profit generation, it must meet the following conditions:

(a) The share of each partner in the capital must be in a nominal form. 

(b) At least two-thirds of the capital must be owned by architects.

(c) The architectural firm may only be managed by one or more architects.
