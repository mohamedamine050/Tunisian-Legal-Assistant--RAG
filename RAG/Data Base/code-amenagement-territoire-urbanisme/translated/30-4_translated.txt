**(Added by Article 3 of Law No. 29 of 2009 dated June 9, 2009 - )** The detailed planning scheme sets out the locations of buildings, structures, and public or private facilities, the nature and purpose of buildings, and other methods of land use. It also sets out the road network, various networks, and the easements to be respected. The urban regulations contained within the detailed planning scheme may amend or replace the regulations in force prior to its approval. The content of the detailed planning scheme file, as well as the planning and development or improvement program, are determined by a decision of the Minister in charge of urban planning.
