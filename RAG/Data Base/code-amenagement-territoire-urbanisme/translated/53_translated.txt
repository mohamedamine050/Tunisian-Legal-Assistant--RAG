The incorporation of plots or real estate properties designated within the scope of the real estate modification process shall be authenticated based on a technical drawing prepared by the Land Cadastre and Mapping Authority, or by a certified surveyor in accordance with the legislation and regulations in force. This shall be done by a decision of the President of the relevant local public authority.  The technical drawing and the decision referred to in this article are mandatory for inclusion in the land registration procedures and in the procedures for updating land registry fees for registered properties. Unregistered plots and real estate properties must be registered upon their incorporation with registered plots or properties.
