(Added by Article 3 of Law No. 29 of 2009 dated June 9, 2009) - The detailed planning scheme specifies the locations of buildings, structures, and public or private amenities, the nature and purpose of buildings, and other land use methods. It also defines the road network, various networks, and easements that must be respected. The urban regulations included in the detailed planning scheme may amend or replace existing regulations prior to its approval. The content of the detailed planning scheme file, the planning and development or improvement program, shall be determined by a ministerial order of the Minister responsible for urbanization.
