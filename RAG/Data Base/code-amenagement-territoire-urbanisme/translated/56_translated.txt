The Association of Owners may be dissolved by a decision of the Minister in charge of Urban Planning, at the request of the President of the Local Public Authority, in accordance with the reference number of the case, and after hearing the Association of Owners.

The following cases may lead to the dissolution:

1) The Association of Owners exceeding the purpose for which it was formed or the completion of its purpose,

2) Failure to comply with the provisions of this Code,

3) Breach of the model statute.
