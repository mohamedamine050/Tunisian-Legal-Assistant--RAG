The list of sensitive areas and major urban agglomerations requiring the preparation of guiding examples for planning shall be established by decree, at the proposal of the Ministers in charge of Spatial Planning and Urban Development, after consultation with the Minister in charge of Regional Development, the Minister in charge of Agriculture, and the Minister in charge of Heritage regarding cultural and archaeological sites and protected areas. The Ministry in charge of Spatial Planning shall prepare these examples in cooperation with the concerned ministries, after consultation with local public authorities and the relevant public services. These examples shall be approved by decree, at the proposal of the Minister in charge of Spatial Planning.
