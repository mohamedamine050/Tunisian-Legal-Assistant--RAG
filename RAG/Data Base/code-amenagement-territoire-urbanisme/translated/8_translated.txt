Except for the territorial areas referred to in Article 7 of this Code,  guidance examples for development can be prepared for the remaining areas on the initiative of local public authorities or by qualified public stakeholders, after consulting with the local public authorities concerned. The Minister responsible for territorial development shall be notified of any decision to prepare a guidance example for development, and the Minister shall, in turn, provide the local public authority or the public stakeholder concerned with the data deemed relevant for preparing this example. The local public authority concerned or the public stakeholder shall prepare these examples in cooperation with the regional public authorities concerned. These guidance examples for development shall be approved by decision of the Minister responsible for territorial development after consulting with the Ministers responsible for construction, planning, and regional development.
