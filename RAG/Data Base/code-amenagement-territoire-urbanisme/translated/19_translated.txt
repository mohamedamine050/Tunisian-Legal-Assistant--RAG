(Paragraphs 1 and 3 have been repealed and replaced by Law No. 71 of 2005 dated August 4, 2005). - Urban planning schemes are approved by ministerial order upon the proposal of the Minister in charge of urban planning. The approved urban planning scheme is displayed at the municipality or governorate concerned, and the public is informed thereof through the audio-visual and written media. The order approving the urban planning scheme constitutes a declaration of public interest for the planned works. Any person has the right to request a copy of the urban planning scheme for a fee determined in accordance with the applicable legislation.
