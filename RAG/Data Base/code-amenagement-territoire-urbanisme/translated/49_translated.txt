The first general meeting of the owners' associations, after its formation, chaired by the president of the local municipality or his delegate, shall be responsible for electing a committee. 

The committee shall be constituted by a formal method of bringing together individuals with relevant experience from all parts of the association. The committee shall consist of eight members tasked with representing the association for a period of two years, renewable. This election shall be by a majority of the owners concerned, provided that at least half of their number are present at the meeting. In the event of the absence of a majority of the owners, the appointment of the members of the association committee shall be made by a majority of the owners present after a subsequent general meeting, whose date shall be set at least fifteen days and at most one month after the first meeting.
