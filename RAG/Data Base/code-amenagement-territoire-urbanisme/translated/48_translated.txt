The Mayor of the concerned local municipality, in accordance with the jurisdiction, shall, prior to proposing the formation of the syndicate, summon the concerned owners to a general information session by means of personal summons or announcements. The Mayor of the concerned local municipality shall, after the formation of the syndicate pursuant to the procedures set forth in Article 45 of this Code, summon the owners of land and properties located within the syndicate's area, or their representatives in the case of minors and absentees, to public sessions whenever necessary. This shall be done through personal registered letters and announcements.
