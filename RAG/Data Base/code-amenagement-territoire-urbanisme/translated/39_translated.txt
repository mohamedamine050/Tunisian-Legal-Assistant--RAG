All owners of properties located within the intervention area that were not subject to expropriation or whose owners did not participate in the project within the scope of the agreement mentioned in Article 36 of this Code, as well as owners of properties bordering the intervention area who benefit from the infrastructure and collective facilities that were concentrated as part of the project, are obligated to contribute to the cost of completing the project. The terms, forms, and amount of this contribution shall be determined by an order issued upon the proposal of the Minister in charge of Urban Planning, after obtaining the opinions of the Ministers of Finance, State Property, and Real Estate Affairs.
