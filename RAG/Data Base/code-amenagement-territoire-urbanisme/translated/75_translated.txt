**(Repealed and replaced by the first section of Law No. 78 of 2003 dated December 29, 2003).** - The owner, or the lessee who has obtained written consent from the owner, shall not be permitted to change the character of a dwelling or any part or accessory thereof into a workshop, office, commercial establishment, or any other activity differing from its original character, except by a prior license issued by the same administrative authority empowered to grant building permits and according to the same procedures followed for obtaining such permits.
