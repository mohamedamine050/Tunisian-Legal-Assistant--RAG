Any owner or real estate developer, being a natural or legal person who, for the purpose of sale or lease, habitually or professionally carries out the following operations: - subdivision and development of land primarily intended for housing - construction and renovation of individual, semi-collective, or collective properties intended for housing, commerce, profession, or administration, who carries out an unapproved subdivision or sells plots therefrom shall be punished by imprisonment for a term of between 16 days and 3 months and a fine of between 500 dinars and 20,000 dinars, or by one of these penalties only. In case of recidivism, imprisonment shall be mandatory.
