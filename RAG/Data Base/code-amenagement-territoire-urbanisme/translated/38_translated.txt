The traders located in the buildings acquired by the intervener by agreement or expropriation for demolition or rehabilitation shall benefit from the right of priority in assigning them premises of the same nature in the buildings under construction, in accordance with the conditions stipulated in Articles 10 and 11 of Law No. 37 of 1977 dated May 25, 1977, governing the relations between lessors and lessees with regard to the renewal of leases of real estate or premises used for commercial, industrial or artisanal purposes.
