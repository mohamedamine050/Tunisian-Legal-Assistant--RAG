Any owner or real estate developer, whether a natural or legal person, who habitually or professionally carries out the following operations for the purpose of sale or lease: - subdivision and development of land primarily intended for residential use - construction and renovation of individual, semi-collective or collective buildings intended for housing, commerce, profession or administration, who has not completed the development work in whole or in part, or who has not completed it in accordance with the technical specifications approved by the competent authority and within the terms of the specifications, shall be punished by a fine ranging from 500 dinars to 50,000 dinars.
