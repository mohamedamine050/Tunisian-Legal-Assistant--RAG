It is not permissible to construct on properties located within the areas defined pursuant to Article 20 of this Law if they are vacant, nor to alter them for the purpose of improving their facilities if they are built. However, it is permissible to plant trees on vacant land located within these areas, as well as to repair and maintain existing buildings, subject to a special permit from the relevant administrative authority. In no event shall buildings and repair or alteration works carried out be considered a violation of the provisions of the first paragraph above when assessing the fine for the expropriation of the land on which these buildings are built or the expropriation of the repair or alteration works.
