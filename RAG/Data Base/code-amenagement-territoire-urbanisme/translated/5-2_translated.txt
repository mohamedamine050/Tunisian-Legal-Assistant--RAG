Added by Article 2 of Law No. 78 of 2003 (December 2003), large commercial areas whose base area at the time of establishment or subsequent expansion exceeds 3,000 square meters or whose sales area exceeds 1,500 square meters may not be established except outside the boundaries of areas covered by urban development plans. This applies at a distance of five kilometers or more from the boundaries of these areas. 

The provisions of the first paragraph of this Article may not be contravened except for objective considerations related specifically to the requirements of urban planning regulation, pursuant to a proposal submitted by the Minister responsible for Trade and the Minister responsible for Land Planning and Urban Development. (The second paragraph was repealed and replaced by Law No. 9 of 2009 dated February 16, 2009).
