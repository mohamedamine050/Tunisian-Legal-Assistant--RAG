"Real Estate Intervention Areas" refers to areas within which the State, local public authorities, or real estate agencies established by Law No. 21 of 1973 dated April 14, 1973, and the Urban Development and Renewal Agency established by Law No. 69 of 1981 dated August 1, 1981, intervene to execute programs for the development and equipping, renewal, or improvement of areas, as defined by the competent authorities according to the urban development plan or, if applicable, the guidelines.
