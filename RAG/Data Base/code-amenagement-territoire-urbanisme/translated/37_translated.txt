If the intervention program involves demolition or renovation of buildings, the intervener entrusted with carrying out the project must provide the residents occupying those buildings under lease agreements or in good faith with compensation equivalent to four years' rent for those benefiting from the right to remain, as stipulated in the legislation in force, or with compensation determined by mutual agreement or by litigation for those who do not benefit from this right, provided that the compensation does not exceed one year's rent.
