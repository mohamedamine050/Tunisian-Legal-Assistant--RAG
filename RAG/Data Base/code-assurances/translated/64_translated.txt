The insured, subscribers to the contracts, beneficiaries, and creditors are notified of any transfer, merger, or absorption operations by an announcement published at the initiative of the concerned institution in the Official Gazette of the Tunisian Republic and in at least two daily newspapers. The parties concerned are given a period of at least three months, starting from the date of publication of the announcement in the Official Gazette of the Tunisian Republic, to submit their observations. Upon the approval of the Minister of Finance of the transfer, merger, or absorption operations, after the expiration of the aforementioned period, the right to object to these operations is granted to the insured, subscribers to the contracts, beneficiaries, and creditors.
