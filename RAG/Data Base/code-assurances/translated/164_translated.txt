The amicable settlement offer must be in the form of an advance payment if the insurer does not receive evidence of the injured party's complete healing or recovery within four months of the date of the amicable settlement request.  The advance payment shall not be less than the amount of medical expenses and compensation for temporary disability. This advance payment shall be disbursed within fifteen days of the insurer's receipt of the report indicating the impossibility of determining the final disability percentage. The final offer shall be submitted within two months of the insurer's knowledge of the complete healing or recovery.
