Insurance institutions are obligated to submit to the "General Insurance Authority" all agreements concluded between them or within their professional associations concerning the tariff or general conditions of insurance contracts, competition, or financial transactions. The agreement shall be implemented if it is not opposed by the "General Insurance Authority" within a period of two months from the date of notification. The aforementioned authority may, however, suspend the implementation of the agreement after the expiry of this period. Agreements concluded within the framework of professional associations are binding on their members.
