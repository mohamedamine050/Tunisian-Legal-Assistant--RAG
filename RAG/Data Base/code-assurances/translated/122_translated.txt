Compensation for victims of road accidents for damages resulting from personal injuries and their consequences shall be awarded without the possibility of objection based on their own fault, except in cases where they intentionally cause the damage [1] 
[1] Harm that occurs in violation of the law and affects a person's body, mental or psychological health, honor, property, or reputation [2] loss or damage caused to property, deprivation of comfort, harm to physical well-being, reputation, or the like - in crimes and quasi-crimes, damage is the loss actually suffered by the claimant and the necessary expenses that they were or will be forced to incur to remedy the consequences of the act that was committed to their detriment, as well as what they were deprived of in the normal range of the consequences of this act
by themselves or by gross negligence that cannot be justified.
