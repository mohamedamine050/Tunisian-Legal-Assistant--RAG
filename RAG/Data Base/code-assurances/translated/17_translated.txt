It may be stipulated in the contract that if it is determined by expert appraisal that the value of the insured object exceeds the amount of insurance coverage on the date of the incident, the insured shall bear the excess amount exceeding the insured value. 

The insured shall bear a proportionate share of the damage upon the partial destruction of the insured object. 

Should this rule be stipulated in the contract, a memorandum explaining its content shall be provided to the insured along with the contract document.
