The Takaful insurance institution must manage Takaful insurance operations based on a contract.

The contract is a legally binding agreement that obligates the contracting parties and defines their obligations and rights concerning the specific transaction.

The agency and management of the investment of membership fees shall be based on a contract.

The contract is a legally binding agreement that obligates the contracting parties and defines their obligations and rights concerning the specific transaction.

The Mudarabah, as defined in the Code of Obligations and Contracts, shall apply.  The Takaful insurance institution shall receive, in return, an agency commission applied to membership fees, acting as an agent, and a Mudarabah commission calculated as a percentage of the investment returns, acting as a Mudarib. The amount of these commissions shall be specified in the specific terms of the insurance contracts.
