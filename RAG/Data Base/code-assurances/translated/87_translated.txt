## English Translation:

**Institutions subject to licensing in the case of breach of obligations imposed upon them under the provisions of this Code shall be subject to the following penalties and procedures:**

**1) Penalties and procedures taken by the “General Insurance Authority”:**

- Notice
- Reprimand
- Placing the company under supervision to implement a corrective program.
The Board of Directors of the concerned institution shall be informed of the penalties and procedures taken. 

**2) Penalties and procedures taken by the Minister of Finance “on the proposal of the Authority”:**

- Withdrawal of the license in accordance with the provisions of Chapter 51 of this Code.
- Enactment of mandatory transfer in accordance with the provisions of Chapter 63 of this Code.
