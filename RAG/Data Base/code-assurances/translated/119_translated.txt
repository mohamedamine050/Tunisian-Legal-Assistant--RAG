The insurer may not oppose victims of traffic accidents or their beneficiaries in the event of death: 
a) by invoking the condition for reducing the amount of compensation as applied pursuant to the provisions of the fifth paragraph of Article 8 of this Code.
b) by invoking any instances of the right being extinguished.
In the cases mentioned in paragraphs a and b of this Article, the insurer shall pay the compensation to the beneficiaries on behalf of the insured, and may bring an action against the insured to recover the amounts paid on his behalf.
