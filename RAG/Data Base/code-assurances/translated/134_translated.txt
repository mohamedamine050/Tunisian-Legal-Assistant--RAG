Compensation for damage shall be calculated based on the following:

**[1] Harm caused by a violation of the law affecting a person's body, mental or psychological health, honor, property, or reputation.**
**[2] Loss or damage to property, deprivation of comfort, or harm to physical well-being, reputation, or other similar matters.**

- In crimes and quasi-crimes, damage refers to the actual loss suffered by the claimant, the necessary expenses they have incurred or will have to incur to remedy the consequences of the act that caused them harm, as well as the loss of benefits within the normal scope of the consequences of this act.
