Insurance supervisors, sworn experts and accredited persons employed by insurance institutions, reinsurance institutions, and those holding professional cards that identify them, shall carry out the supervision referred to in Article 82 of this Code. 

Insurance institutions, reinsurance institutions, intermediaries, experts, and loss adjusters shall provide the supervisors with all documents and information requested by them within the scope of their duties. In this context, entities subject to supervision cannot invoke professional secrecy with respect to the supervisors. 

These supervisors may review, at any time and on-site, the operations carried out by insurance institutions, reinsurance institutions, intermediaries, experts, and loss adjusters.
