The Takaful insurance institution shall distribute the surplus among the subscribers according to the method determined after obtaining the opinion of the Sharia Supervisory Board.  The insurance surplus means the difference between the total net subscription fees after cancellations, and their investment income and all other income on the one hand, and the total amount of compensations paid, the technical reserves, the provisions, and the shareholders' share for the management of both Takaful insurance operations and investment activities, and all expenses related to the subscribers' fund on the other hand.  The Takaful insurance institution may not distribute profits to shareholders from any surplus achieved by the subscribers' fund accounts.
