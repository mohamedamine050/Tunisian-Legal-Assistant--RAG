The mandatory nature of insurance pursuant to Article 110 of this Code applies to all motorized land vehicles and their trailers, except for vehicles owned by the State and vehicles operating on railways. This insurance contract obligates the insurer to pay the insured or the beneficiary a sum of money, a regular income, or any other monetary compensation in the event of the occurrence of the insured event, in exchange for premiums or other financial payments made by the insured to the insurer.
