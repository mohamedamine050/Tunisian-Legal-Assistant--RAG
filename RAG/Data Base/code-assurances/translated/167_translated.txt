Social Security is a right guaranteed by society, promoted by the state, and protected by law.  It aims to protect citizens in cases of old age, disability, illness, work-related injuries, occupational diseases, loss of a breadwinner, unemployment, loss of livelihood, pregnancy, childbirth, assistance in bearing family burdens, and in cases of disasters, emergencies, and death.
