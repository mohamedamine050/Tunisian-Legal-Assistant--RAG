Insurance companies and reinsurance companies shall inform the Minister of Finance of any appointment they intend to make to their Board of Directors, Supervisory Board, Collective Management Body, Legal Audit Body, or Chief Executives, providing a detailed statement of their qualifications and experience. (Amended by Section 2 of Law No. 47 of 2014 dated July 24, 2014). The Minister of Finance may object to such appointment if he finds that any of the persons mentioned in the previous paragraph lacks the necessary competence or experience. This objection must be made within a maximum period of one month from the date of notification. The notification files are submitted to the body responsible for their examination and submission to the Minister of Finance, which may request any clarifications or documents deemed necessary for this purpose.
