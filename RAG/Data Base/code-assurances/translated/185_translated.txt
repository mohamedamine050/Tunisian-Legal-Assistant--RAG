The Chairperson of the Authority shall exercise their duties on a full-time basis and may not be a member of the board of directors of a company subject to the Authority's supervision, nor its general manager, assistant general manager, chairperson of a collective management body, or a member of a collective management body.  Furthermore, the Chairperson of the Authority's Council may not manage a company subject to the Authority's supervision for a period of three years after the termination of their duties with the Authority, except with the authorization of the Minister of Finance.
