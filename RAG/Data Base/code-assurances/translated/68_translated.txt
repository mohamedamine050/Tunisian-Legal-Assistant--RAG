The institutions mentioned in Article 67 of this Code, as well as reinsurance institutions, which do not have their registered office in Tunisia and whose articles of association have been previously approved, shall submit to the Minister of Finance for approval a special representative who shall be entrusted with the management of all operations they intend to carry out in Tunisia. "The Minister of Finance shall give his consent on the basis of a report from the body responsible for informing those concerned." (The second paragraph was added by Article 4 of Law No. 8 of 2008 dated February 13, 2008)
