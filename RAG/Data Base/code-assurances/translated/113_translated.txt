## English Translation:

**It is imposed on any insurance institution that refuses to provide civil liability insurance** for the material aspects of civilization, such as infrastructure, communication, and entertainment, **which corresponds to the intellectual, spiritual, and ethical aspects of civilization**, **a penalty or procedure as prescribed by Article 87 of the Insurance Code,** or a fine **of five thousand to thirty thousand dinars**. 

**The collection**, **which is the process by which the creditor obtains payment of the financial debt owed to him by the debtor**, **of the fines provided for in this article is carried out by a compulsory order issued by the Minister of Finance on the proposal of the Authority.** (The second paragraph was added by Article 4 of Law No. 8 of 2008 dated February 13, 2008).
