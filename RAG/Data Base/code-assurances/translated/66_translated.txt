It carries on the principles of insurance institutions. 

Insurance Contract

The contract is a valid legal agreement that binds the contracting parties and determines their obligations and rights concerning the specific transaction. Under this contract, the insurer undertakes to pay the insured or the beneficiary a sum of money, a periodic income, or any other financial compensation in the event of the occurrence of the insured event, in exchange for premiums or any other financial payment made by the insured to the insurer.

A general privilege is allocated, according to priority, to the beneficiaries of insurance contracts.

Insurance Contract

The contract is a valid legal agreement that binds the contracting parties and determines their obligations and rights concerning the specific transaction. Under this contract, the insurer undertakes to pay the insured or the beneficiary a sum of money, a periodic income, or any other financial compensation in the event of the occurrence of the insured event, in exchange for premiums or any other financial payment made by the insured to the insurer.

This privilege applies first to life insurance beneficiaries, within the limits of the assets allocated to such contracts, followed by beneficiaries of non-life insurance contracts. (Amended by the first section of Law No. 37 of 2002 dated April 1, 2002). This privilege takes precedence over the general privilege of the Treasury, contrary to Article 199 of the Civil Code.
