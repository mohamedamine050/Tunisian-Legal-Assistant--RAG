The services of the Authority consist of the administrative and technical structures defined in the organizational chart stipulated in Article 190 of this Code.  These services undertake the tasks of writing, following up on the Authority's affairs, preparing files, reviewing them, preparing studies, and carrying out tasks assigned to them by the President of the Authority and its Council, in accordance with the provisions of Article 187 of this Code. They are responsible for preserving the documents, files, and registers of the Authority, and those deposited with or sent to them legally. The President of the Authority has authority over all the Authority's staff; he is responsible for their recruitment and appointment to various positions, as well as their dismissal from service, in accordance with the applicable legislation.
