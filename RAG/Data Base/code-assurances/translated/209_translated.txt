The Takaful insurance institution must open separate financial and accounting accounts as follows: - An account specifically for the investment of the shareholders' capital in the insurance institution.  An insurance contract whereby the insurer undertakes to pay the insured or the beneficiary a sum of money, a regular income, or any other financial compensation in the event of the occurrence of the insured event, in return for premiums or any other financial payment made by the insured to the Takaful insurer. - An account, or several accounts according to the types of insurance, specifically for the subscribers' fund, into which subscription fees and their investment returns are deposited, and from which compensations are paid.
