In addition to the provisions of existing legislation, the "Minister of Finance, upon the proposal of the Authority," may, if the actions of the managers lead to a situation where the institutions they manage are unable to meet their commitments or are in full violation of the obligations imposed upon them pursuant to the current regulations, administrative decisions of a general nature and not related to a specific person, request the  "Urgent Court"  to replace the management structures of the institution with a temporary administrator, who shall have full powers to manage, preserve and dispose of the institution's assets until the correction process is implemented.
