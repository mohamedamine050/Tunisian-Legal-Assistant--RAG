If the victim dies as a result of a traffic accident, compensation is granted under the heading of damages.

**Damages**
[1] Harm caused in violation of the law that affects a person's body, mental or psychological health, reputation, property, or honor. [2] Loss or damage inflicted on property, deprivation of comfort, harm to physical well-being, reputation, or the like. - Damages in crimes and quasi-crimes are the actual loss suffered by the plaintiff, the necessary expenses that they have been or will be forced to incur to repair the consequences of the act that caused them harm, as well as the deprivation of profit within the normal limits of the consequences of this act.

**Economic compensation** shall be awarded to the surviving spouse and the divorced spouse receiving an old-age pension in accordance with the provisions of Article 31 of the Personal Status Code, as well as to children, parents, and grandchildren, subject to the following conditions:

* **Surviving Spouse:** For life, unless they remarry.
* **Parents:** For life, on the condition of **actual and continuous financial support**.
* **Children and Grandchildren:**
    * Until the age of twenty, without any condition.
    * Until the end of their studies, not exceeding the age of twenty-five.

* **Disabled individuals unable to work:** Without age limit.
* **Daughters:** Until they are able to support themselves or marry.
