The Authority shall cooperate with all authorities responsible for supervising and monitoring the financial sector, and may, for this purpose, propose concluding agreements with them relating in particular to: - the exchange of information and expertise; - the organization of training programs; - the undertaking of joint monitoring operations.  Within the framework of exercising its duties, the Authority may cooperate with similar foreign institutions and bodies, or those exercising powers similar to its own, and conclude agreements with them after the approval of the competent authorities.  The Authority may also exchange information with authorities responsible for competition within the scope of the exercise of each party's duties. The exchanged information shall be subject to professional secrecy.
