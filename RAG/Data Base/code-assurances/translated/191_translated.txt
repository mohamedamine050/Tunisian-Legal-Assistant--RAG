The Disciplinary Committee shall be composed of the following members: a judge of the third rank, as Chairperson; a counselor from the Administrative Court, as member; a representative of the Ministry of Finance, as member; and a member with expertise in insurance, as member.  The expertise shall cover scientific, technical, and other matters necessary for adjudicating the judicial case, excluding legal matters.  A representative appointed by the group to act on its behalf, and from the General Delegate of the professional association of insurance companies, or his/her legally designated substitute from among the association's employees, shall also be members. The Committee's member with expertise, whose expertise shall cover scientific, technical, and other matters necessary for adjudicating the judicial case, excluding legal matters, shall be appointed by the Committee's council upon the proposal of the council's chairperson.  The Committee is a formal method for assembling individuals with relevant expertise from various parts of the institution.  An insurance contract is an agreement in which the insurer undertakes to pay the insured or the beneficiary a sum of money, a periodic income, or any other financial compensation in the event of the occurrence of the insured event, in exchange for premiums or any other financial payment made by the insured to the insurer.
