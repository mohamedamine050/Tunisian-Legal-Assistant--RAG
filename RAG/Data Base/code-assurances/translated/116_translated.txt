The police officers and national guard officers in charge of road and traffic policing, as well as the judicial police officers concerned, shall jointly examine any violation described as felonies within the meaning of this law, which are crimes punishable by death or imprisonment exceeding five years.
Crimes punishable by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars, shall be described as misdemeanors. 
Crimes punishable by imprisonment not exceeding fifteen days or by a fine of sixty dinars shall be described as offenses. [Article 122, Code of Criminal Procedure] 
The provisions of Article 110 of this Code shall apply.
