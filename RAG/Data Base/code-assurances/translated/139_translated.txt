The insurer shall notify the injured party, at the latest within one month from the date of receipt of the amicable settlement request, of the need to undergo a medical examination by a doctor appointed from the list referred to in Article 138 above, at least fifteen days before the date of the examination. The insurer shall also inform the injured party of the name of the expert doctor, the subject matter of the examination, its date, and its location, by registered letter with acknowledgment of receipt or by any other means leaving a written trace. The insurer shall bear the cost of the expert appointed by it. The insurer shall also inform the injured party that they may consult with a doctor at their own expense. The insurer shall inform the injured party that the amicable settlement offer may be made in the form of an advance payment, in accordance with the provisions of Article 164 of this Code.
