The body shall be tasked, within the scope of its assigned duties, specifically with: 1. Overseeing insurance institutions; insurance being a contract whereby the insurer undertakes to pay the insured or the beneficiary a sum of money, a periodic income, or any other financial compensation in the event of the occurrence of the insured event, in exchange for premiums or any other financial payment made by the insured to the insurer; and reinsurance institutions; reinsurance being a contract whereby the insurer undertakes to pay the insured or the beneficiary a sum of money, a periodic income, or any other financial compensation in the event of the occurrence of the insured event, in exchange for premiums or any other financial payment made by the insured to the insurer; and professions related to the insurance sector; insurance being a contract whereby the insurer undertakes to pay the insured or the beneficiary a sum of money, a periodic income, or any other financial compensation in the event of the occurrence of the insured event, in exchange for premiums or any other financial payment made by the insured to the insurer; and by monitoring their activities. 2. Studying legislative, regulatory, and organizational matters related to insurance operations; insurance being a contract whereby the insurer undertakes to pay the insured or the beneficiary a sum of money, a periodic income, or any other financial compensation in the event of the occurrence of the insured event, in exchange for premiums or any other financial payment made by the insured to the insurer; and reinsurance operations; reinsurance being a contract whereby the insurer undertakes to pay the insured or the beneficiary a sum of money, a periodic income, or any other financial compensation in the event of the occurrence of the insured event, in exchange for premiums or any other financial payment made by the insured to the insurer; and insurance institutions; insurance being a contract whereby the insurer undertakes to pay the insured or the beneficiary a sum of money, a periodic income, or any other financial compensation in the event of the occurrence of the insured event, in exchange for premiums or any other financial payment made by the insured to the insurer; and reinsurance institutions; reinsurance being a contract whereby the insurer undertakes to pay the insured or the beneficiary a sum of money, a periodic income, or any other financial compensation in the event of the occurrence of the insured event, in exchange for premiums or any other financial payment made by the insured to the insurer; submitted by the Minister of Finance, and preparing related texts at his request. 3. Studying all technical and economic matters related to the development and regulation of the insurance sector and submitting proposals thereon to the Minister of Finance. 4. Generally considering any other matters falling within the purview of the body and expressing an opinion thereon. The body may be assigned to represent the state in institutions, bodies, structures, and funds related to insurance.
