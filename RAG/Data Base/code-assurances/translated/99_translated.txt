**Insurance**

Insurance is a contract whereby the insurer undertakes to pay the insured or the beneficiary a sum of money, a regular income, or any other financial compensation in the event of the occurrence of the insured event, in exchange for premiums or any other financial payment made by the insured to the insurer.

**Mandatory Liability Insurance in the Field of Construction:**

* **The state, local public authorities, public institutions with an administrative character, and public facilities as defined by Law No. 9 of 1989 dated February 1, 1989, are required to have this insurance when they undertake construction for their own benefit without resorting to third parties.**
* **Natural persons undertaking the construction of a dwelling, with or without resorting to third parties, are required to have this insurance when the dwelling is to be occupied by the person themselves, their spouse, their ascendants, their spouse’s ascendants, their descendants, or their spouse’s descendants.**
* **Natural or legal persons owning establishments listed in a decree proposed by the Ministers of Finance, Equipment, and Housing, are required to have this insurance.**
