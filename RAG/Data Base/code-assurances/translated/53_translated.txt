Insurance institutions, in order to obtain a license, must be subject to Tunisian legislation and be established in one of the following forms: a limited liability company, a cooperative company, or a cooperative agricultural fund constituted in accordance with the special regulations governing it.  "Reinsurance institutions cannot operate unless they are formed in one of the forms mentioned above" (added by Article 3 of Law No. 37 of 2002 dated April 1, 2002).
