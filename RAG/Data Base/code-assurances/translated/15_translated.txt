The time limit for the lapse of the lawsuit is interrupted by:

[1] One party requesting their rights from another in the presence of a judge; the requester is called the plaintiff and the party from whom the request is made is called the defendant. [2] The lawsuit is the right of any claimant to bring their claim before the judiciary for a judgment in their favor. It is the right of the defendant to present arguments of defense or pleas aimed at refuting the claim.

**The passage of time due to:**

* The usual reasons for interruption,
* The appointment of an expert after an incident occurs,
* The delivery of a registered letter with notification of receipt from the insurer to the insured regarding a claim for the payment of an insurance premium,
* Or from the insured to the insurer regarding a claim for compensation.

**Insurance:**

An insurance contract whereby the insurer undertakes to pay the insured or the beneficiary a sum of money, a fixed income, or any other financial compensation in the event of the occurrence of the insured event, in exchange for premiums or any other financial payment made by the insured to the insurer.
