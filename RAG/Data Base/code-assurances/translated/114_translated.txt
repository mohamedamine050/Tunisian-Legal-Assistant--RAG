## English Translation:

**This chapter's provisions shall be applied to users of motorized land vehicles not registered in any of the recognized registration chains in Tunisia. This includes the requirements for drafting documents proving the existence of an insurance contract.**

**Insurance Contract**

**Contract**

The contract is a binding legal agreement that obliges the contracting parties and defines their obligations and rights regarding the specific transaction. 

The insurer undertakes to pay the insured or the beneficiary a sum of money, a periodic income, or any other financial compensation in case of the occurrence of the insured event, in exchange for premiums or any other financial payment made by the insured to the insurer.
