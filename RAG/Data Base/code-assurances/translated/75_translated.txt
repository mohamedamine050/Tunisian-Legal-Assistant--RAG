The “General Insurance Authority” shall withdraw the professional card and strike the name of its holder from the register mentioned in Article 70 of this Code after obtaining the opinion of the Committee referred to in Article 71 thereof, which shall obligatorily hear the interested party, in the following cases:

1. When one of the conditions stipulated in Article 73 of this Code is no longer met.
2. When the activity is permanently discontinued.
3. When the violation is characterized as a felony, within the meaning of this law, crimes punishable by death or imprisonment for more than five years. 
Crimes punishable by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars are described as misdemeanors. 
Crimes punishable by imprisonment not exceeding fifteen days or a fine of sixty dinars are described as offenses.
