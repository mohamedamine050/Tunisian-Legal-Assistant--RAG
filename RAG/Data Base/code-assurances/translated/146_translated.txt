**Compensation is granted to the spouse, children, and parents on the grounds of damages.**

**Damages** [1] Injury caused by a violation of the law affecting a person's body, mental or psychological health, reputation, property, or honor. [2] Loss or damage inflicted on property, deprivation of comfort, harm to physical well-being, reputation, or similar. - In crimes and offenses similar to crimes, damages refer to the actual loss suffered by the claimant and the necessary expenses incurred or to be incurred to repair the consequences of the act committed against them, as well as any benefits they were deprived of within the normal scope of the consequences of this act.

**Moral damages** incurred as a result of death are granted to the following:

- **Spouse:** Two and a half times the minimum guaranteed annual wage for a forty-hour workweek.
- **Children:** Twice the minimum guaranteed annual wage for a forty-hour workweek for each child, up to a total amount not exceeding six times this wage, to be distributed equally among them.
- **Parents:** Twice the minimum guaranteed annual wage for a forty-hour workweek for each parent.

This compensation is paid in a single installment.
