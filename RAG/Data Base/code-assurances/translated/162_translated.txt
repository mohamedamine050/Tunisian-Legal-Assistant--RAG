In the event of a request for amicable settlement, no legal action may be brought for compensation for damages sustained by persons in traffic accidents except in the following cases:  In the event that no amicable settlement offer is submitted after fifteen days from the deadlines stipulated in Articles 148 to 156 of this Code.  In the event that an amicable settlement offer is submitted without reaching an amicable settlement agreement within the legal timeframes. The insurer remains obligated to continue applying amicable settlement procedures if these deadlines expire without the submission of an offer.
