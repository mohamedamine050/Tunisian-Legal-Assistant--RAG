The appointment of the Chairperson of the Authority shall be by order, proposed by the Minister of Finance.  The Chairperson must be competent in the economic or financial field.

The appointment of the members of the Authority's Council shall be by order, proposed by the Minister of Finance, following nomination by the relevant authorities. The term of membership on the Authority's Council is five years, renewable once.

The Chairperson and members must be Tunisian nationals, enjoying their civil rights, and must meet the conditions stipulated in Article 85 of this Code. The Chairperson and members of the Authority's Council may not hold any direct or indirect shareholding in the capital of an institution operating in insurance or reinsurance, or both.
