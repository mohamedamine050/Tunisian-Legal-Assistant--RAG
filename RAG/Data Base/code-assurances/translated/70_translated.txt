Except for banking institutions, microfinance institutions, and the National Post Office, the persons referred to in Article 69 of this Code must prove that they hold a professional card and are registered in the register maintained by the General Insurance Authority for this purpose, in order for them to be able to offer insurance operations. (The first paragraph was amended by Article 9 of Decree No. 117 of 2011 dated November 5, 2011). The register referred to in the preceding paragraph of this Article is divided into three sections classified according to the types of intermediaries, and these sections are: - Section 1: Insurance brokers and brokerage firms. - Section 2: Insurance agents and insurance agency companies. - Section 3: Life insurance producers.
