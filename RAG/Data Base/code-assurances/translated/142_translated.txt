The expert physician shall deliver a copy of his report to both the insurer and the injured party, as well as to the physician consulted by the latter, within a maximum period of fifteen days from the date of the examination. This deadline may be extended once by the same period upon a reasoned request from the expert physician addressed to the insurer. In the event that the report is not submitted within the deadline stipulated in the first paragraph of this section, another physician shall be appointed by order upon a petition submitted by the most diligent party.
