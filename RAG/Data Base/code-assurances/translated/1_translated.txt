##  Insurance Contract

**Contract**

This contract is a legally binding agreement that obliges the contracting parties and defines their obligations and rights concerning the specific transaction.

**Insurance**

By this contract, the insurer undertakes to pay the insured or the beneficiary a sum of money, a periodic income, or any other financial compensation in the event of the occurrence of the insured event, in exchange for premiums or any other financial payment made by the insured to the insurer.

**Insurance**

It is the agreement by which an insurance company or the insurer undertakes to provide a financial service to a person called the insured in the event of the realization of the risk or the arrival of the term specified in the contract, in exchange for a fee called a premium or subscription fee.
