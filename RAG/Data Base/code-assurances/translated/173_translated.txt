The injured party, or those who inherit their rights upon death, if the person responsible for the accident is unknown or uninsured, must address their claim for compensation to the Guarantee Fund for Victims of Road Accidents by registered letter with acknowledgment of receipt, or by any other means leaving a written trace. This must be done within a period not exceeding three years from the date of knowledge of the lack of insurance.

An insurance contract is one in which the insurer undertakes to pay the insured or the beneficiary a sum of money, a periodic income, or any other financial compensation in the event of the occurrence of the insured event, in exchange for premiums or any other financial payment made by the insured to the insurer. Otherwise, their right shall lapse.  They must prove that they are Tunisian nationals;

Nationality is a legal and political link between an individual and a specific state, which includes this individual among its constituent population.  This link therefore establishes reciprocal rights and obligations between them.

Or that they are resident in the Tunisian Republic, or that they are a national of a state that has concluded a reciprocal treatment agreement with the Tunisian Republic and meet the conditions stipulated in that agreement.
