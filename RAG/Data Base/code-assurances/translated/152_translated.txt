In the event that the injured party or their successors-in-interest upon death submit a request for amicable settlement before the insurer receives the investigation report, the applicant for amicable settlement shall be invited to provide the investigation report. The calculation of the time limit for submitting the offer stipulated in Article 148 of this Code shall be suspended until the insurer receives the investigation report stipulated in Article 167 of this Code or until it is provided by the injured party or their successors-in-interest upon death.
