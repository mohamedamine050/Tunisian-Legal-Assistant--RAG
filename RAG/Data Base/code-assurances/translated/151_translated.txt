The injured party or their successors in interest upon death may not initiate legal proceedings except against the insurer obligated to submit a settlement offer, in accordance with the provisions of the agreement referred to in Article 149 of this Code. Disputes arising between the insurer obligated to submit a settlement offer and the insurer responsible for the accident, excluding the State, shall be subject to arbitration in accordance with the terms and procedures set forth in the agreement referred to in the first paragraph of this Article.
