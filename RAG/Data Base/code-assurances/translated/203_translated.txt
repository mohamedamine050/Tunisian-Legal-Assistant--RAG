The entire portfolio of a Takaful insurance institution or part thereof may only be transferred to another Takaful insurance institution.  The merger or acquisition of a Takaful insurance institution is only permissible by another Takaful insurance institution.

A contract whereby the insurer undertakes to pay the insured or the beneficiary a sum of money, a periodic income, or any other financial compensation in the event of the occurrence of the insured event, in consideration of premiums or any other financial payment made by the insured to the insurer.

The transfer, merger, or acquisition process is subject to the approval of the Minister of Finance, based on a report from the authority responsible for informing those concerned.
