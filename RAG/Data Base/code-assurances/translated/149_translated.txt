In the event of multiple insurers for the vehicles or trailers involved in the accident, and upon the filing of a request for amicable settlement by the victim or their legal successors in case of death, the amicable settlement offer shall be made by one of the insurers, with the exception of the State and the Guarantee Fund for Victims of Road Accidents, pursuant to a subrogation agreement that shall be concluded obligatorily by the parties concerned and approved by a decision of the Minister of Finance. The insurer obligated to submit the amicable settlement offer shall have the right to claim reimbursement from the insurer of the party responsible for the accident or the insured party exempted from coverage, within the limits of the amounts paid, according to the percentages of liability determined in accordance with the liability determination table referred to in Article 123 of this Code.
