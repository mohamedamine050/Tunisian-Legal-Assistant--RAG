The name, surname, and identification number of the professional card holder at the institution who enters into the insurance contract must be included.

The contract is a legally binding agreement that binds the contracting parties and defines their obligations and rights in relation to the specific transaction. Under this contract, the insurer undertakes to pay the insured or the beneficiary a sum of money, a periodic income, or any other financial compensation in the event of the occurrence of the insured event, in return for premiums or any other financial payment made by the insured to the insurer. This is also applicable within any other document that serves the same purpose.
