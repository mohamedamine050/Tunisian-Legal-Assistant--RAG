Compensation for damages resulting from traffic accidents, as per the meaning of this section, includes: 

* **Treatment expenses** incurred as a result of the accident.
* **Loss of income** during the period of temporary disability from work.
* **Physical damage**, **professional damage**, **moral damage**, and **aesthetic damage**, and expenses for hiring another person as a result of permanent disability.
* **Economic damage**, **moral damage**, and **burial expenses** in the event of death.

**Definition of Damage:**

[1] Injury inflicted in violation of the law, affecting a person's body, mental or psychological health, reputation, property, or honor.
[2] Loss or damage inflicted on property, deprivation of comfort, harm to physical well-being, reputation, or other similar aspects.

**Note:** Damage in crimes and quasi-crimes is the loss actually suffered by the claimant, the necessary expenses incurred or to be incurred to repair the consequences of the act causing the damage, as well as any benefits denied within the normal scope of the consequences of this act.
