The Board of the Authority shall be composed of the Head of the Authority, acting as President, and the ten members mentioned below: a judge of the third rank; a counselor at the Administrative Court; a counselor at the Court of Auditors; a representative from the Ministry of Finance (the person appointed by the group to act on its behalf); a representative from the Ministry of Social Affairs, Solidarity and Tunisians Abroad (the person appointed by the group to act on its behalf); a representative from the Central Bank of Tunisia (the person appointed by the group to act on its behalf); a representative from the Financial Market Authority (the person appointed by the group to act on its behalf); and three members chosen from among those with expertise.  This expertise covers scientific and technical matters, and other matters necessary for deciding on the judicial case, excluding legal matters and expertise in insurance (a contract whereby the insurer undertakes to pay the insured or the beneficiary a sum of money, a periodic income, or any other financial compensation in the event of the occurrence of the insured event, in exchange for premiums or any other financial payment made by the insured to the insurer), excluding those working in the sector, including an actuary.
