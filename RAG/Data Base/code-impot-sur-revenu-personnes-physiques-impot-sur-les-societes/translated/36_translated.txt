Other income consists of income from foreign sources that has not been subject to tax in the country of origin.

Also considered as other income are:

- Income derived from gambling, games of chance, and lotteries;

- Income determined based on apparent and evident personal expenses and wealth growth, in accordance with the provisions of Article 43 of this Code, in the event that no income is realized in the category of industrial and commercial profits, profits from non-commercial professions, or profits from agricultural exploitation or fishing. (Added by Article 19-1 of Law No. 53 of 2015 dated December 25, 2015).
