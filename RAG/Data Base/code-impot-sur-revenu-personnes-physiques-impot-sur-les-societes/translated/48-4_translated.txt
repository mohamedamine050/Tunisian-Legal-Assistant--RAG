Chapter 48, Section IV. (Added by Article 14 of Act No. 85 of 2006, dated December 25, 2006, concerning the facilitation of the transfer of businesses)

I. Subject to the provisions of Article 12 of Act No. 114 of 1989, dated December 30, 1989, relating to the enactment of the Code on Income Tax for Individuals and Corporate Tax, legal persons acquiring businesses under paragraph II of Article 11 bis of this Code shall benefit from the deduction of reinvested profits, up to the limit of profits subject to corporate tax for the year of acquisition. (Amended by Article 15 of Act No. 8 of 2017, dated February 14, 2017).

The deduction is granted based on a decision from the Minister of Finance or his/her delegate.  Benefitting from the deduction requires:

- That the divested business operates in sectors specified in Chapter IV of this Code; (Amended by Article 15-8 of Act No. 8 of 2017, dated February 14, 2017)
- That the acquiring business continues to operate the acquired business or unit for a period of not less than three years starting from January 1st of the year following the year of acquisition;
- Maintenance of accounting records in accordance with the accounting legislation for businesses;
- That the annual tax return for the year of the deduction be accompanied by a list of the acquired items under paragraph II of the aforementioned Article 11 bis, including in particular the acquisition value and a copy of the Minister of Finance's decision mentioned above (Amended by Article 15 of Act No. 8 of 2017, dated February 14, 2017).

The business benefiting from the deduction shall be required to pay the unpaid tax on the reinvested amounts, plus the applicable late payment penalties according to the legislation in force, if it ceases to operate the acquired business before the expiry of the three years following the year of the deduction. These provisions shall not apply in the event of cessation of operation for reasons beyond its control, to be determined by order.

II. (Its provisions were repealed by Article 15-6 of Act No. 8 of 2017, dated February 14, 2017)

The tax benefit provided for in this Chapter shall not be granted for subscription operations intended for the acquisition of land. (Added by Article 37-1 of Act No. 66 of 2017, dated December 18, 2017).
