Regardless of the provisions of Articles 12 and 12 bis of Law No. 114 of 1989, dated December 30, 1989, concerning the issuance of the Tax Code on the Income of Natural Persons and Corporate Tax, income or profits derived from direct investments, as defined in Article 3 of the aforementioned Law,  are entirely deducted from the basis of the income tax on natural persons or corporate tax during the first ten years from the date of commencement of actual activity in the agricultural and fisheries sectors.

Benefitting from this deduction requires maintaining accounts in accordance with the accounting legislation for companies, for persons carrying out industrial or commercial activities or a non-commercial profession as defined in this Code.
