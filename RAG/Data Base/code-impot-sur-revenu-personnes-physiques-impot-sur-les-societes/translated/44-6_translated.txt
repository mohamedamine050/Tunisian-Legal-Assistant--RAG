If any of the conditions stipulated in Article 44 bis of this Code are not met, except for those relating to transaction numbers, the estimated assessment shall be withdrawn from the taxpayer by a reasoned decision of the Director General of Taxes or the Head of the Regional Tax Control Center. (Amended by Article 38 of Law No. 53 of 2015 dated December 2015).

The withdrawal decision shall be notified to the interested party through the methods used for the notification of the compulsory tax levy decision.

The taxpayer must comply with the tax obligations stipulated in the actual system starting from January 1st of the year following the year of withdrawal. The taxpayer may challenge the withdrawal decision according to the procedures relating to the compulsory tax levy decision, provided that the case shall be decided within a maximum period of three months from the date of its filing before the Court of First Instance.

The challenge does not suspend the execution of the withdrawal decision.
