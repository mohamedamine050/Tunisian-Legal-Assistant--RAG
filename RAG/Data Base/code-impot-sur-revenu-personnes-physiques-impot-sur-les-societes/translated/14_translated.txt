Contrary to the provisions of Article 12 bis of this Code, and to regulate profit, the following deductions are not allowed:

1. Land,

2. Business assets,

3. (Repealed by Article 42-2 of Law No. 70 of 2007 dated December 27, 2007)

4. Aircraft and pleasure boats placed at the disposal of managers or employees of the company, if their use is not directly related to the company's purpose, as well as secondary residences.

5. Passenger cars exceeding 9 fiscal horsepower, except those whose original purpose is for business use (amended by Article 53 of Law No. 109 dated December 25, 1995).

6. Assets whose acquisition cost equals or exceeds 20,000 Dinars, excluding value-added tax, and which are paid for in cash (added by Article 34-2 of Law No. 54 of 2013 dated December 30, 2013).

7. Assets acquired from individuals residing or established in a country or region with a preferential tax system as defined in Article 14, paragraph 12, of this Code (added by Article 34-2 of Law No. 78 of 2016 dated December 17, 2016 and amended by Article 35-3 of Law No. 56 of 2018 dated December 27, 2018).
