Without prejudice to the provisions of Articles 12 and 12 bis of Law No. 114 of 1989 dated December 30, 1989, relating to the issuance of the Tax Code on the Income of Individuals and Corporate Tax,  revenues or profits reinvested in the subscription to the original capital or in its increase for the institutions provided for in Articles 63 and 65 of this Code are entirely excluded from the tax base for the income tax of individuals or corporate tax, up to the limit of the income or profit exceeding the company's liabilities by including the company's capital among its debts at the end of the tax year.

The tax exemption provided for in this article does not apply to subscription operations intended for the acquisition of land (added by Article 37-1 of Law No. 66 of 2017 dated December 18, 2017).
