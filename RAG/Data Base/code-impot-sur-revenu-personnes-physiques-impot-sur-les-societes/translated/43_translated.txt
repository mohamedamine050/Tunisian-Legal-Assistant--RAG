The estimated assessment shall be applied based on the apparent and evident personal expenses or on the growth of wealth for all tax claims, unless the taxpayer proves the sources of funding for their expenses or the growth of their wealth.

To justify these expenses and the growth of wealth, income subject to tax realized during the period covered by the statute of limitations and which was declared and the tax due thereon paid before January 1st of the year for which the aforementioned estimated assessment is applied shall be accepted, unless the use of this income in other acquisitions is proven.

(Amended by Article 59 of Law No. 85 of 2006 dated December 25, 2006)

This method is used when the amount of this assessment, plus living expenses considering the standard of living of the concerned party, exceeds their declared income, which is determined in the same manner as that followed for the estimated assessment based on elements of the standard of living.
