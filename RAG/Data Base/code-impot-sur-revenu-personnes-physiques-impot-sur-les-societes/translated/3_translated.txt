The tax is also levied on non-resident individuals who realize income originating in Tunisia or realize the added value stipulated in paragraph 2 of Article 27 of this Code, on the basis of this income or this added value alone.

The provisions of this paragraph apply to the added value resulting from the transfer of shares, social units, or shares in funds stipulated in the relevant legislation, or in rights related to the aforementioned securities or their re-transfer, for transfer or re-transfer operations carried out from January 1, 2011. (Added by Article 41-5 of Law No. 58 of 2010, dated December 17, 2010, and amended by Articles 3-4-1 and 2 of Decree No. 100 of 2011, dated October 21, 2011)

The tax is not levied on:

- Interest on deposits and securities in foreign currency or convertible dinars,

- (Repealed by Article 19-1 of Law No. 54 of 2013, dated December 30, 2013, which set an application date for distribution operations carried out from January 1, 2015).

- (Repealed by Article 54 of Law No. 54 of 2013, dated December 30, 2013).

- Remuneration for the lease of ships or aircraft for international navigation.

- (Added by Article 53 of Law No. 127 of 1994, dated December 26, 1994, and amended by Article 59 of Law No. 88 of 1997, dated December 29, 1997, and amended by Article 45 of Law No. 101 of 2002, dated December 17, and repealed by Article 44 of Law No. 58 of 2010, dated December 17, 2010).
