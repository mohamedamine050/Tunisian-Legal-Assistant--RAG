Net income consists of the gross amount of distributed income as defined in Articles 29, 30, 31, and 32 of this Code.

The surplus value referred to in the second paragraph of Article 3 and Article 31 bis of this Code consists of the difference between the sale or transfer price of securities or related rights, on the one hand, and their subscription or acquisition price, on the other hand, resulting from sale or transfer transactions carried out during the year preceding the tax year, after deducting the recorded loss from the aforementioned transactions (added by Article 61-2, Act No. 80 of 2003, dated December 29, 2003, and by Article 41-6, Act No. 58 of 2010, dated December 17, 2011.  Repealed and replaced by Article 8 of Decree No. 100 of 2011, dated October 21, 2011).

The sale or transfer price means:

- The market price on the Tunis Stock Exchange on the date of sale, for shares listed on the Exchange;

- The sale or transfer price included in the contract or adjusted by the tax authorities, in case of proof of an agreement or receipt of an amount exceeding the declared amount, for sales or transfers of social bonds or shares not listed on the Tunis Stock Exchange, and sales or transfers of shares in funds provided for in the relevant legislation.

Where necessary, and at the request of the most diligent party, an expert assessment may be sought with the authorization of the competent court.

(Added by Article 20 of Act No. 53 of 2015, dated December 25, 2015).
