Notwithstanding the provisions of Article 12 bis of Law No. 114 of 1989 dated December 30, 1989, relating to the issuance of the Income Tax Code for individuals and companies, the following shall be deducted from the income tax base:  taxes withheld on total income or income components; two-thirds of income derived from direct investments as defined in Article 3 of the Law; investment in the agricultural and fisheries sectors, as well as exceptional profits provided for in Article 11 bis, paragraph I, of this Code, under the same conditions, after fulfilling the total deduction period stipulated in Article 65 of this Code.  Benefitting from this deduction requires maintaining accounting records in accordance with the accounting legislation for institutions. Profits derived from direct investments as defined in Article 3 of the Law; investment in the agricultural and fisheries sectors, as well as exceptional profits provided for in Article 11 bis, paragraph I, of this Code, under the same conditions, and after fulfilling the total deduction period stipulated in Article 65 of this Code, are subject to corporate income tax according to the rate stipulated in paragraph 1 of paragraph 4 of paragraph I of Article 49 of this Code. (Amended by Article 16-7 of Law No. 56 of 2018 dated December 27, 2018).
