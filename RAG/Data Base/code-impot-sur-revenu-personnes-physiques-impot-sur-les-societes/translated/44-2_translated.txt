Individual entities generating income classified as industrial and commercial profits within a single establishment are subject to income tax under the estimated assessment system if they are:

- Not exporting;
- Not benefiting from brokerage fees, except for those persons referred to in the fourth indent of paragraph "Z" of paragraph 1 of Article 52 of this Code (amended by Article 30-2, Law No. 95 of 2014, dated December 26, 2014);
- Not manufacturing products using alcoholic materials;
- Not engaged in wholesale trade;
- Do not own more than one vehicle for public passenger transport or freight transport, provided that its payload does not exceed three and a half tons;
- Their owners do not generate income classified as profits from non-commercial professions;
- Not subject to Value Added Tax under the actual system;
- Not subjected to income tax on natural persons under the actual system following a tax audit;
- Their annual turnover does not exceed 100,000 dinars (amended by Article 17-1, Law No. 53 of 2015, dated December 25, 2015).

This system is granted for a period of 4 years from the date of filing the declaration of existence provided for in Article 56 of this Code, and is renewable upon submission of the necessary supporting documents concerning the activity as stipulated in paragraph V of Article 59 of this Code, which proves the right to benefit from the aforementioned system.  For the calculation of the 4-year period, entities operating on January 1, 2016, are considered as if they were created on that date. (Repealed and replaced by Article 17-2, Law No. 53 of 2015, dated December 24, 2015, and by Article 16-3, Law No. 66 of 2017, dated December 18, 2017).

The aforementioned 4-year period does not apply to entities established outside municipal areas according to the territorial boundaries of municipalities in force before January 1, 2015. (Added by Article 42, Law No. 78 of 2019, dated December 23, 2019).

Entities operating within municipal areas according to the territorial boundaries of municipalities in force before January 1, 2015, which carry out activities to be specified by order, are excluded from benefiting from this system. (Added by Article 1.45, Law No. 54 of 2013, dated December 30, 2013, and amended by Article 16-2, Law No. 66 of 2017, dated December 18, 2017).
