The estimated tax is levied based on the annual turnover as follows:

- 200 dinars annually for establishments located within municipal areas according to the territorial boundaries of municipalities in effect before January 1, 2015. This amount is reduced by 50% for establishments located in other areas, for turnovers equal to or less than 10,000 dinars (amended by Article 16-1 of Law No. 66 of 2017 dated December 18, 2017).

- 3% of the turnover for amounts between 10,000 dinars and 100,000 dinars.
(The first two paragraphs were repealed and replaced by Article 17-3 of Law No. 53 of 2015 dated December 25, 2015).

The tax due under the provisions of this chapter is increased by 50 percent if the annual tax return is filed after thirty days from the expiry of the legal deadlines. (Added by Article 45-3 of Law No. 54 of 2013 dated December 30, 2013).
