I. The deduction made by each employer or payer of income or salaries using the media shall be equal, for each salary payment, to the annual tax calculated according to the provisions of this Code, applicable to any person who has no income other than their salary or wages, divided by the number of salary payments.

Each deduction made on any additional salary or occasional allowance shall be equal to the difference between this annual tax determined under the conditions set out above based on the annual salary to which this salary or allowance is added, and the annual tax determined without considering this salary or allowance.

II. Each other employer or payer of income or salaries, as well as the persons referred to in the second subparagraph of paragraph III of Article 52 of this Code, shall deduct from the source according to a schedule prepared by the administration.


Any temporary or occasional salary granted in addition to the regular salary and allowances by the same employer is subject to a deduction from its net amount, at a rate of 20% (amended by Article 14-3, Law No. 78 of 2016, dated December 17, 2016).

Temporary or occasional salary is not subject to any deduction from the source if the total gross annual salary does not exceed 5000 dinars (amended by Article 14-3, Law No. 78 of 2016, dated December 17, 2016).

II bis:  Subject to the provisions of the second subparagraph of paragraph II above, payments made to employees and non-employees for temporary or occasional work outside their original activity are subject to a deduction from the source at a rate of 15% based on their gross amount (added by Article 61, Law No. 98 of 2000, dated December 2000).

III. Salaries, wages, bonuses, grants, allowances, as well as the bonuses provided for in paragraph II bis of this Article and paid to non-resident employees working in Tunisia for a period or periods not exceeding a total of 6 months, are subject to a withholding tax at a rate of 20% based on their gross amount, plus in-kind benefits according to their real value. (added by Article 52, Law No. 54 of 2013, dated December 30, 2013).

IV. The deductions referred to in paragraph IV of Article 40 of this Code shall not be taken into account at the level of the deduction from the source. (amended by Article 34-1, Law No. 27 of 2012, dated December 29, 2012).

V. The forms and conditions for taking into account the deductions provided for in the first paragraph of paragraph III of Article 40 of this Code shall be determined by a decision of the Minister of Finance. (added by Article 34-2, Law No. 27 of 2012, dated December 29, 2012).
