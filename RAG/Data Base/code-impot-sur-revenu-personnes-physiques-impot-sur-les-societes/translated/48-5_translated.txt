(Added by Article 13-2 of Law No. 77 of 2008 dated December 22, 2008).  To determine the taxable profit, the income derived from the lease of agricultural land allocated to major crops, subject to lease contracts concluded for a period of not less than three years, is considered.

The application of this provision requires:

- The lessee's commitment, within the lease contract, to allocate the land to major crops;

- The attachment of the corporate tax return with a certificate of receipt from the regional offices of the Ministry responsible for agriculture, attesting that the leased land has been allocated to major crops in accordance with agricultural practices during the year in question.
