Salaries, wages, bonuses, grants, and other related benefits, as well as pensions and retirement income, shall be elements of the gross income constituting the basis of income tax.

Bonuses, grants, and other benefits received by agents in limited liability companies in their capacity as such shall be classified as salaries and wages. (Added by Article 422-1, Act No. 58 of 2010, dated December 17, 2010)
