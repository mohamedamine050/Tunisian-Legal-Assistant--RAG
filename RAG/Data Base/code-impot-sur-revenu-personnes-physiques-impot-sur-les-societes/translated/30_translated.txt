The following are considered distributed income:

1. Unless proven otherwise, amounts placed at the disposal of the partners directly or through third parties as advances, loans, or installments, except those granted between the parent company and its subsidiaries.

If these amounts are returned to the legal entity, the tax portion levied on these amounts shall be deducted from the tax due for the year of return or for subsequent years.

2. Bonuses, benefits, and hidden profits.

3. Bonuses and grants awarded to members of boards, bodies, and committees in limited liability companies and joint-stock companies, in their capacity as such, in accordance with current legislation and regulations. (Amended by Article 30-1 of Law No. 78 of 2016 dated December 17, 2016)
