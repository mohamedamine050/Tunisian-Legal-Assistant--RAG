Article 48, Subsection 3 (added by Article 5 of Law No. 56 of 2005, dated July 18, 2005, concerning the bankruptcy of economic institutions).  Institutions subject to the bankruptcy mechanism, as defined by current legislation, may deduct expenses incurred in completing the bankruptcy process from the tax base for the year in which these expenses were incurred, within limits and according to conditions to be determined by order.
