The provisions of Articles 63, 65, 70 and 71 of this Code shall apply to institutions that have obtained an investment declaration filed starting January 1st, 2017, under the heading of direct investments as defined in Article 3 of the Investment Law.

However, the provisions of these articles shall not apply to institutions established within the framework of transfer operations or following cessation of activity or following a change in the legal form of the institution, for the purpose of carrying out the same activity related to the same product or the same service, except for the transfer of institutions experiencing economic difficulties as provided for in Article 71 of this Code.

To benefit from the provisions of these articles with respect to the aforementioned investments, the following conditions must be met:

- Filing an investment declaration with the relevant authorities in the sector of activity, in accordance with the regulations in force;

- Achieving an investment financing structure including a minimum percentage of equity capital, in accordance with the legislation and regulations in force;

- Attaching the annual tax return with a certificate issued by the competent authorities certifying the commencement of actual activity;

- Being in compliance with social security funds.
