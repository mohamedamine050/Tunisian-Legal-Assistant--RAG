The following shall not be considered as distributed income:

1. Consumption of realized capital by amounts resulting from the disposal of asset elements, as well as the return of contributions in the case of capital reduction.

2. The distribution of the issue bonus to those who contributed.

3. Amounts reimbursed to partners resulting from the liquidation of the company and related to their capital contributions or to previously consumed capital which has borne tax, or related to capitalized reserves exceeding five years old.
