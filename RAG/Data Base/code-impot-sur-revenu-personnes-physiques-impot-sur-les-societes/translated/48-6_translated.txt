Article 48, Sixthly (added by Article 4682-22 of Law No. 71 of 2009 dated December 2009, amended by Article 22 of Decree No. 100 of 2011 dated October 21, 2011, and by Article 15 of Law No. 8 of 2017 dated February 14, 2017). - The dismissal provided for in paragraph VII, twenty-two of Article 48 and in Article 77 of this Code requires, in addition to the conditions stipulated in the aforementioned Article and paragraph, the fulfillment of the conditions stipulated in Article 39, fifthly, of this Code.
