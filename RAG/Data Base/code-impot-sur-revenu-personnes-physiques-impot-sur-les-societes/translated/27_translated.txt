Included within the category of real estate income, unless included in other categories of income:

1. Rent from built and unbuilt properties, including land used as plots.

2. The capital gain realized from the transfer of:

- Social rights in real estate companies.
- Buildings or parts thereof.
- Land, except for agricultural land located in agricultural areas, transferred to non-real estate developers or to persons who, under the contract, undertake not to allocate the land for construction before the expiration of 4 years from the date of transfer. The transferee shall be required to pay the tax due in this respect in the event of breach of the aforementioned obligation.

These provisions shall not apply to transfers made:

- To a spouse, ascendants, or descendants; or
- To the beneficiary of the right of first refusal within the real estate savings schemes established in accordance with the provisions of Articles 40 and 41 of the Land Use Planning and Development Code; or
- Within the framework of expropriation for public interest; or
- In the case of the transfer of a single dwelling unit with a total area not exceeding 1000 square meters, including appurtenances, built and unbuilt, for the first transfer.

The transfer, within the meaning of this paragraph, includes the transfer of ownership, usufruct, freehold, or easement rights.

(Amended by Article 106 of Law No. 122 of 1992 dated December 29, 1992, and by Article 3 of Law No. 73 of 1998 dated August 4, 1998, relating to the simplification of tax procedures, and the reduction in tax rates; and by Article 74 of Law No. 123 of 2001 dated December 28, 2001; and by Article 60-1 of Law No. 90 of 2004 dated December 31, 2004; and by Article 47-1 of Law No. 54 of 2013 dated December 30, 2013).

3. The capital gain realized upon the transfer of plots of land or parts thereof, the original ownership of which stems from a gratuitous transfer of state-owned agricultural land that has lost its agricultural designation. (Amended by Article 106 of Law No. 122 of 1992 dated December 29, 1992, and by Article 4 of Law No. 73 of 1998 dated August 4, 1998, relating to the simplification of tax procedures and the reduction in tax rates).
