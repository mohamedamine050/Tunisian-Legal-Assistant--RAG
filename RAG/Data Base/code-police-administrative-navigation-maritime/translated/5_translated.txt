Contrary to the provisions stipulated in this chapter, vessels and craft of all types, including recreational vessels, bound for the purposes of navigation and manufactured outside the country, may acquire Tunisian nationality.

**A) Proof of deletion from the ship register or lack of registration in the country to which it previously belonged if the vessel is new.**

**B) That its port of registry is in Tunisia.**

**C) That it is owned by a Tunisian national for a period of five years.**

**D) That it has paid the fees and duties prescribed by Chapter III of this code.**
