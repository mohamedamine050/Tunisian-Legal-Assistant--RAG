For the ease of applying this chapter, the following terms are defined as follows:

a) The master or captain is the person tasked with commanding the vessel.

b) The navigating officer is the person, excluding the pilot, who actually supervises navigation and the movements of the vessel.

c) The chief engineer is the person who continuously supervises the department responsible for the vessel's mechanical propulsion.

d) The engine officer is the person who actually oversees the machinery that ensures the vessel's propulsion.
