Decisions made pursuant to Article 48 of this Code may be submitted to the Minister responsible for Merchant Shipping in writing within fifteen days of being notified thereof. This period shall be reduced to forty-eight hours in the circumstances provided for in paragraph four of Article 46 of this Code. The Minister shall issue his decision within five days after obtaining the opinion of the Central Committee for Safety Assurance as provided for in Article 40 of this Code. This period shall be reduced to forty-eight hours in the circumstances provided for in paragraph four of Article 46 of this Code.
