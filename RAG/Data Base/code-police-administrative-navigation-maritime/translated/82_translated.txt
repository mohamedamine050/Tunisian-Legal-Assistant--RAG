**For the purposes of this chapter, any individual acting as a ship's captain, owner, or in any way responsible for the management or disposition of a maritime transport institution, regardless of its legal nature, shall be deemed a "person" and subject to the penalties prescribed herein. The following shall be considered offenses:**

* **Felonies:** Crimes punishable by death or imprisonment exceeding five years, as defined by this Criminal Code.
* **Misdemeanors:** Crimes punishable by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars.
* **Violations:** Crimes punishable by imprisonment not exceeding fifteen days or a fine not exceeding sixty dinars. (Article 122 of the Code of Criminal Procedure)

**These institutions shall be held civilly liable and jointly and severally liable with the perpetrators of the offenses for the payment of fines and court costs.**
