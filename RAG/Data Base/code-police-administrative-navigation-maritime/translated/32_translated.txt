The general rules that vessels must comply with in order to obtain safety assurance documents pursuant to the Order, particularly with regard to:

1) Construction (bulkheads and stability, as well as precautions to prevent fires and means of detecting and extinguishing them) 

2) Electrical equipment. 3) Machinery and auxiliary equipment. 4) Rescue equipment (lifeboats and life rafts, etc.). 5) Wireless telegraph and wireless telephone. 6) Navigation instruments and documents. 7) Maximum passenger capacity. 8) Accommodation capacity and sanitary conditions. 9) Medical and health facilities on board the vessel for persons and equipment, instruments. 10) Cargo arrangements and stowage of grain and dangerous goods.
