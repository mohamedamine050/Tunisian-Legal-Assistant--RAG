## English Translation:

1) Violations of the provisions of this Code may be inspected by officers:

a) Judicial police officers. b) Sworn officials of the Commercial Marine Services. c) Commanders of national marine units. d) Sworn officials of the Maritime Navigation Administration. e) Sworn officials of the Maritime Fisheries Administration.

f) Sworn officials of the National Coast Guard Service. g) Sworn officials of the National Guard assigned to maritime border control.

2) Outside the areas specified above:

a) Commanders of national marine units. b) Sworn officials of the National Coast Guard Service. c)  Officials of the maritime navigation within the maritime navigation area as defined by the provisions of Article 34 of the Maritime Navigation Code.
