## English Translation:

1) Every Tunisian vessel shall have a navigation license. 2) Every Tunisian passenger vessel undertaking international voyages shall possess a document known as a Passenger Vessel Safety Guarantee Certificate. 3) Every Tunisian vessel, excluding fishing vessels, with a gross tonnage equal to or exceeding one hundred cubic meters, and every fishing vessel with a gross tonnage exceeding one hundred cubic meters, shall have a document stipulating the minimum freeboard height. 4) Every vessel undertaking international voyages shall possess safety and security guarantee documents, or exemption certificates, as stipulated by international agreements to which Tunisia is a party. 5) Every Tunisian vessel undertaking international voyages and equipped with lifting devices shall have a register for these devices.
