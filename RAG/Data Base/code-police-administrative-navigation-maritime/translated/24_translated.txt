The Tunisian flag shall be hoisted compulsorily on board the vessel upon its entry into or departure from the port, and whenever the maritime authority requests it in the context of general administration and under circumstances required by maritime custom or imposed by international regulations. These regulations shall be general in nature and not specific to any individual. This obligation shall not apply to:

1) Boats and dinghies belonging to the vessel and listed on its equipment list.
2) Cargo boats, barges, and vessels carrying lifting equipment, and all mobile buoys within the port.
3) Vessels specifically and locally used for laying tuna nets.
4) Fishing vessels with a gross tonnage not exceeding five tons and recreational vessels with a gross tonnage not exceeding ten tons.
