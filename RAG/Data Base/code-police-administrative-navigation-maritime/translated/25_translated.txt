For the purposes of applying this Chapter:

1) **A vessel** shall be considered as any floating craft, such as dredgers, carriers, tankers, and tugboats, regardless of whether they are self-propelled or towed by another vessel.

2) **A fishing vessel** shall be considered as any vessel that is not used for the purpose of harvesting living marine resources.

3) **A recreational vessel** shall be considered as any vessel that is used for navigation without a commercial purpose.

4) **A cargo vessel** shall be considered as any vessel that is not a passenger, fishing, or recreational vessel.

5) **A passenger vessel** shall be considered as any vessel carrying more than twelve passengers.

6) **An international voyage** shall be considered as any voyage undertaken by a Tunisian vessel that calls at a foreign port.
