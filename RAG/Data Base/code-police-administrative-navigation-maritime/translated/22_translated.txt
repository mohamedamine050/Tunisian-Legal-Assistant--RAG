The registration book records the transfers of ownership of the ship and the reasons for its deletion, damage, or sale, which are notified pursuant to legal documents. 

**Mortgage**

A formal mortgage is a contract by which the creditor, for an immovable property designated to secure his debt, acquires a real right that entitles him to priority over ordinary creditors and creditors ranking after him in the order of preference, to collect his right from the proceeds of that property, wherever it may be.

The registration book also records the **mortgage** transactions it covers. It also outlines the processes for **mortgage** documented in the nationality certificate.
