**An appeal may be lodged with the maritime authority by:**

1) The shipbuilder, owner, or outfitter, in the event of a refusal to issue a safety certificate as provided for in Chapter Twenty-six of this Code, or a provisional safety certificate as provided for in Chapter Thirty-one of this Code,

2) The ship's owner, outfitter, or master, in the event of the withdrawal or suspension of a safety certificate, or the refusal to renew or extend its validity as provided for in the last paragraph of Chapter Twenty-nine of this Code,

3) The ship's master who is not authorized to depart,

4) The deputy crew member or the three crew members who have submitted a complaint pursuant to Chapter Thirty-seven of this Code and whose complaint has not been accepted.
