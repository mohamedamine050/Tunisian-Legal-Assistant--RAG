It is permissible to seize shares in simple partnership or limited liability companies, as well as shares and fractions of shares in companies with variable capital, in the hands of the shareholding company. The seized shares and fractions of shares shall be sold in accordance with the procedures set forth in Chapter VI of this title, once a written judgment from the Court, resolving a legal or factual issue, has been issued by a judge affirming the validity of the seizure and becoming enforceable.
