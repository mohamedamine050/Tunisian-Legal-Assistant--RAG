The invocation of the nullity provisions stipulated in the second paragraph of Article 14 and the filing of a request to abstain from hearing the case on grounds of prior publication in another court or on grounds of its connection to another case must be made in one go and before any reply to the original claim. A request for intervention that could lead to the intervenor bearing the burden or guarantee in the case must be filed before the case is assigned to a hearing session.
