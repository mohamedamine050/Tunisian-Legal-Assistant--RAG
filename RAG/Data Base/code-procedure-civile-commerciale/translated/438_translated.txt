## English Translation:

**Grounds for nullity, whether procedural or substantive, raised against real estate attachment proceedings, including the freezing of the real estate subject to attachment and its placement under judicial supervision to ensure debt recovery, shall be filed and separated according to the forms and deadlines set forth in the preceding section.** 

**If the court rules in favor of the grounds for nullity, the proceedings may be appealed starting from the last non-null act, and the specified deadlines for carrying out subsequent acts shall run from the date of notification of the judgment declaring the nullity.** 

**If the court rules against the grounds for nullity, the proceedings shall be appealed starting from the last act in the proceedings.**
