The preventive detention and the notices of referral referred to in the preceding section shall have effect for a period of five years only from the date thereof, unless renewed within the aforementioned period, regardless of any legal acts or judgments that may have been imposed on the preventive detention and notices of referral referred to. Therefore, they shall be compulsorily deleted from the registers in which they are recorded and the certificate referred to in Section 340 shall not be delivered.
