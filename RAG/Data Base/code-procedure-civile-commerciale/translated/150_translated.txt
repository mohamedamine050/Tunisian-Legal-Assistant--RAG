(Amended by Law No. 87 of 1986 dated September 1, 1986). - If a judgment subject to immediate execution is reversed or amended, or if the judgment reversed or amended was issued in response to an urgent request, the judgment of reversal or amendment shall specify the return of any payment or delivery made by the appellant pursuant to the execution of the judgment reversed or amended, or the restoration of the case to its previous state, all within the scope of the reversal or amendment. The court shall, on its own motion, remedy any omission in the return or restoration.
