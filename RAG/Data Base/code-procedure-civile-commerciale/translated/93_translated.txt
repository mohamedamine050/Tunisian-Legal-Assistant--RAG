(Amended by Law No. 14 of 1980 dated April 3, 1980) - If the witness is a foreigner residing outside the Tunisian territory, the "Reporting Judge"(*) or the President of the Court shall send a commission rogatoire to the judicial authority to which the witness is subject by diplomatic means.  If the witness is Tunisian residing outside the Tunisian territory, the Public Prosecutor shall send a request administratively to the diplomatic or consular official nearest to the witness's location.
