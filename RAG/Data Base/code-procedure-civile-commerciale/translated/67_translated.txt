(Amended by Law No. 87 of 1986 dated September 1, 1986).- The clerk of each Court of First Instance and the Court of Appeal shall keep a special register in which shall be recorded the names, titles, and addresses of the parties, the date of the order for payment or the date of its refusal, the amount of the debt, its causes, and the date of the execution of the order. The clerk shall affix the seal of the court on each document submitted for the issuance of the order for payment, indicating its number and date.
