(Amended by Law No. 87 of 1986 dated September 1, 1986).- The **attorney** for the plaintiff shall submit to the court registry, seven days before the hearing date, the original copy of the summons served upon the defendant, accompanied by supporting documents and a list in duplicate containing a statement of the same, signed by the **court clerk** and with one copy returned to him as proof of receipt. The **court clerk** shall then register the case in the designated register after verifying the completeness of the documents, enter it into the schedule of the assigned hearing, and present it to the presiding judge for the appointment of the designated judge.
