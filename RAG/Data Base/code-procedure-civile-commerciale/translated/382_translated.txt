The restrictive measures, interventions, and referrals included in the register referred to in Article 387 shall be struck out of that register by the clerk, either pursuant to a judgment annulling them, or by lifting them, or by an assignment or distribution that completely exonerates the debtor, or by the lifting of the restrictive measures by mutual agreement between the creditor, evidenced by a handwritten document bearing the creditor's signature and registered, or by a signed statement recorded in the aforementioned register. In all cases, the court clerk shall immediately notify the party holding the unreasonable funds of this action.
