The proceedings in the case are legally suspended and temporarily filed by the court in writing upon the death of one of the parties, or their loss of litigation capacity, or the death of their legal representative, or the termination of their power of attorney, unless the lawsuit is ready for judgment on its merits, in which case the court may proceed with it. 

A lawsuit is considered ready for judgment on its merits when the parties have presented their requests and the case has been scheduled for a hearing.
