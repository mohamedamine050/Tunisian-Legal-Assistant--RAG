The minutes of the precautionary attachment shall state the following, failing which it shall be null and void:

Firstly: The decision issued therein, or in the forms provided for in Articles 287 and 290, the writ of execution pursuant to which the attachment was made, and notification to the person against whom the attachment is made of this writ.

Secondly: The presence or absence of the person against whom the attachment is made and, where applicable, the person entrusted with the custody of the enforcement proceedings.

Thirdly: The amount for which the attachment was made.

Fourthly: A detailed description of the property attached.
