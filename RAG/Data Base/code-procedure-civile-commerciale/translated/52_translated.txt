(Amended by Law No. 40 of 1963 dated November 14, 1963). - A person who is in possession of real estate or an incorporeal right in real estate, either personally or through another, may bring a possessory action:
 
First: If the purpose of the action is to maintain possession or to have it recognized in the event of disturbance, or to regain possession in case of dispossession;
 
Second: If the person has an interest in obtaining permission to suspend works that may lead to disturbance if completed;
 
Third: If the purpose of the action is to regain possession or to enjoy it in case of dispossession by force.
