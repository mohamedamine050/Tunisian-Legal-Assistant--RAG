**(Amended by Law No. 82 of 2002 dated August 3, 2002)** - Securities subject to a judicial deposit shall be offered for sale when a final and executory judgment is issued by the court confirming the deposit. These securities shall be sold at the request of the bailiff in accordance with the usual procedures and methods of the market where they are traded. Securities not listed on the stock exchange shall also be sold in accordance with these procedures and methods, at the discretion of the bailiff.
