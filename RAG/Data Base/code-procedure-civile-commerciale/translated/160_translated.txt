(Amended by Law No. 87 of 1986 dated September 1, 1986). - The applicant must secure with the Registry twenty dinars under the specific heading of the penalty to be imposed upon him in the event of his request being rejected, as well as all fees that the law requires to be secured.  The State and the poor who are granted legal aid are exempt from this requirement. The Court Clerk,  responsible for maintaining the court records and managing court documents, shall record the case in the register of proceedings and issue a receipt to the appellant.
