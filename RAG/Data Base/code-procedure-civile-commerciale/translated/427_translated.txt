**(Amended by Law No. 14 of 1980 dated April 3, 1980).** - The court shall decide the case by way of a judgment drawn up in the usual form. This judgment shall be **not subject to any form of appeal**, even by way of cassation. The only remedy available shall be an action for annulment before the **Court of First Instance**, subject to the provisions of Article 438 of this Code.
