Furthermore, no enforcement action can be taken:

Firstly, against Muslims: on Fridays, the last days of Ramadan starting from the 27th and the 3rd day of Eid al-Fitr, the 2nd day of Eid al-Adha, and the day following the Prophet's birthday. 

Secondly, against Israelis: on Saturdays, Rosh Hashanah, Yom Kippur, the first and last two days of Sukkot, Purim, the first and last two days of Passover, and Shavuot. 

Thirdly, against Christians: on Sundays, Ascension Day, the 15th of August (Assumption Day), the 1st of November, and the 25th of December (Christmas Day).
