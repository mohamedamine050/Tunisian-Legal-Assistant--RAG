The judgment issued rejecting the petition and the judgment issued on the merits of the case, after acceptance, shall not be subject to any form of appeal.

[1] One party seeks to obtain his right from another in the presence of the judge; the party seeking the right is called the plaintiff and the party against whom the right is sought is called the defendant. [2] The lawsuit is the right of every claimant to present his claim to the court for a judgment on its merits. It is the right of the defendant to present his defenses or arguments aiming to refute the claim.
