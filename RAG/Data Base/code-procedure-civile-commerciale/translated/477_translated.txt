Within eight days following the expiration of the deadline stipulated in the second paragraph of the preceding section, the judge assigned to hold the meeting shall authorize a meeting within a maximum period of one month in order to reach an agreement on the distribution arrangement.  The clerk shall, at least eight days before the meeting date, by registered mail with proof of delivery, summon the following to attend the meeting: 1) the registered creditors; 2) the unregistered creditors who have filed a claim for participation; 3) the seller and the buyer or, if it concerns a judicial sale, the party pursuing the sale, the party subject to the sale, and the beneficiary of the sale.
