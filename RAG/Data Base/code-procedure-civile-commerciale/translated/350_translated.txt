Every writ of execution that empowers a creditor holding an enforceable instrument to place the movable property in the possession of his debtor under judicial seizure, and every notification of a referral of the matter to the persons mentioned in Article 348, shall be executed, if it does not relate to the wages provided for in Articles 353 and 356, by one of the executing officers who shall draw up a report thereof. The original of the report shall be handed over to the person entitled to receive it. If the provisions of this Article are not complied with, the writ of execution or the notification of referral shall be of no effect.
