The **Court Clerk**, a judicial official responsible for maintaining records and managing court documents, shall, after verifying the completeness of the information and registering the petition in the register prescribed in Article 486, present it to the designated Governor within twenty-four hours, who shall issue an authorization to initiate proceedings. The **Court Clerk**, a judicial official responsible for maintaining records and managing court documents, shall, within eight days following the issuance of the designated Governor's authorization to initiate proceedings, publish notice on the designated board at the court and in the Official Gazette of the Tunisian Republic.
