**(Amended by Law No. 87 of 1986 dated September 1, 1986).** - If the respondent does not appoint a lawyer as stipulated in Article 134, or appoints one but does not submit his response and any defenses he may have, the Court shall continue to examine the case as required by its documents. If the State or a public institution is the respondent and requests postponement at the first hearing, the hearing shall be adjourned for a period of not less than sixty days.
