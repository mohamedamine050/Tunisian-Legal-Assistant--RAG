The increase bidder shall be notified within ten days following the increase, through one of the executive bailiffs who is following the case and who is authorized to act on behalf of the increase bidder, and who has the right to the increase. The increase bidder shall be requested to attend the new hearing on the specified date and time.  He shall also be required, within five days following the date of notification, to register his increase bid at the bottom of the declaration of increase as stipulated in the preceding article. 

If the increase bidder fails to declare and notify as mentioned above within the specified deadlines, the bailiff, his authorized representative, the beneficiary of the increase, or any registered creditor may undertake this within twenty days following the deadline granted to the increase bidder.  If this does not occur, the increase shall be considered null and void.
