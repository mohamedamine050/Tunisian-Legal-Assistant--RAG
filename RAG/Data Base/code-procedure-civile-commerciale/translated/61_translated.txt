(Amended by Law No. 82 of 2002 dated August 3, 2002) - The judge whose jurisdiction encompasses the domicile or chosen domicile of the debtor or one of the debtors, if there are multiple debtors, shall be competent to consider the order for payment, unless otherwise agreed. An order for payment shall not be issued if the debtor's domicile is entirely unknown within the meaning of the provisions of the second paragraph of Article 10 of this Code.
