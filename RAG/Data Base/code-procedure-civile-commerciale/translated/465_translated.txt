Every beneficiary, upon the securing of the funds, may request their distribution by submitting a petition to the clerk of the **Court of First Instance**, where the defendant's residence is located. The petition shall be accompanied by a power of attorney appointing a lawyer whose office is legally deemed the chosen address for the petitioner. This petition shall be further accompanied by a certificate from the Deposit and Trust Fund indicating the amount of the insurance, its cause, date, and number, as well as the names, titles, professions, and addresses of the debtor and all creditors authorized in the insurance claim.
