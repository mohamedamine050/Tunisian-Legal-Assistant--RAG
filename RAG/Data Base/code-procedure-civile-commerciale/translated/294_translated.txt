The executing officer, if the execution procedures require it, shall enter the premises where such procedures are to be carried out. If he is prevented from entering them or the doors are locked, he shall appoint a guard at the doors to prevent any seizure and shall immediately request the assistance of the Chief of the Police Station or the National Guard. He shall, in their presence, open the doors of the rooms and houses, as well as the furniture, whenever the execution procedures require it. The Chief of the Police Station or the National Guard who assisted in the execution procedures shall sign the execution report prepared by the executing officer.
