(Amended by Law No. 87 of 1986 dated September 1, 1986).- An original copy of the judgment must be prepared in accordance with the provisions of Article 123 as soon as possible. In any event, this deadline shall not exceed ten days from the date of its issuance. It must be signed by the judges who issued the judgment. If one of them is unable to sign after declaring so, the remaining judges shall sign it, specifying the reason for the inability to sign.
