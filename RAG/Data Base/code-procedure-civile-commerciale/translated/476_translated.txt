Following the submission of the petition, the provisions of Article 466 shall apply.  Any unregistered creditor seeking to participate in the distribution of the sale price, which may differ from the price agreed upon by the contracting parties, must submit proof of their debt to the court in writing within thirty days following the publication of the notice stipulated in the aforementioned Article, in compliance with the provisions of Article 468. Otherwise, their right to participate in the distribution shall be extinguished. All means of advertising specified in Article 466 must be in conformity with the provisions of the preceding paragraph, otherwise they shall be invalid.
