If an objection is filed concerning the distribution of a legal document, the assigned judge shall refer the file, accompanied by a report, to the court within eight days following the expiration of the objection period. The court shall rule on both the objection and the distribution within thirty days following the referral, after the clerk summons the concerned parties by registered mail at least eight days before the hearing, with notification of delivery. The period for appeal shall commence from the date of this judgment.
