The attorney responsible for the seizure shall, at the latest within forty days and at least twenty days prior to the date of the auction, publish an announcement in the Official Gazette of the Tunisian Republic, signed by him, containing the following: 1) the title, name, description, and address of the reasonable creditor and the reasonable debtor on whose behalf he is acting, 2) the title, name, and address of the attorney responsible for the seizure, 3) the designation of the property subject to the seizure as specified in the terms and conditions, 4) the opening bid amount, 5) the date, time, and Court before which the auction will be held, 6) the conditions under which the property may be visited.
