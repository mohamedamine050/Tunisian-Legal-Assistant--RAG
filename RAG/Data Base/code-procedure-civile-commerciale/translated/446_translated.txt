The procedures for re-auctioning under default consist of a new announcement and a new auction, conducted according to the rules stipulated for the initial auction. The legal announcement shall, in addition to the clarifications stipulated in Article 418, state the name and address of the defaulter in the sale, the amount of the auctioned sum due to him, the opening price set by the auctioneer, and the date and time of the completion of the new auction in accordance with the old terms and conditions.
