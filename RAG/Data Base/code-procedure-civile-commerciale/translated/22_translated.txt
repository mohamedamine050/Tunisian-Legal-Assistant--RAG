If the value of the disputed matter cannot be determined, the Court of First Instance shall have sole jurisdiction to hear the claim and render a judgment in the first instance.

[1] A claim is a request by one party to another for a right in the presence of a judge; the requester is called the plaintiff and the party against whom the request is made is called the defendant. 
[2] A claim is the right of any person to seek a judicial ruling on their claim. For the defendant, it is the right to present defenses or arguments to refute the claim.
