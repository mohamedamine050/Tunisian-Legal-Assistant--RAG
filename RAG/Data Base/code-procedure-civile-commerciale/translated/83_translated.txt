(Amended by Law No. 87 of 1986 dated September 1, 1986).- The lawyers of the parties shall continue to exchange observations between them without any special procedures other than each of them signing upon receipt of what his colleague has submitted, and copies of these observations, as well as the documents, shall be submitted to be added to the case file ten days before the date of the hearing for the plaintiff's lawyer and three days before the date of the hearing for the defendant's lawyer.

The defendant is the person against whom the lawsuit is directed or the person who is being sued.

Reports submitted after the aforementioned deadlines shall not be accepted.
