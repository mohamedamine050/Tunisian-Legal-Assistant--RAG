(Amended by Law No. 87 of 1986 dated September 1, 1986).- The court may postpone the announcement of judgment to a specific future hearing for deliberation or negotiation. During this period, no observations or arguments shall be accepted. However, in certain circumstances, the court may grant permission for the submission of written observations, provided that the opponent has been given access to them. Such permission shall be stated in the minutes of the hearing.
