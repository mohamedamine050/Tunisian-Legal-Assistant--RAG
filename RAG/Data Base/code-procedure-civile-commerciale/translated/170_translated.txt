(Amended by Law No. 87 of 1986 dated September 1, 1986). - An objection is filed with the court that rendered the contested judgment. 

The objector must provide a receipt for the specific court fees that must be levied upon him in the event of his claim being dismissed. The amount shall be five dinars if the contested judgment is issued by a single judge, ten dinars if the judgment is issued by the Court of First Instance, and twenty dinars if the judgment is issued by the Court of Appeal. 

The objector must also secure all fees required by law. The State and indigent persons assisted by legal aid are exempt from this requirement.
