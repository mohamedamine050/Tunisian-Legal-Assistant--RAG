If the unreasonable party under whose control the payment is due fails to make the payment within the aforementioned period, he may be compelled to do so by a decision issued ex officio by the District Governor, specifying the amount of money. Both parties may request the issuance of the aforementioned decision in accordance with the forms stipulated in Article 368. The Court Clerk shall inform the unreasonable party under whose control the payment is due of the decision within three days of its date.
