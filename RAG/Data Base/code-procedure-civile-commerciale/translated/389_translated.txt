The General Receiver of Court Records for the Courts of First Instance shall open a special account at the Deposit and Consignment Fund. The court clerk, a judicial officer responsible for maintaining records and managing court documents, shall, within three days following the issuance of the distribution or assignment order or the return decision, deposit into this account, in the name of each beneficiary, the amount indicated in the order or decision. The beneficiary shall be allowed to withdraw this amount upon signing a receipt.
