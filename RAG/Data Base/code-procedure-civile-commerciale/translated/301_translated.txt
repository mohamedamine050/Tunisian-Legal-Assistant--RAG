The costs of execution shall be borne by the **defendant**.

The **defendant** is an individual against whom a judgment has been issued, determining their responsibility for a crime, and imposing a penalty or a precautionary measure.

This is, however, **subject to any exceptions provided by law**.

The costs of the executive enforcement officer and the sale shall be borne by the **plaintiff**, and these costs shall be deemed **preferential**, meaning they shall be paid in addition to the price.
