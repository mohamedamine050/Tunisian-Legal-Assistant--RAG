The bailiff or the judgment concluding the arrangement proceedings or the delivery of the distribution tables to the secured creditors shall be authorized. The court clerk shall deliver a copy of this order or judgment to the Deposit and Trust Fund within ten days of its issuance, upon the execution of the judicial order.  Within the same period, a copy shall be delivered to each secured creditor, as well as to the debtor, if any part of the purchase price,  remains, over or below the value of the sale, in a distribution table signed by the Republic's agent. The aforementioned fund shall execute the distribution table.
