The party alleging forgery, against whom judgment is rendered, shall be sentenced to a fine ranging from three to ten dinars, without prejudice to the payment of damages to the opposing party, and shall not be subject to any form of appeal.

[2] Loss or damage to property, deprivation of comfort, or harm to physical well-being, reputation, or the like. -  Damage in crimes and quasi-crimes refers to the actual loss suffered by the plaintiff and the necessary expenses that the plaintiff was or will be forced to incur to repair the consequences of the act committed to his detriment, as well as what the plaintiff was deprived of in the ordinary scope of the consequences of this act.
