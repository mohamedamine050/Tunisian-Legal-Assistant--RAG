The natural and civil fruits, or the price, agreed upon by the contracting parties, shall be frozen from the date of notification to the debtor by the executive officer, or his warning, and distributed along with the price of the real estate and in the same manner, except for what may result from the *`aql`*. 

The *`aql`*, which empowers the creditor holding an executory instrument to place the movable property in the possession of his debtor under the custody of the court, is governed by the provisions of Article 402.
