**Lawyer**

**Lawyer**

A lawyer is a legal professional who represents individuals or organizations before the court and provides them with legal advice.

The appellant is to be summoned for the hearing at least thirty days prior to its commencement. If the judgment appealed against was issued in an emergency matter, or if it relates to matters of the type specified in Article 86, the aforementioned period shall be reduced to no less than eight days. This reduction shall be specified in the summons addressed to the appellant's lawyer.
