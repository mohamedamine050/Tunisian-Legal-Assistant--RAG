The President of the Real Estate Registry Department, or the Judge delegated by him, shall assess the costs of the real estate registry proceedings, which include the freezing of the disposition of the property subject to the registry and placing it under the jurisdiction of the court to ensure the recovery of the debt, prior to the hearing for the lifting of the registry. The President or Judge shall, concurrently, assess the fees of the lawyer requesting the registry, which shall be considered part of the costs. The costs and fees assessed by the President or Judge shall be considered final and payable in addition to the price.
