(Amended by Law No. 87 of 1986 dated September 1, 1986).- If the plaintiff's lawyer does not submit the supporting documents for the claim within the prescribed time limit, the case will be considered abandoned unless the defendant has filed it.  Receipt of the defendant's response in writing, along with any defenses to the claim, shall be considered. If the defendant has not appointed a lawyer as specified in Article 70, or if a lawyer has been appointed but has not submitted the response and defenses, the court shall continue to examine the case based on the documents before it.
