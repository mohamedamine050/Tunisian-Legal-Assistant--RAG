(Amended by Law No. 40 of 1963 dated November 14, 1963).- Except in the case of dispossession by force, an action for dispossession shall only be admissible: 1) If the claimant has been in possession for at least one year at the time of the disturbance or dispossession, or if the claimant has completed works which are likely to lead to a disturbance and has not remained silent for one year after the occurrence of such disturbance or dispossession; 2) If the possession has been continuous, unambiguous, uninterrupted and without any overt disturbance, in the capacity of owner.
