(Amended by Law No. 82 of 2002 dated August 3, 2002).- If the successful party fails to pay the balance of the purchase price and expenses within seven days from the date of the judgment, the goods subject to the judgment shall be resold again by way of default on a date determined by the executing judge after obtaining the written opinion of the reasonable creditor, provided that the new date of sale shall not exceed one month from the date of default.
