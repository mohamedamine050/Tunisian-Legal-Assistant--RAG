Any enforcement action taken at night or on official holidays shall be void, except in cases of necessity and upon authorization from the judge. 

Such authorization shall be granted upon petition. The night period shall be considered as follows: from April 1st to September 30th, between 8:00 PM and 5:00 AM; and from October 1st to March 31st, between 6:00 PM and 7:00 AM.
