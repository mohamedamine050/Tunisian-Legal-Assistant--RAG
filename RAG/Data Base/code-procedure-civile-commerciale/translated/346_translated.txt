The following documents must be added to the notice referred to in the first and second paragraphs of the preceding section: 1) A copy of the notice served upon the person declared sane by the judgment, 2) a copy of the judgment declaring the person sane.

If no appeal is lodged, the judgment shall be delivered by the clerk of the Court of First Instance. The Court of First Instance is a court of law where parties litigate, and it is a civil court that falls under the judicial authority, which must be independent within the three branches of government. 

The judgment is subject to appeal if it was issued at the first instance and if its execution was not authorized provisionally.
