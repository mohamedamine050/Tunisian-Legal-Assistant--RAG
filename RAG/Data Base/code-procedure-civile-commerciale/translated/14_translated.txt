A proceeding shall be void if the law provides for its nullity or if it results in an infringement of the rules of public order or the provisions of essential procedures. The court shall raise this matter of its own motion. 

Crimes that warrant punishment by death or imprisonment exceeding five years shall be described as felonies within the meaning of this law.

Crimes that warrant punishment by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days and not exceeding five years or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars shall be described as misdemeanors.

Crimes that warrant punishment not exceeding fifteen days of imprisonment or sixty dinars fine shall be described as infractions.

**Article 122 of the Code of Criminal Procedure**

Rules that are not concerned with interests other than the personal interests of the parties shall not render the proceeding void unless they result in harm to the party invoking the nullity and provided that they are raised before entering into the merits of the case.
