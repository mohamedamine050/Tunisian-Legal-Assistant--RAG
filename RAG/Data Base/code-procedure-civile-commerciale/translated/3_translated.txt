Any agreement that violates the provisions of this law is null and void. 

Crimes that are punishable by death or imprisonment for a period exceeding five years are classified as felonies within the meaning of this law.

Crimes that are punishable by imprisonment for a period exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars, are classified as misdemeanors.

Crimes that are punishable by a penalty not exceeding fifteen days of imprisonment or sixty dinars in fines are classified as violations. 

Article 122 of the Code of Criminal Procedure determines the specific judicial jurisdiction.
