**Challenge** (**تَجْرِيح**) **of an Expert**

The challenge of an expert shall be made in the same manner as the challenge of a witness, and must be filed within five days from the date on which the party became aware of the expert's appointment. The challenge of an expert, **like** the challenge of a witness, shall be ruled on in the same manner as a challenge to conduct that incites or encourages hatred, disgust, contempt, or severe ridicule based on a person's race or religion. This ruling is not subject to any form of appeal.
