The provisions of the preceding section, subject to the conditions set out below, shall apply to the salaries and wages of civil servants and employees belonging to the State, public institutions, and local communities. These provisions shall also apply to the salaries of officers and those similar to them, non-commissioned officers, military personnel, and naval personnel, and those similar to them from the Army, Navy, and Air Force, whether in active service, on temporary leave, on leave of absence, or discharged, or on active duty leave. They shall also apply to the salaries of general officers of the reserve force. 

With regard to Army, Navy, and Air Force personnel, any salary increases considered for the purpose of deductions shall be determined by their own specific regulations, administrative decisions of a general nature not relating to any specific individual, applicable to each branch of the armed forces or services.
