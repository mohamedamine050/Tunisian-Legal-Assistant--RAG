**precautionary seizure**
**precautionary seizure** is a precautionary, temporary measure that preserves the right from threat and the possibility of failing to obtain it, paving the way for the exercise of **enforcement seizure** which enables the creditor to sell the debtor's assets and recover his debt from their proceeds.
 takes effect. 
**enforcement seizure**
**enforcement seizure**
The **enforcement seizure** empowers the creditor holding an enforceable instrument to place the movable property in the debtor's possession under the authority of the court.
  It will not be implemented unless otherwise authorized or a judicial trustee is appointed.
