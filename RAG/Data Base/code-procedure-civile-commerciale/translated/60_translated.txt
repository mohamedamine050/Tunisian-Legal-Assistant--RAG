(Amended by Law No. 82 of 2002 dated August 3, 2002.) - If the debt exceeds one hundred and fifty dinars, the creditor, before filing the claim, must serve the debtor with a notice through an executing bailiff, informing him that if he does not pay the debt within five full days, enforcement proceedings shall be initiated against him. The notice must be accompanied by a copy of the debt instrument. If the debtor resides outside Tunisia, the aforementioned period shall be extended to thirty days.
