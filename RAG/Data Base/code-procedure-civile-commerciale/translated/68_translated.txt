(Amended by Law No. 87 of 1986 dated September 1, 1986).- **Representation by a lawyer is mandatory before the Court of First Instance.**

**Court of First Instance**

The Court of First Instance is generally the first court to hear a dispute and issue a decision, except in matters of personal status. The lawyer's office is deemed to be the chosen address for their client in the instance of litigation in which they are acting as a representative.
