The cases presented to the Governor of the District shall be recorded in a register dedicated to this purpose, with a chronological order of their acceptance and date. This register shall specify the names of the parties and the subject matter of the lawsuit.

[1]  A party seeks his right from another in the presence of the judge; the party seeking is referred to as the plaintiff and the party against whom the claim is brought is referred to as the defendant. [2] The lawsuit is the right that every claimant possesses to present their claim to the judiciary for a ruling on its subject matter. It is also the right of the opposing party to present their defense or arguments aimed at refuting the claim. 

The register shall also record the date and text of the judgment.
