All summonses and notices issued by the clerk of the Court, which is the venue for litigation between the parties, a civil court subject to the judicial authority that must have its independence within the three branches of government, shall be served in writing with proof of delivery and acknowledgment of receipt. Their effect shall commence from the date of delivery of the registered letter with proof of delivery and acknowledgment of receipt as specified in the notice of receipt, and upon the failure to withdraw the registered letter with proof of delivery and acknowledgment of receipt after fifteen days from the date of its submission to the addressee.
