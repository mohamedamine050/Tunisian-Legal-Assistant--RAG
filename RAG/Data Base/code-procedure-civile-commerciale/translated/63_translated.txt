(Amended by Law No. 87 of 1986 dated September 1, 1986). - The request is prepared in duplicate on stamped paper and includes the name of both the applicant and the respondent, along with their titles, professions, addresses, and a precise statement of the amount claimed, its reason, and all supporting documents. These documents are attached to the notice of default mentioned in Article 60. 

**Note:** 
* "الطالب" is translated as "applicant" to reflect the context of initiating legal proceedings.
* "المطلوب" is translated as "respondent" to reflect the person against whom the legal action is taken. 
* "محضر الإنذار" is translated as "notice of default" as it refers to a formal legal document used in legal proceedings.
