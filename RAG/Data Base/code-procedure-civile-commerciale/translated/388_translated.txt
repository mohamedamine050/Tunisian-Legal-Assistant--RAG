All acts, decisions, and obligations referred to in the preceding chapter shall be registered free of charge and their copies shall be drawn up on unstamped paper. Registered letters and powers of attorney, including powers of attorney granted to someone acting on behalf of the principal, and receipts delivered during the proceedings are exempt from stamp duty and registration fees. 

The parties may appoint a duly registered lawyer or any other person of their choice as their representative. In such cases, the powers of attorney issued by the capable creditor must be specific to each case and subject to stamp duty and registration fees. 

Registered letters and notifications of receipt are exempt from postal stamp duty.
