Every offense described as a felony in the meaning of this Criminal Code is a crime punishable by death or imprisonment for a period exceeding five years.

Offenses described as misdemeanors are crimes punishable by imprisonment for a period exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars.

Offenses described as infractions are crimes punishable by a penalty not exceeding fifteen days of imprisonment or sixty dinars of fine. 122 Code of Criminal Procedure
**Article 20** 
The perpetrator shall be punished by a fine of one hundred (100) to one thousand (1000) dinars.
