Any person who, through any of the means set forth in Article 42, incites soldiers with the intention of:
* inducing them to neglect their military duties and to disobey their superiors in all matters they are ordered to carry out in accordance with military laws and regulations;
* preventing young men from joining military service or delaying such enlistment; or
* persuading those who have not yet been called up for military service but are subject to conscription under the conscription law to refuse to comply with their military obligations, shall be punished by imprisonment for a term of one to five years and a fine of 1000 to 2000.
