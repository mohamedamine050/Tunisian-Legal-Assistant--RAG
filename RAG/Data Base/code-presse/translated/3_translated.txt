The following shall not be subject to the legal deposit stipulated in the preceding Article:

- Administrative publications, such as forms, standard formats, invoices, lists, drawings, ledgers, etc.

- Small publications referred to as city publications, such as letters, summons cards, notifications, address cards, visiting cards, and envelopes indicating their source.

- Publications referred to as commercial publications, such as definitions, instruction cards, labels, sample cards, etc.

- Election cards and financial value drawings.
