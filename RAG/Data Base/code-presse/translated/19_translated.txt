All owners, partners, shareholders, financiers, and others who contribute to the financial life of a periodical publication that is not purely scientific, technical, or technological in nature must be Tunisian nationals.

Anyone found to have lent their name, in any manner, to an owner or financier of a periodical publication shall be punished with a fine ranging from 10,000 to 40,000 dinars. This punishment applies to both the original perpetrators and their accomplices. The court may also order the suspension of the publication.

In the event that a company or association is found to have lent its name, the criminal liability stipulated in this chapter shall extend to the chairman of the board, the agent, or the managers, as applicable to the type of company or association.
