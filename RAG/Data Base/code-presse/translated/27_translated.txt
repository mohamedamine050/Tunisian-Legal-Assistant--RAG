The publisher shall be obliged to include the replies of any person who has been explicitly or implicitly attacked. In the event of non-compliance, the publisher shall be punished by a fine of 12 to 120 dinars, irrespective of any other penalties that may result, and damages.

This inclusion shall take place within a period of three days for non-daily newspapers, and in the next issue for daily newspapers. The deadline shall commence from the date of communication of the replies.
