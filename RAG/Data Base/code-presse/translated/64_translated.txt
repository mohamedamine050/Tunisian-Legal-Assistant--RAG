## English Translation of the Arabic Legal Text:

**Publicity regarding defamation cases, as described in paragraphs (a), (b), and (c) of Article 57 of this Code, as well as deliberations related to cases of paternity, divorce, and abortion, is prohibited. This prohibition does not apply to judgments that may be published at any time with the permission of the judicial authorities.**

**In all civil cases, courts and tribunals may prohibit the publication of details of the cases.**

**Publication of the secrets of negotiations in courts and tribunals is also prohibited. The use of sound recording devices and photographic or cinematographic equipment is prohibited in the chambers and during sessions of the courts, unless a permit is issued by the competent judicial authority. Any violation of this provision shall be considered a felony within the meaning of this Criminal Code, meaning crimes that are punishable by death or imprisonment for more than five years.**

**Any violation of this provision shall be considered a felony within the meaning of this Criminal Code, meaning crimes that are punishable by death or imprisonment for more than five years.**

**Crimes that carry a sentence of imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or a fine exceeding sixty dinars, shall be considered misdemeanors. Crimes that carry a sentence of imprisonment not exceeding fifteen days or a fine of sixty dinars shall be considered offenses.**

**Violations of these provisions shall be punished with a fine of 120 to 1200 dinars, and the tools used for the violation shall be confiscated.**
