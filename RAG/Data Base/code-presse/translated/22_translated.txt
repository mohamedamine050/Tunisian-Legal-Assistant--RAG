**Notwithstanding the conditions imposed by current legislation, the acceptance by the owner of a periodical, its manager, or any of its employees of funds or benefits, directly or indirectly, from a foreign natural or legal person, except for funds and benefits received in connection with express authorization from the Ministry of Information, as well as funds paid for advertising within the limits authorized by Article 20 of this code, shall be punishable by imprisonment for a term of one to five years and a fine of 200 to 2,000 dinars, or one of the two penalties. The penalty shall apply to the primary perpetrators and their accomplices.**
