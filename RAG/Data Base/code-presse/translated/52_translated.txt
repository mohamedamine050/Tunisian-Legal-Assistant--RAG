The same punishment shall be imposed on the perpetrator of defamation which is not proven by the same means mentioned above, against a member or members of the government because of their plan or position, or against a deputy or deputies in the House of Representatives, or a public official, or a person employed by the government to perform public duties, or a holder of public authority, or one of the agents of public authority, or a citizen, or a person entrusted with a public service or mandate, whether temporary or permanent, or a witness, for the purpose of giving their testimony.

The punishment shall not be less than the minimum prescribed in the preceding paragraph.

Furthermore, the court shall order the publication of the contents of its judgment in a weekly publication at the expense of the person against whom the judgment is rendered.
