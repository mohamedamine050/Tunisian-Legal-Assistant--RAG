Every periodical publication shall fix for a period of three months its advertising rates and, where applicable, its joint advertising rates with another periodical publication or several other periodical publications, and shall inform all interested parties thereof. The advertiser shall have the right to choose the rate he prefers. The use of a rate contrary to the rate actually fixed for each three-month period shall be prohibited. 

Crimes that carry a penalty of death or imprisonment for a term exceeding five years are described as felonies within the meaning of this law. 

Crimes that carry a penalty of imprisonment exceeding fifteen days and not exceeding five years, or a fine exceeding sixty dinars, are described as misdemeanors.

Crimes that carry a penalty not exceeding fifteen days of imprisonment or sixty dinars fine are described as violations.
