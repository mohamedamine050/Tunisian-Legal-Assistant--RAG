If the accused wishes to be authorized to prove libel in accordance with the provisions of Article 57 of this Code, he must submit to the Public Prosecution, by means of a declaration addressed to the Court's Registry or to the complainant at the place he has chosen as his residence, as the summons may be issued at the request of the former or the latter, within a period of ten days from the date of receipt of the summons:

Firstly: A statement of the acts attributed and described in the notification or summons, which he wishes to prove the truth of.

Secondly: A copy of the documents.

Thirdly: The names of the witnesses he wishes to call, their professions and their residences. In addition, the accused must, within the same time limit, designate a place for communication with him within the jurisdiction of the Court, otherwise he shall forfeit his right to present evidence.
