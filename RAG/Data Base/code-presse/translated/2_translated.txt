## English Translation:

Subject to the legal deposit procedures are:

1) Printed works of all types, including books, periodicals, volumes, drawings, illustrated prints, picture postcards, posters, geographical maps, publications, calendars, magazines, and others.

2) The following works: musical, sound, and visual recordings, photographs, and software programs made available to the public for a fee or free of charge, or delivered with the intention of being republished.
