Any person who violates the obligations imposed upon them pursuant to the provisions of this Chapter and the texts taken for its application shall be punished by a fine ranging from 200 to 400 dinars and, in case of recidivism, from 400 to 800 dinars. 

Furthermore, anything that has been published or introduced into the Tunisian territory in violation of this law shall be classified as follows:

* **Crimes:** offenses that warrant punishment by death or imprisonment exceeding five years.
* **Misdemeanors:** offenses that warrant punishment by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars.
* **Offenses:** offenses that warrant punishment not exceeding fifteen days of imprisonment or sixty dinars fine. [Article 122, Code of Criminal Procedure]

For the aforementioned judgments, it may be seized pursuant to a decision issued by the Minister of the Interior, after obtaining the opinion of the Minister of Culture or the Secretary of State to the Prime Minister in charge of media, according to their respective jurisdictions. 

The court having jurisdiction may authorize the confiscation of copies that have been made available to the public in violation of this law, which shall be classified as follows:

* **Crimes:** offenses that warrant punishment by death or imprisonment exceeding five years.
* **Misdemeanors:** offenses that warrant punishment by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars.
* **Offenses:** offenses that warrant punishment not exceeding fifteen days of imprisonment or sixty dinars fine. [Article 122, Code of Criminal Procedure]
