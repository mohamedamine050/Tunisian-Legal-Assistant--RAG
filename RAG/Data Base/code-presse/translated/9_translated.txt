The distributor shall make a legal deposit of all publications or productions printed or produced abroad and entering Tunisian territory, and publicly offered for sale, rental, or distribution, before being made available to the public.

For all periodical publications issued abroad and imported into Tunisia, the distributor must deposit one copy with the Republic's Agency in Tunis, two copies with the Ministry of the Interior, and six copies with the Secretary of State for Media.

For all non-periodical publications issued abroad and imported into Tunisia, the distributor must deposit one copy with the Republic's Agency in Tunis, one copy with the Ministry of the Interior, and one copy with the Ministry of Culture.

If the matter concerns musical scores or audio recordings produced abroad and imported into Tunisia, the distributor must deposit a copy with the Arab and Mediterranean Music Center before making them available to the public.
