**English Translation:**

Imprisonment for a term of one to five years and a fine of 1000 to 2000 Sudanese pounds shall be imposed on anyone who insults the dignity of the President of the Republic by any of the means set out in Article 42 of this Code.

Imprisonment for a term of three months to two years and a fine of 100 to 2000 Sudanese pounds shall be imposed on anyone who intentionally insults one of the authorized religious rituals by the means mentioned above.
