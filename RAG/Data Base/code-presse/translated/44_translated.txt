Any person who, by the means referred to in Article 42, directly incites hatred between races, religions, or populations, or who promotes ideas based on racial discrimination or religious extremism, or who incites the commission of the misdemeanors stipulated in Article 48, or who encourages the population to violate the laws of the country, shall be punished by imprisonment for a term of two to three years and a fine of 1,000 to 2,000 dinars.
