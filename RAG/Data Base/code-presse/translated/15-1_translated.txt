The manager of every periodical publication with a general news character must at all times demonstrate that they employ full-time journalists who hold a national professional card and are either holders of a certificate of completion of courses in journalism and news sciences issued by a Tunisian higher education institute or a certificate of the same specialization recognized as equivalent thereto.

The number of such employees must be at least equal to half of the permanent editorial staff of each publication.

With respect to publications whose editorial department is staffed by one or two full-time individuals, it is imperative that one of them holds a certificate within the meaning of the first paragraph of this chapter.
