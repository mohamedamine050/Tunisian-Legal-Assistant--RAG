Any person committing an offense of defamation against private individuals by any of the methods mentioned in Article 42 of this Code shall be punished by imprisonment for a term of thirteen days to six months and a fine of one hundred and twenty dinars to one thousand two hundred dinars or by one of these penalties only, notwithstanding the provisions of Article 87 of the Code of Contracts and Obligations.

Any person committing an offense of defamation by the methods mentioned in this article against a group of persons not mentioned in this Article but who belong in origin to a certain race or religion, shall be punished by imprisonment for a term of one month to one year and a fine of one hundred and twenty to one thousand two hundred dinars if the purpose of the defamation is to incite hatred among citizens or residents.
