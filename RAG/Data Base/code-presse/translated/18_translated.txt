Every periodical publication shall inform the public of the names of those who manage it. Furthermore, each periodical publication shall state the number of copies it prints for each issue. Additionally, it must publish its annual budget, accounts of transactions, and results before the 1st of July of the year following the year in which these operations were carried out.

Any violation of these provisions shall be subject to a fine of one hundred (100) to one thousand (1000) dinars for each issue printed in non-compliance with these provisions.
