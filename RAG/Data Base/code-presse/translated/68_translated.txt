Those who commit felonies and misdemeanors through the press, as defined below, shall be punished as principal offenders with the penalties prescribed for such crimes:

**Firstly,** the directors or publishers of publications, regardless of their profession or title.

**Secondly,** in the absence of the aforementioned, the authors.

**Thirdly,** in the absence of the authors, the printers or manufacturers.

**Fourthly,** in the absence of the printers or manufacturers, the sellers, distributors, or those who place advertisements.
