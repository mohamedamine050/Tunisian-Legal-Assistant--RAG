The provisions of Articles 51 to 54 of this Code shall not apply to defamation or insults directed against the deceased, except in cases where the intention is to attack the honor or reputation of the heirs, spouses, or ascendants who are still alive.

Heirs, spouses, or ascendants who are still alive shall have the right to exercise the right of reply provided for in Article 27 of this Code, whether or not the perpetrator of the defamation or insults intended to attack their honor or reputation.
