If the occupation of the public domain of ports involves the construction of buildings, installations, or fixed equipment, this shall only be done pursuant to a concession agreement.

The concession agreement is a legally binding contract that binds the contracting parties and defines their obligations and rights with respect to the specific transaction. 

Attached to the concession agreement is a set of specifications that specifically regulate the conditions for constructing buildings, installations, and equipment, as well as how they are to be used.
