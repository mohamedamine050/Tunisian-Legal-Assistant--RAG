The port equipment and facilities placed at the disposal of port users and stakeholders may be utilized and employed in one of the following ways:

* **Directly by the port authority or the port operator.**
* **Under a concession contract, with or without the obligation of public service, known as a "Concession for Public Port Equipment or Facilities".**
* **Under a license to use specific equipment or facilities, with or without the obligation of public service, known as a "License to Use Specific Port Equipment or Facilities".**
