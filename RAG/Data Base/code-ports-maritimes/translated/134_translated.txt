The inspection of violations of the provisions of this Code shall be carried out by reports drawn up by the officers referred to in Article 133 of this Code, in accordance with the provisions of the Code of Criminal Procedure. These reports must be signed by the aforementioned officers and must include the seal of the authority or administration to which they belong. The report shall state the date, place, time, and nature of the violation inspected, the identity and qualifications of the officers who drew up the report, and the statements and identity of the violator. 

The violator, or their representative, must sign the report. The officer drawing up the report must deliver a copy of it to the violator. In the event that the violator is absent or refuses to sign, this fact shall be stated in the report.
