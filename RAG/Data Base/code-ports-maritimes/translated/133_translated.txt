## English Translation:

The following officials, each within the scope of their respective jurisdictions, are entrusted with examining offenses:

* **Judicial Police Officers** as defined in Articles 1, 2, 3, and 4 of Chapter 10 of the Code of Criminal Procedure.
* **Commanders of National Maritime Units**.
* **Sworn Officials of the National Coast Guard**.
* **Customs Officers holding judicial police powers**.
* **Port Masters and Officers**.
* **Officers of the Maritime Authority**.
* **Sworn Officials of Category A affiliated with the Ministry of Transport**, qualified for this task.
* **Sworn Officials of Category A affiliated with the Ministry of Fisheries**, qualified for this task.
* **Sworn Officials of Category A affiliated with the Ministry of Tourism**, qualified for this task.
* **Sworn Officials of Category A affiliated with the Ministry of Environment**, qualified for this task.
* **Sworn Officials of the General Authority for State Property Supervision**.
* **Economic Control Officers**.

**Crimes** are classified as follows:

* **Felonies**: Crimes that are punishable by death or imprisonment exceeding five years, as defined by this law.
* **Misdemeanors**: Crimes that are punishable by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars.
* **Violations**: Crimes punishable by a maximum of fifteen days of imprisonment or a sixty dinars fine.
