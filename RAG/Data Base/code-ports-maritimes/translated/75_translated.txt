The master of a vessel transporting, loading, unloading, or carrying dangerous goods shall submit to the port authority the declaration stipulated in Article 37 of this Code, indicating the weight, nature, and class of these dangerous goods in accordance with the International Maritime Dangerous Goods (IMDG) Code, the method of packing, source, destination, location of stowage on board the vessel, shipping and transport documents, as well as the shipping permit issued by the competent maritime authority.
