In the event of the spread or spillage of hazardous materials on the quays or uncovered areas during loading, unloading, or storage operations, the intervening party causing such spread or spillage shall immediately fence off the area, take the necessary measures to mitigate risks, and notify the port authority. They shall also immediately undertake removal and cleaning operations, taking the necessary precautions to prevent any damage to health or the environment. 

In the event of negligence, the port authority shall take immediate measures and hold the party responsible for such spread or spillage liable for the cost thereof, regardless of the penalties stipulated in this Code. The party responsible shall be liable to respond to their actions before the law or compensate for the damage caused.
