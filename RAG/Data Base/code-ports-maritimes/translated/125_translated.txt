The Minister in charge of Transportation, the Minister in charge of Maritime Fisheries, and the Minister in charge of Tourism shall convene advisory councils, namely the "National Council of Commercial Seaports," the "National Council of Maritime Fisheries Ports," and the "National Council of Recreational Ports," respectively. 

These structures are entrusted, each according to its competence, with the following:

* Providing opinions, particularly on:

    * Development and maintenance of port infrastructure.
    * Exploitation and management of public domain of ports, equipment, and port facilities.
    * Improving services, simplifying procedures, modernizing, and enhancing the competitiveness of seaports.
    * Safety, security, and health regulations.
    * Activities in seaports.

The composition and functioning of each council shall be regulated by an order proposed by the Minister responsible for the port in question.
