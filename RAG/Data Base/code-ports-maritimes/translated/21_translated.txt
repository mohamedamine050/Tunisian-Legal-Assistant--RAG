If a temporary occupancy license is granted for a period of five years, its withdrawal before the expiry of this period for a reason unrelated to a breach of the terms and conditions of the license shall entitle the occupant to claim compensation and damages.

**Damages:**

[1] Injury inflicted in violation of the law, affecting a person's body, mental or psychological health, reputation, property, or honor. [2] Loss or damage to property, deprivation of comfort, or harm to physical well-being, reputation, or the like. - Damages in crimes and quasi-crimes are the actual losses suffered by the claimant and the necessary expenses incurred or to be incurred to repair the consequences of the act that caused the harm, as well as the loss of benefit within the normal scope of the consequences of this act.

In all cases, the occupant shall vacate the premises occupied and return them to the licensor in the same condition as they were received.
