The amounts of [settlement] shall be determined in accordance with definitional tables to be regulated by ministerial order, upon the proposal of the relevant ministers, each in respect of their respective areas of responsibility.

**[1] Settlement** is a contract entered into to resolve a dispute and terminate litigation. This is achieved by each party relinquishing some of their claims or by the delivery of money or a right.

**[2] Settlement** is a contract whereby the parties terminate an existing dispute or prevent a potential dispute, by each party reciprocally relinquishing their right.
