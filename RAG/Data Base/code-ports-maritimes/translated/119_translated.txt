Except for the professions stipulated in the current legislation concerning maritime professions, customs brokers and intermediaries, professions are practiced at the port subject to the following conditions:

1. **Natural or legal persons wishing to practice a profession at the port must be Tunisian nationals.**

2. **Natural persons wishing to practice a profession at the port must be in full possession of their civil rights.**
This condition applies to the legal representative of the legal person.

3. **Professions at the port must be practiced in accordance with specifications approved by a decision of the Minister responsible for the port's operations.** These specifications specifically define the conditions relating to professional competence and the material resources required for the practice of the activity. The list of port professions is determined by a decision of the Minister responsible for the port's operations.
