Land located outside the port's precinct and belonging to the public domain of ports may be allocated for the development and expansion of the port, the concentration of activities related to it, and for the creation and exploitation of logistics service zones. The port authority shall prepare these lands and ensure safety, security, cleanliness, health, environmental protection, and the regulation of traffic and circulation within them. The port authority may, when necessary, propose the establishment of public port zones to concentrate activities related to the port, international transport, or logistics services.
