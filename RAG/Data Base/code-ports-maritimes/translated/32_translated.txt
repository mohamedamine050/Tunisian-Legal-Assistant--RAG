The master of a vessel, while maneuvering within the port, shall adhere to the rules for preventing collisions at sea. Furthermore, the master shall reduce the vessel's speed to a level that ensures safe navigation when approaching installations, vessels at anchor, or vessels moored to the wharf, or while transiting a strait, canal, or work area in the port, or during salvage operations. The master of a vessel is prohibited from anchoring outside designated areas and from impeding the freedom of navigation in the basins, open anchorage areas, and the port canal.
