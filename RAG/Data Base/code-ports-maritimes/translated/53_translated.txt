The port authority, the port operator, or the operator of a port terminal shall establish special plans for immediate intervention in the public domain of ports or the port terminal it operates, concerning safety, security, health, pollution prevention and control. These plans shall be implemented within a comprehensive system of control over the quality of services, safety, security, health, pollution prevention and control, and energy management. These plans shall be approved by a joint decision of the Minister responsible for port activities and the Minister of the Interior, upon the proposal of the port authority. The port operator shall inform the competent authorities in the event of an emergency that necessitates the implementation of these plans, which shall be executed in accordance with the legislation and regulations in force.
