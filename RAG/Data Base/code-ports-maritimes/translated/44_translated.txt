The conditions for entry, movement, parking, and stopping of vehicles, as well as loading, unloading, and storage equipment within the port area are governed by the specific regulations of maritime ports. The parking of vehicles or cargo loading and unloading equipment in places other than those designated for them within the port is prohibited.

Any violations of this prohibition are classified as follows:

* **Crimes** are defined as offenses punishable by death or imprisonment exceeding five years. 
* **Misdemeanors** are defined as offenses punishable by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars.
* **Offenses** are defined as offenses punishable by imprisonment not exceeding fifteen days or by a fine not exceeding sixty dinars. 

This prohibition allows the port authority to transport such vehicles and equipment at the expense and responsibility of their owners or users.
