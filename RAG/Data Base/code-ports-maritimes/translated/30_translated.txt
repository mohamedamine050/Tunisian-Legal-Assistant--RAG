The ship's outfitter or operator, its captain or their representatives, must, by any means of communication, notify the competent authority and the port operator of their intention to berth before the ship arrives at the port. The details to be included in the berth notification, as well as the methods and deadlines for its submission, shall be determined by a decision of the minister responsible for the port's activities. Any violation of the provisions of this section shall result in the loss of the right of priority to enter the port.
