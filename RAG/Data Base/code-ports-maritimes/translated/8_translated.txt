The public domain of maritime ports consists of open berthing areas, the entrance channel, port protection facilities, basins, affiliated maritime navigation facilities, wharves, surfaces, uncovered areas, warehouses, and the adjacent land belonging to the port. The procedures for determining the maritime boundaries of the public domain of maritime ports are established by an order, upon the proposal of the Minister in charge of transport, after obtaining the opinion of the Minister in charge of equipment, the Minister in charge of State property and real estate affairs, the Minister in charge of the environment, and the Minister responsible for the port’s operation. The maritime and terrestrial boundaries of the public domain of maritime ports are established by an order for each port, in accordance with current legislation, upon the proposal of the Minister in charge of equipment, after obtaining the opinion of the Minister in charge of State property and real estate affairs, the Minister in charge of transport, and the Minister responsible for the port’s operation.
