Settlement 
[1] An agreement made to resolve a dispute and end litigation. This is done by each party in the settlement agreeing to waive some of their claims or by delivering money or a right. [2] An agreement by which the parties end an existing dispute or prevent a potential one, by each party reciprocally waiving their rights.
