The captain or owner of a recreational vessel shall, upon arrival at a port after an international voyage, submit to the port authority the declaration referred to in Article 37 of this Code. The content of this declaration, its form, the number of copies, and the administrative procedures relating to it shall be determined by a joint decision of the Minister responsible for tourism, the Minister responsible for finance, and the Minister of the Interior. The port authority shall deliver to the declarant a copy of the declaration provided for in the first paragraph of this article. The captain or owner of the vessel, or their representative, shall, during the vessel's movements within Tunisian territorial waters, present a copy of the declaration provided for in the first paragraph of this article upon each inspection.
