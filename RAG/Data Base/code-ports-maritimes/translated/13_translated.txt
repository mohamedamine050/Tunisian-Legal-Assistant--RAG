If the access channels to commercial maritime ports lead to fishing or recreational harbors or docks, the responsibilities entrusted to commercial port officers related to maritime navigation safety extend to the shared parts of the channels, docks, and other water areas of the ports, particularly concerning maritime traffic. With due regard to the provisions of the first paragraph of this chapter, if the access channels lead to fishing and recreational harbors or docks, the responsibilities entrusted to the port officers under the port authority having jurisdiction over these channels shall extend to the water areas of joint use, particularly concerning maritime traffic.
