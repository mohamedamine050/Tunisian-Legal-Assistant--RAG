## English Translation:

Assistance to vessels by towing in commercial maritime ports shall be optional, except in the following cases and unless otherwise determined by the port authority:

1. If the maneuvers performed by the vessel are difficult, or if the vessel is unable to control its maneuvers.
2. If the vessel is carrying explosives, fuel, liquefied gases, chemicals, dangerous bulk cargo, or radioactive materials.
3. If the size and technical characteristics of the vessel necessitate it.
4. If the climatic conditions and the safety and security of the vessels, navigation, and port facilities require it.

No salvage operation shall be undertaken within the port or its dependencies without a license from the port authority or from the port operator.
