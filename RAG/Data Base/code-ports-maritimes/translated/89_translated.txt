The ship's master, owner, or operator, or their representative, shall, before each sailing, whether for travel between Tunisian ports or for sea voyages within Tunisian territorial waters, inform the port authority or the port operator of the intention to sail, the time of sailing, and the intended destination.

Upon receipt of the sailing notification, the port authority shall register it in a dedicated logbook and immediately issue a sailing notification to the other relevant authorities. The notification shall include the identity of the ship's owner, its identification details, the time of sailing, and the intended destination.
