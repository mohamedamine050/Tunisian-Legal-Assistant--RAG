The reports prepared in accordance with the conditions stipulated in Article 134 of this Code shall be referred, through the administrative chain, to the **Procurator-General** of the Republic for follow-up, with a copy being sent to the maritime authority. The offender who does not have a fixed residence in Tunisian territory and against whom legal proceedings have been initiated shall be required to provide a financial guarantee equal to the maximum amount of the fine prescribed by Article 143 of this Code, until a **settlement**, as defined in Article 143 of this Code, is reached or a final **judgment** is rendered in the matter. The ship or movable property belonging to the offender who does not have a fixed residence in Tunisian territory may be temporarily seized, at the offender's expense, until the financial guarantee is paid.
