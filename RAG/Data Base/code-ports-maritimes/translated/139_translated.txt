Any person who violates the following provisions of this Code shall be punished with a fine ranging from 500 to 3000 dinars:

- The first paragraph of Article 32.
- The first paragraph of Article 41.
- The first and fourth paragraphs of Article 43.
- The first paragraph of Article 47.
- The second paragraph of Article 52.
- The first paragraph of Article 60.
- Articles 62 and 63.
- The last paragraph of Article 68.
- The first paragraph of Article 78.
- Article 80.
- The second paragraph of Article 81.
- The second paragraph of Article 92.
