It is prohibited to undertake any action that could harm the port facilities and its appurtenances, as well as the water areas, particularly their depth or water quality.  Furthermore, the following are prohibited:

*  Discharging polluted or used water, hazardous materials, or substances detrimental to health or environmental safety into the port waters and its appurtenances. 
*  Discharging soil, debris, wreckage, waste, or any materials or goods into the port waters and its appurtenances. 
*  Loading and unloading fragile or friable materials, or transferring them from one ship to another without prior authorization from the port authority.
