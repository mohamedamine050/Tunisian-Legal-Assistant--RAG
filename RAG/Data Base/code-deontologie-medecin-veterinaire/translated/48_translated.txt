Experts, during examinations, counter-examinations, and counter-inspections of meat or foodstuffs, shall not proceed with these operations without obtaining a license issued by the judge or the administration that commissioned them, or a certified copy of the agreement that appointed them as experts. They must summon, by registered letter with acknowledgment of receipt, the veterinarians involved in the dispute, and demand from them, on their behalf, the provision of all information useful for the performance of their duty.
