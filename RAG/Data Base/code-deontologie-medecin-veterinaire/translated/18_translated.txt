By virtue of a joint decision issued by the Ministers of Health and Agriculture, special provisions may be enacted concerning the following materials in terms of their supply, possession, or sale free of charge: 

1. Viral and bacterial materials intended for the diagnosis, prevention, and treatment of animal diseases.
2. Materials of organic origin intended for the same purposes, excluding those that contain only known chemical models.
3. Estrogenic materials.
4. Toxic materials.
5. Materials that may remain as toxic or dangerous residues in food products of animal origin.
6. Materials that have an effect that could be contrary to the legislation on the suppression of fraud.
7. Materials that may hinder health control related to products derived from animals that have consumed those products.
