In addition to the penalties provided for in the legislation currently in force in the field of foreign trade and consumer protection, and the procedures provided for in Chapters 12, 13 and 14 of this Law, any person who supplies or exports animals and animal products in violation of the provisions of Chapters 2, 3, 5 and 18 of this Law shall be punished with a fine ranging from 10,000 Dinars to 50,000 Dinars. In case of recidivism, the penalty shall be doubled to its maximum amount, and the offender shall be punished with imprisonment from 16 days to 3 months.
