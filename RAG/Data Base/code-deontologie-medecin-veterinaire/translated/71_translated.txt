A veterinarian who has, for a period of at least six months, undertaken interventions, prescribed treatments, conducted monitoring, or received funding from the State, shall not be allowed to establish a private practice, work as an assistant, or receive compensation for a period of five years after ceasing their duties in the province or provinces where they were employed, unless they have submitted their resignation to the relevant administration and obtained, by virtue of a reasoned decision, the approval of the regional council or councils concerned of the Veterinary Surgeons' Order. In the event of a dispute, a legal dispute between two parties, the matter shall be referred to the National Council.
