The health commission shall be delegated at the request of the concerned party and pursuant to a decision of the Minister of Agriculture, after consulting with the National Council. This decision shall specify the area of intervention of the health commission. The Minister of Agriculture may, by decision, temporarily or permanently withdraw the health commission from the veterinarian who does not fully or partially perform the duties entrusted to him, as well as in the case of non-compliance with the legislation relating to contagious diseases. The final or temporary withdrawal of the license to practice veterinary medicine shall terminate the health commission.
