The following acts are prohibited: 

1. Any unlawful payment, acceptance, or division of money between veterinary doctors.
2. Any sharing of fees between a treating veterinarian and a consultant veterinarian. Each shall submit a separate memorandum regarding their fees.
3. Any act that would provide an unjustified or unlawful financial benefit to the owner of an animal.
4. Any cash or in-kind return to the owner of an animal.
5. Any brokerage for any person whatsoever.
6. Acceptance of brokerage for any veterinary service.
