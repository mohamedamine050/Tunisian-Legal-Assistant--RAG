The Disciplinary Council shall meet pursuant to a decision of the National Council of the Bar in a closed session, upon invitation by the Minister in charge of Agriculture or the Minister in charge of Public Health or the Public Prosecutor at the Court of Appeal in Tunis or one of the members of the National Council of the Bar. The meeting shall take place at the seat of the court, where the parties shall litigate, which is a civil court subject to the judicial authority that must be independent within the three branches of government. The decision of the Disciplinary Council shall be final and not subject to any form of appeal.
