All previous provisions that contradict this Law are hereby abrogated, particularly those of Law No. 38 of 1958, dated March 15, 1958, concerning the practice and regulation of the veterinary profession. This Law shall be published in the Official Gazette of the Tunisian Republic and shall be enforced as a law of the State. Tunis, July 14, 1997. Zine El Abidine Ben Ali.
