Every veterinary surgeon has the right, within a period not exceeding three months from the date of his establishment or change of address, and after obtaining the approval of the regional council of the Veterinary Surgeons Order concerned, to notify the public thereof through newspapers in the form of three consecutive non-framed or enlarged announcements that shall only contain the following concise statements:

- The address, telephone number, and clinic hours.
- Officially recognized school, university, or scientific certificates and awards, as well as previous duties obtained through competitive examinations.
- Honorary distinctions recognized by the Republic of Tunisia.

These statements shall not contain any definitions or advertisements of a commercial nature. The veterinary surgeon who establishes or changes his address may make visits to official authorities in the area of his establishment to facilitate this process.
