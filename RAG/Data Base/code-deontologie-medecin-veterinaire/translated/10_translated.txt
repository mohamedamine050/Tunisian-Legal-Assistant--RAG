Guidance articles intended for the general public and drafted or signed by a veterinarian must have an educational character and contribute to bringing the public closer to the veterinary profession. This signature should not be followed by any indication of the veterinarian's place of residence.  If the article is of an advertising or commercial nature, and the author is associated with a company, the author's signature must be followed by the name of the company.  Any veterinarian using the press or audio-visual media must refrain from any personal or commercial advertising.  They must also inform the Regional Council of the Veterinary Association with territorial jurisdiction of their participation.
