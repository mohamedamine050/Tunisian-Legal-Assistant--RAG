Any person who fails to file a declaration or submit a contract,  within the time limits specified by tax legislation, shall be penalized with a fine ranging from 100 dinars to 10,000 dinars. This is in addition to the penalties stipulated in Articles 81 to 86 of this Code.  A contract is a legally binding agreement that obligates the contracting parties and defines their obligations and rights concerning the specific transaction. Any person who fails to file the declaration of existence stipulated in Article 56 of the Code on Income Tax for Individuals and Corporate Tax shall be penalized with a fine ranging from 1,000 dinars to 50,000 dinars. (This paragraph was added by Article 52 of Law No. 53 of 2015, dated December 25, 2015, relating to the Finance Act for 2016).

The fine shall not apply if the taxpayer settles their situation before the intervention of the tax authorities.
