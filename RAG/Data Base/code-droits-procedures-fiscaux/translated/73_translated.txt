Minutes of inspection of tax offenses are drawn up in special registers opened for this purpose by the tax authorities.  This is done according to a sequential numbering system.  For each minute, in addition to the information stipulated in Article 72 of this Code, the procedures followed and the results of these procedures are recorded. Tax revenues are the amounts collected by the state in the form of payments. A payment is a cash amount paid by individuals and companies proportionally to their income or profits.

Settlement [1] is a contract concluded to settle a dispute and end litigation, either by each party relinquishing part of their claims, or by the delivery of money or a right. [2] Settlement is a contract by which the parties terminate an existing dispute or prevent a potential one, each reciprocally waiving their rights.
