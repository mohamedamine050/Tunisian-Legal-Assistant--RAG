Oversights and errors relating to the circulation tax on motor vehicles, the annual tax on tourist cars with heavy-oil engines, and the additional annual tax on vehicles using liquefied petroleum gas may be rectified up to the end of the fourth administrative year following the civil year in which the tax became due, provided that the unsuitability of the vehicle for use is not proven by submitting a certificate issued by the Ministry of Transport services attesting to the vehicle's unsuitability for use, or a document attesting to its non-circulation issued by the competent authorities. (This paragraph was amended by Article 76 of Law No. 54 of 2013, dated December 30, 2013, relating to the Finance Law for 2014, and by Article 55 of Law No. 53 of 2015, dated December 25, 2015, relating to the Finance Law for 2016).
