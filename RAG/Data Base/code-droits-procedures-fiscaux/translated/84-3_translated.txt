Any beneficiary of the work stoppage system for value-added tax who fails to settle the supply request vouchers approved by the tax audit authorities shall be punished.

In accordance with the legislation in force, an administrative tax penalty of 2000 dinars shall be imposed for each unsettled supply request voucher or voucher not presented, for the first five supply request vouchers.  The penalty shall be increased to 5000 dinars for each unsettled supply request voucher or voucher not presented for any subsequent vouchers. This article was added to Article 62 of Law No. 27 of 2012, dated December 29, 2012, relating to the Finance Law for 2013.
