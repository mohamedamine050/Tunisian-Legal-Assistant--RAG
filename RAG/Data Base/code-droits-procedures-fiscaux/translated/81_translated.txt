Any delay in payment, or part thereof, shall result in the application of a delay penalty equal to 0.75% of the amount due for each month or part thereof of delay, if the payment due is made automatically and without prior intervention by the tax authorities (the penalty rate was amended by Article 46 of Law No. 85 of 2006 dated December 25, 2006, relating to the Finance Law for 2007, and by Article 51 of Law No. 56 of 2018 dated December 27, 2018, relating to the Finance Law for 2019).  A fixed penalty shall be added to the delay penalty stipulated in the first paragraph of this article, calculated at a rate of: 1.25% of the amount due if the delay in payment does not exceed 60 days; 2.5% of the amount due if the delay in payment exceeds 60 days. (This paragraph was added by Article 51 of Law No. 56 of 2018 dated December 27, 2018, relating to the Finance Law for 2019).
