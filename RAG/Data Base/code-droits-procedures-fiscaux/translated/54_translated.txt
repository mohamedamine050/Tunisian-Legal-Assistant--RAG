The primary courts have original jurisdiction over claims concerning objections to compulsory employment decisions or concerning the recovery of performance.  These courts also have jurisdiction to hear appeals against acts of service, summonses, notifications, and other procedures related to compulsory employment or the recovery of performance, within the framework of the claims referred to in the first paragraph of this chapter. This paragraph was added by Chapter 60 of Law No. 70 of 2007, dated December 27, 2007, relating to the 2008 Finance Law.
