Any institution that fails to file the declaration as stipulated in the first paragraph of Article 17, subsection 3, of this Code by the deadline shall be sanctioned with an administrative financial penalty of 50,000 dinars.  A penalty of 100 dinars shall be imposed for each piece of information not provided, incompletely provided, or falsely provided in the declaration referred to in the preceding paragraph of this Article, provided that this penalty shall not exceed 10,000 dinars. (This Article was added by Article 34 of Law No. 56 of 2018, dated December 27, 2018, relating to the Finance Act for 2019).
