The tax authorities, within the framework of the monitoring or review provided for in Article 5 of this Code, may request all instructions, explanations, and justifications relating to the tax situation.  The assets collected by the state in the form of payments, wherein a payment is a cash sum paid by individuals and companies proportionally to their income or profits, are subject to demands for payment.  They are entitled to assess the payment and correct the declarations based on legal or factual evidence, particularly comparisons with data relating to similar exploitations, income sources, or operations.
