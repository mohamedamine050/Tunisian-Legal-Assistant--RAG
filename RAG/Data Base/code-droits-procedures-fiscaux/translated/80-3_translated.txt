Without prejudice to the powers entrusted to the tax authorities in examining and pursuing tax offenses, the officers of the Research and Combating Tax Evasion Unit shall be responsible for uncovering tax offenses and collecting evidence thereof throughout Tunisian territory, unless a decision has been issued to initiate an investigation into them.  The officers of the Research and Combating Tax Evasion Unit shall also prepare reports on tax offenses in which the Public Prosecutors have initiated investigations, and shall conduct the investigative procedures authorized by the Investigating Judges.
