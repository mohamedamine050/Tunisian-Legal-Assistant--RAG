The institutions stipulated in paragraphs one and two of Article 17 of this Code are obligated to provide the tax authorities, whenever requested in writing or periodically, with the information in their possession that is required from states associated with Tunisia by agreements on the exchange of information and assistance in tax matters, as required by each agreement. This must be done within twenty days of notification of the request, or thirty days before the deadline for transmitting the information abroad, in accordance with the agreement or understandings concluded for its implementation, regardless of whether a preliminary or in-depth tax review is required, and regardless of any prior request for the submission of this information by the taxpayer. Electronic communication may be used to request and obtain documents and information. (This Article was added pursuant to the provisions of Article 38 of Law No. 78 of 2016, dated December 17, 2016, concerning the Finance Law for 2017).
