The record of the inspection of the violation must include the following information:

1. The date, time, and place of the record. 2. The type of violation committed. 3. The name, surname, and profession of the offender if a natural person, or the company name and registered address if the offender is a legal person. 4. The seizure procedures followed, specifying the documents, goods, and objects seized. 5. The signature of the offender or their representative if present at the time of drawing up the record, or a statement indicating their absence or refusal to sign, as the case may be. 6. The stamp of the competent authority, the names, surnames, and signatures of the two officers who drew up the record.
