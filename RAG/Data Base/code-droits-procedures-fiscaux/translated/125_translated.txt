The limitation periods relate to the loss of the right to pursue a claim due to non-compliance with deadlines.  These periods commence from the date of submission of the formal demand for payment stipulated in Article 122 of this Code, or from the date of summons by the Conciliation Committee, if the file was submitted to the Committee at the initiative of the tax authorities and no prior formal demand for payment had been submitted.  Notification is considered effective upon the signature of the defendant or their agent, in the presence of the competent official, on a notification document or on the summons to appear at the appointed time. (This article was amended by Article 69 of Decree No. 21 of 2021 dated December 28, 2021, relating to the Finance Act for 2022).
