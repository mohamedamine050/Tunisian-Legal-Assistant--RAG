Notwithstanding any conflicting provisions in this Code, communications and correspondence relating to performance between tax authorities and those liable for performance may be exchanged by means of reliable electronic means, which shall confer evidentiary weight upon such communications and correspondence when invoked. The scope and methods of application of the provisions of this chapter shall be determined by a decision of the Minister of Finance. (This chapter was added by Chapter 24 of Law No. 78 of 2019, dated December 23, 2019, relating to the Finance Law for 2020, Chapter 11).

Subject to the specific deadlines set forth in this Code, the party liable for performance must respond in writing to the demands and notifications of the tax authorities addressed to it within thirty days of the date of service of the demand or notification.
