Subject to the provisions of Article 123 of the Code of Civil and Commercial Procedure, the judgments issued by the courts in cases relating to appeals against compulsory employment decisions shall include, in addition to the sums awarded as principal payments and penalties, the amendments adopted relating to performance surplus, losses, and deferred depreciation. (This article was added by Article 61 of Law No. 27 of 2012 dated December 29, 2012, relating to the Finance Law for 2013).
