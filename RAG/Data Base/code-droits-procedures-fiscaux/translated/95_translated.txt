Any person who issues invoices or fee notes without respecting the provisions of paragraph 11 of Article 18 of the Value Added Tax Code shall be punished by a fine ranging from 250 dinars to 10,000 dinars. This penalty, imposed in the name of society upon a person criminally liable for a committed crime, based on a court judgment, shall be applied to each violation.  Crimes which warrant punishment by death or imprisonment exceeding five years are described as felonies under this law.  Crimes which warrant punishment by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars, are described as misdemeanors. Crimes which warrant punishment not exceeding fifteen days imprisonment or sixty dinars fine are described as offenses. (Article 122 of the Code of Criminal Procedure). This penalty is applied regardless of the number of invoices or fee notes subject to the violation.  (This paragraph was amended by Article 22 of Law No. 53 of 2015, dated December 25, 2015, concerning the Finance Law for 2016). The same fine stipulated in the first paragraph of this Article shall apply to any person who fails to declare the names and addresses of their suppliers of invoices or fee notes to the competent tax office, and to any person who does not respect the requirements of paragraph 3 of paragraph 1 bis of Article 18 of the Value Added Tax Code. (This paragraph was amended by Article 22 of Law No. 53 of 2015, dated December 25, 2015, concerning the Finance Law for 2016, and by Article 31 of Law No. 78 of 2016, dated December 17, 2016, concerning the Finance Law for 2017). Any person who transports goods not accompanied by invoices or documents serving as such within the meaning of Article 18 of the Value Added Tax Code, or not accompanied by the transport documents required by tax legislation, shall be punished by a fine equal to 20% of the value of the goods transported, with a minimum of 500 dinars. The means of transport and the goods transported shall be seized until the fine is paid. The seizure shall be limited to the registration card for means of transport carrying perishable goods or goods intended for export, if justified. (This paragraph was amended by Article 23 of Law No. 54 of 2014, dated August 19, 2014, concerning the Supplementary Finance Law for 2014). The fines stipulated in this Article shall be doubled in case of recidivism within two years.
