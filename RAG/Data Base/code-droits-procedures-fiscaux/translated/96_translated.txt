Any person who prints invoices or fee notes that are unnumbered, or numbered in an irregular or discontinuous series, shall be punished by a fine ranging from 1000 Dinars to 50000 Dinars. (This paragraph was amended by Article 22 of Law No. 53 of 2015, dated December 25, 2015, relating to the Finance Law for 2016).

Any person who uses invoices or fee notes that are unnumbered, or numbered in an irregular or discontinuous series, shall be punished by a fine ranging from 50 Dinars to 1000 Dinars for each invoice or fee note. (This paragraph was amended by Article 22 of Law No. 53 of 2015, dated December 25, 2015, relating to the Finance Law for 2016).

The fines referred to in this article shall be doubled in case of recidivism within two years.
