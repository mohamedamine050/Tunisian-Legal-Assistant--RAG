Failure to pay the tax stamp duty due by the prescribed method of payment via declaration, or payment of this duty in a deficient manner, shall result in the application of a penalty equal to 50% of the unpaid duty amount, in addition to payment of the principal amount due. The tax stamp duty payable by declaration is subject to the late payment penalties stipulated in Articles 81 and 82 of this Code. (This article was amended by Article 49 of Law No. 85 of 2006, dated December 25, 2006, relating to the Finance Act of 2007).
