In the event of a transfer of ownership of vehicles subject to the road tax, the annual supplementary tax on used vehicles using liquefied petroleum gas, and the annual tax on tourist vehicles with heavy-oil engines, the delivery of the aforementioned vehicle registration certificates requires the presentation of a receipt from the competent departments of the Ministry of Transport, proving payment of the aforementioned taxes for the last year due. (This article was added by Article 34 of Law No. 77 of 2008, dated December 22, 2008, relating to the Finance Law for 2009).  The delivery of vehicle insurance certificates by insurance institutions or intermediaries requires the submission of a copy of the receipt for payment of road taxes for the period whose collection deadline fell on the date of delivery of the insurance certificates. (This paragraph was added by Article 56 of Law No. 53 of 2015, dated December 25, 2015, relating to the Finance Law for 2016).
