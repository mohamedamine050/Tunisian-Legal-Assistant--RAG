The National Council for Taxation is tasked with evaluating the tax system and its suitability to the established objectives, particularly those related to the balance of public finances, the achievement of economic efficiency, and tax justice.  It is not affiliated with any specific nation or geographical area in a particular or exceptional manner compared to other countries.

The assets collected by the state in the form of payments, wherein a payment is a cash sum paid by individuals and companies proportionally to their income or profits.

The National Council for Taxation issues its opinion on all tax-related matters submitted to it. The composition and management methods of the National Council for Taxation are regulated by decree.
