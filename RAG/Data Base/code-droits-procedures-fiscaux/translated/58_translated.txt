The tax authorities may effect service.  Service shall be deemed effected by the signature of the defendant, or their agent in the presence of the competent officer, on a service document, or on the writ of summons for attendance at the appointed time.  Summonses and notifications relating to the claims stipulated in Article 54 of this Code, as well as judgments and decisions issued concerning these claims, may be served through their officers or by Treasury bailiffs or executing bailiffs. The phrase "officers of the financial services" has been replaced by the phrase "Treasury bailiffs" pursuant to Article 6 of Law No. 18 of 2012 dated September 25, 2012, relating to the amendment of certain provisions of the Public Accounting Code in order to create a special corps of Treasury bailiffs under the Ministry of Finance.
