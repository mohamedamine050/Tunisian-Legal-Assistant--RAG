Any taxpayer subject to value added tax who makes sales under a system of suspension of this tax and the taxes due on the transaction number based on general certificates, without possessing the original supply request vouchers required to be submitted by the beneficiary of the suspension of the value added tax, shall be subject to an administrative tax penalty equal to 50% of the amount of the tax and the subject matter of the suspension. (This article was added to Article 41 of Law No. 54 of 2013, dated December 30, 2013, relating to the Finance Act for 2014).
