Officers of the Research and Tax Evasion Control Unit shall receive, as needed, statements from suspects of committing tax offenses, as well as statements from anyone they deem useful to hear.  Minutes shall be drawn up thereof. A legal copy of the minutes may be requested within a maximum period of ten days from its date. This does not preclude the tax authorities from conducting preliminary, in-depth, or limited reviews of the tax situation of taxpayers.

Taxpayers are not prevented from the intervention of officers of the Research and Tax Evasion Control Unit during the review, to conduct the investigations they have been entrusted or tasked with by the competent authorities. (This paragraph was amended by Article 48 of Decree No. 21 of 2021 dated December 28, 2021, relating to the Finance Act for 2022).
