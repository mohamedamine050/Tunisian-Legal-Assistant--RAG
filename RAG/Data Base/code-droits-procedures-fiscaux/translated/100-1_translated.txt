Anyone violating the provisions of Articles 17 and 17 bis of this Code shall be punished by a fine ranging from 1000 Dinars to 20,000 Dinars.  This shall be supplemented by a fine of 100 Dinars for each piece of information not provided, provided incorrectly, or incompletely. This paragraph was amended by Article 63 of Law No. 66 of 2017, dated December 18, 2017, concerning the Finance Law for 2018. The violation may be observed once every thirty days, starting from the previous observation, and the fine shall be doubled starting from the second observation. (This article was added by Article 15 of Law No. 54 of 2014, dated August 19, 2014, concerning the Supplementary Finance Law for 2014).
