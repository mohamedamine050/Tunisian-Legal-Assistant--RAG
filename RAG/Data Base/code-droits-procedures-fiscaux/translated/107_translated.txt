The corporal punishments stipulated in Articles 92, 94, 97, 98, 99, and 101 of this Code shall be applied, in personam, to the heads, agents, managers, or any person with the capacity to represent the legal entity or who effectively managed it, and whose responsibility for the committed acts has been established. (This paragraph was amended by Article 54 of Law No. 53 of 2015, dated December 25, 2015, relating to the 2016 Finance Act).
