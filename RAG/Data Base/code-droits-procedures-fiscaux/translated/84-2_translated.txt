Institutions that carry out transfers of income or profits without respecting the conditions stipulated in Article 112 of this Code shall be subject to an administrative tax penalty assessed as follows:

- 20% of the income or profits transferred if the transfer concerns income or profits subject to tax in Tunisia;

- 1% of the income or profits transferred if the transfer concerns income or profits not subject to tax in Tunisia. (This Article was added by Article 39 of Law No. 1 of 2012, dated May 16, 2012, relating to the Supplementary Finance Law for 2012).
