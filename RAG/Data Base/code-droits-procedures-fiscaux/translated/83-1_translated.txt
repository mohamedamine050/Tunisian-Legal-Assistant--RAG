Any person who fails to employ the advance payment stipulated in Article 51, fourthly, of the Code of Income Tax on Individuals and Corporate Tax, or who employs it deficiently, shall be punished by a fine equal to double the amount of the unemmployed or deficiently employed funds. (This article was added to Article 51 by Decree No. 21 of 2021, dated December 28, 2021, relating to the Finance Act for the year 2022).
