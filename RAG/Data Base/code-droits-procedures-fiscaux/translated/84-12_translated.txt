Any person who intentionally stipulates, within the contracts specified in the first paragraph of Article 45, Section 1, of Law No. 56 of 2018, dated December 27, 2018, relating to the 2019 Finance Law,  for settlement through an erroneous or fraudulent banking or postal method, and pays the price or part thereof in cash exceeding 5000 dinars, shall be punished with a fine equal to 20% of the amount paid in cash, provided that this fine shall not be less than 1000 dinars for each transaction. (This section was added to Article 45 of Law No. 56 of 2018, dated December 27, 2018, relating to the 2019 Finance Law).
