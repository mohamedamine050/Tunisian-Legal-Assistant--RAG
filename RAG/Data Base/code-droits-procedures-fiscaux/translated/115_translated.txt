The tax conciliator may seek assistance from the tax collection and recovery departments to investigate the petitions submitted to him or request clarifications from them for this purpose.  These departments shall assist him accordingly with the required diligence. If the tax conciliator finds the submitted petition justified, he may submit recommendations to the relevant department to address the issue raised in the petition, and this department shall inform him of the outcome of the efforts undertaken.  Should the department maintain its position, as communicated to the petitioner, the tax conciliator may submit a report on the matter to the Minister of Finance, accompanied by his proposals.
