Subject to the provisions of Article 81 of this Code, a penalty of 0.1% of the principal amount due shall be levied on each tax declaration filed without taking into account the legislation in force concerning the electronic submission and filing of tax returns via secure remote means. This penalty shall have a minimum of 200 Dinars and a maximum of 2000 Dinars. (This Article was added by Article 33 of Law No. 59 of 2014 dated December 26, 2014, relating to the Finance Law for 2015, and amended by Article 43 of Law No. 78 of 2016 dated December 17, 2016, relating to the Finance Law for 2017).
