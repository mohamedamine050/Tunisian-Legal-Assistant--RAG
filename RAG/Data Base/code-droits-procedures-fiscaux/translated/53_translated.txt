Disputes of substance include claims related to the principal performance, as referred to in Article 54 of this Code, as well as the offenses stipulated in Article 32 and Articles 81 to 86 of this Code.  This Article was amended by Article 42 of Law No. 59 of 2014, dated December 26, 2014, relating to the Finance Law for 2015, and by Article 35 of Law No. 78 of 2016, dated December 17, 2016, relating to the Finance Law for 2017.
