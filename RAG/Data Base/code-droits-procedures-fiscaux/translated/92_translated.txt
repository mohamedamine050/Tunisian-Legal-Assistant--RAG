Any person who employs the value-added tax, the consumption tax, or other indirect taxes due on the transaction number, including tax stamp duties paid by declaration, or who deducts the tax from the supplier, or who employs the advance payment provided for in Article 51, fourthly, of the Tax Code on the income of natural persons and corporate tax, and who fails to pay the amounts due to the treasury within six months from the first day following the expiry of the payment deadline, shall be punished by imprisonment for a period ranging from sixteen days to three years and a fine ranging from 1000 dinars to 50000 dinars. This is in addition to the payment of the principal tax and the penalties provided for in Articles 81 to 86 of this Code (this article was amended by Articles 51 and 54 of Decree No. 21 of 2021 dated December 28, 2021, relating to the Finance Act for 2022).
