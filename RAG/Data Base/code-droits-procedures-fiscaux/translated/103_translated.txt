Anyone violating the obligations stipulated in Articles 85, 98, 99, and the first paragraph of Article 100, and in Articles 101 and 135 of the Registration and Stamp Duty Code shall be punished by a fine ranging from 100 dinars to 1000 dinars.  In these cases, the offender shall be personally liable for the payment of the due fees and fines.  Anyone violating the provisions of Articles 96 and 97 of the Registration and Stamp Duty Code shall be punished by a fine ranging from 100 dinars to 1000 dinars.
