The refund of Value Added Tax (VAT) overpayments shall be made within a maximum period of one hundred and twenty days from the date of submission of the refund request fulfilling all legal requirements. (This paragraph was amended by Article 30 of Law No. 71 of 2009, dated December 21, 2009, relating to the Finance Act for 2010).

This period is reduced to sixty days for entities whose accounts are legally subject to audit by an auditor and whose accounts have been approved for the last closed financial year, the deadline for filing the corporate tax return for their results being the date of submission of the request, provided that the audit report does not contain reservations affecting the basis of the payment. (This paragraph was added by Article 30 of Law No. 71 of 2009, dated December 21, 2009, relating to the Finance Act for 2010).  The period is reduced to thirty days for VAT overpayments resulting from (This paragraph was amended by Article 31 of Law No. 71 of 2009, dated December 21, 2009, relating to the Finance Act for 2010):

- (The provisions of this paragraph relating to export transactions were repealed by Article 10 of Law No. 69 of 2007, dated December 27, 2007, relating to the stimulation of economic initiative)

- Deferred payment sales,

- Deduction of VAT from the supplier,

- Direct investment operations as defined in Article 3 of the Investment Act, undertaken by entities other than those operating in the financial sector and energy sectors, except for renewable energies, mines, real estate development, on-site consumption, trade, and telecommunications network operators (amended by Article 29 of Law No. 71 of 2009, dated December 21, 2009, relating to the Finance Act for 2010, and by Article 17 of Law No. 8 of 2017, dated February 14, 2017, relating to the review of the tax incentive system). - Rehabilitation investments carried out within the framework of a rehabilitation program approved by the Rehabilitation Program Management Committee. The deadline is reduced to seven days for the export of goods or services, calculated from the date of submission of the refund request accompanied by documents proving the export operation (This paragraph was amended by Article 31 of Law No. 71 of 2009, dated December 21, 2009, relating to the Finance Act for 2010).

The seven-day deadline also applies to the VAT overpayment referred to in paragraph 11 bis of Article 15 of the Value Added Tax Code and in the second sub-paragraph of the first paragraph of paragraph 1 bis of Article 54 of the Code of Income Tax on Individuals and Corporate Tax (This paragraph was added by Article 19 of Law No. 59 of 2014, dated December 26, 2014, relating to the Finance Act for 2015).  A penalty of 0.5% per month or part thereof from the date of the refund until the end of the month in which these amounts were paid, the debt was acknowledged, or the results of the tax audit were notified shall apply to amounts refunded without cause.  This is in addition to the penalties provided for in Articles 81 and 82 of this Code (The penalty rate was amended by Article 47 of Law No. 85 of 2006, dated December 25, 2006, relating to the Finance Act for 2007). Regardless of the provisions of the preceding paragraph, an administrative tax penalty of 100% of the VAT overpayment referred to in paragraphs 1 and 2 of item 1 of item 11 and in item III bis of Article 15 of the Value Added Tax Code and in Article 47 of Law No. 53 of 2015, dated December 25, 2015, relating to the Finance Act for 2016, which was refunded without cause, shall apply. (This paragraph was added by Article 35 of Law No. 78 of 2016, dated December 17, 2016, relating to the Finance Act for 2017).
