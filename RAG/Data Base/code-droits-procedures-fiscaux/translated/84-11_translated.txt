Any institution that fails to submit to the tax authorities the supporting documents for its transfer pricing policy, as stipulated in Article 38 bis of this Code, or submits incomplete or misleading documents within 40 days of the notification date specified in the same article, shall be subject to an administrative tax penalty equal to 0.5% of the amount of transactions concerned by the unsubmitted, incomplete, or misleading documents, with a minimum of 50,000 Dinars for each fiscal year subject to review. (This article was added by Article 34 of Law No. 56 of 2018, dated December 27, 2018, relating to the Finance Act for 2019).
