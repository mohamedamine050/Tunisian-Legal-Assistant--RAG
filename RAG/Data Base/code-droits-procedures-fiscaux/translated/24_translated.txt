The claim for the single compensatory fee for road transport due for a given year shall be made until the end of the fourth year following it. However, the period considered in calculating the fee cannot exceed six months for vehicles and trailers whose useful load exceeds 2 tons but does not exceed 5 tons, as well as for other vehicles not intended for the transport of persons or goods and authorized to use the road. (This paragraph was amended by Article 61 of Law No. 54 of 2013, dated December 30, 2013, relating to the Finance Law for 2014). The claim for the fee for vehicles and trailers whose useful load does not exceed 2 tons shall be made until the end of the calendar year in which the fee became due. This paragraph was added by Article 61 of Law No. 54 of 2013, dated December 30, 2013, relating to the Finance Law for 2014).
