The tax control process shall encompass the assets collected by the state in the form of payments, the payment being a cash amount paid by individuals and companies proportionally to their income or profits.

This is within the remit of tax officials and other qualified officials according to the law, each within the limits of their remit.  Tax officials, within the framework of control operations requiring special expertise and technical competence, may utilize the assistance of state and public institution officials or non-competing experts with respect to those liable for payment. This is done by assignment from the Minister of Finance or from one whom the Minister of Finance has authorized in this regard.
