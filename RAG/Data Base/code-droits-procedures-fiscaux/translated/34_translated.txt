The claimant shall benefit from the surplus of a refund at a rate of 0.5% of the amounts paid without cause as a result of compulsory employment, calculated for each month or part thereof elapsed from the date of collection to the date of the refund decision. (The penalty rate was amended by Article 47 of Law No. 85 of 2006, dated December 25, 2006, relating to the 2007 Finance Act).
