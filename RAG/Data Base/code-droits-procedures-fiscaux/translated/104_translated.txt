Any person who commits any of the following acts relating to the collection of products listed under numbers 22.03 to 22.08 of the Customs Tariff shall be punished by a fine ranging from 1000 Dinars to 50000 Dinars:

Producing and packaging these products in the same location contrary to the provisions of tax law;  Exploiting warehouses for these products without obtaining prior authorization or without providing the bank guarantee or providing an incomplete guarantee contrary to the provisions of tax law; Using distilling machinery to manufacture these products that is not sealed or closed by the tax authorities, or using it in circumstances where sealing or closing is technically impossible without the presence of qualified officers of these authorities; Extracting these products and disrupting them without the presence of qualified officers of the tax authorities contrary to the provisions of tax law. The fine shall be doubled in the event of a repeat offense within two years.
