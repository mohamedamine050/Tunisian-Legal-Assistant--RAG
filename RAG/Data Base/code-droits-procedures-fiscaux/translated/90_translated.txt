Subject to the provisions of Article 92 of this Code, and in the event of recidivism within five years, any person who fails to submit to the tax authorities the declarations, contracts, and documents required to be submitted under tax law within sixty days from the date of notification to settle their situation shall be punished by a fine ranging from 1000 dinars to 50000 dinars.  This is in addition to the penalties stipulated in Articles 81 to 86 of this Code.
