The issuance of building permits, certificates of registration for all types of motor vehicles, and registration of real estate lease agreements requires the submission of a copy of the receipt for the deposit of the latest declaration due for income tax, tax on income, taxes withheld on total income or income components, or corporate tax.  Similarly, registration of real estate transfer deeds or related rights, and deeds of transfer or lease of commercial assets requires the deposit of the tax returns for the preceding three years. (This article was amended by Article 11 of Law No. 54 of 2014 dated August 19, 2014, relating to the supplementary budget law for 2014).
