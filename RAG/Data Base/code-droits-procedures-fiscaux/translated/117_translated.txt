A committee, officially constituted within the tax collection authorities, composed of individuals with relevant expertise from various parts of the institution, called the "National Reconciliation Committee," is tasked with rendering opinions on tax review files,  before the issuance of a compulsory employment decision. (This paragraph was amended by Article 48 of Decree No. 21 of 2021, dated December 28, 2021, concerning the Finance Act of 2022).  The National Reconciliation Committee is responsible for examining files that the regional reconciliation committees referred to in Article 119 of this Code are not competent to examine.  Assets collected by the State in the form of contributions; a contribution is a cash amount paid by individuals and companies proportionally to their income or profits.
