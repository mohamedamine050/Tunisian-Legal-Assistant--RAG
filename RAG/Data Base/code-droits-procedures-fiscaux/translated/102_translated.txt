The penalties stipulated in Article 254 of the Criminal Code shall apply to anyone who violates the duty to maintain professional secrecy as stipulated in Article 15 of this Code.  The penalty is multiplied fivefold in the case of disclosure of information obtained within the framework of Articles 17, 17 bis, and 17 ter of this Code. This penalty, a sanction and remedy imposed in the name of society on a person criminally liable for a crime committed pursuant to a court judgment. (This paragraph was added by Article 49 of Law No. 53 of 2015 dated December 25, 2015, relating to the Finance Law for 2016, and amended by Article 63 of Law No. 66 of 2017 dated December 18, 2017, relating to the Finance Law for 2018, and by Article 35 of Law No. 56 of 2018 dated December 27, 2018, relating to the Finance Law for 2019).
