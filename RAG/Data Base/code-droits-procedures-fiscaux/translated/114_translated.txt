The tax auditor is appointed by order for a period of three years, renewable. The tax auditor may have regional representatives.  The placement of the appropriate individual in the position consistent with the conditions and requirements for its performance, in terms of qualifications and competencies, is undertaken. The appointment of the tax auditor's regional representatives and the determination of their territorial jurisdiction are effected by order. The organizational structure of the tax auditor's services and the operating procedures of these services are determined by order.
