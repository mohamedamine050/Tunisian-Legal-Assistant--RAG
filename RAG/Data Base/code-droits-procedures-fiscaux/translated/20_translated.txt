The time limit stipulated in Article 19 of this Code is extended to ten years for unauthorized payments and six years for authorized payments not exceeding the minimum amount stipulated in Article 48 of this Code. This time limit shall be calculated from the date of the contract, referral, letter, or judgment, in the case of registration fees. (This Article was amended by Article 34 of Law No. 78 of 2019, dated December 23, 2019, relating to the Finance Law for 2020).
