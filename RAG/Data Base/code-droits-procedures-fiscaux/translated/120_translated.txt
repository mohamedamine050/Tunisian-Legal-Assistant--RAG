The Regional Reconciliation Committee shall be composed of:

The Regional Representative of the Tax Collector, and in his absence, the Head of the Regional Center for Performance Monitoring or his representative:

- As Chairperson,

- An investigator who did not participate in the review of the files submitted to the committee, as a member,

- The Head of the structure responsible for supervising the offices, as a member,

- Two representatives of the tax claimants, as members, appointed by the Regional Representative of the Tax Collector or, in his absence, the Head of the Regional Center for Performance Monitoring, for each meeting and for each file from a list comprising representatives of professional organizations and bodies represented in the National Tax Council, determined by a decision of the Minister of Finance for a period of three years, renewable once, based on proposals from these organizations and bodies. (This article was amended by Article 69 of Decree No. 21 of 2021 dated December 28, 2021, relating to the Finance Act for 2022).

An employee of the Regional Center for Performance Monitoring shall act as the Committee's rapporteur without participating in the vote; he shall have at least the rank of Inspector of Financial Affairs.  The presence of the person who undertook the review of the file is mandatory without participating in the vote. The tax claimant shall be invited to attend the Committee in accordance with the provisions of Article 10 of this Code, or to appoint a representative in accordance with the law, and may also be assisted by a person of his choice.  The non-attendance of the tax claimant shall not affect the progress of the file. The Chairperson of the Committee may invite anyone whose presence he deems useful to participate in its work.
