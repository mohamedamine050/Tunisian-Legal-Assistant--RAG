The appointed hearing date for the case cannot be less than thirty days from the date of service.

Service is deemed effected by the signature of the defendant, or their representative in the presence of the competent official, on a service document, or on the writ of summons for the appointed date.  A copy of the writ of summons constitutes service.

[1] A request by one party for their right from another in the presence of a judge; the requestor is called the plaintiff and the one from whom it is requested is called the defendant. [2] A claim is the right of any claimant to bring their claim before the court for a judgment on its subject matter.  With respect to the defendant, it is the right to present grounds of defense or pleas aiming to refute that claim to the court.
