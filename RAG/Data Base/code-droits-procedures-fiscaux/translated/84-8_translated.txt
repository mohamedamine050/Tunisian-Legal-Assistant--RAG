Any person who fails to comply with the provisions of the first paragraph of Article 19, Section Four, of the Value Added Tax Code shall be fined 1000 Dinars. Without prejudice to the preceding provisions, any person who fails to return the certificate of use of the tax exemption provided for in Article 19, Section Four, of the Value Added Tax Code, and where applicable, the unused stamped supply request vouchers, within the time limit stipulated in the fourth paragraph of Article 47 of this Code, except in cases of force majeure proven in accordance with the law, shall be fined 1000 Dinars for each day of delay, up to a maximum of 30,000 Dinars. (This article was added by Article 30 of Law No. 66 of 2017, dated December 18, 2017, relating to the Finance Act for 2018).
