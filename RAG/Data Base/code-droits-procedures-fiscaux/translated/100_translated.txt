Anyone violating the provisions of Article 16 of this Code shall be punished by a fine ranging from 100 Dinars to 1000 Dinars, plus a fine of 10 Dinars for each piece of information not submitted, submitted incorrectly, or submitted incompletely. (This paragraph was amended by Article 14 of Law No. 54 of 2014, dated August 19, 2014, relating to the Supplementary Finance Law of 2014).

The violation may be observed once every ninety days starting from the previous observation, and this requires the application of the same fine.
