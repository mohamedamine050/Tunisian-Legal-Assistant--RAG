The Ministry of Finance shall establish a body called the "General Authority for Taxation, Public Accounting, and Collection."  The Authority's personnel shall primarily ensure control over the tax system, support tax compliance, and improve the collection of public resources, combating tax fraud and evasion within an integrated and comprehensive framework.  They shall be provided with the necessary legal guarantees to perform their duties. The Authority's organization and the regulations governing its personnel shall be established by a government order.  Collection is the process by which a creditor obtains payment from a debtor for a financial debt contracted with the creditor.
