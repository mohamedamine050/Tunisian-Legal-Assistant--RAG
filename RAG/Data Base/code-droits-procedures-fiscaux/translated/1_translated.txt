This Code sets forth the provisions relating to the rights and obligations of those claiming payment, and the procedures related thereto at the level of oversight and disputes concerning payments, duties, taxes, fees, and other tax revenues.

The assets collected by the State in the form of payments—payment being a monetary sum paid by individuals and companies proportionally to their income or profits—are subject to the provisions of this Code.  The term "payment" as used in this Code refers to payments due to the State. The provisions of this Code do not apply to customs duties and other duties and payments levied upon import, which remain subject to the provisions of the Customs Code.
