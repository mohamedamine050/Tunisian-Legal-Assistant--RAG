The payment by the State, local authorities, public administrative bodies, and public establishments of sums due to their suppliers of goods, services, works, and property equaling or exceeding 1000 Dinars, including Value Added Tax, requires the submission of a receipt from the tax authorities certifying that the supplier concerned has filed all tax returns whose deadline has passed and which have not lapsed due to the passage of time, on the date of authorization for payment of the aforementioned sums; or, where necessary, the submission of proof of the recording of a collection schedule by the financial collector concerning outstanding tax debts in their name on the date of authorization for payment of the aforementioned sums. (This article was added by Article 62 of Law No. 54 of 2013, dated December 30, 2013, relating to the Finance Act for 2014).
