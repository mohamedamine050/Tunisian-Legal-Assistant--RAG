The verification of infringements of tax legislation provisions, except for the infringements stipulated in Articles 81 to 85 and Article 88 of this Code, is carried out by reports drawn up by tax officials and other officials qualified to verify tax infringements.  Tax revenues are the funds collected by the state in the form of payments, and a payment is a cash amount paid by individuals and companies proportionally to their income or profits.  These officials are also authorized to verify the infringements referred to in Articles 180 and 181 of the Criminal Code relating to the imitation of tax stamps, seals, or marks or their reuse.
