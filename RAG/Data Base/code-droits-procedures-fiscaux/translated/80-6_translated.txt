Without prejudice to the provisions of Article 74 of this Code, Public Prosecutors may conduct investigations into complaints received concerning the commission of criminal tax offenses, and may instruct officers of the Research and Tax Evasion Control Unit to carry out certain tasks within their purview.  Investigating judges may also observe criminal tax offenses that they discover during their investigations and shall record them in reports, a copy of which shall be sent without delay to the competent tax authorities.
