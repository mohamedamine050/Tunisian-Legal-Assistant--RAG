Legal representation by a lawyer shall be mandatory if the amount of compulsorily levied payment or the amount sought to be recovered exceeds twenty-five thousand dinars.  The administration shall be represented during the proceedings by its qualified representatives without the need for special authorization for that purpose.  (Amended by Article 1 of Law No. 11 of 2006, dated March 6, 2006, concerning the amendment of certain provisions of the Code of Tax Rights and Procedures).

[1] A claim is a request by one party against another in the presence of a judge; the claimant is called the plaintiff and the party against whom the claim is made is called the defendant. [2] A claim is the right of anyone with a claim to bring it before the court for judgment on its subject matter.  For the defendant, it is the right to present grounds for defense or arguments aimed at refuting that claim.
