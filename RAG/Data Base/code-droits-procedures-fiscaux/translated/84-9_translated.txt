Any institution that fails to file the annual declaration relating to transfer pricing, as stipulated in paragraph II bis of Article 59 of the Income Tax Code for Individuals and Corporations, by the deadline shall be penalized with an administrative tax penalty of 10,000 Dinars.  A penalty of 50 Dinars shall be imposed for each piece of information not submitted, incompletely submitted, or falsely submitted within the declaration referred to in the preceding paragraph of this Article,  provided that this penalty shall not exceed 5,000 Dinars. (This Article was added by Article 34 of Law No. 56 of 2018 dated December 27, 2018, relating to the Finance Act for 2019).
