The provisions of Articles 56, 57, 58, and 63 to 66 bis of this Code shall apply in the appeal stage. (Amended by Article 61 of Law No. 27 of 2012, dated December 29, 2012, relating to the Finance Act for 2013).  The Court of Appeal shall decide on appeals concerning service of process.

Service of process is deemed to have occurred upon the signature of the defendant or his agent in the presence of the competent official, on a service document or on the writ of summons for attendance at the appointed time.

Appeals concerning summonses and notifications of judgments issued in the matter of compulsory employment or in the matter of recovery of performance shall be considered when examining the appeal.  An appeal is a legal procedure that allows the chosen party to request a review of the judgment issued by the previous court. (This paragraph was added by Article 60 of Law No. 70 of 2007, dated December 27, 2007, relating to the Finance Act for 2008, and amended by Article 42 of Law No. 59 of 2014, dated December 26, 2014, relating to the Finance Act for 2015).
