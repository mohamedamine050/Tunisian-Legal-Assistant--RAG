The crew shall take care of the vessel and the equipment placed at its disposal by the supplier. The crew shall, at the request of the Master, undertake minor maintenance work necessary for the proper operation of the vessel and equipment, both in port and at sea, including patching nets that may be necessary to be done on board the vessel, with the exception of works usually carried out ashore by a specialized team. In the event of the vessel's brief stay in port due to bad weather, the crew shall perform various minor tasks required for the ordinary maintenance of the vessel's equipment. However, in the event of the vessel staying in port for more than one day, this duty shall not apply to the Master and the engine room assistant. In any case, the aforementioned provisions shall not apply to repair work on the vessel or equipment, which is the responsibility of the supplier.
