A fine of between 1 and 100 dinars and imprisonment for a term of between 3 days and one month, or either punishment alone, shall be imposed for committing any offense described as a misdemeanor within the meaning of this law.

Crimes that carry a penalty of death or imprisonment for a period exceeding five years shall be described as felonies.

Crimes that carry a penalty of imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or a fine exceeding sixty dinars, shall be described as misdemeanors.

Crimes that carry a penalty not exceeding fifteen days of imprisonment or a fine of sixty dinars shall be described as violations.

(Article 122 of the Code of Criminal Procedure, Sections 6 and 7 of this Code).
