The fishing gear supplier shall grant the fisherman an annual leave period, the duration of which shall be determined by calculating one and a half days for each month of work, provided that it does not exceed 21 days, of which 18 days are working days. The enjoyment of the leave shall be on a rotational basis and by choice according to seniority on board the vessel. During his annual leave, the fisherman shall receive his wages as if he were at sea.
