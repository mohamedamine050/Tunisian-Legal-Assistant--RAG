The lease agreement, regardless of its type, may be terminated by: 

1) Mutual consent of the parties.

2) Notice of termination given by one party to the other in accordance with the provisions of this Code. 

3) The application of the provisions of Chapters 18 and 19 of this Code.

4) A court order dissolving the agreement or authorizing its dissolution, issued by the competent court. 

5) The maritime fisherman being called up for military service.

6) The death of the maritime fisherman.

7) Damage to or unseaworthiness of the vessel, officially certified, or its seizure or confiscation.

8) The maritime fisherman's descent to land due to illness or injury.

9) The expiry of the term of the agreement or the end of the agreed-upon voyage.
