Any person who disembarks a marine fisherman from a vessel under circumstances that constitute a violation shall be punished with a fine ranging from 10 dinars to 500 dinars.

Crimes that warrant punishment by death or imprisonment for a term exceeding five years shall be classified as felonies within the meaning of this law.

Crimes that warrant punishment by imprisonment for a term exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars, shall be classified as misdemeanors.

Crimes that warrant punishment not exceeding fifteen days of imprisonment or sixty dinars fine shall be classified as violations.

This provision is subject to the provisions of Article 19 of this Code of Criminal Procedure.
