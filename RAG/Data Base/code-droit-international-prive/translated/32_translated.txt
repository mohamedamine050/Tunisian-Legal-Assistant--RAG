The judge may, ex officio, establish the content of the designated foreign law based on the principle of referral, within the limits of his knowledge of it and within a reasonable time, with the assistance of the parties when necessary. In other cases, the party whose claim is based on foreign law is required to prove its content. Proof shall be in writing, including expert opinions. If the content of the foreign law cannot be proven, Tunisian law shall be applied. In all cases, the principle of adversarial proceedings must be respected.
