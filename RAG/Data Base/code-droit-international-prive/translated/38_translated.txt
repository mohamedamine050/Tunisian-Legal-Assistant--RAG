The Tunisian law whose application is necessary, considering the purpose intended by its enactment, shall be applied directly, regardless of the designated rules of conflict of laws. The judge shall apply the provisions of the foreign law not designated by the rules of conflict of laws, if this law has close ties to the legal situation and the application of the aforementioned provisions is necessary, considering the purpose intended by them.

The application or consideration of foreign law is not prevented by its character as public law.
