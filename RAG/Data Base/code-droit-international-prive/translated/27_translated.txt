Adaptation is carried out if its purpose is to determine the conflict of laws rule that enables the identification of the appropriate individual for the position, one that aligns with the qualifications and competencies required to perform it. The applicable law is determined in accordance with the categories of Tunisian law.  Adaptation aims to analyze the elements of legal systems not covered by Tunisian law, according to the foreign law to which it belongs.  In considering adaptation, various international legal categories and the characteristics of international law, which governs relations between states and international actors, are taken into account.

Adaptation within the scope of international treaties is undertaken by relying on the specific categories relevant to the treaties in question.
