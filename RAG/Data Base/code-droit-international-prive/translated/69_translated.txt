Contracts relating to intellectual property shall be subject to the law of the state in which the habitual residence of the person transferring the intellectual property right is located, 
or the person to whom it was assigned, unless the parties have designated a different law. Contracts concluded between the lessor and the lessee relating to intellectual property rights, including patents, trademarks, and copyrights, that were created by the employee in the performance of his work shall be governed by the law applicable to the employment contract.
