Actions relating to requests for authorization to enforce, non-recognition, or declaration of non-opposition to foreign judgments and decisions shall be brought before the **Court of First Instance** in whose jurisdiction the domicile of the party against whom the foreign decision is invoked is located. If such domicile is absent in Tunisia, the actions shall be brought before the **Court of First Instance** of Tunis.  Actions relating to requests for recognition or authorization to enforce arbitral awards in Tunisia shall be brought before the **Court of First Instance** of Tunis, in accordance with the provisions of Article 80 of the Arbitration Code.
