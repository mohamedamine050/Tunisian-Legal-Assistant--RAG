The will, being a transfer of ownership by way of donation taking effect after death, whether it be of a specific thing or a benefit, is subject to the personal law of the testator at the time of his death. The form of the will, being a transfer of ownership by way of donation taking effect after death, whether it be of a specific thing or a benefit, is subject to the personal law of the testator or to the law of the place where it was made.
