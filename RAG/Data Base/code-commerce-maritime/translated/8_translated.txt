All small and large boats belonging to the ship and listed in the inventory are exempt from all documents.  The following are also exempt from the aforementioned documents, except for the license, safety certificates, and inspection log: 

- Boats, barges, carriers, cranes, and other floating equipment operating within a single port.
- Boats intended for local use with fishing nets.
- Fishing vessels with a gross tonnage of five tons or less, and pleasure vessels with a gross tonnage of ten tons or less. 

The license required for the vessels mentioned in the second paragraph is known as the regular license and is renewed annually.
