The master shall exercise his commercial duties in the manner prescribed by laws, regulations, and maritime custom, for the benefit of the owner and the outfitter. Any limitation or restriction of these duties shall not be invoked against third parties. 

The master shall be specifically responsible for the engagement of the crew, the material outfitting of the vessel, the conclusion of contracts for the carriage of passengers and goods, the signing and delivery of bills of lading, the receipt of cargo on board the vessel, its delivery, the collection of freight, and the taking of all measures to ensure its performance.

Generally, the master shall fulfill all obligations and duties that laws, regulations, international conventions and treaties, and maritime custom stipulate as requirements of his function. 

The master shall not exercise his commercial duties at the place of residence of the outfitter, one of his representatives, or in a place where he has an agency or branch.
