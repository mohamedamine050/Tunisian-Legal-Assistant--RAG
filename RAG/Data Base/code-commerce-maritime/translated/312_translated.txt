The insured is obligated to: 

1. Pay the premiums, fees, and expenses at the agreed upon time and place. This obligation applies only to the party who has signed the insurance contract.
2. Exercise reasonable care in respect of all matters relating to the insured object.
3. Accurately disclose, upon entering into the contract, all circumstances known to him that would enable the insurer to assess the extent of the risk assumed.
4. Inform the insurer of any increase in the severity of risks during the term of the contract, whenever such increase becomes known to him.
5. Preserve, for the benefit of the insurer, all rights and claims he may have against the liable party.
