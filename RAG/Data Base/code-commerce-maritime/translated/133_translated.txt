**Liability**

The liability of the [unspecified party] for damages caused by their actions is determined as follows:

1. **Material Damages:** For debts arising from compensation for material damages, the liability is a total amount of **24 Dinars** per cubic ton of the existing cargo.
2. **Physical Damages:** For debts arising from compensation for physical damages, the liability is a total amount of **40 Dinars** per cubic ton of the existing cargo.

This liability determination applies to the situation described in the fourth paragraph of Article 19. The amounts mentioned above may be modified by an order.
