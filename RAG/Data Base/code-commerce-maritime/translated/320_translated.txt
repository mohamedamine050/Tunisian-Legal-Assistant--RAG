The insured and the insurer, while preserving all their rights, shall take all precautionary measures or initiate or request their implementation. They shall ensure the salvage of the insured objects or carry out such salvage and preserve the right to recourse against any non-liable party. The insured shall be liable for the amount of the damage that arises due to their negligence or the negligence of their agents in performing the actions stipulated in the preceding paragraph. They shall also be held responsible for any obstacles they may place in the way of the insurer.
