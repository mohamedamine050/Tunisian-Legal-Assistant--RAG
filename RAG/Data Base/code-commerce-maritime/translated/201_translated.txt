The lessor shall place the agreed-upon vessel at the disposal of the lessee at the agreed-upon time and place. Otherwise, the lessee may terminate the agreement by sending a notice of arrival with a content that reaches the other party without paying the freight and with the right to claim damages. The lessor has the same right if the lessee does not undertake the shipment within the specified period, which does not end until the waiting period expires, all in accordance with the provisions of Article 187.
