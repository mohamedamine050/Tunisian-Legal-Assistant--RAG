If the vessel is in a state of defect while in a port or at a location where it can be repaired to continue the voyage with its full cargo, and it is decided, notwithstanding, to reduce expenses by either towing it to the intended port, or transferring all or part of the cargo to another vessel, or reloading the cargo by any other means, then the expenses of towing, transferring from one vessel to another, reloading, or any one of them, shall be borne by all those interested in the voyage to the extent of the amount of the expense that has been replaced.
