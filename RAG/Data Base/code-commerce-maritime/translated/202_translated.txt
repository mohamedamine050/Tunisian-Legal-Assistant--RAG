## Contract of Time-Bound Charterparty

**Contract**

This contract is a legally binding agreement that obligates the contracting parties and defines their obligations and rights with regard to the specific transaction.

It is hereby agreed that the Charterer, in exchange for a fixed or determinable rent, shall undertake to make the voyage(s) requested by the Charterer by a specific vessel, either for a stipulated duration or for an unspecified period but determinable in advance.
