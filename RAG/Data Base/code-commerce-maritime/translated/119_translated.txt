If the applicant for the tracking procedure is not redeemed before the specified day and time for judgment, the sale shall be carried out in accordance with the forms set forth in Articles 356 to 358 of the Code of Civil Procedure.

The material aspect of civilization, such as infrastructure, communications, and entertainment, is countered by the intellectual, spiritual, and moral aspects of civilization. The sale shall have the consequences stipulated in the first paragraph of Article 361 and in Article 362 of the Code of Civil Procedure.
