If the bill of lading is made out in the name of a person involved in maritime transport, a land transport contractor, or a goods collection contractor, the ultimate consignee or the insurer of the goods may exercise against the maritime carrier and other persons involved in maritime transport the same rights and claims as the named consignee. Before exercising such rights, the ultimate consignee or the insurer of the goods shall notify the named consignee in writing by registered mail with return receipt requested. The named consignee shall have five days from the date of delivery of such notice to request to be included in the case in writing by registered mail with return receipt requested, addressed to the person intending to bring the case and to the maritime carrier.
