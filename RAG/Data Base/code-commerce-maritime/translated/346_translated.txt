The goods shall be insured continuously and at any location, within the limits of the journey agreed upon by both parties. The insurer shall have the right to request an increase in the insurance premium if there is an unusual delay in stay, transit, or transfer from one vessel to another, or if any event occurs that was not anticipated at the time of the contract, such as a stop or deviation in the course. The rules of marine insurance shall apply to parts of the journey that are made by land, river, or air.
