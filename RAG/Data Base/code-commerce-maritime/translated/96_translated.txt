The request for sale must be notified by the executing judicial officer to both the buyer and the registered or preferred creditors opposing the sale, before the end of the twenty-day period referred to in the previous article, otherwise the request will be dismissed. The request shall include a summons to appear before the **Court of First Instance** to determine the location of the ship's registration for the purpose of ordering the required public auction. The judgment issued is not subject to any form of appeal except by cassation. The appeal must be lodged within five days of the issuance of the judgment, otherwise it will be dismissed. The deadlines provided in Articles 185 and 186 of the Code of Civil Procedure apply to this matter.
