The master of every Tunisian vessel shall, within twenty-four hours of arrival, obtain from the maritime authority a stamp on his logbook and submit his nautical report. The report shall state: - the time and place of departure. - the route taken. - the dangers encountered. - the damage suffered by the vessel or cargo and, generally, all noteworthy circumstances encountered during the voyage. These provisions do not apply to pleasure vessels, vessels engaged in coastal fishing, or vessels not subject to any form of appeal.
