**English Translation:**

Every offense described as a felony within the meaning of this penal law refers to crimes punishable by death or imprisonment exceeding five years.

Offenses are described as misdemeanors when they are punishable by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars.

Offenses are described as infractions when they are punishable by imprisonment not exceeding fifteen days or by a fine not exceeding sixty dinars. 122 Code of Criminal Procedure

The provisions of Articles 49, 50, 51, 53, and 55 are punishable, except in cases of force majeure, by imprisonment for fifteen days and a fine of four dinars and eight hundred millimes, or by one of these penalties only.
