In the event of damage, defect, or deterioration of the goods, the recipient shall, at the time of delivery, submit in writing to the carrier or its representative, at the latest, all of their reservations. Otherwise, the goods shall be deemed to have been delivered as described in the charter party or bill of lading, unless proof to the contrary is presented. If the damage or deterioration is not apparent, reservations may be submitted within five days following the date of delivery.
