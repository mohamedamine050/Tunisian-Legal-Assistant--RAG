All changes made to the elements listed under number 2 of Article 24 of the amendment shall be entered in the Register following the initial numbering, upon presentation of the Certificate of Tonnage Assessment proving such changes. Every new Certificate of Tonnage Assessment shall include a reference to the old Certificates, specifying the letters and numbers they bear, their dates, and the name of the vessel to which they pertain. The aforementioned reference, its date, and its number shall be drawn on the Certificate of Tonnage Assessment.
