In addition to what was stated in the previous chapter, the following subsidiary registers are maintained in duplicate:

1. A deposit register which records, in order of arrival, all documents, papers, and evidence submitted for registration or provisional registration.
2. An alphabetical index of the names of the rights holders registered or provisionally registered for the registered vessels.
3. An alphabetical index of the names of the registered vessels.

The duplicates of the three subsidiary registers mentioned above are deposited with the **Court of First Instance** located in the district of the relevant maritime area within thirty days following the date of their stamping.
