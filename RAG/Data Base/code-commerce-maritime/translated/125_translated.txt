If the vessel is registered in Tunisia, the cancellation and deletion of entries relating to preferential rights, mortgages, claims for rescission, or precautionary measures referred to in Article 124 shall be effected upon the submission of the following documents by the applicant to the Vessel Registration Office:

1.  A court decision [judgment, ruling, decree]  annulling [cancelling, striking out] the entry.
2.  Receipt issued by the applicant's lawyer certifying payment of the court fees.
3.  Receipt issued by the Deposit and Trust Fund certifying the deposit of the cancellation fee.
