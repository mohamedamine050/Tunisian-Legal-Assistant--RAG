The "waiting period" does not run on holidays and other days that are considered days of rest by local custom. The "additional waiting period" includes holidays and other days that are considered days of rest by local custom. Force majeure suspends the running of the "waiting period." However, the "additional waiting period" is not suspended at all, even in cases of force majeure. However, if force majeure persists, the governor may reduce the amount of the penalty.
