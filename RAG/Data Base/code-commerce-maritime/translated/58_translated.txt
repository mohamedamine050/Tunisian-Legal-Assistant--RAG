The master of the vessel is obligated to personally supervise, or through his crew, the proper execution of the loading, unloading, stowing, and unstowing of cargo, and to take all necessary measures to ensure the smooth transport and preservation of the goods. He must also prevent any risk, damage, or loss to these goods before departure, during the voyage, and upon arrival, or strive to minimize such occurrences. Furthermore, he must ensure the comfort, safety, and security of the crew and passengers.
