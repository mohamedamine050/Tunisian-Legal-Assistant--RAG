The sale known as "FOB" (Free on Board) is a type of sale of goods by shipment wherein the seller is obligated to deliver the goods free of all costs on board the vessel. The sale known as "FAS" (Free Alongside Ship) is another type of sale of goods by shipment wherein the seller undertakes to deliver the goods free of all costs alongside the vessel at the shipping dock. The acceptance of the goods is made at the port of shipment prior to loading, unless otherwise agreed.
