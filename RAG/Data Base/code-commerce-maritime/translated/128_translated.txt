The procedures for distribution, timeframes, and appeals are the same as those for the distribution or ordering of creditors as set forth in Articles 379, 380, and 382 to 394 of the Code of Civil Procedure.  

The provisions of Article 391 of the Code of Civil Procedure relating to registered immovable property apply to ships registered in Tunisia. The provisions of the second and third paragraphs of Article 393 of the Code of Civil Procedure are not applicable.
