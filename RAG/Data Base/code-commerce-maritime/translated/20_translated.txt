A partner-captain in the ownership of a ship, upon being dismissed, may withdraw from the partnership and compel the remaining partners, each according to their share, to purchase his share after its valuation by mutual agreement or by experts. He must notify all partners of his decision by a bailiff within thirty days following his dismissal. Payment must be made within one month following the final valuation by mutual agreement or by litigation, with legal interest commencing from the date of the aforementioned notification.  

It is permissible to appoint an expert by an urgent court order.
