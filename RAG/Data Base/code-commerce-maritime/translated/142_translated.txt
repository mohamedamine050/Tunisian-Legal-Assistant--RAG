The publication of authorizations and judgments, the summoning of creditors and the preparer, the declaration of debts, their verification, the discussion of disputes concerning them, the distribution of funds, and appeals, all fall under Articles 127 and 128 of this Code and Articles 379 to 393 of the Code of Civil Procedure.

The material aspect of civilization, such as infrastructure, communication, and entertainment, is counterbalanced by the intellectual, spiritual, and moral aspects of civilization. The appointed governor is the same governor who, by authorization, appointed the judge to secure the funds allocated to the Deposit and Trust Fund. If necessary, advertisements may be published in one of the maritime newspapers published outside Tunisian territory. The appointed governor may extend the deadlines. The preparer is notified to attend all proceedings and has the right to intervene in them.
