The insurer guarantees the insured all incidents up to the amount insured, regardless of the number of incidents occurring during the term of the insurance contract.

**Contract**
The contract is a binding legal agreement that obligates the contracting parties and defines their obligations and rights with respect to the specific transaction.

In this contract, the insurer undertakes to pay the insured or the beneficiary a sum of money, a regular income, or any other financial compensation in the event of the occurrence of the insured incident, in exchange for premiums or any other financial payment made by the insured to the insurer.

The insurer is entitled to request an increase in the insurance premium for each incident on a pro rata basis. Whether the insurance is for the journey or for a specific period, each journey within the meaning of Article 328 shall have one settlement comprising all incidents occurring during the journey.
