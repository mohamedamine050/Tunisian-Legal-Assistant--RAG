The loss of freight charges incurred due to damage or destruction [1] to the cargo or the ship is considered a general average loss.  If the loss of freight charges is deemed a general average loss, it follows that the expenses that should have been incurred to make the freight charges due, as well as the port charges, should be deducted from the freight charges.

[1] Harm that occurs in violation of the law and affects a person's body, mental or psychological health, reputation, property, or reputation.
