If the vessel is registered in Tunisia, copies of the writ of attachment and the summons to appear shall be served within the same period: 

1. To the vessel's registration office for the registration of the attachment in the registration book. 

2. To the registered creditors, or those who have a right of rescission, or those who have filed a preliminary claim, so that they may intervene in the case if they so desire.

The originals of these copies shall be added to the file submitted to the Court, along with a list of the entries registered on the vessel.
