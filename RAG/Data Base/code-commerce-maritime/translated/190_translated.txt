The carriage fee shall be due: 1- If the non-delivery is caused by the act of the hirer. 2- If the goods perish due to a defect inherent to them. Goods considered to have perished due to a defect inherent to them include perishable goods due to their nature and the length of the journey, as well as animals that die during the journey, regardless of the reason, except for the fault of the lessor. Also considered as such are dangerous, harmful, and prohibited goods that had to be destroyed during the journey.
