If the expenses are not paid and the insurance is not secured, the vessel shall be resold pursuant to default within fifteen days after carrying out the same publicity and announcements stipulated in Articles 115 to 117. This shall take place at a hearing set by the Head of the Real Estate Registry Department following a petition submitted to him, and without summons. The plaintiff shall be obliged to pay the deficiency and expenses by a separate judgment issued immediately upon conclusion of the ruling, without further proceedings.
