The sale of a mortgaged vessel within Tunisian territory is prohibited from being sold voluntarily in foreign countries. Any such sale is void and cannot be registered in the Register of Ships. The seller shall be subject to the penalties stipulated in Article 291 of the Penal Code. For the purposes of this Article, he shall be considered as the owner or seller, and anyone who violates its provisions, whether a lessee or entrusted with any capacity whatsoever to manage or operate maritime exploitation institutions, regardless of their legal form, shall be punished with the penalties provided therein. These institutions shall be held civilly liable, and in particular, jointly liable with the violators for the amount of fines, expenses, and penalties.
