The insured value of the goods shall not exceed the highest amount of either:

* The purchase price, if any; or if not, the price agreed upon by the contracting parties, provided that it does not exceed or fall short of the prevailing market price at the time and place of shipment, plus all expenses incurred in reaching the destination, as well as the profit earned by the company over and above its liabilities, by including the company's capital in its liabilities at the end of the fiscal year; or
* The value of the goods at the destination on the date of their arrival, or if not arrived, on the date they should have arrived; or
* The price agreed upon by the contracting parties, provided that it does not exceed or fall short of the price obtained from the sale of the goods by the insured.
