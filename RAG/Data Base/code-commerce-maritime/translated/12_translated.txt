The owner or outfitter of a vessel lacking all or some of the legal documents shall be punished by imprisonment for a period of one year and a fine of one thousand dinars, or by one of these penalties only. For the purposes of this section, a lessee or a person entrusted with any capacity whatsoever with the administration or management of maritime exploitation institutions, regardless of their legal form, shall be considered as an outfitter or owner, and shall be punished by the penalties set forth herein, regardless of whether they are a lessee or a person entrusted with any capacity whatsoever with the administration or management of maritime exploitation institutions, regardless of their legal form. These institutions shall be civilly liable, and in particular jointly liable with the offenders for the amount of the fines and expenses. The captain shall be subject to the same penalties. In addition, the Court may order the seizure of the vessel.
