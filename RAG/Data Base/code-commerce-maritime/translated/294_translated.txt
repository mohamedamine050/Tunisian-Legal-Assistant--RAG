The provisions of this book shall apply:

1. To all collisions, salvage operations, and general average losses occurring in Tunisian waters.

2. To all collisions, salvage operations between vessels at least one of which flies the Tunisian flag, and to all general average losses related to a vessel flying that flag.

3. To all collisions, salvage operations, and general average losses which result in a case brought before the Tunisian courts and within their jurisdiction.
