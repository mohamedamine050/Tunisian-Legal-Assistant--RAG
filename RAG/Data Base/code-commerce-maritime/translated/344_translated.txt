The provisions of this chapter shall also apply to vessels that were insured for the duration of their presence in ports, harbors, or other places, whether afloat or in dry docks. The insurance contract obligates the insurer to pay the insured or the beneficiary a sum of money, a regular income, or any other monetary compensation in the event of the occurrence of the insured event, in exchange for premiums or any other financial payment made by the insured to the insurer.
