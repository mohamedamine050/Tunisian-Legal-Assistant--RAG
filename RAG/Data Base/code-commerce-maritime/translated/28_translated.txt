**(Amended by Law No. 3 of 2004 dated January 20, 2004)** Anyone who violates the provisions of Articles 15 and 16, the second paragraph of Article 23, and Article 27 of this Code shall be punished with a fine of one thousand dinars. The competent authority shall have the power to seize the vessel's documents until its administrative situation is regularized. The violations referred to in the first paragraph of this Article shall be examined in accordance with the procedures set forth in the Code of Administrative Organization of Maritime Navigation. The provisions of the first paragraph of this Article shall apply to any person entrusted with the management and operation of maritime exploitation institutions, regardless of their legal form.
