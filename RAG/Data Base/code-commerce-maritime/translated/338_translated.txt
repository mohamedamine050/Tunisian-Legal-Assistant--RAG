It is permissible to abandon a vessel in the following circumstances: 1) The total loss of the vessel. 2) The absence of any news concerning the vessel for a period of three months from the date of the last communication, in which case the loss is presumed to have occurred on the date of the last communication. 3) If the vessel is beyond repair, either definitively or due to the lack of material resources for repair at the location where it is found, and it is impossible to move it to another location where it can be repaired. 4) If the total value of the repairs required to be performed on the vessel following its damage is equal to or exceeds three-quarters of the value of the vessel.
