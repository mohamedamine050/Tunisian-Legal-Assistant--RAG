The provisions of this book aim to clarify and define the rights and obligations of the parties to agreements related to maritime transport, solely to avoid any deficiency, ambiguity, or obscurity in such agreements or in the provisions that the parties refer to for application. The parties are free to enter into their agreements in any manner they deem appropriate, particularly by referring to the application of any foreign law, any model agreement, or by quoting from several laws or model agreements, or by creating new model agreements, all while taking into account the limitations and restrictions stipulated in this Code.
