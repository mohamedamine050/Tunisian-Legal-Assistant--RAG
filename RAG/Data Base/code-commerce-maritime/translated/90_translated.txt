The rights of preferential preemption in a ship expire under the following conditions:

1. The transfer must be registered in the Ship Register.

2. The transfer must be announced twice in the official gazette of the Republic of Tunisia and in one of the newspapers published in Tunis. There must be a period of at least eight days between the two announcements.

3. The creditor must not have notified his opposition to both the former owner and the new owner within one month following the second announcement.
