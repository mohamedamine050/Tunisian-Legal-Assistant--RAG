The rules set forth in Chapters Five and Six of Title Eight of the Code of Civil Procedure shall apply to civil and commercial matters, except insofar as they conflict with the provisions of this Chapter. 

**Note:**  The phrase "الجانب المادّيّ من الحضارة كالعمران ووسائل الاتّصال والتّرفيه، يقابلها الجانب الفكريّ والرُّوحيّ والخلقيّ من الحضارة" appears to be a broader societal commentary and not directly relevant to the legal text. It has therefore been omitted from the translation.
