The process of removal from registration or cancellation is a recognized mechanism in real estate mortgages, and its legal regime differs between registered and unregistered deeds. It is subject to the following: 1- Submission of duly signed documents from the creditor to lift the lien. 2- A court judgment that has acquired the force of res judicata. Cancellation from the registry shall be carried out by the Maritime Authority, ex officio, if it is not renewed within the time limit specified in the first paragraph of Article 81.
