The party seeking the legal acknowledgment (aqala) shall notify the party against whom the acknowledgment is sought, within fifteen days, with a copy of the acknowledgment document, summoning the latter to appear before the Real Estate Acknowledgment Session of the First Instance Court of the place where the acknowledgment took place, for a ruling on the completion of the sale of the vessel. The aforementioned copy of the acknowledgment document may be served on the captain, or, in his absence, on the person representing the debtor. If the latter resides outside Tunisian territory, and the captain is absent, and there is no one representing the debtor, the procedures stipulated in Article 9 of the Code of Civil Procedure shall be followed.
