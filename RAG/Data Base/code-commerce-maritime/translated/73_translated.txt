**All that pertains to the ship and the freight, as per Chapter 71, is as follows:**

**First:** The compensation the owner is entitled to for material damages sustained by the ship that have not been repaired, or for the loss of freight.

**Second:** The compensation the owner is entitled to for general average losses, in terms of material damages sustained by the ship that have not been repaired, or in terms of the loss of freight.

**Third:** The fees the owner is entitled to for assistance or salvage operations carried out before the completion of the voyage, provided that the amounts due to the captain and other crew members are deducted. The passenger fare shall be considered as freight. 

**Compensation due to the owner under insurance contracts shall not be considered as part of the ship or freight.**
