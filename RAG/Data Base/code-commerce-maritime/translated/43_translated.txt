The administrative system governing seafarers, their disciplinary and penal system, their employment contracts, their treatment in cases of illness or injury, their repatriation, their working methods, the termination of their employment contracts, their remuneration, and their insurance against occupational hazards, as well as their participation in retirement or social welfare funds, are all regulated, as applicable, by the laws, orders, and regulations in force, and by collective and individual agreements governing them, provided that such regulations do not conflict with the provisions of this Code.
