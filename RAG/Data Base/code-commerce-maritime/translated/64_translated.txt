The pilot is the person entrusted by the maritime authority with the function of **piloting**. 

**Function** 
is an individual's contribution or role in society through an occupation or activity, often regular and usually performed in exchange for financial compensation for sustenance. 

**Piloting** is a temporary, compulsory, or optional function, as the case may be, requiring the pilot to board the vessel upon its entry into a port, anchorage, or river, and upon its departure therefrom, and to technically steer the vessel under the **responsibility** of the master, subject to his orders and authority. 

**Responsibility**
is the obligation to answer for one's actions before the law or to compensate for the damage caused.
