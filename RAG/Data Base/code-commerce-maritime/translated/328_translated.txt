In the case of **insurance** 

**Insurance** is a contract by which the insurer undertakes to pay the insured or the beneficiary a sum of money, a regular income, or any other financial compensation in the event of the occurrence of the insured event, in return for premiums or any other financial payment made by the insured to the insurer.

For a voyage, the insurer's guarantee runs from the time the goods begin to be loaded until the end of their unloading, provided that such guarantee does not exceed a period of fifteen days from the arrival of the ship at the intended port or the time the goods begin to be loaded for another voyage. In the case of a voyage without cargo, the insurer's guarantee begins from the time the ship is unmoored or its anchor is raised and ends at the time of its anchoring or mooring at the intended location.

As for **insurance**

**Insurance** is a contract by which the insurer undertakes to pay the insured or the beneficiary a sum of money, a regular income, or any other financial compensation in the event of the occurrence of the insured event, in return for premiums or any other financial payment made by the insured to the insurer, for a specific period, it begins and ends at the specified times regardless of the location of the ship.
