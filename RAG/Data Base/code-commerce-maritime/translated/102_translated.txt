It is permissible for any person holding a claim among those listed in Article 101, with the authorization of the judge, to proceed with the **attachment** of any vessel owned by the debtor, even if it is prepared for travel, upon presentation of an enforceable instrument by the creditor. However, no **attachment** may be placed on any vessel other than the one that is the **subject** of the claim in debts listed in Articles 14, 15, and 16 of the preceding chapter.
