The following expenses constitute general average: a rate of two percent (2%) of the expenses which have the character of general average, excluding the wages and expenses of the master, officers, and crew, as well as fuel and provisions not reimbursed during the voyage. If the money is not advanced by one of those who will be called upon to contribute to the general average, then the usual expenses incurred in obtaining the necessary funds, as well as the loss incurred by the owners of the goods sold for this purpose, shall also be considered general average.
