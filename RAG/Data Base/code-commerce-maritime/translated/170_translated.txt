**(Amended by Law No. 22 of 1998 dated March 16, 1998)** The functions of Ship's Agent, Cargo Agent, or Cargo Handling Contractor may be combined. In such a case, the rights, duties, and liabilities shall be those pertaining to the function or functions actually performed at the time of the event giving rise to a claim or the filing of an action in liability. The Ship's Agent, Cargo Agent, and Cargo Handling Contractor are subject, in respect of claims arising from their respective activities, to the rules governing the determination of liability or exemption therefrom, and to the rules of limitation. 

The Court of Cassation is the final court of appeal for all disputes arising from these provisions, either in favor of or against the ship's equipment.
