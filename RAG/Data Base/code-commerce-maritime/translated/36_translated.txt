The Maritime Authority may, on its own initiative, correct minor clerical errors made by the Head of the Maritime Zone Authority, such as discrepancies of all types between the contents of the registration book sheet and the information contained in the subsidiary registers, drawings, and documents relied upon in the application for registration or provisional entry. This correction shall not prejudice the rights of any third party who, in good faith, registered or provisionally entered their rights prior to the correction being made.
