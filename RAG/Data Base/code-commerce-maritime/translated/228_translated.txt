The warranty of sale transfers to the buyer upon delivery, unless otherwise agreed. In the case of a sale by shipment, delivery occurs at the departure port dock and alongside the ship. Delivery may also take place on the ship's deck, in a covered area, or on a railroad or freight car. In the case of a sale by unloading, delivery occurs at the arrival port dock and alongside the ship. Delivery may also take place on the ship's deck, in a covered area, or on a railroad or freight car. With regard to goods identified by type, delivery is not completed until they are distinguished. The act of distinguishing or specifying involves separating those goods from a larger group to ensure that those separated correspond to the goods sold and identified by type. Acceptance of the goods takes place upon delivery or distinction.
