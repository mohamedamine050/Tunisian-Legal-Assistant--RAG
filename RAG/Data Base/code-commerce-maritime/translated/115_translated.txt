The sale resulting from the seizure, which empowers the creditor holding an enforceable instrument to place the movable assets in the possession of his debtor under judicial control, shall take place at a session of the Real Estate Seizures Court of the Court, at least thirty days after the posting of the notices and the publication of their text in the Official Gazette of the Tunisian Republic and in one of the daily newspapers published in Tunisia, in addition to other publication methods that the Court may determine at the request of one of the parties, at his prior expense.
