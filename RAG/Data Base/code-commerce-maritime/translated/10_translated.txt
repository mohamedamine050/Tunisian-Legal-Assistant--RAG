The following vessels are exempt from submitting their documents and crew list for endorsement upon arrival and departure:

1. Vessels engaged in fishing along the Tunisian coast. 
2. Vessels regularly operating between the islands belonging to Tunisia and the nearest point of land in Tunisia. 
3. Vessels used to transport passengers and goods between the mainland and the port, and between the port and the mainland. 
4. Vessels equipped for the exploitation of agricultural, industrial, or commercial establishments. 
5. Pleasure vessels with a home port in Tunisia when engaged in coastal navigation along the Tunisian coast. 
6. Vessels temporarily anchored, provided the period of stay does not exceed twenty-four hours. 

However, the captains of the vessels mentioned in this article must present their ship's documents whenever requested by the maritime authorities or by health or customs officials.
