The charter party shall stipulate the following: 

1. The name, nationality, and net tonnage of the vessel, specifying whether it is chartered in its entirety or otherwise. 
2. The full names and addresses of the parties.
3. The method of calculating the freight rate.

Furthermore, the charter party shall, if applicable, stipulate:

4. The name of the master.
5. The type of cargo, specifying whether it is specific or providing sufficient details to identify it.
6. The agreed upon place and time for loading and unloading.
7. The duration of the contract; if it is for an indefinite period, the method of termination shall be specified.
8. A statement of the voyage or voyages to be undertaken.
9. A statement of the technical, administrative, navigational, and commercial responsibilities assigned to each party.
