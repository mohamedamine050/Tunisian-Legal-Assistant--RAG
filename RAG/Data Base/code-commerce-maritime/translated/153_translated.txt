## Responsibility

Responsibility entails the legal obligation to respond to one's actions or compensate for the harm caused.

## Carrier of Passengers by Sea

A carrier of passengers by sea cannot exceed the previously outlined boundaries, regardless of the basis for the claimant's request, except in cases of **deception**.

## Deception

Deception is a misleading act perpetrated with the intention of compelling a person to enter into a legal transaction or inducing them to contract under more detrimental circumstances.

## Claimant

The **claimant** refers to the person who initiates legal proceedings.
