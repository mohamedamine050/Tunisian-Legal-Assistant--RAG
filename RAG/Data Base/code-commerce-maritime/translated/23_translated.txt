(Amended by Law No. 3 of 2004 dated January 20, 2004) Every ship flying the Tunisian flag and engaged in navigation must be registered with a maritime zone center, which shall become its home port. The owner shall register the fully constructed ship upon delivery or acquisition thereof, as soon as ownership is transferred, in accordance with the form stipulated in the first paragraph of Article 16 of this Code. If a foreign ship is acquired abroad, the purchaser must submit a written request for registration within seven days of its entry into a Tunisian port. The registration procedures shall be completed within a maximum period of sixty days from this date.
