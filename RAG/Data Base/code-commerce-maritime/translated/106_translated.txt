The court within whose jurisdiction the authorization to perform the arrest was issued shall consider the merits of the dispute in addition to the cases stipulated in Article 2 of the Code of Civil Procedure.

The court shall consider the merits of the dispute in the following cases, even if the vessel is not Tunisian: 

1. Where the debt is secured by a maritime lien on the arrested vessel. 
2. If the maritime debt itself originated in Tunisia. 
3. If the maritime debt arose during a voyage where the arrest was performed. 
4. If the debt resulted from a collision that falls under the jurisdiction of the court. 
5. If the debt originated from assistance or rescue that falls under the jurisdiction of the court.
