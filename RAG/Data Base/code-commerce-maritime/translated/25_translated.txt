The owner of a ship under construction as referred to in Article 15 of this Code shall register it by means of a request submitted to the maritime authority in the maritime district where the construction is taking place (amended by Law No. 3 of 2004 dated January 20, 2004). The maritime authority shall record in the register, under a specific number, the following: 1- Date of the request. 2- The location where the construction of the ship is taking place, its name, the type of its power plant, its structural materials, its measurements, its potential cargo capacity, and, if applicable, the power of its propulsion system. Once the tonnage certificate is issued, and upon the request of the ship owner, the final numbered document shall be issued in accordance with the forms and formats prescribed in Article 24.
