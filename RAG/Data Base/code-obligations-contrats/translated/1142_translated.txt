The principal shall fulfill the following obligations:

Firstly: To reimburse the agent for any expenses incurred and previously disbursed from his own funds for the necessary requirements of the agency, and to pay the agent's due remuneration, if the agent is remunerated, without regard to the outcome of the transaction, unless there is negligence or default on the part of the agent.

Secondly: The principal shall bear responsibility for what the agent has legitimately incurred in his name for the necessities of the transaction.  The principal shall not bear responsibility for what the agent incurs due to his own negligence, default, or any other matter outside the scope of his agency.
