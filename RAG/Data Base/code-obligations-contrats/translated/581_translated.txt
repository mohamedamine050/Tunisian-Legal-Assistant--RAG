(Amended by Law No. 47 of 1992, dated May 4, 1992). If the subject matter of the sale is real estate, real estate rights, or other things that can be mortgaged, the sale must be in writing with a legally valid instrument showing the date.  The aforementioned contract may not be invoked against third parties unless it is registered with the tax office, taking into account the provisions relating to registered real estate.
