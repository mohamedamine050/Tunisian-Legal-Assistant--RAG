The aforementioned period shall not be calculated in cases of duress except from the day of its cessation; in cases of mistake or deceit, except from the time of becoming aware of it;  with regard to contracts of minors, except from the day of their majority; and with regard to those under guardianship and those lacking legal capacity, except from the day the guardianship is lifted.  Concerning the heirs of a person under guardianship who died while under guardianship, and the rational person who has been wronged, the calculation begins from the day of possession of the subject matter of the contract.
