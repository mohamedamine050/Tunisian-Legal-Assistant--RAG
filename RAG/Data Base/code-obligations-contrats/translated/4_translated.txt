Subject to the provisions of Article 45 of this Code, the following are personally subject to income tax:

* Partners in partnerships, de facto companies, simple joint ventures, partners in limited partnerships, and members of economic interest groups who are natural persons or legal entities, as well as members of civil companies which do not actually have the status of capital companies, based on their share of social profits according to their rights in the companies or groups that have a place of business in Tunisia and based on the capital gains they realize from the transfer of their rights in these companies or in these groups. (Amended by Article 55 of Law No. 123 of 28 December 2001 and Article 19-6 of Law No. 53 of 25 December 2015).

* Joint owners of common debt funds as defined in Law No. 83 of 24 July 2001 concerning the issuance of the Code of Collective Investment Institutions, who are natural persons or legal entities, based on their contribution shares in the aforementioned funds. (Added by Article 25 of Law No. 123 of 28 December 2001).
