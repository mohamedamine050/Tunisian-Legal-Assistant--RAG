II. The depreciation provisions stipulated in paragraph I of this Article shall apply to fixed assets or their elements, the useful life of which differs from that of the original assets, which are subject to loss of value and owned by the institution, including buildings built on land belonging to third parties. Depreciation shall be calculated from the date of acquisition, construction, manufacture, or commencement of use or exploitation if it occurs later, or from the date of entry into production in the case of agricultural plantations, on the basis of the purchase price, cost of construction or manufacture, or plantations.

Depreciation shall be calculated for mobile telephone stations from the date of receipt of the final acceptance certificate for the said stations. (Added by Article 20-1 of Law No. 59 of 2014, dated December 26, 2014).

The depreciation calculation rule does not include the deductible value added tax.
