If the lessee receives the defective or non-conforming manufactured item and becomes aware of its defect or deficiency, and fails to promptly return it or fails to preserve his right to take action as provided for in Article 875, then Article 652 concerning the sale of a defective movable item received by the purchaser shall apply to him, as shall Article 672 regarding the period within which he may address the defect if he did not have prior knowledge of it.
