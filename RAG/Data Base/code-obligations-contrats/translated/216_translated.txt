If the assignment concerns only part of a debt, the assignor and the assignee shall share in the claim to the assigned debt, each according to their respective share. However, the assignee shall have the right of priority over the assignor in the following two instances:

First: If this priority is expressly stipulated.  Second: If the assignor guarantees the debtor's ability to pay the assigned debt or undertakes its payment if the debtor fails to pay.
