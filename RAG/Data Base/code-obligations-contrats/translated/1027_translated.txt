The statement of the depositary is accepted as true by his oath regarding the occurrence of the deposit, the nature of the deposited item, and its return to its owner or to the person entitled to receive it, unless its receipt is witnessed or documented in writing.  No effect shall be given to a stipulation excluding the possibility of oath-taking in all the aforementioned cases.  Furthermore, the provisions of this chapter shall not apply if the depositary is shown to have committed an act of encroachment upon or betrayal of the deposit.
