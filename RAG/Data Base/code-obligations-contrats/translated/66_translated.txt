Notwithstanding the provisions of Section 12bis of Law No. 114 of 1989, dated December 30, 1989, concerning the publication of the Income Tax Code for Natural Persons and Corporate Tax, the following shall be deducted from the tax base:

* Taxes withheld on gross income or income elements.
* Two-thirds of income derived from direct investments within the meaning of Section 3 of Law No. 114 of 1989, concerning the publication of the Income Tax Code for Natural Persons and Corporate Tax, in the agriculture and fisheries sectors, as well as exceptional profits as provided for in Section 11bis.1 of this Code, under the same conditions and after the full deduction period provided for in Section 65 of this Code has been met.

The benefit of this deduction requires accounting according to the accounting legislation for businesses. Profits derived from direct investments within the meaning of Section 3 of Law No. 114 of 1989, concerning the publication of the Income Tax Code for Natural Persons and Corporate Tax, in the agriculture and fisheries sectors, as well as exceptional profits as provided for in Section 11bis.1 of this Code, under the same conditions and after the full deduction period provided for in Section 65 of this Code has been met, shall be subject to corporate tax at the rate provided for in Section 49.1.4.I of this Code. (Amended by Section 16-7 of Law No. 56 of 2018, dated December 27, 2018).
