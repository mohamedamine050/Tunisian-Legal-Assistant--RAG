Notwithstanding the provisions of Article 12 bis of Law No. 114 of 1989 dated 30 December 1989 concerning the issuance of the Code of Income Tax on Individuals and Corporate Income Tax, the following are excluded from the tax base: 

* Income tax withheld on total income or income elements. 

* Two-thirds of the income derived from direct investments as defined in Article 3 of the Investment Law, made in Regional Development Zones, as well as exceptional profits referred to in paragraph I bis of Article 11 of this Code under the same conditions and after fulfilling the total deferral period stipulated in Article 63 of this Code.

Benefiting from this deferral requires maintaining accounting records in accordance with the accounting legislation for businesses. Profits derived from direct investments as defined in Article 3 of the Investment Law and made in Regional Development Zones, as well as exceptional profits referred to in paragraph I bis of Article 11 of this Code under the same conditions and after fulfilling the total deferral period stipulated in Article 63 of this Code, are subject to corporate income tax at the rate stipulated in paragraph 1 of the fourth paragraph of paragraph I of Article 49 of this Code. (Amended by Article 16-7 of Organic Law No. 56 of 2018 dated December 27, 2018).
