**1.** The provisions of Chapters 10 to 20 of this Code shall apply to the tax on companies.

**However,** provisions for doubtful debts and guarantees granted to clients by lending institutions as provided for in Law No. 65 of 2001 dated July 10, 2001, concerning lending institutions and non-resident lending institutions operating within the framework of the Financial Services Act for Non-Residents issued by Law No. 64 of 2009 dated August 12, 2009, resulting from the financing they provide, shall be deducted in full.

**Furthermore,** until the deduction of provisions for doubtful debts by the abovementioned institutions, and the deduction of provisions for doubtful debts owed to the State, local authorities, public institutions and public establishments, the condition relating to the initiation of judicial proceedings referred to in paragraph 4 of Chapter 12 of this Code shall not apply. (Amended by Chapter 25 of Legislative Decree No. 53 of 2015 dated December 25, 2015). 

**(Amended by Chapter 34 of Legislative Decree No. 98 of 1991 dated December 31, 1991, and by Chapters 37 and 39 of Legislative Decree No. 113 of 1996 dated December 30, 1996, and by Chapter 6 of Law No. 92 of 1999 dated August 17, 1999, concerning provisions aimed at stimulating the financial market, and by Chapter 14 of Legislative Decree No. 123 of 2001 dated December 28, 2001, and by Chapter 37 of Legislative Decree No. 85 of 2006 dated December 2006, and by Chapters 47-1 and 48 of Legislative Decree No. 70 of 2007 dated December 27, 2007, and repealed and replaced by Chapter 35-1 of Legislative Decree No. 71 of 2009 dated December 25, 2009).**

"The aforementioned lending institutions shall also deduct the collective provisions they form to cover the risks inherent in their commercial commitments, and those requiring special monitoring according to current regulations, up to 1% of the total amount of these commitments included in their financial statements for the year in question for the deduction of the aforementioned provisions, as certified by the auditors."

**The deduction requires the submission of a statement with the corporate income tax declaration containing the total amount of current commitments and those requiring special monitoring, including the amount of collective provisions, the provisions formed in their name, and the provisions that have been deducted to determine the taxable income.**

**The collective provisions deducted pursuant to the provisions of this paragraph shall be included in the taxable income for the year in which they cease to be required.** (Added by Chapter 27 of Legislative Decree No. 27 of 2012 dated December 29, 2012).

**Furthermore,** the condition relating to the non-continuation of the institution's dealings with the debtor, as stated in point 3 of Chapter 12 of this Code, shall not be required for the deduction of debts relinquished by institutions providing services to the public. (Added by Chapter 24 of Legislative Decree No. 53 of 2015 dated December 25, 2015).

**IX. The loss recorded for a fiscal year, as evidenced by the accounting in accordance with the accounting legislation of the institutions, shall be deducted in succession from the income of the following years, up to the fifth year inclusive.**

**The deduction shall not include losses resulting from the deduction of income and profits according to current tax legislation.** (Added by Chapter 37-2 of Legislative Decree No. 1 of 2012 dated May 16, 2012).

**However, losses that have been recorded for more than four years may be deducted from the exceptional income realized by the institutions as a result of benefiting from the relinquishment of debts owed to them within the framework of paragraph VII Tertio Decimo of this Chapter, up to the amount of the exceptional income realized. **

**In this case, the deduction shall be made up to the amount of losses recorded for a period not exceeding ten years in the year of deduction, provided that the accounts for the years in which the losses were recorded have been certified by an auditor without the certification containing any reservations that would affect the basis of performance.**

**In order to benefit from these provisions, the corporate income tax declaration for the year of deduction of the losses that have been recorded for more than four years shall be accompanied by a detailed list showing the amount of losses that could not be deducted from the income of previous years, the year in which they were recorded, the amount of debts subject to relinquishment, and the year in which the relinquishment was benefited from.**

(Added by Chapter 36-2 of Legislative Decree No. 71 of 2009 dated December 21, 2009).
