The party with the right of option shall declare whether to ratify or rescind the sale within the following periods:

Firstly: Within thirty days from the date of the contract if the object of the sale is a plot of land or real property.

Secondly: Within five days if the object of the sale is movable property or domestic animals.  The contracting parties may stipulate a shorter period, but not a longer one; if a longer period is stipulated, it shall be reduced to the period specified in the above two instances.
