If a person hires another to work with him in his craft, and the hired person suffers bodily harm in the course of the assigned work due to a violation—

Violations described as felonies under this law are crimes punishable by death or imprisonment exceeding five years.  Crimes punishable by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars, are described as misdemeanors.  Crimes punishable by imprisonment not exceeding fifteen days or a fine not exceeding sixty dinars are described as infractions. (Article 122, Code of Criminal Procedure)  The violation may be due to the hirer’s failure to comply with the regulations pertaining to his craft or profession, or his breach thereof.
