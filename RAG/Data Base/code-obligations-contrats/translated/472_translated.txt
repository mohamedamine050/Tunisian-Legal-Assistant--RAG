It is not permissible for the litigants in the cases stipulated in the above-mentioned articles to request the submission of the original evidence existing in the court's registry; rather, they have the right to request a comparison of their copy with the original or with the copy existing in the court's registry if the original is missing. They also have the right to request a photographic copy of what is mentioned, the cost of which shall be borne by them. If neither the original nor the copy preserved in the public archive registry exists, the official copies prepared in accordance with Articles 470 and 471 shall be relied upon, provided they are free from any erasures, alterations, or anything else that raises doubt.
