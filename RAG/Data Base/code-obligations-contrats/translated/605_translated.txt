The buyer shall bear the expenses of transporting the goods from the place of delivery, the expenses of acceptance, the payment of the price including exchange fees, the fees of the conveyancer for drawing up the purchase deed, stamp duty, registration fees, packaging of the goods, their weighing, and their transport.

The acceptance expenses include customs duties, taxes, and transit center fees during transport and upon arrival at the destination.

All of the foregoing, unless otherwise stipulated in the contract or customary practice.
