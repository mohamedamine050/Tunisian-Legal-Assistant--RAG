If the worker travels for trade in a valid Qirad contract, or for the purpose of *istikhraj* (recovery), he may take from the Qirad funds his expenses for travel to and from, and for his stay outside his home country.  *Istikhraj* is the process by which a creditor obtains repayment of a financial debt contracted by his debtor.  However, expenses not related to the Qirad business, such as expenses for pilgrimage (Hajj), vows (Nazar), or medication, shall be borne by him.
