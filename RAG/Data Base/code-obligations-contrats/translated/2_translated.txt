Subject to the provisions of international treaties and specific agreements, the income tax, which is levied on the total income or income elements, shall be due on the first of January of each year on every natural person residing habitually in Tunisia, on their total profits or income earned during the preceding year.

Natural persons who are considered habitually resident in Tunisia are:

1. Those who have a main residence at their disposal in Tunisia.

2. Those who reside in Tunisia on a continuous or intermittent basis for at least 183 days during the civil year, if they do not have a main residence at their disposal in Tunisia.

3. State employees and officials who perform their duties or tasks in a foreign country, provided that they are not subject in that country to a personal tax on their total income.
