The seller guarantees to the buyer the soundness of the sale from defects that diminish its value noticeably or render it unfit for its intended use according to its type or by virtue of the contract.  Defects that do not diminish its value or use except to a negligible extent are not guaranteed by the seller, as are defects excused according to custom and usage. The seller also guarantees the qualities he declared at the time of sale or those stipulated by the buyer.
