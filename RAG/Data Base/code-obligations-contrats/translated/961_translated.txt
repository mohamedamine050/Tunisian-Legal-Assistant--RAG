The mortgagor may dispose of the property as an owner would in his ownership, and may build upon it or plant trees, or benefit from it in all ways, or change its condition. He is the owner of all that he has created, together with all exploitation of the property and what has followed or been added to it. From the date of the contract, he has the right to pursue all claims related to the aforementioned property. As for his rights concerning any treasures, minerals, quarries, and mines found therein, they are subject to special rules.
