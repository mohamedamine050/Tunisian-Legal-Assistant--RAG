All work necessary for the enjoyment of the premises, such as opening water channels, cleaning and maintaining ditches, roads, pathways, and fences, and minor repairs necessary for buildings and structures located on the agricultural land, shall only be the responsibility of the lessee if stipulated in the contract or customary in the location. In such a case, he must: carry out such work at his own expense without recourse for reimbursement, and he shall be liable to the lessor for any loss arising from his negligence.

As for major construction and repairs to buildings or other appurtenances of the land, these shall be the responsibility of the lessor, as shall the repair of wells, streams, water channels, and cisterns. If the lessor delays in carrying out such repairs, the provisions of Article 742 shall apply.
