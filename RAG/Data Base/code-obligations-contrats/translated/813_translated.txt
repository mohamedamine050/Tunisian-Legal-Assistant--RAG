If the area of the land is found to be greater or less than that stated in the contract, this shall necessitate a reduction or increase in the rental value, or the termination of the lease, according to the circumstances and in accordance with the principles established in the law of sale.  The right to bring this claim is extinguished after one year from the date of the contract and from the date the lessee was given possession of the leased property, if stipulated in the contract.  A claim [1] is a request by one party for their right from another in the presence of a judge; the claimant is called the plaintiff and the respondent is called the defendant. [2] A claim is the right of any claimant to bring their claim before the court for a judgment on its subject matter. It is, for the opposing party, the right to present grounds for defense or arguments aimed at refuting that claim.
