## English Translation:

I. To determine the net income amount, the following items shall be deducted from the gross income amount referred to in Chapter 25 of this Code, including in-kind benefits: 

- Mandatory deductions made by the lessor for the purpose of forming revenue, pensions, or allowances, or for covering compulsory social security schemes. 

- Professional expenses, estimated at 10% of the remaining amount after deducting these deductions, but not exceeding a deduction of 2,000 dinars annually. (As amended by Chapter 14-2 of Law No. 78 of 2016 dated December 2016)

II. Pensions and retirement benefits shall be subject to a deduction equal to 25% of their gross amount for the purpose of applying the tax thereon.

III. In-kind benefits shall be assessed according to their actual value; however, individuals who legally benefit from a housing allowance and who occupy housing owned by the employer without benefiting from the allowance, shall be subject to tax based on the amount of the allowance they would have been entitled to. 

The same procedure shall apply when using a company car for personal purposes.
