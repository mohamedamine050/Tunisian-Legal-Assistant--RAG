The employee shall guarantee the unforeseen circumstance or force majeure, if it results from his transgression, negligence, or violation described as felonies under the meaning of this law; crimes punishable by death or imprisonment exceeding five years.  Crimes punishable by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars, are described as misdemeanors. Crimes punishable by imprisonment not exceeding fifteen days or a fine not exceeding sixty dinars are described as violations.  Article 122 of the Code of Criminal Procedure. The employee must fulfill the conditions correctly imposed upon him by the employer.
