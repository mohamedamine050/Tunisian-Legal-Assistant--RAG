The tax is also levied on non-resident natural persons who realize income arising in Tunisia or realize the capital gains provided for in paragraph 2 of Article 27 of this Code, on the basis of such income or such capital gains only.

The provisions of this paragraph apply to capital gains resulting from the transfer of shares or social bonds or shares in funds provided for in the legislation relating thereto, or rights related to the aforementioned securities or their transfer, with respect to transfer or retransfer operations carried out as of January 1, 2011. (Added by Article 41-5 of Law No. 58 of 2010 dated December 17, 2010 and amended by Articles 3-4-1 and 2 of Decree No. 100 of 2011 dated October 21, 2011)

The tax is not levied on:

- Interest on deposits and securities in foreign currency or in convertible dinars,

- (Abrogated by Article 19-1 of Law No. 54 of 2013 dated December 30, 2013 which set a date of application for distribution operations carried out as of January 1, 2015).

- (Abrogated by Article 54 of Law No. 54 of 2013 dated December 30, 2013).

- Rewards for the leasing of ships or aircraft for the purpose of international navigation.

- (Added by Article 53 of Law No. 127 of 1994 dated December 26, 1994 and amended by Article 59 of Law No. 88 of 1997 dated December 29, 1997 and amended by Article 45 of Law No. 101 of 2002 dated December 17 and abrogated by Article 441 of Law No. 58 of 2010 dated December 17, 2010).
