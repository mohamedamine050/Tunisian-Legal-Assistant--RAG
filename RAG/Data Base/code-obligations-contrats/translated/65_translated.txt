**English Translation:**

Notwithstanding the provisions of Articles 12 and 12 bis of Law No. 114 of 1989, dated December 30, 1989, concerning the issuance of the Tax Code on Income of Natural Persons and Corporate Tax, the income or profits derived from direct investments, as defined in Article 3 of the Law on Investment in the Agricultural and Fisheries Sector, shall be excluded from the basis of the tax on income of natural persons or corporate tax entirely for the first ten years commencing from the date of the actual commencement of the activity.

The benefit of this exclusion shall require the keeping of accounting records in accordance with the accounting legislation for companies, in the case of persons engaged in industrial or commercial activity or a non-commercial profession, as defined in this Code.
