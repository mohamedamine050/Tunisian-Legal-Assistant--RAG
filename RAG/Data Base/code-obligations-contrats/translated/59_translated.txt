## English Translation of Arabic Legal Text:

**I.** For the purpose of income tax, corporate income tax, taxes withheld at source on total income or income components, or corporate tax, every person subject to or exempt from one of these taxes shall, according to a model prepared by the administration, prepare an annual declaration of their income or profits, or a declaration of capital gains in the case of a transfer referred to in paragraphs 2 and 3 of Article 27 of this Code, and deposit it with the tax office of the place of taxation.

This declaration shall include, obligatorily, all income and profits, regardless of their tax regime. (Added by Article 69 of Law No. 127 of 1994 dated December 1994 and amended by Article 7 of Law No. 82 of 2000 dated August 9, 2000, concerning the issuance of the Code of Tax Rights and Procedures).

**I bis.** Natural persons subject to or exempt from income tax shall attach to their annual income tax declarations, a declaration of their standard of living elements, including elements relating to persons they are financially responsible for, who do not declare a personal income, according to a model prepared by the administration. (Added by Article 42 of Law No. 78 of 2016 dated December 17, 2016).

**II.** Legal persons, as well as natural persons subject to income tax under the real system, including those who have simplified accounting according to the accounting legislation of companies, shall attach to their annual declarations:

(Amended by Article 82-1 of Law No. 54 of 2013 dated December 30, 2013).

- Financial statements,
- Table of the calculation of the taxable result, 
- Detailed list of depreciation,
- Detailed list of provisions under the heading of non-recoverable receivables, showing the identity of the debtor, the nominal value of each receivable, the provision amount, and the net accounting value,
- Detailed list of provisions under the heading of inventory write-downs, showing the original cost, the provision amount, and the net accounting value for each type of inventory,
- Detailed list of provisions under the heading of write-downs of shares and social securities, showing the original cost, the provision amount, and the net accounting value of shares and social securities,
- Detailed list of donations, grants, and subsidies provided, including the identity of the beneficiaries, the amounts provided to them, and the cost of acquiring or building homes on the date they were provided to the beneficiaries. (Amended by Article 79 of Law No. 88 of 1997 dated December 29, 1997, and by Article 49-2 of Law No. 54 of 2014 dated August 19, 2014, and completed by Article 622-2 of Law No. 78 of 2016 dated December 2016). 
- Document showing the account numbers opened with banks and the National Post Office in the name and for the account of the persons concerned, as well as the date of opening these accounts. (Added by Article 2-54 of Law No. 56 of 2018 dated December 27, 2018).

However, the persons referred to in paragraph III of Article 62 of this Code, who are not required to keep accounting according to the accounting legislation of companies, shall attach to their declarations a statement indicating their annual turnover or invoice numbers, as applicable, and the amount of their purchases and expenses that they claim to deduct to determine the excess profit or income. (Amended by Article 76 of Law No. 88 dated December 29, 1997, by Article 39-3 of Law No. 58 of 2010 dated December 17, 2010, and by Article 82-2 of Law No. 54 of 2013 dated December 2013).

"The parties concerned, excluding those who realize the income referred to in Article 27 of this Code, shall also attach to their annual tax declarations the account numbers opened with banks and the National Post Office in their name and for their account, as well as the date of opening these accounts." (Added by Article 54-3 of Law No. 56 of 2018 dated December 27, 2018).

(Paragraph amended by Article 39-2 of Law No. 58 of 2010 dated December 17, 2010, and repealed by Article 82-3 of Law No. 54 of 2013 dated December 30, 2013).

The persons referred to in paragraph III, thirdly, of Article 62 of this Code, shall attach to their annual declarations:

- A calculation of results according to a model prepared by the administration,
- A detailed list of depreciation.

(Added by Article 18-2 of Law No. 53 of 2015 dated December 25, 2015).

- Document showing the account numbers opened with banks and the National Post Office in the name and for the account of the persons concerned, as well as the date of opening these accounts. (Added by Article 54-4 of Law No. 56 of 2018 dated December 27, 2018)

**II bis.** (Added by Article 30 of Law No. 56 of 2018 dated December 27, 2018). Companies resident or established in Tunisia, which are subject to a relationship of dependency or control within the meaning of Article 48, seventhly, of this Code, and whose annual gross turnover is equal to or exceeds 20 million dinars, shall deposit the annual declaration relating to transfer pricing through reliable electronic means, according to a model prepared by the administration, within the same deadlines provided for in paragraph 2 of paragraph I of Article 60 of this Code.

This declaration shall include:

A. Information relating to the group of companies, including in particular:

- Information on the activity, including changes that have occurred during the year,
- Information on the transfer pricing policy adopted by the group of companies,
- List of assets of the group of companies used by the company subject to the declaration, as well as the corporate name of the company owning the assets and its tax residence.

B. Information relating to the company subject to the declaration, including in particular:

- Information on the activity, including changes that have occurred during the year,
- Summary table of the financial and commercial transactions carried out with companies with which it has a relationship of dependency or control within the meaning of Article 48, seventhly, of this Code. This table shall specify the nature of the transactions, their amount, the corporate name of the companies with which it has a relationship of dependency or control concerned by the transactions, and their tax residence,
- Methods of setting transfer prices used and changes that have occurred during the year,
- Information on loans granted and loans received from companies with which it has a relationship of dependency or control within the meaning of Article 48, seventhly, of this Code,
- Information on financial and commercial transactions carried out with companies with which it has a relationship of dependency or control within the meaning of Article 48, seventhly, of this Code, without consideration or for non-monetary consideration,
- Information on transactions carried out with companies with which it has a relationship of dependency or control within the meaning of Article 48, seventhly, of this Code, which are subject to a prior agreement on transfer pricing methods concluded between the company concerned by the transaction and the competent tax authorities of another country, or subject to correspondence in this regard issued by the said tax authorities.

**III.** Partnerships, limited liability companies, and economic interest groups, even if they are not subject to tax in their own name, must file a declaration stating their gross profit determined in accordance with the provisions of this Code. This declaration shall be accompanied by a statement of the distribution of these profits to the partners, shareholders, or members who are subject to tax in their own name. (Amended by Article 61 of Law No. 123 of 2001 dated December 28, 2001).

Managers of the joint debt funds provided for in Article 4 of this Code shall file an annual declaration containing the income from transferable securities realized, the identity of the joint owners who are beneficiaries and subject to tax in their own name, the amounts of source deductions made, and the amounts of source deductions deducted in accordance with the provisions of Articles 52 and 54 of this Code. (Added by Article 28 of Law No. 123 of 2001 dated December 28, 2001).

**IV.** Legal persons subject to corporate income tax shall attach to their corporate income tax declarations a list, according to a model prepared by the administration, of their participations exceeding 10% of the capital of other companies, including in particular the name of the companies, their registered office, their tax identification number, and the percentage of their participation. (Added by Article 70 of Law No. 101 of 2002 dated December 17, 2002).

**V.** The persons referred to in Article 44 bis of this Code shall provide, in their annual tax declarations, the necessary information on their activity, including in particular:

- The amount of purchases of goods, services, etc.,
- The value of goods inventories,
- Operating assets and their method of financing,
- The area of land intended for exploitation and the rent fixed in the case of exploitation through leasing.

(Added by Article 64 of Law No. 90 dated December 31, 2004 and amended by Article 37-3 of Law No. 58 of 2010 dated December 17, 2010).

**VI.** The persons referred to in paragraph 1 of Article 62 of this Code shall file a unified tax file within the same deadlines provided for in paragraph 1 of Article 60 of this Code. The conditions and procedures for filing shall be determined by a decree issued by the Minister of Finance. (Added by Article 41-6 of Law No. 78 of 2016 dated December 17, 2016).
