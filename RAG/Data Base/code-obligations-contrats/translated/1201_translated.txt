The agent of absolute Qard (loan) has the authority to dismiss, postpone, reject due to defect, buy, sell, lease, sublease, for cash or credit, but not for a term not used by merchants, and to accept assignment of the price.

The price is that agreed upon by the contracting parties, whether it exceeds or falls short of the sale price.  He may also authorize another to manage his affairs, mortgage, hypothecate, and appear before the courts in matters relating to the transactions he has concluded, whether as claimant or defendant, and to secure his liability with bills of exchange.

All of this is to the extent necessary to carry out what is under his charge, subject to any conditions stipulated.
