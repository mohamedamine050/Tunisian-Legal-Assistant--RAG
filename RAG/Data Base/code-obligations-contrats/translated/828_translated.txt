Lease of Service Contract

Contract
Contract

The contract is a binding legal agreement that obligates the contracting parties and defines their obligations and rights with respect to the specific transaction.  One contracting party undertakes to provide the other party directly with a specific service for a period or to complete a work in exchange for remuneration that the other party undertakes to pay.


Contract for Work

Contract
Contract

The contract is a binding legal agreement that obligates the contracting parties and defines their obligations and rights with respect to the specific transaction.  It is based on the manufacture of a specific thing with a precise description in exchange for a specific remuneration.


The perfection of the contract in both instances is by the mutual consent of the parties.
