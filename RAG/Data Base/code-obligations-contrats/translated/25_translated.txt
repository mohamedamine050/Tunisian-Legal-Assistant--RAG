Salaries, wages, bonuses, grants, and other related benefits, as well as pensions and lifetime income, shall be considered elements of the gross income that constitutes the basis for income tax.

Bonuses, grants, and other benefits received by agents in limited liability companies in their capacity as such shall be classified as salaries and wages. (Added by Article 422-1 of the Commercial Code No. 58 of 2010 dated December 17, 2010)
