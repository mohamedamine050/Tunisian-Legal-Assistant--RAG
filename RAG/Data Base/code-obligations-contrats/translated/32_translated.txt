## English Translation:

**Not considered as distributed income:**

1. **Consumption of realized capital** through amounts resulting from the disposal of assets, as well as the **return of contributions** in case of capital reduction.

2. **Distribution of issue bonus** to contributors.

3. **Amounts recovered by partners resulting from the** liquidation of the company, **related to their contributions to capital or previously consumed capital that was subject to tax or related to capitalized reserves exceeding five years in duration.**
