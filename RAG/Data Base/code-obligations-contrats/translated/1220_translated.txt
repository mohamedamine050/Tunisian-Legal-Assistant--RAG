The Qirad contract terminates in the following ways:

First: By rescission of the contract by one of the contracting parties.

Second: By judicial rescission.

Third: By the death of the worker or by his loss of legal capacity.

Fourth: By the expiration of its term, if it is for a term, or by the completion of the work for which the Qirad contract was concluded.

Fifth: By the loss of the capital before commencing work or during its course.
