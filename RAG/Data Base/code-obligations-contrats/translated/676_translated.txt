The buyer shall pay the price agreed upon by the contracting parties, whether it exceeds or falls short of the value of the goods, on the date and in the manner agreed upon in the contract.  In the absence of any agreement in the contract, the sale shall be considered a cash sale, and the buyer shall be obligated to pay the price agreed upon by the contracting parties, whether it exceeds or falls short of the value of the goods, upon receipt of the goods.

The expenses of paying the price agreed upon by the contracting parties, whether it exceeds or falls short of the value of the goods, shall be borne by the buyer.
