Every person engaged in an industrial, commercial, or non-commercial professional activity, except those involved in the distribution of goods, products, and services as stipulated in Article 51, Sixth, of this Code, as well as every legal entity with legal personality, having its own resources and a financial balance sheet independent of the financial balance sheets of its members or shareholders, as referred to in Article 45 of this Code, shall, before commencing their activity, deposit a declaration of existence with the tax control office having jurisdiction over them, according to a form prepared by the administration. (Amended by Article 29-2, Law No. 53 of 2015 dated December 25, 2015). 

This declaration shall be accompanied by:

- A copy of the constitutive documents for legal entities. 
- A copy of the certification or administrative license, where applicable, when the activity or the place where it is carried out is subject to a prior license.

(Paragraph added by Article 54-1, Law No. 56 of 2018 dated December 27, 2018, and repealed by Article 12 of Law No. 47 of 2019 dated May 29, 2019)

The competent tax control office shall issue a tax identification card to the persons referred to above. The persons concerned shall be required to display it at the place where the activity is carried out. (Added by Article 65, Law No. 90 of 2004 dated December 31, 2004).

(Paragraph added by Article 6-1, Law No. 54 of 2013 dated December 30, 2013, and repealed by Article 39, Law No. 54 of 2014 dated August 19, 2014).

The provisions of this chapter shall also apply to associations, regardless of their tax regime. (Added by Article 44, Law No. 56 of 2018 dated December 27, 2018).

"Notwithstanding the provisions of the third paragraph of this chapter and the territorial jurisdiction of the tax control office having jurisdiction over the company concerned, the public entities responsible for the legal formation of institutions are authorized to issue the tax identification card and the declaration of existence issued by the General Directorate of Taxes within the public entity mentioned in this paragraph." (Fifth paragraph added by Article 13 of Law No. 47 of 2019 dated May 29, 2019).
