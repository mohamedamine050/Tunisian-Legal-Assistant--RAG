The appointment must ensure placement of the suitable individual in the position that corresponds to the conditions and requirements for its performance in terms of qualifications and competencies.  The rent value may be in cash, produce, foodstuffs, or other movables, specifying the nature thereof. The rent may also be a shared portion of the produce of the leased property. If the property is land, it may be stipulated that the lessee carry out specific works supplementing the determined rent value in cash or produce.
