## English Translation: 

**Shall be considered as distributed income:**

1.  Unless proven otherwise, amounts placed at the disposal of partners directly or through third parties under the title of advances, loans or installments, excluding those granted between the parent company and its subsidiaries.

If these amounts are returned to the legal entity, the tax installment levied on these amounts shall be deducted from the tax due for the year in which the return took place or for subsequent years. 

2.  Bonuses, benefits and hidden profits.

3.  Bonuses and grants awarded according to the legislation and regulations in force to members of the boards, bodies and committees of anonymous companies and shareholding partnerships, in their capacity as such. (Amended by Article 30-1 of Law No. 78 of 2016 dated December 17, 2016).
