## English Translation:

**Net income consists of:**

* **Amounts actually received from abroad**
* **The net amount received from gambling, games of chance, and lotteries**
* **Income determined based on apparent and clear personal expenses and wealth growth.** (Amended by Article 19-2, Law No. 53 of 2015, dated December 25, 2015).

**Net income for salaries, wages, pensions, and annuities shall be determined in accordance with the provisions of Article 26 of this Code.** (Added by Article 61, Law No. 88 of 1997, dated December 29, 1997).

**Notwithstanding the provisions of the second paragraph of this Article, net income for pensions and annuities shall be determined after deducting 80% of the gross amount, provided that the pensions and annuities are transferred to a bank or postal account in Tunisia, or a declaration of their receipt is submitted, along with proof, with the annual tax return.** (Added by Article 35, Law No. 85 of 2006, dated December 25, 2006).
