The seller is not liable under the following circumstances:

Firstly: If the sale item was seized by force or due to force majeure.

Secondly: If the seizure was ordered by the authorities and this order was not based on a pre-existing right that the authority was enforcing or protecting, or if it resulted from an act committed by the seller.

Thirdly: If a third party infringed upon the buyer's possession without claiming a right in the sale item.
