## English Translation:

1. Surpluses, returns, shares, reimbursements, and other proceeds from public bonds and securities, including bonds and loan securities issued by the State, local public authorities, public institutions, associations of any kind, and civil and commercial companies. 

2. Surpluses from debts.

3. Surpluses and returns from deposits of monetary funds (amended by Article 16-2 of Law No. 53 of 2015 dated December 25, 2015).

4. Surpluses and rentals of guarantees (amended by Article 66 of Law No. 80 of 2003 dated December 29, 2003).

5. Proceeds from current accounts.

6. Income from shares and remaining liquidation proceeds of the common debt fund as stipulated in Law No. 83 of 2001 dated July 24, 2001 concerning the issuance of the code of collective investment institutions (added by Article 27 of Law No. 123 of 2001 dated December 28, 2001).

7. Net profit of sukuk, their returns, and the results of the liquidation of the common sukuk fund as stipulated in the legislation related thereto (added by Article 28 of Law No. 54 of 2013 dated December 30, 2013).
