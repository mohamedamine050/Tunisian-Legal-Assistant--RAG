(Amended by Law No. 57 of 2000, dated June 13, 2000).

If the value of the deposit exceeds one thousand dinars, it must be evidenced in writing, except in the case of a forced deposit, which is one necessitated by force majeure or an emergency such as fire, shipwreck, and the like. In such cases, the deposit may be proven by all types of evidence, regardless of the amount of the deposit.
