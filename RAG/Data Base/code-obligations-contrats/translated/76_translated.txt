Subject to the provisions of Articles 12 and 12 bis of Law No. 114 of 1989 dated December 30, 1989, relating to the issuance of the Tax Code on Income of Natural Persons and the Tax Code on Corporations, the following is fully deductible, up to the amount of income or profit exceeding the company's liabilities, by including the company's capital in its liabilities at the end of the fiscal year:

Re-invested income or profits subscribed in the original capital or in its increase for newly established enterprises by young holders of higher degrees not exceeding forty years of age on the date of the company's formation, who bear personal and permanent responsibility for managing the project (amended by Article 21-1 of Law No. 66 of 2017 dated December 18, 2017).

Benefiting from the aforementioned deduction requires compliance with the conditions stipulated in Article 75 of this Code.
