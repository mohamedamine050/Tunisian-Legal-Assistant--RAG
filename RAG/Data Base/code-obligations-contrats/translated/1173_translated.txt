If there is no stipulation in the contract regarding the monetary compensation for the workers' services, the agricultural overseer's remuneration shall be determined as follows:

First: If the agricultural activity involves three to ten head of livestock, the agricultural overseer shall receive one *qafiz* of wheat and one *qafiz* of barley.

Second: If the agricultural overseer is hired specifically for winter or summer services, he shall receive half of the aforementioned amount.

Third: If the agricultural overseer resides on the farm where he works and has dependents, he shall receive the aforementioned two *qafiz* of wheat and barley, and he may sow an allowance of two *wibe* of wheat and two *wibe* of barley.
