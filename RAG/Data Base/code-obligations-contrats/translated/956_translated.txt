The contract is a legally binding agreement that obligates the contracting parties and defines their obligations and rights regarding the specific transaction.  The execution is effected by mutual agreement of the parties on the contracted property, the extent of the execution, and the remaining terms of the contract.  Its proof must be in writing, bearing a fixed date, and including a precise description of the property, its appurtenances and rights, any plantings or buildings, and other works thereon. The contract shall specify the agreed-upon value of the property as it stood at the time of the contract.
