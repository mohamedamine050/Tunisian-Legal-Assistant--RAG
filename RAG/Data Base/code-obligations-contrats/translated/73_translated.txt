Notwithstanding the provisions of Articles 12 and 12 bis of Law No. 114 of 1989 dated December 30, 1989, relating to the issuance of the Income Tax Code on Natural Persons and the Corporate Tax Code, the income or profits reinvested in the subscription of the original capital or in its increase for the institutions referred to in Articles 63 and 65 of this Code are entirely excluded from the income tax base of natural persons or corporate tax, up to the amount of income or profit that exceeds the company's liability by increasing the company's capital within the debts incurred by it at the end of the fiscal year.

This exemption is not granted for subscription operations dedicated to the acquisition of land (added by Article 37-1 of Law No. 66 of 2017 dated December 18, 2017).
