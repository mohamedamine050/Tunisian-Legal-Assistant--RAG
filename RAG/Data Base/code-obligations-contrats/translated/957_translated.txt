The mortgagor shall provide the mortgagee with possession of the property subject to the mortgage and enable him to register the deed.

The mortgagor shall bear the costs of delivery and the costs of registering the deed, as well as the fees of experts and brokers; these costs shall be borne by both parties unless otherwise stipulated by a condition or custom.

The provisions concerning delivery in sales, as stipulated in Article 592, shall apply to mortgages, except as otherwise agreed by the parties.
