The depositor shall pay the depositary the fee for safekeeping the deposit, any expenses incurred for its transportation, customs duties, and any other expenses necessary for the deposit.

Expenses advanced by the depositary from his own funds shall be reimbursed upon request without delay.

As for the fee for safekeeping the deposit, it is only payable at the agreed time, the customary time, or upon delivery of the goods if before the agreed term.

If the depositor receives part of the deposit, the depositary is entitled to claim the proportionate part of the fee upon delivery of that part.

All of the foregoing is subject to contrary stipulation.
