Contract

The contract is a valid legal agreement that binds the contracting parties and defines their obligations and rights concerning the specific transaction.  The owner or the administrator of the Waqf assigns the possession of the property and its disposal in perpetuity, provided that the assignee undertakes to pay a specific annual or monthly sum that does not change, and retains the right to purchase the assignment stipulated in the order dated January 22, 1905.  It is permissible to stipulate that the assignee make certain improvements, such as building or planting trees, which are considered part of his undertaking under the contract.
