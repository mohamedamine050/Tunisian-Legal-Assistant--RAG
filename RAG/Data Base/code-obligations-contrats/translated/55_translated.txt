## Chapter 55

**I.** The debtor of amounts subject to withholding at source, pursuant to the provisions of Chapter 52 and the provisions of paragraph II bis of Chapter 53 of this Code, shall deliver a certificate to the beneficiaries of these amounts upon each payment (amended by Article 93 of Law No. 123 of 2001 dated December 28, 2001).

This certificate shall contain:

- The identity and address of the beneficiary,
- The gross amount paid to him, including the amount of withholding at source,
- The net amount paid to him.

The companies and groups referred to in Chapter 4 of this Code shall deliver a certificate to the partners or members containing:

- The identity and address of the partner or member,
- The gross share in profits,
- The amount of the advance related to the net share in profits.

(Added by Article 45 of Law No. 125 of 1993 dated December 27, 1993 and amended by Article 60 of Law No. 123 of 2001 dated December 28, 2001).

**II.** Every natural or legal person paying salaries, wages, allowances, and life annuities shall deliver an annual certificate to the beneficiaries containing, in addition to the information mentioned above:

- The number of children in foster care considered for the calculation of deductions.
- The amount of the grant for work expenses.
- Details of in-kind benefits.
- The amount of exempt investments made by the concerned party and considered for the calculation of deductions.

**III.** The debtors mentioned above and the institutions subject to the obligation to allocate the advance referred to in Article 51, Fourth of this Code, shall deposit, within a maximum period of 30 April of each year, with the tax administration office or the financial cashier to which they are subject, a statement of the amounts referred to in paragraphs I and II of this chapter, and the advances they have allocated, specifying the full identity of the beneficiaries of these amounts and the persons to whom the advance has been allocated. (Amended by Article 69 of Law No. 101 of 2002 dated December 17, 2002, and by Article 37-3 of Law No. 27 of 2012 dated December 29, 2012).

The statement must be deposited within the deadlines stipulated in Article 58 of this Code in the event of transfer or termination of employment.

The obligation to declare as stipulated by this paragraph also applies, under the same conditions, to institutions subject to the obligation to allocate the percentage stipulated in Article 51, Sixth of this Code. (Added by Article 29-5 of Law No. 53 of 2015 dated December 25, 2015).

**IV.** Persons who collect cash in exchange for providing goods, services, or property to customers, in amounts exceeding the limit set in Article 14, number 11, and Article 15, number 6, of this Code, shall declare the aforementioned amounts, specifying the full identity of the customers concerned, within the statement referred to in paragraph III of this chapter. (Added by Article 35-1 of Law No. 54 of 2013 dated December 30, 2013).
