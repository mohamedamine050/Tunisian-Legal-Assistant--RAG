Qard Contract

Contract

The contract is a legally binding agreement that obligates the contracting parties and defines their obligations and rights regarding the specific transaction.  Pursuant to it, a person, being the owner of the capital, delivers a specific sum of money to another person, the worker, on the condition that this worker undertakes to trade with it in the name of, and for the benefit of, the owner of the capital. The worker shall be entitled to a specific share of the profit.
