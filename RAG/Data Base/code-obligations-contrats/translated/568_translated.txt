Bankruptcy

Bankruptcy is a legal status where a person or company is unable to repay its debts. This may lead to liquidation.

Liquidation

The process of settling debts and distributing assets in the case of bankruptcy.  Assets are used to pay creditors.

The persons appointed to liquidate the accounts of companies upon their dissolution may not accept for themselves the transfer of any funds under their consideration (...).  Nor may they accept for themselves the transfer of any debts owed to them by those under their consideration.

However, the transfer or sale may be approved by those in whose right it occurs, if they have the capacity to dispose of property, or by the court or other relevant bodies.
