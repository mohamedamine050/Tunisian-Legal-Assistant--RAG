The seller may not refuse delivery:

Firstly: If a third party is assigned the receipt of the price agreed upon by the contracting parties, whether it exceeds or falls short of the value of the goods sold, or what remains thereof.

Secondly: If the buyer assigns it to a third party, for the entire price or what remains thereof, and the assignment is accepted.

Thirdly: If the seller grants the buyer a period to pay the price after the sale.
