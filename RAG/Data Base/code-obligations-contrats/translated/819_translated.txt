The lease of land intended for agriculture expires upon the expiration of the period specified in the contract.

(The second paragraph was repealed by Article 16 of Law No. 30 of 1987, dated June 12, 1987, concerning the regulation of agricultural leases).

Notice of termination must be given six months before the expiration of the current year.

(The fourth paragraph was repealed by Article 16 of Law No. 30 of 1987, dated June 12, 1987).

If the land is irrigated and the year consists of twelve months, and if the tenant has green crops at the time the months are completed, the landowner is obliged to allow the tenant to harvest them all, provided that the tenant pays rent for the additional period.
