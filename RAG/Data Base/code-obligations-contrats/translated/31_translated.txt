## English Translation:

I. If a legal entity, possessing legal personality, independent financial resources separate from the financial resources of its members or shareholders, is no longer subject to corporate tax, its profits and reserves, integrated into the capital for less than five years, shall be distributed to the partners according to their respective shareholdings. 

II. Except in the case of a sole proprietorship limited liability company as defined in the Commercial Companies Code, the collection of shares or social contributions by a single partner shall be considered a cessation of activity. (Amended by Article 622-2 of the Commercial Companies Code, Law No. 98 of December 2000).
