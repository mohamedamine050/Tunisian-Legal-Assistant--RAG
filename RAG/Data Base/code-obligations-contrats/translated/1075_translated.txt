The borrower shall guarantee the loss or damage of the loan, except in the event of an unforeseen occurrence or force majeure, if they have been negligent in its use, particularly:

- If they used it in a manner inconsistent with its nature or contrary to the agreement concerning its use.

- Or if they delayed its return.

- Or if they failed to safeguard it with the necessary means, or if another person used it without the lender's permission, if it was for the borrower's personal use.
