Partial repairs and maintenance costs shall only bind the lessee if stipulated in the contract or established by custom.  These are as follows:

Firstly: Repair of a small number of broken floor tiles.

Secondly: Replacement of glass panes, unless their breakage is due to weather events such as hail, or similar unusual events, force majeure, or events not attributable to the lessee's negligence.

Thirdly: Repair of doors, windows, partition boards, shopfronts, locks, keys, and similar items.

As for the whitening of houses, repainting, replacement of damaged wallpaper on their walls, whitening, cladding, and repair of surfaces, all of this is the responsibility of the lessor.
