I. Except for those engaged in agriculture, fishing, and individual businesses subject to the estimated tax referred to in Article 44 bis of this Code, legal persons subject to corporate income tax and natural persons subject to income tax engaging in a commercial activity or non-commercial profession are required to pay three advance payments called “advance installments,” for the tax due on their income or their total profits. (Amended by Article 28 of Law No. 113 of 1996 dated December 30, 1996, by Article 62 of Law No. 111 of 1998 dated December 28, 1998, and by Article 35-1 of Law No. 58 of 2010 dated December 17, 2010).

Natural persons engaged in traditional crafts are exempt from paying the first advance installment referred to in paragraph IV of this Article. (Amended by Article 36 of Law No. 125 of 1993 dated December 27, 1993).

Persons referred to in Article 4 of this Code are exempt from paying the advance installments for the income tax due on their share of profits and income realized by the companies, associations, and funds referred to in the same Article. (Added by Article 56 of Law No. 101 of 1999 dated December 31, 1999, and amended by Article 57 of Law No. 123 of 2001 dated December 28, 2001).

II. The advance installments payable from the second year of activity are collected in installments, each equal to 30% of the tax due for income or profits of the previous year.

The calculation base for the advance installments shall not take into account the capital gain or loss resulting from the disposal of tangible fixed assets, intangible fixed assets, and bond portfolios, without prejudice to the minimum tax stipulated in Articles 44 and 49 of this Code. (Added by Article 45 of Law No. 85 of 2006 dated December 25, 2006).

(Added by Article 29 of Law No. 113 of 1996 dated December 30, 1996 and repealed by Article 35-2 of Law No. 58 of 2010 dated December 17, 2010).

(Added by Article 73 of Law No. 123 of 2001 dated December 28, 2001 and repealed by Article 35-2 of Law No. 58 of 2010 dated December 17, 2010).

III. The amounts deducted from the source in the form of advance payments as stipulated in Article 52 of this Code shall be deducted from the advance installments due under paragraph 1 of this Article.

In case of partial or total inability to apply this deduction, the remaining installments shall be deducted from future installments, or if necessary, from the income tax or corporate income tax. 

IV. Advance installments shall be declared and paid:

- Within the first twenty-five days of the sixth, ninth, and twelfth months following the closing date of the fiscal year for natural persons.

- Within the first twenty-eight days of the sixth, ninth, and twelfth months following the closing date of the fiscal year for legal persons. (Amended by Article 36 of Law No. 125 of 1993 dated December 27, 1993 and by Article 71 of Law No. 85 of 2006 dated December 25, 2006).
