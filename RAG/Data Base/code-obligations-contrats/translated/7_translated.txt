## English Translation:

1. Tax is levied annually on the total amount of profits or income earned or received during the preceding year.

2. Tax is levied on any person not residing in Tunisia who becomes a resident during a given year, on their income earned or received from the date of their change of residence to Tunisia.

3. When a Tunisian resident taxpayer changes their residence to a location outside of Tunisia, tax is levied on their income earned or received during the year of departure, up to the date of departure, as well as on any income earned but not received before that date.

4. Upon death, tax is levied on the income earned or received by the taxpayer up to the date of their death.

The legislation in effect on January 1st of the year of departure or death shall apply in the cases referred to in paragraphs 3 and 4 of this article.
