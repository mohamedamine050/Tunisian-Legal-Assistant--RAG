The depositor shall pay the depositary all necessary expenses incurred in preserving the deposit, and shall pay the agreed-upon remuneration, whether stipulated by contract or custom, if any.  Furthermore, the depositor shall compensate the depositary for any loss incurred due to the deposit. However, the depositary's beneficial expenses shall not bind the depositor except under the conditions specified regarding the management of the affairs of a third party without authorization. The depositor is not liable to compensate the depositary for losses arising in two instances:

Firstly: If the loss is caused by the depositary's negligence.

Secondly: If the depositor was duly notified of the cause of the potential loss and failed to take the necessary precautions.
