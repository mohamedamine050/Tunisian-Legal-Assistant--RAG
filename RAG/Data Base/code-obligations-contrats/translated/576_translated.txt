The sale of another's property is permissible:

First: If authorized by the owner.

Second: Or if the sold property becomes the property of the seller.

If the owner does not authorize the sale, the buyer may request rescission of the sale, and the seller shall be liable for damages if the buyer was unaware at the time of purchase that the seller was acting without authority.  This seller shall not be able to contest the validity of the sale on the grounds that he acted without authority.
