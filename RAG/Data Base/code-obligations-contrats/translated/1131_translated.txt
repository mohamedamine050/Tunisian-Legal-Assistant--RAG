The agent shall perform the mandate with utmost care and diligence.  He is liable for any losses incurred by his principal due to his negligence, as if he had violated his mandate voluntarily, or contravened specific instructions received from his principal, or acted contrary to established practices in transactions.

If there is a justifiable reason for contravening his instructions or established practice, he must immediately inform his principal and await his response, unless time is of the essence.
