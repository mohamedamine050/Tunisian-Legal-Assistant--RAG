**Real Estate Income**

The following are deemed to be included in the category of real estate income if not already included in other categories of income:

1. Rent from built and unbuilt properties, including land used as plots.

2. Capital gain realized from the transfer of:

    * Social rights in real estate companies.
    * Buildings or parts thereof.
    * Land, except for agricultural land located in agricultural areas, which is transferred to non-real estate developers or to persons obligated to **[insert the missing information related to obligations]** within the transfer contract before the expiry of 4 years from the date of transfer. The transferee shall be liable for the payment of the tax due under this heading in the event of a breach of the aforementioned obligation.

These provisions shall not apply to transfers made:

* To a spouse, ascendants, or descendants, or
* To the beneficiary of the right of pre-emption within the real estate savings schemes established in accordance with the provisions of Articles 40 and 41 of the Land Planning and Urban Development Code, or
* Within the framework of expropriation for public interest, or
* Upon the transfer of a single dwelling unit with a total area not exceeding 1000 square meters, including built and unbuilt dependencies, for the first transfer.

For the purposes of this paragraph, transfer includes transfer of ownership, right of enjoyment, right of usufruct, right of superficies, or right of easement.

(Amended by Article 106 of Law No. 122 of 1992 dated December 29, 1992, by Article 3 of Law No. 73 of 1998 dated August 4, 1998, relating to the simplification of tax procedures, and by Article 74 of Law No. 123 of 2001 dated December 28, 2001, by Article 60-1 of Law No. 90 of 2004 dated December 31, 2004, and by Article 47-1 of Law No. 54 of 2013 dated December 30, 2013).

3. Capital gain realized upon the transfer of plots of land or parts thereof, the original ownership of which derives from the transfer without compensation of state-owned land with an agricultural character which has lost this character.

(Amended by Article 106 of Law No. 122 of 1992 dated December 29, 1992, and by Article 4 of Law No. 73 of 1998 dated August 4, 1998, relating to the simplification of tax procedures and the reduction in the rate of taxes).
