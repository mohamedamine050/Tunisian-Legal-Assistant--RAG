All documents indicating amendments to the basic laws, transfer of the head office, transfer of establishment, increase or decrease in capital, confirmation of the results and their distribution method, as well as reports of auditors and investigators, shall be deposited with the tax monitoring center or office responsible for the matter concerned. This deposition shall be done against a receipt of delivery or by registered mail within thirty days from the date of the general meeting deliberations that approved or informed them.

Likewise, legal persons referred to in Article 4 of this Code, sole proprietorship companies with limited liability, and natural persons who engage in industrial, commercial, craft, or non-commercial professional activities shall deposit a declaration of change of establishment address, social headquarters, or head office with the performance monitoring office responsible for them, using the method described above, within thirty days of the date of the change of establishment or headquarters. (Added by Article 68 of Law No. 85 of 2006 dated December 25, 2006).
