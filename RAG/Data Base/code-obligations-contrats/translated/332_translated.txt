Dismissal of the Claim

The Claim
[1] A claim is a request by one party to another for the enforcement of a right, in the presence of a judge; the requesting party is called the claimant, and the party against whom the request is made is called the defendant. [2] A claim is the right of any party with a claim to submit it to the court for judgment on its subject matter.  It is, for the opposing party, the right to present reasons for defense or arguments aimed at refuting that claim.

Lapse of the claim after one year applies as indicated above to the State, municipalities, and other legal entities. The aforementioned period is calculated from the day the contract becomes final in accordance with the laws and administrative regulations.
