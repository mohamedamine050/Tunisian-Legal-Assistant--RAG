The action for a defect or lack of the desired description must be brought within the following time limits, otherwise the right is extinguished.

If the object sold is immovable property, the action must be brought within three hundred and sixty-five days from the date of delivery.

If it is movable property or an animal, the time limit is thirty days from the date of delivery, provided the seller is notified in accordance with Article 652.

The parties may agree to increase or decrease the aforementioned time limits.  The extinction of the action for a defect is governed by Articles 384, 385, 386, 387, 388, 389, and 390.
