Other income consists of income of foreign origin that has not been subject to tax in the country of origin.

"The following are also considered as other income:

- Income earned from gambling, games of chance, and lotteries,

- Income determined based on apparent and manifest personal expenses and wealth growth, in accordance with the provisions of Chapter 43 of this Code, in the absence of income in the category of industrial and commercial profits, profits from non-commercial professions, profits from agricultural exploitation, or fishing." (Added by Article 19-1, Law No. 53 of 2015 dated December 25, 2015).
