The mandate terminates:

Firstly: Upon completion of the matter entrusted.

Secondly: Upon the occurrence of the condition terminating the agency or upon expiry of its term.

Thirdly: By the dismissal of the agent.

Fourthly: By his renunciation of his mandate.

Fifthly: By his death or the death of the principal.

Sixthly: By a change in the legal capacity of the agent or the principal, such as interdiction or bankruptcy, unless the purpose of the agency is to perform acts not prevented by such change in circumstances.

Seventhly: By the impossibility of performing the agency due to an impediment that neither party can overcome.
