The financial privileges stipulated in Articles 24, 29, 31, 32, 33, 34, 35, 36, 42, 42 bis, 145, 46, 46 bis, and 47 of the Investment Promotion Code shall continue to apply to institutions that fulfill the following conditions:

- Obtaining a declaration of investment prior to the entry into force of the Investment Law, 

- Obtaining a declaration of investment prior to the entry into force of Law No. 71 of 2016, dated September 30, 2016, concerning the Investment Law, and obtaining, within a maximum period of December 31, 2020, a decision granting this privilege and entering into the stage of activity (amended by Law No. 47 of 2019 dated May 29, 2019).
