Institutions benefiting from the incentives stipulated by this law are subject to monitoring and supervision by the competent administrative authorities. 

The investment declaration shall be deemed null and void if the investment is not commenced within one year from the date of its issuance. 

The incentives shall be withdrawn from the beneficiaries in the following cases:

- Non-compliance with the provisions of this law or its implementing texts.
- Failure to complete the investment program within four years from the date of the investment declaration, which may be extended exceptionally and once for a maximum period of two years by a reasoned decision of the Authority. 
- Unlawful diversion of the original purpose of the investment.
