The State shall continue to bear the contribution of the customary practices in the legal regime of social security, as stipulated in Articles 25, 25 bis, 43, and 45 of the Investment Promotion Code, until the expiration of the period granted to them for this purpose:

- Institutions that have obtained an investment declaration certificate before the date of entry into force of Law No. 71 of 2016 dated September 30, 2016, which is capped at December 31, 2020, regarding the decision to grant this privilege and have entered into the operational stage (amended by Law No. 47 of 2019 dated May 29, 2019);

- Institutions operating before the date of entry into force of the Investment Law.
