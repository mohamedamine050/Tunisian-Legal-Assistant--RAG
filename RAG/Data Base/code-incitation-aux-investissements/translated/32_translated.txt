## English Translation:

1) The **Supreme Investment Committee**, as stipulated in Chapter 52 of the Investment Promotion Code, shall continue to carry out the tasks entrusted to it pursuant to the current legislation until the **Supreme Investment Council** commences its duties, leading to the dissolution of the Committee.

2) The privileges stipulated in Chapters 51 bis, 51 ter, 52, 52 bis, 52 ter, and 52 sexies of the Investment Promotion Code shall continue to apply to institutions that have obtained the approval of the **Supreme Investment Committee** prior to the date of entry into force of the Investment Law. 

The phrase "Supreme Investment Committee" shall be replaced with "Supreme Investment Council" wherever it appears in current legislation, taking into account the difference in terminology.
