**Effective upon the date of entry into force of this law, all previous provisions contrary to it are hereby repealed, particularly:**

- **Article 9 of Law No. 81 of 1992, dated August 3, 1992, concerning Economic Activity Zones, as amended and supplemented by subsequent texts,**

- **Article 465 of the Commercial Code,**

- **Article 16 of Law No. 6 of 1996, dated January 31, 1996, concerning Scientific Research and Technological Development,**

- **Article 26 of Law No. 65 of 1998, dated July 20, 1998, concerning Professional Associations of Lawyers,**

- **Article 5 of Law No. 94 of 2001, dated August 7, 2001, concerning Healthcare Establishments providing Full Services to Non-Residents,**

- **Article 11 of Law No. 13 of 2007, dated February 19, 2007, concerning the Establishment of the Digital Economy,**

- **Law No. 18 of 2010, dated April 20, 2010, concerning the Creation of an Incentive System for Innovation and Renewal in the Field of Information and Communication Technologies,**

- **Decree No. 2819 of 2000, dated November 27, 2000, concerning the Establishment of the Higher Council for Export and Investment, its Scope, Composition, and Procedures, except for the provisions of Article 7 thereof.**

**This law shall be published in the Official Gazette of the Tunisian Republic and shall be enforced as a law of the State.**
