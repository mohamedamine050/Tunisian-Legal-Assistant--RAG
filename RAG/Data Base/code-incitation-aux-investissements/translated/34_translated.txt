## English Translation:

1) The provisions of Chapter 6 of the Law on Investment shall apply to institutions established within the three years preceding the issuance of this Law as if these institutions had been established on the date of entry into force of this Law. 

() The provisions of Chapter 6 of the Law on Investment shall not apply to health institutions providing their entire services to non-residents as specified in Law No. 94 of 2001 dated August 7, 2001 concerning health institutions providing their entire services to non-residents, and to economic activity zones as specified in Law No. 81 of 1992 dated August 3, 1992 concerning economic activity zones.
