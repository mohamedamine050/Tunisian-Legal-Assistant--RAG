The Fund shall manage its financial resources according to programs established based on development priorities in the field of investment. Its interventions shall include:

- Disbursing grants as stipulated in Chapter Five of this Law,

- Subscribing to joint investment funds in development capital and development capital funds, as well as funds assisting start-ups, directly or indirectly.

The proportions, ceilings, and conditions for benefiting from capital contributions shall be regulated by a Government Decree.
