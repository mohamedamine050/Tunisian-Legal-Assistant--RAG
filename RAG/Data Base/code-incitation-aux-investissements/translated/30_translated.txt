## English Translation:

1) The provisions of Articles 63, 64, and 65 of the Investment Promotion Code shall continue to apply with respect to privileges enjoyed based on the aforementioned Code.

2) The provisions of Articles 3, 5, 6, 7, and 8 of Law No. 21 of 1990, dated March 19, 1990, relating to the Tourism Investment Code shall remain in effect.
