**Effective from the date of entry into force of this Law, the provisions of the last paragraph of Chapter 2 (new) of Law No. 37 of 1991 dated June 8, 1991, concerning the establishment of the Industrial Real Estate Agency, as amended and supplemented by subsequent texts, particularly Law No. 34 of 2009 dated June 23, 2009, are hereby repealed and replaced by the following:**

**"Local communities and real estate developers shall benefit from the same incentives provided for in Chapter 19 of the Law on Investment for industrial developers in the field of infrastructure works in regional development areas."**
