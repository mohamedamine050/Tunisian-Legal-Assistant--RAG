Each institution may employ personnel of foreign nationality within a limit of 30% of the total number of personnel within the institution, and this applies until the end of the third year from the date of its legal formation or from the date of its actual commencement of operations, as chosen by the institution. This percentage must be reduced to 10% starting from the fourth year of this date. In all cases, the institution may employ four personnel of foreign nationality.

In the event of exceeding the percentages or limits stipulated in the preceding paragraph, the employment of foreign personnel is subject to a license from the Ministry in charge of employment, in accordance with the provisions of the Labor Code.

The procedures for employing foreign personnel are subject to the provisions of the Labor Code, with the exception of paragraphs 2, 3, 4, and 5 of Article 258-2 thereof.
