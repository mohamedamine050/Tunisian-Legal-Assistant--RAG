The Council shall set the policy, strategy and programs of the State in the field of investment, and shall be specifically entrusted with the following:

- Taking the necessary decisions to promote investment and improve the business and investment climate.

- Evaluating the State's investment policy in an annual report which is published,

- Approving the strategies, action plans and annual budgets of the Agency and the Fund,

- Approving the annual allocation of public financial resources allocated to the Fund in accordance with the objectives of the State's investment policy within the framework of the preparation of financial laws,

- Supervising, monitoring and evaluating the work of the Agency and the Fund,

- Approving incentives for projects of national importance as stipulated in Article 20 of this law.

The Agency shall provide the Council with permanent secretariat services. The Council shall meet periodically at least once every three months.
