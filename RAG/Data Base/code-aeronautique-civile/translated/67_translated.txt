Every person intending to manufacture an aircraft or parts thereof, or to carry out maintenance or repairs, must:

a.  Inform the competent authorities of the Ministry responsible for civil aviation. 
b.  Submit all documents or technical instructions requested of them.
c.  Carry out all necessary tests.
d.  Inform the competent authorities of the Ministry responsible for civil aviation of any modifications they intend to make during manufacture to the aircraft or any part thereof.
