The exercise of air transport activities is subject to an air exploitation license granted by decision of the Minister responsible for civil aviation, following an administrative investigation and the obtaining of an opinion from the National Council for Civil Aviation. The air exploitation license specifically defines the subject matter of the activity, its validity period, the routes or geographical areas that the entity can cover, and the fleet that it can utilize. The form and content of the air exploitation license are regulated by decision of the Minister responsible for civil aviation.
