**(Fifth)** (Added by Law No. 57 of 2004 dated July 12, 2004). Real rights, buildings, installations, and fixed equipment may only be mortgaged as security for loans contracted by the obligor for the purpose of financing the construction, modification, or expansion of the installations and equipment completed within the framework of the obligation. Creditors other than those whose debt arose in connection with the completion of the works mentioned in the preceding paragraph may not take precautionary or enforcement measures against the rights and property stipulated in this chapter. Mortgages registered on real rights, buildings, installations, and fixed equipment expire upon termination of the obligation contract.
