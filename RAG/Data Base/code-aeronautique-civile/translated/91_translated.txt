The establishment, development, operation, maintenance, and expansion of airports open to public airspace may be carried out by the state, local communities, public institutions, as well as by natural and legal persons. The technical and financial conditions that must be met by local communities, public institutions, and natural and legal persons for the establishment, development, operation, maintenance, and expansion of airports open to public airspace shall be regulated by an order, as well as the conditions related to honor for natural and legal persons.
