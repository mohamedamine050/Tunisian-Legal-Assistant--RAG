Civil aviation personnel holding valid licenses are subject to the oversight of the Minister responsible for civil aviation, particularly with regard to:

- Professional competence.
- Their employment by the operator in accordance with current legislation.
- Physical and mental fitness. 

This oversight is exercised by:

- Personnel of the relevant departments of the Ministry responsible for civil aviation.
- Specialized institutions designated by the Minister responsible for civil aviation.
- Personnel appointed by the Minister responsible for civil aviation, based on their competence or experience in the field.

The oversight process involves a mandate that authorizes the aforementioned personnel to freely access facilities, departments, and to examine documents relevant to their duties. 

The costs associated with oversight activities shall be borne by the facilities and institutions subject to oversight.

The terms and methods of implementing this oversight are determined by a decision of the Minister responsible for civil aviation.
