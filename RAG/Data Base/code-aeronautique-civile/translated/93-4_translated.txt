**(Fourthly)** (Added by Law No. 57 of 2004 dated July 12, 2004). In the event of the death of the leaseholder, the lease and the real rights arising therefrom may be transferred to the heirs, provided that the person agreed upon amongst them shall, within six months of the date of death, apply to the Minister in charge of Civil Aviation and obtain his approval. In the absence of an agreement between the heirs, each of them may apply for the appointment of a representative on their behalf, by means of a non-appealable authorization order issued by the President of the First Instance Court of Tunis within the time limit referred to in the first paragraph of this section.
