The Minister in Charge of Civil Aviation has established a Civil Aviation Medical Board, specifically tasked with the following:

a.  Studying and coordinating matters of a physiological, medical, medico-social, and health prevention nature in the field of civil aviation.

b.  Deciding on appeals against the final ruling on cases of physical and mental unfitness for civil aviation personnel holding licenses. 

c.  Providing an opinion on the approval of civil aviation medical examination centers. 

The composition, structure, and operating procedures of the Civil Aviation Medical Board shall be regulated by a decree.
