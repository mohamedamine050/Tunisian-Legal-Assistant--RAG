Whenever the aerial servitude rights on the property subject to them cause actual and permanent harm, the owner and any holder of a right may obtain compensation determined according to the damages suffered. The claim for compensation must be submitted to the Minister of Civil Aviation within one year from the date of publication of the order stipulated in Article 99 of this code in the Official Gazette of the Tunisian Republic, otherwise it will be null and void. If no amicable agreement is reached between the parties concerned and the Minister of Civil Aviation, disputes concerning this compensation shall be settled by the judicial courts.
