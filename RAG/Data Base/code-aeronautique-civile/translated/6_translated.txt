Nationality is a legal and political bond between an individual and a specific State, which includes this individual among the population that forms it. Therefore, this bond establishes mutual rights and obligations between them.

Every aircraft registered in the Tunisian Register of Civil Aircraft shall acquire Tunisian nationality. Each aircraft must bear visible markings related to nationality and registration, to be determined by a decision of the Minister in charge of Civil Aviation. The competent departments of the Ministry in charge of Civil Aviation shall maintain the Register of Civil Aircraft. The operations that require registration in the Register of Civil Aircraft, as well as the conditions and procedures for registration and inscription in this register, shall be determined by an order.
