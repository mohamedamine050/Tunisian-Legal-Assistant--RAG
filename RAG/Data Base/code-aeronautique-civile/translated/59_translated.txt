If the expenses are not paid and the insurance is not secured, the aircraft shall be resold pursuant to default within fifteen days after the same advertisements and notices prescribed in Articles 52 and 53 of this Code are published again, and at the session appointed by the Head of the Real Estate Cadastre Department following a petition submitted to him, without summons to appear. The plaintiff shall be compelled to pay the deficiency and costs by a separate judgment issued upon the conclusion of the judgment, without further proceedings.
