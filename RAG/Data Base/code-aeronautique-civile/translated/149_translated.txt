The penalties stipulated in Article 147 of this Code shall be imposed on:

Anyone who carries aboard an aircraft a camera or cinematographic device that is prohibited from being transported or used.
Anyone who infiltrates the airport or roams within it or in any restricted area not open to the public, or deliberately uses the airport without authorization for purposes for which it was not intended.

The penalties stipulated in Article 145 of this Code shall be imposed if these acts are committed under any of the following circumstances:
a) Using climbing, breaking in, or counterfeit keys
b) With intent to embezzle
c) With intent to cause harm
d) Using violence or threats
