(Amended by Law No. 25 of 2009 dated May 11, 2009) The payment of fees is required for all services provided to users and the public in the Aviation Media Area and in the Upper Aviation Media Area in Tunisia and in all airports open to public air traffic. These fees are established, and their amounts and collection methods are set by decree. Notwithstanding the provisions of the second paragraph of this chapter, the fees are subject to administrative approval, with their list, forms of approval, and monitoring of their application being established by decree.
