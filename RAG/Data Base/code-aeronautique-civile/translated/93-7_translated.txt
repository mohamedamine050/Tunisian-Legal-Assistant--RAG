**(Seventh)** (Added by Law No. 57 of 2004 dated July 12, 2004) The license shall be withdrawn from its holder after hearing him in the event of his failure to comply with the obligations incumbent upon him. In this case, the creditors whose rights have been registered shall be notified in writing with acknowledgment of receipt and notice of delivery three months before the date of the decision to withdraw the license, enabling them to propose the substitution of another person in place of the license holder.
