If the aircraft is registered in Tunisia, the cancellation and striking off of the entries related to rights of lien, mortgages, rescission claims, or precautionary measures shall be effected by the applicant presenting the following documents to the competent department for aircraft registration:

a. A written judgment, decision, or order issued by a Court resolving a legal or factual issue regarding the cancellation.
b. A receipt issued by the lawyer requesting the cancellation and stating that the judge has been notified of the expenses related to the cancellation.
c. A receipt issued by the Deposit and Consignment Fund of the Tunisian Treasury confirming the payment of the security for the cancellation costs.
