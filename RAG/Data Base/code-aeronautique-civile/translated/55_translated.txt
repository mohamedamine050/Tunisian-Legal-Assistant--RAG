The sale shall take place within the jurisdiction of the Real Estate Court on the date set by the Court.  The appointment of the appropriate individual to the position that aligns with the qualifications and requirements for carrying it out shall not be subject to any form of appeal. The date of the sale shall be six weeks prior to the date of the inventory report. The plaintiff's lawyer shall add to the case file:

(a) A notice of advertisement prepared in accordance with the provisions of Article 54 of this Code.
(b) A copy of the Official Gazette of the Tunisian Republic and of the daily newspaper in which the advertisement was published.
