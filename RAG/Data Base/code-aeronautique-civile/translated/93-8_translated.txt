**(Eighth)** (Added by Law 57 of 2004 dated July 12, 2004) The obligee is entitled to compensation for the material and direct damage he incurs in the event of the withdrawal of the guarantee before the expiration of the agreed term, for a reason other than a breach of the obligations incumbent upon him. The creditors, for the purpose of settling their debts, shall replace the obligee in respect of the penalty he is entitled to, if their debts are registered in the ledger stipulated in Article 93(bis) of this Code.
