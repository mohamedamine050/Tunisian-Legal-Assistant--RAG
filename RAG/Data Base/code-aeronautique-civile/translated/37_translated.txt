**Precautionary Attachment Not Applicable To:**

* State aircraft.
* Any other aircraft already in service on a regular public transport line.
* As well as necessary reserve aircraft.
* Any other aircraft dedicated to commercial air transport if it is ready to travel to perform that transport, provided that the debt was not contracted for the flight that the aircraft will make or arose during it.
* The provisions of this chapter do not apply to the precautionary attachment by the owner whose aircraft was unlawfully seized.
