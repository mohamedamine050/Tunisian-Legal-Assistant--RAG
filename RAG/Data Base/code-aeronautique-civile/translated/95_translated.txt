Airports are subject to the supervision of the Minister responsible for civil aviation with regard to the implementation of current legislation relating to the protection of airports, infrastructure and equipment, as well as their exploitation. This supervision is exercised by sworn inspectors attached to the departments concerned of the Ministry responsible for civil aviation. Inspectors are authorized, in the exercise of their duties, to enter at any time the airport and its annexes upon presentation of their mission order. The airport operator shall bear the costs resulting from the supervision activities.
