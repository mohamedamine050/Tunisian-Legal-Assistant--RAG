(Repeated) (Added by Law No. 57 of 2004 dated July 12, 2004) The lessee shall have a special real right over the buildings, structures and fixed equipment that he/she undertakes for the practice of the activity stipulated in the lease agreement. This right confers on the lessee, for the duration of the lease, the rights and obligations of the owner within the limits of the provisions of Articles 93 bis to 93 octies. The real rights constituted over the buildings, structures and fixed equipment shall be recorded in the register of interests of the competent departments under the Ministry responsible for State property and real estate affairs. The manner in which this register is kept shall be regulated by decree. The procedures and formalities stipulated in the legislation in force in the matter of real rights shall apply to the registration of the real right and the rights of creditors constituted on it.
