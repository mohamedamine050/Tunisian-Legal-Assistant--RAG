The following services and processes require the payment of fees:

- Issuance and renewal of licenses and qualifications for civil aviation personnel. – Approval of civil airports.
- Supervision of the exploitation of airports and air navigation facilities. – Participation in civil aviation personnel examinations. – Issuance and renewal of documents related to aircraft registration, airworthiness and operation. – Every registration in the civil aircraft registration book and the issuance of every copy or summary thereof. – Supervision of the exploitation and airworthiness of the aircraft. – On-site technical supervision. – Issuance of the approval decision.

The amounts of these fees and the methods of their collection shall be determined by an order.
