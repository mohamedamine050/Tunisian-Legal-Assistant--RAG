The agents referred to in Article 152 may, within their respective jurisdictions, seize explosives, weapons, photographs, postal correspondence, radiotelegraph and radiotelephone devices, as well as all items found in violation of current legislation.

They may seize photographic equipment and plates found aboard aircraft authorized to transport such items in cases where these aircraft pass over restricted zones as specified in Article 76 of this Code.

They may also seize carrier pigeons as well as the messages they carry.

The seized items and devices shall be confiscated by virtue of a court judgment.
