The registration of the civil aircraft shall be mandatorily deleted from the Register of Civil Aircraft in the following cases:

a. If it is taken out of service because it has become unusable.
b. If there has been no news of it for six months, starting from the day of its takeoff or the day the last news about it was received.
c. If it no longer meets the registration requirements stipulated in Article 4 of this Journal.

The compulsory deletion shall be ordered by a court order.
