In the event of the aircraft's total loss or damage, the secured creditor shall take the place of the owner to the extent of the amount of his debt in relation to the right to claim insurance compensation.

**Insurance**

**Insurance** is a contract in which the insurer undertakes to pay the insured or the beneficiary a sum of money, a regular income, or any other monetary compensation in the event of the occurrence of the insured event, in exchange for premiums or any other financial payment made by the insured to the insurer. 

The insurer shall request a listing in the Register of Mortgages before paying any compensation. The payment of any compensation shall not be exonerating without taking into account the rights of secured creditors.
