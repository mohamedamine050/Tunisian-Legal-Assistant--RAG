Repeated (Second) (Added by Law No. 84 of 2005 dated August 15, 2005). The same penalties referred to in Article 150 (new) of this Code shall be imposed on any person who: deliberately and illegally, using a tool, substance, or weapon:
a) Commits an act of violence against a person in an airport open to public air traffic that causes or may cause them serious injuries as defined in the first paragraph of Article 219 of the Code.
b) Destroys airport facilities or causes serious damage to them or to an aircraft not in service parked on its ground, or disrupts the services of this airport.
This applies provided that the act referred to in paragraph (a) or (b) of this Article affects or is likely to affect safety within this airport.
