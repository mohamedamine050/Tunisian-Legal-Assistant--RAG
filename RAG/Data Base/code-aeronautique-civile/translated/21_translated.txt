Privileges other than those mentioned in Article 18 of this Code shall be ranked after mortgages registered in the Register of Civil Aircraft. 

Privileges established before the creation of these privileges shall be ranked first. In the event of the sale of an aircraft in Tunisia, subject to rights in a State party to the Geneva Convention of 19 June 1948 concerning the international recognition of rights in aircraft, the rights stipulated in Article 1 of said Convention shall be exercised without prejudice to the rights recognized to victims of damages caused by this aircraft on the surface.
