**(Sixth)** (Added by Law No. 57 of 2004 dated 12/2004). The holder of the leasehold must demolish the buildings, structures, and fixed equipment that he has constructed at his own expense, unless otherwise expressly provided in the lease agreement or by a decision of the Minister in charge of Civil Aviation. Buildings, structures, and fixed equipment that have been approved to be retained are subject to no encumbrances or mortgages.
