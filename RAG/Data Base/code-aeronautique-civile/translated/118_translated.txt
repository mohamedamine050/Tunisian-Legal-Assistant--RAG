The obligations, commitments, or undertakings of the establishment towards third parties in return for receiving goods, services, or loans from them, as well as the liability of the carrier of persons, baggage, and goods, shall be subject to the provisions of the Convention for the Unification of Certain Rules Relating to International Carriage by Air, concluded in Warsaw on 12 October 1929, and any convention amending or replacing it ratified by the Tunisian State, even if the carriage does not have an international character within the meaning of this Convention. However, the carrier may fix a limit of liability higher than the limit provided for by this Convention or any convention amending or replacing it ratified by the Tunisian State, even if the carriage does not have an international character within the meaning of this Convention.
