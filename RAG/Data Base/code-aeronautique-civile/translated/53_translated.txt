The notices and announcements shall indicate: (a) The name, surname, profession, and address of the applicant for enforcement; (b) The instrument upon which the application is based; (c) The place chosen by the applicant; (d) The name, surname, profession, and address of the debtor, as well as the name, surname, profession, and address of the owner of the movable aircraft; (e) The type of aircraft; (f) The location of the aircraft; (g) The agreed-upon purchase price, provided it does not exceed or fall short of the value of the sale; (h) The date, place, and time of the auction.
