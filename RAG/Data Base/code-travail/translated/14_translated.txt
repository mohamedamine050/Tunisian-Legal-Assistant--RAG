The contract, which is a legally binding agreement that obligates the contracting parties and defines their obligations and rights with respect to the specific transaction, terminates:

For a contract of work concluded for a definite period, upon the expiration of the agreed period or upon completion of the work.

A contract of work concluded for an indefinite period terminates upon the expiration of the notice period for termination of the work.


The contract, which is a legally binding agreement that obligates the contracting parties and defines their obligations and rights with respect to the specific transaction, also terminates, whether concluded for a definite or indefinite period:

(a) By agreement of the parties;

(b) At the will of one party due to a serious breach by the other party;

(c) Upon impossibility of performance resulting from an unforeseen event or force majeure occurring before or during the execution of the contract, or upon the death of the worker;

(d) By rescission authorized by the court in the cases specified by law;

(e) In other cases provided for by law.
