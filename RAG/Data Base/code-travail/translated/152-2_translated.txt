Every lessor shall take the necessary and appropriate measures to protect workers and safeguard them from occupational hazards.  In particular, he/she shall:

- Ensure the health and safety of workers in the workplace;
- Provide suitable working conditions and environment;
- Protect workers from the hazards of machinery, equipment, and materials used;
- Provide appropriate collective and individual protective equipment and train workers on its use;
- Inform and educate workers about the risks of the profession they practice.
