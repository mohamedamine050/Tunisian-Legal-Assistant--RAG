Occupational health centers shall be established

on the initiative of the relevant employers' professional institutions or organizations. These centers are subject to registration procedures in the commercial register.  For this purpose, a copy of the signed statutes, in accordance with the provisions of Article 2-154 of this Code, must be deposited with the court within whose jurisdiction the center's head office is located. One of the initiators of the center's establishment shall undertake all the administrative and judicial procedures required for its formation.
