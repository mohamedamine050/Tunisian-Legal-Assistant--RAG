The worker must comply with the provisions relating to occupational health and safety and must not commit any act or omission that could hinder the application of these provisions.  He is specifically required to:

- Implement instructions relating to the protection of his health and safety and the health and safety of his fellow workers in the establishment;
- Use and maintain the preventive measures placed at his disposal;
- Participate in training courses and awareness-raising activities related to occupational health and safety organized by or in which the establishment participates;
- Immediately inform his direct supervisor of any defect he observes that could cause a risk to occupational health and safety;
- Undergo medical examinations requested of him.
