The Regional Arbitration Council shall be composed of:

- A President appointed by order;

- An arbitrator chosen by the employers' party: Member;

- An arbitrator chosen by the workers' party: Member.

The Central Arbitration Council shall be composed of:

- A President appointed by order;

- An arbitrator chosen by the employers' party: Member;

- An arbitrator chosen by the workers' party: Member.

The regional conciliation offices, and in case of failure, the territorially competent labor inspectorates, shall act as the secretariat of the regional arbitration councils.

The central conciliation office, and in case of failure, the General Directorate of Labor Inspection, shall act as the secretariat of the central arbitration council.
