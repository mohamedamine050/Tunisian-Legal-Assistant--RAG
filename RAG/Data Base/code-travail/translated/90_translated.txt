Overtime hours are considered to be the hours worked in excess of the regular weekly working hours.

The remuneration for these hours is calculated based on the basic hourly wage, increased according to the following rates:

- For a full-time work system of 48 hours per week: 75%

- For full-time work systems of less than 48 hours per week: 25% up to 48 hours and 50% thereafter,

- For part-time work systems: 50%
