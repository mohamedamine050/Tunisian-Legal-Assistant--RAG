With regard to the institutions referred to in Article 338 of this chapter, the organized and comprehensive training for certain industries or professions falling within the normal scope of the activity of these institutions may only be organized according to the provisions of an order taken after consultation with the relevant State Ministers and the Vocational Training Council.

This order shall mandatorily specify the conditions governing the training in question, as well as the methods for the recruitment of young people undergoing vocational training in this manner.
