Leave of absence with full pay not exceeding six working days must be continuous.

Leave exceeding six working days may be divided by the employer with the employee's consent.

If the leave coincides with the closure of the establishment, the division may be carried out by the employer with the consent of the workers' representatives, if any.

In the case of division, each part must be at least six working days, between two weekly rest days.  No part may be less than a full day.

The following shall not be included in the calculation of annual leave with full pay:

A. Official holidays.

B. Days of absence from work due to illness or accident.
