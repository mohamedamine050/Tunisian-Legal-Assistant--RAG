Joint agreements referred to in the previous article must contain at least the following provisions:

A. Freedom of association and freedom of expression.

B. Wages applicable to each industrial category and procedures for registering workers in each of the aforementioned categories.

C. Conditions for the engagement and dismissal of workers, without prejudice to freedom of association or freedom of expression.

D. Notice period for termination.

E. Procedures for a joint, equally balanced committee, officially constituted to bring together individuals with relevant expertise from various parts of the establishment, tasked with resolving difficulties arising from the application of the agreement.
