Disputes shall be brought before the Labour Court within whose jurisdiction the establishment where the work is performed is located.

If the work is performed outside the establishment, the dispute shall be brought before the Labour Court within whose jurisdiction the worker's place of residence is located.

In all cases, the worker may bring the claim before the Labour Court within whose jurisdiction the contract was concluded.

If the Labour Court is divided into several branches, the competent branch shall be determined according to the activity of the establishment, regardless of its nature.

The Courts of First Instance dealing with such disputes shall apply the same rules provided for in Article 185 of this Code, in accordance with Article 185.
