The procedures, notifications, judgments, and all necessary documents for their execution are exempt from registration and postal fees.

The expenses required for examinations, particularly expert fees, are advanced by the State Treasury according to the conditions set by the law concerning occupational accidents.

These provisions apply to appealed cases and those brought before the Court of Cassation, as well as to notifications of final decisions.

These provisions also apply to all cases within the purview of Labour Courts, which are handled by Nahiyah judges or primary courts in places where no Labour Court exists.

Enforcement of judgments is carried out by bailiffs, who receive their fees only by deduction from the proceeds of execution.
