Regional Councils and the Central Arbitration Council may only consider the points of dispute submitted to arbitration.

In their rulings, Arbitration Councils shall follow legal rules regarding disputes concerning the interpretation or application of legal, regulatory, or contractual provisions, and rules of equity and fairness regarding disputes concerning other matters.

Arbitration Councils have all the powers necessary to conduct the investigations required for the performance of their duties.  They may consult experts and any persons whose opinions may be enlightening.
