Under no circumstances shall a violation be permitted.

Crimes punishable by death or imprisonment exceeding five years shall be described as felonies within the meaning of this law.

Crimes punishable by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars, shall be described as misdemeanors.

Crimes punishable by imprisonment not exceeding fifteen days or a fine not exceeding sixty dinars shall be described as offenses. Article 122 of the Code of Criminal Procedure.
