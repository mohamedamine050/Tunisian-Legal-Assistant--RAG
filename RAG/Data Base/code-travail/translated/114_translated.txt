A period equivalent to twenty-six working days shall be considered as a period of actual work for the purpose of calculating annual leave.  Periods of paid leave, the maternity leave period stipulated in Article 64 of this Code, and periods during which the execution of the contract is suspended due to a work-related accident for a continuous period not exceeding one year, shall also be considered as periods of actual work.

A contract is a legally binding agreement that obligates the contracting parties and defines their obligations and rights with respect to the specific transaction.
