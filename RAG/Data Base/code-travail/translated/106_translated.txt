Landlords are required to grant their employees, whether permanent or temporary, a weekly rest period of twenty-four consecutive hours, except in the case of urgent work.  In such cases, compensation shall be provided within the following thirty days. This rest period shall be granted on Friday, Saturday, Sunday, or the weekly market day. It may be assigned on another day of the week by agreement between the parties within the establishment or with the permission of the Governor of the region if the necessity of work so requires.
