Any person who practices the profession of itinerant representative or commercial agent on Tunisian territory without holding a professional identification card, or who knowingly makes false statements to obtain this card, shall be punished by a fine ranging from 72 to 288 dinars, and in the event of recidivism, by a fine ranging from 288 to 2880 dinars.

The same penalties shall be imposed on any person found to have delivered documents or certificates gratuitously, as well as on anyone who violates the provisions of Article 420 and the last paragraph of Article 421 of this Code.
