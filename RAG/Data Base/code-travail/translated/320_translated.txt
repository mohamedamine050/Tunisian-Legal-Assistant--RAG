The violations of the provisions of this chapter or the texts enacted for its implementation shall be attested to by judicial police officers, mining service officials, and all other officials assigned this task by the Secretary of State for Planning and National Economy.

Reports drawn up pursuant to this chapter and the texts enacted for its implementation shall be accepted by the courts unless proven otherwise.

Regardless of the department to which the official issuing the violation report belongs, all reports must be immediately forwarded, via the direct superior, to the Chief Engineer of the Mining Service, who shall transmit them to the judicial authorities, appended with his observations, within ten days of their date.
