In any instance where the rest of all users falls on the same day, the rest period may be reduced to half a day for persons employed in operating power-generating and power-driving machinery, and in lubricating and inspecting power distribution machinery, and in cleaning industrial premises, shops, and offices, as well as for guards and gatekeepers.  Workers subject to this procedure are entitled to compensatory rest equal to the duration of the reduced rest period. The permission stipulated in this section does not apply to boys under the age of sixteen and girls under the age of twenty.
