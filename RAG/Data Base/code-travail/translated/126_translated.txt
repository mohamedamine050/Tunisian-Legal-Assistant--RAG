Notification of entry into the leave must be given to the beneficiary one week before leaving work.  The placement of the suitable individual in the job that matches the conditions and requirements for carrying it out, in terms of qualifications and competencies, shall be determined by the lessor and, where necessary, by rotation throughout the entire establishment or for certain types of professions after consultation with the workers concerned or their representatives.  In this, the family situation of the beneficiaries of the leave, the duration of their employment in the establishment, and the necessity of carrying out various tasks in that establishment satisfactorily shall be taken into account.
