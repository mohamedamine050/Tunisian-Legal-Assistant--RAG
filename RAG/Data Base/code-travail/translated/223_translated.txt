The Court Clerk, a judicial officer responsible for maintaining the court records and managing the court's documents, shall register the appeal request in a special register.  The Court Clerk shall deliver a receipt to the appellant and a summons for the hearing at which the case will be heard. This hearing must not be scheduled later than fifteen days from the date of receipt of the appeal request. The Court Clerk shall then summon the appellee to the same hearing, in accordance with the procedure stipulated in Article 202 of this Code, eight days before the hearing date.
