No one may accept apprentices under the age of twenty.  No apprentice under the age of 14 or over the age of 18 may be accepted for training unless authorized to do so by the competent authority, namely the Secretary of State for Youth, Sport and Social Affairs.

The conditions for implementing the preceding paragraph shall be determined by a decision of the Secretary of State for Youth, Sport and Social Affairs, particularly with regard to the medical examinations conducted on apprentices under the age of 14.
