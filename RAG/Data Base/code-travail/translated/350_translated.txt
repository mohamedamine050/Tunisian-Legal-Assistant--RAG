The contract shall be void ipso jure:

1. Upon the death of the trainee or the cessation of the institution.

2. If either the master craftsman or the trainee is subjected to one of the judicial penalties or judgments listed in Article 348.

3.  With respect to minor girls placed with a master craftsman, in the event of:
    a. Divorce;
    b. Legal dissolution of the marriage by court order;
    c. The master craftsman's separation from his wife or her death.
