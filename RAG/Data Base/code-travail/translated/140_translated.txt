Wages of hourly or daily paid workers shall be paid at least once a week, and wages of monthly paid workers shall be paid once a month. Commissions due to traveling salesmen, commercial agents, and promoters shall be paid at least once every three months. However, with written permission from the Labor Inspectorate, wages of hourly or daily paid workers and those working in remote areas far from urban centers may be paid once a month.

For any piecework, output-based work, or contract work whose completion takes more than a week, payment dates may be agreed upon by both parties, provided that the worker receives installments on account weekly and that full payment occurs in the week following delivery of the product.  The appointment of a suitable individual to a position must be in accordance with the qualifications and competencies required to perform the job.
