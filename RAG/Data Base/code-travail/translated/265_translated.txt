Violations of Articles 259, 261, and 262 of this Chapter shall be punishable by a fine ranging from 12 to 30 Dinars per day and per worker, commencing from the date the violation began until the date of its inspection.  The reports drawn up by the officers mentioned in Article 270 must precisely detail the circumstances of the violation inspected and its commencement date, basing this in particular on the documents stipulated by the Labour Code and on the notifications made by the concerned establishment or employer to the National Social Security Fund.
