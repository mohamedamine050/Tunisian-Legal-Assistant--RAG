The method of determining the professional training, recruitment methods, extraction, and monitoring, as well as the allocation of its income, shall be regulated by order.  Penalties for violations of the provisions of this chapter shall be imposed; investigations and proceedings shall be conducted, and judgments shall be issued in cases as if the matter concerned the subject matter of the "patente".

Errors, omissions, and lack of notification observed regarding those required to pay for professional training shall be penalized, in respect of this payment, by a fine equal to double the payment evaded.

The Secretary of State for Planning and National Economy is authorized to grant exemption from all or part of the aforementioned fines.
