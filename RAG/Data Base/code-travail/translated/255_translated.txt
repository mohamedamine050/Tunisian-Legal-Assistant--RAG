The assets of the union, if it is dissolved in accordance with the provisions of its constitutive law, shall be disposed of according to those provisions.  If the constitutive law contains no provisions on this matter, then the rules stipulated for the sessions of civil society organizations shall apply.  These sessions, held publicly according to the procedural rules contained in this guide, aim to obtain specific information regarding the works or activities carried out by the general administration, to ensure the soundness, integrity, transparency, and conformity of its performance with the requirements of the public interest.  These assets are not, under any circumstances, distributable to the members of the union.
