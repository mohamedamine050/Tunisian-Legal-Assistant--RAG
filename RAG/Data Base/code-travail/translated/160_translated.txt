The advisory committee of the institution shall be consulted in the following matters:

A.  Organizing the work within the institution to improve production and productivity.

B.  Matters related to social projects within the institution for the benefit of employees and their families.

C.  Promotion and professional reclassification.

D.  Training and vocational training.

E.  Disciplinary matters; in this case, the committee shall constitute a disciplinary board and shall follow the procedures stipulated in the legal, regulatory, or contractual texts to which the institution is subject.
