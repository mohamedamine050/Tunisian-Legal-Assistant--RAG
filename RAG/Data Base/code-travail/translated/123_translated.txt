The employee is entitled to one rest day per month for each month elapsed since commencing employment, provided they have worked continuously for at least six months.  The total rest period shall not exceed fifteen days, twelve of which shall be working days.

The rest period specified in the preceding paragraph is increased by two days per month of actual work for employees under eighteen years of age on December 31st of each year. The total rest period shall not exceed thirty days, twenty-four of which shall be working days.

The rest period is also increased by one and a half days per month of actual work for employees aged between eighteen and twenty years on December 31st of each year.  The total rest period shall not exceed twenty-two days, eighteen of which shall be working days.

The duration of continuous work is determined in accordance with the provisions of Article 114 of this Code.
