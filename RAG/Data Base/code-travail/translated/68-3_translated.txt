The application of the provisions of Article 68-2 of this Code is prohibited for working women for a period of at least sixteen weeks, eight weeks before and eight weeks after childbirth. This prohibition may be lifted by permission from the head of the territorially competent Labour Inspectorate, after obtaining the opinion of the Labour Medical Inspectorate. This permission is granted upon a written request from the working woman concerned, provided that it does not endanger her health or the health of her child. The prohibition stipulated in the first paragraph of this Article applies during other periods for which medical certificates are presented showing that the prohibition during these periods is necessary for the health of the mother and child. These periods may be during pregnancy or during a specific period extending the post-partum period stipulated in the first paragraph of this Article.
