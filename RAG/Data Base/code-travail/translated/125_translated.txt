The annual paid leave may be divided into periods of one or more days, up to one-half of its full duration.

The days considered as working days for the purpose of enjoying annual paid leave are those normally scheduled for work at the establishment, even if they are days of total or partial temporary unemployment due to slack periods or unforeseen circumstances, excluding weekly rest days and public holidays as stipulated in Articles 107 to 111.  Days of sick leave and maternity leave cannot be deducted from the annual leave.

The compulsory period spent in military training shall also be excluded.
