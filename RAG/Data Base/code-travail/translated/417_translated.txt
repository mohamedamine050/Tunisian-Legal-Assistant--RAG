Each lessor is authorized to be relieved of this joint and several liability by granting authorization to a traveling representative, agent, or promoter to execute an insurance policy guaranteeing the payment of wages and fines stipulated by the aforementioned Law dated December 11, 1957.

In this context, the insurance premiums related to the aforementioned policy shall be reimbursed by each lessor to the traveling representative or paid directly, proportionally to the amount of wages paid to the worker, in relation to the total wages declared by the insurance beneficiary.
