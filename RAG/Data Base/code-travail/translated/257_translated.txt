Violations of the provisions of this chapter shall be pursued against the founders, directors, or managers of trade unions, and shall be punishable by a fine ranging from 30 to 300 dinars. In the event of recidivism, the offender shall be punished by a fine ranging from 60 to 600 dinars and imprisonment for a period of 6 days to one year, or by one of these penalties.

If false statements are made concerning the statutes, names, status, or nationality of the managers or directors, the maximum fine shall be increased to 600 dinars, and Article 53 of the Penal Code may be applied in this instance.
