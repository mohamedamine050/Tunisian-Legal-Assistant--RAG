Every lessor intending to dismiss or suspend some or all of its employees for economic or technical reasons must notify the territorially competent labor inspectorate in advance.

This notification must include the following information:

- The name and full address of the establishment, the name of the person responsible for it, the start date of its activity and its nature,

- The reasons that led to the request for dismissal or suspension from work.

The notification must also be accompanied by the necessary supporting documents for the request for dismissal or suspension from work and a list of all the establishment's employees, stating their civil status, date of recruitment, and professional specialization.

Likewise, the employees concerned by the dismissal or suspension from work must be listed.
