The Central Committee for Monitoring Dismissals shall be chaired by the Director General of the Labour Inspectorate.  In addition, it shall include:

- A representative of the most representative central trade union of the workers concerned, as a member;
- A representative of the central professional organization of employers to which the employer concerned belongs, as a member.

When it concerns a public establishment, the representative of the employers' professional organization shall be replaced by a representative of the ministry supervising the establishment.

The Committee may, at the request of its chairman, summon any person whose presence it deems useful.

The General Directorate of the Labour Inspectorate shall act as the Committee's secretariat.
