The following categories of establishments are legally authorized to grant weekly rest on a rotational basis:

1. Establishments manufacturing fast-moving consumer food products.

2. Hotels, restaurants, and beverage establishments.

3. Tobacco shops and natural flower shops.

4. Hospitals, clinics, shelters, nursing homes, dispensaries, healthcare centers, pharmacies, drugstores, and shops selling medical and surgical instruments.

5. Baths and hydrotherapy establishments.

6. Book rental shops, chair rental businesses, and transportation services.

7. Press and news projects, entertainment venues, museums, and exhibitions.

8. Lighting projects, water distribution, and power generation for machinery.

9. Transportation, shipping, and unloading projects.

10. Factories using perishable materials.

11. Factories where work stoppage would result in damage to the material being manufactured or a decrease in its value.
