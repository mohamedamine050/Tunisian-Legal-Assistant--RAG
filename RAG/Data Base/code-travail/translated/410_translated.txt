The above-mentioned contracts must be executed in writing.

They are concluded, at the contracting parties' discretion, either for a fixed term or an indefinite term. In the latter case, the contract must stipulate that the notice period for termination shall be at least equal to the term specified in joint employment contracts; otherwise, it shall be determined in accordance with custom, provided that it shall never be less than one month in the first year of the agreement, nor less than two months in the second year, nor less than three months after two years.

The notice period for termination shall be extended for travelling agents and representatives working outside Tunisian territory by the normal duration of their return journey, should the termination of their contract necessitate their return to Tunisia.

A probationary period not exceeding three months may be stipulated.
