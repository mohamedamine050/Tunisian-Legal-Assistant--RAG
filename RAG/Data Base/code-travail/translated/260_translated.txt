Pursuant to implementing orders issued upon the proposal of the Secretary of State for Youth, Sports and Social Affairs, after obtaining the opinion of the Secretary of State for Foreign Affairs, special regulations may be imposed regarding the use of foreign labor.  These orders may stipulate, specifically for the entire territory of the Republic or part thereof, or for certain professional categories, or for some companies as a whole or some of the concerned establishments, the percentage of foreign labor that may be used, as well as the deadlines given to employers to adjust their situation.
