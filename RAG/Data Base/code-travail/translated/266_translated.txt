Foreign currency transactions used without legal basis shall be stopped immediately upon observation of the violation, at the request of the officers specified in Article 270 of this Code.  Should the lessor concerned fail to implement the aforementioned order to cease operations, he shall be subject to the penalties stipulated in Article 265, doubled. The worker who continues to work despite an order to cease operations from one of the officers mentioned in Article 270 shall be punished by imprisonment for a period ranging from one to fifteen days and a fine ranging from 120 to 300 Dinars, or by one of these penalties only.
