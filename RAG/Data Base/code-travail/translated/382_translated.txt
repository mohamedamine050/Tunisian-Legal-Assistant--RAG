The dispute file shall be submitted to arbitration, as the case may be, by the Head of the Central Conciliation Office; and in case of his inability, by the General Manager of the Labour Inspectorate or the Head of the Regional Conciliation Office; and in case of their inability, by the Head of the territorially competent Labour Inspectorate. This shall be done within a maximum period of forty-eight hours from the date of the agreement to arbitrate. The file shall include the arbitration agreement and all documents related to the dispute.
