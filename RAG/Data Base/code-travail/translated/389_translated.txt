The institution or its funds may be commandeered by order of the court if a strike is decided upon, commenced, or threatened, and if it is likely to disrupt the normal operation of an essential service.

Notification shall be effected by the signature of the defendant or their agent in the presence of the relevant official, on a notification document or on the writ of summons, for attendance at the appointed time.  Commandeering shall be notified to those concerned individually to their last registered address with the institution, by judicial officers.

If the commandeering encompasses an institution or all its members, notification may be effected by posting at the institution concerned or through the media.
