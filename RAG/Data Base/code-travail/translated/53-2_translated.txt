The provisions of Article 53 of this Code shall not apply to child labor in public, vocational, or technical schools, or in other training institutions.  Furthermore, they shall not apply to work performed in institutions by persons at least fourteen years of age if such work constitutes an essential part of:

a. A training or educational course whose primary responsibility lies with a school or training institution;

b. A vocational training program approved by the competent public authorities, the majority or entirety of which is carried out in an establishment;

c. A guidance or orientation program aimed at choosing a profession or type of training.
