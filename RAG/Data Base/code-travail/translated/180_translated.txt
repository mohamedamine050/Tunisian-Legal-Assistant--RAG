These officials shall, in addition, verify the conditions contained in contracts concluded by the State and public entities concerning employment relations and the use of manpower.

They must submit detailed reports every three months on the application of the provisions they are tasked with implementing.

These reports must specifically mention serious incidents suffered by workers, stating their causes.  Likewise, the true causes of collective and individual labor disputes and all elements that can contribute to regional or national economic development, and to the development of social labor relations and raising the standard of living of the population, must be stated.
