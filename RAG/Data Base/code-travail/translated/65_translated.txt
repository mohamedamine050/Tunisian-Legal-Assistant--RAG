Children under the age of fourteen years may not be employed for a period exceeding fourteen consecutive hours, which includes the period between 8:00 PM and 8:00 AM.  Given local conditions or circumstances, the Secretary of State for Youth, Sports, and Social Affairs may, after consultation with the relevant trade unions, issue decisions substituting this time period with another twelve-hour period, which may not begin after 8:00 PM and end before 6:00 AM.
