Any offense described as a felony under this law means crimes punishable by death or imprisonment exceeding five years.  Offenses are described as misdemeanors if punishable by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars. Offenses are described as infractions if punishable by imprisonment not exceeding fifteen days or a fine not exceeding sixty dinars.  Pursuant to Articles 346, 347, 348, and 360, and the provisions of Articles 359, 361, and 362 adopted for their application, penalties shall be imposed in accordance with Articles 234, 236, 237, and 239 of the Labour Code.
