Specialized personnel from the ministries responsible for industry, public health, the environment, and land use planning, as well as labor inspectors and labor medical inspectors, shall monitor hazardous, unhealthy, or nuisance establishments, whether or not they are established.

The owners, managers, or agents of the aforementioned establishments shall, at all times, enable the aforementioned personnel to conduct their inspections, or following their legal summons, to carry out the necessary inspections, or to examine, upon request, the permits related to the establishments or the documents serving as substitutes thereof.
