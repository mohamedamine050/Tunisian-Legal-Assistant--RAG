Punishment is prescribed for:

Felonies, as defined by this law, are crimes that warrant punishment by death or imprisonment exceeding five years.

Misdemeanors are crimes that warrant punishment by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or a fine exceeding sixty dinars.

Offenses are crimes warranting punishment not exceeding fifteen days' imprisonment or sixty dinars fine.  Article 122 of the Code of Criminal Procedure.

The provisions of Article 333, in accordance with Articles 234, 236, and 237 of this Code, shall apply. The court shall set deadlines for the convicted person to deliver to the workers the work and protective clothing to which they are entitled.
