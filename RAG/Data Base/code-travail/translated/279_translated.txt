Notification must be made within one month:

a. From the commencement of actual activity for a newly formed entity;

b. From the occurrence of a partial or total change in the entity's activity or the transfer of its registered office;

c. From the change in the legal status of the lessor, specifically due to inheritance, sale, lease, merger, transfer of assets, or conversion to a company;

d. From the replacement of the entity's manager or agent.

Notification must also be made one month prior to the date of cessation of the entity's activity.
