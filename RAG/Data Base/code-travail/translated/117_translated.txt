Annual leave shall be granted between June 1st and October 31st of each year.  It may be granted at another time of year by collective or individual agreements, or by the lessor if the necessity of work requires it, after obtaining the opinion of the institution's advisory committee or employee representatives. The lessor shall regulate the entry into leave periods as necessary, after consultation with the institution's advisory committee or employee representatives, taking into account the needs of the work and the situation of the beneficiary of the leave, including their family situation and seniority.  Notification must be given at least fifteen days before implementation.
