Any offense described as a felony under this law means crimes punishable by death or imprisonment exceeding five years.  Misdemeanors are defined as crimes punishable by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars.  Offenses are defined as crimes punishable by imprisonment not exceeding fifteen days or a fine of sixty dinars. Article 122 of the Code of Criminal Procedure.  Violations of the provisions of this chapter or the texts adopted for its application shall be punished by a fine ranging from 24 to 60 dinars and imprisonment from one to three days, or by one of these penalties only.

In the case of recidivism, the offender shall be punished by a fine ranging from 48 to 120 dinars and imprisonment from sixteen days to two months, or by one of these penalties only.
