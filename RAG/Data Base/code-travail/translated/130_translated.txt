To ensure monitoring of the application of the provisions of this chapter, landlords must maintain a register bearing their name and address, specifying:

1. The standard period for granting leave in the establishment.

2. The date of commencement of employment for each worker.

3. The duration of the annual leave for those entitled.

4. The date of commencement of the leave.

5. The amount of the allowance paid to each of them for the duration of their leave, specifying the elements considered in calculating this allowance.

This register shall be signed by those entitled to leave and shall be made available to labor inspectors upon request.
