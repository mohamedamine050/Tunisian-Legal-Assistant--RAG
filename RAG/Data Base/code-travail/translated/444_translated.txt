The list of names of those awarded the medal will be published in the Official Gazette of the Tunisian Republic.

Those awarded the Order of Labour will receive a certificate and a medal for each grade.

The certificate, signed by the Secretary of State to the Prime Minister in charge of Social Affairs and Housing, will specify the grade to which those awarded the Order of Labour are promoted, as well as the date of promotion.

This medal is awarded free of charge to the awarded workers and will correspond to their grade.  The costs of this medal will be borne either by the awarding authority or by the employer if the award was not conferred by an official authority.
