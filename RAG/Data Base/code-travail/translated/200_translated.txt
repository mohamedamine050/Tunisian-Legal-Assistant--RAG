Sessions

Public sessions shall be held by the competent bodies, in accordance with the procedural rules set forth in this guide, with the aim of obtaining specific information concerning the work or activities carried out by the public administration, to ensure the soundness, integrity, transparency, and compliance with the requirements of the public interest.

The Labour Court or its branch shall hold public sessions. If the pleadings are likely to affect public order or good morals, the presiding judge may declare the pleadings confidential.

The pronouncement of the judgment must always take place in a public session.
