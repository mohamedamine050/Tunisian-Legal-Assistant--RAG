Medical inspectors are specifically tasked with the following:

1. Ensuring the application of legislation related to occupational health and safety, in coordination with labor inspectors.

2. Providing landlords and workers with technical advice and guidance on the most effective means of implementing legislation related to occupational health and safety, and informing the competent authorities of any shortcomings or violations observed in this area.

3. Collecting and utilizing statistics to improve the protection of workers' health and safety.

4. Supervising occupational health services and facilities and approving the premises allocated to them.

5. Contributing to the preparation of a set of cards to record occupational diseases affecting the workforce.

6. Ruling on disputes related to workers' medical examinations.

7. Monitoring the treatment provided to those injured in workplace accidents and occupational diseases.
