Vocational training includes:

Firstly: For young men and women, and exceptionally for adults, in training.

Secondly: For young workers and adults, in:

a.  Professional upgrading,

b.  Professional retraining,

c.  Rapid vocational training.


The training includes general training containing the acquisition of essential elements of general education and the acquisition of theoretical and practical professional skills.

Professional upgrading aims to enable each worker to advance in the professional hierarchy by acquiring essential elements of general and technical culture.

Professional retraining aims to employ workers who have become unemployed due to a shortage in their original profession or who have become unable to perform this profession.

Rapid vocational training aims to meet the unusual needs for manpower in certain professions or industrial fields.
