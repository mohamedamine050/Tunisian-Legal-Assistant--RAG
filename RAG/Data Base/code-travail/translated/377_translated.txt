The Regional Conciliation Office shall handle the matter; in case of impossibility, the territorially competent Labor Inspectorate shall handle it after gathering the data and shall present the subject of the dispute to the Regional Conciliation Committee.

This Committee shall be chaired by the Governor of the region or his representative, assisted by the head of the Regional Conciliation Office; in case of impossibility, the head of the territorially competent Labor Inspectorate shall assist.  The Committee shall further include:

- Two representatives of the relevant workers' unions, one of whom shall represent the central trade union organization.

- Two representatives of the employers or the relevant employers' unions, one of whom shall represent the central trade union organization.

If the dispute concerns a public establishment, the representative of the central trade union organization of employers shall be replaced by a representative of the ministry supervising the establishment.
