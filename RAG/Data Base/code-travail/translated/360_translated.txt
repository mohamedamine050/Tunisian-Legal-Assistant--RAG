It is prohibited:

1. To employ trainees who do not hold a training contract approved by the competent authority of the Ministry of Youth, Sports and Social Affairs.

2. To issue a training contract for a type of activity other than that specified in the officially published gazette, in accordance with the provisions of Article 359.

3. To employ a number of trainees exceeding the maximum percentage specified by the decision stipulated in Article 359.

The competent authority of the Ministry of Youth, Sports and Social Affairs may temporarily, and pending the inclusion of the aforementioned list, refuse any employment contract issued for a profession that, according to custom, does not involve regular and comprehensive training.  This decision may be appealed to the Ministry of Youth, Sports and Social Affairs.
