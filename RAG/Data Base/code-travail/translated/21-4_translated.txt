The regional committee for monitoring dismissals shall be chaired by the head of the Labour Inspectorate.  Furthermore:

- A representative of the most representative trade union of the workers concerned shall be a member;
- A representative of the employers' professional organisation to which the employer concerned belongs shall be a member.

When it concerns a public establishment, the representative of the employers' professional organisation shall be replaced by a representative of the ministry overseeing the establishment.

The committee may, at the request of its chairman, summon any person whose presence it deems useful.

The territorially competent Labour Inspectorate shall act as the committee's secretary.
