Non-agricultural establishments of all types and their subsidiaries are obligated to grant their employees a weekly rest period of twenty-four consecutive hours, except in the exceptional cases stipulated in the following articles. This rest period shall be granted on Friday, Saturday, or Sunday. It may be assigned on another day of the week by agreement of both parties in the establishment or by permission from the Governor of the region if the necessity of work so requires. Each establishment must inform the territorially competent labor inspectorate of the day chosen.
