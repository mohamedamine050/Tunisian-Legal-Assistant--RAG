Priority in recruitment to fill vacant or newly created full-time positions shall be given to part-time employees wishing to transition to a full-time work system, who possess the same specialization and required qualifications.  Similarly, priority in recruitment to fill vacant or newly created part-time positions shall be given to full-time employees wishing to transition to a part-time work system, who possess the same specialization and required qualifications.  An employee's transition from a full-time to a part-time work system, or vice versa, can only occur with their written consent.
