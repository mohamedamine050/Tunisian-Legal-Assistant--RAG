Occupational Medicine Services

Occupational medicine services, whether private or in the form of associations, primarily play a preventative role in occupational health.  They are specifically tasked with examining and monitoring the health and physical fitness of workers to perform the required tasks, both at recruitment and during employment, as well as protecting them from hazards that may affect their health due to their profession.  They are legal entities of public interest, possessing legal personality and financial independence, and are subject to the provisions of this chapter. These associations include the institutions referred to in Article 153 of this Code and falling within their scope of activity.
