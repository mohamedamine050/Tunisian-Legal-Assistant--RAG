The legal working hours in agricultural institutions are set at two thousand and seven hundred hours per year, considering three hundred days of actual work.  The daily distribution of these hours shall be determined according to the share, the region, and where necessary, according to the type of agriculture, by decisions taken jointly by the Secretary of State for Youth, Sports and Social Affairs and the Secretary of State for Planning and National Economy, after consultation with the agricultural labor committees. The provisions of the first paragraph shall not apply to guards and shepherds.  The duration of their work shall be determined by a decision of the Secretary of State for Youth, Sports and Social Affairs, in cooperation with the Secretary of State for Planning and National Economy, and after consultation with the relevant trade unions.
