Any person found to have knowingly employed, as trainees, workers, or young employees, individuals under the age of 18 who have not fulfilled the obligations of their training contract or have not legally severed their ties with a previous employer shall be fined, the amount of which shall be determined, payable to the head of the establishment or workshop from which the worker left. Any training contract entered into without fulfilling the obligations stipulated in the previous contract, or without the previous contract having been cancelled or terminated for the reasons and in the manner referred to in Articles 350 and 351, shall be considered legally void.
