The joint agreement set forth in the preceding article must be concluded between the trade union organizations of employers and workers that best represent the branches of activity concerned in the region where it will be applied.  Its provisions shall be binding upon all employers and workers of the professions covered by its scope from the date of its registration upon request of the party most diligent in obtaining the written approval of the Ministry of Youth, Sports and Social Affairs. The latter shall issue a decision accepting or rejecting, with reasons given, without the power to modify the text of the agreement submitted to it.  Approval may only be refused after obtaining a reasoned opinion from the committee referred to in the preceding article. In the event of refusal to approve the agreement, it cannot be implemented even between the contracting parties.
