The Labor Courts shall consider all claims of opposition or counterclaims that fall within their jurisdiction by nature, as well as claims for damages.

[1] Harm caused in violation of the law affecting a person's body, mental or psychological health, honor, property, or reputation. [2] Loss or damage to property, deprivation of comfort, or harm to physical well-being, reputation, or the like. - In crimes and quasi-crimes, damages are the losses actually suffered by the claimant and the necessary expenses that he has been or will be compelled to incur to repair the consequences of the act committed to his detriment, as well as what he has been deprived of within the normal limits of the consequences of this act.

The professional [courts/tribunals] shall rule on the initial claim or the claim of opposition.
