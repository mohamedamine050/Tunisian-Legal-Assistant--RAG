1. The findings shall include the results of the monitoring conducted in accordance with the conditions stipulated in Article 62 of this Code, and generally, the results of the investigation and interrogation conducted by customs officers in inspection reports.

2. The inspection reports must state the following details:

- The date and place of the monitoring and investigations conducted.
- The nature of the inspections carried out and the information obtained.
- The seizure of documents, where applicable.
- The identity, capacity, and administrative residence of the officers who drew up the report.
- Notification of the persons subject to monitoring and investigation of the date and place of drawing up this report and that they were summoned to attend.
- Reading of the report to those present and requesting them to sign it; in the event of their refusal to sign, this shall be stated in the inspection report.
