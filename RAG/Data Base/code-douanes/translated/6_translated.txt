1. By order, after obtaining the opinion of the Minister of Finance and the relevant ministers, it is possible to suspend the application of customs duties, including the legal minimum for collection, or to reduce them, or to reinstate them, wholly or partially. However, these measures shall only be effective during the year in which they were taken.

2. For the purpose of protecting domestic products, it is possible to increase the rates of customs duties by order, in accordance with the legislation in force in the field of safeguard measures upon import, taking into account the international agreements in force.

3. For the purpose of protecting domestic industry, it is possible to increase the rates of customs duties or to reinstate them, wholly or partially, by order, in accordance with the provisions of the international agreements in force concluded by the Tunisian State concerning the possibility of enacting exceptional safeguard measures.
