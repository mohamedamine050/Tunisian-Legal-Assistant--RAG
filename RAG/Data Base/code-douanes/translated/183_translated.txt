1. Goods may, throughout their stay in customs warehouses, be subject to simple operations for the purpose of securing their preservation, improving their presentation, enhancing their commercial quality, or preparing them for promotion or resale.

2. The simple operations outlined in paragraph 1 above may only be carried out after obtaining prior authorization from the customs authorities, which shall specify the conditions for their execution.

3.  Customs authorities may, where economically necessary and ensuring that customs control requirements are not compromised, exceptionally authorize the carrying out of processing operations within customs warehouses under the effective processing system, according to the conditions of that system.
