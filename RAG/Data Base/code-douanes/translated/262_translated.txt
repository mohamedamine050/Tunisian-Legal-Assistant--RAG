1. Customs authorities shall determine the time limit within which exported goods must be re-imported or allocated another customs destination. This time limit shall be sufficient to achieve the permitted uses.

2.  In justified cases, and at the request of the interested party, customs authorities may extend the aforementioned time limit within reasonable limits, in order to allow the authorized use, provided that these extensions do not exceed three years from the date of registration of the customs declaration relating to the temporary export.

3. However, in the case of temporary export of equipment and facilities for the purpose of carrying out work abroad, customs authorities may authorize an extension of the time limit specified in paragraph 2 of this article, based on the deadlines stipulated in the contract terms.
