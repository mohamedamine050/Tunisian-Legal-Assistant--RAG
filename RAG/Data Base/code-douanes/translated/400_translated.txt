In addition to the original penalties stipulated in this Code, the following shall be confiscated:

1. Goods that have been substituted for others or that were intended to substitute them, in the cases stipulated in sub-paragraph (a) of paragraph 2 of Article 382, sub-paragraph (d) of paragraph 2 of Article 390, and paragraph 2 of Article 394 of this Code.

2. Goods previously displayed upon departure, in the case stipulated in paragraph 1 of Article 395 of this Code.

3. Means of transport when the driver fails to comply with the instructions of customs officers, in the case stipulated in Article 57 of this Code.
