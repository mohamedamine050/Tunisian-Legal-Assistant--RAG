In the event of application of the provisions of paragraphs (1) and (2) of Article 190 of this Code, then:

- The duties and charges required to be applied are those in effect on the date of registration of the detailed declaration of status for consumption.

- The value to be considered for the application of the required duties and charges is the value of the goods on the date of registration of the declaration of status for consumption and according to their condition on that date and according to the conditions stipulated in Articles 22 to 36 of this Code.
