1- Any person possessing or transporting goods specifically identified by a decision of the Minister of Finance must, upon the first request from customs officers, police officers, National Guard officers, economic control officers, or tax control officers, produce either receipts proving the legal importation of said goods, or purchase invoices, or manufacturing documents, or any other document issued by legally established persons or companies within the customs territory, attesting to the origin of those goods.


2-  Furthermore, anyone who possessed, transported, sold, transferred, or exchanged the aforementioned goods, as well as anyone who provided a document to prove their origin, must present the documents referred to in the first paragraph above, upon any request from customs officers, within a period of three years commencing either from the date of submission of the origin-related evidence or from the date the goods ceased to be in their possession.
