The license is granted to:

A- Persons residing in Tunisia.

B- In cases where it is possible to prove that the imported compensatory products originate from the processing of temporarily exported goods.

The cases in which exceptions to point (B) apply and the methods of applying the aforementioned exceptions shall be determined by a decision of the Minister of Finance.

C- In cases where the granting of the ineffective transformation system does not result in harm to the essential interests of local manufacturers.
