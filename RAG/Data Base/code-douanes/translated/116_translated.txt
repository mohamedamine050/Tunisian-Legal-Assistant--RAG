For the application of the provisions of this Code, particularly regarding duties, taxes, seizures, and other measures, declarations lodged before the arrival of goods shall not acquire their actual effect, including the consequences resulting from their registration, until the date on which the arrival of the goods is established in accordance with the conditions and deadlines stipulated in paragraph 3 of Article 100 above, provided that the said declarations meet the required conditions on that date in accordance with the provisions of Article 111 of this Code.
