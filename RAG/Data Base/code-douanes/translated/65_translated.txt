1. Within the meaning of this Code, "manifest" means the cargo list in accordance with the provisions of the Maritime Commerce Code. 2. Goods arriving by sea must be recorded in a manifest. 3. This document must be signed by the ship's master and should specify the type and number of packages, their marks and numbers, the type of goods, and the places from which they were shipped. 4. The manifest registration shall encompass several closed and grouped packages, regardless of how they are assembled, as a single unit. 5. Seized goods and goods subject to restrictions must be recorded in the manifest under their true designation, according to their type and class.
