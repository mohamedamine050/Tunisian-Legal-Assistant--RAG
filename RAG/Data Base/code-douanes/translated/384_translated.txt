1. A fine ranging from one to three times the value of the goods in dispute shall be imposed for each offense described as a misdemeanor under this law.  Crimes punishable by death or imprisonment exceeding five years are described as felonies. Crimes punishable by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars, are described as misdemeanors.  Crimes punishable by imprisonment not exceeding fifteen days or a fine not exceeding sixty dinars are described as infractions.  This applies to violations of laws and regulations entrusted to the customs administration for enforcement, if the violation concerns goods of a type prohibited upon entry or exit for which this code does not prescribe specific penalties. (Article 122, Code of Criminal Procedure)


2. The same fine shall be imposed for offenses stipulated in the second paragraph of Article 382 of this code, if they relate to goods of a type prohibited upon entry or exit.


3. The same fine shall be imposed for each purchase or possession, even outside the customs area, of goods imported by smuggling or without declaration in quantities exceeding family consumption needs.
