1. The customs department shall dispose of the confiscated goods, pursuant to a court order,  in accordance with the procedures specified by order,  or goods surrendered by virtue of an approved settlement.

2. Judgments and authorizations ordering the confiscation of goods seized from unknown persons who have abandoned them and have not claimed them shall not be executed until one month after the date of posting of these judgments and authorizations, in accordance with the provisions of Article 305, paragraph 3, of this Code. No request for return shall be accepted after the expiry of the aforementioned period.
