1. The entry of foreign goods into, and exit from, logistics activity zones shall be subject to a summary declaration, unless otherwise provided.

The form of the summary declaration and the documents that may substitute it shall be determined by a decision of the Minister of Finance.

2. (a) Goods originating from the customs territory, upon entry into logistics activity zones, shall be subject to a detailed declaration and the completion of customs procedures, regardless of their previous customs status.

(b) The cases in which the detailed declaration is replaced by a substitute document shall be determined by a decision of the Minister of Finance.
