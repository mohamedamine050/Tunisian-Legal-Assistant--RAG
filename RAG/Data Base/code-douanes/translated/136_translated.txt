1- Aircraft departing Tunisian customs territory may only take off from airports with customs offices.

2- The aircraft commander or his legally authorized representative must:

a- Submit to customs authorities a cargo manifest of goods shipped from the aforementioned airport for their indelible endorsement.

b- Deliver a copy of this manifest to the customs authorities.

3- The provisions referred to in the first paragraph of Article 75, Article 76, the first paragraph of Article 77, and Article 78 of this Code shall apply to aircraft and their cargo.
