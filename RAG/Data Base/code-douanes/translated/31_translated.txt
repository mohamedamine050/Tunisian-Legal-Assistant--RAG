The customs value shall not include the following elements, provided they are separate from the price:

The price:  What the contracting parties agreed upon, whether it exceeds or falls short of the value of the goods.

Amounts actually paid or payable on the imported goods: A - Transportation costs of the goods after their arrival at the point of entry into Tunisian territory. B - Expenses related to construction, assembly, installation, maintenance, and technical assistance performed on the goods after their importation. C - Expenses related to the right to reproduce the imported goods in Tunisia. D - Purchasing commissions. E - Duties and taxes upon importation into Tunisia.
