1. Goods for which the obligations have not been fulfilled are subject to the payment of duties and taxes in effect on the date of registration of the documents or instruments serving as substitutes.  Where applicable, penalties due shall be determined according to those duties and taxes, or according to the value of the goods in the local market on the same date.

2. If the goods referred to in the first paragraph of this section are damaged, and this damage is due to force majeure legally proven, the customs authorities may exempt the original contractor and their guarantor from paying the duties and taxes.
