Punishment shall be imprisonment for a term ranging from six months to three years, and confiscation of the goods, the things used to conceal the fraud, and a fine ranging from three to four times the value of the smuggled goods.

1. Perpetrators of smuggling offenses exceeding six persons, whether all or some of them carry the smuggled goods.

2. Perpetrators of smuggling offenses using an aircraft, motor vehicle, vessel, or boat with a net tonnage of less than 100 cubic meters, or any other means of transport intended for the carriage of goods or persons.
