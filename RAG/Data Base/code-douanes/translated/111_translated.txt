1- Detailed declarations must be submitted in writing or using media or electronic means,

in accordance with the model established by the legislation and regulations in force.

2- These declarations must include all the data necessary for the application of customs measures

and the preparation of statistics.

3- They must be signed by the declarant.

4- The detailed declaration and the accompanying documents constitute a single, indivisible document.

5- The Minister of Finance shall determine, by decision, the form of the declarations and the data they must contain,

as well as the documents that must be attached to them.  He may, in certain cases, authorize the replacement of a written declaration with an oral declaration.
