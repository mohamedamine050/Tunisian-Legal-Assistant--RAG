1- Judgments issued against several persons for committing the same offense or misdemeanor,  before the Court of First Instance, shall be joint and several, whether concerning financial penalties in lieu of confiscation or concerning fines and costs.

2- These provisions shall not apply to the offenses stipulated in the first paragraph of Article 51 and the first paragraph of Article 57 of this Code, the perpetrators of which are punished with individual fines.
