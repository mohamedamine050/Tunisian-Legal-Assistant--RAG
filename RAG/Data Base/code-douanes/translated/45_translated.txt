Customs officers may exercise their powers within a contiguous zone extending from 12 to 24 nautical miles from the outer limit of the territorial waters, taking into account international agreements ratified by Tunisia to define borders with neighboring countries, in the following cases:  a) Preventing violations of the laws and regulations entrusted to the Customs Administration for enforcement within the customs territory. b) Pursuing perpetrators of violations of the laws and regulations entrusted to the Customs Administration for enforcement and committed within the customs territory. The methods of applying the provisions of this article shall be determined by order.
