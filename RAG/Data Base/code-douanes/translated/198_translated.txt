1- When the imported goods, on the date they are placed on the market, meet the conditions for benefiting from a preferential tariff treatment, these goods shall benefit from this preferential treatment granted to similar goods on the date of registration of the declaration of placing on the market.

2- Imported goods shall be accepted with full or partial exemption from the payment of duties and taxes upon importation when they meet the conditions for benefiting from one of the exemption systems provided for in the legislation in force on the date they are placed on the market.
