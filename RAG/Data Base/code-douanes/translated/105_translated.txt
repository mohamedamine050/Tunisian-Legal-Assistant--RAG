The accepted mediators at the Customs Administration shall elect a professional body called "The Body of Accepted Mediators at the Customs Administration," in which the Director General of Customs shall be represented.  This body shall have internal regulations subject to the approval of the Minister of Finance.

The Body of Accepted Mediators at the Customs Administration shall express its opinion regarding requests for obtaining or withdrawing a customs mediator license.  Furthermore, it may propose the withdrawal of a license. The body shall also undertake all activities related to the professional supervision of mediators and the organization of training courses for its members.
