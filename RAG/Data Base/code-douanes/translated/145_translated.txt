The Director General of Customs may authorize compensation for the full guarantee of duties and taxes due on goods placed or transported under a suspensive system or an economic customs system, in one of the following forms:

- A total annual financial guarantee covering several operations;

- A personal guarantee;

- Continuous customs control of the premises where the goods are located;

- Customs accompaniment of the means of transport under a transit system;

- Any other form that serves as a substitute for the financial guarantee and provides the same guarantees.
