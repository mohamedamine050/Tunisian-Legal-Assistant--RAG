The following operations shall be considered as undeclared supply of seized goods:

1. The unloading, using fraudulent means, of the items referred to in the second paragraph of Article 395 of this Code.

2. The registration of motor vehicles, motorcycles, or aircraft in their normal registers, without prior fulfillment of customs obligations.

3. The registration of seagoing vessels in the Tunisian register using fraudulent means, and any presence of seagoing vessels, regardless of their tonnage, in territorial waters or ports if the documents relating to the vessel or to the registration are incorrect, forged, or non-compliant.

4. The diversion of seized goods from the destination for which the authorization was granted.
