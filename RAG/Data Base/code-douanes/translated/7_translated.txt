1- Pursuant to an order, special identification measures may be taken against products from a state or group of states that treat Tunisian products with less favorable treatment than that afforded to products of other states, or that take measures that could hinder Tunisian exports, taking into account applicable bilateral or multilateral agreements.

2-  The measures taken pursuant to the provisions of the preceding paragraph shall be reviewed according to the same procedures concerning how those measures are taken and implemented, as appropriate.
