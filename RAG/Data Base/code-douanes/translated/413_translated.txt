1. The Conciliation and Administrative Examination Committee is composed of:

- A second-rank judicial judge, as President.
- A Counselor from the Administrative Court.
- Assistants appointed based on their technical expertise.


2. The Committee shall express its conclusions by a majority vote of its members; in case of a tie, the President's vote shall be decisive.


3. The appointment of the appropriate individual to the position that matches the requirements and qualifications for its performance shall be made by the judicial judge, President of the Conciliation and Administrative Examination Committee, and the Counselor from the Administrative Court and their deputies, by order.
