1- The operator or lessee shall:

a. Guarantee that the goods remain under customs control for the entire duration of their stay in the warehouse and not withdraw them without authorization from the customs authorities.

b. Fulfill the obligations arising from the storage of goods under the customs warehousing system.

c. Respect the specific conditions stipulated in the license.

2- The depositor shall in all cases be responsible for fulfilling the obligations arising from placing the goods under the customs warehousing system.
