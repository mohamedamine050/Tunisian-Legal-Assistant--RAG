1. Upon examination of the goods, and in the event that Customs authorities do not agree with the declaration statements relating to the type of goods, their origin, or their value, and if the declarant does not accept the Customs authorities' examinations, then the dispute shall be referred to the Conciliation and Customs Examination Committee referred to in Section Six, paragraph ten of this Code.

2. However, recourse to the aforementioned Committee shall not be made if the law provides for a special procedure for determining the type of goods, their origin, or their value.
