In the event of the commission of the offenses stipulated in the second paragraph of Article 395 and the first paragraph of Article 398 of this Code, confiscation may only be ordered with respect to the objects that are the subject of the fraud. However, confiscation of the goods used to conceal the fraud, as well as the means of transport used to unload and load those objects, shall occur if it is proven that the owner of the aforementioned means of transport participated with the perpetrators of the fraud.
