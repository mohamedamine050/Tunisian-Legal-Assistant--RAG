1- Customs officers may allow the release of goods progressively as they are inspected, and before the settlement and payment of the due duties and charges, in return for an undertaking from the claimants to pay, legally binding, obligating them to pay the surplus, together with the principal, within fifteen days.

2- The methods for distributing the percentage of the surplus between the accountant and the public treasury shall be determined by a decision of the Minister of Finance.
