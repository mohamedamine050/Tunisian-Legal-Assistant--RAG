1. Goods intended for export by sea or air shall be placed on board ships or aircraft upon completion of customs formalities.

2. Goods intended for export by land shall be directly dispatched via the designated route to the exterior, as determined in accordance with the provisions of Article 72 of this Code.

3.  A derogation from the provisions of paragraphs one and two above may be granted to allow the deposit of such goods in warehouses or export areas pending their shipment or dispatch abroad.
