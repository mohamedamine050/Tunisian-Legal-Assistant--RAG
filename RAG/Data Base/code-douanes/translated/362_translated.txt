1. The President of the First Instance Court may, immediately and upon request of the Customs Administration, authorize the public auction of goods and means of transport confiscated pursuant to a default judgment. This may occur after the expiry of a six (6) month period from the date of issuance of the aforementioned judgment.

2. The proceeds of the sale, after deduction of the expenses referred to in Article 271, paragraph 1, points (a) and (c) of this Code, shall be secured in the Deposit and Consignment Fund.
