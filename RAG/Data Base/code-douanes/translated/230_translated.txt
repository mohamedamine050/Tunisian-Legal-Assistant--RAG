1. Within the framework of the active processing system, it is possible to carry out a temporary export of all or some of the compensatory products or goods in their current state for the purpose of carrying out supplementary processing outside the customs territory, provided that prior authorization is obtained from the customs authorities, in accordance with the conditions of the passive processing system.

2. When goods that have been re-imported are placed into consumption, the applicable duties and taxes shall be calculated as follows:

- The duties and taxes applicable to the compensatory products or goods referred to in the first paragraph of this chapter, in accordance with the provisions of Chapters 224 and 225 of this Code.

- And the duties and taxes applicable to the added value for the products that have been imported after carrying out the passive processing outside the customs territory.
