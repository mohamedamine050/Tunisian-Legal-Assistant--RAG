1. The Customs authorities shall determine the percentage yield of the transfer operation, or the method of determining this percentage.

The percentage yield shall be determined according to the actual circumstances in which the transfer operation was or is to be carried out.

2. If it is impossible to determine the percentage yield in accordance with the provisions of the first paragraph of this section, the Customs authorities may seek the opinion of the technical departments of the relevant ministry for the purpose of determining this percentage.

3. By decision of the Minister of Finance, after obtaining the opinion of the minister responsible for the relevant sector, estimated sectoral yield percentages may be determined.
