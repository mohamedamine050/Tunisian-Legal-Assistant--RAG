1. The effective processing system is granted by customs authorities upon request from the interested party, in cases where this system contributes to promoting exports without prejudice to the essential interests of producers within Tunisia.

2. The license is granted subject to the following conditions:

a) The applicant must be a person residing in Tunisia.

b) He must possess the necessary equipment and facilities to carry out the intended processing operations, or he must prove that he has appointed another person who possesses this equipment and facilities.

3.  Taking into account the provisions of the last sub-paragraph of paragraph 2(a) of Article 218, the effective processing system is granted only if the customs authorities can:

- Identify the imported goods in the compensatory products.

- Or verify that the conditions relating to the equivalent goods are met, as stipulated in Article 219.
