1. Goods are considered seized for the application of the provisions of this Code, all goods whose import or export is prohibited under any title whatsoever, or those subject to restrictions or regulations relating to quality, packaging, or special requirements.

2. When import or export is subject to the presentation of a license or certificate, the goods are considered seized if they are not accompanied by a valid document or if they are presented under a document that does not apply to them.

3. Under no circumstances can the documents relating to licensing for import or export be subject to loan or transfer, and generally, no commercial transaction can be carried out concerning them by their nominal holders.
