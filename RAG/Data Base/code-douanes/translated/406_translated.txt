If the amounts of duties and taxes actually due cannot be determined, or if the true value of the goods in dispute cannot be determined, particularly in cases of violations stipulated in paragraph 2(a) of Article 382, paragraph (t) of Article 390, paragraph [number omitted] of Article 394, and paragraph 1 of Article 397 of this Code, then the penalties shall be calculated on the basis of the highest applicable tariff for goods of the same nature, using the average value shown in the customs statistics for the last three months.
