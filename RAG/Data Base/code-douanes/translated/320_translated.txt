If the perpetrator of a felony,  meaning,  according to this law, crimes punishable by death or imprisonment exceeding five years, or a misdemeanor, meaning crimes punishable by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars, or a violation, meaning crimes punishable by imprisonment not exceeding fifteen days or a fine of sixty dinars, dies before the issuance of a final judgment or the occurrence of a settlement, the Customs Administration has the right to bring an action against the heirs, within the limits of inheritance, before the competent court, to seize the forfeitable objects or to obtain an amount equal to their value, determined according to the current market price at the time the violation was committed, if the seizure of the objects in question proves impossible.
