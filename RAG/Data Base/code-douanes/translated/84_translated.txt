1. The maximum period for goods to remain in customs clearance warehouses and areas, and export warehouses and areas, shall be determined by a decision of the Minister of Finance.

2. Upon expiry of the period specified in the first paragraph of this Article, and in the event that a detailed declaration for the assignment of a final customs regime to the goods is not submitted, the operator must direct these goods to the premises of the public warehouse or other premises designated for customs warehousing, where they shall be placed under deposit.
