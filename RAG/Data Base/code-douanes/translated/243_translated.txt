1- Subject to the specific provisions applicable to the system of barter as stipulated in Articles 252 to 259 of this Code, the ineffective transfer system allows for the temporary export outside the customs territory of Tunisian goods or goods that have been Tunisised for the purpose of carrying out processing operations on them, and the placing of the products obtained following these operations for consumption, with total or partial exemption from the applicable duties and taxes upon importation.

2- The temporary export of Tunisian goods or goods that have been Tunisised results in securing the duties and taxes payable upon export.
