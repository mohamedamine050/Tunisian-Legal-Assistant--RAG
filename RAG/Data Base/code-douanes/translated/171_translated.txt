Subject to the provisions of Article 172 of this Code, the following goods are accepted in customs warehouses, according to the conditions stipulated in this chapter:

- Goods subject, upon import, to customs duties, taxes, seizures, or other economic, fiscal, or customs measures.

- Goods originating from the domestic market and intended for export.

The methods and procedures by which these goods may benefit from the privileges granted upon export shall be determined by a decision of the Minister of Finance.

Goods previously placed under a suspensive system or another economic customs system may be accepted in customs warehouses within the framework of settling this system, pending their export or assignment to any other acceptable customs destination.
