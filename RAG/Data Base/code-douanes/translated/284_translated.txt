The list of goods subject to customs control is established by order, and other goods may be added to or deleted from this list according to the evolution of smuggling trends and economic necessities.  This list may differ, if necessary, between different parts of the region.  Furthermore, if the interests of border residents so require, temporary flexibility may be introduced to the legislation stipulated by this code, or its application may be temporarily suspended in the whole region or part thereof, by order taken upon the proposal of the Minister of Finance and after obtaining the opinion of the relevant ministers.
