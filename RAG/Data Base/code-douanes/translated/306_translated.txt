1- If the reason for the seizure is the falsification or fabrication of documents necessary for the transport of goods, this shall be stated and clarified in the seizure report.

2- The seizing officers shall:

- Sign and seal the aforementioned documents in a manner that prevents their alteration.

- Add these documents to the seizure report, which shall include a statement that the suspected party (suspicion here refers to the presumption of committing a crime before the charge is filed) was summoned to sign these documents and a record of their response.
