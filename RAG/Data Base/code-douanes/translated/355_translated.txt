If the seizure on goods subject to an offense is lifted, offenses described in this law as felonies are those which incur punishment by death or imprisonment exceeding five years.  Offenses described as misdemeanors are those which incur punishment by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars.  Offenses described as infractions are those which incur punishment not exceeding fifteen days imprisonment or sixty dinars fine. Article 122 of the Code of Criminal Procedure.  If a judicial decision, a written court order resolving a legal or factual issue,  appealed to the Court of Cassation, the seized goods shall not be released to the party benefiting from the decision except against sufficient security to guarantee their value.  It is absolutely impossible to rule on the release of goods whose entry has been seized.
