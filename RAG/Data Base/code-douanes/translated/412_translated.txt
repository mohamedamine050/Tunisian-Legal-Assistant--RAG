1. Except in the case where the Director General of Customs decides to settle the dispute, he must inform the declarant within one month of the date of submission of the application for review of the reasons upon which the administration based its assessment, inviting him to respond either by acceptance or by submitting a counter-memorandum within one month of the date of receipt of the notification.

2. If the disagreement persists, the Director General of Customs shall refer the matter to the Customs Conciliation and Review Committee by forwarding the case file to it within one month of the date of the response or of the expiry of the response deadlines mentioned above.

3. The declarant may directly refer the matter to the Customs Conciliation and Review Committee if he does not receive a response from the Director General of Customs to his reply, within one month of the date of his reply.
