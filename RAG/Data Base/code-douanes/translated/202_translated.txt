1. In the case of placing compensatory products for consumption, the applicable duties and charges shall be levied according to the type and condition of the goods at the time they are placed under the partial export processing system, and on the basis of the nature of these goods used in the manufacture of compensatory products when placed for consumption.

2. The rates of duties and charges applied shall be those in effect on the date of registration of the declaration of placement for consumption. The value to be declared for the levy of this charge shall be the value of the goods on the same date and according to the conditions set out in Articles 22 to 36 of this Code.
