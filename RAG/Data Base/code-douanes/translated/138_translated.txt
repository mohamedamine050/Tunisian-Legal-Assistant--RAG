1.  Customs and economic warehousing systems may store, process, use, or move goods while suspending the payment of customs duties and internal taxes due, as well as any other duty or tax to which these goods may be subject. 2.  Subject to the restrictions provided for in Article 172 of this Code and the exceptions granted by a decision of the Minister of Finance pursuant to Article 173 of this Code, customs and economic warehousing systems may suspend the application of restrictions, foreign trade procedures, and other economic measures upon import or export, unless other conflicting provisions exist.
