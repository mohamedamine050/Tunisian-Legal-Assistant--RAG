In the context of this Code, the following terms shall mean:

A- Goods intended for temporary export: Goods placed under the system of ineffective transformation.

B- Transformation operations: The operations referred to in Article 218, paragraph 2, points (a), (1), (2), and (3).

C- Replacement products: All products resulting from transformation operations.

D- Yield rate: The quantity or percentage of replacement products obtained following the transformation of a specific quantity of exported goods.
