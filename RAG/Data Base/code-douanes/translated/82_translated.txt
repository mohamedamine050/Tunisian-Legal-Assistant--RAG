1. Goods destined for customs may be deposited, in accordance with the conditions stipulated in Articles 65 to 81 of this Code, in customs clearance warehouses and areas and export warehouses and areas, according to the methods specified in this chapter, unless other contrary special provisions exist.

2. The establishment, operation, and management of customs clearance warehouses and areas and export warehouses and areas are subject to a set of conditions approved by a decision of the Minister of Finance, after obtaining the opinion of the Minister of Transport.

3. The aforementioned set of conditions referred to in paragraph (2) above shall specify, in particular, the construction and development standards and the working conditions in customs clearance warehouses and areas and export warehouses and areas; it shall also specify the costs to be borne by the operator relating to the provision, repair, and maintenance of the equipment necessary for the operation of customs.
