1- Subject to the provisions of Article 192 of this Code, the system of conversion for the domestic market may involve the conversion of goods in premises placed under customs supervision until the converted products are made available for consumption in the domestic market.

2- Upon making the converted products available for consumption, the duties and taxes due shall be collected based on the elements of taxation specific to them on the date of registration of the declaration of making available for consumption.

3- Continuous customs supervision of the premises may be replaced by a guarantee of the duties and taxes due upon import.
