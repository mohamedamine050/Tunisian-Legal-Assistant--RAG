1. The District Judge may, immediately and upon request of the Customs Administration, and without special procedures, authorize the sale by public auction of:

- Seized means of transport, regarding which the other party was offered the relinquishment thereof in return for providing security, and which offer was not accepted.

- Seized animals or goods which cannot be preserved without deterioration or damage.

2. The investigating judge in charge of the case may also authorize the sale and execute his decision regardless of any appeals.

3. The sale shall be conducted by the Customs Administration, and its proceeds shall be secured in the Deposit and Consignment Fund pending the issuance of the final judgment by the competent court.
