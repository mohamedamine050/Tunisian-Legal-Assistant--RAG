For the application of the provisions of this Code, "goods subject to high duties" shall mean goods on which the total duties, taxes, and various charges levied upon importation exceed 50% of their value at customs.  It shall also mean goods designated by order, proposed by the Minister of Finance, from among goods on which the total customs duties, taxes, and various charges levied upon importation range between 20% and 50% of their value at customs.
