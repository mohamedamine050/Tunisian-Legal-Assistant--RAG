1.  Any person who participates, in any capacity whatsoever, as a beneficiary in the commission of a misdemeanor described as offenses punishable by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars,  for smuggling or a misdemeanor described as offenses punishable by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars, for importation or exportation without declaration, shall be punished with the same penalties imposed on the perpetrators of the misdemeanor, as well as the penalties of deprivation of rights stipulated in Article 402 of this Code.


2. The following shall be considered beneficiaries:

    a. Managers and members of fraud organizations, insurers, insured parties, financiers, and owners of goods, and generally anyone with a direct interest in the fraud.

    b. Anyone who contributed in any way to acts committed by a number of persons in agreement among themselves, according to a fraudulent scheme they devised to achieve the result they all seek.

    c. Anyone who intentionally concealed the actions of the perpetrators of the fraud and attempted to spare them punishment, or intentionally purchased or possessed, even outside the customs area, goods originating from a misdemeanor described as offenses punishable by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars, for smuggling or importation without declaration.
