1. Logistics activity areas shall be fenced, and their entry and exit points shall be subject to the continuous surveillance of customs authorities.

2. The entry and exit of persons and vehicles to and from logistics activity areas shall be subject to the surveillance of customs authorities.

3. The entry and exit of persons and vehicles to and from logistics activity areas shall be subject to authorization.

The authorization form, procedures, and methods of delivery shall be determined by a decision of the Minister of Finance.

4. Customs authorities shall monitor goods entering, residing in, and exiting logistics activity areas.

The procedures and methods of customs control shall be determined by a decision of the Minister of Finance.
