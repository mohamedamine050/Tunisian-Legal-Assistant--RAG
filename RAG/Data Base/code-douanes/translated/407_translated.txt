1. In no case whatsoever may the amount of fines imposed for the application of the provisions of this Code, calculated on the basis of duties or value, be less than one hundred dinars.

2. In the cases stipulated in paragraph 2, points (a) and (b) of Article 382 of this Code, relating to the shortage in the number of parcels or packages of goods, and also in the cases stipulated in paragraph 2(c) of Article 382 of this Code, relating to the embezzlement or substitution of goods during transport, the amount of the fine imposed may not be less than one hundred dinars for each parcel or for each ton or part of a ton if the goods are unpackaged.
