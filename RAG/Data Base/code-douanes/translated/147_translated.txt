The facilities set forth in Articles 145 and 146 of this Code shall only be granted to persons who have not committed any offense described as felonies, within the meaning of this law,  being crimes punishable by death or imprisonment exceeding five years.  Offenses described as misdemeanors are those punishable by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars.  Offenses described as infractions are those punishable by imprisonment not exceeding fifteen days or a fine not exceeding sixty dinars.  This is in accordance with Article 122 of the Code of Criminal Procedure.  The same applies to serious customs or tax offenses, and those whose financial situation enables them to fulfill their obligations towards the customs administration.
