1- In addition to the fine resulting from the failure to submit documents in accordance with the conditions stipulated in Articles 62 and 107 of this Code, the Court orders the offender to submit the required documents that have not been submitted, and compels him to pay a sum of money not less than fifty dinars and not more than one hundred dinars for each day of delay.

2- The period for calculating this sum begins from the date of the parties' signing of the record of refusal to execute the judgment or from the date of notification of the content of the record by legal means.

3- The calculation of this fine ceases from the date of the drafting of the record of receipt of the required documents.
