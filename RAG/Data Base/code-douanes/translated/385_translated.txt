1. Imprisonment for a period ranging from one to fifteen days and a fine ranging from five hundred to three thousand dinars shall be imposed for:

- Any violation described as felonies within the meaning of this law; felonies being crimes punishable by death or imprisonment exceeding five years.  Crimes punishable by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars, shall be described as misdemeanors.  Crimes punishable by imprisonment not exceeding fifteen days or a fine not exceeding sixty dinars shall be described as infractions. (Articles 51(1), 57(1), 66(b), 68 and 135(2) of the Code of Criminal Procedure).

- Failure to submit documents, concealment of documents or transactions in the cases provided for in Articles 62 and 107 of this Code.

- Presentation of goods to the designated destination under a tampered or invalid seal.

2. The same penalty shall be imposed on:

A - Any person who continues to carry out operations on behalf of others, directly or indirectly, relating to customs procedures concerning the detailed declaration of goods after their customs intermediary license or temporary license to carry out customs clearance procedures on behalf of others, as provided for in Articles 102(3) and 103 of this Code, has been withdrawn; and any customs intermediary or any person holding a temporary license to carry out customs clearance procedures as provided for in Articles 102(3) and 103 of this Code, who allows others to use the intermediary license or the temporary license to carry out customs clearance procedures.

B - Any person who intentionally assists the persons referred to in paragraph (2 A) of this Article in evading the effects of the withdrawal of the license or the withdrawal of the temporary license to carry out customs clearance procedures on behalf of others.
