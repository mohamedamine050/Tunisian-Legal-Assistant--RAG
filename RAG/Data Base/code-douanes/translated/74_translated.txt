1. Every carrier of goods must deliver to the customs office, immediately upon arrival at its address, a concise declaration and a waybill specifying the goods being transported.

2. Seized goods must be recorded on the waybill under their true designation as to their class and type.

3. The concise declaration is not required for goods that have been declared in detail upon arrival at the office.

4. Goods arriving after the closing of the customs office are deposited in its dependent locations free of charge until its opening.  In this case, a concise declaration must be delivered to the customs authorities upon opening of the office if a detailed declaration of the goods has not been made beforehand.
