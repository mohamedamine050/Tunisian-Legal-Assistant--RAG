Subject to the additional specific conditions stipulated within the framework of the relevant customs system, the license referred to in Article 139 of this Code, as well as the license referred to in Article 174, paragraph 2, and Article 179 of this Code, may only be granted: - To persons who possess all the necessary guarantees for the proper operation of the processes. - If the customs authorities can ensure the supervision and monitoring of the system in question without this necessitating the implementation of administrative mechanisms disproportionate to the economic viability of this system.
