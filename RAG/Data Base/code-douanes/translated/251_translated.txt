1- Full or partial exemption from the payment of duties and charges payable upon import shall not be granted unless it is established that the compensatory products have been declared for consumption in the name and on behalf of:

a- The licensee himself;

b- Or any other person residing in Tunisia, provided that he has obtained the approval of the customs authorities and the approval of the licensee, and that he fulfills all the conditions relating to the license.


2- Full or partial exemption from the payment of duties and charges payable upon import shall not be granted when it is established that the conditions have not been met or that the obligations relating to the inactive transfer system have not been fulfilled.

However, full or partial exemption from the payment of duties and charges payable shall be granted if it is established that the irregularities noted have no actual impact on the proper functioning of this system.
