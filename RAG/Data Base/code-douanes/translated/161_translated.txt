1. A discharge from the written obligations may only be granted by the customs office of destination if:

- The goods in transit are deposited in the customs clearance warehouses and areas, or

in export warehouses and areas, in accordance with the conditions stipulated in Articles 82 to 86

and paragraph (3) of Article 133 of this Code;

- Or these goods are exported;

- Or they are declared under another customs regime.

2. Goods in transit under the customs transit system and authorized for consumption at the office of destination are subject to payment of the customs duties and charges due on the date of registration of the detailed declaration for their consumption.

3. Subject to the provisions of paragraph 2 of Article 152 of this Code, the value of the goods used to calculate the customs duties and charges due may not be less than their accepted value upon entry into the customs territory.
