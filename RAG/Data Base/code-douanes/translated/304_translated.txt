1. If the seized goods are not confiscated, the seizure shall be lifted from the means of transport upon provision of a guarantee by a solvent person or by securing the value of the means of transport.

2.  The record shall include the offer to lift the seizure and the concerned party's response thereto.

3. If it is established that the owner of the means of transport acted in good faith, having concluded a contract of carriage or a financial lease agreement binding him to the offender in accordance with the applicable laws, regulations, and professional custom, then the lifting of the seizure on the means of transport may be granted, provided that the expenses incurred by the administration for the guarding and preservation of this means of transport are reimbursed.
