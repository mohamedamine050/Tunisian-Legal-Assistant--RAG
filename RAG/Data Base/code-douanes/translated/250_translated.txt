When the subject matter of the speaker or writer's statement is the process of converting, repairing goods temporarily exported, and this repair is carried out for consideration, the amount of duties and taxes due shall be determined on the basis of the taxation elements applicable to the compensatory products on the date of registration of the customs declaration placing these products for consumption. The value adopted by customs for calculating the duties and taxes due shall be the amount of the repair costs, provided that these costs are the only consideration paid by the license holder and have not been affected by any relationships that may exist between him and the person who carried out the repair.
