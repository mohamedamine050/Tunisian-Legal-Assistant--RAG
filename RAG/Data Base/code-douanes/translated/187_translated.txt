1- Upon expiry of the deadlines stipulated in Articles 177 and 182 of this Code, the depositor must place the goods located in the customs warehouse under another customs regime, in accordance with the applicable laws and regulations.

2- In the absence thereof, the depositor shall be notified by registered letter with acknowledgement of receipt, inviting him to fulfill his obligations within one month from the date of receipt of the notification.  If he fails to comply, he shall be compelled to pay a monthly penalty of 1% of the value of the goods, from the expiry of the deadlines referred to in paragraph 1 of this Article until the date of removal of the goods or their sale by public auction according to the conditions specified in paragraph 3 of this Article.

3- If this notification does not produce results within one month, a writ of execution shall be issued against the depositor for the recovery of the mandatory penalty stipulated in paragraph 2 of this Article. The customs authorities may then proceed to sell by public auction the goods that have not been removed from the warehouse.
