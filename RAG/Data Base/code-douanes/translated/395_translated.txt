They are considered imported goods without notification:

1. Goods declared for temporary export or to obtain a transit permit within the customs area, in the event of failure to return the goods to the customs authorities or in the event of a difference in nature or type between the goods mentioned and those previously presented upon departure.

2. Seized goods or goods subject to high import duties or internal taxes, found on board vessels within commercial ports, regardless of goods legally listed in cargo manifests or comprising the ship's cargo and stores, which were presented in accordance with standard procedures before the search commenced.

3. Goods specifically designated by order, found on board vessels with a net tonnage of less than 100 cubic meters sailing or moored in the maritime area of the customs territory.
