1. Goods that have not been collected within four months of the date of their registration in the deposit register shall be sold by public auction.

2. Perishable goods or goods in poor condition may be sold during this period with the authorization of the President of the Primary Court,  following a request from the customs collector, and subject to the territorial jurisdiction of the relevant customs office.

3. Goods whose value does not exceed one thousand dinars and which have not been collected upon expiry of the four-month period referred to in paragraph 1 above shall be deemed abandoned in favor of the state.  The customs administration may sell them by public auction or donate them to the Tunisian Union for Social Solidarity or to social welfare charities.
