1. Every person directly or indirectly involved in supply operations shall provide the customs authorities with the invoices and all documents and information necessary to determine the customs value.

2. The customs authorities are bound by absolute confidentiality regarding confidential information or information provided to them under a confidentiality undertaking for customs valuation purposes, and may not disclose it without express authorization from the persons or governments that provided it, unless compelled to do so within the scope of judicial proceedings.
