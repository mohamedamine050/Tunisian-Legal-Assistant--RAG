1. If the judgment is not in favor of the administration, the insured money or the portion of the insured money shall be returned to the declarant, with the addition of 7.5% for each month or part thereof elapsed from the date of insurance of the money to the date of the return decision.

If the declarant has provided a financial guarantee, the expenses incurred thereby shall be returned to him within the limits and according to the procedures determined by a decision of the Minister of Finance.

2. The administration shall be required to pay a fine calculated in accordance with the provisions of Article 373 of this Code if it has refused to release the goods when the judgment was not in its favor.

3. If the judgment is against the declarant, the amount of the duties and taxes due, which were not insured, shall be increased by adding the delay interest stipulated in paragraph 3 of Article 130 of this Code.
