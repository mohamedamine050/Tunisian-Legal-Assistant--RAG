The customs value of imported goods shall be determined in accordance with the provisions of this chapter based on the assessable value.  The assessable value shall consist of the sum of the following elements: A- The cost or value of the materials and manufacturing or other processes used to produce the imported goods. B- The amount of profit and general expenses equal to the amount usually included in the price of goods of the same nature or kind as the goods to be assessed when sold by producers in the exporting country for export to Tunisia. C- The cost or value of the elements stipulated in paragraph 1(c) of Article 30 of this Code.
