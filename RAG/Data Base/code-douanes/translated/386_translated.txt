Punishment shall be imprisonment for a term ranging from sixteen days to one month, and confiscation of the smuggled goods, means of transport, and objects used to conceal the fraud, and a fine ranging from one to two times the value of the smuggled goods for each act of smuggling, and for each import or export without notification if it concerns goods of the kind of prohibited goods or goods subject to high import duties, or goods subject to internal duties, or prohibited goods, or goods subject to duties upon export.
