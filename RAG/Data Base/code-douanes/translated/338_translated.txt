Subject to the deadlines stipulated in the Code of Criminal Procedure, temporary release of a suspect residing abroad, who is in pre-trial detention for a misdemeanor,  – a misdemeanor being defined as an offense punishable by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars –  cannot be granted except after the provision of a guarantee to pay the financial penalties resulting from the aforementioned misdemeanor.  The term "suspect" refers to the suspicion of committing a crime before the charge is filed.
