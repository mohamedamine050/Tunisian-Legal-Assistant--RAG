The Customs Administration may be requested:

- To return duties and taxes within three years from the date on which these duties and taxes became refundable according to the legislation in force, and in the most extreme cases, within five years from the date of collection. However, for duties and taxes that became refundable by virtue of a written court judgment resolving a legal or factual issue, or a court decision on a specific matter, the period for reimbursement shall be three years from the date of the judgment or court decision, regardless of the date of collection.

- To return goods within two years from the date of their deposit.

- To return expenses incurred due to the seizure or deposit of goods within two years from their due date.
