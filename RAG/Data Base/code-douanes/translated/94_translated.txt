1- a) The following operations may be carried out on goods during their stay in logistics activity areas:

- Simple operations referred to in Article 183 of this Code.

- Transfer operations provided for in Article 93 of this Code.

b) The methods of customs control for the aforementioned operations shall be determined by a decision of the Minister of Finance.

2- Non-national goods placed in logistics activity areas may be temporarily imported into the customs territory and placed under:

- The effective transfer system.

- The customs-controlled transfer system.

- The temporary admission system.

This shall be in accordance with the conditions stipulated for each system, as applicable.
