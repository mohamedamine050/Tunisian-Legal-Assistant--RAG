1- By way of exception to the provisions of Article 202 of this Code, compensatory products may, by order, be subject to the payment of duties and taxes due thereon, according to their specific tax elements, on the date of registration of the declaration of placing into consumption.

2- When the compensatory products referred to in paragraph 1 of this Article meet, on the date of their placing into consumption, the conditions for benefiting from a preferential tariff treatment, these products shall benefit from the preferential tariff treatment granted to conforming goods on the date of registration of the declaration of placing into consumption.

3- Compensatory products are accepted with full or partial exemption from the payment of duties and taxes upon import when they meet the conditions for benefiting from one of the exemption systems provided for in the legislation in force on the date of their placing into consumption.
