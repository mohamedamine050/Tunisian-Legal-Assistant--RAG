1. The temporary admission system may be used within the customs territory with the total or partial suspension of duties and charges payable upon the import of goods intended for re-export, without any changes being made to them, except for the normal wear and tear resulting from their authorized use under this system.

2. For the purposes of this chapter, the term "goods" means:

a.  Goods and baggage, including the means of transport, imported by a traveler ordinarily resident abroad and coming to reside temporarily in Tunisia.

b. Equipment, materials, products, and animals that will be re-exported in their original state after their use.
