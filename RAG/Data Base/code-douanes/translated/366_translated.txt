The ship's master is exempt from all liability:

A- In the case stipulated in paragraph 2 of Article 395 of this Code when it is proven that he fulfilled all his duties relating to surveillance or if the perpetrator of the offense is identified.

B- In the case stipulated in paragraph 3 of Article 395 of this Code if he proves that a serious fault compelled the ship to change its course and that these events were recorded in the ship's logbook before its inspection by customs authorities.
