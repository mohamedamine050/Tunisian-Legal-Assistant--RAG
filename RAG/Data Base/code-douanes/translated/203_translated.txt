1- The accepted raw materials under the partial export conversion system cannot be re-exported or used for consumption in their original state. However, the Director General of Customs may, upon a justified request from the beneficiary of the system and after obtaining the opinion of the technical departments of the ministry responsible for the sector, authorize the re-export or consumption in their original state of the raw materials.

2- When the goods supplied on the date of their release for consumption meet the conditions for benefiting from a preferential tariff treatment, these goods shall benefit from this preferential treatment granted to similar goods on the date of registration of the declaration of release for consumption.

3- Imported goods are accepted with total or partial exemption from duties and/or taxes upon import when they meet the conditions for benefiting from one of the exemption systems provided for in the legislation in force for similar goods upon import on the date of their release for consumption.
