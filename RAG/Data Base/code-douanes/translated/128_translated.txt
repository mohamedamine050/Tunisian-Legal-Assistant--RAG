Users of the integrated automated system for processing foreign trade procedures may pay the duties, fees, and penalties related thereto, levied upon import or export, by reliable electronic means, in accordance with the legislation in force concerning electronic transactions.  Payment of duties, fees, and penalties by the methods stipulated in the first paragraph of this chapter exempts from undertaking any other procedure with the same purpose. The scope and methods of application of the provisions of this chapter shall be determined by an order.
