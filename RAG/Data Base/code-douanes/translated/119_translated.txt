1- Following registration of the declaration, the Customs authorities shall undertake the following procedures:

a- Review of the data contained in the declaration and the accompanying documents.  They may request the declarant to submit further documentation to verify the accuracy of that data.

b- Examination of the goods, either fully or partially, if deemed necessary, and taking samples where appropriate for the purpose of submitting them for testing or further scrutiny.

2- In case of dispute, the declarant has the right to reject the results of the partial examination and request a full examination of the goods.
