The following shall be considered as import or export operations of prohibited goods without notification:

1. Any offense described as felonies under this law, meaning crimes punishable by death or imprisonment exceeding five years.  Crimes punishable by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars, shall be described as misdemeanors.  Offenses punishable by imprisonment not exceeding fifteen days or a fine not exceeding sixty dinars shall be described as infractions. (Article 122 of the Code of Criminal Procedure).  This includes obtaining or attempting to obtain one of the documents stipulated in the third paragraph of Article 39 of this Code, or attempting to recover customs duties paid pursuant to the provisions of Article 298 of this Code, whether by forging official seals, by making false statements, or by using other fraudulent means.

2. Any false statement intended to, or having the effect of, evading the application of prohibition measures.

3. Any false statement for the purpose of benefiting from a preferential tax regime.

4. False statements regarding the type, value, or origin of goods, or the designation of the true sender or recipient, if these offenses are committed using forged, incorrect, incomplete, or inapplicable invoices, certificates, or other documents.
