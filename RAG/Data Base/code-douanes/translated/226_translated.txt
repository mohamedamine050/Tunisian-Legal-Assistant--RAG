The Director General of Customs may authorize, upon a justified request from the beneficiary under the system, the destruction of compensatory products or products supplied under the effective transfer system.

The destruction process must render the supplied materials or compensatory products valueless.

The destruction process must take place in the presence of Customs officials.

Subject to current legislation, scrap, upon its disposal, is subject to payment of the applicable duties and taxes according to its type, condition, and value on the date of its disposal.
