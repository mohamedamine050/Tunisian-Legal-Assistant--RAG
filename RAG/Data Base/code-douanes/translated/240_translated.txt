1. Upon release for consumption of goods imported under a temporary admission system, the amount of duties and taxes payable shall be determined based on the tax elements applicable to the goods on the date of registration of the declaration for placing them under the temporary admission system.

2. However, for the cases stipulated in Article 237, the amount of duties and taxes payable may be determined based on the tax elements applicable to the goods concerned on the date of registration of the declaration for release for consumption.

3. Upon release for consumption of goods imported under a temporary admission system with partial exemption from duties and taxes payable upon import, the amount of duties and taxes shall be equal to the difference between the amount of duties and taxes calculated based on the provisions of paragraph 1 of this Article and the amount paid in accordance with the provisions of Articles 238 and 239 of this Code.
