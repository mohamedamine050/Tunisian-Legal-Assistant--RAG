1. The Tunisian customs territory referred to in this Code as "customs territory" includes the Tunisian continental territory, its internal and territorial waters, the Tunisian natural islands and the internal and territorial waters surrounding these islands, artificial islands, installations and structures erected in the exclusive economic zone or the continental shelf, as well as the Tunisian airspace.

2. Free zones may be established within the customs territory, exempted automatically or partially from the customs regime, in accordance with current legislation.  The term "free zone" refers to any independent territorial area established so that goods located therein are considered as being outside the customs territory with regard to the application of import duties and taxes, as well as restrictions relating to the control of foreign trade and exchange.
