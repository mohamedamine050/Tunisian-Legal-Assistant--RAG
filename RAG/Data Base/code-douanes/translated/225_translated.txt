1. When the compensatory products stipulated in Article 224 of this Code, at the time they are made available for consumption, meet the conditions for benefiting from a preferential tariff treatment granted to conforming goods, then these products shall benefit from the same preferential tariff treatment.

2. The compensatory products referred to in the first paragraph of this Article shall be accepted with full or partial exemption from the duties and charges payable upon importation when they meet the conditions for benefiting from one of the exemption systems stipulated in the legislation in force.
