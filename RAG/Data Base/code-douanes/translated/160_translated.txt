1.  Goods transported under the customs transit system, which were presented to the customs authorities at the office of entry or exit, must be re-presented with the exemption documents, or documents replacing them:

    a.  Upon each request issued by the customs authorities during transit.

    b.  At the destination office or at the location previously designated by the customs authorities, upon arrival.


2. The carrier or recipient of the goods, knowing that they are subject to the customs transit system, is obliged to present the goods intact at the destination office within the stipulated timeframe, respecting the goods identification procedures adopted by the customs authorities, taking into account the obligations or undertakings of the original undertaking referred to in paragraph 1 of this chapter.
