1. In the case of placing compensatory products for consumption, the applicable duties and charges shall be levied according to the type and condition of the goods when placed under the export processing regime, and based on the origin of these goods used in the manufacture of compensatory products when placed for consumption.

2. The rates of duties and charges applied shall be those in effect on the date of registration of the declaration of placing for consumption.  The value to be declared for the levy of this duty shall be the value of the goods on the same date and according to the conditions set forth in Articles 22 to 36 of this Code.
