Traders established in accordance with the regulations in force may hold, for the purpose of resale, a stock of goods subject to customs control, provided that the value of this stock does not exceed five hundred (500) dinars for each type of goods.

Customs officers may retain, without restriction, goods intended for family consumption within limits consistent with said consumption.

Persons residing within the customs area may hold grains and other products of the region, as well as livestock belonging to them.

By virtue of a decision of the Minister of Finance, these persons may be required to keep an accounting register containing the quantities of the aforementioned goods, subject to the supervision of customs officers.
