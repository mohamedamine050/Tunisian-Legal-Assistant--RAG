1. Customs records, when authenticated until proven false, serve as the basis for obtaining a court order, in accordance with general law, to take all necessary precautionary measures against individuals who are criminally or civilly liable, in order to secure various debts owed to Customs arising from these records.

2. The court competent to hear precautionary measures, including applications for the total or partial lifting of a precautionary seizure, is the court within whose jurisdiction the record was drawn up.
