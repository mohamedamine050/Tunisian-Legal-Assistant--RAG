1- When the subject matter upon which the speaker or writer bases his statement is the process of repairing goods temporarily exported, then their placement for consumption is done with complete exemption from the payment of duties and taxes due upon import. This is the case if the party concerned proves to the customs authorities that the repair was carried out free of charge pursuant to a contractual or legal obligation of guarantee or due to a manufacturing defect. 2- The provisions of the first paragraph of this chapter shall not apply if the poor condition of the goods concerned was taken into account when they were first placed for consumption.
