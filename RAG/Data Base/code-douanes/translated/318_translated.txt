1. The Minister of Finance, or the individual delegated by the Minister of Finance who holds the position of Director of a Central or Regional Customs Administration, shall initiate the legal action, submitting the duly completed records, along with the Customs Administration's requests, to the Attorney General at the competent court.

2. The Minister of Finance, or the individual delegated by the Minister of Finance who holds the position of Director of a Central or Regional Customs Administration, shall lodge an appeal before the Court of Appeal against judgments rendered against the Customs Administration.

3. The Minister of Finance, or the Director General of Customs by virtue of a delegation from the Minister of Finance, shall lodge an appeal before the Court of Cassation against judgments rendered against the Customs Administration.
