1. The ship's master must deposit the following with the customs office within twenty-four hours following the ship's arrival at the port:

a.  Under the heading of a concise declaration:

   - A cargo manifest, accompanied, where necessary, by its official translation.  - Lists of provisions and the crew's personal effects.

b.  Ship charter party documents or shipping documents, as well as nationality certificates and other documents that the customs administration may request for the purpose of applying customs measures.

2. The concise declaration must be deposited even if the ship is not carrying cargo.

3. The twenty-four-hour period stipulated in the first paragraph above does not include Sundays and public holidays.
