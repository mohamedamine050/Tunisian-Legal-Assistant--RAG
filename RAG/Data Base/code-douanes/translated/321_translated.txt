1. The mandatory payment cards issued by customs officers and signed by the Minister of Finance or by a person authorized by the Minister of Finance to do so, in accordance with the legislation in force, shall be used:

    a. To collect duties and taxes entrusted to the customs administration for collection, as well as penalties and all amounts due in the event of non-compliance with the commitments included in exemption bonds and undertakings.

    b. In all cases where financial amounts are payable to the customs administration.

2. The notice or reminder of payment shall be served by customs officers, bailiffs, or officials of the financial services, in accordance with the provisions of the Public Accounting Code.
