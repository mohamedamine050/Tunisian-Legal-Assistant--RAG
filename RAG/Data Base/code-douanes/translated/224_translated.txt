By way of exception to the provisions of Article 223 of this Code, and pursuant to authorization from the Minister of Finance:

a) Compensatory products, upon their release for consumption, may be subject to the payment of customs duties and charges levied thereon, according to their specific tax elements on the date of registration of the declaration of release for consumption.

b) Secondary compensatory products resulting from the transformation process may be subject to the payment of customs duties and charges levied thereon, according to their specific tax elements on the date of registration of the declaration of release for consumption; provided that their quantities are proportionate to the quantities exported of the primary compensatory product.

In this case, the delay interest charge stipulated in Article 130, paragraph 3 of this Code, shall not be applied.
