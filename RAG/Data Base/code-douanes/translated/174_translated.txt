1. The public warehouse is a public warehouse open to any person for the deposit of various types of goods, except those excluded by the provisions of Articles 172 and 173 of this Code.

The public warehouse is considered a specialized warehouse when it is equipped to accept goods:

- which present specific hazards or which may damage the quality of other products;

- the preservation of which requires special facilities.

2. The concession for the public warehouse is granted by order of the municipalities or the Chambers of Commerce and Industry or public sector institutions, and this concession may not be transferred to a third party.

3. The costs of management are borne by the concessionaire.

4. The concessionaire collects storage fees, the amount of which is determined by a decision of the Minister of Finance, after obtaining the opinion of the Minister responsible for Trade.
