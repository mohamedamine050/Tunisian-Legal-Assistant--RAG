The following works within the boundaries of the cultural site are subject to prior authorization from the Minister in charge of Heritage:

a) Works of complete or partial demolition of any existing building within the cultural site.

b) Works of electricity, telephone, water, gas, sanitation, roads, wired and wireless communications networks, and anything that may disfigure the external appearance of the area and the buildings within it.

c) Placement of advertising boards, sign holders, posters, and other commercial advertising signs.

The request for authorization for the above-mentioned works shall be responded to within a period not exceeding two months.
