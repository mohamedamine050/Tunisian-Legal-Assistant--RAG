Excavations and explorations shall be conducted by the licensed parties under their responsibility, in accordance with the terms and conditions stipulated in the license and under the supervision of the competent authorities of the Ministry in charge of heritage. The licensed party shall inform those authorities of any discovery of movable or immovable objects upon their discovery. The aforementioned authorities shall register the discoveries and authorize the taking of all necessary measures to preserve them. In the event that excavations and explorations are not carried out in accordance with the terms and conditions stipulated in the license, or if the deadlines for announcing discoveries are not respected, the competent authorities may withdraw the license, either temporarily or permanently, as the situation requires.
