Owners undertaking improvement works on historical monuments that are protected, classified, or authorized by the Ministry responsible for Heritage are eligible for assistance from the National Housing Improvement Fund, established by the Decree dated August 23, 1956.

New construction works and works of a purely cosmetic nature are not eligible for this benefit.

The terms and methods of intervention by the National Housing Improvement Fund are determined by a joint decision of the Minister of Finance, the Minister of Urban Development, and the Minister responsible for Heritage.
