**(As per the first paragraph of Article 4 of Law No. 118 of 2001 dated December 6, 2001)**

The relevant departments within the Ministry responsible for Heritage shall prepare a protection and revitalization plan for the cultural site, following the publication of the decision establishing it and within a period of five years from the date of publication, subject to renewal by a joint decision of the Minister responsible for Heritage and the Minister responsible for Urban Planning.

The preparation of this plan shall be subject to the same procedures applicable to the preparation of urban development plans. It shall be approved by ministerial order, upon the proposal of the Minister responsible for Heritage and the Minister responsible for Urban Planning, after consultation with the National Heritage Committee.
