## English Translation:

**Appointment**

**Appointment**

Placing the appropriate individual in the position that aligns with the qualifications and requirements necessary to perform it.

Cultural sites, as defined in Article 2 of this Journal, are defined by a joint decision of the Minister in charge of Heritage and the Minister in charge of Urban Planning, after obtaining the opinion of the National Heritage Committee. The decision establishing the cultural site is published in the Official Gazette of the Tunisian Republic.
