The order of arrangement shall result in the state's financial contribution to the works aimed at preserving the monument, and the competent authorities of the ministry responsible for heritage shall determine the contribution rate on a case-by-case basis, not exceeding fifty percent of the value of those works. The owner shall be notified of these works and requested to carry them out within a maximum period of three months. After the expiry of the specified period and in the event that the owner refuses to carry out the aforementioned works, he shall be given a warning to carry out the works within a period of fifteen days. In the event that the aforementioned works are not carried out, the Minister responsible for Heritage shall authorize the competent authorities to carry out the works immediately, and the owner shall be required to reimburse the expenses within the limits of the required proportions.
