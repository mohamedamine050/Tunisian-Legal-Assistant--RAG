If it is proven to those interests that it is necessary to preserve these discoveries in the name of the public interest, the Minister in charge of heritage must take a decision to protect them as a historical monument and to protect the land on which they are located or surrounding it, in accordance with the provisions of Chapter Four concerning the protection of historical monuments. The decision shall specify the location of the discovered artifacts and the area of the land on which they are located or surrounding it that must be protected.
