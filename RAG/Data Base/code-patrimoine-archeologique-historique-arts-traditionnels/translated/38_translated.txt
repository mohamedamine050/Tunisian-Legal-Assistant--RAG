The order of arrangement shall result in the State's financial contribution to works aimed at preserving the monument. The authorities within the ministry responsible for heritage shall determine the percentage of contribution on a case-by-case basis, not exceeding fifty percent of the value of such works. 

The owner shall be notified of these works and required to complete them within a maximum period of three months.

Upon the expiration of the designated period, and if the owner refuses to carry out the aforementioned works, they shall be notified to do so within a period of fifteen days.

If the aforementioned works are not carried out, the Minister responsible for heritage shall authorize the relevant authorities to immediately undertake the works, obligating the owner to reimburse the expenses within the limits of the claimed percentages.
