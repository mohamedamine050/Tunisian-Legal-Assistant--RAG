**(Repealed and replaced by Article 1 of Decree No. 43 of 2011 dated May 25, 2011)** 
Any person who, for commercial purposes, adulterates or imitates protected movable property without obtaining prior authorization from the Minister in charge of Heritage shall be punished with imprisonment for a period of six months and a fine of five thousand dinars.

In the case of recidivism, the penalty shall be doubled.
