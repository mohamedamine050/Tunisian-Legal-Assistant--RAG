The historical and traditional groups recognized by Article 3 of this law shall be designated and their boundaries determined as "protected areas" (1) by a joint decision of the Minister responsible for Urban Planning and the Minister responsible for Heritage, upon the proposal of the latter. This decision shall be taken after consultation with the concerned local communities and the National Heritage Committee. The decision regarding the establishment and definition of the "protected area" (1) shall be published in the Official Gazette of the Republic of Tunisia.
