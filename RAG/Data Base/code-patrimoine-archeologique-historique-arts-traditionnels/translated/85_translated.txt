Excavations and explorations shall be carried out by authorized parties and under their responsibility, in accordance with the procedures and conditions specified in the license and under the supervision of the competent authorities within the ministry responsible for heritage. The authorized party shall inform these authorities of any discovery of movable or immovable objects upon their discovery. The aforementioned authorities shall register the discoveries and authorize the taking of all necessary measures to preserve them. In the event that the excavations and explorations are not carried out in accordance with the procedures stipulated in the license or the deadlines for reporting the discoveries are not respected, the competent authorities may revoke the license temporarily or permanently, as the circumstances may warrant.
