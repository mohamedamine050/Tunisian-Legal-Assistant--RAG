**[Repealed and replaced by Article 1 of Decree No. 43 of 2011 dated May 25, 2011].** -  Any person who hinders or obstructs the operations of the competent authorities referred to in Articles 12, 21, 33, 36, and 86 of this Code shall be punished by imprisonment for a period of one year and a fine of ten thousand dinars.  In the event of recidivism, the penalty shall be doubled.
