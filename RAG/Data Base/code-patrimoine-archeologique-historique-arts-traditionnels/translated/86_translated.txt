The specialized departments within the Ministry responsible for heritage, acting in the public interest, shall carry out exploratory and excavation works with the aim of uncovering archaeological remains from prehistory and history on all land that is owned by the Ministry or owned by others. The Minister responsible for heritage may declare the public interest of excavations and exploratory works to be conducted on land, by virtue of a decision. He may also authorize the specialized departments under his authority to occupy the property temporarily for a period specified in the decision, not exceeding five years.
