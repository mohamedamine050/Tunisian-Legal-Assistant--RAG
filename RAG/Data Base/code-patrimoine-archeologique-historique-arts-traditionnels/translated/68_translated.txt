Movable property owned by private individuals is protected by a decision of the Minister responsible for Heritage, with the consent of the owner and after consulting the National Heritage Committee. In the event of the owner's refusal, they can be compelled to consent by a writ issued upon a petition from the Governor of the Region where the owner of the movable property resides. In the event of its sale, the right of pre-emption may be exercised in accordance with the procedures prescribed by Article 89 of this Code.
