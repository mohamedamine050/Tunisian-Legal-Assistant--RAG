The following works within the "protected" area (1) are subject to prior authorization from the Minister in charge of Heritage:

a) Total or partial demolition works of any existing building within the boundaries of the "protected" area.

b) Works related to electrical, telephone, water and sanitation networks, paved and unpaved roads, wireless networks, and anything that may disfigure the external appearance of the area or its interior.

c) The placement of advertising boards, poster holders, posters themselves, and other commercial signage.

The application for authorization shall be answered within a period not exceeding two months from the date of receipt of the application for the aforementioned works.
