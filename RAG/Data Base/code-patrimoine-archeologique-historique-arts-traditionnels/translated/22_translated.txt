The model for preservation and revitalization consists of a land plot model and regulatory provisions. It specifically encompasses the following data:

- Built or unbuilt real estate subject to preservation,
- Deteriorating real estate subject to restoration,
- Buildings subject to complete or partial demolition for public or private works,
- Engineering standards to be respected,
- Essential infrastructure and equipment,
- Rules for the arrangement of public spaces,
- Prohibited activities that do not comply with the requirements for maintaining "protected" areas (1) in light of the specific characteristics of these areas.
