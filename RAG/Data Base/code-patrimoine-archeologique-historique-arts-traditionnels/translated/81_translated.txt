(Repealed and replaced by Article 1 of Decree No. 43 of 2011 dated 25 May 2011)  Whoever obstructs or hinders the operations of the competent authorities referred to in Articles 12, 21, 33, 36, and 86 of this Code shall be punished by imprisonment for one year and a fine of ten thousand dinars. In case of recidivism, the penalty shall be doubled.
