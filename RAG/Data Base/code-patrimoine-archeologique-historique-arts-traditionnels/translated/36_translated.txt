The owner of the monument is informed by the Minister responsible for heritage of his intention to classify his property. The owner is requested to allow the relevant services of the Ministry responsible for heritage to visit the monument and conduct the necessary technical studies to prepare the classification file. 

The owner may submit his observations and suggestions to the National Heritage Committee within a period not exceeding one month from the date of his notification. 

Should the owner prevent these services from carrying out the aforementioned actions, he shall be compelled to do so by an order issued upon petition from the Judge of the District where the property is located.
