A Committee, formally constituted to bring together individuals with relevant expertise from across the institution, is to be established by the Minister in charge of Heritage. This Committee, known as the "National Heritage Committee," is tasked with providing its opinion and submitting proposals to the Minister on the following matters:  protection and arrangement of historical landmarks, protection of movable archaeological objects, revitalization of "protected" areas (1), and protection of cultural sites. The Committee is also responsible for providing its opinion on programs, projects, and plans related to the protection of cultural property that the Minister may present to it. The composition and organization of the Committee's work are to be regulated by decree.
