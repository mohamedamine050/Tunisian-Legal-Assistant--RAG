Owners undertaking improvement works on protected, classified, or designated historical monuments, as authorized or determined by the Ministry responsible for Heritage, shall benefit from assistance from the National Housing Improvement Fund, established by the Order dated 23 August 1956. New construction works and purely cosmetic works are not eligible for this benefit. The terms and methods of intervention by the National Housing Improvement Fund shall be determined by a joint decision of the Minister of Finance, the Minister responsible for Urban Development, and the Minister responsible for Heritage.
