**(Repealed and replaced by Article 1 of Decree No. 43 of 2011 dated May 25, 2011.)** - The export of movable property specified in Article 5 of this Code, whether protected or not, is prohibited. The temporary export of such movable property is subject to a license from the Minister responsible for Heritage. Notwithstanding legal proceedings, any movable property described in the preceding paragraph that is attempted to be removed from the national territory without a license from the Minister responsible for Heritage shall be seized and confiscated in favor of the State.
