Compensation shall be paid to the landowner if it is established that the excavation or exploration works carried out on the land have caused material and permanent damage to buildings legally authorized for construction or have prevented the natural exploitation of the land. The right to compensation shall be extinguished if the claim for compensation is not submitted to the competent authorities of the Ministry responsible for heritage within a maximum period of three months from the date of notification to the owner of the completion of the excavations and explorations.
