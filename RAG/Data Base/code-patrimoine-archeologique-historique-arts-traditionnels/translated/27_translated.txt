The owners are notified of the protection decision by the Minister responsible for heritage.

The protection decision is published in the Official Gazette of the Republic of Tunisia and posted at the municipal headquarters of the location, or if none exists, at the headquarters of the district.

The Ministry responsible for heritage shall place a sign on the property indicating that it is a protected historical landmark.

The protection decision is registered on the title deed at the request of the competent services of the Ministry responsible for heritage, in case the property is registered in the land registry.

In the absence of registration of the property, the Minister responsible for heritage shall register it on behalf of the owners.
