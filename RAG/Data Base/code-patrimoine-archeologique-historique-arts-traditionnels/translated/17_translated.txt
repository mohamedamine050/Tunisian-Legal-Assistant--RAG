(Article 1 of Chapter 2 of Law No. 118 of 2001 dated December 6, 2001, has been amended) The departments concerned within the Ministry responsible for heritage are entrusted with preparing a "Model for the Preservation and Revitalization of Historical and Traditional Groups" following the decision of its creation and within five years of the date of its publication. This period is renewable by a joint decision of the Minister responsible for heritage and the Minister responsible for urban development. The preparation of the preservation model is subject to the same procedures as those applicable to the preparation of the urban development model. Its approval is made by order upon the proposal of the Minister responsible for heritage and the Minister responsible for urban development, after consulting with the National Heritage Committee.
