**(Added by Article 2 of Decree No. 43 of 2011 dated May 25, 2011)**  Subject to the provisions of Article 56 of this Code, any person who engages in the trade of movable property as specified in Article 5 of this Code, whether protected or unprotected, or any other movable property whose national, historical, scientific, aesthetic, artistic, or traditional value in its country of origin has been established, without obtaining a license from the Minister responsible for heritage, shall be punished by imprisonment for a term of five years and a fine of fifty thousand dinars.  An attempt to commit this offense shall be punishable.  In case of recidivism, the punishment shall be doubled.
