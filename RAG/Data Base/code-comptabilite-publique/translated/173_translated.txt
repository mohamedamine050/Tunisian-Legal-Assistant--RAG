In special circumstances, it is permissible to pay the value of the transactions referred to in Article 172 above without the presentation of lists or memoranda from the beneficiaries. Such payment is made in accordance with an administrative certificate issued by the head of the center, specifying the special circumstances that dictated this procedure. The certificate shall state the name and address of the beneficiary, the type and amount of the disbursement, the date of completion of the work performed or services rendered, or the date of receipt of the purchased items, together with a reference to their quantity and unit price, and their registration number, if applicable, in the relevant register.
