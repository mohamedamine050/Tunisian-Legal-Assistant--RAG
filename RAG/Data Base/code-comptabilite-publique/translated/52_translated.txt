Operations outside the budget include:

- Studying the revenues, costs, and circumstances of a project during a specific period.

- All operations related to the circulation of money and similar values, the management of entrusted funds, deposits, and securities of various types, and the issuance of short-term loans.

- The management and repayment of these loans to their owners upon maturity.

- Generally, all operations related to the state's or administrative body's assets and liabilities that are outside the scope of the budget.
