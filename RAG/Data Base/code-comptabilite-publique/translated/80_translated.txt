**(Amended by Law No. 84 of 1984 dated December 31, 1984).** - Payments of dues and fees related to the Directorate of Customs may be made using guaranteed promissory notes. These notes carry interest payable to the State. The **Minister of Finance or his delegate** shall determine the dues that can be paid in this manner, the conditions for accepting these notes, as well as the terms of payment of their contents and the amount of interest accruing on them. 

Customs duties levied on imported goods may be paid directly by state agencies using administrative promissory notes for the payment of customs duties, in accordance with the terms and procedures to be determined by the **Minister of Finance or his delegate**.
