**(Amended by Law No. 86 of 1996 dated November 6, 1996)** - A central accountant, appointed by a joint decision of the "Minister of Finance or his delegate" and the minister concerned, shall be responsible for the collection and disbursement of the attached budget. Some of the responsibilities of the central accountant may be entrusted, by decision of the minister concerned, to regional receivers of the attached budget, appointed under the same conditions.
