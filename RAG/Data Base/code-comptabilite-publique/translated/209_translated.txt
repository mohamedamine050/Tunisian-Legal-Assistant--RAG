(Amended by Law No. 86 of 1996 dated November 6, 1996) - The "Minister of Finance, or his delegate," shall review the accounts of the year submitted by the General Treasurer, the Secretary-General for Expenses, and the Keeper of the Stamp Duty Repository, and certify them, verifying their conformity with their records, before submitting them to the Court of Audit. This must be done before the end of July of the year following the year to which they pertain. The Minister shall also deliver the General State Account before the end of the same year.
