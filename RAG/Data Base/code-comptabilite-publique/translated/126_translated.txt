(Added by Law No. 56 of 2018 dated December 27, 2018) - Subject to the rules governing public expenditures, electronic invoices issued in accordance with the applicable legislation and regulations, as well as liquidation documents and other expense justifications, as well as transfers completed electronically or through information systems approved in the field of public expenditures, may be adopted. These documents and data shall have the same evidentiary and exculpatory force as paper documents.
