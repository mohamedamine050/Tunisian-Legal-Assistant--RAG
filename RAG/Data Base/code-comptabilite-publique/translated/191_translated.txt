(Amended by Law No. 86 of 1996 dated November 6, 1996).- The Financial Collector is not authorized to directly pay state expenses, and he may not pay the value of payment cards or judicial expense lists unless these cards or lists bear the endorsement of the competent accountant. However, under the conditions stipulated in the applicable regulations, he may pay urgent judicial expenses in criminal and misdemeanour cases on behalf of the competent accountant and without his prior endorsement.
