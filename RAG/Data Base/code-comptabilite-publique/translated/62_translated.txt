**(Added by Law No. 91 of 1982 dated December 13, 1982).** - The Treasury may grant secured loans to individuals, secured by a pledge consisting of jewelry made of precious metals bearing the stamp of the Guarantee Department of the Performance Management Department. The terms and methods of granting these loans are determined by a decision of the Minister of Planning and Finance, including those relating to the annual total amount, expert fees, and financial collectors in respect of various secured loan operations. All previous provisions relating to secured loans are repealed, effective July 1, 1983.
