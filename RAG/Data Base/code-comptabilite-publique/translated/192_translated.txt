**(Added by Law No. 86 of 1996, dated November 6, 1996).** - Regional Treasurers, in addition to all tasks entrusted to them under current laws and regulations, shall carry out transactions for the benefit of private individuals or other parties dealing with the Treasury, authorized to do so by the "Minister of Finance or any person delegated by the Minister of Finance."  Regional Treasurers shall be entrusted with the administration of deposits and safekeeping entrusted to the Treasury by judicial authorities under their jurisdiction, according to instructions issued by the "Minister of Finance or any person delegated by the Minister of Finance." They shall also conduct transactions related to the circulation of funds at the regional level on behalf of the General Treasurer.
