The **Agent**, acting on behalf of another person, shall prepare and submit **statements** every three months. These statements shall be a general disclosure of his agency and shall contain a statement of the funds managed, with details of his payments that are subject to reimbursement, along with the justification for the expenditure. These statements shall be addressed to the **Minister of Finance** or to the individual delegated by the Minister for this purpose, enabling them to oversee the agency's activities. A copy of these statements shall also be sent to the relevant accountant.
