**(Amended by Law No. 86 of 1996 dated November 6, 1996).** The Regional Treasurer of the Annexed Budget shall oversee the accounting operations, both receipts and disbursements, carried out by the private Treasurers of the Annexed Budget working in his region. He shall also be responsible for reviewing and consolidating these operations with his own accounts. These Treasurers shall be accountable to him for their accounting operations, as he supervises and monitors their financial affairs under his own responsibility and consolidates all their operations in his accounts.
