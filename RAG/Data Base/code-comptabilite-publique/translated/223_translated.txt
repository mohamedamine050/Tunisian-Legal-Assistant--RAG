(Amended by Law No. 111 of 1990 dated December 31, 1990). - Ministers are the primary authorizers of the revenues and expenditures listed in the annexed budgets. However, this task may be delegated to the directors of the relevant departments by orders issued in their organization. The primary authorizers may, after the approval of the Minister of Economy and Finance, and by way of decisions, transfer the process of commitment and issue payment orders for certain expenditures of the annexed budgets or withdraw them from assistant authorizing officers.
