The "Minister of Finance or his delegate" is responsible for supervising the administrative aspects of the work of public accountants, carried out by sworn officers holding professional cards. (The first paragraph was amended by Article 49 of Finance Law No. 58 of 2010, dated December 17, 2010). The Minister responsible for implementing a particular budget may also supervise the work of public accountants under his jurisdiction. Moreover, public accountants are subject to judicial supervision exercised by the Court of Auditors.
