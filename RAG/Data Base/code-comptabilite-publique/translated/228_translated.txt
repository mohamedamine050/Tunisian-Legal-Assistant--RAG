**(Amended by Law No. 86 of 1996 dated November 6, 1996).** - Regional and Private Collectors of the Annexed Budget shall be responsible for collecting all revenue entrusted to them, and shall carry out all collection and disbursement operations as provided for in the regulations specific to the service concerned with the Annexed Budget. They may also be assigned to carry out collection or disbursement operations for the general state budget.

**Collection**
Collection is the process by which a creditor obtains payment from a debtor for a financial debt contracted with the creditor.
