(Amended by Law No. 106 of 2005 dated December 19, 2005) - The President of the House of Representatives, the President of the Council of Advisers, and the Prime Minister bear the responsibility stipulated by the current laws in their capacity as authorized officers for the expenditure of state funds, as do Mayors for the expenditure of municipal funds. 

Assistant authorized officers of the state, authorized officers for the expenditure of public institutions and local communities other than municipalities, as well as appointed Mayors, shall be tried by the Financial Crimes Division if they commit errors in spending or violations while performing their duties, regardless of any disciplinary or criminal sanctions that may be incurred due to the violations observed.
