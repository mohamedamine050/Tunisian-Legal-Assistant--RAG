(Amended by Law No. 115 of 1989 dated December 30, 1989).- Heads of administrations may not increase the amount of appropriations allocated in the budget by any income whatsoever, unless it is added to public revenues. Subject to special legislative provisions, the management of state property shall directly or under its supervision sell movable and immovable property belonging to the state, and the sale shall be by public auction unless it concerns objects of minor value, in consideration of the price agreed upon by the contracting parties, except where the price is higher or lower than the value of the sale, which shall be the value resulting from the valuation added to an increase of ten percent, from which shall be deducted advertising expenses and other expenses incurred in connection with the sale, and this shall be paid to the financial collector appointed for this purpose. The original price, together with the remainder of the aforementioned increase, shall be included in the revenues of the current fiscal year. In some cases, it may be possible to deviate from the provisions of the preceding paragraph by order.

**Crimes:**

* **Felonies:** Crimes punishable by death or imprisonment exceeding five years.
* **Misdemeanors:** Crimes punishable by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars.
* **Offenses:** Crimes punishable by imprisonment not exceeding fifteen days or by a fine not exceeding sixty dinars.
