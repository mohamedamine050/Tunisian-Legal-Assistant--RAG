(Amended by Law No. 111 of 1990 dated December 31, 1990.) - The accountant mentioned shall have the status of a Senior Accountant, and when necessary, shall be assigned the status of a Central Accountant.  It is permissible to appoint Senior Accountants or Assistant Accountants in accordance with the method outlined in the previous chapter.  The operations carried out by them shall be included in the schedule of work of the Central Accountant referred to above.
