Disbursement orders and their accompanying documents shall be forwarded to the relevant accountant in accordance with their numbering sequence and according to separate schedules prepared for each of the headings, chapters, sections, and chapters contained in the current fiscal year's budget. The accountant shall retain all aforementioned documents and shall, within a timeframe determined by the Minister of Finance or his delegate, return the payment cards for expenses paid in cash, after his endorsement, to the disbursing officers for delivery to their rightful owners.
