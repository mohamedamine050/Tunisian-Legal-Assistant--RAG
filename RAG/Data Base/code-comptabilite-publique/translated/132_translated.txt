If the documents proving [the disbursement of] expenses are lost, damaged, or stolen while in the possession of the relevant accountant, the Minister of Finance, or his delegate, may authorize the accountant to be compensated for the loss. If these documents are lost, damaged, or stolen while in the possession of the Disbursing Officer, he may, with the approval of the Minister of Finance, or his delegate, issue an order to pay the expenses proven by these documents, based on an administrative certificate issued by him in this regard, detailing the circumstances and reasons for the loss, damage, or theft.
