(Amended by Law No. 101 of 1974 dated 25 December 1974).- The State Accountant assigned as the Accountant for the Municipality shall handle its receipts and expenses. This Accountant, in his capacity as the Chief Collector, shall collect all its revenues and monitor its authorized expenditures, which shall be disbursed by order of its President. He may be assisted in these tasks by Assistant Accountants appointed by a decision of the Minister of Finance, or by anyone authorized by the Minister of Finance to do so.
