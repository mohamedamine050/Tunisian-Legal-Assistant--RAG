(Amended by Law No. 145 of 1988 dated December 31, 1988.) - Payment agents may be entrusted with the payment of certain public expenses if these expenses are minor or if their payment could not be carried out through a prior payment order. The nature and amount of expenses that may be paid through payment agencies shall be determined by a decision of "the Minister of Finance or the person authorized by the Minister of Finance" issued for each agency.
