The annual statement of account shall show: 1) the state of the accounting position at the beginning of the year; 2) a breakdown of transactions of all kinds, both receipts and disbursements, completed during the year, specifying those that were part of the budget and those that were outside it; 3) the state of the position at the end of the year. In addition to the account, a general list shall be added in the disbursement papers relating to the expenses paid during the year, which shall be presented along with the monthly accounts.
