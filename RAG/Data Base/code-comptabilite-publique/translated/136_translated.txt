The competent accountant must ensure, under his responsibility and before affixing his signature to the payment orders issued to him:

* That the necessary and legally authorized credits are available.
* That the expenses are correctly entered under the appropriate heading, chapter, section, subsection, paragraph, and subparagraph according to their type or subject matter.
* That the work performed is proven and that the liquidation accounts are correct.
* That the State is free from any debt obligation.
* That the Expenditure Control Department has approved its contract.
* That the rules of **prescription** and **forfeiture of rights** are applied. 
* That all supporting documents are present and valid.
