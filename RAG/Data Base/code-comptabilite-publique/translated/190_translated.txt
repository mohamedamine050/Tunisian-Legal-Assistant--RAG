(Amended by Law No. 86 of 1996 dated November 6, 1996). - The Financial Officer assigned to manage the regional council's funds shall assume the role of a specialized accountant for state expenses authorized for payment by assistant disbursement officers. He shall also assume the same role for judicial expenses in matters of felonies, misdemeanors, and offenses, as well as similar expenses issued by the Court of the region where he works, which is a civil court belonging to the judicial authority that must maintain its independence within the three branches of government.
