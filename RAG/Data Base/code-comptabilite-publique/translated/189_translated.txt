**(Amended by Law No. 86 of 1996 dated November 6, 1996)** - The Financial Collector, for crops, revenues, and taxes he is tasked with collecting, shall submit to the Regional Treasurer, at the end of each year, along with his annual account prepared for the Accounting Department: 
1) A detailed schedule of the debts outstanding from his accounts, accompanied by the warrants for their issuance and justifications. 
2) A detailed list of the debts that he has not collected. Based on this list, the "Minister of Finance or the person authorized by the Minister of Finance in this regard" shall determine the amount to be charged against the Financial Collector to establish his responsibility and the amount that should continue to be collected. The Financial Collector shall be liable to pay the amount owing from his personal funds immediately.
