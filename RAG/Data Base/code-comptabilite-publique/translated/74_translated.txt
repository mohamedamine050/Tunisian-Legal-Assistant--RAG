Accountants shall collect the taxes and revenues entrusted to them during the same fiscal year in which the assignment was made. They must also prove the impossibility of collection if they fail to collect them before the end of the same year. This must be done in accordance with the requirements established for this purpose by the laws, regulations, and special instructions issued in this regard. Revenues not collected by the end of each year shall be carried over to the following fiscal year to be included in the revenues of that year, starting on the first of January.
