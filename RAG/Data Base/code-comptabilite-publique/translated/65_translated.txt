It is not permissible to borrow on behalf of the State or a public institution by issuing bonds with short, medium, or long terms, or by assuming loans contracted for the benefit of public or private entities, or by commitments undertaken by them, or by commitments payable at a future date or in annual installments, except within the limits prescribed by the Finance Law. It is also not permissible to make any modification to the form of the loans concluded or to the amount of interest determined for them, except within the same limits. The method of application of these provisions shall be regulated by decree.
