Salaries and wages shall also be paid through transfer, provided that the net amount of the monthly salary or wage exceeds an amount to be determined by "a decision of the Minister of Finance or by a person authorized by the Minister of Finance." The net amount refers to the remaining amount of the full monthly salary or wage after deducting amounts due to the Old Age and Social Security Fund, personal contributions, and it includes the full salary, family allowances, and generally, all grants awarded against actual costs.
