Every accountant shall submit an account of their management during their term of administration of the center. The accountant's administration encompasses all operations undertaken during the entire fiscal year or during their tenure within that year. The account shall indicate the status of the center at the beginning of the year or upon the accountant's assumption of office, the various types of operations completed during the year or the duration of the administration, and the status of the center at the end of the year or upon the termination of that period, with a statement of the funds and assets present at that time.
