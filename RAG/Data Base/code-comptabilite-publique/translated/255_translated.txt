The accountant shall prepare an annual financial statement for the administration of the institution at the end of each year. The financial statement shall be drawn up according to a model prescribed by "the Minister of Finance or the person delegated by the Minister of Finance for this purpose" and shall include, in particular, the following: 

* A final balance of all accounts.
* Detailed explanations for each category of receipts and expenses within the scope of the annual budget.
* Detailed explanations of the results of the institution's administration.
* A balance sheet of the institution's assets and liabilities at the end of the year.
