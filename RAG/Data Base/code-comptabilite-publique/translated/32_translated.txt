(1) The following exceptions apply: 

In the event that no one participates in the bidding or the bids submitted are unsatisfactory, the State, institution, or local community conducting the proceedings may request to purchase the property for its own benefit at the initial price set. In such cases, the State, institution, or local community acquiring the property is not required to pay the price immediately.

The price agreed upon by the contracting parties shall be paid in accordance with the procedures laid down in the laws or regulations of the State, institution, or local community, provided that it does not exceed or fall short of the value of the property sold.
