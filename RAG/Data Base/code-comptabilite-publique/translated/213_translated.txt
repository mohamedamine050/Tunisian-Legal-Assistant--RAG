The accounting for materials shall be handled by:

- The General Treasurer, in respect of bonds, vouchers and various values deposited with him.
- The Custodian of the Stamp Depot, in respect of printed materials, paper, and tax stamps.
- The heads of equipment services attached to the various ministries, the accountants in diplomatic and consular missions abroad, and the central accountant for state-owned property, in respect of other movable and immovable assets.

The state collects funds in the form of taxes, which are cash amounts paid by individuals and companies proportionally to their income or profits.
