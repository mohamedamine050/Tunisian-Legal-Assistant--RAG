Accountants are prohibited from delivering state-owned materials for which payment was made by check, as well as goods deposited as security for taxes paid in the same manner, to their owners, unless the check is certified by the issuing bank in accordance with the conditions stipulated in Article 350 of the Commercial Code, or after verification of the check payment by the bank. The accountant who fails to comply with the provisions of this section shall be held financially liable in the event the check is not paid by the bank.
