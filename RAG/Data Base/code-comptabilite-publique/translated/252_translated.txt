**English Translation:**

**The establishment of clearing agencies and payment agencies is carried out by a decision of the "Minister of Finance or his delegate," upon the request of the institution's director and the proposal of the minister responsible for supervision.** **The appointment of suitable individuals to positions** **that align with the qualifications and competencies required for their performance is** **determined in the same manner.** **The provisions of this Code governing the operations of government agencies apply to these agencies.**
