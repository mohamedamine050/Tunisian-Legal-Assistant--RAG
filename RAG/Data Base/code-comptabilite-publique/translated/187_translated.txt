The Financial Collector is mandatorily responsible for managing the funds of local communities within their respective areas. Additionally, they can be entrusted by a decision of the "Minister of Finance or the person authorized by the Minister of Finance in this regard" with the management of funds for other public institutions or entities. In both these cases, they must adhere to the specific regulations of those communities, institutions, or entities. They are also obliged to include, at the end of each month, a summary of all receipts and expenditures undertaken in their capacity within their extra-budgetary operations.
