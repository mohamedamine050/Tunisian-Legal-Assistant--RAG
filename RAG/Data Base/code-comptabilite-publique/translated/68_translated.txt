**(Added by Article 87-2 of Law No. 54 of 2013 dated December 30, 2013).**- A National Council for Public Accounting Standards is established, which is specifically and exceptionally affiliated with a particular nation or geographical region, distinct from other countries.  This Council is tasked with providing an advance opinion on public accounting standards applicable to the State, local authorities, and public institutions subject to the Public Accounting Code. The Minister of Finance, or the person designated by the Minister of Finance for this purpose, shall preside over this Council. The composition and operation of the National Council for Public Accounting Standards are regulated by a decree(2).
