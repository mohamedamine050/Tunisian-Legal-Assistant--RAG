(Amended by Law No. 42 of 1989 dated March 8, 1989). - Transactions aimed at purchasing materials, commissioning works or services, as well as studies contracts, for the benefit of the institution, are concluded according to the same forms and rules stipulated for transactions concluded for the benefit of the state. The composition of the committees entrusted with overseeing these transactions and the limits of their powers are determined by order.
