**(Added by Article 53 of Law No. 78 of 2016 dated December 17, 2016).** - The collection of Golan fees requires the following:

**Collection**

Collection is the process undertaken by the creditor to obtain payment from the debtor of the financial debt contracted towards them.

The fines incurred by the offender resulting from non-compliance with the provisions of the Road Traffic Code are subject to collection. If multiple fines are incurred, the offender is required to pay, at a minimum, the amount of the first three fines as of the date of the violation. The remaining fines are to be paid in installments until the end of the year in which the collection is initiated, under the heading "Golan Fees."
