**(Amended by Law No. 86 of 1996 dated November 6, 1996).** - Private collectors of the supplementary budget shall submit monthly tables of their operations and an annual account accompanied by supporting documents for expenses to the regional collector to whom they are affiliated. The regional collector of the supplementary budget shall review the annual accounts submitted by private collectors charged with the collection of expenses of the supplementary budget and certify them in conformity with their records before submitting them to the Court of Audit. Regional collectors of the supplementary budget shall submit monthly tables of their operations and an annual account accompanied by supporting documents for expenses to the central accountant for his examination and certification in conformity with their records before submitting them to the Court of Audit.
