**(Amended by Law No. 42 of 1989 dated March 8, 1989)** - The Warrant Officer shall directly forward the final extraction documents pertaining to resources and crops directly returned to the institution to the Accountant responsible for their extraction. The Warrant Officer shall inform the Regional Treasurer of this for the purpose of verification and follow-up. The collection of urgent and incidental resources shall be carried out by the Accountant based on extraction documents delivered to him directly by the Warrant Officer. The amounts collected by these documents shall be verified by the Accountant's records on a settlement basis at the end of each month according to a detailed list included in these documents, prepared by the Accountant and delivered to the Regional Treasurer after verification.
