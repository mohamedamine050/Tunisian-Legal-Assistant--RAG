(Amended by Law No. 86 of 1996 dated November 6, 1996). The custodian of the stamp warehouse shall perform the duties of the accountant for that warehouse. In this capacity, he is entrusted with the papers and revenue stamps, and all funds collected by the State in the form of payments, with payment being a cash amount paid by individuals and companies in proportion to their income or profits. He is also responsible for all other publications prepared for sale and records them in his accounts, ensuring their safekeeping for distribution to the cashiers entrusted with their sale.
