(Amended by Law No. 86 of 1996 dated November 6, 1996.) - Payment orders for state budget expenditures issued by the first paying officers are drawn on the funds of the custodians of expenses. Payment orders for treasury fund expenditures are drawn on the fund of the custodian of public funds. Payment orders issued by the assistant paying officers are drawn on the fund of the regional council's cashier belonging to their province or the area of their administrative headquarters unless the "Minister of Finance or his delegate" decides otherwise.
