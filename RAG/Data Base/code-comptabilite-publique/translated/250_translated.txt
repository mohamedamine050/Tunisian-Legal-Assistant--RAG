(Amended by Law No. 42 of 1989 dated March 8, 1989). - If the value of the purchase, sale or exchange exceeds an amount determined by a decision of the Prime Minister, in addition to what is mentioned in the preceding article above, authorization to conclude the contract shall be granted by the Prime Minister based on a report from the Minister of Finance or from someone authorized by the Minister of Finance.  Authorization of the Prime Minister shall also be required if the lease is for a period exceeding nine years.
