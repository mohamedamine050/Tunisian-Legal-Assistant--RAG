**(Added by Law No. 56 of 2018 dated December 27, 2018).** - Subject to the provisions of Articles 81 and 82 of this Code, in the case of remote payment, the public accountant shall not issue a receipt for amounts paid remotely, and the payer's liability shall be discharged by an electronic receipt bearing a unique reference to confirm the payment process. The payer may extract an electronic receipt containing codes that enable verification of the authenticity of the data included therein.
