(Amended by Law No. 86 of 1996 dated November 6, 1996). - The Regional Budget Collector attached to the duty of Chief Budget Accountant, in this capacity, is accountable to the Court of Auditors. In addition to any tasks entrusted to him according to the laws and regulations in force, he may be assigned the duties of Assistant Budget Disbursement Officer of the attached budget. In this case, the disbursement of expenses authorized for payment by him shall be the responsibility of a Special Budget Collector attached to the budget.
