(Amended by Law No. 86 of 1996 dated November 6, 1996).

The following individuals are considered State Accountants:

- The General Treasurer.

- The General Secretary of Expenditures.

- The Secretaries of Expenditures.

- The Finance Collectors.

- The Regional Treasurers.

- The Accountants at Diplomatic and Consular Missions abroad.

- The Keeper of the Stamp Treasury.

- The Central Accountant for State Private Property.

- The Customs Collector.

Furthermore, pursuant to decisions issued by the Minister of Finance or by those authorized by the Minister of Finance, the appointment of accountants to specific administrative departments to carry out designated accounting tasks in accordance with the provisions of this Code, is permitted.
