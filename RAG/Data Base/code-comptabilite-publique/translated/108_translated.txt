**(Amended by Law No. 43 of 2003 dated June 9, 2003).** - Payments arising from concluded contracts may be paid in installments. The terms and conditions for the grant of these installments shall be determined by an order. The contracting party may also be granted an advance payment. The percentage of this advance payment, the terms and conditions for its grant and recovery shall be determined by an order. The amount of the advance payment shall not exceed 20% of the original amount of the contract.
