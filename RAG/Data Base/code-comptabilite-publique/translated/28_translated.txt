**(Added by Article 77 of Law No. 85 of 2006 dated December 25, 2006).** - Notwithstanding the provisions of Article 28, Paragraph 5 of this Code, prior tracing actions for serving the writ of execution shall consist of sending a notice with a receipt of delivery to the debtor, if it is established that he has ceased his business activity or started dissipating his assets or if another creditor has taken enforcement measures against him or commenced proceedings for the distribution of his assets. The notice shall include the total amount claimed from the debtor with an invitation to pay within a maximum period of seven days from the date of receipt. The writ of execution shall be served and tracing actions shall commence upon expiry of this period.
