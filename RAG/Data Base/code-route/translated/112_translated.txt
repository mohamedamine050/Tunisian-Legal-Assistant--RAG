(Amended by Law No. 54 of 2006 dated July 28, 2006). - If the offender provides evidence of insurance of the fine amount within the period mentioned in Section 111 of this Code, the head of the security center or the National Guard to whom the matter is referred, who witnessed the violation, shall refer the report to the competent court. If the offender does not provide insurance within the period mentioned, he shall be considered to have waived his right to refer the report to the competent court, and the fine shall be subject to final collection. If the offender does not provide evidence of insurance within the period mentioned, the insurance he has taken out shall be considered to have been finally exhausted for the amount of the fine.
