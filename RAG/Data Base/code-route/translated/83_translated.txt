(Amended by Law No. 66 of 2009, dated August 12, 2009). - Violations of the provisions of this Code and its implementing texts are classified into three categories:

- Offences
- Misdemeanors
- Felonies

Offences are further classified into five categories, and each offender shall be punished with a fine of: 

- Six (06) dinars for offences of the first category
- Ten (10) dinars for offences of the second category
- Twenty (20) dinars for offences of the third category
- Forty (40) dinars for offences of the fourth category
- Sixty (60) dinars for offences of the fifth category

The list of offences shall be regulated by an order.
