If it is established that the driver does not hold a driver's license or the required category, he shall be barred from taking the driving license examination for a period of:

- Two years in the event of a traffic accident resulting in death or bodily injury. 
- One year in the event of committing any of the misdemeanors stipulated in numbers 1, 2, 4, 8, 9, and 10 of Article 85, in numbers 3 to 7 of the third paragraph of Article 86, and in numbers 1, 3, 4, and 5 of Article 87 of this Code (amended by Law No. 66 of 2009).
- Six months when he has not committed another crime stipulated in this Code or in the implementing order referred to in Article 83 thereof. This period shall be counted from the date of the offense or from the date of reaching the legal age if the offense was committed before reaching this age.
