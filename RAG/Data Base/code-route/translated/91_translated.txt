The prison sentence of ten (10) years is increased if it is proven that the driver causing unintentional killing or injury has attempted to flee, thereby trying to evade criminal responsibility.

**Criminal responsibility** is the legal obligation to be held accountable for committed crimes, which includes penalties or sanctions in the event of conviction.

**Civil responsibility** is the material aspect of civilization, such as infrastructure, communication, and entertainment, which is contrasted with the intellectual, spiritual, and ethical aspects of civilization.
