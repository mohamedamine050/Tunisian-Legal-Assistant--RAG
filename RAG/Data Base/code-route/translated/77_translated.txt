No one shall be permitted to drive a motor vehicle unless they hold a driving license issued by the competent authorities of the Ministry responsible for transport. The categories of driving licenses, conditions for their issuance, validity, and renewal shall be determined by order. The provisions of this chapter shall apply to motorcycles, except that with regard to small motorcycles as defined in Chapter II of this Code, their drivers shall be required to undergo an examination in the knowledge of traffic rules. The provisions of this chapter shall not apply to drivers while they are training or taking their driving test on the road.
