(Amended by Law No. 54 of 2006 dated July 28, 2006). - The fine amount is doubled if it is not paid within 15 days from the date of the violation. Upon the expiration of one month from the date of the violation, without the payment of the fine, the financial collector shall notify the National Register of Golan Crimes. From that date, the driving license is considered suspended and invalid for driving until the fine is paid or the situation is settled. The claim for the collection of fines arising from violations stipulated in the second paragraph of Article 83 of this Code shall lapse after five years from the date of the violation.
