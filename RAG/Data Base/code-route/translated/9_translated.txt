Every driver of a vehicle, when driving normally, shall drive their vehicle close to the right edge of the roadway, even if the roadway is empty, to the extent permitted by the condition of the roadway or its engineering characteristics or congestion. However, in cases of heavy traffic, driving in parallel rows on a roadway with two or more lanes in one direction is permitted.  In the case of ordinary driving, a driver of animals shall lead their animals close to the right edge of the right shoulder of the road, or close to the right edge of the roadway, if the driving of animals is permitted.
