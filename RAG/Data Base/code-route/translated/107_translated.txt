If an offense results in a violation described as a felony, as defined by this law, the crimes that require a punishment of death or imprisonment for more than five years are considered felonies.

Crimes that require a punishment of imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years or a fine exceeding sixty dinars are considered misdemeanors.

Crimes that require a punishment of no more than fifteen days imprisonment or a fine of sixty dinars are considered infractions. [Article 122 of the Code of Criminal Procedure]

If the provisions of this Code result in damage to public roads or their appurtenances, the perpetrator shall be sentenced, in addition to the penalties provided for in this Code, to pay the costs of repair.
