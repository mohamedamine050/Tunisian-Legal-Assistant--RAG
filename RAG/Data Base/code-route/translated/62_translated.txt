Every car, trailer, and semi-trailer, prior to being placed in circulation, shall be subject to acceptance and certification by the competent authorities of the Ministry responsible for transport, to ensure that it conforms to the current dimensions and specifications.

No substantial changes may be made to any car, trailer, or semi-trailer without prior authorization from the competent authorities of the Ministry responsible for transport. In this case, these vehicles shall be subject to a separate acceptance procedure. The conditions for applying this provision shall be determined by a decree of the Minister responsible for transport.
