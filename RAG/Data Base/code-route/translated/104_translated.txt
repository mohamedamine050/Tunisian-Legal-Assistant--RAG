The seizure of the vehicle is the authorization issued by the officers referred to in Article 100, as a precautionary measure, to the driver of the vehicle to seize his vehicle at the scene of the crime or nearby, taking into account the rules of parking. The vehicle remains, during its seizure, under the legal custody of its driver or owner. The procedures for applying precautionary seizure are regulated in certain cases stipulated by Article 105 by decree.
