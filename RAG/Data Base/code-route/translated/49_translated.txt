Prior authorization, by decision of the Minister in charge of Equipment after consulting with the concerned local authorities, shall be required for the exceptional transfer, conversion, or management of:

-  Indivisible objects,
-  Public works equipment,
-  Agricultural equipment,
-  Vehicles and towed trailers intended for carrying indivisible objects,

provided that the dimensions or weights of the aforementioned objects, equipment, and trailers exceed the legal limits set forth in this law. If the transfer takes place within the area of a single local authority and on its roads, this prior authorization shall be granted by the concerned local authority. The conditions for applying this article shall be determined by decree.
