Every vehicle or group of vehicles used in international transit is subject to the provisions of this Code, except for exceptions resulting from an agreement between the concerned governments. A vehicle is considered to be in international transit in Tunisian territory:

- If it is owned by a natural person or legal entity whose registered office is located outside Tunisian territory - if it is not registered in Tunisia

- If it is imported temporarily. A group of vehicles is considered to be in international transit if at least one of the vehicles composing it meets the definition set out in the preceding paragraph.
