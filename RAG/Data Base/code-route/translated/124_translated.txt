(Added by Law No. 101 of 2001 dated October 22, 2001). 
- Failure to register a vehicle within fifteen days and no later than three months from the date of signing the vehicle ownership transfer document by its owner as identified in this certificate or by the person authorized to sell it, or from the date of obtaining the registration certificate issued by customs authorities, shall result in the payment of a late fee of twenty dinars for each month or part thereof. This fee shall be collected for the benefit of the Tunisian Public Treasury.
