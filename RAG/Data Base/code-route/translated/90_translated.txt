A person who commits involuntary manslaughter resulting from a traffic accident due to failure to take the necessary precautions while driving shall be punished by imprisonment for a maximum term of one year and one month and a fine of a maximum of one thousand and one hundred (1,100) dinars, or by one of these penalties only.

The punishment shall be imprisonment for a maximum term of three years and a fine of a maximum of three thousand (3,000) dinars, or by one of these penalties only, if the accident resulted from the commission of one of the crimes mentioned in Articles 83, 84, 85, 86, 87, and 88 of this Code. The prison sentence shall be increased to five years and the fine to five thousand (5,000) dinars if it is proven that civil liability was not insured, or if the driver at the time of the accident:

- Was under the influence of alcohol.

- Did not hold a driver's license or the required type for driving the vehicle.

- Was driving contrary to the decision to revoke the driver's license.
