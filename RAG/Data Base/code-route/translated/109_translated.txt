Any person who is subject to a **judgment** arising from one of the crimes listed in this Code, excluding ordinary offenses, and who commits a **crime** described as a felony within the meaning of this law, namely crimes that carry a punishment of death or imprisonment for more than five years, shall, upon being subject to a new **judgment** of the same kind within twelve months from the date of the issuance of the final **judgment**, be subject to the maximum penalties of imprisonment and fine, or the maximum of either. 

Crimes described as misdemeanors are those that carry a punishment of imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or a fine exceeding sixty dinars.

Crimes described as offenses are those that carry a punishment not exceeding fifteen days of imprisonment or a fine of sixty dinars. (Article 122 of the Code of Criminal Procedure)
