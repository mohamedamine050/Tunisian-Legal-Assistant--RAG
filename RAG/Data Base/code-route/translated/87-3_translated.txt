(Added by Law No. 67 of 2001 dated July 10, 2001). - The activity of a driving school or a center specializing in driving training shall be definitively suspended in the following cases:

- Non-compliance with the provisions of the specifications relating to the professional competence requirement that must be met by the natural person or the legal representative of the legal entity operating the institution or center. - A judgment sentencing

to imprisonment for more than three months without suspension of execution

or for more than six months with suspension of execution against the natural person or the legal representative of the legal entity operating the institution or center, or a judgment

of bankruptcy, and has not recovered his rights. - Acquisition of the status of an employee of the State or local public authorities with an administrative nature or public establishments as defined by the current legislation, in respect of the natural person or the legal representative of the legal entity operating the institution or center.
