The equipping of any vehicle with lighting or signaling devices not specified in the current regulations is prohibited. These provisions do not apply to the interior lighting devices of the vehicle, provided that such devices do not cause inconvenience to other drivers. The use of any advertising by means of light or reflective signals on vehicles is prohibited. The conditions for advertising on vehicles are determined by a decision of the Minister in charge of Transport. (Added by Law No. 66 of 2009 dated August 12, 2009)
