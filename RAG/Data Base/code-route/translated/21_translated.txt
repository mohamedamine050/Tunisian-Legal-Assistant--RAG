## English Translation:

**Contrary to the rule stipulated in Article sixteen (16) of this Code, overtaking to the right of a vehicle is permissible if its driver announces or commences a change of direction to the left, in accordance with the conditions set forth in Article nineteen of this Code. Overtaking a vehicle traveling on a railway track extending on the road should be done on the right.**

**The oath referred to by one party to another in a dispute, to resolve a disagreement where legal evidence to support the claim is lacking, is a matter of conscience and religious obligation of the party taking the oath.**

**If the distance between the vehicle and the edge of the road is sufficient, overtaking on the left is permissible in the following cases:**

- **On roads where traffic flows in one direction.**
- **On other roads, if overtaking will leave the entire left half of the road clear.**
