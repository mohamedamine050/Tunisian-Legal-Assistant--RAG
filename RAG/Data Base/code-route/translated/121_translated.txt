The provisions of Articles 48 and 49 of this Code shall not apply to military convoys and military transport, which are subject to special regulations. The technical regulations referred to in Article 61 of this Code shall not apply to vehicles and equipment belonging to the National Army. The administrative regulations contained in Articles 62, 63, 64, and 65 shall not apply to vehicles and equipment belonging to the National Army that are subject to special registration and accepted by the technical services of the National Defence. The provisions of Articles 77 to 88 and 92 to 103 of this Code shall not apply to military convoys and military transport, which are subject to special regulations.
