(Amended by Law No. 66 of 2009 dated August 12, 2009). -  A driving license shall be withdrawn for a maximum period of six months upon the commission of one of the misdemeanors stipulated in numbers 1 and 2 of Article 92 of this Code. In these two cases, the report shall be referred within seven days to the competent technical committee, which shall consider it within one month from the date of the offense. The driving license shall be withdrawn for a maximum period of one year in the event of the commission of one of the misdemeanors stipulated in numbers 1 and 2 of Article 92 of this Code, within one year from the date of the expiration of the withdrawal period, pursuant to the first paragraph of this Article. The withdrawal period shall be doubled in the event of driving contrary to the provisions of the withdrawal decision, in respect of all cases mentioned in this Article and in Article 94 of this Code.
