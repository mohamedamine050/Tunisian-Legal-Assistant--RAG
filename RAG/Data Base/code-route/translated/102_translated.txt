The following offenses related to the Golan are proven through devices and methods, whose specification and conditions of use shall be determined by an Order:

- Exceeding the maximum permissible speed.
- Exceeding the driving duration indicated in Article 8 of this Code.
- Exceeding the authorized gross weight, authorized gross carrying weight, and legal axle load.
- Driving under the influence of alcohol.
- Pollution and noise emitted by vehicles.
