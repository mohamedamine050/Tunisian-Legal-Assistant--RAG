The Minister in charge of Postal Services shall bring public actions, except for the crimes stipulated in Article 29 of this Code.

**Footnotes:**

[1] An action is a request by one party to another to have their rights recognized and enforced by a judge. The party making the request is called the plaintiff, and the party against whom the request is made is called the defendant. 

[2] An action is the right of any person to seek a judicial decision in their favor. For the defendant, it is the right to present defenses or arguments to refute the plaintiff's claim.
