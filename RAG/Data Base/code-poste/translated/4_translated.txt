Within the framework of postal activity as defined by this law, the provision of postal services is subject to prior licensing by the Minister in charge of Post. The conditions and procedure for the granting and revocation of this license shall be regulated by an order. These services shall be provided in accordance with a specification document, which must include the method of its authentication by a decision of the Minister in charge of Post. Public postal centers are excluded from the provisions of this chapter and their operation is subject to the requirements of a specification document approved by a decision of the Minister in charge of Post. The third paragraph was added by Law No. 40 of 2007 dated June 25, 2007.
