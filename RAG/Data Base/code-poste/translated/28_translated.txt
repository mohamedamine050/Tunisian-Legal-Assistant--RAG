In addition to the administrative penalties outlined in Article 27 of this Code, any intervener who has obtained a license in accordance with the forms set out in this Code, who has not respected the conditions for the exercise of postal activity and the requirements of the terms of reference stipulated in Article 4 of this Code, or has failed to ensure the provision of the basic postal services entrusted to him, shall be punished with a fine ranging from 1,000 to 10,000 dinars.
