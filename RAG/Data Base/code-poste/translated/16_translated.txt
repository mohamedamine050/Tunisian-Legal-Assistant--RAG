Except as provided in Article 17 of this Code, interveners shall be liable for:

- The obligation to respond to the acts of the person before the law, or
- Compensation for damages caused by the loss, damage, or deterioration of registered mail and mail with declared value. 

The minimum amount of the compensation due to the sender shall be fixed by order, or at the sender's request, to the addressee, as well as the procedures and deadlines for its payment.
