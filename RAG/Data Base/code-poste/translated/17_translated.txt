## English Translation:

**Interveners shall be exempt from the liability referred to in Article 16 of this Code in the following cases:**

- In the case of force majeure or unforeseen circumstances. 
- When it is proven that the damage resulted from the sender's failure to comply with the usual rules of packaging or from the nature of the contents of the correspondence.
- When it is proven that the sender intentionally declared a value exceeding the actual value of the contents of the correspondence.
- In the case of the seizure of correspondence by the competent authorities in accordance with the applicable legislation.
