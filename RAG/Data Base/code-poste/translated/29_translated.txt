Except for the cases stipulated in Article 10 of this Code or other laws, anyone who discloses, incites, or participates in the disclosure of the content of correspondence belonging to another person shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of Article 253 of the Penal Code. The same penalty shall be applied to anyone who violates the sanctity of correspondence by deliberately changing its destination, destroying it, disposing of it, or illegally seizing it temporarily or permanently.
