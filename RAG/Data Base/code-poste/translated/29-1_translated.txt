**(Added by Basic Law No. 43 of 2001 dated May 3, 2001, after being withdrawn from the Press Code)** - Imprisonment for a term of 16 days to six months and a fine of 120 Dinars to 1200 Dinars or one of these penalties only shall be imposed upon anyone who, by mail, sends an open correspondence containing slanderous attacks, either against individuals, bodies, or persons specified in articles 48, 51 to 53 of this Code. If the correspondence 

contains insults, the sender shall be punished by imprisonment for a term of 16 days to two months and a fine of 120 Dinars to 1200 Dinars, or one of these penalties only.
