## English Translation: 

The State protects the right to privacy, the sanctity of the home, and the confidentiality of correspondence, communications, and personal data.

**Citizen:**
A citizen is any individual who enjoys all rights, be they civil, political, economic, social, cultural, or solidarity rights in the state to which they belong.

Every citizen has the freedom to choose their place of residence, to move freely within the country, and the right to leave it.
