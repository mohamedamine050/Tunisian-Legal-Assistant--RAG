The National Assembly shall consider the proposed amendment after a decision by it by an absolute majority, and after its subject matter and study by a special committee. 

The special committee shall employ a formal method for assembling individuals with relevant expertise from different parts of the institution.

In the event that a referendum is not held, the draft constitutional amendment shall be approved by the National Assembly by a two-thirds majority of its members in two readings, the second of which shall take place at least three months after the first.
