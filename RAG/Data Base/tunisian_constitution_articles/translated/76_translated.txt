## English Translation:

**The provisions that do not fall within the scope of the law revert to the general legislative authority, and the previous texts related to these provisions may be amended by a decree.**

**It shall be mandatory to present to the Administrative Court, the Administrative Court, or any court that deals with matters related to public administration, and a decision shall be issued based on its opinion.**

**The President of the Republic may object to any draft law or any draft amendment that includes an intervention in the realm of general legislative authority.**

**The President of the Republic shall refer the matter to the Constitutional Court for a decision within a maximum period of ten days from the date it reaches the Court.**
