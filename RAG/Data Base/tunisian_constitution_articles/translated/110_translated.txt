The President of the Republic enjoys immunity throughout his term of office. All statutes of limitations and bars to action against him are suspended. 

**Statutes of limitations** refer to the loss of the right to pursue a claim due to failure to meet deadlines.

The procedures may be appealed after the end of his term. 

**Appeal** is a legal process that allows the chosen party to request a review of the judgment issued by the previous court.

The President of the Republic is not liable for actions taken in the performance of his duties.
