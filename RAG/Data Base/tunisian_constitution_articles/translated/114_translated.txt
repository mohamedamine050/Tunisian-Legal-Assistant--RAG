## English Translation:

Members of the government have the right to attend the House of People's Representatives and the National Council of Regions and Governorates, whether during a plenary session or within the framework of committees. 

Every member of the House of People's Representatives or the National Council of Regions and Governorates may address members of the government with written or oral questions. 

The House of People's Representatives and the National Council of Regions and Governorates may invite the government or a member thereof to a dialogue on the pursued policies and the results achieved or pursued.
