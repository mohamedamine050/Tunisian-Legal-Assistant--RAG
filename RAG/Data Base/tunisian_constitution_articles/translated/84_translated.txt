## English Translation:

**The National Council of Regions and Provinces shall be presented, as a matter of obligation, with draft legislation concerning the state budget, as well as regional, provincial, and national development plans.**

**This ensures balance between the regions and provinces.**

**No law, financial law, or development plan shall be ratified unless approved by a majority of members present in both councils, this majority being no less than one-third of the members of each council.** 

**The law is a rule agreed upon by a competent authority that regulates the behavior of citizens and institutions.**
