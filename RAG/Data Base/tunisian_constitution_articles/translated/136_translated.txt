## English Translation:

The President of the Republic or at least one-third of the members of the House of Representatives have the right to request a constitutional amendment, provided that this amendment does not affect the republican system, the form of the state, or the number of presidential terms and their duration. 

The President of the Republic may submit constitutional amendment proposals to a referendum.

Any initiative to amend the Constitution shall be submitted by the initiating party to the Constitutional Court for a decision on the matters that cannot be amended as stipulated in this Constitution.
