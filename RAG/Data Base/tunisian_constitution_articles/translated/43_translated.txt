## English Translation:

**Health**

Health is a state of complete physical, mental, and social well-being and is a right for every human being. 

The state guarantees the prevention and provision of healthcare for every citizen, and provides the necessary resources to ensure the safety and quality of healthcare services.

The state guarantees free treatment for those without means of support and those with limited income. It also guarantees the right to social coverage as regulated by law.
