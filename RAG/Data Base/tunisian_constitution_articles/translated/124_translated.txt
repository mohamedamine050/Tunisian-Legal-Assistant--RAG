## English Translation: 

**Everyone has the right to a fair and public hearing within a reasonable time by an independent and impartial tribunal established by law. The parties to a dispute are equal before the courts.**

**The right to a fair trial and the right to defense are guaranteed. The law facilitates access to justice and ensures legal aid for those who are financially unable to afford it.**

**The judge is an official who makes legal decisions in court and contributes to the enforcement of the law and the achievement of justice.**

**Public hearings are held by competent bodies, in accordance with the procedural rules outlined in this document, to obtain specific information regarding the actions or activities of the public administration. This is to ensure the soundness, integrity, transparency, and conformity of performance with the requirements of the public interest.**

**Courts are public unless the law requires secrecy. Judgments are only pronounced in a public hearing.**
