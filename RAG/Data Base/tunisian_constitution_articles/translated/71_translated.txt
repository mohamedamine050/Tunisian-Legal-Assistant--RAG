## English Translation:

The House of People's Representatives shall hold an ordinary session commencing in October and ending in July, with the first session of the legislative term of the House of People's Representatives commencing within a maximum of fifteen days from the date of the announcement of the final election results, upon the invitation of the outgoing Speaker or, in the event of the dissolution of the House of People's Representatives, upon the invitation of the President of the Republic.

In the event that the commencement of the first session of the legislative term of the House of People's Representatives coincides with its annual recess, an extraordinary session shall be convened for a period of fifteen days.

Furthermore, the House of People's Representatives may convene during its recess for an extraordinary session upon the request of the President of the Republic or one-third of its members, to consider a specific agenda.
