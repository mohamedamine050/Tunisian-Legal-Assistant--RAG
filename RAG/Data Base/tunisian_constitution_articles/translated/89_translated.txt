## English Translation:

The candidacy for the office of President of the Republic is a right for every Tunisian man or woman who does not hold another nationality, born to a father and mother, or to a father, or to a mother, who are all Tunisians without interruption.

The candidate must be at least forty years old on the date of submission of his/her candidacy and must enjoy all his/her civil and political rights.

The submission of candidacy shall be made to the Independent High Authority for Elections in accordance with the procedures and conditions stipulated by the electoral law.
