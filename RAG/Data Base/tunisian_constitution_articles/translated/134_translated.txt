The High Independent Electoral Commission shall manage, organize, and supervise all stages of elections and referendums, ensuring the integrity, fairness, and transparency of the electoral process and declaring the results.

The Commission shall have regulatory powers in its area of competence.

The Commission shall be composed of nine independent, neutral members with competence and integrity, who shall serve for a six-year term not subject to renewal. One-third of its members shall be renewed every two years.
