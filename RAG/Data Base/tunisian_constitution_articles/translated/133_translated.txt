## English Translation:

**Municipal Councils**

Municipal Councils are local communities endowed with legal personality, administrative and financial autonomy. They are responsible for managing municipal affairs in accordance with the principle of free administration. They work towards the economic, social, cultural, environmental, and urban development of the region. They provide services to their constituents, listen to the concerns of their residents, and involve them in the management of local affairs. 

**Regional Councils, Provincial Councils, and other entities granted the status of local communities by law** exercise local and regional powers as defined by law.
