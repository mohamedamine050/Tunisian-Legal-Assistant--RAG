The National Council of Regions and Provinces shall be composed of elected deputies from the regions and provinces.

The members of each Regional Council shall elect three members from among themselves to represent their region in the National Council of Regions and Provinces.

The elected members of the Regional Councils in each province shall elect one deputy from among themselves to represent this province in the National Council of Regions and Provinces.

The deputy representing the province shall be compensated in accordance with the provisions of the electoral law.
