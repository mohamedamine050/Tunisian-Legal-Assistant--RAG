## English Translation:

**Work is a right for every citizen.**

A citizen is defined as any individual who enjoys all rights, whether civil, political, economic, social, cultural, or solidarity rights within the state to which they belong. The state shall take the necessary measures to ensure this right based on efficiency and fairness.

**Every citizen has the right to work in decent conditions and receive fair wages.**
