## English Translation:

The elected President shall take the following oath before the House of Representatives, the National Council of Regions and Provinces, assembled together:

"I swear by Almighty God to preserve the independence and integrity of the nation, to respect the Constitution, and to uphold the interests of the nation with the utmost care."

If, for any reason, the President is unable to take the oath before the House of Representatives and the National Council of Regions and Provinces, the President shall take it before the Constitutional Court.

The President shall not hold any other office, including any political party office.
