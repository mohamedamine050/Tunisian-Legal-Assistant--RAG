(Amended by Law No. 65 of 2005, dated July 27, 2005)

If a public invitation to contribute to savings has not been issued, the provisions of Chapter I of Book IV of this Code shall apply, except for Article 163, paragraphs 3, 4, and 5 of Article 164, and numbers (5) and (7) of paragraph 1 of Article 167, and Article 175.
