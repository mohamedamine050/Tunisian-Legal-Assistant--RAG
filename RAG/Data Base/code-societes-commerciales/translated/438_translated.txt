Without prejudice to the penalties provided for in the legislation in force, imprisonment for a period ranging from one to five years and a fine of one thousand to ten thousand dinars, or one of these two penalties only, shall be imposed on:

- Anyone who intentionally provides false or misleading information that has had an impact on the completion of a merger, demerger, or change of company form.  - Anyone who effects a merger, demerger, or change of company form for the purpose of obtaining a dominant position in the domestic market that is likely to impede or prevent the normal operation of competition rules.
