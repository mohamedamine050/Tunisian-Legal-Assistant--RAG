The creation of investment certificates and voting rights certificates shall be offered simultaneously to all shareholders in proportion to their shareholding in the capital, in the event of a split of the existing shares.  Any remaining capacity for the creation of unallocated certificates at the end of a deadline set by the extraordinary general meeting shall be distributed among the shareholders who requested to benefit from this additional allocation, in proportion to their shareholding in the capital and within the limits of their requests.  The board of directors or the collective management body shall then distribute any remaining potential quantity.
