The company may be nullified if a partner promised to contribute an in-kind contribution to a company in the process of formation and this contribution was lost before its delivery.  The company shall be dissolved if the property granted for exploitation is damaged before its delivery. In both cases, the representative of this company is required to convene the constitutive general meeting, in accordance with the conditions stipulated in the constitutive contract, to deliberate on the continuation or dissolution of the company.
