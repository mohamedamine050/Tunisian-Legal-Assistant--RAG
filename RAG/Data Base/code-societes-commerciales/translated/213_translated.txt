The chairman and managing director is elected by the board of directors from among its members in companies and institutions.  The chairman and managing director of the company is considered a trader in the application of the provisions of this Code. In the event of a judgment declaring the company bankrupt, the chairman and managing director is disqualified from carrying out any business function for a period of five years from the date of the bankruptcy judgment. (Amended by Law No. 36 of 2016 dated April 29, 2016).

The same ruling applies to the deputy managing director or the delegated member of the board of directors if the chairman and managing director is unable to perform his duties. (Amended by Law No. 36 of 2016 dated April 29, 2016).
