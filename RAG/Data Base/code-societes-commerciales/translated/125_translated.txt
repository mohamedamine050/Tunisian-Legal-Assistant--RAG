(Amended in the first paragraph by Law No. 96 of 2005 dated October 18, 2005)

Taking into account the provisions of Article 13 bis of this Code, in all cases, the appointment of the appropriate individual to the position that matches the conditions and requirements for performing it in terms of qualifications and competencies shall be made.  Auditors shall be appointed for a period of three years. Their powers and responsibilities, as well as the conditions of their dismissal and their salaries, shall be determined in accordance with the provisions of Articles 258 to 273 of this Code.  This also applies to the incompatibility regime and the system of prohibitions.
