In the event that convertible warrants are issued, the holders of investment certificates, within the limit of the number of warrants they hold, shall have a preferential subscription right to those warrants, which is not subject to reduction. They may waive this right during a special meeting convened and held in accordance with the rules governing extraordinary general meetings of shareholders.  These warrants may only be converted into investment certificates.  The updated voting certificates issued with the investment certificates issued upon conversion of the warrants shall be granted to the holders of voting certificates, within the limit of their respective rights, except in the case of their waiver in favor of all the holders or some of them. The aforementioned voting certificates shall be granted at the end of each financial year in respect of warrants convertible at any time.
