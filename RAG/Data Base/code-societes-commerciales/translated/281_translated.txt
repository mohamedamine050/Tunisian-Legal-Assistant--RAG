The general meeting shall be chaired by the person appointed in the constitutive contract. In case of his inability, the chairmanship shall be entrusted to the Chairman of the Board of Directors or the Chairman of the collective management body. If necessary, it shall be entrusted to a shareholder chosen by the partners present. The chairman of the general meeting shall be assisted by two persons and a clerk who shall constitute the meeting's secretariat, appointed by those present.
