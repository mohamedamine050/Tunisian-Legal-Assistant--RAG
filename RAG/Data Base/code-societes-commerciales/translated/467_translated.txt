A non-share company cannot own shares in the capital of a share company exceeding ten percent.  In the event of a violation, the crimes described in this law that warrant punishment by death or imprisonment exceeding five years are classified as felonies.  Crimes that warrant imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or a fine exceeding sixty dinars, are classified as misdemeanors.  Crimes warranting punishment not exceeding fifteen days imprisonment or sixty dinars fine are classified as infractions.  Pursuant to the provisions of the first paragraph of this chapter, the company that has acquired these shares is obligated to inform the other company thereof within a period not exceeding fifteen days from the date of acquisition and to divest itself of those shares within a period not exceeding one year from the date of acquisition. It cannot exercise the voting rights associated with those shares until the divestiture is completed.
