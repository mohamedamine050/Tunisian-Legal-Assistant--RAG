The report relating to the actions of the group of companies shall specifically include the following data:

- The situation of each company included in the consolidated financial statements,

- The expected development of the situation of the group of companies,

- The various activities in the field of research and development and investment relating to the group of companies,

- The significant events that occurred between the date of closing the consolidated accounts and the date on which they were prepared,

- The modifications made to the contributions held in the consolidated companies.
