Agreements concluded directly or indirectly between the company and one of its agents or a member of the "Board of Directors" are subject to the provisions applicable to agreements concluded by the company's de facto managers.  The foregoing paragraph applies to agreements between the company and any entity in which one of the agents or a member of the company's "Board of Directors" is an owner, a partner with unlimited liability, an agent, a board member, a member of a collective management body, or a general manager. The "Board of Directors" must approve the agreement.
