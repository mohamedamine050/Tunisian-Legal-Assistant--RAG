The company shall be in liquidation, a process of settling debts and distributing assets in the event of bankruptcy, from the date of its dissolution, regardless of the reason.  The company name must be followed by the phrase "Company in Liquidation" in all documents issued by it. However, the legal personality of the company remains in existence until the completion of liquidation proceedings. The company cannot oppose third parties in this regard except from the day following the publication of the company's dissolution in the Official Gazette of the Tunisian Republic after its registration in the Commercial Register.
