(Amended by the first chapter of Law No. 65 of 2005, dated July 27, 2005)

Every member of the supervisory board of a limited liability company must inform the company's legal representative of his appointment as an agent  — a person acting on behalf of another person — or as a member of the board of directors, chief executive officer, general manager, member of a collective management body, or member of the supervisory board of another company, within one month of his appointment. The company's legal representative must inform the ordinary general meeting of shareholders thereof at its first meeting. The provisions of the second paragraph of Article 192 of this Code apply.
