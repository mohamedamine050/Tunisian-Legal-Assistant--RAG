The beneficiary companies shall be jointly and severally liable to all creditors of the divided company for the debts relating to the divided company,

whatever their nature, whether settled or unsettled, regardless of the value of the portion allocated to each company.  Their substitution shall not constitute

a novation vis-à-vis the creditors of the aforementioned company.  Creditors’ objections, whatever the nature of their debt, shall be subject to the same conditions required in the case of merger, in accordance with the provisions of

Articles 419 and subsequent articles of this Code.
