The liquidator must, before the expiry of his mandate, convene the general meeting and submit to it the liquidation accounts, together with a report on the related operations.  Each partner may receive, before the meeting, the company's documents according to the provisions of the constitutive contract or in accordance with the provisions of this Code.  In the event that the general meeting is not convened, any interested party may apply to the Urgent Judge to appoint a representative charged with convening the general meeting for that purpose.
