The Supervisory Board shall elect from among its members a Chairperson and a Vice-Chairperson, who shall be responsible for convening the Board and conducting its deliberations.  The Board may determine their remuneration. The Chairperson and Vice-Chairperson of the Supervisory Board shall be natural persons; otherwise, their appointment shall be void. They shall perform their duties for the entire duration of the Supervisory Board's term.
