(Added by Law No. 96 of 2005 dated October 18, 2005)

The managing bodies and those in charge of the financial and accounting affairs of commercial companies subject, by virtue of the provisions of this Code, to the appointment of one or more auditors registered on the register of the Tunisian National Body of Accounting Experts, shall sign an annual declaration submitted to the auditors, attesting that they exercised the necessary diligence to ensure the completeness and compliance of the financial statements with accounting legislation. The content of this declaration shall be determined by a decision issued by the Ministry of Finance.
