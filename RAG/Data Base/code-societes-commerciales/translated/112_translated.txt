A natural person, or several natural persons, may manage a limited liability company.  A natural person is a legal term referring to a human being recognized as possessing legal personality; that is, the capacity to exercise a certain number of rights and take legal actions. The agent or agents may be among the partners or outsiders appointed by the constitutive contract or subsequent instruments. If the constitutive contract or the appointment decision does not specify the term of the agency, it shall be for three years, renewable. The agent shall represent the company before third parties and before the courts, whether the company is a plaintiff or a defendant.
