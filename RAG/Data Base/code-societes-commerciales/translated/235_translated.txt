The Supervisory Board shall continuously monitor the company's operations managed by the Collective Management Body.  The Supervisory Board may, at any time of the year, undertake such monitoring activities as it deems appropriate and may request any documents it considers necessary to fulfill its duties. The Collective Management Body must submit a written report to the Supervisory Board at least once every three months.  It must also submit its report on the annual financial statements to the Supervisory Board for review and audit within three months of the close of each year. The Supervisory Board shall present its observations on the report of the Collective Management Body, as well as on the annual accounts, to the General Meeting.
