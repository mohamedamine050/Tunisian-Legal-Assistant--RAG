The minutes of the general meeting shall state the following:

- The date and place of the meeting, the method of convening it, its agenda, the composition of the board, the number of shares participating in the vote, and the quorum. - The documents and reports submitted to the general meeting. - A summary of the discussions and the text of the resolutions put to the vote and the result thereof.  The members of the board shall sign these minutes; if any member abstains, this shall be stated therein.
