(Amended by the first chapter of Law No. 65 of 2005, dated July 27, 2005)

The valuation of the assets and liabilities submitted for referral shall be carried out by a specialized expert registered on the list of judicial experts,

in the same manner as adopted in the merger and under his personal responsibility. The extraordinary general meeting of the company benefiting from the division shall issue a decision approving or rejecting the in-kind share valued by

the expert.
