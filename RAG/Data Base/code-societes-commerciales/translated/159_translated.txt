A partner in a sole proprietorship with limited liability shall be punished by a fine ranging from five hundred to five thousand dinars who:

1) Failed to prepare an annual inventory of the valuation and accounts and a report on transactions in accordance with the provisions of Article 153 of this Code.

2) Failed, within three months following the date of preparation of the "financial statements," to take the necessary legal measures when the company's losses equal or exceed one-third of its social assets.
