All acts and documents issued by the grouping and addressed to third parties, particularly letters, invoices, announcements, and various advertisements, must clearly indicate the name of the grouping followed by the words "Economic Interest Grouping" or the abbreviation "EIG". In the case of liquidation, the process of settling debts and distributing assets in the event of bankruptcy, these acts and documents must, after the name, include the designation "Economic Interest Grouping in Liquidation".
