Merger between companies, one or all of which are in liquidation, is possible.  Liquidation being the process of settling debts and distributing assets in the event of bankruptcy, provided that the distribution of its assets among the partners has not commenced.  A merger between companies, one or all of which are subject to rescue procedures, is also possible based on a judicial authorization. In all cases, the companies concerned must observe the formal rules prescribed for the new company resulting from the merger.
