The transfer of shares is not permissible without the consent of all partners. However, the constitutive contract may include the following:

1. That the transfer of shares of partners contributing capital is free among the partners.
2. That the transfer of shares of partners contributing capital to non-partners is permissible, provided that it has the consent of all partners contributing work and a majority of partners contributing capital, both in number and contribution.
3. A partner contributing work may transfer part of his shares to a partner contributing capital or to a non-partner, subject to the same conditions stipulated in the second paragraph of this article.
