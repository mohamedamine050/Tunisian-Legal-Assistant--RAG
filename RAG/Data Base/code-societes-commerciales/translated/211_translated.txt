The Chairman of the Board of Directors shall manage the company's general administration under his responsibility, and he shall represent it in its dealings with third parties.  The Chairman of the Board of Directors enjoys broad powers to act in all cases in the name of the company and within the scope of its object, except for the powers explicitly granted by the constitutive contract to the general meetings of shareholders or the powers specifically conferred upon the Board of Directors. However, the provisions contained in the constitutive contract or the decisions of the Board of Directors that define these powers cannot be opposed by third parties in accordance with the last paragraph of Article 197 of this Code.
