The contract is a binding legal agreement that obligates the contracting parties and defines their obligations and rights with respect to the specific transaction.

The economic interests of the grouping are brought together in accordance with the mandatory legal provisions.  It is drawn up and publicized in accordance with Articles 3 and 16 of this Code. It must include the following details:

1) The name of the grouping. 2) The name or collective name of the companies, their legal form, and the address of their registered office or social headquarters; and if necessary, the registration number in the commercial register for each member of the grouping.
