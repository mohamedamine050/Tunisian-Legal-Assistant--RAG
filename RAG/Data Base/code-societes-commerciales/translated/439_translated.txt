An economic interest group shall consist of two or more persons, natural or legal, for a limited period, 

whether for the purpose of facilitating the economic activity of its members or for improving and developing the results of that activity.  Its activity must be connected to the economic activity of its members and can only have a character connected to that activity. (The first paragraph of Article 1 of Law No. 65 of 2005, dated July 27, 2005, has been amended).
