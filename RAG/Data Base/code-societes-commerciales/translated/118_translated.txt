(Paragraph two amended by Law No. 16 of 2009, dated March 16, 2009)

Each partner may individually initiate a legal action against another party, be it an individual or a private entity, claiming a right or compensation for damages caused.  Partners representing ten percent of the company's capital may jointly initiate a legal action against the agent(s) responsible for the damages. The proceedings of the aforementioned legal action shall continue even if the aforementioned percentage of capital changes after the action has been initiated.
