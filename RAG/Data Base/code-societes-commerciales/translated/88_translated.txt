Upon the dissolution of the company, the partners must submit their final financial statements and immediately distribute its profits and assets or bear its losses in accordance with the provisions of Article 85 of this Code.  Each partner holding an in-kind share remains its owner and has the right to recover it.  The assets acquired during the company's existence or the common assets are divided among the partners in accordance with the provisions of Article 85 of this Code.  In the event of impossibility, the division shall be carried out in accordance with the provisions of Article 116 and following of the Code of Real Rights.
