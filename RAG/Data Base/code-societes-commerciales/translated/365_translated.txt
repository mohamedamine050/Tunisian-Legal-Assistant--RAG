The special general meeting of preferred shareholders may express its opinion beforehand on the matters submitted to the ordinary general meeting of shareholders, and this opinion shall be recorded in the minutes of the latter meeting.  Any decision resulting in an infringement of the preferred shareholders' rights to profit, exceeding the company's profit over its liabilities by including the company's capital among its debts at the end of the financial year, shall not be considered final without a right to vote until it is approved by the special general meeting convened in accordance with the conditions stipulated in Articles 357 and subsequent articles of this Code.
