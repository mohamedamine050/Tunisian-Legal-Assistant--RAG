(Amended by Article 1 of Law No. 65 of 2005 dated July 27, 2005)

If the general meeting to consider the matters stipulated in Article 37 of this Code does not convene within two months from the date of the closing of the liquidation proceedings, or if the approval of the final liquidation account is refused, then the liquidator must resort to the competent court to obtain a decision approving the aforementioned account.  Similarly, any interested party may take the same action.  Third parties may not oppose the decision closing the liquidation and approving the final account except from the day following the publication of the decision closing the liquidation in the Official Gazette of the Tunisian Republic after its registration in the commercial register.
