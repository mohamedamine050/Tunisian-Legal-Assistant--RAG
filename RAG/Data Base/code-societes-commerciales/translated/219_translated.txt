The term of office of a member of the board of directors shall terminate:

- Upon the expiration of his appointment.  - Due to a personal circumstance preventing him from performing his duties. - Upon the dissolution, transformation, or liquidation of the company. - By a change in the company's form. - By dismissal. - By voluntary resignation.  The cessation of a board member's duties must be publicized in accordance with Article 16 of this Code.
