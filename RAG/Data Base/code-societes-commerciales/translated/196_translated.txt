A member of the board of directors may be appointed from among the company's employees, unless the company's constitutive document provides otherwise.  An employee may not hold both positions, unless his prior employment with the company precedes his appointment by at least five years and he is actively working for the company. Any appointment in breach of the preceding paragraph shall be considered void. However, this nullity shall not invalidate the deliberations in which the aforementioned board member participated.
