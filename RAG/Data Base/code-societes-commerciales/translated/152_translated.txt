Every agreement concluded directly or through an intermediary between the sole shareholder and the company must be attached to the annual accounting documents, in addition to a report; a report in a form of information receipt, which is drafted in a semi-final form for transmission to the auditor, if any.  In the event of non-compliance with the conditions specified in the first paragraph of this chapter, the sole shareholder shall be personally liable for damages incurred by the company or third parties.
