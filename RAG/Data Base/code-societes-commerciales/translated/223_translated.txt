Imprisonment for a term not less than one year and not exceeding five years, and a fine of two thousand to ten thousand dinars, or one of these penalties only, shall be imposed upon:

1) Members of the board of directors who, without preparing a list for the inventory or by using falsified inventory lists, proceed to distribute fictitious profits to shareholders.
2) Members of the board of directors who intentionally, even if no profits were distributed, publish a balance sheet that does not conform to reality to conceal the true state of the company, or intentionally submit it to the shareholders.
3) Members of the board of directors who misuse, with malice aforethought, the company's assets or reputation for purposes they know to be unlawful for their own benefit to achieve personal aims or to favor another company with which they are directly or indirectly linked by beneficial relationships.
4) Members of the board of directors who misuse, with malice aforethought, their authority or the votes they are entitled to dispose of for purposes they know to be unlawful for the company's interests to achieve personal aims or to favor another company with which they are linked by beneficial relationships in any way.


Crimes punishable by death or imprisonment exceeding five years are classified as felonies under this law.  Crimes punishable by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars, are classified as misdemeanors. Crimes punishable by imprisonment not exceeding fifteen days or a fine of sixty dinars are classified as infractions.  Article 122 of the Code of Criminal Procedure.
