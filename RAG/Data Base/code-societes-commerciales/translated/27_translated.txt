The company whose net assets have fallen below half its capital as a result of losses evidenced by its accounting documents may be dissolved.  In this case, the legal representative of the company is required to convene a general meeting, in accordance with the conditions stipulated in the company's constitutive documents, to decide on its dissolution or the continuation of its activity with the settlement of its situation. All this is subject to the provisions of the law concerning the rescue of businesses experiencing economic difficulties.
