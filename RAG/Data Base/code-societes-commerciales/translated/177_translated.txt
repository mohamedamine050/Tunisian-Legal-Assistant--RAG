The founders are jointly and severally liable, towards the company, the shareholders, and third parties, for damages resulting from the inaccuracy or insufficiency of the information they provided to the constitutive general meeting concerning the subscription of the company's capital, the issuance of shares, the use of funds received, the company's incorporation expenses, and in-kind contributions.  They also bear, jointly and severally, the responsibility for damages resulting from negligence or failure to comply with any procedure required by law for the incorporation of the company.  The claim against the founders shall lapse three years from the date of the company's incorporation.
