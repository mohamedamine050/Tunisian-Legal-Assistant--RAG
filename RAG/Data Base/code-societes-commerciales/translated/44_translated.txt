(Paragraph 3 of Article 1 of Law No. 65 of 2005, dated July 27, 2005, has been amended).

The decisions of the general meetings referred to in Article 43 of this Code, convened in ordinary session, are adopted in accordance with the majority and quorum requirements obtained in the manner prescribed for each company.  Liquidating partners have the right to vote.  In the event that these conditions are not met, the liquidator must refer the matter to the judge of summary proceedings, who shall decide as he deems appropriate. Any interested party may also take the same action.
