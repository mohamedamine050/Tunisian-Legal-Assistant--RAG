The general meeting of shareholders may delegate to the Board of Directors or the collective management body the necessary authority to issue bonds, determine the conditions of their issuance, and establish the methods thereof, either in one instance or in several instances. The resolution of the general meeting must include the total amount of the bond loan and the period within which the bonds must be issued. (Amended by Article 1 of Law No. 65 of 2005 dated July 27, 2005)
