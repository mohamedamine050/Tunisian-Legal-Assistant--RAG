(Added by Law No. 96 of 2005, dated October 18, 2005)

Any manager of a commercial company or economic interest group who obstructs the work of the auditor(s) or refuses to provide them, upon request, with any means leaving a written trace in the necessary documents for the performance of their duties, shall be punished by imprisonment for six months and a fine of five thousand dinars, or by one of these two penalties.
