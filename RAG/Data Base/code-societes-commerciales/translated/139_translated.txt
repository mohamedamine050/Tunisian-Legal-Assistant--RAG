One or more partners representing at least ten percent of the company's capital may, individually or collectively, request from the judge of the summary proceedings the appointment of an expert or a group of experts to submit a report on one or more transactions.  The expert shall deliver a copy of the report to the claimant, the agent, and if necessary, to the auditor. The auditor's report shall be appended and served on the partners before the ordinary general meeting, in accordance with the conditions stipulated in Article 130 of this Code.
