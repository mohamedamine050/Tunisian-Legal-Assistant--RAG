The appointment of an auditor as a member of the board of directors or the collective management body of the companies they audit, for the five years following the end of their duties with the company, is prohibited. Any such appointment is null and void.  Any appointment of an auditor in violation of this chapter and chapters 258, 259, and 260 of this Code shall result in a financial penalty against the offending company of at least two thousand dinars and at most twenty thousand dinars. The same penalty shall also be imposed on the company in the event of failure to appoint an auditor by its general meeting.  The appointment of a suitable individual to a position commensurate with the qualifications and competencies required to perform it is not possible.
