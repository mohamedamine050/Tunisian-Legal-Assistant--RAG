Priority shareholders whose profits exceed the company's liabilities upon inclusion of the company's capital within its outstanding debts at the end of the fiscal year,  without voting rights, may convene in a public meeting held by civil authorities, according to the procedural rules contained in this guide, to obtain specific information regarding the actions or activities undertaken by the general management, to ensure the soundness, integrity, transparency, and conformity of performance with the requirements of the public interest.  Such meetings are specific to them.
