(Amended by Article 1 of Law No. 65 of 2005 dated July 27, 2005)

The General Meeting may grant members of the Supervisory Board an attendance allowance, the amount of which is determined annually. The Supervisory Board may grant exceptional allowances for missions or mandates entrusted to its members. The granting of these allowances is subject to the approval of the General Meeting of shareholders, in accordance with Articles 200 and 202 of this Code.  Compensation and allowances are charged to operating expenses.
