The parent company shall submit the consolidated financial statements, along with a report, in a format that constitutes a form of information transmission, drafted in a substantially final form for future reference.  The act of consolidating the companies and the auditor's report on the parent company's accounts shall be at the disposal of all partners at its registered office, one month prior to the date of the general partners' meeting. The parent company shall publish the consolidated financial statements in one of the Tunisian daily newspapers published in Arabic within one month of their approval.
