The sole partner must prepare a report, a form of information transmission, drafted in a near-final form for transmission to the recipient.  The same applies to the preparation of an inventory and the company's financial statements, to which a report, a form of information transmission, drafted in a near-final form for transmission to the recipient, shall be added.  The auditor, if any, shall be involved. The sole partner shall approve these documents within three months of the closing date of the accounts. The provisions of Articles 126 to 132 of this Code shall not apply to the sole proprietorship limited liability company.
