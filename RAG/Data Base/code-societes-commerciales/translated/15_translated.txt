All companies, except limited partnerships (commandite), must publicize their incorporation contracts.  Publication shall be made in the Official Gazette of the Tunisian Republic within one month of the company's formation or from the date of the minutes of the meeting or deliberations of the constitutive general meeting of the company. (The second paragraph was amended by Law No. 16 of 2009 dated March 16, 2009).

The publication procedures shall be undertaken by and under the responsibility of the company's legal representative.
