(Paragraph 3 amended by Law No. 96 of 2005 dated October 18, 2005)

The constitutive general meeting shall verify the total subscription of the company's capital and the payment of the required percentage of the share value.  It shall also approve the constitutive contract, which may only be amended by unanimous agreement of the subscribers.  It shall further appoint the first members of the board of directors and auditors, in accordance with the provisions of Articles 189, 260 and following of this Code. The first members of the board of directors shall be appointed for a term of three years. Their appointment may be renewed for the management of the company, unless the constitutive contract stipulates otherwise.  Auditors shall be appointed for a term of three years, subject to the provisions of Article 13bis of this Code. The minutes of the meeting shall record the acceptance of their duties by the members of the board of directors and the auditors.
