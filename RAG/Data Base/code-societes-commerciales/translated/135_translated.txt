In the event of an increase in capital, whether in whole or in part, by means of in-kind contributions, these contributions shall be valued in accordance with the provisions of Article 100 of this Code.  If the approved value differs from that proposed by the contribution auditor, the partners at the time of the capital increase and the subscribers to the capital increase shall be jointly and severally liable to third parties for a period of three years for the value assigned to the in-kind contribution.
