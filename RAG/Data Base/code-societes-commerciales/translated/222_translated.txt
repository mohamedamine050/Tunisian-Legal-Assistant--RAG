(Added by the second paragraph of Law No. 16 of 2009 dated March 16, 2009)

A fine of between five hundred and five thousand dinars shall be imposed upon the Chief Executive Officer elected by the board of directors from among its members in companies and institutions, or the General Manager, or the Chairperson of the meeting who does not prepare the minutes of the meeting or does not keep a special register at the company's headquarters containing the deliberations of its board of directors.  The same penalties stipulated in the first paragraph of this chapter shall also be imposed upon members of the board of directors who have not placed the documents and reports that must be presented to the general meeting at the disposal of the partners within the deadlines and under the conditions stipulated in the provisions of this Code.
