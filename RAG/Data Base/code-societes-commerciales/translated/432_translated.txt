The decision on division taken by the extraordinary general meeting must be published in the Official Gazette of the Tunisian Republic in the month following the date of the said meeting, and also in two daily newspapers, one of which is in Arabic, in accordance with Article 16 of this Code.  Each company resulting from the division remains bound to its creditors for the duration of the division proceedings until the completion of the publication and registration procedures in the commercial register. The provisions of Articles 424 to 426 of this Code apply to the division.
