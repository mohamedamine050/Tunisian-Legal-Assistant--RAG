Agents who:

1) did not prepare an annual inventory, or financial statements, or any form of information delivery, formulated as a near-final draft for future reference;

2) did not convene a meeting of the partners at least once a year;

3) did not send the partners, one month before the general meeting, the annual financial statements, the management report, and the proposed resolutions, and where applicable, any form of information delivery, formulated as a near-final draft for future reference;

4) did not consult the partners to take appropriate measures within the month following the approval of the financial statements, which showed that their own funds had fallen below half their capital as a result of subsequent losses;

5) did not comply with the provisions of Article 123 of this Code;

shall be punished by a fine ranging from five hundred to five thousand dinars.
