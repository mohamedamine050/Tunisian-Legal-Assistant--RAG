(Paragraph one amended by Law No. 65 of 2005 dated July 27, 2005)

The company may not grant loans to its agent or to its partners who are natural persons in any form whatsoever, nor may it guarantee or ensure the fulfillment of their obligations to third parties. This prohibition extends to the legal representatives of partners who are legal persons, as well as to the spouses, ascendants, and descendants of the aforementioned persons.  Any interested party may invoke the nullity of any contract concluded in violation of the aforementioned provisions.
