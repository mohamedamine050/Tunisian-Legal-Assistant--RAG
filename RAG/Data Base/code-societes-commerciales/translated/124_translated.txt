(Paragraph 1 amended by Law No. 96 of 2005, dated October 18, 2005)

If a limited liability company does not meet the conditions for appointing an auditor as stipulated in Article 13 of this Code, the appointment of an auditor or auditors becomes mandatory if requested by one or more partners representing at least one-fifth of the company's capital.  The President of the Court within whose jurisdiction the company's registered office is located shall appoint the auditor or auditors by summary order upon application from the aforementioned partner(s). In all cases, the articles of association may stipulate the appointment of an auditor or auditors.
