The penalties stipulated in this Code apply to the Chairman and Chief Executive Officer.  The Chairman and Chief Executive Officer is elected by the board of directors from among its members in companies and institutions. The Chairman and Chief Executive Officer, the Chief Executive Officer, and the members of the board of directors, each according to their specific powers,  are subject to these penalties, as are the members of the collective management body and the members of the supervisory board of companies with concealed names subject to the provisions of Articles 224 to 256 of this Code.
