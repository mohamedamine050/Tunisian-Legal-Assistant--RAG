In any company that has issued preferred shares with respect to profit—the excess of the company's assets over its liabilities at the end of the financial year—without voting rights, any changes concerning the subject matter of the article upon which the speaker or writer bases his statement,  regarding the company or its type, shall not be valid unless approved by the general meeting of the holders of preferred shares with respect to profit—the excess of the company's assets over its liabilities at the end of the financial year—without voting rights, convened to discuss this matter.
