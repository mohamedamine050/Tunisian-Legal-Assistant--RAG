(Amended by Law No. 47 of 2019 dated May 29, 2019)

A sole partner cannot delegate the management of the company except to a single agent.  All company decisions are signed by the sole partner or the agent and deposited in a special register kept for this purpose, which is examined and stamped by the clerk of the primary court within whose jurisdiction the company's registered office is located. Any act or decision taken in violation of the aforementioned provisions is considered null and void. Any interested party may request from the judge of urgent matters authorization to suspend such act or decision within a maximum period of sixty days from the date of becoming aware of the aforementioned decision.
