A supervisor or several supervisors of shares shall be appointed at the request of the board of directors or the collective management body, in accordance with the provisions of Article 173 of this Code, in the case of an in-kind contribution. The extraordinary general meeting shall approve the valuation of the in-kind contributions; once this is done, it shall authorize the increase in capital. If the meeting reduces the valuation of the in-kind contributions, the express approval of the shareholder is required.  The increase in capital shall not be effected in the absence thereof. All shares relating to the in-kind contributions shall be fully issued upon their issuance.
