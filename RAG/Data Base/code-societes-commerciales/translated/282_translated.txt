Before commencing consideration of the agenda, an attendance sheet must be prepared. This sheet shall state the names of the shareholders or their respective representatives, their addresses, and the number of shares belonging to them or to the party they represent.  The attending shareholders or their agents must sign the attendance sheet.  The signature's authenticity must be attested to by the General Meeting's office. The sheet must be deposited at the company's head office and be accessible for inspection by any interested party. Based on the prepared list, the total number of shareholders present in person or by proxy, as well as the total amount of capital they hold, shall be determined, specifying the portion of the company's capital belonging to shareholders entitled to vote.
