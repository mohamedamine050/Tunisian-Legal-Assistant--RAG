Members of the collective management body and members of the supervisory board, as well as any person summoned to attend the public sessions held by the civil society organizations, in accordance with the procedural rules set forth in this guide, with the aim of obtaining specific information concerning the activities undertaken by the general management, to ensure the soundness, integrity, transparency, and compliance with the requirements of the public interest,  are required to attend. These bodies are bound by confidentiality regarding information deemed confidential by the presiding officer of the session.
