The fourth paragraph of Law No. 16 of 2009, dated March 16, 2009, is repealed.

The partners in the negotiation have the status of a merchant. However, the company's creditors cannot claim payment from a partner except after fifteen days from the date of their notice of payment.  Partners belonging to the company at the time the obligation arises shall be jointly and severally liable with their personal assets. Creditors must bring their action within three years from the date the debt falls due.
