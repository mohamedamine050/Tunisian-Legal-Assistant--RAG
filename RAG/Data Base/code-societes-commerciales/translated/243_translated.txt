(Paragraphs 1 and 3 were amended by Law No. 65 of 2005, dated July 27, 2005)

In the event of a vacancy or vacancies on the Supervisory Board, following death, resignation, incapacity, or loss of capacity, the Board

may, between general meetings, proceed with a temporary appointment. When the number of members of the Supervisory Board falls below the legal minimum, the collective management body must convene

an ordinary general meeting to fill the vacancy in the number of members of the Supervisory Board. The appointment made by the Board pursuant to the first paragraph of this section is subject to ratification by the next ordinary general meeting. If ratification is impossible, the decisions taken and the actions previously undertaken by the Board shall remain valid

and effective. If the Board neglects to make the required appointment, or if the general meeting is not convened, any interested party is entitled to request

the appointment of an agent

an agent
a person acting on behalf of another person
to convene the general meeting to make the appointment or to ratify the appointment provided for in the first paragraph

of this section.
