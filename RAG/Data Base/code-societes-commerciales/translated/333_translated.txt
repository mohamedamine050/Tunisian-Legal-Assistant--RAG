Article 2 was amended by Law No. 65 of 2005 dated July 27, 2005.

The owners of the bonds may convene a special general meeting.  This special general meeting of bondholders may express its opinion beforehand on the matters submitted to the ordinary general meeting of shareholders. This opinion shall be recorded in the minutes of the latter meeting. The special general meeting of bondholders shall elect one of its members to represent it and defend the rights of the bondholders. The provisions of Articles 355 to 365 of this Code shall apply to the special general meeting of bondholders and its representative. The representative of the general meeting of bondholders shall have the capacity to represent it before the courts.
