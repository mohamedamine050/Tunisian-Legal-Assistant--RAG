He shall be punished by imprisonment from one to five years and a fine from five hundred to five thousand dinars, or by one of these two penalties only, the sole partner in a single-person limited liability company who:

1) Knowingly made a false statement, when establishing the company or on the occasion of increasing its capital.
2) Knowingly and maliciously valued in-kind contributions at more than their true value.
3) Knowingly prepared and submitted financial statements to conceal the true state of the company.
4) Maliciously used the company's assets or reputation for purposes known to be contrary to its interests, to achieve personal aims or to favor another company with which he is directly or indirectly linked by beneficial relationships.
