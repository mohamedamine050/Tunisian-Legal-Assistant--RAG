If the nullity of the company or the nullity of the deliberations subsequent to its establishment is based on a breach of publicity rules, then anyone with an interest in avoiding the nullity may give notice to the company to initiate procedures to regularize its situation within a period of thirty days.  If it proves impossible to take the regularization procedures within the aforementioned period, anyone with an interest may request from the judge of urgent matters the appointment of an agent to undertake the procedures.
