(Amended by Law No. 65 of 2005 dated July 27, 2005)

Distributable profits consist of the net accounting result after adding or deducting deferred results from previous accounting years, and after deducting the following:

- A percentage equal to five percent of the profit, determined as mentioned above, as legal reserves. This deduction becomes unnecessary if the legal reserve reaches ten percent of the company's capital.
- The reserve stipulated by the specific legislative texts, within the limits indicated therein.
- The reserves stipulated by the constitutive contract.  Any decision contrary to the provisions of this chapter shall be null and void.
