(Amended by Law No. 65 of 2005 dated July 27, 2005)

A member of the collective management body of a limited liability company must inform the supervisory board of his appointment as an agent or a member of the board of directors, or chief executive officer, or general manager, or a member of a collective management body, or supervisory board of another company, within one month of his appointment.  The collective management body must inform the ordinary general meeting of shareholders thereof at its first meeting. The provisions of the second paragraph of Article 192 of this Code shall apply.
