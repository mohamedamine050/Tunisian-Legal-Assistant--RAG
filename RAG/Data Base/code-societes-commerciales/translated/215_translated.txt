The constitutive contract of the company may include a separation of the duties of the Chairman of the Board of Directors and the duties of the General Manager of the company.

This separation of duties shall be mandatory for companies listed on the stock exchange. (Amended by Law No. 47 of 2019, dated May 29, 2019, relating to improving the investment climate)

In this context, the functions and responsibilities are defined in accordance with the provisions of Articles 216 to 221 of this Code.
