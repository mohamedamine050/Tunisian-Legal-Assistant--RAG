The auditor shall, under his responsibility for the integrity of the company's financial statements, ensure their fairness in accordance with the applicable legal and regulatory provisions.  He shall ensure compliance with the provisions stipulated in Articles 12 to 16 of this Code, and he must inform the annual general meeting by means of a report, a form of information transmission, drafted in a substantially final form for the benefit of the recipient.  Any violation of the provisions of these Articles shall [be reported/result in consequences -  the original is ambiguous and requires clarification].
