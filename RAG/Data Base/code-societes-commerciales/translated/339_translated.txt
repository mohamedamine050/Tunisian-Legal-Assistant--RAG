The chairman, general managers, and each member of the board of directors or collective management body shall be punished by a fine ranging from three hundred to six thousand dinars if they issued or permitted the issuance of banknotes in a manner contrary to the provisions of this law.  Crimes that warrant punishment by death or imprisonment exceeding five years are described as felonies under this law. Crimes that warrant punishment by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars, are described as misdemeanors. Crimes warranting punishment not exceeding fifteen days' imprisonment or sixty dinars' fine are described as infractions.  This is without prejudice to the penalties provided for in the legislation in force, particularly with regard to Article 122 of the Code of Criminal Procedure and any violation of its provisions; this is without prejudice to the penalties provided for under current legislation, particularly regarding monetary matters.
