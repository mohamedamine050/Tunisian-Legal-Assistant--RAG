The number of partners in a limited liability company may not exceed fifty. If the number of partners exceeds fifty, the company must transform into a joint-stock company within one year, unless the number of partners is reduced to fifty or fewer.  In the event of a violation, offenses that warrant punishment by death or imprisonment exceeding five years are classified as felonies within the meaning of this law. Offenses that warrant punishment by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars, are classified as misdemeanors. Offenses warranting punishment not exceeding fifteen days imprisonment or sixty dinars fine are classified as infractions.  Article 122 of the Code of Criminal Procedure.  In that case, any interested party may judicially demand the dissolution of the company. However, the court competent to hear the request may grant an additional period to enable the partners to remedy the violation.
