Members of the collective management body shall perform their duties and attend to them with the care of a prudent entrepreneur and a diligent agent. They must maintain the confidentiality of the transactions they have examined in the course of their activity within the body. A member of the collective management body who breaches his duties shall be liable to the company for compensation for any resulting damage, even if the supervisory board approves such detrimental actions. A member of the collective management body shall be exempt from the obligation to compensate if his actions are based on a legal decision issued by the general meeting.
