The company, operating under a fictitious name, is managed by a collective management body that bears responsibility for the management and performs its duties under the supervision of a Supervisory Board.  This collective management body may consist of one or more individuals, not exceeding five, and must be natural persons.  The tasks assigned to the collective management body may also be performed by a single natural person in companies operating under a fictitious name with a capital of less than one hundred thousand dinars.  Any violation described as a felony under this law means crimes punishable by death or imprisonment exceeding five years.  Crimes punishable by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars, are described as misdemeanors.  Crimes punishable by imprisonment not exceeding fifteen days or a fine not exceeding sixty dinars are described as infractions.  Any violation of the provisions of this chapter results in nullity.
