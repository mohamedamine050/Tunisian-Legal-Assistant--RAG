Within fifteen days from the date of closing the subscription, the founders shall call upon the subscribers to attend the constitutive general meeting, held in accordance with the procedures and deadlines included in the instruction card.  A statement of all work completed on behalf of the company by the founder(s) shall also be placed at the company's registered office and at the disposal of the shareholders, at least fifteen days before the date of the first constitutive general meeting. This meeting shall decide on the matter of the company's adoption of prior commitments undertaken by the founders.
