The constitutive contract of the limited partnership shall specify the methods of dismissal and resignation of the company's agent, a person acting on behalf of another person.  These methods of dismissal and resignation are subject to the rules applicable to the agent, a person acting on behalf of another person.


A limited partnership is a traditional type of company characterized by flexibility and full transparency, with the partners' liability being joint and several. Each partner is liable to third parties to the full extent of their assets without limitation, and for all the company's debts.
