Proof of subscription and payment shall be obtained through a written statement from the founders, received by their financial officer at the company's registered office.  This statement shall be accompanied by a certificate from the institution where the funds were deposited, certifying the payment of the deposited funds.  The financial officer legally authorized to accept the aforementioned statement shall issue a subscription certificate. The original statement for the company's establishment shall be attached to a list of subscribers' names, a statement of payments received, and a copy of the company's constitutive contract. The financial officer is authorized to provide subscribers with copies conforming to the originals of the received statements and accompanying documents. A copy of the constitutive contract shall be deposited at the company's registered office, and another copy shall be deposited with the clerk of the Court of First Instance within whose jurisdiction the aforementioned registered office is located.
