The Board of Directors shall elect from among its members a chairman who shall hold the position of Chief Executive Officer.  He must be a natural person and a shareholder in the company; otherwise, his election shall be considered void. The Board of Directors shall determine the remuneration of the Chief Executive Officer.

In companies and institutions, the Board of Directors shall elect from among its members a chairman who shall hold the position of Chief Executive Officer. The Chief Executive Officer shall be appointed for a term not exceeding the duration of his membership on the Board and may be elected once or several times. The Board of Directors may also dismiss him at any time. Any provision contrary to this shall be considered void.
