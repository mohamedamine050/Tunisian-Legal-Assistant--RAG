(Added by Law No. 96 of 2005 dated October 18, 2005)

Regardless of their legal obligations, auditors shall provide the Central Bank of Tunisia with a copy of every report they submit to general meetings, for the following companies:

- Public limited companies;
- Companies required to prepare consolidated financial statements under current legislation, if the total of their consolidated accounts exceeds an amount to be determined by order;
- Companies whose total commitments to lending institutions and their debt issuance exceed an amount to be determined by order.
