The plenary extraordinary session decided to change the form of the share-based contracting company to a limited liability company.

A limited liability company is a form of commercial company where the liability of the shareholders is limited to the amount of their shares.  Or a company with limited liability, that is, the obligation to respond to the actions of a person before the law or to compensate for the damage caused, with the agreement of all the contributing partners in work and by a majority of the contributing partners in capital. A share-based contracting company cannot be changed less than two years from its formation date, except in the case of the death of one of the contributing partners in work, unless the constitutive contract stipulates otherwise. The change of form of the share-based contracting company must be publicized in accordance with the provisions of Article 16 of this Code.
