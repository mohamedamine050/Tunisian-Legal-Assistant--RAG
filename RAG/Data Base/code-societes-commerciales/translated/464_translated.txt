A company group cannot have objectives that contravene the law, such as tax evasion or violation of competition rules.  Crimes described in this law that warrant punishment by death or imprisonment exceeding five years are classified as felonies.  Crimes that warrant imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or a fine exceeding sixty dinars, are classified as misdemeanors.  Crimes warranting punishment not exceeding fifteen days imprisonment or sixty dinars fine are classified as violations.  (Article 122 of the Code of Criminal Procedure).
