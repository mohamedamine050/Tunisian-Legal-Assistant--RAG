(Added by Law No. 96 of 2005 dated October 18, 2005) Subject to the appointment of two or more registered auditors listed in the register of the Tunisian Institute of Chartered Accountants:

- Publicly held lending institutions and multi-line insurance companies;
- Companies required to prepare consolidated financial statements under current legislation, if the total of their consolidated accounts exceeds an amount to be determined by order;
- Companies whose total commitments to lending institutions and their debt security issuances exceed an amount to be determined by order.  The auditors must not be linked by any partnership or other transactions of any kind that could limit their independence, and they must define the terms and methods of preparing their reports based on the principle of confrontation in auditing. The rules and professional standards relating to the double audit of company accounts shall be set by professional standards.
