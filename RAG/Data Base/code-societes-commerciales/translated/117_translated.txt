(Added as paragraphs three and four by Law No. 16 of 2009 dated March 16, 2009)

The agent or agents shall be liable individually or jointly and severally, as the case may be, towards the company or towards third parties, for:

violation described as felonies, within the meaning of this law, crimes punishable by death or imprisonment exceeding five years;
violation described as misdemeanors, crimes punishable by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars;
violation described as infractions, crimes punishable by imprisonment not exceeding fifteen days or a fine not exceeding sixty dinars. (Article 122 of the Code of Criminal Procedure)
legal provisions specific to the limited liability company, or for breach of the constitutive contract, or for

their errors in management. If the acts giving rise to liability are committed by several agents, the court shall, where necessary, determine the share of each

of them in the compensation of damages. The court shall order the legal or de facto agent to return any money taken from the company, together with any profits derived from its use

for his own benefit or for the benefit of a third party. The partners retain the right to claim further losses and to bring a

civil action against him, where appropriate.  The compensation awarded shall benefit the company.
