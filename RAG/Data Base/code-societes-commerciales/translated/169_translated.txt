Withdrawal of funds resulting from the subscription shall be carried out by the company's legal representative upon submission of a true copy of the minutes of the constitutive meeting and of the minutes of the first meeting of the board of directors or the collective management body, and a copy of the certificate of registration of the company in the commercial register.  If the company is not established within six months of the date of deposit of the constitutive deed with the clerk of the primary court within whose jurisdiction the company's registered office is located, each subscriber may, by petition and with the authorization of the presiding judge of the aforementioned court, request the withdrawal of the funds deposited, after deduction of their proportionate share of distribution expenses.
