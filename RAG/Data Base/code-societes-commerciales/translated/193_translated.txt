(Amended by Law No. 36 of 2016 dated April 29, 2016)

The following individuals cannot be members of the board of directors:

- Those declared bankrupt for a period of five years from the date of the bankruptcy judgment. - Individuals against whom a written court decision has been issued prohibiting them from managing and administering companies; minors; and those lacking legal capacity.  Also,

- Individuals convicted and prohibited from exercising public office. - Individuals convicted of a felony or misdemeanor affecting public morals, public order, or the regulations governing companies, as well as those who cannot practice commerce by virtue of their profession. - Employees in the service of the administration, except with special authorization from the supervising ministry.
