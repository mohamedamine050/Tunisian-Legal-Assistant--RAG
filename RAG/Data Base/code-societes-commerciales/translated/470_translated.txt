Every parent company must register with the trade register the companies belonging to the group of companies.  Every company belonging to it must register with the trade register its membership, its termination of membership, and the parent company having influence over it.  It must also register, in its relevant transaction report where applicable, its membership in the group of companies.  The holding company must register its status as a holding company with the trade register, and the termination of that status if it occurs. The provisions of the first and second paragraphs of this chapter apply to companies whose head offices are located in Tunisia and are subject to the influence of a parent company whose head office is located abroad.
