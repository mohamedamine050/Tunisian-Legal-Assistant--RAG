The provisions of this section aim to combat water pollution in pursuit of satisfying or reconciling the following requirements:

- The provision of potable water.
- Public health.
- Agriculture, industry, and all other human activities of public interest.
- The biological life of the receiving environment, particularly fish, as well as recreational activities related to water sports and the protection of natural sites.
- The preservation of water flow.

This section applies to the discharge, runoff, dumping, and direct or indirect deposition of materials of all types, and more generally to anything that could lead to the contamination or deterioration of water by altering its natural, chemical, biological, or bacteriological properties, whether the water is surface water, groundwater, or marine water within territorial waters.
