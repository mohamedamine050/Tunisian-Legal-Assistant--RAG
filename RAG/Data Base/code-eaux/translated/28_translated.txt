Owners and beneficiaries claiming acquired water rights shall, within one year from the date of publication of this Code, submit a request to the Minister of Agriculture to prove their rights, accompanied by all relevant supporting documents. Failure to do so shall result in the forfeiture of their right. The Minister of Agriculture shall adjudicate on these rights, except in the event of legal proceedings before the courts.  Proof of these acquired water rights in public domain waters shall be subject to the conditions set out in the following articles.
