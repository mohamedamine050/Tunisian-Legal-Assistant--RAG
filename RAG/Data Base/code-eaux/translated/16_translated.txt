Areas designated for the development and use of water resources may be subject to an order issued after consulting the National Water Council, in areas where resources appear or are feared to be insufficient for current needs or planned priorities.  Distribution plans for water resources within the aforementioned areas are established by ministerial decree of the Minister of Agriculture, following an administrative investigation with the relevant natural or legal persons and after obtaining the opinion of the National Water Council, taking into account the nature and location of the needs to be met. As required, the order referred to in the first paragraph of this section may specify water diversion programs and works programs designed to allow for the implementation or realization of the water distribution plan. It may declare that all or some of the diversion or works programs defined in this manner are in the public interest.
