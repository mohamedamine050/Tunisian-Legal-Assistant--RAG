The discharge of wastewater or the dumping of waste that may be harmful to public health into natural percolating wells, wells, excavations, and permanent tunnels, whether their original purpose has been altered or not, is prohibited. Permits will only be granted for the discharge of wastewater or used water into percolating wells, provided that they are preceded by a seepage pit. A declaration must be submitted regarding wells, excavations, or tunnels whose intended use has been altered, and these are subject to the oversight of the administration, which may,
