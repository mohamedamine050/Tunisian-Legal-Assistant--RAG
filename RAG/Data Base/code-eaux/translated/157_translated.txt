Records of violations and misdemeanors are submitted by the agents mentioned in the previous article to the relevant ministries, which, after a maximum of one month from the drafting of the said records, refer them to the competent court.

The records drafted pursuant to this Code and the orders issued for its implementation have evidentiary value unless proof is provided to the contrary. However, in the event of confirmation, the records shall be forwarded without delay to the Ministers of Agriculture and Public Health, who shall authorize, each within his respective jurisdiction, and by means of a decision, the immediate demolition of the structures at the expense of the violator, if the structures erected on public state property without a permit threaten the safety of transportation routes, are likely to damage private property, or their continued existence is detrimental to public tranquility or a threat to public health.
