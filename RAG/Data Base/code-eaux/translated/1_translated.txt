**Public Waters Belonging to the State:**

* Watercourses of all types, including the land within their free banks. 
* Waters confined to valleys.
* Springs of all types.
* Water layers in the earth's subsurface, of all types.
* Lakes and salt marshes. 
* Water channels, wells, and basins used by the public, as well as their appurtenances.
* Navigation, irrigation, or drainage channels constructed by the state or at its expense for the public good, as well as the land within their free banks and their appurtenances.
