The performance of works referred to in Article 10 of this Law, undertaken without a permit, shall be punishable by a fine equivalent to ten times the estimated value of the works completed.  These works, if commenced in this manner, may be suspended, either temporarily or permanently, by the Minister of Agriculture, irrespective of any precautionary measures which the Administration may authorize if the preservation of water or its quality is endangered. The performance of works which are proven to have been completed in contravention of the provisions of the authorization decision shall be punishable by a fine that may reach ten times the value of the works completed.
