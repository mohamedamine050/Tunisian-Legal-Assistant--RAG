The inspection of violations of the provisions of this Code and the orders issued for their implementation shall be carried out by:

- All police and National Guard officers. - The agents and employees of the Ministries of Agriculture and Public Health, and the sworn experts, by law.

Regardless of the right granted to all officials and agents mentioned in this chapter regarding the drafting of reports for acts of damage that may be committed under their supervision, the damage inflicted on public state property, public health, and the health of the population, as stipulated in the previous chapters of this Code, shall be inspected by the engineers of the Ministry of Agriculture and the physicians and engineers of the Ministry of Public Health, who are legally qualified for this task.
