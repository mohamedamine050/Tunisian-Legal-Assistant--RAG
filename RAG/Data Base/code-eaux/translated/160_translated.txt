## English Translation:

**Offenses, Misdemeanors, and Violations:**

**Article 122:**

Crimes, within the meaning of this law, which are punishable by death or imprisonment for more than five years, shall be considered **felonies**.

Crimes punishable by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars, shall be considered **misdemeanors**.

Crimes punishable by imprisonment not exceeding fifteen days or by a fine not exceeding sixty dinars shall be considered **violations**.

If the provisions of this Code, or the orders and decisions issued for its implementation, are damaged by an act of the public domain, the offender shall be sentenced to pay the cost of repair as determined by the Minister of Agriculture, in addition to the penalties provided for in this law.

The Court may authorize, at the expense of the offender, the removal of any illegal works or structures.
