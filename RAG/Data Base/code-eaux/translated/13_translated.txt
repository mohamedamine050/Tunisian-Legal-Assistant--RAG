## English Translation:

Within each designated restricted area:

a) The construction of any well or excavation, or the undertaking of any work involving the conversion of wells or excavations with the aim of increasing the quantity of water extracted from them, is prohibited.

b) The following shall be subject to prior authorization from the Minister of Agriculture:

- Works related to the replacement or rehabilitation of wells or excavations, provided that they are not intended to increase the quantity of water extracted from the aforementioned wells or excavations.

c) The following shall be subject to authorization and instructions from the Minister of Agriculture:

- The exploitation of groundwater. These instructions may specify the maximum quantity of water to be exploited from wells or excavations, and may include the prohibition of the use of certain wells or excavations, or any other measure necessary to avoid harmful interactions and ensure the preservation of existing water resources.
