(Repealed and replaced by Law No. 116 of 2001 dated 26/2001). A permanent quota system for water consumption may be established. The regulation of these quotas and the methods of monitoring them shall be determined by order at the proposal of the Minister responsible for Agriculture. Furthermore, due to climatic or technical circumstances, a temporary quota system or a temporary ban on certain uses of water may be established. The quota or ban system shall be established by a decision of the Governor if the effects of the climatic or technical circumstances causing it do not extend beyond a single governorate, and by a decree of the Minister responsible for Agriculture in all other cases. The decisions of the Governors and the decrees of the Minister responsible for Agriculture shall be published in two daily newspapers, one of which shall be in Arabic.
