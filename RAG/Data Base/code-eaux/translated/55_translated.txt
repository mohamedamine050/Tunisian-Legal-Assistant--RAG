Claims for privileges may be rejected if they are:

**Contravening**

**Contravening**

**Described as felonies**, within the meaning of this Penal Code, crimes that are punishable by death or imprisonment exceeding five years.

**Described as misdemeanors**, crimes that are punishable by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars.

**Described as offenses**, crimes punishable by imprisonment not exceeding fifteen days or by a fine not exceeding sixty dinars. [Article 122, Code of Criminal Procedure]

**Contrary to the public interest**

**Or to the rights of others, which have been legally proven.**
