With regard to water retention dams intended for the supply of potable water, the following shall be established:

1) A direct maintenance zone consisting of the land adjacent to the water retention dam at its highest level and extending ten meters in width, which shall be purchased in full ownership by the entity responsible for operating the dam. 
2) A benefit zone fifty meters in width beyond the rectangular land plot adjacent to the dam, within which all works and activities that could directly or indirectly lead to the contamination of the impounded water are prohibited.
