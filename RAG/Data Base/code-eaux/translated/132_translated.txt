The discharge of drainage water into waterways, the sea, and lakes is not permitted except after obtaining the opinion of the authorities responsible for the preservation of the public domain of water or the public maritime domain and neighboring installations concerning the measures intended to be taken for the treatment or distribution of water. These measures are to be determined by a joint decision of the Minister of Agriculture and the Minister of Health. 

The biological and chemical characteristics that must be present in wastewater treatment plant facilities are to be defined, except in cases where there are specific provisions.
