Every public sanitation service, regardless of the method of its exploitation, shall entail the following:

**Recovery**

Recovery is the process by which a creditor obtains payment from a debtor for the financial debt contracted by the latter. 

**Fees for Sanitation**

The determination of sanitation fees shall be made by order. Any service entrusted with the collection, transport, or treatment of wastewater, as required, shall be considered a public sanitation service.
