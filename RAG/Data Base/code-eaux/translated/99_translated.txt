Public or private groups or institutions that may have their own water supply methods shall regularly verify the type of water distributed and shall also continuously conduct bacteriological analysis of the distributed water in accordance with the standards set forth by order. The Ministry of Public Health, after consultation with the Supreme Council for Public Health, shall issue a license for any possible methods of water remediation or the use of simple or combined chemical additives for treating the aforementioned water. In no event shall the potential additives alter the organic composition of the water.
