(Repealed and replaced by Law No. 116 of 2001 dated November 26, 2001). The planning of the use of national water resources must focus on the principle of maximizing the value of each cubic meter of water at the national level, subject to acceptable economic and technical conditions. Water transfer projects from one basin to another must be preceded by an economic study demonstrating the optimal utilization of the water quantities intended to be transferred. The transfer of water from one basin to another for the purpose of meeting the drinking water needs of the population is not subject to any form of appeal.
