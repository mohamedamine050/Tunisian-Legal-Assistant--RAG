**(Repealed by Law No. 116 of 2001, dated November 26, 2001)**

- Authorization may be granted for the production and use of non-conventional water resources that meet the specific conditions for water consumption and use for private purposes or for the benefit of others in a designated integrated industrial or tourist area. Production and use of water for private purposes shall be governed by terms and conditions, and for the benefit of others by terms and conditions and a concession agreement in accordance with the provisions of this Code. These terms and conditions, which are approved by order of the Minister in charge of Agriculture upon proposal, shall specify the obligations and technical means for providing water, the characteristics of this water, the methods of its use, the specific health conditions, and, where applicable, the distribution area.
