The execution of the works referred to in paragraphs A and B of Article 13 of this Code may be suspended by a decision of the Minister of Agriculture, notwithstanding the precautionary measures that the administration may authorize. These precautionary measures may include the partial or complete demolition of the facilities, as well as the restoration of the sites to their previous condition.  

The completion of preparatory works carried out in contravention of the provisions of the authorization decision shall be punishable by a fine that may amount to ten times the estimated cost of the completed facilities.
