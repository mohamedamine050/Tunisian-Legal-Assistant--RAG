The right to use water shall remain attached to a specific piece of land within the scope of use centered on the maximum cubic meter of water assessment. The holder of the right to use water may not use it for the benefit of another land except in cases of absolute necessity and after obtaining the approval of the "National Water Council". In the event of the transfer of the land, the right to use water shall be compulsorily transferred to the new owner, who must notify the "National Water Council" of the transfer within six months from the date of the transfer of the land.

Any transfer of the right to use water that occurs outside the land for which the transfer was granted shall be considered null and void. In the event of the division of the land, the distribution of water to the resulting plots shall be subject to new rights to use water that replace the original right to use water.
