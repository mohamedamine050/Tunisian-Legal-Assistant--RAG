Irrespective of the obligations arising from the current legislation, owners of casting facilities existing before the issuance of the order referred to in Article 124 of this Code shall take all necessary measures to comply, within the deadlines specified in the aforementioned order, with the conditions imposed on their liquids so that the receiving environment achieves the characteristics it must have at the end of the aforementioned period. In light of the potential threat to public safety and health protection, the administration may take any promptly implementable measures in order to prevent the possible danger.
