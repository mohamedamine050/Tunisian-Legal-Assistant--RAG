If the operations of searching for, exploiting, or exploiting a section on the surface of the land, are likely to cause damage:

[1] Injury that occurs in violation of the law, affecting a person's body, mental or psychological health, honor, property, or reputation [2] Loss or damage to property, deprivation of comfort, damage to physical well-being, reputation, or the like - damage in crimes and quasi-crimes is the loss actually suffered by the claimant and the necessary expenses that they were or will be forced to incur to repair the consequences of the act that caused them damage, as well as what they were deprived of in the normal scope of the consequences of this act.

To preserve water and the use of springs and water layers that supply the population, the administration takes various measures to maintain the water extraction operations that have been authorized as being of public interest and that are intended to supply communities with water, and to maintain the effectiveness of the general measures decided within water development areas.
