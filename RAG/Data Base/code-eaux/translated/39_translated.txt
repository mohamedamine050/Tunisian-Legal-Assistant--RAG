Owners of lands adjacent to or crossed by the water are authorized to use the works constructed in accordance with the preceding Article for the flow of water from their lands. In this case, they shall bear:

1) A proportional share of the value of the works from which they benefit. 
2) The costs arising from any changes that may be required by this diversion.
3) In the future, a contribution towards the maintenance of the works which have become shared in nature.
