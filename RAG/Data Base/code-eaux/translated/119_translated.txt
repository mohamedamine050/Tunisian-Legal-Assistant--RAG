No person suffering from diseases that may be transmitted through drinking water, particularly in facilities related to the collection, treatment, and distribution of water, shall be allowed to work in any capacity related to the management, operation, or exploitation of drinking water supply systems. The list of these diseases shall be determined by a decision of the Minister of Health. 

Every person employed in any facility mentioned above shall undergo a medical examination according to the instructions established by a decision of the Minister of Health.
