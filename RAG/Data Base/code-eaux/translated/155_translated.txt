(Repealed and replaced by Law No. 35 of 1987 dated July 6, 1987). - The statutes of associations with common interest shall conform to the model statute to be approved by order.  The Irrigation Syndicates, the professional associations with common interest, and the private associations of owners shall have a period of one year from the date of publication of the model statute of associations with common interest to adopt this model statute. After the expiry of the aforementioned period, and in the event of non-compliance with this procedure, these associations shall be deemed dissolved de jure.
