Any refusal to provide the documents referred to in Article 68 of this Code shall be punished by a fine of up to two hundred dinars for each day of delay commencing from the date of inspection by the officers of the competent authority. The Governor of the Central Bank of Tunisia, after consulting with the Minister of Finance or the Chairman of the Financial Markets Authority, shall determine the final amount of the fine, which shall be collected for the benefit of the Tunisian Public Treasury in accordance with the conditions specified in Article 124 of this Code.
