The revocation of the license shall terminate the provision of banking and investment services by the service providers, subject to the conditions and taking into account the requirements stipulated in the chapters of this part. A non-resident financial service provider cannot be dissolved or liquidated except after the revocation of the license. The revocation of the license shall necessarily lead to the liquidation process of settling debts and distributing assets in case of bankruptcy, if the service provider is authorized by the competent authorities for the reasons stipulated in paragraphs 3 to 6 of Chapter 81 of this code.
