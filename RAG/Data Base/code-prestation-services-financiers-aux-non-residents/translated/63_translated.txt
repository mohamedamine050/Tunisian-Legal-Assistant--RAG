The Deposit, Clearing and Settlement Company, established by Law No. 117 of 1994 dated November 14, 1994, concerning the reorganization of the financial market, shall carry out deposit, clearing, and settlement operations for transactions concluded in the non-resident section of the stock exchange. The method of settling forward financial contracts referred to in Chapter 3 of this law, which are concluded in the non-resident section of the stock exchange, shall be determined by the Financial Market Authority.
