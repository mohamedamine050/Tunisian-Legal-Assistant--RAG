## Funds 

**Expertise**

Expertise addresses scientific, technical, and other matters necessary for adjudicating a legal case, excluding legal matters. 

**Expertise Funds**

Expertise Funds are investment vehicles specifically designed for certain categories of non-resident investors with expertise. These investors are considered as such due to their status, experience, or investment size, in accordance with criteria set forth by decree. For the purposes of this law, Expertise Funds are collective investment institutions in securities subject to relaxed investment rules, capable of investing in various financial instruments as stipulated in Chapter 3 of this law, within the limits authorized according to their specific investment rules.
