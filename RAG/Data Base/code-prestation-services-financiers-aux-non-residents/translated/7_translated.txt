The assets of expertise funds, which address scientific and technical matters and other matters necessary for resolving a legal dispute without legal issues, must be deposited with a sole depositary having the status of a non-resident bank established in Tunisia. The assets of expertise funds may also be deposited with a sole depositary having the status of a resident bank, subject to conditions set by decree.

The depositary must ensure:

- The legality of the decisions of the manager of the expertise fund;

- That the subscriber or acquirer of shares or units of the expertise fund is an investor within the meaning of Article 5 of this Code;

- That the subscriber or acquirer of shares or units of the expertise fund has declared that he has been informed that this institution is subject to the provisions applicable to expertise funds.
