## Banking Services for Non-Residents

Banking services include:

1. Accepting deposits from non-residents regardless of their duration or form,

2. Granting loans to non-residents in all their forms, 

3. Providing and managing payment instruments for non-resident customers,

4. Carrying out exchange transactions with non-residents within the limits allowed by legislation and regulations applicable to residents.

The definitions of deposits, loans, and payment instruments shall be as provided in Chapters 3, 4, and 5 of Law No. 65 of 2001 dated July 10, 2001, relating to credit institutions.
