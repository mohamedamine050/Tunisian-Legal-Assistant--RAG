**Without prejudice to their legal obligations, auditors of non-resident financial service providers shall:**

**1)** Immediately inform the Central Bank of Tunisia or the Financial Market Authority, as applicable, of any action that may pose a risk to the interests of the service provider, depositors or investors.

**2)** Deliver to the Central Bank of Tunisia or the Financial Market Authority, as applicable, within four months following the close of each fiscal year, a report on the audit performed. This report shall be prepared in accordance with the terms and procedures established by the Central Bank of Tunisia or the Financial Market Authority.

**3)**  Forward to the Central Bank of Tunisia or the Financial Market Authority, as applicable, a copy of their report addressed to the general meeting and to the structures of the financial service providers subject to their audit.
