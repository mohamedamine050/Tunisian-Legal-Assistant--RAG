The license granted to non-resident financial services providers shall be withdrawn by:

- The Minister of Finance, based on a report, in the form of information transmission, formulated as a semi-final draft for future access, from the Central Bank of Tunisia if it is a non-resident credit institution;

- After consulting with the Financial Market Authority if the credit institution engages in investment activities;

- The Financial Market Authority if it is a non-resident investment firm or a non-resident portfolio management company.

The license shall be withdrawn after hearing the party concerned and consulting with the professional association referred to in Article 91 of this Code.
