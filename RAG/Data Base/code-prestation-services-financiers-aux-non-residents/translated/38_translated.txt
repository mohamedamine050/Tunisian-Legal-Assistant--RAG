The minimum amount of assets to be collected by the fund upon its formation shall be equivalent to 800,000 Tunisian dinars in a convertible currency.

The shares shall be fully paid up upon subscription.

The auditor shall verify the value of the contribution in the form of financial instruments and shall prepare, under his responsibility, a report on the purpose, which he shall submit to the manager and the Financial Market Authority.

The registered office and the actual management of the manager shall be located in Tunisia.
