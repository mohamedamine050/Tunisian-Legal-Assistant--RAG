Non-resident lending institutions shall establish a **Standing Committee for Internal Audit**, which shall be a formal mechanism for gathering individuals with relevant expertise from different parts of the institution. 

The **Standing Committee for Internal Audit** shall, in particular:

* Oversee the establishment of internal control mechanisms by the institution.
* Review the annual report, including the institution's financial statements, and provide comments thereon before it is referred to the Board of Directors or the Supervisory Board for approval.
* Review each disclosure of the institution before it is referred to the supervisory authority.
* Audit any employment or transaction that may harm the institution's financial position, which is brought to its attention by the auditors or external auditors.
