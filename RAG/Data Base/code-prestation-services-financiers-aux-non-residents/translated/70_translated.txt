No person who has been subject to a final judgment for forgery, fraud, theft, embezzlement, deception, or embezzlement committed by a public official or his equivalent, or a public trustee, or a public controller, or issuing a check without sufficient funds, or participation in any of these crimes, or a felony as defined by this law, or a violation of the banking regulations or legislation relating to combating money laundering and financing of terrorism, can manage, operate, administer, or supervise a non-resident financial service provider, agency, or branch, or a non-resident credit institution, or act on its behalf.

No person who has been subject to a final judgment of bankruptcy can manage, operate, administer, or supervise a non-resident financial service provider, agency, or branch, or a non-resident credit institution, or act on its behalf.
