## English Translation:

Investment institutions not resident in the Kingdom shall be authorized by the Capital Market Authority.

In addition to the conditions stipulated in Chapter 72 of this Code, the following shall be regulated by a Ministerial Decree:

- The nature and scope of guarantees that investment institutions must provide, particularly those related to their organization,

- Measures to safeguard the interests of their clients,

- The rules applicable to the licensing of investment institutions and the necessary rules for monitoring their activities.
