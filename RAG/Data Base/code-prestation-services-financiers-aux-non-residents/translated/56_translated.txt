Non-resident lending institutions may:

- Contribute, through their own currency funds, to the capital of resident institutions, in accordance with the regulations currently in force.

- Grant, from their currency resources, medium and long-term financing to resident institutions.

- Finance, from their currency resources, import and export operations carried out by residents.

- Grant loans from their resources in dinar, as referred to in Article 55 of this Code, to finance productive operations carried out in Tunisia by residents, excluding consumer loans and housing loans.
