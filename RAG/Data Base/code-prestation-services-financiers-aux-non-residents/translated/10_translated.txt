## English Translation:

Expert Funds shall be established. 

**Expertise**

Expertise shall cover scientific, technical, and other matters necessary for adjudicating legal proceedings, excluding legal issues. These Funds shall be established as Joint Investment Funds, initiated jointly by:

- The Depositor, as referred to in Article 7 of this Code,

- and a Financial Portfolio Management Company responsible for managing the Funds. 

Both the Depositor and the Manager shall be responsible for preparing the Fund's internal regulations. Subscription to or acquisition of shares in the Joint Investment Fund constitutes acceptance of its internal regulations after reviewing them.
