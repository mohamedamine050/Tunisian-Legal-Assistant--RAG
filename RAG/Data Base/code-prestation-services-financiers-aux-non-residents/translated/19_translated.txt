The assets of a collective investment undertaking subject to light investment rules shall consist of:

According to the terms and conditions set by order of:

1) Financial instruments as defined in Chapter 3 of this Code,

2) Deposits with credit institutions having the status of a bank,

3) And, secondarily, liquidity.

Variable capital investment companies subject to light investment rules shall not be allowed to own real estate other than that necessary to conduct their business and in accordance with the legislation in force. They may not create savings or provisions.
