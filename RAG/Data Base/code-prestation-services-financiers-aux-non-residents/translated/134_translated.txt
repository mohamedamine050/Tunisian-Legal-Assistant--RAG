Any legal professional or individual who operates, in fact or law, an institution that engages in the business of expert reports without obtaining a license, or who continues to engage in this activity after their license has been revoked and the one-year period specified in the last paragraph of Article 12 of this Code has expired, shall be punished by imprisonment for a term of sixteen days to one year and a fine ranging from 5,000 to 50,000 dinars, or by one of these two penalties. The penalty shall be doubled in case of recidivism.

**Expert Reports:**

Expert reports address scientific, technical, and other matters necessary for adjudicating a legal dispute, excluding legal issues.
