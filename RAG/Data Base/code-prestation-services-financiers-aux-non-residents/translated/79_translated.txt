The following shall be subject to prior authorization by the competent authorities in accordance with the conditions set out in this Code:

- Any change in the program of activities of the non-resident financial services provider licensed compared to the original scope of the license.

- Any change in the composition of licensed persons pursuant to point 5 of paragraph I of Article 72 of this Code.

In the case of a non-resident lending institution, the Banque Centrale de Tunisie shall consult with the Ministry of Finance regarding changes or new appointments. The silence of the Banque Centrale de Tunisie for a period of one month from the date of notification shall be considered consent.

- Any acquisition, directly or indirectly, by a person or group of persons, of shares in the capital of the non-resident financial services provider leading to a change in control, and in all cases, any operation leading to the ownership of one-tenth, one-fifth, one-third, one-half, or two-thirds of voting rights. The relevant authority shall take a decision on authorization within a maximum period of one month from the date of receipt of all required instructions.

- Any act that could result in the transfer of a significant portion of the service provider's assets or its commercial origin, and which could lead to a change in its financial structure or its business orientation.

- Any merger between non-resident financial service providers. The assessment carried out by the non-resident financial service provider concerned to determine the amount of the capital of the service provider resulting from the merger must be approved by the Banque Centrale de Tunisie and the Financial Market Authority, in accordance with the provisions of Article 74 of this Code.

- Any reduction in capital.
