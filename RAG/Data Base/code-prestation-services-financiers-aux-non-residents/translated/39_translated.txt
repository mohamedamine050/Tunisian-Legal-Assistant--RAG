The number of shares increases with the subscription of new shares and decreases with the repurchase of shares by the collective investment fund subject to previously subscribed simplified investment rules. The repurchase of previously subscribed shares is not permitted if the original value of the circulating shares falls to the equivalent of 400,000 dinars in convertible currency. If the original value of the total circulating shares remains below the equivalent of 800,000 dinars in convertible currency for a period of ninety days, the manager shall dissolve the fund.
