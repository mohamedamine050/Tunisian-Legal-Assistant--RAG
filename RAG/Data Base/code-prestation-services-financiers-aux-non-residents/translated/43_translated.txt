The manager and the custodian shall be individually or jointly liable, as the case may be, to third parties and to the holders of shares for any violation of the legal provisions and regulations applicable to the common investment fund, or breach of its internal regulations, or commission of errors in its respect.

Violations described as felonies in the meaning of this law are crimes punishable by death or imprisonment for a term exceeding five years. Violations described as misdemeanors are crimes punishable by imprisonment for a term exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars. Violations described as offenses are crimes punishable by imprisonment for a term not exceeding fifteen days or by a fine not exceeding sixty dinars. (Article 122 of the Code of Criminal Procedure)
