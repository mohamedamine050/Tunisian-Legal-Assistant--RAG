Non-resident lending institutions shall be:

- Either legal entities in the form of a limited liability company subject to Tunisian law,

- Or agencies or branches of legal entities whose registered office is located abroad in the form of a limited liability company or, where appropriate, in another form accepted upon granting the license, provided that it is in accordance with the legislation in force in their country of origin.

Non-resident investment service providers shall be legal entities in the form of a limited liability company subject to Tunisian law, with their registered office located in Tunisia.
