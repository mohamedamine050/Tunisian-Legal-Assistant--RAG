## English Translation:

The appointment of an individual to a position shall be made in accordance with the qualifications and skills required to perform the duties thereof, as set forth in the Articles of Association or the internal regulations of the expertise fund. 

The positions of manager and depositor for the same expertise fund cannot be held by the same person.

Moreover, the expertise fund, the manager, and the depositor shall conduct their activities with complete independence and for the benefit of the subscribers only. They must possess adequate guarantees in terms of organization, technical and financial resources, governance, and especially the integrity and competence of their managers.

Furthermore, they shall take all necessary measures to ensure the proper execution of operations.

The application forms for the second and third paragraphs of this chapter shall be determined by order of the Capital Market Authority.
