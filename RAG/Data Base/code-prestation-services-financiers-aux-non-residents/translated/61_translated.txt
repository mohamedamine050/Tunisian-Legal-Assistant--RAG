The providers of investment services for non-residents include non-resident credit institutions licensed as banks and non-resident investment institutions authorized to provide one or more investment services among those indicated in Chapters 53 and 54 of this Code, as well as non-resident portfolio management companies. Providers of non-resident investment services must comply with the rules applicable to the markets in which they operate. Providers of non-resident investment services, with the exception of non-resident portfolio management companies referred to in Chapter 65 of this Code, are authorized, within the framework of providing the services referred to in Chapter 53 of this Code and related to instruments defined in Chapter 3 thereof, to intervene in the non-resident segment of the Tunis Stock Exchange, in accordance with the conditions stipulated in the legislation regulating the financial market and the order of the Financial Market Authority concerning transactions on the non-resident segment of the exchange.
