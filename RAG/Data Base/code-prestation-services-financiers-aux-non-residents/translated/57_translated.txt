Non-resident lending institutions may, for the benefit of the clients they finance, carry out operations relating to foreign trade, including in particular:

* **Appointment**
* **Appointment**
* Placement of the appropriate individual in the position that matches the requirements and conditions for performing it, in terms of qualifications and competencies.
* Issuance of foreign trade fees and the opening of documented letters of credit.

Non-resident lending institutions shall enjoy the status of accepted intermediaries for foreign exchange and foreign trade operations they carry out with residents within the framework of the first paragraph of this chapter. Such institutions shall be subject, in this regard, to the same obligations as resident accepted intermediaries.
