The Central Bank of Tunisia sets the conditions for the practice of banking, the rules of conduct, and the prudential measures that non-resident lending institutions must comply with, particularly the rules relating to:

- The mandatory reserve for deposits in dinars,

- Liquidity ratios,

- Assistance provided by non-resident lending institutions to their independent branches,

- Risks in general,

- The use of own funds,

- The capital adequacy ratio, which is the ratio between own funds and liabilities,

- The ratio between own funds and assistance to each borrower, including assistance to persons related to the non-resident lending institution within the meaning of Article 71 of this Code.

Providers of non-resident investment services are subject to prudential rules established by order of the Financial Market Authority.
