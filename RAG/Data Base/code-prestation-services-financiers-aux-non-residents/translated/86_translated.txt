A non-resident financial service provider shall lose its status as a non-resident credit institution, non-resident investment institution, or non-resident portfolio management company upon the entry into force of the withdrawal decision. It shall be obliged to change its business name. The public shall be notified of the license withdrawal decision in accordance with the publicity procedures stipulated in the Commercial Companies Code. In the case of non-resident investment service providers, a notice shall also be published in the Official Gazette of the Capital Market Authority.
