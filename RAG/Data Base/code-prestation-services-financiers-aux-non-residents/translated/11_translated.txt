The establishment of an expertise fund, which addresses scientific, technical, and other matters necessary for adjudicating a legal dispute, excluding legal issues, is subject to licensing by the Capital Market Authority.  The Capital Market Authority shall respond to a licensing request within a maximum period of one month from the date of its submission along with the necessary documents. The forms for implementing this chapter shall be determined by an order of the Capital Market Authority. This fund is not subject to any form of appeal, modification, merger, division, or liquidation, pursuant to the applicable law.
