Any violation described as a felony, as defined by this law, meaning crimes that incur a punishment of death or imprisonment exceeding five years, shall be punished by imprisonment from three months to three years and a fine ranging from 5,000 to 50,000 dinars, or by one of these two punishments. Crimes that incur a punishment of imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or a fine exceeding sixty dinars, shall be described as misdemeanors.  Crimes that incur a punishment not exceeding fifteen days of imprisonment or sixty dinars fine shall be described as violations. 122 Code of Criminal Procedure

For the provisions of the first and second paragraphs of Article 66 of this Code, the punishment shall be doubled upon recidivism. The punishment is a penalty and a remedy imposed in the name of society on a person who is criminally liable for a crime committed based on a judicial ruling.
