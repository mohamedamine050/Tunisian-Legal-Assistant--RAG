The issuance of shares of an investment company with variable capital subject to relaxed investment rules or mutual fund units subject to relaxed investment rules, and their repurchase, shall be undertaken at any time upon the request of the shareholders or unit holders based on the liquidation value after adding or subtracting commissions, as applicable. The forms for subscribing to, acquiring, and repurchasing the units or shares issued by the investment company with variable capital subject to relaxed investment rules or the mutual fund subject to relaxed investment rules shall be regulated by the Capital Market Authority.
