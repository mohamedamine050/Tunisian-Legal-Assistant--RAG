Investment services relate to the financial instruments stipulated in Chapter 3 of this Code and include the following services and activities:

1. Receiving and forwarding orders on behalf of others,

2. Executing orders on behalf of others, subject to the exclusive intervention of the regulated section of the Tunis Stock Exchange, granted in accordance with the established regulations for the benefit of licensed stockbrokers under Law No. 117 of 1994 dated November 14, 1994, relating to the reorganization of the financial market,

3. Managing financial portfolios on behalf of others,

4. Underwriting issues and guaranteed placements,

5. Ordinary placements.

The Capital Market Authority shall define these services by means of a regulation.
