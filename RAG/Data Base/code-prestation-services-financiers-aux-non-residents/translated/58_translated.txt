Non-resident financial service providers shall include non-resident lending institutions and non-resident investment service providers as defined in the subsequent sections of this chapter. Financial service providers licensed under this Law shall be considered non-residents for the purpose of legislation pertaining to currency exchange. They shall be referred to in this Law as non-resident financial service providers. Non-resident financial service providers, except those licensed as public limited banks, shall be considered as resorting to a non-resident investment service provider or any means of advertising or lobbying when they seek to employ their securities.
