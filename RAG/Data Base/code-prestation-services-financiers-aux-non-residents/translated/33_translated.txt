Investment companies with variable capital subject to relaxed investment rules shall conduct an inventory of their assets under the supervision of their custodian within thirty days from the end of each three-month period. They shall publish the composition of their assets in the official bulletin of the Capital Market Authority at the end of each three-month period, within thirty days from the end of each three-month period. The auditor shall certify the accuracy of the inventory before publication. 

Investment companies with variable capital subject to relaxed investment rules shall also prepare annual financial statements in accordance with the applicable accounting regulations and publish them in the Official Gazette of the Tunisian Republic at least thirty days before the date of the ordinary general meeting. 

Furthermore, investment companies with variable capital subject to relaxed investment rules shall republish their financial statements if any changes are made to them after the ordinary general meeting.
