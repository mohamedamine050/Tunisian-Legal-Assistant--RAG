## English Translation:

The services related to investment services include the following:

1. Safekeeping and administration of financial instruments for the benefit of others and ancillary services related to these activities, such as keeping accounts of funds related to these financial instruments.

2. Providing advice to companies on capital structuring, industrial strategy, and related matters, as well as providing advice and services in the field of mergers and acquisitions.

3. Providing advice and conducting research in the field of investment and financial analysis, and providing other general recommendations regarding transactions related to financial instruments.

4. Services related to underwriting.

5. Services and activities similar to investment services or services related to them, and related to the underlying assets of derivative financial instruments as defined in Chapter 3 of this Code.

6. Participation in the capital of existing companies or companies in the process of formation.
