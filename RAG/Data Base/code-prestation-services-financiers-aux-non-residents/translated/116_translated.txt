For the purpose of exercising its supervisory functions, the Central Bank of Tunisia or the Financial Market Authority, as the case may be, may conduct an investigation with any natural or legal person. In addition to the officials referred to in numbers 3 and 4 of Article 10 of the Code of Criminal Procedure, sworn officers, qualified for this purpose by the Central Bank of Tunisia or the Financial Market Authority, as the case may be, shall conduct these investigations. These officers must be at least of the equivalent rank of “A” as referred to in Law No. 112 of 1983 dated December 12, 1983, concerning the regulation of the general statute of state officials, local authorities and public institutions of an administrative nature. The Central Bank of Tunisia or the Financial Market Authority cannot be challenged on the grounds of professional secrecy when performing their supervisory duties.
