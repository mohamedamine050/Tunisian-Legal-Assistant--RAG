The response to the bequest must be made within a maximum period of two months from the date the legatee is notified of the will after the testator's death. The silence of the legatee, after being notified of the will, until the expiry of the aforementioned period, shall be considered acceptance. If the legatee dies during this period, his/her heirs shall take his/her place from the date they are notified of the will.
