**Heirs entitled to one-fifth:**

1. The husband, provided that the wife has no male or female descendant inheriting from her.
2. The daughter, provided that she is the sole descendant from the deceased’s loins, whether male or female.
3. The daughter of the son, provided that she is the sole descendant from the deceased’s loins, whether male or female, and the son of the son.
4. The full sister, provided that the father, the male or female descendant from the deceased’s loins, and the son of the son, as well as the brother of the full sister, are all absent.
5. The paternal half-sister, provided that she is the sole descendant from the deceased’s loins, excluding the full sister, the brother, and the other paternal half-sisters.
