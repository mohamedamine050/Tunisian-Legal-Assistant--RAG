The rightful inheritors, by themselves, are ranked, with each rank preceding the next, and they are: 1) Sons; 2) Then, Fathers; 3) Then, Grandfathers and Brothers, forming a single rank; 4) Then, Sons of Brothers; 5) Then, Uncles and their sons, forming a single rank, however, their order within the rank is determined by proximity; 6) Then, the State Treasury.
