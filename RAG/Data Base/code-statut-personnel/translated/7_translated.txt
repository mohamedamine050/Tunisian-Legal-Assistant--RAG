The pursuit of Civil Status Officers and Notaries who do not comply with the provisions of the first chapter of this law shall be before the **Court of First Instance** within whose territorial jurisdiction they are located, and they shall be punished by a fine of one hundred dinars. This law shall be published in the Official Gazette of the Tunisian Republic and shall be enforced as a law of the State. Issued in Gafsa on the 29th of Jumada al-Thani 1384 (November 3, 1964). President of the Tunisian Republic Habib Bourguiba.
