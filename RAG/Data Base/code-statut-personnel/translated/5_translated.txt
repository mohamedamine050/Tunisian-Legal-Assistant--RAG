(Amended by Decree No. 1 of 1964 dated February 20, 1964, ratified by Law No. 1 of 1964 dated April 21, 1964, and by Law No. 32 of 2007 dated May 14, 2007). - Both spouses must be free from any legal impediments. Furthermore, neither of them may be under the age of eighteen years to enter into a marriage contract.  Entering into a marriage contract before the specified age requires special authorization from the Courts, which shall only be granted for serious reasons and for the clear benefit of the spouses.
