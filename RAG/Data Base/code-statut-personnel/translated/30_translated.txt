The officer who prepared the document shall, upon the ship's arrival at the port where its equipment is removed, deliver to the Ship's Register a copy of each birth certificate prepared on board the ship, for which a copy has not been deposited in accordance with the provisions of the aforementioned article. This deposit shall be made with the Maritime Authority office. The deposited copy shall then be forwarded to the Secretary of State for the Navy, who in turn shall forward it in accordance with the provisions of the aforementioned article.
