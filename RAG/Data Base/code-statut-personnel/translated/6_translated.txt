The record shall specify the year, day, and hour of its receipt, the name of the civil status officer, and the names, titles, professions, and addresses of all persons mentioned in the record, including:

a)  The father and mother's dates and places of birth in birth records.
b)  The deceased's date and place of birth in death records.

These details shall be recorded when known. If not known, the ages of the persons mentioned shall be specified by stating the year (as is the case for the informant's age). With respect to the witnesses, it shall be sufficient to state that they are of legal age.
