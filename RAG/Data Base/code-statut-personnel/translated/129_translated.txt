**The sixteen legal heirs who are barred from inheritance are:**

1) The son. 
2) The son's son, and so on down the male line.
3) The daughter.
4) The daughter of the son. 
5) The full brother.
6) The paternal half-brother.
7) The son of the full brother.
8) The son of the paternal half-brother.
9) The full paternal uncle.
10) The son of the full paternal uncle.
11) The daughter or granddaughter together with the full sister.
12) The two full sisters.
13) The father.
14) The paternal grandfather.
15) The mother.
16) The maternal grandmother.
