## Will (Wasiyya)

**Will (Wasiyya)** is a transfer of ownership that takes effect after death, by way of gift, whether it be in the form of a specific asset or a benefit.

**In the case of a benefit in a specific thing, the term specified for the beneficiary shall be implemented. If no term is specified, the beneficiary shall have the right to enjoy the benefit for their lifetime, unless the Will indicates otherwise.**
