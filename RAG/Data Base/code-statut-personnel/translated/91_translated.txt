A share is a predetermined portion allocated to an heir in the inheritance. The inheritance process begins with those who are entitled to specific shares ("al-farā'iḍ"). These include:

**For men:**

1. The father
2. The paternal grandfather, regardless of how high in the lineage he is
3. The maternal uncle
4. The husband

**For women:**

1. The mother
2. The grandmother
3. The daughter
4. The son's daughter, even if she descends from several generations
5. The full sister
6. The paternal half-sister
7. The maternal half-sister
8. The wife
