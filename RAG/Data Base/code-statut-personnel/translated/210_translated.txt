The donor, while taking into account the legally acquired rights of third parties, may request revocation of his gift for one of the following reasons, unless there is any impediment stipulated in Article 212:

1. If the donee fails to fulfill his obligations towards the donor in a manner constituting grave ingratitude.

2. If the donor becomes unable to provide for his own subsistence in accordance with his social status, or if he becomes incapable of fulfilling his legal obligations regarding maintenance.

3. If the donor begets a child who remains alive at the time of the revocation.
