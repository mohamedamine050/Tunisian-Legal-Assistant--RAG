A son and his son, each, exclude the husband, reducing his share from one-half to one-fourth, and the wife, reducing her share from one-fourth to one-eighth. 

The agreed upon price between the contracting parties is valid, except when it exceeds or falls short of the value of the object sold.

The mother, her share is reduced from one-third to one-sixth, and the father and grandfather, their shares are reduced from their rightful share (ta'seeb) to one-sixth.
