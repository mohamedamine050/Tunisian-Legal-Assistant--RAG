**(Amended by Law No. 7 of 1981 dated February 18, 1981).** The minor's guardian is his/her father or mother if the father dies or loses his/her legal capacity, taking into account the provisions of Chapter Eight of this Code concerning marriage. The father's testamentary disposition shall not be effective until the death or loss of legal capacity of the mother. Upon the death or loss of legal capacity of both parents and if the minor has no guardian, the governor shall be appointed as guardian.
