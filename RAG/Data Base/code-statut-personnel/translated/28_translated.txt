In the event of a birth during a sea voyage, the birth certificate shall be drawn up within twenty-four hours following the birth, according to the father's declaration if he is on board the ship, or according to the declarations of two witnesses from among the ship's officers, or failing that, from among the ship's crew. If the birth occurs while the ship is docked in a port, the certificate shall be drawn up according to the same provisions if communication with land is impossible or if there are no Tunisian diplomatic or consular agents in the port with the status of civil status officer, particularly if the port is located in a foreign country. This certificate shall be drawn up on board state ships by the captain, and on other ships by the ship's owner. It shall state the circumstances mentioned above under which the certificate was drawn up. The certificate shall be included in the ship's logbook.
