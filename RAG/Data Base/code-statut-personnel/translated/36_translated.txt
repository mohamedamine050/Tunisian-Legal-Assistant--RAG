(Amended by Decree No. 2 of 1964 dated February 20, 1964, ratified by Law No. 2 of 1964 dated April 21, 1964).- A marriage concluded in violation of the provisions of Article 31 above is considered null and void. Furthermore, both spouses shall be punished by imprisonment for a period of three months. If criminal proceedings are initiated pursuant to the provisions of the preceding paragraph, a single judgment shall be issued on the crime and the annulment of the marriage. If the spouses continue to cohabit after the declaration of the annulment of their marriage, they shall be punished by imprisonment for a period of six months. Article 53 of the Criminal Code shall not apply to the offenses prescribed by this article.
