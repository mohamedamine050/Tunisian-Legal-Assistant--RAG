The officer who has drawn up a civil status record shall, as soon as possible upon the completion of the  **appeal**, forward a copy thereof to the Secretary of State for National Defense, who shall register it in the civil status registers at the last place of residence of the father or of the mother if the father is unknown in the case of births, and in the civil status registers at the last place of residence of the husband in the case of marriage contracts, and at the last place of residence of the deceased in the case of deaths. If the last place of residence is unknown, the registration shall be made in the municipality of Tunis.
