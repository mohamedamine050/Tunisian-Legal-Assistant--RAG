The holders of the two-thirds share are four:

1. Two or more daughters, provided that they are alone (i.e., without a son).

2. The daughters of the son, provided that they are alone (i.e., without a son of the son, whether male or female, and a son of the son).

3. Two sisters, provided that they are alone (i.e., without the father, or a son of the son, whether male or female, or a male sibling).

4. Two paternal half-sisters, provided that they are alone (i.e., without those mentioned in the case of sisters, or a maternal half-brother).
