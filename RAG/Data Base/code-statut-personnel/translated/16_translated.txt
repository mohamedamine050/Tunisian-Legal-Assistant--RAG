Every civil status document relating to Tunisians and foreigners issued outside the Kingdom shall be recognized if it was issued in accordance with the regulations in force in that country. The documents relating to Tunisians shall be registered in the current year's civil status registers held by the officers of the diplomatic or consular service with territorial jurisdiction. This registration shall be noted briefly in the margin of the registers on the date of the document's issuance. In the event of a break in diplomatic relations or the closure of the competent diplomatic or consular post, and if registration becomes impossible under the conditions stated above, the document shall be deposited exceptionally with the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, which may issue a copy of it. As soon as circumstances permit, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs shall register this document under the aforementioned conditions.
