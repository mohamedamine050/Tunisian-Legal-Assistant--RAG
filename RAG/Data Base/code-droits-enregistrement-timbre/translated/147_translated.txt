The rate of the fee is set at:

- 6% for insurance contracts.  A contract is a legally binding agreement that obligates the contracting parties and defines their obligations and rights concerning the specific transaction.  In it, the insurer undertakes to pay the insured or the beneficiary a sum of money, a regular income, or any other financial compensation in the event of the occurrence of the insured event, in exchange for premiums or any other financial payment made by the insured to the insurer. This applies to marine and air navigation risks and was specified by Article 45 of Law No. 98 of 2000, dated December 25, 2000, amended by Article 27 of Law No. 70 of 2007, dated December 27, 2007, and by Article 48 of Law No. 66 of 2017, dated December 18, 2017.

- 12% for other insurance contracts. A contract is a legally binding agreement that obligates the contracting parties and defines their obligations and rights concerning the specific transaction. In it, the insurer undertakes to pay the insured or the beneficiary a sum of money, a regular income, or any other financial compensation in the event of the occurrence of the insured event, in exchange for premiums or any other financial payment made by the insured to the insurer. This was specified by Article 48 of Law No. 66 of 2017, dated December 18, 2017.
