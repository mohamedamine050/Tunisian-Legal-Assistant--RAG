Registration is mandatory within thirty days from the date of the instruments:

1. Notarial deeds that do not affect the legal status of real estate and commercial assets.
2. Powers of attorney, promises to sell, and contracts transferring ownership, and generally all instruments drawn up within the scope of the profession of an intermediary for the sale and purchase of real estate or commercial assets, as well as instruments drawn up within the scope of the profession of purchasing such assets for the purpose of resale, unless they are drawn up by notarial deeds.
