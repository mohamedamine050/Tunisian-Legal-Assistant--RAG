I. The registration fees for judgments and decisions are calculated and collected based on a summary card of the judgment or decision issued by the court clerk. This card is mandatory in duplicate; one copy is delivered to the party required to pay the fee, and the other is sent with the judgment or decision to the relevant financial collection office. II. The fees are calculated on the card stipulated in the first paragraph of this chapter and in the margin allocated for this purpose, based on the judgment or decision. III. The financial collector shall effect the registration at the initiative of the party claiming the fee, after comparing the two copies of the card referred to in the first paragraph of this chapter and specifying the fees collected on these two copies.
