Judgments and decisions issued by all courts,  judging:

- Conviction or liquidation; - Transfer of ownership of real estate, its partition, exchange, or recognition of a tenant's right to a property in lease, or determination of its value, or transfer of shares or bonds granting their holders the right to exploit a property; transfer of ownership of a commercial asset or its lease, or transfer of rights in clientele, or recognition of a tenant's right to a commercial asset in lease; - Public sales of movable property; - Partition of movable property belonging to an estate or to the assets of a company, must be registered within one hundred and twenty days from the date of their pronouncement. (Amended by Article 1 of Chapter 69 of Act No. 78 of 2016 dated 17/12/2016)
