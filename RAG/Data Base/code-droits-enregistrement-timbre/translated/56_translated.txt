The payment of registration fees shall be borne by:

1. The first attesting notaries for their deeds attesting to leases, or real estate mortgages, or those that do not affect the legal status of real estate and commercial assets, as well as for the handwritten contracts attached thereto.  2. The executing notaries and other persons qualified to draw up minutes and notices for documents related to their functions. 3. The parties for judgments and decisions. 4. The heirs and legatees, or their guardians, for the fees levied on inheritances, bequests, and other donation contracts due to death. 5. The parties for all other contracts and transfers subject to mandatory registration.
