The following employees are exempt from registration fees on inheritances:

1.  Legacies granted to projects and organizations of public interest, or those of a charitable, educational, scientific, social, or cultural nature, whose list is determined by order. (Amended by Article 95 of Law No. 127 of 1994, dated 26/12/1994).
2.  Legacies granted to the State, local public entities, public administrative institutions, and public hospital institutions.
