The stamp duty required for administrative documents listed under numbers 1, 2, 5, and 7, and the first paragraph of number 8 of paragraph II of Article 117 of this Code, shall be paid via receipts issued by the financial collection offices (as specified in Article 35 of the Complementary Finance Law No. 54 of 2014, dated August 19, 2014). The date and methods of implementing this procedure shall be determined by a decision of the Minister of Finance.  Added by Article 40 of Finance Law No. 27 of 2012, dated December 29, 2012.
