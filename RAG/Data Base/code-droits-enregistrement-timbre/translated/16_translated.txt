I. The registration fees due shall be proportional, progressive, or fixed, depending on the nature of the contracts and transfers subject thereto. II. Proportional or progressive fees shall be applied to amounts and values without regard to anything less than one dinar. III. In the event that the amounts and values are not specified in a contract subject to a proportional or progressive fee, the parties are required to specify them before registration by means of an estimated declaration, the authenticity of which is attested, and signed at the bottom of the contract.
