I -  Each beneficiary of a safe deposit box, and any person aware of the death of a beneficiary of this box, or one of its beneficiaries, or the spouse of this beneficiary, or his partners, shall be personally required to open this box themselves or through another, without regard to the provisions of Article 96 of this Code, to pay the registration fees levied on inheritances and the penalties incurred on the sums, securities, or objects contained within the safe deposit box, while retaining their right to pursue claims for these fees and penalties, if necessary. II - The heir and the legatee shall be jointly and severally liable for the payment of these fees and penalties with the person or persons mentioned in the first paragraph of this Article, if they failed to mention these securities, sums, or objects in the inheritance declaration.
