1 - Civil officers, public officials, judges, experts, and public administrations cannot issue any decision or order based on a contract or document that has not been stamped with the required tax stamp in accordance with the law. II - The provisions of the third subparagraph of Article 83 and Article 87 of this Code concerning the duties of public officials regarding contracts based on other contracts, apply to the matter of the tax stamp. III - Judges or public officials cannot number or sign a document subject to tax stamp if its papers have not been stamped with this tax. IV - Notwithstanding the provisions of Article 6, paragraph seven, of the Code of Civil Procedure, bailiffs must clearly indicate at the bottom of the original notice and each copy thereof:

- The number of copies served and the number of pages used for each copy of the original or of the documents served.  The amount of the tax stamp required.
