The registration fee due on the contracts stipulated in Article 19 (new) of Chapter 20 of this Code shall be calculated on the basis of their value, considering all the fees and payments due according to the legislation in force. However, for the aforementioned contracts concluded for an indefinite period or for a period exceeding three years, the registration fee due shall be calculated on the basis of the contract value for the first three years. (Added by Article 51, Law No. 27 of 2012 dated 29/12/2012 and amended by Article 4 of Chapter 33 of Law No. 66 of 2017 dated 18/12/2017)
