II. The fixed registration fees set out in the first paragraph of this section apply to each page of each copy of the contract submitted for registration, except for the copy retained by the tax office in accordance with the provisions of the first paragraph of Article 92 of this Code. The tax collector must indicate at the bottom of the retained copy the number of copies submitted for registration.  For deeds of notarial attestation and contracts relating to companies referred to in numbers 19, 20, and 21 of the first paragraph of this section, the fee applies per contract.

III.  The benefit of registration with the fixed fee for the contracts referred to in numbers 9, 10, and 11 of the first paragraph of this section is conditional upon the submission of a certificate issued by the governor, attached to the aforementioned contracts and documents, expressly stating that the transfer or lease is part of the consolidation of agricultural land ownership. In the event of non-compliance with the conditions mentioned in numbers 10 and 11 above, those benefiting from registration with the fixed fee must pay the proportional registration fee due on these transactions as of the date of the contract, plus the late payment penalty provided for in the second paragraph of Article 102 of this Code.

IV. The benefit of registration with the fixed fee for the contracts referred to in number 12(b) of the first paragraph of this section is conditional upon attaching to the aforementioned contracts:

A copy of the decision approving the subdivision for plots, except for contracts relating to acquisitions made from the Industrial Real Estate Agency and the Tourist Real Estate Agency. (Amended by Article 84 of Law No. 53 of 2015 dated 25/12/2015). A copy of the investment declaration certificate provided for in current tax legislation. (Added by Article 20 of Law No. 98 of 2000 dated 25/12/2000 and amended by Article 22 of Law No. 8 of 2017 dated 14/02/2017 concerning the review of the system of tax privileges).

IV(a). Registration with the fixed fee provided for in number 18(b) of the schedule of fixed registration fees set out in paragraph I of this section shall be granted once every five years for each property right relating to the same property. (Added by Article 1 of Section 34 of Law No. 66 of 2017 dated 18/12/2017).

IV(b). The benefit of registration with the fixed fee provided for in number 12(h) of the first paragraph of this section is conditional upon the Agency's undertaking within the contract to allocate the acquired properties to the purposes mentioned in this number. In the event of the Agency's failure to comply with the obligation stipulated in this paragraph, either by transferring the properties concerned or by using them contrary to the terms of the obligation, it shall be required to pay the outstanding registration fees waived under the exemption, plus the applicable late payment penalties in accordance with tax legislation. (Added by Article 2 of Section 45 of Law No. 78 of 2019 dated 23/12/2019).

V.  Benefitting from the provisions of number 21 of the schedule contained in this section requires:

1. That the companies participating in mergers or complete divisions, or created within the framework of the same operations, are subject to corporate tax.
2. That the accounts of the companies concerned by the merger or complete division, or benefiting from the contribution, are legally subject to audit by an auditor, and that their accounts have been approved for the financial year preceding the year of the merger, complete division, or contribution.
3. That the company to which the assets have been transferred does not transfer the assets benefiting from registration with the fixed fee during the three years following the year of the merger, complete division, or contribution, except for transfers within the framework of a merger or a complete transfer of the business. In the event of a transfer of some of these assets during the aforementioned period, the proportional fee applicable to sales shall be due on the asset(s) transferred up to the amount of the liabilities assumed, plus late payment penalties calculated in accordance with tax legislation from the date of expiry of the legal deadline for registering the merger, complete division, or contribution. (Added by Article 38 of Law No. 90 of 2004 dated 31/12/2004).

VI. In addition to compliance with the conditions referred to in numbers 2 and 3 of paragraph V above, benefiting from the provisions of number 21(a) of the schedule contained in this section requires:

1. That the owner of the sole proprietorship has filed a declaration of existence for the activity of his business and that the business has commenced actual operation on the date of the contribution.
2. That the owner of the sole proprietorship is subject to income tax under the real system and that the commercial assets and real estate subject to the contribution are included in the assets of the balance sheet for the year preceding the year of the contribution. (Added by Article 40 of Law No. 90 of 2004 dated 31/12/2004).

VII. (Repealed by Article 23 of Section 15 of Law No. 8 of 2017 dated 14/02/2017 concerning the review of the system of tax privileges).

VIII. In the case provided for in number 20(d) of the schedule contained in this section, if operations cease before the expiry of three years from January 1st of the year following the transfer, the institution shall be required to pay the fee applicable to sales, plus the applicable late payment penalties in accordance with current legislation. (Amended by Article 24 of Section 15 of Law No. 8 of 2017 dated 14/02/2017 concerning the review of the system of tax privileges). These provisions shall not apply if operations cease for reasons beyond the control of the institution, as determined by order. (Added by Article 18 of Law No. 85 of 2006 dated 25/12/2006).

IX. The provisions of number 23(a) of the schedule contained in this section shall apply regardless of the provisions of number 23 of the same schedule. (Added by Article 23 of Law No. 58 of 2010 dated 17/12/2010).

Article 24. The declaration of subscription and payment received by the tax collector pursuant to the provisions of Article 170 of the Commercial Companies Code shall incur a fee of 150 dinars. (Amended by Article 91 of Law No. 123 of 2001 dated 28/12/2001, by Article 59 of Law No. 90 of 2004 dated 31/12/2004, and by Article 48 of Law No. 27 of 2012 dated 29/12/2012).
