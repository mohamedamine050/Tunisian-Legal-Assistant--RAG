I - The following shall be considered jointly and severally liable for the payment of stamp duty, penalties, and fines related thereto:

- All signatory parties in respect of bilateral contracts.

- Creditors and debtors in respect of loans and letters of credit.

- Notaries public, executing notaries, arbitrators, experts, and court clerks who concluded contracts without affixing stamp duty, or who received or drafted contracts mentioning contracts or documents without affixed stamp duty.

- The sender and the carrier mentioned in contracts and transport documents.

- Generally, all other persons who drafted contracts or writings subject to stamp duty.

II - The stamp duty due on contracts concluded between the State and private individuals shall be borne solely by the private individuals, regardless of any provision to the contrary.
