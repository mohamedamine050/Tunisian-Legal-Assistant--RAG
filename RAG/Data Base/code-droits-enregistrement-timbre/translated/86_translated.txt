The court clerks and the general secretary of the Administrative Court shall deposit the judgments and decisions referred to in Article 5 of this Code with the competent financial cashier within sixty days following the date of the judgment's pronouncement.  They shall also provide the competent financial cashier with a copy of each judgment or decision, written by the court, that resolves a legal or factual issue;  this includes judgments of conviction or executive instruments concerning costs in cases of legal aid, within ninety days from the date of the judgment's pronouncement.
