The registration fee levied on real estate transactions shall be calculated on the value of one of the properties, or on the value of the smallest property in the event of a supplemental difference or increase in value.  The supplemental difference or increase in value shall be subject to the registration fee levied on the sale of real estate.  Properties, regardless of their nature, shall be valued according to their actual commercial value on the date of transfer, as per the parties' valuation declaration.
