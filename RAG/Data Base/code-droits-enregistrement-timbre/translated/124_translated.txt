Payment shall be made compulsorily by declaration by legal entities subject to corporate tax, even if exempt therefrom, for the stamp duty levied on invoices, international air and sea transport tickets for individuals, technical inspection certificates for means of transport, and telephone or internet financing cards and operations. (Amended by Article 47 of Law No. 106 of 2005 dated 19/12/2005 and by Article 50, paragraph 5, of Law No. 66 of 2017 dated 18/12/2017).

However, the tax administration may authorize other persons to pay the duty levied on invoices, tickets, and certificates by declaration. (Repealed and replaced by Article 93 of Law No. 80 of 2003 dated 29/12/2003).
