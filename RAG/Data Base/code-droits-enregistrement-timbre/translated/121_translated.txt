The tax stamp is collected according to one of the following methods:

1. By affixing transferable tax stamps.
2. By using papers of a specified value. (Amended by Article 97 of Law No. 80 of 2003, dated December 29, 2003).

3. (Abrogated by Article 97 of Law No. 80 of 2003, dated December 29, 2003).
4. By means of a financial collector's endorsement.
5. By means of a declaration. (Added by Article 87 of Law No. 88 of 1997, dated December 29, 1997).
6. By means of collection receipts. (Added by Article 39 of Law No. 27 of 2012, dated December 29, 2012).

7. By means of reliable electronic methods. (Added by Article 3 of Article 23 of Law No. 78 of 2019, dated December 23, 2019).
