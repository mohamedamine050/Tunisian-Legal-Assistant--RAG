I - In the event that the contract contains independent or not necessarily interdependent clauses, the registration fee shall be calculated based on the tariff for each clause.  The contract is a legally binding agreement that obligates the contracting parties and defines their obligations and rights concerning the specific transaction.

II - It is not possible to extract multiple fixed duties on the same contract. In the event that the contract contains several clauses subject to the fixed duty, the highest duty among these duties must be extracted. The extraction is the process by which the creditor obtains repayment of the financial debt contracted by his debtor.
