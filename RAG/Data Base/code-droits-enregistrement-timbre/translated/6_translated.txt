Verbal Agreements

I - In the absence of written documents, the transfers referred to in the third paragraph of Section Three of this Code are subject to declaratory permits to be submitted within sixty days of the date of acquisition of the properties subject to these transfers. However, the previous owner enjoys an additional period of thirty days to submit these permits. II - In the absence of written agreements concerning the transfer of the right to exploit real estate, these agreements shall be replaced by detailed declaratory permits to be submitted by the beneficiary within sixty days of the date of exploitation.
