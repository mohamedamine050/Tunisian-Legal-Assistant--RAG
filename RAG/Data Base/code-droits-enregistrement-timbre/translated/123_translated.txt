I - The levy is the process by which the creditor obtains payment from his debtor of the financial debt contracted towards him.  The tax stamp duty for shipping documents prepared for the maritime transport of goods is levied by affixing one or more transferable tax stamps to the original copy given to the captain, according to the number of original copies.  A mark, indicated by a control stamp, is placed on each of the other originals. When the original is not submitted, the tax stamp is affixed to the customs declaration.  Captains of Tunisian and foreign vessels must present to customs officers, upon entry or exit, the shipping documents in their possession. II - The tax stamp duty for air transport contracts for goods is levied by affixing a transferable tax stamp to the contract; failing that, it is affixed to the customs declaration.
