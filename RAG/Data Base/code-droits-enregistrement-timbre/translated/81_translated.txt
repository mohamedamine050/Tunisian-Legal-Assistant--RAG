To claim registration fees and penalties, the following shall be considered sufficiently established unless proven otherwise:

1. Transfer of ownership or ownership of the freehold of a property or the right to use it, namely:

- By filing a registration application in the name of the new owner.

- By all contracts and documents that may indicate the existence of the transfer or the establishment of the new owner's right in the property.

2. Transfer of ownership of the commercial business or the right to clientele, namely by all contracts and documents that may indicate the existence or establishment of the new owner's right, or by payment of taxes levied on traders.
