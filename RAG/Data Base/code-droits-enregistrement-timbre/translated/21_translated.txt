I. To benefit from the progressive registration fee referred to in Article 20, Section IV of this Code, the purchase agreement must include a statement specifying that the land was acquired for this purpose.  The contract is a legally binding agreement that obligates the contracting parties and defines their obligations and rights with respect to the specific transaction. II. The competent authorities may not issue building permits except in accordance with the commitment undertaken by the purchaser in the purchase agreement. III. The purchaser shall be deprived of benefiting from the progressive fee and shall be required to pay the balance of the due fees, plus the late payment penalty stipulated in paragraph 2 of Article 102 of this Code, in either of the following cases: - Transfer of the land before the completion of construction; - Change of the intended use of the purchased land stipulated in the purchase agreement.
