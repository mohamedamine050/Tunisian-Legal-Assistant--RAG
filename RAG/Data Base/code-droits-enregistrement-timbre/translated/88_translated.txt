I - Executing bailiffs shall submit the register required to be kept in accordance with the texts governing their profession every three months to the tax collector whose jurisdiction covers their place of residence for endorsement.  This endorsement shall specify the number of registered contracts. This submission shall be made within the first fifteen days of January, April, July, and October. II -  Notaries shall submit their draft registers and work registers, required to be kept in accordance with the texts governing their profession, every three months to the competent tax collector for endorsement. The aforementioned submission shall take place within the last fifteen days of January, April, July, and October.  In addition, notaries shall submit monthly to the tax collection office a statement of the contracts for which they were entrusted with the collection of registration fees levied thereon, immediately upon their entry in their register according to their date. This statement shall be made on a form provided by the administration.
