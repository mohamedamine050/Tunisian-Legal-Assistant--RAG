Subject to the provisions of Article 58 of this Code, the parties not ordered to bear costs and those benefiting from the registration of the judgment or decision at the minimum fee pursuant to Article 36 of this Code, shall be required to pay the applicable proportional fee on the amounts collected for their benefit from the execution of the judgment or decision within a maximum period of thirty days from the date of collection. This shall be done based on a statement using a form prepared by the administration, specifying in particular the number and date of the judgment or decision, the amount awarded, and the amount collected, and accompanied by a copy of the document proving the execution of the judgment or decision.

The penalties relating to deductions from the source in the context of income tax on individuals and corporate tax shall apply to the proportional fee due on the collected amounts.
