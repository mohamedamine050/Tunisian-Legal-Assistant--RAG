The stamp duty payable on machine-readable promissory notes shall be paid by the person authorized to print them, via a monthly declaration deposited with the financial receiver, within the deadlines stipulated in paragraph III of Article 119 of this Code.  The printing of machine-readable promissory notes requires prior authorization. The procedures and conditions for printing and distributing machine-readable promissory notes, the obligations borne by the person undertaking the printing, and the date of entry into force of the procedure shall be determined by a decision of the Minister of Finance (added by Article 77 of Law No. 123 of 2001 dated 28/12/2001).
