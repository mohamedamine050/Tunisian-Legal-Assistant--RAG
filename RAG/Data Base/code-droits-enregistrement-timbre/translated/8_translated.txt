I - The date of the contract and the date of the opening of the succession shall not be taken into account for the purpose of calculating the deadlines set for registration.  If the expiry date for registration or payment of the required fees coincides with a day on which the financial departments are closed, this deadline shall be extended to the first working day following. II - For administrative contracts subject to the approval of the supervisory authority, the registration period shall commence from the day the contracting authority submits the contract for the approval of the supervisory authority. III - For transfers executed pursuant to expropriation orders for the public interest, the registration period shall commence from the date of the offer to the expropriated party of the expropriation compensation (added by Article 63 of Law No. 101 of 1999 dated 31/12/1999).
