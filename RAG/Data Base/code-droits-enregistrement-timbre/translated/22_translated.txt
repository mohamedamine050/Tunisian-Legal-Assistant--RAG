(Repealed and replaced by Article 3 of Law No. 78 of 2016 dated December 17, 2016).  It is not permissible to effect recovery of less than 40 Dinars when registering contracts, transfers, judgments, decisions, and writings subject to a relative or progressive registration fee.  Recovery is the process by which a creditor obtains payment from his debtor of the financial debt contracted towards him.
