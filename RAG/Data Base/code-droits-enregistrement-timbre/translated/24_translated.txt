The declaration of the receipt and payment received by the financial receiver, pursuant to the provisions of Article 170 of the Commercial Companies Code, requires collection.

Collection is the process by which a creditor obtains payment from his debtor for the financial debt contracted towards him.

The amount of the receipt and payment is specified as 150 Dinars (amended by Article 91 of Law No. 123 of 2001 dated 28/12/2001, Article 59 of Law No. 90 of 2004 dated 31/12/2004, and Article 48 of Law No. 27 of 2012 dated 29/12/2012).
