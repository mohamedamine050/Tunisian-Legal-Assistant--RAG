I. The financial collector's visa is used for contracts and instruments subject to a proportional or progressive registration fee, as well as for contracts and instruments on which the fee has been improperly applied.  Crimes punishable by death or imprisonment exceeding five years are described as felonies within the meaning of this law. Crimes punishable by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars, are described as misdemeanors. Crimes punishable by imprisonment not exceeding fifteen days or a fine not exceeding sixty dinars are described as offenses. (Article 122 of the Code of Criminal Procedure). The provisions of this Code apply, and this visa is affixed concurrently with the registration procedure. II. This process consists of placing the following specifications on the contracts and instruments:

- Indication of the tax stamp.
- Name of the relevant tax office.
- Date of the visa.
- Amount of the fee in writing.
- Seal and signature of the collector.
