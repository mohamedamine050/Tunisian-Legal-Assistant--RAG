**(Amended by Article 1 of Law No. 13 of 2005 dated January 26, 2005.)** - Any person who engages in land breaking, either personally or through another, without prior authorization shall be punished with a fine ranging from 100 to 1000 dinars for each hectare of land broken. Provided that the fine shall not be less than the minimum amount if the area of the land is less than one hectare. In the case of recidivism, the maximum fine shall always be applied, and a prison sentence of eight days may be imposed in addition.
