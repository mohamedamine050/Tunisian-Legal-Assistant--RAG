Forest products transported or offered for sale in violation of the provisions of Articles 105 and 106 above shall be confiscated. The goods contained therein, and where applicable, the vehicles, draft animals, and animals used to transport them, shall be seized and deposited with a custodian. The seizure referred to in the second paragraph of this Article, and where applicable, the sale of the goods, vehicles, draft animals, and pack animals, shall be carried out according to the procedures laid down in Articles 124 to 129 of this Code.
