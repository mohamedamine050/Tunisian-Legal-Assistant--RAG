**(Amended by Law No. 28 of 2001 dated March 19, 2001).** - The offering, sale, purchase, transportation, possession, and export of different types of game during their closed season is prohibited. Animals of the same species as different types of game, born and raised in captivity, may be offered, sold, purchased, transported, possessed, and exported subject to a set of conditions approved by a decision of the Minister in charge of Forests (1). The trade in certain types of game, as well as offering them for consumption in restaurants and hotels, is also prohibited during their hunting season. These types are specified by a decision of the Minister in charge of Forests. **(Paragraphs 3 and 4 were added by Law No. 13 of 2005 dated January 26, 2005).**
