(Amended by Law No. 28 of 2001 dated March 19, 2001).- No industrial establishment using fire or storing flammable materials may be established within a forest area at a distance of less than 500 meters from it. Violations of this provision shall be punished as follows:

Crimes, as defined in this law, that are punishable by death or imprisonment exceeding five years, shall be classified as felonies.

Crimes punishable by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars, shall be classified as misdemeanors.

Crimes punishable by imprisonment not exceeding fifteen days or a fine not exceeding sixty dinars, shall be classified as offenses.

Violations of this provision shall be punished by a fine ranging from 100 to 600 dinars, with the demolition of the buildings at the expense of the offender within a period of three months from the date of the judgment.
