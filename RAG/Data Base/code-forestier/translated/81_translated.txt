Any person who wilfully or negligently damages or demolishes a passage (road, lane, or transportation route), technical installations, a warning sign, or any other essential equipment within the lands subject to the forest regime shall be punished in accordance with Article 73 of this Code. The same penalties mentioned above shall be imposed upon persons who wilfully or negligently damage within the lands subject to the forest regime, the installations established to resist erosion or preparatory afforestation works such as barriers, runways, planting pits, or who damage sand stabilization works.
