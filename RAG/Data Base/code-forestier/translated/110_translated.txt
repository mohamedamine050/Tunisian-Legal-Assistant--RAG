Every person engaging in the trade of forest products mentioned in the decision referred to in Article 105 of this Code, as well as manufacturers who process these products, which are transported under the conditions set out in Article 105 above, must, under their responsibility, demand from the seller the delivery of the transportation license or certificate of origin of the products stipulated in Article 106 of this Code. They must always carry the aforementioned documents proving the origin and type of products they trade or process and which are located in their warehouses or stores. They cannot prevent officers of the "Ministry responsible for Forests" (1) or other judicial police officers from inspecting their warehouses or stores.
