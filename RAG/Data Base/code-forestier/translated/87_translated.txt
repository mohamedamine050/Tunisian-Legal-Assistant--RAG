The extraction of virgin cork shall be subject to a fine of 3 dinars for each tree stripped of its bark. This fine may be increased to 10 dinars for each tree that has been wounded or from which a part has been cut. The removal of virgin cork lying on the ground shall be subject to a fine ranging from 5 to 25 dinars for each quintal. If the quantities removed are less than one quintal, the fine imposed may not be less than the minimum amount.
