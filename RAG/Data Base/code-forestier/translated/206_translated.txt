(Amended by Law No. 59 of 2009 dated July 20, 2009) - Tourist hunting may be practiced only upon payment of a fee to the State Treasury through a payment authorization, the amount of which is determined for each season by the annual decree concerning the regulation of the hunting season.  Tourist hunters must present a receipt for payment of the fee before being issued a temporary license for the importation and possession of hunting weapons upon any request by the security and customs authorities at the border.
