**(Amended by Law No. 13 of 2005, dated January 26, 2005)** - The conditions for engaging in hunting, the types of game, the dates for opening and closing the hunting season, and the protected areas where hunting is prohibited shall be determined by a decision of the Minister in charge of Forestry, after consulting the Advisory Committee for Hunting and Game Preservation. This decision shall be published at least fifteen days before the opening of the hunting season. Hunting, capturing, possessing, trading, exporting, and importing of animals not specified in this decision is prohibited, except with an exceptional permit from the Minister in charge of Forestry for scientific research, animal health, the creation of public zoos, wildlife breeding centers, in accordance with the provisions of Article 211 of this Code, or in the context of exchange with relevant institutions.
