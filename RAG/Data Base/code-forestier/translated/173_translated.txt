Companies, associations, and fishing groups may obtain a **judgment** from the court obliging perpetrators of misdemeanors to pay fines allocated to compensate for the damage they have suffered. Owners, associations, and fishing groups may appoint special hunting guards at their own expense. These guards must obtain prior approval from the **Ministry responsible for Forests** and must take a **legal oath** before the **District Judge** within whose jurisdiction they are located. These special guards, whose specific attributes are determined by a decision of the **Minister of Agriculture**, must carry an assignment card issued by the **Ministry responsible for Forests** at the request of their employers. They are qualified to draw up reports concerning violations they witness, and these reports are admissible in court unless evidence contradicts them.
