Forest engineers and technicians have the right to request the assistance of public authorities, directly or in writing, for the purpose of inspecting and following up on all violations stipulated in this Code. They may apprehend anyone found in the act of committing a misdemeanor and bring them before the Court or before one of the judicial police officers referred to in paragraphs 2, 3, 4, 5, and 6 of Article 10 of the Code of Criminal Procedure if the violation is punishable by imprisonment or a more severe penalty, or if the offender is unable to provide proof of their identity or a permanent place of residence.
