(Amended by Law No. 28 of 2001 dated March 19, 2001) - Administrative campaigns to hunt wild animals and animals harmful to agriculture may be carried out, at the request of the administrative authorities or farmers concerned, and after an assessment of the damage to crops by qualified forestry officers. The capture of olive and grain birds on state-owned forest land is subject to the provisions of a specification document approved by a decree of the Minister in charge of forestry.
