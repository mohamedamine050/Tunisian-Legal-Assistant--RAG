Owners of animals found illegally grazing during daylight hours in grazing lands shall be punished, as stipulated in Article 58 of this Code, with a fine of seven dinars for each camel or goat and four dinars for each animal of other types. In addition, the shepherd may be sentenced to imprisonment for a period ranging from two to fifteen days. In case of recidivism or if the violation is committed at night, the penalty of imprisonment shall be mandatory, with the possibility of impounding the animals.
