The manner in which the lands specified in the second and fifth paragraphs of the seventh paragraph of Chapter Four of this Code shall be subjected to the forestry regime, as well as the conditions for the management and protection of their affairs, shall be regulated by a decision of the Minister responsible for Forests (1).  Contracts for afforestation concluded pursuant to the second and fifth paragraphs of the seventh paragraph of Chapter Four above are exempt from registration fees and stamp duty.
