## English Translation:

Proceedings initiated at the request of the Ministry responsible for forests, as well as all decisions related to lawsuits stipulated in this Code, shall be registered as definitive and binding upon the defendant.

**Defendant:** A person against whom a judgment has been issued, a person who has been found liable for a crime and sentenced to a penalty or precautionary measure. 

**Judgment:** A written decision of the court resolving a legal issue or fact.

Upon the issuance of the judgment, the Ministry responsible for forests shall be exempt from the payment of fees, as well as from all forms of insurance and guarantees.
