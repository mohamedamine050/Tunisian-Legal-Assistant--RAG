The term "forest" means any plant aggregation, whether of natural or artificial origin, composed of one or more species of trees, shrubs, or forest plants, in a pure or mixed state. The term "forest-prone land" means any land suitable for the creation of forests due to environmental and economic factors. The term "halophyte vegetation cover" means all lands primarily covered with halophyte plants. The term "pasture lands" means all lands not under cultivation and covered with natural or introduced herbaceous or fibrous plants suitable for serving as "food" for animals. The term "wild animal" means all species of non-domesticated vertebrate or invertebrate animals. The term "wild plant" means all species of natural plants growing in any environment.
