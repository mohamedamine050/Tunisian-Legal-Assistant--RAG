(Amended by Article 1 of Law No. 28 of 2001 dated March 19, 2001).- Tourist fishermen may only engage in fishing in Tunisia through a Tunisian travel agency or a local hotel establishment, subject to the provisions of a set of conditions approved by a decision of the Minister in charge of Forests. The agency and establishments mentioned above are responsible under the current fishing legislation, and are obligated to ensure the respect of said legislation by their foreign clients.
