**(Amended by Law No. 28 of 2001 dated March 19, 2001).** - A committee shall be formed by the Minister in charge of Forestry(1)  to:

**- Advise on the following:**
    -  Regulatory texts concerning hunting and fisheries conservation.
    - Improvement of hunting and fisheries conservation.
    - Coordination between stakeholders in hunting and fisheries conservation. 
    
**-  Composed of:** 
   - Individuals with relevant experience from various parts of the institution, assembled through a formal process. 

**(1) The exact translation of "الوزير المكلف بالغابات" may vary depending on the specific legal context.  It could be "Minister of Forests," "Minister in Charge of Forestry," or similar.**
