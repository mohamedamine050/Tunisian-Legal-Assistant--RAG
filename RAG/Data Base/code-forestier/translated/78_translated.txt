**English Translation:**

Any person who has taken possession of state-owned forest land in violation of the provisions of Articles 75 to 77 of this Code, or who has erected a building, shelter or fence thereon, regardless of its type, even if temporarily, without prior authorization from the "Ministry in charge of Forests"(1), shall be punished with a fine ranging from 20 dinars to 100 dinars, and with the demolition of the building, shelter or fence at their own expense within a period of 30 days from the date of the judgment. Upon recidivism, the maximum fine shall always be imposed, as well as imprisonment from 15 to 30 days or either penalty alone.
