**As amended by Law No. 13 of 2005 dated January 26, 2005** - Fees derived from temporary residence permits and leases, as determined by the current legislation governing agricultural leases, shall be paid into the state treasury. Temporary residences declared to be in the public interest are exempt from the payment of these fees. Temporary residences declared to be in the public interest shall be determined by a joint decision of the Ministers responsible for Forestry and Finance.  Development associations in the agricultural and fisheries sector are also exempt from paying these fees when undertaking projects and activities stipulated in Article 75 of this Code.
