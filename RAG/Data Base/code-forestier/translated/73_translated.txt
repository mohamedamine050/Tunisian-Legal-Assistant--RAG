Any person who breaks, damages, defaces, alters, or removes a boundary marker, trench, guidance sign, wall, or any other form of fence designed to delimit forests, national parks, nature reserves, or pastures subject to the forestry regime, shall be punished with a fine ranging from twenty (20) dinars to one hundred (100) dinars and may also be sentenced to imprisonment for a period ranging from twenty (20) days to three (3) months, in addition to the fines, which shall not be less than the necessary expenses incurred to restore the aforementioned elements to their original condition.
