(Amended by Law No. 13 of 2005 dated January 26, 2005). - The conditions for practicing fishing, the types of fish caught, the dates for opening and closing the fishing season, and the reserves in which fishing is prohibited are regulated by a decision of the Minister in charge of Forests, after consulting the Advisory Committee on Fishing and the Conservation of Fish. This decision is published at least fifteen days before the opening of the fishing season. The hunting, capture, possession, trading, export, and import of animals not specified in this decision is prohibited, except by an exceptional license from the Minister in charge of Forests for the benefit of scientific research, animal health, the establishment of public zoos, and wild animal breeding centers in accordance with the provisions of Article 211 of this Code, or within the framework of exchanges with relevant institutions.
