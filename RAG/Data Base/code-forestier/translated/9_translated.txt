The qualified personnel affiliated with the General Directorate of Forestry shall wear an official uniform.  This uniform, being of a governmental nature, shall be governed by established procedures.  The number, composition, characteristics, and accessories of the uniform shall be determined by a decision of the Minister in charge of Forestry, upon the proposal of the Director General of Forestry, with the State bearing the expenses of this uniform. Qualified personnel shall always wear their official uniform and the distinguishing marks of their rank while performing their duties.  Exemption from wearing the uniform may be granted by a special authorization from the Minister in charge of Forestry.  They may be permitted to carry weapons, the type, features, and manner of handling of which shall be determined by a joint decision of the Minister in charge of Forestry and the Minister of Interior.
