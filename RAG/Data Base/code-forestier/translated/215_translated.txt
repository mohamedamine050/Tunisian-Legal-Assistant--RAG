The right to hunt wild game in state forests and lands subject to the forestry regime may be exercised pursuant to:

- A **contract**
A **contract** is a legally binding agreement that obligates the contracting parties and defines their obligations and rights with respect to the specific transaction.
- **Reforestation** or
- **Works** for the stabilization of sand dunes, provided this is done either under a personal license or by bidding, in accordance with the procedures and conditions set forth in a decision by the Minister of Agriculture.
