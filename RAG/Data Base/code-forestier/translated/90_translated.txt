(Amended by Law No. 28 of 2001 dated March 19, 2001). - No herd may be introduced into grazing lands subject to the forestry regime, as stipulated in Article 58 of this Code, unless it is guarded by a shepherd who is at least 16 years old, failing which the owner of the herd shall be punished with a fine ranging from 20 to 100 dinars.
