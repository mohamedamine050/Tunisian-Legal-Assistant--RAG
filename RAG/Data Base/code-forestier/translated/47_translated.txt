Every offense described as a felony, within the meaning of this law, is a crime that warrants punishment by death or imprisonment exceeding five years.

Crimes described as misdemeanors are those that warrant punishment by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars.

Crimes described as infractions are those that warrant punishment not exceeding fifteen days imprisonment or a fine of sixty dinars. [122 Penal Procedure Code]

The provisions of Articles 45 and 46 of this Code result in the suspension of the extraction site and the seizure of the equipment used in the work of the site by the Ministry responsible for forests. Every violator shall be punished by a fine of one to one hundred dinars and imprisonment for a period ranging from six days to three months, with confiscation of the seized tools and equipment, regardless of the fines.
