Forest products belonging to the State forest domain, irrespective of their type, may not be sold except through public auction, notice of which shall be published in newspapers fifteen days prior to its occurrence.  The relevant advertisements shall be posted at the "Ministry in charge of Forests" (1), at the regional center, and at the delegation where the products are located. However, for justified reasons or when it is established that a public auction is impossible, the products may be sold by private treaty. The terms of sale by private treaty and the minimum powers of the authorities authorized to sell by private treaty shall be determined by an order proposed by the Minister in charge of Agriculture. (The second and third paragraphs were amended by Law No. 28 of 2001 dated March 19, 2001).
