(Amended by Law No. 13 of 2005 dated January 26, 2005). - The offenses stipulated in this Code shall be investigated and examined by the judicial police officers referred to in articles 1, 2, 3, 4 and 7 of Article 10 of the Code of Criminal Procedure, as well as forest engineers and technicians referred to in Article 7 of this Code. With regard to hunting offenses, in addition to the officers mentioned above, the investigation shall be conducted by officers of economic control and by qualified officers of the Ministry responsible for forests, appointed for this purpose by a decision of the Minister responsible for forests.
