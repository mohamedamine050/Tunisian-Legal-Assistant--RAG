Except as provided for in Articles 36 and 39 of this Code, the extraction, uprooting, or removal of any forest products, except green firewood, pumice stone, and tanning bark, in violation of the provisions of Article 24 of this Code, shall be punished with a fine of 50 dinars for each vehicle load, 7 dinars for each draft animal, 5 dinars for each pack animal, and 2 dinars for each person's load. (The first paragraph was amended by Law No. 28 of 2001 dated March 19, 2001). These provisions shall apply to the removal of dry wood, except for wood removed under the exercise of the right of usufruct.
