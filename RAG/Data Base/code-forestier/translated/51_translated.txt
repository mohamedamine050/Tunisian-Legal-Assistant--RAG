(Amended by Law No. 28 of 2001 dated 19 March 2001) - The production of charcoal on state-owned forestland and land subject to forestry regulations shall be subject to conditions determined by ministerial decree of the Minister of Agriculture, regardless of the type of wood intended for burning. The production of charcoal outside these areas shall be carried out in accordance with specifications approved by ministerial decree of the Minister of Agriculture, except for charcoal intended for personal consumption. Charcoal produced in violation of the foregoing provisions shall be confiscated, in addition to the imposition of a fine on the offender ranging from 10 to 100 dinars.
