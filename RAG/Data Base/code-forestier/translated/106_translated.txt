The transportation permit referred to in the previous section shall be delivered by the local forestry officer and shall be valid for one shipment within a specific time frame on a designated day. In the event of force majeure events that hinder the transportation within the specified timeframe, the forestry officer, national guard, or police in the vicinity shall endorse the transportation permit with the necessary extension period, specifying the reasons for the extension and signing and stamping it with the seal of the authority granting the extension. The transported products shall not be offered for sale unless they are accompanied by a valid transportation permit.  Exploiters and traders of forestry products are required to present a certificate, sealed by the forestry officer responsible for the areas from which the products originate, to their buyers.
