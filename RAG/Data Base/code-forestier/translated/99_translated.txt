Notwithstanding the penalties provided for in Article 78 of this Code, all premises erected in violation of the law during the period between May 1st and October 31st shall be transferred or demolished within a period of five days, pursuant to authorization from the local administrative authority and upon a reasoned request from the Ministry responsible for Forests (1). In this case, the provisions of the first paragraph of Article 315 of the Penal Code may be applied.
