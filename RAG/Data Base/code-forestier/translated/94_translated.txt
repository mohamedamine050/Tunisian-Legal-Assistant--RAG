The setting of fire, burning of brushwood, forest herbaceous plants, and other plants, regardless of their type, is prohibited from the first of May to October 31st, within a distance of 500 meters from all forests or lands covered with forest herbaceous plants. However, during the same period, the conditions for the use of fire in exploitation buildings, shelters, work barns, factories, metal furnaces, and sealed transportable charcoal-making machines located in forests or within the 200-meter zone specified in Article 93 mentioned above, shall be determined by a decision of the Minister in charge of Forests.
