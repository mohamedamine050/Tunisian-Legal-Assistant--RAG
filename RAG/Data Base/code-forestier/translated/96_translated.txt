Anyone who violates the provisions of Articles 93 to 95 or the decision taken pursuant to Article 94 shall be punished with a fine ranging from 50 dinars to 150 dinars and imprisonment from 16 days to 3 months, or with one of these punishments only. In case of recidivism, imprisonment shall be mandatory. If the fire spreads to the forests as a result of the violation, the perpetrator shall be punished with imprisonment for a term ranging from three months to two years, regardless of the fines.
