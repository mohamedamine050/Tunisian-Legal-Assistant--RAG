Upon the expiry of this period, which commences from the date of the suspension, the Governor shall submit to the Minister of Agriculture a certificate attesting to the deposit and suspension referred to in Article 147, accompanied by the statements contained therein pursuant to Article 148 of this Code.

The boundaries of the sand dune stabilization zones established by order shall be determined by specifying, in an approximate manner, the area of the properties included in those zones, as well as the names of the apparent owners or those presumed to be the owners, and the legal consequences of the establishment of these zones. The boundaries of the sand dune stabilization zones may be modified by order, taking into account the specific nature of the land covered by sand dunes.
