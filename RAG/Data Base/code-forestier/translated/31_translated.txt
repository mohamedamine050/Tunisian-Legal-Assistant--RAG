An inspection of every sale shall be conducted within three months following the expiration of the deadlines set for the completion of the eviction and cleaning works. However, if the works are completed before the expiration of the set deadlines, the "Ministry in charge of Forests" (1) shall be notified in writing by registered mail with acknowledgement of receipt, informing it of the need to conduct the aforementioned inspection. The seller or buyers shall be relieved of any liability if the inspection is not conducted within three months from the date of expiry of the deadlines or within six months from the date of receipt of the registered letter.
