The practice of grazing on pasture lands subject to the forestry system is regulated by the pasture development programs stipulated in Chapter 16 of this Law, with respect to lands classified as first category as mentioned in Chapter 58 above. As for the practice of grazing on lands classified as second and third categories as stipulated in Chapter 58 above, it is regulated pursuant to the provisions of Chapter 60 above. The practice of grazing shall be subject to the provisions stipulated in Chapters 62 to 65 of this Law.
