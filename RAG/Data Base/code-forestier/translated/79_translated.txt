The deposition or disposal of any type of material within forests or within lands subject to the forestry regime is prohibited. Any violator shall be punished with a fine ranging from 20 dinars to 100 dinars, regardless of other fines. Additionally, the violator shall be required to remove the deposited material within a period of 7 days from the date of a written notice sent by registered mail with proof of delivery. In the event that the deposited material is not removed, or in the case of repeat offense, the violator shall be sentenced to the maximum fine and may also be subject to imprisonment for a period ranging from 5 days to 15 days. The removal of deposited or illegally placed materials shall be at the violator's expense.
