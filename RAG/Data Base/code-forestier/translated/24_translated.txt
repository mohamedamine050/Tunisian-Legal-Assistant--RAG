**(Amended by Law No. 28 of 2001, dated March 19, 2001).** - Neither the seller nor the buyer in a market may commence the exploitation or removal of the products sold prior to the execution of the contract of sale and the full payment of the purchase price.

**Contract**

A contract is a legally binding agreement that obligates the contracting parties and defines their obligations and rights with respect to the specific transaction.

**Purchase Price**

The purchase price is the amount agreed upon by the contracting parties, whether it exceeds or falls short of the value of the product sold. Failure to adhere to this requirement shall subject them to prosecution as violators.
