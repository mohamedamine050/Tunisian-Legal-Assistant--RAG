The auction sale shall take place at the nearest market to the location, conducted by the authorized collector, who shall publicize the sale announcements twenty-four hours prior to the sale. The net proceeds resulting from the sale of animals or objects seized in the international forests, excluding those seized in private lands subject to the forestry regime, shall be recorded in the accounts under the heading "Forest Products" after deducting the deposit and sale expenses. If the proceeds from the sale exceed the fine and expenses, the refund, if authorized, shall be made through a payment authorization from the budget according to the procedures specified in the regulations in force. The same rules shall apply to objects and animals seized in lands subject to the forestry regime that are owned by individuals, except that the proceeds from the sale shall be transferred to the Tunisian Deposit and Consignment Fund after deducting the administrative disposal expenses.
