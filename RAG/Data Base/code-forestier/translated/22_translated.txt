Any collusion or engagement in suspicious, clandestine activities between exploiters and traders of forest products, with the intent to cause harm through price manipulation or unfair pricing – where the agreed price exceeds or falls short of the actual value of the goods – shall be subject to the penalties stipulated in Article 303 of the Penal Code, in addition to all applicable fines.  

Furthermore, any agreement reached in favor of the colluding parties or perpetrators of such suspicious activities shall be null and void.  The nullity declared by this article, as well as by Articles 20 and 21 of this Code, pertains to public order and is not subject to any form of appeal.
