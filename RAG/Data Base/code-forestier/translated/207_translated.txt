The prosecution of violators of the provisions of this chapter and its implementing texts shall be undertaken partially. Animals and plants held unlawfully may be seized in the event of a violation.

Crimes punishable by death or imprisonment exceeding five years shall be considered felonies within the meaning of this law. Crimes punishable by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars, shall be considered misdemeanors. Crimes punishable by imprisonment not exceeding fifteen days or by a fine not exceeding sixty dinars shall be considered offenses. 122 Code of Criminal Procedure

In addition to the prosecutions mentioned in the first paragraph of this chapter, a judgment may be issued ordering the closure of any institution referred to in Articles 211 and 212 of this Code, if the prosecution is conducted by one of those institutions.
