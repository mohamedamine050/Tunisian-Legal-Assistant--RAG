(Amended by Law No. 13 of 2005 dated January 26, 2005). - Forest engineers and technicians are entrusted with the implementation of the provisions of this Code and its implementing regulations and they carry out their duties throughout the national territory. To this end, they are entrusted with monitoring the works carried out under their responsibility and with conducting periodic inspections by day and by night in the areas under their supervision, and with drawing up dated and signed reports on all crimes committed in those areas. These engineers and technicians are qualified by a decision of the Minister responsible for Forestry.
