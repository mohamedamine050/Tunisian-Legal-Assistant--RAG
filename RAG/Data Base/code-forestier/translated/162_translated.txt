Every offense described as a felony in the meaning of this law shall be considered a crime that warrants punishment by death or imprisonment for a period exceeding five years.

Offenses that warrant punishment by imprisonment for a period exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars, shall be considered misdemeanors.

Offenses that warrant punishment not exceeding fifteen days of imprisonment or sixty dinars in fines shall be considered violations. 

As per Articles 160 and 161 of this Code of Criminal Procedure, offenses subject to the provisions of Articles 160 and 161 of this Code shall be punished by a fine of between 15 and 50 dinars, and in case of recidivism, a heavier fine shall always be imposed.
