## English Translation:

**Every offense described as a felony within the meaning of this law shall be punishable by death or imprisonment for more than five years.**

**Offenses punishable by imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or by a fine exceeding sixty dinars, shall be described as misdemeanors.**

**Offenses punishable by imprisonment not exceeding fifteen days or by a fine not exceeding sixty dinars shall be described as violations.**

**The provisions of this section are punishable by a fine ranging from 3 dinars to 250 dinars. Imprisonment for a period ranging from six days to three months may also be imposed.**

**In case of recidivism, the fine shall be imposed at its maximum amount, along with the confiscation of the instruments used in the offense. Imprisonment shall be mandatory.**

**122 Code of Criminal Procedure**
