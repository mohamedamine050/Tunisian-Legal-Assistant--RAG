If the concessionaires or purchasers in the concessions fail to fulfill the obligations imposed upon them, as per the terms and deadlines stipulated in the tender documents, in terms of the required work or the provision of the firewood they are obliged to provide, or if they neglect to do so, such obligations shall be fulfilled by agency and at their expense, through the efforts of the "Ministry of Forests" (1) and with authorization from the "Minister of Forests" (1) who shall prepare a list of expenses incurred and render it enforceable against the concessionaires for the settlement of the aforementioned matters.
