**(Amended by Law No. 28 of 2001, dated March 19, 2001)** - The offering for sale, sale, purchase, transport, possession, and export of various types of game is prohibited during the closed season for each species.  Animals of the same species as the various game animals, born and raised in captivity, may be offered for sale, sold, purchased, transported, possessed, and exported in accordance with a set of specifications approved by a decree issued by the Minister in charge of Forests (1). The trade in certain species of game and their offering for consumption in restaurants and hotels is also prohibited throughout their hunting season.  These species are determined by a decree issued by the Minister in charge of Forests.  **(Paragraphs three and four were added by Law No. 13 of 2005, dated January 26, 2005).**
