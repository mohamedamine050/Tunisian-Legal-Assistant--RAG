Whoever unlawfully possesses land subject to the forest regime shall be held criminally and civilly liable for all offenses arising from this possession. 

However, if the individuals engaged in felling trees, clearing forest areas, breaking ground, or extracting materials from the land refuse to cease their activities after being warned by the representative of the "Ministry in charge of Forests" (1) or other public officials authorized to intervene, a report shall be drawn up concerning their refusal, and they shall be punished by imprisonment for a term ranging from five to fifteen days.
