A sentence of imprisonment from 5 days to 15 days may be imposed for violations stipulated in Articles 82 and 83 of this Code, and from 16 days to two months for violations stipulated in Articles 84 to 86 above. In case of recidivism, the offenses stipulated in Articles 82 to 87 shall always be assessed at their maximum penalty, and imprisonment shall be imposed, as indicated in the previous paragraph and in Article 47 of this Code.
