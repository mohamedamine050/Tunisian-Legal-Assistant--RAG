The conditions imposed on the bidders or purchasers in the auction, regulating the manner of felling trees, exploitation, clearing the site, cleaning, setting up work shelters, housing, warehouses and kilns, and generally all conditions governing services in the woodcutting areas, are to be recorded in the auction minutes and in the general and specific condition books of the sale by auction. Any person who violates the aforementioned conditions shall be punished with a fine ranging from fifty dinars to five hundred dinars, in addition to fines that cannot be less than the amount of the pure fine. The administration may take any precautionary measures deemed necessary in relation to the products of the trees before felling or placed in the forest after felling, in order to ensure payment of the fine and penalties.
