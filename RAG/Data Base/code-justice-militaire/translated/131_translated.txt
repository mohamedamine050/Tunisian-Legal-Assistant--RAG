Any person licensed to establish a political party, institution, or association, and the individuals responsible for its management, shall be punished by imprisonment for a period of six months to two years if they accept military personnel as members. The license of the party, institution, or association shall be permanently revoked.

An association is an agreement between two or more individuals working permanently towards achieving objectives, excluding profit-making. Associations accepting military personnel shall be closed, and their offices and meeting venues shall be shut down.
