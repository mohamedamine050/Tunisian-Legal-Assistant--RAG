(Amended by Section 8 of Law No. 23 of 1989 dated February 27, 1989).- Any military personnel who intentionally attempts to commit any of the crimes mentioned in the preceding article during wartime or in the presence of rebels shall be punished by life imprisonment. If the crime is committed in circumstances other than these two, the perpetrator shall be sentenced to ten years imprisonment. In addition, the perpetrator shall be subject to dismissal from service if he is an officer, and mitigating circumstances may be considered.
