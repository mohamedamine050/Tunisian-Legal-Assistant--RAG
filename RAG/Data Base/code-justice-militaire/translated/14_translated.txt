(Amended by the first section of Decree No. 69 of 2011 dated 29 July 2011). -  The following are appointed to the **Permanent Military Primary Court**: a **Procurator General**, a **First Assistant Procurator**, **Assistant Procurators**, a **Chief Investigating Judge**, and **Investigating Judges**. Individual judges and presiding judges are also appointed (*). The following are appointed to the **Military Court of Appeal**: a **Procurator General**, a **First Assistant Procurator**, **Assistant Procurators**, and **Counselors**.
