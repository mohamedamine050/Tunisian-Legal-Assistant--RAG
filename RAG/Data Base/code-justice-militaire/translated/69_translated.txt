(Amended by Article 8 of Law No. 23 of 1989, dated February 27, 1989). - Any military personnel who commits the crime of desertion to the enemy shall be punished by death.  If the desertion occurs in the presence of the enemy, the punishment shall be fifteen years of imprisonment.  If the deserter is an officer, the punishment shall be twenty years of imprisonment, and in all cases, the deserter shall also be subject to dismissal from service.
