Any military personnel who intentionally, by any means whatsoever, burns, demolishes, or damages buildings, structures, warehouses, watercourses, railway lines, electrical and telephone lines and centers, aviation centers, ships, boats, vessels, or any other immovable property belonging to the army and used in national defense shall be punished by death with military stripping. In addition to this, the officer shall be sentenced to dismissal if mitigating circumstances are present and he is not sentenced to death.
