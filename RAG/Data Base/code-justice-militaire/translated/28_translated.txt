(Amended by Section 1 of Decree No. 69 of 2011 dated July 29, 2011).- The permanent Chamber of Indictment within the Court of Appeal to which the seat of the **Court of First Instance** is attached shall hear appeals against decisions of the **military investigating judge**, pursuant to the same procedures and deadlines as laid out in the Code of Criminal Procedure. However, one of its counselors shall be replaced by a military judge, of the judicial rank required to hold such positions within the judicial system, appointed by order.
