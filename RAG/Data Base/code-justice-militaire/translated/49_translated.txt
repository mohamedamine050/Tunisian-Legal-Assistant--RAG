## English Translation:

1. In the event of war or a state of emergency, it is permissible to suspend the execution of any **judgment** issued by military courts, by an order issued by the Council of Ministers, for reasons related to the interest of national defense. 

2. The suspension of execution may include the suspension of **secondary penalties**, in whole or in part, and in such case, this shall be expressly mentioned in the order suspending execution.

3. In the event of a declaration of general mobilization, the execution of judgments issued for crimes committed in order to evade military service shall be **automatically suspended**. In the case of a declaration of partial mobilization, the execution shall be suspended for individuals summoned to bear arms.
