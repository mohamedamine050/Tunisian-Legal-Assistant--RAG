Any military personnel who insults a superior officer in rank, whether verbally, in writing, through gestures, or by threats, during service or in connection with service, shall be punished by imprisonment for a term of three months to two years. If the offender is an officer, he shall be punished by imprisonment for a term of six months to three years, and by dismissal from service, or by one of these punishments. If the insult is not committed during service or in connection with service, the punishment shall be imprisonment for a term of two months to one year. If the offender is an officer, this punishment shall be doubled.
