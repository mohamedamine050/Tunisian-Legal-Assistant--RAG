**(Amended by Article 8 of Law No. 23 of 1989 dated February 27, 1989).**- Any watchman or guard who abandons his post before completing the assigned task shall be punished by imprisonment for a term of six months to two years. The punishment shall be imprisonment for ten years if the watchman or guard is in the face of rebels. He shall be punished by death if he is in the face of the enemy. The perpetrator shall be punished by imprisonment for a term of two to five years if the crime is committed in a territory under a state of war or a region where a state of siege has been declared, but not in the face of the enemy or rebels.
