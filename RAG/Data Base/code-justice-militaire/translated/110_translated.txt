Any military personnel who sleeps while performing guard duty or patrol shall be punished by imprisonment for a term of six months to one year. If the offense occurs in the presence of the enemy or rebels, the punishment shall be imprisonment for a term of two to five years. If the offense occurs in a territory under a state of war or in an area declared under a state of siege, but not in the presence of the enemy or rebels, the punishment shall be imprisonment for a term of one to three years.
