**(Amended by Article 8 of Law No. 23 of 1989 dated February 27, 1989).**- Whoever, for a criminal purpose, whether by himself or through another, damages the means of defense, military materials, weapons, ammunition, supplies, equipment, clothing, or any movable property belonging to the army or used in national defense, shall be punished by imprisonment for a term of ten years.

The punishment shall be **imprisonment for life** if the aforementioned damage occurs during wartime or against rebels. In addition, the perpetrator shall be sentenced to **dismissal** if he is an officer and extenuating circumstances are granted.
