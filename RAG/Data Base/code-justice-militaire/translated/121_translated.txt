**Shall be considered a spy and punished by death with military stripping:**

**a)** Any military personnel who enters a war zone, or a military center, or a military institution, or a military dining hall, or a camp, or a military facility, to obtain documents or information that would benefit the enemy, or that they believe would benefit the enemy.

**b)** Any military personnel who provides the enemy with documents or information that could harm military operations or endanger the safety of military sites, centers, and institutions, or that they believe could do so.

**c)** Any military personnel who intentionally conceals, either personally or through others, spies or enemies.
