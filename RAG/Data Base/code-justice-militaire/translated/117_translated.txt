Any Tunisian military personnel, or those serving in the Tunisian army, who take up arms against Tunisia shall be punished by death and stripped of their military rank. 

Any prisoner who is captured a second time after breaking an oath and taking up arms shall be punished by death.

Any Tunisian military personnel, or those serving in the Tunisian army, who fall into the hands of the enemy and are released from captivity only after pledging to the enemy that they will not take up arms against them again, shall be punished by imprisonment for a term of three to five years. 

If the perpetrator is an officer, they shall additionally be subject to dismissal from service.
