**Added by Article 2 of Law No. 47 of 2009 dated July 8, 2009.** - A military member may be granted, upon request, unpaid leave for a period not exceeding three months per year. This leave shall not be considered as part of actual service for purposes of promotion, advancement, participation in competitions, and retirement. The conditions and procedures for granting unpaid leave shall be regulated by the special statute governing military personnel.
