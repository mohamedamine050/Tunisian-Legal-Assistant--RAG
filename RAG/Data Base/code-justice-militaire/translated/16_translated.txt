(As amended by Article 1 of Decree No. 69 of 2011 dated July 29, 2011). - The functions of the military judicial police are exercised under the authority of:

1. The Public Prosecutor at the Military Court of Appeal and his assistants, and the Public Prosecutor at the permanent Military Court of First Instance and his assistants, 
2. Investigating Judges, as defined in the Code of Criminal Procedure, 
3. Military Justice Officers, Military Police Officers and their NCOs,
4. Commanders of Brigades, Bases, Battalions, Units, Directors of Administrations, and Military Garrison Chiefs, each within their respective area of competence regarding their subordinates and military crimes committed within their jurisdiction.
