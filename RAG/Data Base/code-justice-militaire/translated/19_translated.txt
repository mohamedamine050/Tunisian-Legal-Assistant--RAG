Retirement is the final status of an officer or non-commissioned officer upon their return to civilian life. They are granted a retirement pension based on seniority or a proportional retirement pension. Military personnel retired pursuant to the law enjoy a retirement pension according to the conditions stipulated in Law No. 18 of 1959, dated February 5, 1959, concerning the regulation of retirement pensions. Officers and non-commissioned officers retired pursuant to a law enacted to reduce personnel are entitled to a retirement pension according to the conditions established by that law.
