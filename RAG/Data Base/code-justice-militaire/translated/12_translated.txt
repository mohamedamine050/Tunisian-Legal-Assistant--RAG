**(Amended by the first chapter of Decree No. 69 of 2011 dated July 29, 2011).** - The military courts of first instance and the military Court of Appeal shall be composed of the same composition as that adopted in peacetime, provided that the President of the Court or the Chamber shall be replaced by a military judge of the rank of Colonel at least when considering criminal cases at first instance and appeals in criminal and misdemeanor cases, and by a military judge of the rank of Lieutenant Colonel at least when considering misdemeanors at first instance.
