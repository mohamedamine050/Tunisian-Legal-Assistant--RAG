## English Translation:

Exemption refers to the status of a contracted military personnel whose call for direct service has become impossible and who is not entitled to a seniority pension or a proportional pension. Exemption may be decided:

1. **Due to physical incapacitation resulting from service:** In this case, the State Secretary for National Defense decides the exemption upon the recommendation of a 
[**Formal Method of Gathering Experts from Different Parts of the Organization**]
Exemption Committee. The military personnel exempted for physical incapacitation resulting from service is entitled to a monthly salary for each year of active service.

2. **Due to disciplinary reasons:** For the same reasons as those applicable to regular military personnel.
