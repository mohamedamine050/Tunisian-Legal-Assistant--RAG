The period of limitation for the penalty, which is a punishment and remedy imposed in the name of society on an individual criminally responsible for a crime committed based on a judicial judgment, or the lapse of the right to prosecution due to the passage of time, shall not be calculated except from the date the fugitive reaches the legal age specified for his rank under the military regulations. The penalty, a punishment and remedy imposed in the name of society on an individual criminally responsible for a crime committed based on a judicial judgment, shall not lapse, nor shall the public right in the first three cases referred to in Article 71 be subject to any form of appeal.
