(Amended by Article 8 of Law No. 23 of 1989 dated February 27, 1989).-  Any military personnel who steals, sells, mortgages, embezzles, or fraudulently exchanges equipment, devices, clothing, weapons, ammunition, animals, or any item belonging to the Tunisian Army or an allied army shall be punished with imprisonment for a term of one to three years.  The perpetrator shall be punished with imprisonment for a term of six years if they were entrusted with those items, and shall be required to compensate for the value of any items that cannot be returned.  In addition, if the perpetrator is an officer, they shall be sentenced to dismissal from service, and mitigating circumstances shall be considered.
