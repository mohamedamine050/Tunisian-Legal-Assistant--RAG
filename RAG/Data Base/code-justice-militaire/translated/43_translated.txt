The salary of officers and non-commissioned officers working after the statutory period of military service shall be fixed by order.  Military personnel who are recalled to active duty shall receive the active duty salary determined for military personnel of the same rank and grade they held at the time of their separation from service.  The daily salary payable to non-commissioned officers working during the statutory period and the daily salary payable to sergeants and soldiers shall be fixed by order.  The system of grants paid to military personnel shall also be fixed by order.  The material benefits that may be granted to military personnel assigned to certain job plans shall be fixed by order. (Added as paragraph five by Article 2 of Law No. 47 of 2009 dated July 8, 2009).
