**(Amended by the first section of Decree No. 69 of 2011, dated July 29, 2011)** - Military courts shall consider military cases as follows:
1. Permanent Military Courts of First Instance in Tunis, Sfax, and Kairouan. When necessary, they may hold sessions in any other location.
2. A Court of Appeal, with its headquarters in Tunis.
3. Military Chambers of Indictment.
4. A Military Chamber of Cassation.

Furthermore, in times of war, or whenever the interests of internal or external national security so require, other permanent or temporary military courts may be established by decree, based on the proposal of the Minister of National Defence, which shall specify their jurisdiction.
