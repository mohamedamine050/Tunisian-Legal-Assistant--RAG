Loss of rank is a subsidiary penalty for certain crimes specifically defined by law. While it has the effects of dismissal, it does not deprive the individual of the right to a retirement pension or compensation for past services. 

A sentence of loss of rank may be imposed on any military officer for the following offenses:

* **Felonies:** (Article 193) - Forgery of Administrative Documents; (Article 264) - Simple Theft; (Article 283) - Forgery of Signature or Endorsement; (Article 291) - Fraud; (Article 297) - Treason, as defined in the Tunisian Penal Code.
