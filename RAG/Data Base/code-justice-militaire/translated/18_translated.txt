Disciplinary dismissal shall be decided upon for one of the following reasons, after taking into account the opinion of the disciplinary committee, a formally constituted body of experts from different parts of the Ministry of National Defense whose composition and operating procedures are determined by instructions from the Secretary of State for National Defense:  

* Persistent unsatisfactory conduct;
* A serious error in the performance of duty or a breach of discipline; or
* A conduct detrimental to honor.
