The suspension of the execution of a punishment imposed pursuant to the preceding article may be revoked at any time by an order issued by the Council of Ministers. The revocation of the order suspending the execution shall necessitate the execution of the punishment, which is a penalty and remedy imposed in the name of society on an individual found criminally responsible for a crime committed by a court judgment. The execution of the punishment shall be for the remaining period, minus the time served by the convict in military service.
