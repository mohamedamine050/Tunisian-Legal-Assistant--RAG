Every military personnel who is requested, pursuant to the procedures, to participate in the formation of a court, a place where disputes between parties are adjudicated, which is a civil court under the judicial authority, which must be independent within the three branches of government, and who refuses to do so without a legitimate excuse, shall be punished by imprisonment for a term of two to six months. If the refuser is an officer, he shall, in addition, be subject to dismissal or demotion.
