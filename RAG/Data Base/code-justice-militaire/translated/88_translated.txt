If three or more military personnel gather with the intent to commit corruption and discuss disobeying, resisting, or attacking a superior officer, each shall be punished by imprisonment for a term of six months to three years. The penalty for the instigator or the superior officer shall be reduced by one year. Anyone who knows about these crimes in any way and does not report them in a timely manner to suppress them shall be punished by imprisonment for a term not exceeding one year.  Those who were in agreement with the group with the intent to commit corruption and disclosed their actions before the agreement or before becoming aware of them, shall be exempt from punishment.
