## English Translation:

**Sentences 1-2:** 

Penal sanctions of deprivation of liberty, as pronounced by military courts, shall be enforced in military prisons or civilian prisons, as appropriate. 

**Sentences 3-7:**

The material aspects of civilization, such as infrastructure, communication, and entertainment, are countered by the intellectual, spiritual, and moral aspects of civilization. When the convicted person, a defendant against whom a judgment has been issued determining his responsibility for a crime and imposing a penalty or a precautionary measure, is stripped of his military status or is a civilian, the penalty, a punishment and remedy imposed in the name of society on an individual criminally responsible for a crime committed based on a judicial decision, shall be enforced in civilian prisons.
