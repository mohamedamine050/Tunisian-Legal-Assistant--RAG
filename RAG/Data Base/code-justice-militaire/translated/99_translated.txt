(Amended by Article 8 of Law No. 23 of 1989 dated February 27, 1989). - Any person, military or civilian, who intentionally robs a wounded, sick, or deceased military personnel in a military operations area shall be punished by imprisonment for a term of six years. The punishment shall be death if the assault involves serious violence resulting in the deterioration of the health of the wounded or sick military personnel to achieve the robbery.
