Any military personnel who violates general orders and instructions given to a specific unit or to military personnel in general, or instructions entrusted to him personally to execute, or rebels against orders given to another military personnel, shall be punished with imprisonment for a period of two months to two years.

The punishment is increased to five years of imprisonment if the crime is committed in the presence of rebels, or inside a fortress, arsenal, or ammunition depot, or in a territory in a state of war, or in an area where a state of siege has been declared.
