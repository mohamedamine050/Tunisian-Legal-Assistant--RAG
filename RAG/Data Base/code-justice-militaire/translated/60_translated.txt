1. The term "guard" as used in this law refers to an armed military personnel assigned to a specific location to maintain security and order, or to conduct surveillance according to specific instructions in times of peace and war. 

2. The term "patrol" refers to a group of armed military personnel under the command of an officer for the previously mentioned purposes in times of peace and war.

3. The term "constable" refers to a mobile armed military personnel performing duties within a specific area for the previously mentioned purposes in times of peace and war.
