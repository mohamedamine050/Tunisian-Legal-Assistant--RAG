(Amended by Article 8 of Law No. 23 of 1989 dated February 27, 1989). - Military personnel who, acting as a gang, commit robbery or damage to food, goods, and objects, whether by using weapons, by using apparent force, by breaking down doors and barriers, or by using violence against persons, shall be punished with life imprisonment. They shall be punished with imprisonment for a term of ten years in other cases and, in addition, shall be dismissed from service if they are officers and are granted mitigating circumstances.
