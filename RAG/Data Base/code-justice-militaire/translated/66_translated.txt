Any individual obligated to perform military service who fails to respond to the summons during peacetime within the specified timeframe for joining the designated unit shall be punished by imprisonment for a period ranging from one month to one year. In the event of war, this punishment is increased to a period ranging from two to five years. Moreover, those who evade service shall be directed to their respective units to perform the required military service, taking into account the relevant special laws pertaining to military service.
