The third-class medal is similar to the grander class medal, having a diameter of 50 millimeters.  At its center, it bears the same design as the grander class, but smaller in size.

This medal is worn on the left side of the chest, suspended by a sash 37 millimeters wide, and has the same specifications stipulated in Article 21 of the Code.  On the sash, there is a white rose, 27 millimeters in diameter, with its colors matching those of the medal.
