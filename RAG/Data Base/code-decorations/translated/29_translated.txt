The National Order of Merit is awarded to recognize individuals who have distinguished themselves in performing public service or engaging in private activities, and who, through their competence and dedication, have contributed to the country's growth and the strengthening of its institutions.

The sectors and areas of activity covered by the Order of Merit shall be determined by decree.

The Order is accompanied by a badge and a certificate signed by the Minister responsible for the sector and the field of activity covered by the Order of Merit.
