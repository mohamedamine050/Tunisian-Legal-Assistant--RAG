In the event of the death of a worker due to a work accident, the Order of Labor may be awarded posthumously without the employer having submitted a proposal for it.

In the situation set forth in Paragraph 2 of Article 42 of this Code, a member of the deceased worker's family may address a simple request to the employer, who, if deemed beneficial, shall submit a proposal for the award of the Order to the concerned party posthumously, in accordance with the forms stipulated in Article 48 of this Code.
