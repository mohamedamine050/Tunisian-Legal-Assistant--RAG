The Order of Merit of Customs shall grant a reward to Customs officers who have distinguished themselves in the performance of their duties through their courage, integrity, or competence, subject to the following conditions:

- **For the Second Class:** This applies to all officers and staff, regardless of their rank, who have satisfactorily served for a minimum of ten years in the performance of their duties.

- **For the First Class:** This applies to all officers and staff, regardless of their rank, who have satisfactorily served for a minimum of eight years in the performance of their duties since receiving the Second Class Order of Merit.

The Order of Merit shall be accompanied by a badge or a certificate signed by the Minister of Finance.
