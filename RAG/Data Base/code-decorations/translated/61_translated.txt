The Medal of Honor for the Internal Security Forces may be awarded to officers and security personnel of the President of the Republic, official figures, the National Security, the National Guard, the Penitentiary Services, Correctional Work, and Civil Protection, regardless of their rank, subject to the following conditions:

- For the Second Class: Officers and personnel, regardless of their rank, who have served satisfactorily for at least eight years in the performance of their duties.

- For the First Class: Officers and personnel, regardless of their rank, who have served satisfactorily for at least eight years in the performance of their duties since receiving the Second Class Medal.

- The Medal shall be awarded with a badge and a certificate signed by the Minister concerned.
