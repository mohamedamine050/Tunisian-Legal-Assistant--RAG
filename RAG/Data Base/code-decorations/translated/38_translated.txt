The Order of Labor shall be awarded to Tunisian workers employed in institutions of industry, commerce, services, agriculture, and their subsidiaries, regardless of their type.

This Order shall also be awarded to salaried workers in the liberal professions, traditional industrial institutions, mutual benefit societies, civil companies, labor unions, associations, and groups, regardless of their type.

The Order of Labor may be awarded to employees of the state, local public authorities, and public institutions with administrative character.
