The nomination files must indicate the candidates' names, titles, nationalities, professions, and residences, as well as the qualifications that make them eligible for the distinction for which they have been nominated. Where applicable, the ranks held in this order should be stated, along with the date of their last promotion within it.

These files shall be accompanied by a statement of birth and a criminal record extract. However, this requirement shall not apply to:

a) Candidates for a special decoration.

b) Public officials.

c) Military personnel.

d) Internal security forces personnel.

e) Foreign nationals and members of the diplomatic and consular corps accredited to the Tunisian Republic.
