## English Translation:

National decorations shall comprise the Grand Cordon of the Grand Chancellor of the Order, reserved for the President of the Republic, the Cordon assigned to the President of the Republic upon the completion of his term, and a Cordon specifically awarded to Tunisian or foreign individuals on a merit basis.

Furthermore, they shall encompass the following classes and ranks:

**Grand Class:** Grand Rank

**First Class:** First Rank

**Second Class:** Second Rank

**Third Class:** Third Rank

**Fourth Class:** Fourth Rank
