The Grand Collar of the Grand Chancellor shall be identical to the Grand Collar stipulated in Article 12 of this Code. The decorations shall be painted in blue.

The insignia shall consist of a medal similar to the medal of the second class, with the following differences:

- It shall be slightly larger in size.
- Its arms shall be studded with diamonds.

The names of the Presidents of the Republic, Grand Chancellors of the Order, and the dates of their assumption of office and, if applicable, the dates of their termination of office shall be engraved on its back.

The Commander's collar shall be gilded and identical in shape to the Grand Collar of the Order, provided that the insignia shall not be studded with diamonds.
