The third-class medal is similar to the larger class medal, with a diameter of 50 millimeters.  The center of the medal features the same design as the larger class, but smaller in size.

This medal is worn on the left side of the chest, suspended by a ribbon 35 millimeters in width.  The ribbon has the same specifications as those set out in Article 14 of this code.  A rosette, 27 millimeters in diameter, is attached to the ribbon, with colors matching those of the medal.
