The third-class decoration is similar to the larger class decoration, with a diameter of 50 millimeters.  At its center, it features the same design as the larger class, but smaller in size. 

The decoration is worn on the left side of the chest, suspended by a sash measuring 37 millimeters in width.  The sash possesses the same specifications as those outlined in Article 34 of this decree.  On the sash, there is a rosette suspended from a loop, whose colors match those of the decoration.
