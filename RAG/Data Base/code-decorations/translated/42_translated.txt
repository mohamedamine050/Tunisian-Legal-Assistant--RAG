The Order of Labor may be awarded to workers, provided that they meet the required conditions for its award.

This Order may be awarded posthumously.

-1 Without any conditions, to workers and employees who are killed in an accident while performing their work.

-2 To workers and employees who, at the time of their death, met the conditions specified in Articles 37 to 41 of this Code, provided that a request for the Order is submitted within a period of two years from the date of their death.
