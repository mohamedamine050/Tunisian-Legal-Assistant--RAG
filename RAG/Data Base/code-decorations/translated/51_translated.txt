The Order of Labor comprises five grades:

- **Exceptional Grade** ("Golden Order") awarded without any condition and exceptionally to individuals who have rendered valuable services in the field of labor.

- **Grand Grade** ("Golden Order") awarded after 30 years of actual services.

- **First Grade** ("Silver-Gilded Order") awarded after 25 years of actual services.

- **Second Grade** ("Silver Order") awarded after 20 years of actual services.

- **Third Grade** ("Bronze Order") awarded after 15 years of actual services.
