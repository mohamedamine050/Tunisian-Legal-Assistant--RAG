Students whose second stage of medical studies have been validated are eligible to register for Family Medicine, provided they meet one of the following criteria: 

* **Selected Family Medicine at the end of the third year of the second stage of medical studies (M.S.M.D. 3) according to procedures established by a joint decision of the Minister in charge of Higher Education and the Minister in charge of Health (amended by Government Order No. 1195 of 2015 dated September 4, 2015);** 
* **Are not candidates for the residency examination in medicine;**
* **Successfully passed the residency examination and definitively chose Family Medicine, in which case these students will have priority when choosing their internship department;**
* **Chose to train in Family Medicine after completing a maximum of two years in residency in another medical specialty.**
