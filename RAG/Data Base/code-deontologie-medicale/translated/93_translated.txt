In addition to the information provided in the first paragraph of Chapter 24 of this Code, physicians practicing in a group clinic shall place a sign above the door of the clinic or at the entrance of the building indicating "Group Clinic." This sign shall be followed by the names and titles of the physicians practicing in this clinic. The size of this sign shall not exceed 40 cm by 60 cm. The indication "Group Clinic" must be placed on the documents referred to in Chapters 23 and 27 of this Code.
