The free practice of medicine is carried out within individual or collective clinics, specializing in one or multiple fields, or within professional civil companies. Unless otherwise stipulated by legislation, regulations, or general administrative decisions not related to a specific individual, medical clinics, whether individual, collective, or affiliated with professional civil companies, shall not be established outside the premises of private healthcare institutions.

Crimes defined as felonies in the meaning of this law are those that incur a penalty of death or imprisonment exceeding five years.

Crimes defined as misdemeanors are those that incur a penalty of imprisonment exceeding fifteen days but not exceeding five years, or a fine exceeding sixty dinars.

Crimes defined as violations are those that incur a penalty not exceeding fifteen days of imprisonment or sixty dinars in fines. (Article 122 of the Code of Criminal Procedure)
