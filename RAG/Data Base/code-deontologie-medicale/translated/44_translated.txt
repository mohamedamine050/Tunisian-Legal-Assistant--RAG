**English Translation:**

**Transitionally, three periods, each lasting four months, shall be added to the internal internship prescribed by Order No. 487 of 2008 dated February 18, 2008, amending Order No. 2601 of 1995 dated December 25, 1995, referred to above. This measure shall apply to students who completed the two-year internal internship starting from the academic year 2011-2012.**
