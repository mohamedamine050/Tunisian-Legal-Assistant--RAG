The noticeboard at the entrance of the premises, if it concerns a professional civil company, must include the following indication: "Professional Civil Company for Doctors," along with the company's social name and the names and titles of the partners. This noticeboard must not exceed 40 cm by 60 cm in size. The physician partner must place the phrase "Professional Company for Doctors," above the noticeboard on the door of his private examination room, along with the company's social name and the information stipulated in the first paragraph of Chapter 24 of this journal. The indication "Professional Civil Company for Doctors" must also be placed on the documents referred to in Chapters 23 and 27 of this journal.
