Licenses to practice medicine or dentistry may be granted:

a) By the Minister of Health, for the benefit of internal or resident trainees in medicine or dentistry who are called upon to practice in state hospitals and health facilities.

b) By the regional council of the relevant professional order, for the benefit of internal or resident trainees in medicine or dentistry who are called upon to perform replacements in private clinics and health facilities.

The conditions and forms of granting these licenses and the procedures for these replacements shall be determined by a decision of the Minister of Health, after consulting with the National Council of the relevant professional order.
