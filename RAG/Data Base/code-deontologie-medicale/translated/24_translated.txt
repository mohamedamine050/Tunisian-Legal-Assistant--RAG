A physician is permitted to display only the following information on the plaque located at the entrance of the building or on the door of his clinic: name, surname, degrees, specialties, days and hours of clinic opening.  The length of this plaque shall not exceed 30 cm and its width shall not exceed 25 cm. As for the information that he is permitted to display on the door of his residence, it shall be: name and surname preceded by the word "Doctor", regardless of the form of practice.
