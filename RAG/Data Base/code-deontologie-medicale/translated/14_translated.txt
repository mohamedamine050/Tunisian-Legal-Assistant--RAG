The deliberations of the Regional Council shall be closed and no person outside the Council may attend them, except that the President of the National Council of the Bar or a member of the National Council duly authorized may attend the sessions of the Regional Council with an advisory vote. Public sessions may be held by professional bodies, in accordance with the procedural rules set out in this Guide, in order to obtain specific information on the work or activities carried out by the public administration, to ensure the soundness, integrity, transparency and consistency of its performance with the requirements of the public interest. The President of the Regional Council may also invite a representative of the Ministry of Public Health and/or a legal counsel to attend the work of the Council with an advisory vote. Decisions shall be taken by a majority of the members present. In the event of a tie, the President's vote shall be decisive.
