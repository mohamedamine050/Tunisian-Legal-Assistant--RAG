The following actions are prohibited:

Firstly: Any act that would provide an unjustified or unlawful material benefit to a patient.

Secondly: Any monetary or in-kind reimbursement to a patient.

Thirdly: Any sharing of fees between physicians, particularly any arrangement involving the referral, acceptance, or division of fees among them in a hidden manner.

Fourthly: Brokerage for any person whatsoever.

Fifthly: Accepting brokerage for any medical service, especially in exchange for examinations, prescriptions for medicines and equipment, referral to rehabilitation centers, or to private healthcare institutions.
