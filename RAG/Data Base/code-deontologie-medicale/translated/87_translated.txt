A group of physicians from the same specialty or from different specialties may be formed as follows:

a) Within the framework of a group practice.
b) Within the framework of a professional civil company.

The purpose of this group of physicians must be to improve the physical organization of their work and to share in the use of professional equipment and premises.

Sharing in fees is only acceptable in the event of the creation of a professional civil company of physicians or a group practice with a single specialty.
