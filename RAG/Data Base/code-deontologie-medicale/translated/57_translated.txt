A physician may delegate his practice to colleagues or medical students among his peers, in accordance with the terms and procedures stipulated in the applicable regulations and after obtaining the approval of the Regional Council of the Order, which shall be notified immediately to assess the availability of the required ethical and professional conditions. The compensation must be prominently displayed at the entrance of the physician's office who has been delegated, and it must also be mentioned in the documents delivered by the physician performing the delegation.
