**(Amended by Government Order No. 1195 of 2015 dated September 4, 2015).**  Medical schools shall grant a National Doctor of Medicine degree to students who meet the following conditions collectively: 

1.  They have successfully passed the examinations of the second stage and obtained confirmation of the completion of their internships for this stage.
2.  They have successfully passed the clinical examinations. 
3.  They have obtained a qualification to practice family medicine granted by the National Family Medicine Authority or have completed a fully certified residency program in accordance with the provisions of Article 37 of this Government Order.
4.  They have successfully defended their dissertations.
