**English Translation:**

The following shall be considered to be practicing medicine or dentistry illegally:

1. Any person who does not meet all the conditions stipulated in Articles 1, 2, and 3 of this law and habitually, in any manner whatsoever, diagnoses or treats diseases or surgical ailments, whether hereditary or acquired, real or potential, through personal actions, oral or written consultations, or by any other means. 

2. Any physician or dentist who possesses legal qualifications and exceeds the scope of practice authorized by law, particularly by assisting the individuals referred to in the preceding paragraph with the intent to circumvent the provisions of this law.

3. Any physician or dentist who practices their profession during periods of suspension stipulated by this law.
