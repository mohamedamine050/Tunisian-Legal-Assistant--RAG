(Amended by Government Decree No. 1195 of 2015 dated September 4, 2015).- Tunisian medical schools award a Certificate of Specialty in Medicine to medical residents who have completed a full residency period, as stipulated in Article 37 of this Government Decree, and who have successfully passed the national specialization examination, encompassing both practical and written assessments. Candidates for this examination must hold the national Doctor of Medicine degree.
