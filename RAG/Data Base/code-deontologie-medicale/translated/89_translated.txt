The following provisions must also be observed when practicing medicine within a collective clinic or a professional civil partnership:

a) Respect for the professional independence of each physician.
b) Freedom of the patient to choose their physician.
c) Each physician must have a personal examination room at their disposal.
d) Every document must bear the name of the signing physician.
e) Compensation between physicians in the clinic must take place in the examination room of the physician providing the compensation.
f) A written agreement must be concluded that sets out the means of practice as well as the rights and obligations of the physicians involved. This agreement must be submitted to the regional council of the medical order for endorsement after verifying its conformity with the laws and regulations in force.
