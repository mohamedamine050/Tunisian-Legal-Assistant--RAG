Successful candidates in the competitive examination shall assume their positions as residents within the first week of January following the year in which the results of the examination are announced. Contrary to the provisions of the first paragraph of this chapter, successful candidates in the competitive examination whose internships for the fourth year of the second stage of medical studies have not been validated or who are still subject to a deferment, shall have their assumption of office postponed until January of the following year in order to settle their situations. (Added by Government Decree No. 1195 of 2015 dated September 4, 2015).
