The internal internship or the fourth year of the second stage of medical studies (M.S.M.S. 4) begins on the first of January of each year and comprises three periods of internal internship, each lasting four months. One of these periods is in medicine, and another in surgery. The third period of internship shall be undertaken, as a matter of obligation, in the departments of Obstetrics and Gynecology or in Pediatrics for internal interns in Family Medicine.
