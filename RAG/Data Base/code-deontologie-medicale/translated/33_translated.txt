(Added by Government Decree No. 834 of 2017, dated July 19, 2017) - The resident who was previously registered on the list of priority entities shall choose the specialization and the entity within the scope of that list. Before commencing his duties, he shall submit a commitment to the relevant authorities of the Ministry of Health, to be certified by his signature, to work at the entity concerned for a period equivalent to the period of residency after obtaining the national certificate of specialization in medicine. The form of the commitment referred to in the first paragraph of this section shall be determined by a decision of the Minister of Health.
