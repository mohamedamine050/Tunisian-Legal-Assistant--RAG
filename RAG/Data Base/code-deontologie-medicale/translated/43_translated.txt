All previous provisions contrary to this order are hereby repealed, particularly Order No. 2601 of 1995, dated December 25, 1995, concerning the setting of the general framework for the study system and the conditions for obtaining the National Doctorate in Medicine, as well as the provisions of Articles 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7 (paragraphs 1 and 2), 8, 9, 16, 17, and 18 of Order No. 1440 of 1993, dated June 23, 1993, concerning specialization in medicine and the legal regime of residents. The phrase "specialization in medicine" is also deleted from the title of Order No. 1440 of 1993, dated June 23, 1993, concerning specialization in medicine and the legal regime of residents.
